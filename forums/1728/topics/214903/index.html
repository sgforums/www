    Ghost is a formless consciousness yet taking a non physical shape according to their desires and attachment and manifesting according to their karma, that is invisible to our naked eyes. It's impossible for our consciousness to 'possess' these non physical shapes, whereas it is relatively easier for a consciousness to take up the shape of physical things. Therefore we have Tree Spirits, and other kind of spirits that inhabit physical things. It is not that the tree have consciousness, the tree originally have no consciousness in itself but consciousness takes up the shape of the form, become attached to the form. When my master stayed at my dharma teacher's house for lodging, he told my dharma teacher to immediately take down.. I think it was some kind of animal head hanging on the wall or something. Something taken from an animal. It was only years later that she realised that a spirit had inhabited in that form.

    Is there any way to prevent possession? There are many ways.. usually a person is possessed because there is little 'brightness' in him. In such cases as I mentioned before, chanting buddha and bodhisattva names will be advisable as this will increase one's mental 'brightness'. Spirits will not harm such a person.

    The most assured and important way is to practise the dharma. Through correct practise, we can completely block off the possibility of spirit possession.
