    Dear Franceso Totti,

    This is a hard one. It is too easy to slide into the hole and too difficult to get out.

    Firstly, you got to have lots of determination and patience with yourself and never give up no matter how many times you give in to your desires. Unlike other kinds of wants, this is a really tough one. I do not know how deep you are into it, but regardless, never give up.

    Next, it takes a whole lot of restraining. Restraining your eyes is the first step. If you know that some sights or some pictures are going to stimulate you, then force yourself not to look at it. Restrain yourself. However, take everything gradually. If you feel that the more you restrain yourself, the worse your urge becomes, then release some restraint. You got to adjust yourself. Always make the training tough but not so tough until it breaks and it back-fires on you.

    Next, find a temple or teacher and practice concentration meditation. Establish yourself FIRMLY in it. I mean really firmly that you really like to be meditating and it becomes almost a second nature to you. This takes a whole lot of time. So have patience. A deep addiction in sex and lust can easily take several years to cure and many many relapses. I mean really many! But you just have to keep on trying. So during this whole time, cultivate your concentration meditation skill. The best is that you can get into Jhana. A monk, Ajahn Brahm mentioned that the bliss in Jhana is better than sex. Anyhow, if you have established your mind enough in concentration, then whenever such thoughts arise, you are able to disengage yourself from such thoughts and hold it at arms length without getting affected. Actually if you notice, these sexual thoughts come only when your mind is relatively free. When you have a lot of work to do and you throw yourself into your work, you realise that it does not appear. Sometimes, when they appear, and someone calls you to do something impt and that issue takes your mind away, you realise the sexual thoughts are immediately gone and replaced by those other thoughts. So these urges can disappear. It is just the mind. These sexual thoughts really do not have power over your mind. They are also guests. so they can leave. Realising this, you know that sometimes when you feel totally gripped by these thoughts and you have no choice, it is just like they have a veil over you. But surely you can make them leave if you master your mind and or if you have the right thought replacing techniques.

    The third thing you can do is that you can make a promise to someone you really respect and love that you will restrain yourself. And you try really hard not to break the promise. This helps. So whenever you think of these thoughts, then you will also think of your promise and it gives you strength when your mind is weak.

    Basically, these kind of things happen when one's mind is weak and one is lacking in determination. So training of the mind is essential. Thus while training do not be too concerned with failures. Once your mind is well trained enough, plus using the third technique, you should be able to do it.

    Lastly, I would like to say that the training of the mind takes time, so sometimes it may be unrealistic to expect results in like months or maybe a year. A few years is more like it. And never give up in the face of failures. One's mind is not constant. Sometimes it's strong, sometimes weak. And when these thoughts meet you when you are strong, no problem, you can overcome them and not get affected by them. And you are happy when you have overcome them. However, sometimes when your mind is weak and these thoughts arise, you just succumb to them so easily and you become sad. Don't worry about the failures, just keep trying. You will make it one day. It is a gradual process.

    Hmmm....when these thoughts come, it is like a battle ground, you become very 'high', your whole body changes (i think you understand), but just try to live through it with great determination and steadfastness.

    Hopefully this helps and may the Buddha and all the devas bless you in your endeavour!

    Metta_(|)_

    TS'IG SUM NÄD DEG

    THREE WORDS STRIKING THE ESSENCE

    BY

    RIG'DZIN GA'RAB DORJE

     

    WITH A BRIEF COMMENTARY BY KYABJE DÜD'JOM RINPOCHE, 'JIGDRÄL YESHE DORJE

     

    Directly meeting with one's own nature

    This fresh and present awareness beyond all consideration of the three times, is, in itself, the primordial awakenedness that is spontaneously present pure awareness. Such is direct introduction to one's own nature.

    Directly make one single decision

    Since all phenomenal appearance of both cyclic existence and the state beyond suffering do not go beyond the innate creativity of pure awareness, firmly decide that there is no other thing besides the continuous flow of this pure awareness .

    Whatever discursive thoughts arise, be they gross or subtle, by recognising them for what they are, they all arise and simultaneously dissolve into the vast expanse of the dimension of pure reality — awareness–voidness inseparable. Remain confident in this liberation.
