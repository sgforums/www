    Routing of a Dharma Drum Monastery MOnk

    4am Wake up /Wash-up

    4:30am Morning Exercise

    5am  Chan and Pureland tradition Morning Chanting

    7am meditation

    7:30am Breakfast

    8am - 10am Lesson

    10am -3pm Visit by Layman ,Q and A session+Afternoon Puja to the Buddhas,Lunch

     

    3pm -3:30pm rest

    3:30pm Preparation for Chanting

    5pm Evening chanting

    6pm -9pm Lesson

    9:30pm Resting the Wood(Last activity of the day , washup)

    Sleep

     

    I went there for the first day i wasnt accustomed .because i still a layman to go and understand monastic lifestyle

    After the two days there , i feel quite peaceful
