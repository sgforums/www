    "The Innate Drive for Happiness

    This innate drive to be happy is the true nature of existence itself just being as it is. The drive appears due to our sense of separation from this true nature. The more out of harmony we are, the stronger we seek after happiness. This struggle for happiness is ironically unhappiness. Happiness is not achievable through this struggle. When we no longer seek after it, a true innate joy is naturally present."
