    The citations of the antarabhava can be found in several Mahayana sutras, like Ksitigarbha Sutra, Amitabha Sutra, etc (sinweiy could tell us more?), and several commentaries like the 'Essay on the Meaning of Mahayana' (da cheng yi zhang), in Vajrayana it is clearly described in the Tibetan Book of the Dead, and there are also several Pali Suttas which described the Anatarabhava.

    Ajahn Brahmavamso and Ajahn Chah themselves also believed in the existence of the intermediate state (antarabhava). If there is no intermediate state, then there cannot be near-death experiences taking place... or 'mind floating out of the body'.
