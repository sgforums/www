    Volunteers needed to help out in the following event, do feel free to forward this to all your friends.

    Event : Heart Flag Day
    Date : 8 July 2006
    Organiser : Singapore Heart Foundation
    Timing : Shift 1 (AM) 10.30am to 2.30pm
    Shift 2 (PM) 1.30pm to 5.30pm
    Shift 3 (night) 4.30pm to 8.30pm
    Locations : Islandwide

    Collection Centres :
    Woodlands MRT
    Jurong East MRT
    Jurong Point Shopping Centre
    Outram MRT
    Somerset MRT (afternoon and night shift only)
    Aljunied MRT
    Tampines MRT

    The Singapore Heart Foundation is hoping to raise the funds for the
    following

    - Providing financial aid to needy cardiac patients
    - Promoting community-based research programmes
    - Running the SHF Cardiac Rehabilitation Centre
    - Promoting the JRFH (Jump Rope for Heart) programme in primary
    schools
    - Conduction public educational programmes to promote heart
    health in our community

    For those who are interested, please do provide the following informations to Charles at ccltan@singnet.com.sg of Volunteer Resource Network:

    1) Name
    2) NRIC
    3) Contact No
    4) Email address
    5) Shift (Full day are welcome)
    6) Collection Centres
