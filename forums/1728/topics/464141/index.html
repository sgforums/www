    Today, Archaya Mahayogi Shridhar Rinpoche personally asked his student to inform me about their free online magazine, I thanked the guru and signed up. I also mentioned that I have dreamt of receiving teachings from him before.

    Below is the biography     of this great teacher Archaya Mahayogi Shridhar Rinpoche. He used to practice the Hindu tantras and Vedanta under a qualified Vedanta teacher for nine years intensely in the cemetaries, etc. Eventually he realized the Atman-Brahman, the ultimate goal of Hinduism, and his realization was confirmed by his Vedantic masters to be correct and profound.

    However, still unsatisfied with his realization, he continued searching, first into Zen Buddhism, then later into the Vajrayana teachings, including the tantras, mahamudra and dzogchen teachings, and practiced them thoroughly until he attained realization and was asked to start teaching by his teachers. But he continues to go into deep meditation retreats for many many years. He continues to be in practice retreats and share his knowledge with others.
