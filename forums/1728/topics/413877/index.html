    H.E. Tulku Mugsang Kuchen Rinpoche will be distributing the late His Holiness Penor Rinpoche Kudhung Rilbu on 31st October 2010 after the Namcho Mahakala Puja.
    Kudhung Rilbus are holy pills made of nectar droplets from His Holiness holy body during his thugdham (meditation stage).

    When His Holiness passed away, he was resting in the clear light nature for 7 days experiencing the primordial wisdom. During that time, nectar flowed out from his holy body as a sign of enlightenment.

    The Holy Pill is very precious and sacred where one can receive blessings. You can put this Holy Pill in a locket pendant and wear it as a protection. You may also consecrate it in a statue, stupa or any holy object and placed it on the shrine or in front of you, when you are doing your prayers, practices and prostrations to receive blessing and protection too.

    Namcho Mahakala Puja from 29/10 to 31/10 in conjunction with Lha Bab Duchen, which falls on 29/10.Lha Bab Duchen is one of the 4 great festivals of Lord Buddha, Sakyamuni. On this day, Buddha Sakyamuni descent from heaven. Buddha's mother was reborn in Indra's Heaven. In order to repay her kindness, to liberate her and to benefit the Gods, Buddha spent 3 months teaching in the realm of God. On this day, all positive and negative actions are multiplied by 10 million times.

    Mahakala, a protector of the Buddhist teachings, is also known as the chief dharmapala.  Dharmapalas are basically Buddhas who undertake the role of removing obstacles on the path of practitioners.The objective of Mahakala is to assist practitioners in removing anything that impedes their practice, as well as to encourage exertion and devotion and at the same time purify obscurations and wrongdoings.  Blessings will be bestowed and obstacles will be removed if one prays earnestly to Mahakala.

    So long as one attends the prayer sessions and continually makes prayers and offerings to Mahakala, the protections of Mahakala and His retinue will not depart from oneself until perfect awakening is experienced.  Mahakala prayers also foster peacefulness in a country, harmony at home and better career prospects.
