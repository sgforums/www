    Oddiyana Retreat and Pilgrimage 2011

    Wogmin Thubten Shedrup Ling Monastery in Rewalsar have great pleasure in announcing the 5th Oddiyana Retreat and Pilgrimage Starting on 2nd February 2011. 

    Drikung Lho Ontul Rinpoche will be teaching on the following subjects:

    Sun and Moon Amulet (Breathing Yoga) Yangzab by Drikung Rinchen Phuntsok 
    Empowerment, Teaching and Practice of The Three Kayas:
    Dharmakaya – Amitabha
    Sambhogakaya – Avalokiteshvara
    Nirmanakaya – Padmasambhava
    Rainbow body attainment Chod by Terton Nuden Dorje
    Swiftly perfecting the two accumulations through the offering of the body
    Each day will end with a Red and White Smoke Offering

    The teaching will last for 7 days after which Ontul Rinpoche will lead a pilgrimage to Sanchi, Ellora, Ajanta and Kenheri Caves.
