    There are currently 3 books I know to be the most popular introductory books to Buddhism, not in any particular order:



    Buddhism Plain and Simple
    Steve Hagen



    Awakening the Buddha Within: Tibetan Wisdom for the Western World
    Lama Surya Das


    The Heart of the Buddha's Teaching
    Thich Nhat Hanh
    You can find them in all major bookstores, like MPH, Times, Popular, Borders, Kinokuniya, etc.

    And not to forget the library (you can check the availability of books in libraries here: http://vistaweb.nlb.gov.sg/)

    Meanwhile, the internet also have some useful info: http://www.buddhanet.net/e-learning/basic-guide.htm

    Enjoy reading.

    Hi... you can also try the following :

    Good Question Good Answer



    In this book Ven. Dhammika gives clear, thoughtful and lively answers to more than a hundred commonly asked questions about Buddhism. A popular book, Good Question Good Answer has been translated into fourteen languages and more than 150,000 copies are in print.
    Please also go to www.goodquestiongoodanswer.net to read this book.

    Price : Free
    Available @ Buddha Dhamma Mandala Society (BDMS)
    http://www.bdms.org.sg/

    All About Buddhism



    If you have ever wondered about what the Buddha really taught, you must read this book. Written by a well-known Australian Buddhist monk in a clear and accessible style, All About Buddhism is an in-depth study of the most important teachings of the Buddha.
    What is a Buddha ?
    Should Buddhists be ambitious ?
    What does Buddhism say about sex, marriage and divorce ?
    How is meditation practised ?
    These are just some of the many questions answered in this book.
    All About Buddhism presents this ancient teachings as it is;
    a humane and dynamic philosophy of life as relevant today as
    when it first proclaimed nearly twenty-five centuries ago.

    Price : S&15
    Available @ BDMS & Evergreen Books
    http://www.evergreenbuddhist.com/
