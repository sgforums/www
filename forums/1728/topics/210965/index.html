    Yes thats right! It's a neverending cycle, so unless you are a practitioner who recognises such folly and is detached from anger, there is endless cycle of samsaric suffering.

    Now in Chinese we always say 'Yuan1 Qing1', that is not refering only to spirits. It's refering basically to 'love and hate' relationships, 'yuan' means hate, while 'qing' means very close - they fluctuate from one state to another. The one you are closest to is often the one you are most angry with (at times). This is because the ego is functioning very strong. This is the viscious cycle.

    But once that cycle reaches a glimpse of truth (seen the dharma), it stops. You see the truth, you see the way to end all these nonsense and the nightmare ends. When you stop, the opposite party also stops.
