    In conjunction with the anniversary of Lord Gampopa on 25th July 2010 Sunday, we are organizing robes offering to the invited monks of the 3 Yanas and Lamas at Jangchub Choeling Monastery as well as Anis at Tarpa Choeling Nunnery.

    It is said that by offering robes to the Sangha, one could accumulate immeasurable merits. To date, the Theravada Tradition still practises the Kathina Robes Offering to the Sangha as one of the important merit-making events for devotees. The teaching says that a devotee who offers the Kathina robe would gain the following five beneficial results like the Sangha:

    1. They can travel everywhere without coming across dangers;
    2. Their garments can be obtained effortlessly;
    3. Whatever food consumed by them would yield only benefits to the body without incurring any harm;
    4. They will enjoy abundant possessions; and
    5. Their property will always be secured and not be destroyed by fire or water, confiscated by authorities, stolen or fall into the hands of the unworthy.

    The robe offering to the invited monks includes gifts and devotees may donate any amount towards this act. To enable as many devotees to participate in this meritorious event and gain immeasurable merits for the practice of enlightenment, each person is limited to offer one set of robe to Lamas and Anis at the cost of $50. Rejoice!
