    Hi Diaboltical

    Is not what you chant that can make the different in the person life! It is what you must do.

    Before one think of changing another person, one needs to change oneself. Am I angry with that person for the etc things...... Is there room in my heart to forgive him or her.

    Personally, no matter what mantra or sutra one is chanting. We need to internalise the mantra essence in our life first before it can take effect on others.

    When it took effect in one life, it will become part of our daily life. It will be reflect in our body, speech and mind which will influence the other person.
