    Just kidding. Anyway not so good to discuss in detail on open forum and may not be the path of everyone... but anyway,

    Since that day you have already received fa wang fa, fa wang fa should become your natural state. Fa wang fa isn't practised, it isn't a method, it is a 'wu wei fa'. If you can just remain in this natural state, then you can 'guan tong' everything... even precepts, samadhi, wisdom is in it. Therefore master says, 'xiu1 xing2 bu ru xiu1 shen1' (cultivate moral conduct is better than cultivating our actions), 'xiu1 xing2 bu ru xiu1 xin1' (cultivating mind is better than cultivating moral conduct), 'xiu1 xin1 bu ru wu2 sheng4' (therefore birthlessness is our natural state, and is not called 'cultivation'/'xiu', is wu wei). We should maintain this natural state, which is to 'chi2' this 'fa', as far as possible in our lives. But most of us can't (maintain natural state throughout our living), so we can practise certain methods like meditation, chanting, etc... to gather strength. But if you can sit in your natural state, that is also 'methodless meditation', wu wei sitting. The difference between natural state and breathing mindfulness is that for breathing mindfulness, the awareness/mindfulness is on breathing, while natural state is 'whole awareness'. Sitting meditation is impt to keep as a habit (at least 30 min a day) especially those who 'chuan fa'. For me I still practise breathing mindfulness. If you received 'shi wei yi fa' you can practise that, that is also 'you wei fa' but you haven't receive and neither has I.

    Furthermore I must say, this cultivation thing in Buddhism isn't just following methods... although depending, they may help. 8 Fold Path is not a method.. following certain methods gather strength but doesn't mean we will become enlightened just by that. Cultivation is the entire way of being, living the enlightened way. If you have any habits left, that is an opportunity to cultivate. If you meet someone and is angry, that is an opportunity to cultivate. When you are in a tense situation you can't just tell him 'excuse me, let me meditate for 5 minutes before continuing my argument'.
