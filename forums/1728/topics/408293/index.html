    hi 18th Vow, i think the fo guang mentioned by Positron comes when you are sincere enough with your practice and Amitabha blesses you.  It is a sign of blessing. 

    The light from body is your own energy... also will flux according to your own practice, health so forth.

    As AEN said, if you really serious to practice, then don't bother too much about the various light phenomenas happening... tat is only eye consciousness, we should only aim at liberation, transcend even the 8th consciousness.

    All these lights are energy. It is nothing special deserving interest. For eg, we walk, that requires energy. Food gives us energy. Electricity is energy. All these are simply physics of life. It is catchy because it is little talked about. eg body aura... but not important for real spiritual progress and ending of suffering.

    Look inwards not outwards for progress. Eg, in the past, i am very attached to something, something happening will make me angry easily etc. now after my practice, did i change?

    Real practice result show in our daily life. It is not a flashy thing... but such things are heartfelt changes and will give us true freedom.  Freedom from boredom, loneliness, selfishness, indifference,  desire, anger.  And will make us alive , vibrant, joyful, carefree, loving, kind and compassionate. And when death comes, we have ultimate confidence and freedom.
