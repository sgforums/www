Sakya Prayers for Dharma Teachings and Empowerments

    For Dharma Teachings the general sequence of prayers is:

    1. Lineage Prayers
    2. 37 Heap Mandala Offering (with Gang Gi Cho Dzay Ma offering)
    3. Request to Turn the Wheel of Dharma
    Teachings
    4. 7 Heap Mandala Offering
    5. Dedication (Long)

    For Empowerments the general sequence of prayers is:

    1. Vajrasattva mantra (optional)
    2. 7 Heap Mandala Offering
    3. The Sevenfold Prayer (optionally covered in “Empowerment”)
    “Empowerment”
    4. Pledge to Follow Instructions
    5. 7 Heap Mandala Offering
    6. Offering of Oneself
    7. Dedication (Short)
    8. Long Life Prayers
    6. Long Life Prayers
