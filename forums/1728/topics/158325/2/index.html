    Anyway reminds me of some something. If a person's self-power is not strong, he will have to rely on other-power. However to rely on other-power implies that at death, there is still Antara-bhava (7th consciousness) to be delivered by Amitabha Buddha. On the other hand, a person who's self-power is enough, who is able to liberate from birth and death, may enter instantaneously into Ji guang jing tu - his antarabhava (7th consciousness) dissolves like a drop of rainwater enters the great ocean. Is there possibility of coming back? Yes, in both case, they may come back as Bodhisattvas out of their vows, but not as sentient beings undergoing samsaric rebirth.
