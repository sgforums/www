Luangpor Teean had come to Singapore to spread the teaching a few decades ago, but it seems that very few people know about this method of practice currently. His senior disciple, Luangpor Thong Abhakaro is currently busy spreading the teaching across the globe, including Taiwan & the west. Another senior disciple, Luangpor Kamkean Suwanno is also spreading the teaching to China. I quite like the simple teaching of Luangpor Kamkean. I think he is one with great wisdom.
