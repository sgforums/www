    Actually metta meditation is fine because it is done with kind intentions and good will (Buddha did advise the monks to do metta meditation when the spirits disturbed, and it worked very well), but it is important that one does not try to 'exorcise' or 'tackle' the spirits which might anger the spirits or inflict more harm upon yourself.

    If metta meditation doesn't work, or the spirit does not wish to leave, then it is advisable to get help from someone experienced -- like the venerables or rinpoches.
