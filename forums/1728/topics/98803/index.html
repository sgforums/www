    Mahayana - the Noble path of Exalting other on the Bodhi path

    Arhatship is about self-liberation ,but the limited strength of an arhat makes Them unable to save their parents and many past-lives parents who were
    still "stuck" in the sea of suffering

    So the Buddha preached the Path of the Mahayana "the Great Wheel"

    the path of helping others on the path of Bodhi path , becos so many many many living beings have not been able to hear the teachings of the Buddha
    when Buddha lived in India

    the central idea of a Mahayana Buddhist is to walk the Bodhisattva path
    (Bodhi mean awakened , sattva means sentient being )

    many Great Models of the Mahayana path are Avalokiteshvara
    Manjushri , Maitreya and so on ..

    Avalokiteshvara is popularly known as Guanyin
    Manjushri is commonly known as Wen shu pu sa
    Maitreya is known as mi le pu sa
