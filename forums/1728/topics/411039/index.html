    Attachment to Amituo is in line with dharma gate in Pureland buddhism as stated in Amitabha Sutra. From attachment of Amituo, it will gradually developed into detachment and achieve a higher land in Amitabha Paradise. If practitioner still did not achieve detachment, it will rebirth at the lower land in Amitabha Pureland. Having said this, the lower and higher lands are intertweaved together like the school students of different capacity studying in the same classroom :)
