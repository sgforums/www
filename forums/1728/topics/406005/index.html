Below are the two kinds of garudas: Multi-Colored Garuda and Black Garuda.

    When you get this protection card, first consecrate it well.Then put it above the front door or place it in different parts of the house: above windows, to the side of front door (outside), etc. The image should be protected and not destroyed by rain. By displaying this card, all the outer and inner evil enemies (living beings or bad feng shui) are stopped. It was said by Vajradharma, who is the All Knowing One, that this instruction can stop any kind of harm, including the nine harms. By displaying this protection in the house, activities that take place in the house and have no meaning will become meaningful and virtue will be created. In this way, having this protection in the home becomes good feng shui, a way of creating an auspicious environment.
