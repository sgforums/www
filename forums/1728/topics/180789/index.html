    renunciation: giving things up and away.....

    leaving one's homeland is accomplishing half the Dharma.
    - Milarepa

    Trungpa Rinpoche said, "usually we think of renunciation as celibacy, poverty, obedience, shaving your head, going off somewhere and leaving everything behind."
    he then gave a wider tantric interpretation of renunciation:
    "renunciation means to let go of holding back."

    it does mean abandoning our intense emotional attachments and compulsive preoccupations. do we really have to turn our backs on family, friends and work in order to practice Dharma? don't think so......

    if there is clinging, the practitioner's view is not the vast view.
    if there is resistence, the meditation is weak.
    if there is partiality, the activity is not enlightened action.
    free from these three is the way of the Natural Great Perfection.
    - Dzogchen Verse
