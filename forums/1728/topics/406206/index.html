    REPENT - PROSTRATE - WISH
    In conjunction with this Buddhist noble celebration of Avalokitesvara
    Enlightenment Day, Amitabha Centre is organizing a Three Step a Bow
    prayer in the Buddhist Kulai Charity Village which is also known as
    the Little Pratalaka. This is to commemorate the enlightenment of
    Avalokitesvara. Taking part in this event will help to purify your
    karma and yield blessing for you and your family.

    THREE STEPS A BOW - VEGETARIANISM
    The Prayer Path is specially tailored for Three Steps a Bow prayer in
    Buddhist Charity Village. The total steps for Prayer Path is 369 (3
    denotes the Triple Gem; 6 denotes the Six Paramitas and 9 is deemed to
    be auspicious number in Vajrayana Buddhism.) times which is equivalent
    to 123 bows. Buddhist Charity Village will also prepare a scrumptious
    vegetarian lunch for devotees during the event is held
