    Definitely a myth. Unless one practices deviant meditation, or focus in between the eyebrow, etc. Which are not Buddhist practices, and Thusness did mention that these practices could possibly open the channel to the spirit realm and interferences.

    On contrary, proper BUDDHIST meditation especially for experienced practitioners WILL close the channels to spirit attacks or possessions.

    Thusness said before, when Presence is experienced correctly, such channels to the spirit realms and possessions and interferences is being closed, and as Longchen said, "maintaining a nondual awareness should overcome the interference" (to all spirit attacks, possessions, etc).

    My taiwanese teacher spoke of his experiences of encountering a past life creditor coming to take his life during his meditation but couldn't affect him because of his meditative state.

    Oh and... remember Shariputra being hit by a powerful spirit, but shariputra wasn't affected because he was in a state of samadhi (diamond samadhi or something) and could not be harmed. The spirit immediately fell into avici hell.
