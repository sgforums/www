    Pilgrimage to Ladakh with Khandro Thrinlay Chodon July 24- August 9

    Travelling with Khandrola you will be
    welcomed into caves, hermitages and
    monasteries that are 700 years old; eat, drink
    and dance with locals; partake in a prayer
    festival and cultural activities with nomads,
    (soon to be an extinct way of life); cross the
    highest road in the world; travel into the
    famous Nubra valley; glimpse the silk road
    from China; visit yogis, ancient stupas,
    turquoise lakes; see nomad tents, starry
    nights, spotless skies, and deeply rest in the
    lap of vastness.

    Pilgrimage to Bhutan With Khandro Thrinlay Chodon Oct 20 - Nov 3

    Journey to the heart of the ancient kingdom of the Thunder Dragons, guided by Khandro Thrinlay Chodon, a genuine lineage holder of this ancient tradition.Khandrola is in a unique position to show you this land. She is the consort of the late His Holiness Ngawang Jigme, the ninth Shabdrung of Bhutan – an indispensible figure in the history of Bhutan. In addition she is the great granddaughter of the renowned yogi Togden Shakya Shri  an integral master of the esoteric meditation lineage of the Thunder Dragons. With her deep spiritual knowledge, her connections to the Land of Thunder Dragons as well as her understanding of the Western world, Khandro Rinpoche is the perfect host for this very special journey.
