  • An Eternal Now
    17 Jan 15, 10:40

    Nihilism is the wrong thinking that there is no karma, no cause and effect, no rebirth, etc.

    Non-attachment just means you no longer grasp or attach to wrong notions of self and the world... and attached to possessions, etc.



    An example of nihilism: there is only one life, so why bother about doing good?

    An example of non-attachment: the body is not me and mine, therefore seeing the body as impermanent and not-self, grasping to it has ceased.
