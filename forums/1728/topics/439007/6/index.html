    With regards to the pills, I assume you could be referring to Mani Pills. 

    Another forummer Weychin gave me this information in the past.

     

    About the Precious Mani Pills These pills have been prepared according to the proper Vajrayana ritual and method laid down in the Karma Kagyud Lineage. Under the guidance of the most Venerable Shangpa Rinpoche and Venerable Dupsing Rinpoche, the monks from Jangchub Choeling Monastery and their devotees have meditated and recited the Avalokiteshvara's Mantra (OM MANI PADME HUNG) over one hundred million times to bless the Pills.

     

     

    The sacred substances that were used in the production of the Pills are: Precious Mani pills made by Tripon Pema Chogyal Nectar pills made by Guru Rinpoche (Padmasambhava) and his twenty-five renowned disciples, and Mani Pills made by the 3rd Gyalwa Karmapa, Rangjung Dorje Black Pills of the 16th Gyalwa Karmapa Longevity pills made by the Great Translator Vairocana Collection of all precious blessing pills from the past sixteen Gyalwa Karmapas Material from Guru Rinpoche (Padmasambhava)'s hat Relics of Lord Gampopa, Urgyen Lingpa and 16th Gyalwa Karmapa Robes of Jetsun Milarepa and the 14th, 15th and 16th Gyalwa Karmapas Many other precious herbs from the sacred mountains Some of the Benefits

     

    According to Guru Rinpoche (Padmasambhava), whoever consumes the properly prepared "Mani" pills will be blessed both physically and spiritually. These pills can purify one's negative karmic forces and turn the mind towards the Dharma. They can prevent epidemic disease, poison, etc. and remove obstacles such as curses and black magic. A dying person will be liberated if he takes it.

     

    The pills can be consumed by themselves or dissolved in water for drinking. Alternatively, one can also inhale the fumes from burning the pounded pills. After taking the pills one should recite the Six-Syllable Mantra (OM MANI PADME HUNG) several times for the pills to be more effective.
