15Sep & 16Sep-full day workshop on empowering your life

  • anatta
    10 Sep 07, 08:50
    Venerable Thitadhammo (Shih Fa Shan) was raised in a Christian environment in Singapore. As a youth, he already exhibited a strong affinity to religion, leading him to devote himself to religion. However, his continual search for the Truth and the meaning of life and the end to suffering led him to Buddhism. To attain a better understanding of Buddhism, he started by enrolling for a short term meditation retreat in Thailand. His fervent pursuit of Buddhism saw him taking his monastic vows and his ordination as a monk later.

    Venerable Thitadhammo will be holding 2 full day workshops on 15 Sep 2007(Sat) and 16 Sep 2007 (Sun) from 9.00am - 5.30pm.
    Venue: Pohernshih temple.
    for more information, http://www.pohernshih.org/Home(E).htm

    On 15 Sep(Sat) Empowering Your Life
    Venerable Thitadhammo will expound on Buddha's teaching to teach us how to empower our life by using Mangala Sutta and Sallekha Sutta.

    On 16 Sep (Sun) Purposeful Living
    The session will be devoted to Sigalalovada Sutta that focus on the Buddha's advice to a lay person. Sigala, who is still in his youth. Ven will show us in the sutta how Buddha in all his wisdom did to advice the correct way to perceive the 6 directions of living. It is not only practical but it focuses on the emotional developments as well.
