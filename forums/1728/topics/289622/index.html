    hi, like what bohiruci said, the buddha never wrote anything on sanskrit. All our buddhist scriptures are derived only after the first buddhist council that happened shortly after buddha's parinirvana and his passing away. The teachings were recited mostly by ananda, Buddha's attendant, in the witness of five hundred enlightened monks.

    Next, it is true that reciting may provide you with some sort of protections from the negativities. If you cannot chant sutras, the habit of chanting the names of buddhas and bodhisattvas will be good enough. My understanding is that ghosts can appear more easily to someone without mental brightness. Through chanting, for example the names of buddhas and bodhisattva, such as na mo di zhang wang pu sa.. our minds will have more brightness and be less vulnerable to those negativities.
