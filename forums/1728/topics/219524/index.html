    Focus and concentration is key in studying.

    If these 'mara' come only when you are studying and not in other times like playing or enjoying yourself in other things, it is probably because there is an inner aversion to studying, a mental thought 'how boring can that be'.

    I suggest you watch your mind if there is any kind of aversion to studying, and if there is, learn to drop them and let go of them. Your heart must be there, you must be willing to study, like you are willing to do other things like watching TV, etc. Learn to enjoy studying. It doesn't need to be boring, it is only boring to the ego. The ego is always trying to escape what is present... it seeks something other than what is happening now. It wants to do other things or think of other things rather than studying. Always the 'self' that is causing problem. Therefore when studying, it is important to let go of the ego and the 'self' - there is no one studying, therefore no problem.

    A summary: Don't force yourself to study. Study wholeheartedly without self. There is no question of overcoming or resisting the self when the self is absent.
