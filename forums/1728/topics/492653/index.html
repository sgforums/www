    When chant with a giving mind, we arouse our compassion and develop our selflessness.

    We strengthen our resolve in our aspiration of bodhicitta:-

    Jhang chub sem ni rin po che, ma kye pa nam kye gyur chig,

    May the precious enlightened mind, arise where it is not arisen

    Kye pa nyam pa med pa dang, gong ne gong du phel war shog.

    And may it be where it is arisen, never wane but constantly grow.

    This is in itself a merit.
