    “You may look rich but in actual fact it may be a chore and burden for you to protect what you have,” said Rinpoche who will be speaking at the Eastin Hotel, Petaling Jaya next month.

    The eight-hour talk will focus on “Living Fully with Health, Wealth and Happiness,” a topic, Rinpoche feels is important for all.“Once a person is living fully, that is when he can say he is truly living his life,” said the Rinpoche who is now based at the Shyalpa Monatery in Nepal.

    An empowerment ceremony of the Secret Essence of Padma Sambawa will also be held after the talk where Rinpoche is expected to perform the ritual and explain every step of it.“It is a traditional Tibetan ceremony, but people usually get confused because they don’t understand what is going on,” said Rinpoche who speaks fluent English as he was once based in New York.
