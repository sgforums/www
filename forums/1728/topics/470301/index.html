    Kyle Dixon:

    The insight (which is along these lines) that stuck with me was;

    [paraphrased]
    When we are young we don't have time to practice because we are too distracted with having fun, and so we don't practice the dharma.

    When we are middle aged we don't have time to practice because we are too busy with our responsibilities, and so we don't practice the dharma.

    When we are old we cannot practice because we are too frail and weak, and so we don't practice the dharma.
