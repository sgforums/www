Chod Melodies of Kyabje Zong Rinpoche

  • RU45@(
    21 Apr 10, 22:46

    In 1983, Kyabje Zong Rinpoche Jetsun Lobsang Tsundu Thupten Gyaltsen gave this exclusive teaching on the Chod melody at the request of Venerable Geshe Tsultim Gyeltsen, Spiritual Director of Thubten Dhargye Ling Center of Tibetan Buddhism in Los Angeles. At that time Kyabje Rinpoche remarked, There may appear a time in the future, when the Chod melodies are learned from the copy of this Chod video. Foreseeing this, Kyabje Rinpoche gave consent to film and preserve this teaching
