900th Anniversary Celebration of 1st Karmapa November 15

  • RU45@(
    9 Sep 10, 02:20

    On 15th of November, 2010, the Official Administration of Rumtek
    Seat organises and conducts in Bodhgaya the 900th year anniversary
    celebration in memory of the first Karmapa Dusum Khyenpa.

    Event : 900th Anniversary Celebration of 1st Karmapa
    Date : Monday 15th November 2010
    Time :  08:00AM
    Venue: Karma Dhargye Chorkhorling Monastery, Bodhgaya, India
    Website : http://www.khyenkong-tharjay.org/event.php

    Karma Dhargye Chorkorling Monastery is celebrating its 30th anniversary
    since land was acquired to build  this monastery. H.H. 17th Karmapa Trinley Thaye Dorje, Kunzig Shamar Rinpoche and Jamgon Kongtrul Rinpoche will be presence
    to grace this occasion.

    Event : 30th Anniversary of Karma Dhargye Chorkorling Monastery
    Date : Friday 24th December 2010 to Saturday 25th December 2010
    Venue: Karma Dhargye Chorkhorling Monastery, Bodhgaya, India

    Event : Kagyu Monlam Chenmo
    Date : Monday 27th December 2010 to Monday 3rd January 2011
    Venue: Karma Dhargye Chorkhorling Monastery, Bodhgaya, India
