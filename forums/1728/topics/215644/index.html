    Just to share with everyone what is tientai 1 mind ,3 views (contemplation)

    the 3 views is Emptiness,Virtual and Conditioned Existence(fakly rea ) and
    Madhyamika (Middle View)

    ChenW33: this is a wisdom aspect as master like Tientai Zhizhe expounded throughout his life

    with this 3 view.One understand the Buddha teaching like the noon sun

    bright and unshadowed from the extreme of indulgence or unfettered by unreal emotions

    More on Middle View: Middle View is the view which all sentient beings adopt in order to follow the practise of the Bodhisattva ,especially the Mahasattva

    Mahasiddhi(Great Attainer) took on the Madhyamika line of thought in order to
    achieve Great Awareness like

    Naropa, Gampopa ,Atisha ,Devadatta

    and many tibetan master

    In china ,tientai Chizhe and Huayan School Xianshou achieve high state of samadhi with the understanding of the middle view

    The underlying ideas of Middle View is there is no middle ,no extreme

    but completely break free from both pessimistic idea (negative emptiness and negative virtual existence)

    negative virtual existence is the state where bodhisattva fear the samsaric existence and refuse to go back to saha world to help sentient beings

    negative emptiness is when bodhisattva sees the non-vital contribution of one
    in savings sentient beings (due to Good understanding of Dependent Origination ) and pessimistically stay in state of emptiness ,refuse to cultivate Great Wisdom to save all sentient beings

    Without Great Wisdom and Compassion ,the practice toward Buddhahood is incomplete .

    Great Compassion must be a serious and sincere wish to save all sentient beings out of Samsara .

    Milarepa evoke his Compassion when he sees a sick dog on the road.
