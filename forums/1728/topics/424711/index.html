    The benefits of 100,000 Tsog Recitation of Krodikali
    Krodikali is a wrathful form of wisdom dakini in Dudjom New Treasure Lineage. She is the embodiment of 5 dakini who bestow the common & uncommon siddhi with body, speech, mind, merit & activity blessing of Buddha and Boddhisattva.
    Tsog offering is a supreme offering in the Tibetan Vajrayana, its can be classify as outer, inner & secret offering.
    a Outer offerings are 8 offering, 8 auspicious & 7 precious jewels etc.
    b Inner offering used symbolic of 5meats, 5nectar & torma;
    c Secret offering is offering of oneselfÂ  all these are the special offering of Vajrayana. Through the meditation, recitation of mantra, all these are offering tranform into nectar of great bliss, visualizing pervading the sky and offer to 10 directions Buddha of 3 times, guru, yidam, khandro, protector, wealth deity, mara, ancestor & karmic creditors and all sentient beings.
    The benefit of Tsog Bum can remove the outer, inner & secret obstacles:
    Outer- purify the calamity caused by the disorder of 4elements, weapons, burglary, karmic creditors, demon, wild animals, poisons, sickness, untimely dead, poverty & unfulfilled wish.
    Inner- protect the practitioner from the harms of the 4maras such as: Skhandha Mara(Evil of Aggregates), Klesha Mara(Evil of Obscuration), Devaputra Mara(Evil of Lust), Yama Mara(Evil of Death).
    Secret- Dispel the 5 poisons such as desire, hatred, ignorance, pride & jealousy.
    The merits of make offering to Krodikali are same as makin offering to 100 000 thousand dakini. The place of offering instantly becomes the pureland of Dakini. Krodikali Tsog is a feast of Cho which mean offering of one own body as secret offering so it is the most profound offering. The main purpose of this practice to cut off one attachment. It is a secret tantra which enable us to accumulate tremendous merit and finally attain liberation with its powerful blessing.
