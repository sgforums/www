    The story of Manjusri Appearance in Sung Dynasty

    There is a puja held every year on Mt Wutai at Temple of Clear and Cool (Qingliang )

    The abbot was very particular on the amt to be given to the laity for free vegetarian food

    One day , there is a pregnant woman with its dog came up to the Puja ,and queuing up for food .

    The pregnant woman ask for more food as she have a unborn baby and her dog to feed.But the abbot was unwilling to give more , and he pushed the pregnant lady aside

    Suddenly ,the pregnant lady ascended to the skies and reveal to the assembly as Bodhisattva Manjusri .All the lay man saw the Mahasattva on the skies put their food aside and prostrate sincerely to the Image above .

    Manjusri Bodhisattva said ," I thought you (the abbot) have practise equality to the assembly .but sadly today i have saw u didnt

    the dog that was with the pregnant woman ascended to the sky to be the kirin which Mahasattva Manjusri

    Seeing this and upon hearing it , all the monks and the abbot himself fell to their knees and cried sorrowfully ,for being failing the "test " set by Manjusri

    The abbott beseech Manjusri for a 2nd chance not to react in such a wrong way, and Manjusri Bodhisattva drop a human hair from the skies to show his approval

    at this time .all the members of the monastery vows with the abbot to give universal equality to the layman who come to participate in this puja ceremony

    today if u went to mt wutai , you will still see the pagoda with the hair by Manjusri Bodhisattva

    Thus is a part of the wonderful story that happened in relation to the Flower Adornment Sutra

    Homage to the Great Vaipulya Buddha spoke of Flower Adornment sutra
