  • Aik TC
    10 Nov 10, 22:26

     

    You would need more than the five precepts to be assure of rebirth as human again. According to the Visuddhimagga of the Theravada tradition, beside the Purification of Virtue, Purification of Mind (which centre on the process of meditation), one have to also complete the Purification of View and Purification by Overcoming Doubt before one can arrive at becoming a ‘lesser Stream-enterer’ (culla-sotapanna). If this knowledge is intact up to the time of death, then only can one be assured that one will not be reborn into the four lower worlds. Of course if you are thinking of going all the way to be an Arahant, than there are three more purifications stages that will still need to be completed.
