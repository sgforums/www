    To commemorate His Holiness Drubwang Padma Norbu Rinpoche's Parinirvana 2nd Year Anniversary (4th April 2011, 1st day of the 2nd Tibetan Month,) our centre will conduct a 3 days puja from 2nd to 4th April 2011 on Vajrasattva Purification Puja to pacify any negativities and obstacles.

    During the 3 days anniversary puja, there will be large candles available for sponsorship at S$60 per piece. The candles will be lit throughout the 3 days puja. Due to numerous requests from devotees to increase total numbers of large lamps, we have decided to increase and accept all large lamps registration.

    Devotees may also register for normal lamp offering (which will last for 1 day) at $10 per lamp.The lamp will be offer to the late H.H. Drubwang Padma Norbu Rinpoche, to symbolise the enlightened clear mind of His Holiness, where one will be able to accumulate merits and wisdom. Through the merits accumulated, one will receive great benefit of wealth, to be reborn in deva or human realm, and to swiftly attain liberation and enlightenment. Just like how a lamp illuminates, to dispel the darkness of ignorance.We will like to request devotees, students of the late H.H. Penor Rinpoche, to attend this 3 days puja
