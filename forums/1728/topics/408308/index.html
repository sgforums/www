    18 Aug 10, 09:09

    just sharing how i practice personally

    morning once wake say the precepts, then meditate for an hour. wake up earlier to do this before going to work.

    during the course of the day, use noting as the main practice. must practice until this becomes a habit, so even when rushing will note 'rushing'. and i also in the process of forming the habit of wishing whoever i talk to (or come across) well, just in my mind saying 'may you be well, may you be happy'.

    just by the act of maintaining attention to whatever we are doing is a form of practice. so try to get used to reminding myself that there's no special place for practice.

    then before sleep meditate again, and end off with sleeping meditation until fall asleep mindfully.

    may everyone be mindful and be happy :)
