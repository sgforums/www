    What is the sutra you have most 'affinity' with?

    For me, I think it is Heart Sutra. Since very young age I have listened to it very often on tape although I couldn't understand what it meant. Then when I was 12, it suddenly dawned on me that 'form is emptiness, emptiness is form', that truth is truly beyond conceptuality and we are often so caught up in concepts, creating dualities like 'form' and 'emptiness'. Heart Sutra suddenly made sense to me and I felt really happy.

    But of course, Emptiness has much deeper meanings as I learnt in the years to come.
