    The Bodhivastu Stupa will be of unprecedented importance, helping to ensure peace and prosperity by quelling the myriad sufferings of our modern times. Stupas both large and small yield incalculable merit energy.

    Stupas both large and small yield incalculable merit energy. For this reason, many wisdom representatives from foreign countries have constructed stupas in America.

    They are commonly constructed in Thailand, Burma, Sri Lanka, Nepal, China, and Tibet, because it is a Buddhist custom. According to Buddhist thought, stupas increase wisdom, compassion, enlightened activity, and energy, thereby decreasing the likelihood of misfortunes such as famine, wars, or damage from floods, earthquakes, tsunamis, and hurricanes.

    The Bodhivastu Stupa will be of unprecedented importance, helping to ensure peace and prosperity by quelling the myriad sufferings of our modern times. The meaning of Bodhivastu:
    Bodhi: Altruistic, compassionate, enlightened mind; perfect knowledge or wisdom by which a human becomes a Buddha (an awakened being); the extinction of greed, hatred, and delusion.

    Vastu: The auspicious design and building of objects and environments in harmony with physical and metaphysical forces. The seat, realm, or place of an abiding substance, essence, or worthy object.

    Bodhivastu: The Auspicious Manifestation of Perfected Compassionate Awakening

    Bodhivastu will have the power to herald in a new era of ultimate wisdom, peace, and brother- and sisterhood. Bodhivastu will be constructed on consecrated ground with a pure motivation of inclusiveness. It will be made with perfect proportions and contain sacred consecrations, universal prayers, and several major Buddhist relic troves of past saints, extending back to Kashyapa Buddha.

    The blessing waves that emanate from the sacred relics of great beings of peace, those who have passed beyond confusion, have the power to pervade all realms when properly enshrined in such an enlightened architecture. By our pure intention, these blessing waves will reach out throughout the world, anchoring our pure intention for peace and prosperity for all.

    The goal is to make Bodhivastu of a similar size to Borobudur Stupa in Indonesia (over one hundred yards on each side) and bigger than Bodhanath Stupa in Nepal.

    The visions of the construction of Bodhivastu arose simultaneously in the minds of two close Lama friends, Lama Rangbar Nyimai Ozer (Yugyal Tulku) of the United States and Lama Tsampa Laykyi of Bhutan. Although the idea seemed almost impossible in scale, various meditative signs arose naturally while considering what could be done to help save the only planet we have come to know as ours.

    These two Lamas have already erected a 65-meter prayer flag on Prophecy Mountain (Nagarjuna's mountain in Kathmandu, Nepal) to usher in and form an auspicious mark for Lama Rangbar's trip to the United States to commence the Bodhivastu project.
