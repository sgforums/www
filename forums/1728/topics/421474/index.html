    Diamond Cutter Sutra Essence Mantra
    Transcribed by Khen Rinpoche (Jangchup Choeden)
    Abbot of Gaden Shartse Monastery


    One who recites this essence mantra one time is said; that person will accumulate
    the merit equal to reciting the Diamond Cutter sutra 19,000 times.


    NAMOBHAGAVATE / PRANJAPARMITAYE / OMNATADTITA / ILISHI / ILISHI /
    MILISHI/MILISHI/ BHINAYANBHINAYAN / NAMOBHAGAVATE /
    PRADATYAMPRATI/ IRITI/ IRITI/ MIRITI/ MIRITI/ SHURITI/ SHURITI/ USHIRI/
    USHIRI/ BHUYUYE/ BHUYUYE/ SOHA
    There is another mantra to purify negativities:
    One who reads this mantra one time; will enjoy purification of negativities, of nonvirtuous


    deeds or bad karma of a thousand eons.
    OMGHETSANAHUM (phonetised in original style of writing mantra in Sanskrit)
    OM GHE TSA NA HUM (phonetics rendering is modified for easy pronunciation)


    Dedication:
    By the merits of whoever sees, reads, remembers, talks or thinks about the Diamond
    Cutter Sutra Essence Mantra never be reborn in unfortunate circumstances, receive only rebirths in situations conducive to the perfect practice of dharma, meet perfectly qualified Dharma practitioners attain bodhicitta and enlightenment quickly for the sake of all sentient beings.


    May there be no war, drought, famine, disease, injury, disharmony or unhappiness, may there be only great prosperity, may everything be easily obtained, may all enjoy the happiness of Dharma, have love and compassion for all beings and only benefit and never harm each other.

    http://www.norbucholingcentre.org/downloads/dc_mantra.pdf


    And may Spiritual Masters, Sangha members and all sentient beings are well protected and achieve whatever they wish.
