    Yes, karma is totally real.

    However, karma will not ripen immediately. It may ripen decades later, and many of the times it will only ripen in future lifetimes.

    Similarly a good person may meet many bad fortunes, because of bad karma from past lives.

    And furthermore we have immeasurable past lives, aeons of past lives...
