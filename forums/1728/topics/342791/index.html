    Having loving kindness to every sentient beings does not mean we condone actions that we deem as unskillful or unwholesome. Similarly to have loving kindness does not mean we condone the actions of homosexuality. Regarding this issue there are varying views on this, from my dharma teacher's perspective he thinks we should discourage it.

    However this does not mean that we discriminate them, not at all. I think I can get along well with anyone whether gay or straight.
