    1) Does the statue need to be bronze if doing consecreation. Where is a good place for consecreation.
    [My input] No.  The statue could be made of any other metals.  As for good place for consecreation, I assume that you would like to do it in Vajrayana tradition.  To do so, first, you have to buy the consecreation materials, which is available in Geylang.  As to which centre to approach, there are many vajrayana centres in Singapore with resident lamas who could do the consecreation, and your contribution amount is entire up to you.  But you have to be mindful in choosing the right centre.
