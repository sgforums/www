    Hi guys,

    Found this booklet and thought you may enjoy reading it.

    Thaddeus wrote this in 1972 at the height of his "acid" days.

    He talks about vibrating at a higher or lower frequency, compare this with what Eckhart has said about frequency holders.

    Truth can be expressed in an infinite number of ways.

    Each expression is a different perspective on the truth.

    Eckhart has given us a wonderful and insightful way of perceiving truth, while guiding us from the noise to silence.

    Thaddeus' way is another perspective.

    Which, seems to me, compliments and adds to Eckharts'.
