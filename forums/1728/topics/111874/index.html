    Hi Goggles,

    It might be a sleep disorder called "Sleep Paralysis". Some people snore, some people grind their teeth, and some people have Sleep Paralysis. It happens when you mind wakes up (& you are fully awake/conscious) but your body is still asleep. Thus, people think that they are being "pressed" by spirits or harrassed by petas.

    I've had Sleep Paralysis for about 15 years now and I've learnt to deal with it without medical aid. When you find yourself in that situation, besides chanting Amituofo, Namo Tassa Bhagavato..., or whatever other chants, the main thing to remember is that you have to relax (which is what those chants help you to do). If you don't relax, you mind will not go back to "sleep" mode. If your mind does not go back to "sleep" mode, it cannot synchronize with your body to wake up.
