    Khenpo Tsering Dorje of the Palyul Nyingma



    Khenpo Tsering Dorje is the seniormost Khenpo-Graduate of Penor Rinpoche's main seat in-exile, of the Nyingma Palyul Lineage, the grand Namdroling Monastery. Khenpo has taught also in the late Dilgo Khyentse Rinpoche's main seat in-exile in Nepal.

    Being a distant relative of the late Dudjom Rinpoche from Pema Kod, Arunachal Pradesh, India, Khenpo Tsering Dorje has brought the precious Dharma into some of the remotest corners of India and has built retreat centers, clinics and schools for many of the poorest communities of people there.

    HH the Dalai Lama and Kyabje Drubwang Penor Rinpoche have both graced Khenpo Tsering Dorje's monastery in the far-flung region of India ( via helicopters !! ) with Their holy presence and have both given their complete blessings and expressed Their great happiness at Khenpo's wide-reaching activities for the holy Dharma.

    Many of Khenpo's own students are themselves teaching in some of the most important centers of learning within and without the Nyingma Lineage, throughout the world.

    This will be Khenpo Tsering Dorje's first public teaching in Singapore.

    Khenpo will be giving the complete teachings on the practice of the Wrathful Guru Takhyung Barwa of the Longchen Nyingthig Cycle as well as 3 of the major empowerments in the cycle.
