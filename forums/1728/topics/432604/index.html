    Come join us in a free screening of the film "My Reincarnation" to help raise funds for its worldwide distribution!
     
    Spanning 20 years-in-the-making, 1000 hours of footage in over 17 countries, "My Reincarnation" tells the inside story of one of the last reincarnate teachers trained in Tibet and his son's stubborn reluctance to accept the destiny he inherited at birth. 
     
     

     
    CATCH THE FILM TRAILER AT: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BfJtcRxw3eo

     
    After the film's completion, Director Jennifer Fox discovered that a co-producer was unable to contribute a promised US$100,000 portion of the film’s budget.
    Until funding costs are fully met, the film cannot be released to worldwide audiences.
