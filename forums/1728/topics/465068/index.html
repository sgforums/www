    Hi, hope you find additional helpful info from below link: http://www.fpmt.org/death.html When my mum got sick (not from cancer), I thot her life span could be coming to an end. When she finally passed away, a relative arranged for a Rinpoche to do phowa for her. We also do other things like sponsor pujas for her, donate to earthquake victims using her money on her behalf, enter her name for those pujas to benefit the dead etc. The impt thing is to make sure she is comfortable physically, help her to be peaceful, good also to chant Amitabha Buddha's name if she can do it. Also, if she is at the end of her lifespan, tell her to let go and not cling to this life. After death, the first 49 days are important, especially the first 3 weeks. Ask her loved ones to send loving thots to her, sponsor pujas to be done for her and dedicate merits for her good rebirth/rebirth in Amitabha Buddha's pureland. It doesn't stop after the 49 days. The merits of any virtuous deed done can still be dedicated to her good rebirth, her name can still be entered for pujas to benefit the dead etc.
