  • An Eternal Now
    30 Jun 11, 15:22
    The answer is no, no, and yes. No, there is no karmic calculator, karma is not a mathematical thing as each karmic cause can have complex karmic result, much more complex than chemical reactions in laboratories. But the basic truth is that wholesome deeds, speech and thought will result in a pleasant karmic effect either here and now, in the future, or in an afterlife, and unwholesome deeds, speech and thoughts result in an unwholesome karmic effect now, in future, or in an afterlife. You also attain complete freedom from mental suffering, you attain bliss and clarity in the here and now, in this life, so enlightenment and liberation is seen here, now, not as an afterlife state. No, Buddha is not a creator god, simply one who has fully awakened to the true nature of reality and obtained complete liberation from sufferings. Countless people before and after him, including even today, has achieved awakening and liberation. To attain enlightenment is the goal of all Buddhists. Once you are liberated, you are free from the uncontrolled cycle of rebirth in samsara. Karma is a natural law of cause and effect that plays itself out, it is an impersonal, causal process that requires and has no judge. Here is a sutra about karma: http://www.accesstoinsight.org/tipitaka/mn/mn.136.nymo.html
