    Relics are manifested by Buddha and many realized masters for the
    great benefit of sentient beings. One can purify bad karma and gain
    good merits by making prostration to holy relics.The presence of Relic
    is equivalent to the presence of Buddha himself. Malaysian Buddhists
    are extremely honored and lucky to enjoy opportunity to pay homage to
    Peshawar Relic and other invaluable relics. Bring along your friends
    and family to join this blissful Puja that you can't afford to miss!
