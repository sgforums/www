    ‎"The essence of Buddha's teachings is the truth. It is the purpose of Buddha's teachings to remove our ignorance and introduce us the truth. For this reason, Buddha gave so many teachings to so many sentient beings, but these all are based on one truth.

    The puja is a part of meditation. When we do pujas, we have to visualize and meditate as we read in the books. When we perform ritual dances, we also need to visualize and meditate according to tantras. So actually, monastery is a center for meditation and institute is a center for studies, but we build retreat centers to concentrate more on the meditation.

    ‎"You can go to any buddhist masters to receive teachings. Doesn't matter whether that master belongs to Kagyu, Gelug or Nyingma. Because, what you are looking for is the teaching, not the lineage. "

    When one is strongly attached to something or some one, one can meditate on it's nature so that one can transform the attachment into meditation by the means of concentration

    One can choose whether one should emphasize meditating on view or visualization. But it necessary to practice both of them in our daily practice.
