    Metta (loving kindness) to all victims of hurricane Katrina
    At this moment of pain and anguish, may we radiate thoughts of loving kindness to all those affected by the destruction caused by hurricane Katrina. While we grief for the many victims of the natural disaster in New Orleans and Mississippi, may we all cultivate a sense of compassion and radiate thoughts of loving kindness to relief helpers who are at this moment doing all they can to provide needed assistance for the victims. May all beings be calm and wise. May they be free from suffering.
