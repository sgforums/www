    love, hate, feelings, come from self-consciousness and until more research comes in, it is more than likely to be "an emergent".

    Something that nobody can replicate because nobody understand how it works.

    What is an emergent? It is something that pops up unexpectedly. It has roots, that is to say, it comes as a result of of interaction of "individual separate units" communicating an interacting with each other. Nobody knows why the interaction of these individual units in such simple ways results in the phenomenon....

    Like life. How is it that when carbon molecules interact in a certain way, you get "life"? Life is said to be an emergent because nobody can explain why when carbon molecules interact in certain ways, you have life.

    Like consciousness. How is it that when you structure cells in a certain way, (brain) you have consciousness?

    Some people think that there is a universal something that fuses with the brain to produce consciousness while others think that it is because of the split second feedback loops within the brain that results in consciousness.

    Get it?
