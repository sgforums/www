Focus on Impermanence Nature of all things

  • MiddleWay87
    7 Oct 14, 23:58

    There is a saying: 'we do not know what will happen to us tomorrow when we wake up'. Yes, that is true. Life is so impermanent and ever changing, everything can be totally different tomorrow.

    Think about how many ups and downs, and up and then down, up, down up, down, up, down, up, down, up, down, up, down, in life.

     

    Some say, 'that is just life. Life is full of ups and downs'. But should you allow yourself to just go along and be happy and sad with it, allowing it to control your emotions?

    There is a way to see clarity, to see the true nature of our mind and attain liberation. We can feel always at peace, regardless of life conditions.

    This is what the Buddha Dharma offers us. Take this gift, it is more precious than any gold or diamond.  Start Practising Diligently today. If you have just 5 minutes a day, do some chanting. or read some dharma books. There is ALWAYS time to practise.
