    Avalokitesvara Bodhisattva Enlightenment Day
    By Poh Ming Tse Temple

    Date: 19 July 
    Day: Tuesday 
    Time: 9am - 4.30pm 
    Course Dana: Please refer to form 
    Number of students: All are welcome

     

    Poh Ming Tse Temple will observe the Enlightenment day of Avalokitesvara Bodhisattvawith a Dharma Function focusing on the recitation and prostration to the names of 1,000 Buddhas. We would like to invite you to put up names of your family members and companies / organizations for blessing on this auspicious occasion, via the attached form.     Vegetarian lunch will be served to all participants.

     

    All proceeds will be used to fund the activities in PMT. May you and your family be blessed with good health and harmony. Please contact us at Tel: 6466-0785 or Email:pmt@pmt.org.sg if you have any queries.
