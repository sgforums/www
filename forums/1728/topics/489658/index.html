Then you put a blind mirror, the magic screen, on that table and lean it against the wall. You put also two candles in silver holders on both sides of the mirror. Afterwards you place the symbols of the 4 elements near the corners of the small table. First a lighted oillamp for the symbol fire, second a small cup of water for the symbol water, third a smokecandle for the symbol air and at last a small stone for the symbol earth. Then you place a crystal rich goblet, symbol for the Holy Grail, in the center of the small table. A biger banknote you put then into the goblet. After you had all that installed you switch off lights in your room, inflame the two candles, the oillamp, the smokelamp and burn also the banknote in the crystal goblet, as the sacryfice of your ritual. Now you knel down and summon the ghost to appear. You may now see the summoned ghost in the blind mirror, the magic screen, if you had made the ritual in the right way.
