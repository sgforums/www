    Palelai Buddhist Temple

    To mark the official opening of Phra Maha Chedi Dhammasathit (stupa), the temple will be holding a Grand Consecration ceremony from 24th - 26th Feb. Ven. Somdej Phra Yanvarodom from Thailand will be the distinguished Guest of Honor while monks of three traditions from Singapore and overseas will be there to grace the occasion. All are welcome to attend its program of Dhamma talks and chantings.

    Around 180 invited venerables from the Theravada, Mahayana & Vajrayana tradition from singapore, thailand, Srit Lanka , Myammar and Malaysia will also be going continuos chanting to consecrate the stupa and new building.
