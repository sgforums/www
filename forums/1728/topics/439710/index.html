The 11th 100 Million Mani Retreat

  • Mani Retreat Association
    15 Oct 11, 19:13

    Dear Brothers and Sisters in the Dharma, 

    In the year 2001, His Eminence the Late Drubwang Konchok Norbu Rinpoche of the Drikung Kagyu lineage, came to Singapore and initiated the first 100 Million Mani Recitation Retreat. Drubwang Rinpoche led more than 400 participants to chant "Om Mani Padme Hung", the six-syllable heart mantra of the Great Compassionate Avalokiteshvara Bodhisattva (Guan Shi Yin Pu Sa) and accomplished the retreat in 10 days. All the participants were immersed with great joy and deeply inspired by this simple and yet powerful practice. Many directly felt the blessing of Avalokiteshvara Bodhisattvas, which warmed their hearts and transformed their lives. A lot experienced a new level of insight and strengthened their faith in the Buddha Dharma. Many good and positive conditions had also appeared in their lives. 

    With the blessing of the Triple Gems and also your very kind support, the Mani Retreat had since been held annually with great success. We would like to take this opportunity to thank all of you who had previously participated in this precious retreat. Your presence had helped to provide a strong encouragement to each other, especially to the organisers. We sincerely hope that you will once again continue to give your time and effort in this meaningful practice and together complete the accumulation of the 100 million mantras in this upcoming 4 days Mani retreat. 

    The merit accumulated will be dedicated for world peace, freedom from sickness and diseases. The world now is filled with a lot of uncertainty and changes. The Mani practice is very helpful in generating a calming effect to the surrounding environment, bringing back the balance in the ecological system and helping to heal the Earth. From within our heart, let us spread this energy of compassionand kindness throughout space, as we chant "Om Mani Padme Hung" and make aspiration that all the beings enjoy happiness and be free from suffering. As we chant the Mani mantra wholeheartedly, the Buddhas and Bodhisattvas will rejoice in our effort and give their blessing. The Devas and Dharma protectors will be with us days and nights, and many of the beings whom we have connection with, especially those suffering in the lower realms, will soon be liberated and attain a good rebirth. 

    For those of you who are new to this retreat, please take this rare opportunity to try it out. If you are hoping to explore the meaning of life, experience inner peace and tranquility, which is quite a tough challenge in our present fast-pace society, this practice itself is like a golden key, ready to unlock the hidden treasure from within. So do not miss this, take a step forward and experience it for yourself.     

    Details of the 11th 100 Million ManiRecitation Retreat

    Venue: Hall 402, Suntec Singapore International Convention & Exhibition Centre      

    Sales of tickets will be available through SISTIC from 15 October 2011 onwards.     


    For more information, please refer to the poster attached for details     or visit our websites at http://maniretreat.sg or email us at enquiry@maniretreat.sg orcontact us at 83716183. 

    December is coming and this is an excellent opportunity to cleanse our negative actions and make positive aspiration for year 2012, so get your tickets now and we look forward to see you soon in the retreat.     
    Om Mani Padme Hung!

    With Metta,      
    Organizing Committee
