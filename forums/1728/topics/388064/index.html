    Buddhism originated from India. Burning of joss sticks and burning incense paper are the practice of Taoism.

    Sometimes you will see Buddhist Temples burning joss sticks and incense paper, that is to convenience the taoists who also go to Buddhist Temple for prayers.

    Most of the Buddhists who have taken the 3 refuge will not burn incense paper but will hold joss sticks. For me, I hardly hold joss sticks. I pray to Buddha according to Buddhist practice.

    Buddhism does not practice medium (god possess body). If you see medium in the temple, it is definitely not a Buddhist Temple.

    Buddhism also discourage feng shui and fortune telling.
