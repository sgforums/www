    Absolute Evil, no. There cannot be Absolute in the realm of Dualism (good, bad, short, tall). Absolute implies static, which is against the law of Impermanence.

    On a deeper level, no matter how evil or good you are, at the deepest level we are one and equal, already wholly complete. Our true nature is Buddha Nature, and that also means each of us will and has the potential to become Buddha. Even the Mara (The Evil One) will become Buddha, and the Mara king got converted by the Buddha after Buddha gave him this assurance.
