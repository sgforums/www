    From ABC :

    Our Vesak Day celebrations over the many years have benefited many people, enabling them to create merit and purify their negativities through acts such as offering and homage to the Three Jewels. Through performing these and other virtuous activities at our celebrations, many seeds have been planted in their minds to achieve liberation and enlightenment. It is a time when people come into contact with the Buddha's teachings and for many, the start of their spiritual journey to enlightenment. Having a successful celebration means benefitting people. More success means more benefit to many more people.

    We are honoured to have Khen Rinpoche, Lama Lhundrup (Abbot of Kopan Monastery) and the Kopan monks around again this Vesak to conduct the pujas at the celebration.

    The celebrated Stages of the Path ( Tibetan : Lam Rim ) teachings represent a synthesis of the entire path to enlightenment. Presented in a clear & concise way , they are easy to understand & apply in our daily lives. Based on Lama Tsongkhapa’s ‘ Condensed Stages of the Path ‘ . Khen Rinpoche Lama Lhundrup will explain the essential practices of the ‘ beings of the three scopes ‘ , including correct guru devotion , renunciation , the altruistic wish for enlightenment & the view of the middle way.

    Venue : Amitbha Buddhist Centre , 44 Lorong 25A Geylang , Tel : 6745 8547

         

    Dates  : 23th May ( Fri ) 7.30pm , 25th May ( Sun ) 2pm , 27th May (Tue) 7.30pm , 29th May ( Thur) 7.30pm , 3rd June (Tue) 7.30pm , 5th June (Thur) 7.30pm

    ·         A long life punja will be offered to Khen Rinpoche Lama Lhundrup on 1st June (Sun) at 9.30am.
