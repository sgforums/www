    No. The notion that all Buddhas originate from India is a misunderstanding of the term 'Jambudvipa'.

    In Buddhist cosmology, all Buddhas in our galaxy or universe appears in Jambudvipa. What is Jambudvipa? It means the southern continent. Some people think that this refers to India, it is wrong. Jambudvipa is the homeland of our mankind, our species. It is referring to our entire planet earth itself.

    In other words, in the southern part of this galaxy, there is planet earth. There is other planets with beings like us, in the northern, western, and eastern parts of the galaxy. However, Buddhas will always appear in our planet Earth.

    Amitabha is an Indian name, however Amitabha is from another universe and is not Indian.
