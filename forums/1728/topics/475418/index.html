    I'm just wondering why do people take up Buddhism as a religion? Technically speaking, the 'real' objective of a 'true blue' Buddhist is to renounce Samsara and seek liberation from it (attaining Nibbana). In another words, a Buddhist seek to BECOME a Buddha. How many Buddhists are actually pursuing this goal? And if you are not, then why are you a Buddhist? More importantly, if you are a true seeker of Truth, what SPECIFIC TECHNIQUE can get you there? Out of the 84,000 dharma doors, are there any CLEAR INSTRUCTIONS on how we can do this?

    For me personally I practice:

    2) anapanasati (anapanasati sutta)

    3) four foundations of mindfulness (satipatthana sutta)
