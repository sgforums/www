    It temporarily removes craving but it does not remove grasping and seeking (to maintain the permanency of indulgence), which is craving in another form. Then one will constantly be living in dukkha.

    The highest sort of mundane bliss can be found in Jhana, where mental disturbances temporarily subside into calmness and ecstatic joy. But even that sort of state cannot be maintained forever, and there is still a subtler form of dukkha. Even if you stay in the 8th Jhana realm for 84000 kalpa you will eventually fall back to realms lower than that.

    See the previous topic on dukkha, mundane joy and bliss: The Pursuit of Happiness, Bliss and understanding Sufferings
