    Who and why Green Tara?

    It is said that in her course of saving sentient beings,
    Avalokitesvara (Guan Yin) was saddened by the innumerable beings that
    needed help.  It was from her tears of compassion that Green Tara
    arose and gave the assurance of helping to save sentient beings.  As
    such, Green Tara is swift in responding to those who pray for her
    assistance.

    It is an auspicious start to the new year to jointly make an
    aspiration in improving ourselves.  The Green Tara practice will help
    us transform our own lives positively so we can be of benefit to our
    family and friends initially and society at large eventually.

    About His Eminence Khamtrul Rinpoche

    His Eminence the 9th Khamtrul Rinpoche holds an M.A. in Buddhism from
    Bhutan Tango Monastic University.  He is the heart disciple of His
    Holiness the 12th Gyalwang Drukpa, spiritual head of the Drukpa
    Lineage.  The 8th Khamtrul Rinpoche was the root teacher of Jetsunma
    Tenzin Palmo, subject of the book Cave in the Snow
