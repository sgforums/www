    Our annual Camp Ehi Passiko is back once again! To provide you with a greater, more enjoyable and meaningful learning experience from 17th to 20th December 2010!

    CEP is a Buddhist camp organized by Singapore Buddhist Mission Youth for youths aged 13 - 21. Through the camp, we aim to let youths know that Dharma can be fun and applicable to our daily lives!

    For youth, it will be a very good chance to experience fun outdoor activities and make life-long friends who will develop and grow with you!

    For parents, your child will have the opportunity to train their leadership and interaction skills. Additionally, they will get to learn moral teachings which is applicable to their everyday's life!
