    <Guru Rinpoche Long-life Empowerment>

     Guru Rinpoche is the embodiment of the perfect generosity of all Buddhas and Bodhisattvas in the past, present and future.

    Life presents so many uncertainties. It’s unpredictable when this life comes to an end. This empowerment of Guru Rinpoche is a blessing for long life and other favorable conditions so that practitioners have as much time as possible to practice the Dharma.

    Through the empowerment, with deep and strong faith to Guru Rinpoche, it will also help to purify our negative karma and defilements, removal of negative force and to accumulate immense merit.

    About Chene Lhendup Rinpoche

    The Long-life empowerment of Guru Rinpoche will be conferred by Chene Lhendup Rinpoche, 79 year-old, the founder of Orgyen Choling Monastery and Kunzang Shedrup Choling Retreat Centre in Bhutan.

    Rinpoche received all the four Vajrayana traditions teachings from H.H Dudjom Rinpoche, Dilgo Khyentshe Rinpoche and H.H The 16th Karmapa. He is also a disciple of great Dzogchen master, H.H Polo Khen Rinpoche, who recognized him as the reincarnation of Drupthob Chene Dorji    , the reincarnation of one of the 25 main disciples of Guru Rinpoche.

    We provide free offering of butter lamps & free registration of blessing tablets.
