    Not necessarily chanting means you shouldn't understand emptiness etc.

    And developing insights doesn't mean you can't chant... you can still chant, but further than that you also start contemplating in your direct experience what your true nature is.

Yet I am still having further insights... (but not sitting regularly also has its cons: my mind is not as stable as when I do regular sittings..)
