    After i had listened to the dharma talk today on mindfulness and concentration
    which was informative. /\ sadhu x 3

    I started to meditate and unlearn the mistakes i had made in the past.. i discovered i had been practising wrongly in some ways.

    I had started observing my mental state, feelings, thought and neutral or bad feeling towards objects... letting go of every tensions and sensation.. for my objective is to remain a calm and relaxed state... just watching and not analysis, judging, comparing, pondering, attaching to feelings and conceptualised.

    For every sensation felt:: pain, anger, ill-willness or blissful feeling as there are all just passing feelings.

    There are absolutely no point holding on to an sensation. It is okay to feel pain and to let it go. > as told by the venerable

    I had experience a different mental state than the one that i had have had. It is in a much clearer and more calming in a very subtle way... i find the mind not cluttered and find myself less lost in thought.. more awared of the surrounding..

    Just some sharing here. Im quite surprised with the result..
