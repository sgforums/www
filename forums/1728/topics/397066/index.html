    Hi all Dharma Brothers and Sisters Vesak Day is approaching. I will be doing the 3 Step 1 Bow on the night of the eve of Vesak which is 27th May Thursday. The 3 Step 1 Bow starts at 6pm on eve 27th May through 28th May Friday (Vesak Day/Holiday) itself. As usual, there will be long queue of people/devotees queueing for few hours just to do this 3 Step 1 Bow ritual. So its best to do it on the starting day which is the eve 27th May Thursday. If you are at Kong Meng San by 6pm, you'll start the walk probably around 9pm. In between you \ can have your free vegetarian dinner there or do meditation at one of the hall first. Anyone interested to join me for this 3 Step 1 Bow walk/ritual please message me. But just as a pre-warn, this walk is 2 and half hour. (you'll will be circumbulate walking 3 step 1 bow around the 10 over temple in Kong Meng San which is a very large area.) I have done the ritual walk for about 6 years. If you manage to finish the walk, you really feel a sense of accompanishment when you reach the ending point, whereby a buddha statue spourting holy water onto you, which is just nice as you feel abit hot and sweaty too after the marathon walk. So anyone interested in forming a group and walk together please message me. Or you can leave your handphone number please, so I can sms you faster to arrange for time and venue to meetup on that day. Thanks. With Metta Lavender
