    "The Spiritual Songs of Milarepa"

    7 May & 9 May (Monday & Wednesday)
    7.30pm

    Garchen Rinpoche is a great teacher, who's truly an embodiment of loving-kindness and compassion. When he gives teachings, he often teaches from the most basic to the most direct essence.

    Yea there are many inspiring stories about him.

    Hmm he was imprisoned for many years by the Chinese Liberation Army because at that time they would imprison all the Tibetan Rinpoches.

    Then in the prison, he saw some ants crawling on the floor....and at that point, loving-kindness & compassion towards this ants arose within him.... and he made the vow not to lie down on the floor to sleep (to avoid hurting these little animals). So for all the years he was in prison, he always sat on the table or some other platform to sleep, and never lied down.

    When he was eventually released from the prison, he was full of gratitude towards the Chinese Liberation Army, as he said that it was them who gave him the conditions to practice and gain merits & realizations.
