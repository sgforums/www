    rough translation:

    what's 'patient of birth'? when people insult, hit or kill you, and you can tolerant, that's 'patient of birth'.
    what's 'patient of dharma'? it's being able to be patient when learning the vast dharma.
    what's 'patient of birthlessness?' it's a state of not seeing more dharma nor less dharma. in this state, the person is void, the dharma is also void. it's knowing the voidness of both person and dharma, that even if you want to said it, but you can't say, that's 'patient of birthlessness'.
