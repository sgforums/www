I ask her again, you are so use to being a tai tai for so many years at home. Wouldn't your life be so boring after noon cannot eat, at home cannot do all these above to past time? 8. I undertake the precept to refrain from sitting, lying on a high or luxurious sleeping place. She is tai tai living in landed property of course got nice comfy double bed. I ask her then how?
