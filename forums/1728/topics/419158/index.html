    Conducted by Songda Lungi Trulpa Rinpoche.  Rinpoche is the grandson of the 2nd Dudjom Rinpoche and holds both the dharma and blood lineage of the Dudjom Treasures.  In his previous incarnation, Songda Rinpoche was the Guru of the 2nd Dudjom Rinpoche.  Orgyen Menla practice can remove health obstacles, bring prosperity, success and purify negative karma that bring rebirth in the lower realms as well as help a practitioner to gain rebirth in Amitabha’s pureland. During this puja, we will be accumulating prayers many times collectively for the benefit of all sentient beings.  Everyone is welcome to join in this auspicious event.
