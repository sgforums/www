    To be able to view chinese characters in internet explorer, these are what you do with WinXP Home/Pro :

    First, ensure that Windows XP's CD is loaded in your CD-ROM drive
    1. Goto Control Panel
    2. Click on 'Date, Time, Language, and Regional Options'
    3. Click on 'Regional and Language Options'
    4. Click on 'Languages' tab
    5. Tick on the checkbox for 'Install files for East Asian languages'
    6. Click on Apply.
    7. If you are unable to view Chinese characters after Step 6, Goto Step A

    In Internet Explorer:
    A. Click on 'File' Menu
    B. Choose 'Encoding'
    C. Choose 'More'
    D. Choose 'Chinese Simplified (HZ)'
