  • wisdomeye
    5 Nov 10, 23:39

    pure emptiness,

    You are not able to fully purify the karma of eating meat... to perform COMPLETE purification, one has to have the factor of regret and resolve not to do it again.  If one is still continuing to eat, then obviously the factor of regret is not strong and also there is no resolve to abstain in future.  However, don't let this discourage you from reciting mani mantras and the Buddhas' names to purify yourself to whatever extent there is, as it will always be better to do more practice than none.

    Although full purification is not possible, it is always proposed that one should dedicate merits to benefit the animals that we consume.  Dedicate merits from your practice so that they will have a good rebirth, meet with the dharma, and attain liberation from samsara.

    A master who attained enlightenment once said that animals are the most suffering because their ignorance stops them from regretting their past misdeeds (which led to their present sufferings...) and so revolve for a long time in the lower rebirths.  The hell beings and hungry ghosts still have the ability to see their past and repent or regret, thus lightening their karma and giving them a way out in future...

    So if one is eating meat, at the same time, by this karmic link one is creating with the animal, one should try one's best to make positive wishes and prayers for the animals and dedicate merits to this end.  One should do it everyday... as much as possible.  This will also lighten the karmic debt between you and these animals.

    Hope this helps!
