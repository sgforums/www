    Looks like the easiest would be for you to attend a class at Guang Ming Shan in Bishan. 

    You can keep checking this space  http://kmspks.org/courses/meditation for upcoming classes.  By next year (it's already year-end now), there should be new ones starting up.

    They also have group practice every Wednesday evening.  http://kmspks.org/featured/group-practice-2

     

    If you also wanted to read some books to get you started on meditation, here are a few you can explore.  They can be borrowed from the public libraries.

    The Miracle of Mindfulness: An Introduction to the Practice of Meditation by Thich Nhat Hanh

    Mindfulness In Plain English - Urban Dharma  (PDF version you can download)

    Meditation Now or Never by Steve Hagen
