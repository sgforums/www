    Do you know why the mantra of the Heart Sutra is the supreme and utmost mantra which is able to relieve all suffering?

    If we chant 'Gate Gate Paragate Parasamgate Bodhi Svaha' all the time, then our mind would be like 'one mountain cannot abide two tigers'.

    - Master Ken Yung

    So when we are attached to something, and we cannot empty it, singing the above song or chanting the mantra would be a good practice.
