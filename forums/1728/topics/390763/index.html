    Iron Tiger year is around the corner and Resident Lamas Tsewang & Kunkjab would like to wish all of you a very Happy, Healthy,Prosperous and Spiritually Full New Year! Hoping to help dispel misfortunes and obstacles so that the path for the New Year will be smoother, ZKBC Lamas will be conducting the following pujas for the benefit of everyone. All are welcome to attend.

    13th Feb. 2010 (Sat) night from 9pm to 10.30pm
    -- 4-Armed Mahakala Puja

    14th Feb. 2010 (Sun) afternoon at 3pm
    --Guru Rinpoche Puja

    15th Feb. 2010 (Mon)afternoon at 3pm
    --Zambala Yangok Puja

    16th Feb. 2010(Tues)afternoon at 3pm
    --Green Tara (incl. Praises to 21 Taras) Puja

    The basic purpose of any dharma practice is to purify all karmic obstacles, to increase and enhance one's accumulation of merits and wisdom and puja is one of the most helpful ways of practice....
    Even the simple offering of a lamp to all the Buddhas & Bodhisattvas with the sincere motivation of wanting all beings to be free from disturbing emotions gives immeasurable merits, as taught in several Sutras, such as "Sutra of the Essence of the Earth", "Tune of Brahma Sutra", "Arya Maitreya Sutra" and also the "Root Tantra of Chakrasamvara"..
    The lamp symbolizes the light of wisdom that dispels the darkness of ignorance.
