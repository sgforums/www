    There has been a similar topic discussed recently: Buddhism & Taoism

    Ancestor worship is not part of Buddhism, but we do pay our respects to our ancestors.

    The Taoist teachings taught by Lao-Tzu, Chuang Tzu, etc are deep and profound and similar to Buddhism. However the modern form of religious Taoism is not the same as Buddhism, so you need to know the difference. (pls see my posts in the Buddhism & Taoism topic)
