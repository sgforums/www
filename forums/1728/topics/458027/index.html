    Hi, I can pm you the printing companies which specialise in printing dharma books. There are fewer and fewer temples which allow us to put copies there for distributions. Reason being there's a "flooding" of dharma books. It is better to check with the persons in charge of the temples whether do they allow you to put the books at their place for free distributions. My shifu discourages us to print dharma books because too many out there. Instead, he encourages us to donate to the temples and charity organisation or you are very sure that there are people who need the books that you are going to print.
