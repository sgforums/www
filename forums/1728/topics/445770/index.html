    <White Dzambhala Wealth Puja>

     

    Through the Wealth Puja & the blessing of White Dzambhala, one is able to increase the energy of wealth, luck, financial prosperity, wisdom and health.

    Led by Venerable Nuptul Tenpei Nyima Rinpoche whom was recognized by His Holiness, the 4th Dodrupchen Rinpoche as the reincarnation of the great meditator, Khendrup Tenpei Gyaltsen.

    Rinpoche resides at Sangchen Rabten Norbuling (Great Secret, Absolutely Firm, and Precious Location) Monastery. The monastery is located deep in the Himalaya mountains in an incredibly beautiful valley called Kyimo Lung (    Valley of Happiness), near the Nepal-Tibetan border in the central Himalaya which was blessed by Padmasambhava and Jetsun Milarepa.
