    Hi Beautiful951,

    If a person has taken rebirth in say, a heavenly realm, then as gods, they can see their past lives as their minds are in what humans will call a kind of 'meditative state', their clarity and clairvoyance surpasses humans... and so they can see the reason why they reborn in heaven and past lives etc. 

    If a person takes rebirth in Amitabha's pureland, similarly he can see his past family etc.  That's why in the aspiration prayer to take rebirth in Amitabha's pureland, it is aspired that after taking rebirth in pureland, the person will also be able to bring all his family, disciples, retinue etc into the pureland together with him.

    In the higher realms, as long as they are not out of samsara, there is still attachment. For eg, some gods like people to make offerings to them.  They can get angry too.  Anger also belies attachment somewhere.  As long as you are still in samsara, no matter how high a realm you reborn into, there will be attachment, whether subtle or gross, and this will cause suffering that will continue on until one reaches full liberation.

    Hope this helps
