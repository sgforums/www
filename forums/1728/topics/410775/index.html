     This very special series of 13 empowerments will be offered by Lama Kunga Thartse Rinpoche at Ewam Choden. The dates are Friday, Sept. 24, Saturday Sept. 25 and Sunday Sept. 26., starting each day around 10 a.m. and lasting until 5 p.m. with a lunch break. Final times to be confirmed soon.

    The donation is $425 for the whole program. Empowerments may not be taken separately. This will include the Hevajra Blessing Empowerment on Sept. 23, which is a prerequisite for receiving the 13 Golden Dharma series (if you haven’t previously received a Hevajra or Cakrasamvara empowerment from a Sakya Lama).

    This very special series of 13 empowerments over a three-day period will be offered by Lama Kunga Rinpoche on Friday, Sept. 24, Saturday Sept. 25 and Sunday Sept. 26. The cost will be  US$400  for registration before Sep 1st and US$425 after Sep. 1st.  This will include the Hevajra Empowerment on Sept. 23, which is a prerequisite for receiving the 13 Golden Dharma series (if you haven’t previously received a Hevajra or Cakrasamvara empowerment from a Sakya Lama).Lamas are welcome at no charge and all ordained sangha at half price.

    The 13 individual empowerments will be offered starting at 10am on each day with an anticipated ending time of 5pm, with a 1-1/2 hour lunch break. No lunch is provided, though it is a 5-10 min. drive to restaurants and stores.
