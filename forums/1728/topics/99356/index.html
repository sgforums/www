    Hi there,

    Just as the topic suggests, we are starting a new informal Buddhist group. Our objective is to come together to learn, discuss and share on Buddhism and how we can apply it to enhance our lives and enrich it with meaningful living.

    Not solely "talk-only-no-action" sessions, our focus will be on useful application of the Buddha's teachings making the discussions lively and dynamic; and not just theorical scholar-talk.

    The primary lauguage used will be simple Chinese/Mandarin (even my cannot-make-it standard can understand I don't see why you can't), but we speak bilingual so don't worry.

    We are fortunate to have a kind and wise teacher to guide us through the sessions, she's a very experienced and accomplished practitioner. She's always patient with us, encouraging us to pose questions to make sure we are cleared of our doubts.

    The aim is simple and our intention is sincere, we hope for more like-minded people to gather and make friends as we progress together. However this is not a boy-meet gal session, so pls don't mistaken it as a group of SBU nature.

    If you are interested, please email: humanbliss@gmail.com for more details or browse through http://www.humanbliss.com.sg to get to know about the good teachings offered.
