    I do not wish to associate with Yi Guan Dao / Tian Dao. Can I safely withdraw myself from this or break the vow?

    Breaking a vow and get strike by lighting? It sounds rather ridiculous to me. You can tell that to a little child and frighten the hell out of him. To me it is just a silly little tactic to instill fear and guilt in a person so as to try to exert some control over the person. There is nothing for you to withdraw from, just do not visit their place of worship again. In some religions, the last physical stage to bring in the new converts is to get them to visit their place of worship. Just don’t go again.


     Can I seek forgiveness from my buddhist religion due to my ignorance/lack of understanding?

    There is no one all mighty being that you need to seek forgiveness from in Buddhism. It is not a religion of commandments. Just go and read up more on basic Buddhism for starter.


     I have actually left my contact to the devotee who brought me in and I should be expecting the contact soon. How do I reject nicely without offending their religion/belief?

    To me a simple ‘no’ is good enough. You can always give whatever reason that is valid and compelling. Refrain from talking about the person faith, that way, there is nothing to offend at all. 


    I'm in confused state now. Hope someone can enlighten me or let me know if I need to go to any recommended buddhist temple and talk to who on this?

    Go to any Buddhist temples around where you a staying, you should be able to get some reading material and also courses available there. You may find some that may be of interest to you. Better still talk to the monks or members of the temple there. It would be useful and they may be able to assist you further.
