    Vajrakilaya Puja is conducted to purify negative energy, remove obstacles for 2011 and accumulate vast merits

    The Venerable Nuptul Tenpei Nyima Rinpoche was born in Nubri, Nepal on December 25, 1984 At the age of 7, the 4th Do Drupchen Jigme Thubten Trilay Pal Zangpo recognized him as the reincarnation of the great meditator Khedrup Tenpei Gyaltsen

    Having recieved initiations transmissions and reveled treasures from
    His Holiness he spent 15 years deeply engaged in dharma practice
    including the completion of a 3 year retreat He engages the broader
    Dharma Community to support the livelihood of the monks and nuns at
    the monastery

    Sangchen Rapten Norbu Ling Monastery is located deep within the mountains and valleys of the Nepal Himalaya.  This monastery was established in 1992 in the Beyul Kyimolung (hidden land peaceful valley) and blessed by second Buddha PadmaSambhava (Guru Rinpoche).  The monastery follows the Nyingmapa tradition of Tibetan Buddhism.  Its untouched beauty and serenity provides an undisturbed environment for the practice of Dharma. 

    The geographic isolation of the monastery causes many hardship.  It is located 6 days walk away from the nearest road.  During winter, the road can be covered by four feet of snow.  The monks and nuns suffer from the cold weather and from illnesses that are not easily treated due to the long distance for getting medical aids.  During summer, there will be lots of rain and this cause too much water leaking from the roof to their accommodation.  Most of the wood inside the rooms  damaged by water leakage and some of the walls has collapsed
