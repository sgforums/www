    I have not heard about the Green Satchet Sutra before. But I think bodhruici made a good point. It is indeed possible that a kamma planted way beyond the 60 lifetimes could have come into effect at a much latter time. However this would have to depend on the conditions too.

I think the main reason would be that the Buddha was worried buddhists would end up spending time seeking divinations rather than working on their lives. And probably, human just like to listen to the good words. If the divination turns out to bad, probably that person will end up wallowing his sufferings than to make his life better.

    If reading Green Satchet Sutra just for pure knowledge, I think it doesn't matter. Since it is good that we learn a wide variety of subjects.
