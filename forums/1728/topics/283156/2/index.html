    BTW at a higher level, since you requested someone to write something on how to go to the next level and be completely free from such thoughts...

    This is actually very simple. It is actually quite effortless as long as you maintain an awareness that constantly lets go -- all phenomena simply self-liberates (which means the thought and experience liberates by itself because it is impermanent!), nothing stays... nothing leaves a trace. As my taiwanese teacher said... don't resist or reject thoughts, the more you try to find ways to suppress your thoughts or reject them the worse it becomes.. instead don't even treat your thoughts as if they are events happening (it is as if nothing has happened). Not even thoughts of sexual lust.. Perhaps that is why AndrewPKYap said "The more you try to get rid of lust, the more you affirm its existence when the reality is that lust does not exist."

    So there is no need to condemn your thoughts or resist them, if you know how to practise this. It is much more direct. However if you have difficulty practising this, you may try the methods I have posted above in the previous post.

    Please read http://www.accesstoinsight.org/lib/thai/kee/inward.html -- the part on Aware Right at Awareness, November 3, 1975 by Upasika Kee Nanayon.
