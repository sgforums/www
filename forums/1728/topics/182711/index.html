    At the Lotus Sutra Assembly , Buddha Sakyamuni display the wonderfull array of examples from His Compassionate teaching and leads the Assembly of Vulture Peak to the Samadhi of Lotus Sutra

    thus the teaching of One -Buddha Vehicle is born.

    The teachings of tientai (Heavenly Platform) is a systematic and detail study on all the teachings of Buddha and his Periods of teaching and

    categorised similar to a Sun movement

    At Sunrise ,the Sun jumps out of the Mountain from the East

    Which Symbolise the first turning of Wheel to the Mahasattva and the Dharmadhatus of Lotus Store Assembly

    The Avatamsaka Sutra or Flower Adornment Period

    However Buddha Sakyamuni realised the faculties of his disciples are small and seek arhatship

    so he began the 2nd period which is the slow progress of the Sun to the 8-9pm position

    Which the explanation of 4 noble Truths ,Noble 8fold paths

    Next as Sun reaches the Zenith , Symbolise the time is ready for the Mahayana Preaching ,which is Known as Fang1 Guang3 Period

    or the Valpuya Period

    Next, the Sun reaches the noon period ,which Buddha preach on Maha Prajnaparamita ,the 22 years of Teaching on Emptiness

    When finally at 4- 5 pm the sun progress down to the west which Buddha preach to the essence of the One -Buddha Vehicle known as the Lotus Sutra Period

    as the Sun comes down to the Sunset position , Buddha started on the Maha Parinirvana Sutra which summarise and reiterates the several essence of his 49 years of Compassionate teaching
