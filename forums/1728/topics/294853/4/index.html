    Regarding the difference of karma for the butcher, eater and witnesser, my opinion is:

    1) Do it yourself or not
    If one kill the animal himself, the impression to his mind and 8th conscious will be deeper. Over time, he will become more violent and develop the habit of killing easily.
    If you ask the meat eater go and slaughter the animal himself, he rather pay extra to buy from supermarket because he feel terrible and sad to see the animal suffer. At least he still has a little bit compassion. Due to this difference in mind and attitude, the bad karma that they get will also be different.

    2) Buy a packaged one or live one
    If let's say the customer buy a live chicken to be killed. For sure, the chicken is going to suffer and die because of this. But if he buy from supermarket instead, the supermarket may have extra stock that even if the demand increase by let's say 1%, the number of chicken to be slaughtered at the butcher will remains the same. Anyway, the unsold chicken once reach expiry date, will be disposed.

    3) Witness or not witness
    Likewise, if you witness a animal to be killed because of your ordering, then you are the one who directly contribute to its death and suffering. If you witness it and feel happy seeing the fish to be slaughtered, you will have equal bad karma or even more than the butcher who kill it.
    Worse is, after a few times witnessing without any regret, you become used to it and become the next butcher

    So, it is all in the mind. You can claim that even though you choose which fleshy chicken to be killed, witness the killing or even do it yourself but in your heart you feel sad for the chicken. But even if the whole world including you cheated by yourself, you will still suffer the same bad karma.
