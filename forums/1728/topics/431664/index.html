    Hi all,

    There's a story about a dharma practitioner who gave a wrong answer about the question regarding karma... He answered that enlightened people are not bounded by karma (did i remember wrongly?) and unfortunately created lots of negative karma due to this wrong view... He was reborn as a fox because of that.

    After he consulted Master Baizhang, Baizhang's answer was "not ignorant of karma" and the fox was freed from animal realm.

    What happens if we share dharma by our kindest intention to help people understand but ours is wrong too? Are we going to be "punished" by similar negative karma like the fox?
