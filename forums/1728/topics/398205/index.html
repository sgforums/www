    HOW TO PRACTISE THE SOLITARY HERO YAMANTAKA
    by Khen Rinpoche

    Khen Rinpoche will explain how to meditate on the extensive practice of Solitary Hero Yamantaka, the extremely wrathful aspect of Manjushri who pacifies obstacles.
    If you have received the Highest Yoga Tantra initiation of Solitary Hero Yamantaka, this is a golden opportunity to understand how to practise the long sadhana of the deity.

    If you have the intention to enter into retreat on Yamantaka at some point in the future, this is your chance here and now to receive firsthand instruction on the practice.  Note that when performing the retreat, one is required to engage only in the extensive practice. 

    Dates: 4, 6, 11, 13, 18, 20 May at 7.30pm and 9 May at 2pm.    

    Requirements for Receiving these Teachings

        * You MUST have already received the initiation of Solitary Hero Yamantaka from a qualified master (E.g. if you had received the Yamantaka initiation from Lama Zopa Rinpoche in February)
        * You are doing the daily practice of the Six Session Guru Yoga
        * You are prepared to accept a Guru-disciple relationship with the teacher (Khen Rinpoche)

     

    Dates: 4, 6, 11, 13, 18, 20 May at 7.30pm and 9 May at 2pm.
