    Yayy! finally a vegetarian friendly burger place
    Nov 28, 2010
    I went to Vegan Burg on a friend's recommendation (all the way from Queenstown mind you), and I was not disappointed. I had a Charcoal BBQ burger and my boyfriend had a Satay burger. The patty was absolutely delightful as was the healthy buns around it. The satay burger had a nice generously gingered peanut sauce which was amazing! It was served with a side of crispy fries, which did not at all feel oily. The icing on the cake was that this kind of combo, comes with healthy juice! Made me think of all the food options we take these days and how best to be as healthy as possible. I would definitely go back but i do sincerely hope they start shop in the west as well! (Hide)

    OMG so good!
    Nov 16, 2010

    We were absolutely blown away at how good this vegan option of hamburgers and hotdog were! We're both big meat eaters, just went here due to my milk allergy to get something 100% free from dairy and try something unusual, expecting weird taste and dry food, but oboy were we wrong. Everything was completely fresh, juicy and scrumptious :)

    We tried the satay burger, Cracked pepper mayo burger, BBQ burger, vegan dog in bread and potato balls. The potato balls were also surprisingly good and juicy, had almost a cheesy taste to them, and no disgusting greasyness from being fried. The vegandog was my favourite (just had to add some ketchup), had I not known it was vegan I would have thought it was ordinary meat in it. Of the three burgers, we both liked the BBQ burger best, seriously delicious...

    The atmosphere and decor inside were not nice at all, but considering it is an alternative to a fast food place, it is ok. The staff receiving the order spoke english well and was very pleasant. There was advertised on a chalk board that they had brown rice ice cream, but when we ordered it, they answered that they would only be starting selling that in some days... oh well, means we have to come back :D Which we gladly will do. However, living and working quite far away from this place, we hope they will start delivery soon! (Hide)

    Delicious Vegan & Healthy Burger
    Oct 29, 2010

    I was eager to try this out when I read on the newspaper and oh boy, it was a treasure to be found alright!

    I've been waiting for a restaurant that has eco-consciousness and organic value and Vegan Burg is certainly the answer.

    The outlet was easy to spot on with their Big Round Logo on top of the entrance door, just before the Eunos MRT Rail Track on Eunos Road

    Loved the ideas of the tables having an open squared compartment that stores some magazines.

    Ordered the Set Menu, consisted of 1 Burger, French Fries and Drinks (no soft drinks here!!)

    I tried the Smoky BBQ which was delicious; the patty felt fresh and I assured you it didn't taste funny, strange or that kind of sort; it was smooth with mushroom combination.

    The organic ciabatta was fresh and I don't usually like ciabatta but for this one, I admitted defeat..

    Fries wasn't Oily and it came in just a right amount...

    Ordered the Juice for drink, a bit let down by having too much ice cubes...

    Thumbs up for Vegan Burg by being considerate, the burger was cut in half, the fries and burger came in a square tin box, really put a saving on paper waste.

    Tried my colleagues burgers; a Hawaian which had fresh sliced pineapple and Tangy burger; which had soft patty with mayonaisse.

    Must try their Spinach Pops which was crispy on the outside and soft fluffy in the inside; combination of potato and spinach never felt this good

    The staff were young and could see a bit inexperienced but fast serving for our burgers delivery.

    Loved the ideas of having a place where I know it's a gem for environment and tasty on the same time...

    Definitely will be a regular there...
