    Hi netteoh,

    There are few printers available in Singapore. Usually the minimum lot size is 1,000 pcs to start with.

    If your budget does not allow you to do that, you can 'share' the printing costs by informing the printer, and they will wait for right quantity to commence printing. The costs varies, depending on whether the printer has the master copy, the book needs editing etc

    Get a quotation before you start.

    You can personally distribute the books or the printer can distribute as well.
