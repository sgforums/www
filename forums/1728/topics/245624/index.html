    While on other forum we see this Cundi Bodhisattva practice

    The Buddhist forum is more towards the proper understanding of the

    meaning behind Cundi Bodhisattva

    The mantra of Cundi Bodhisattva is very powerful.

    It is stated in the sutra that reciting the mantra can purify negative karma, bring blessings, cure illness, cure those possessed by evil spirits, fulfill wishes, make those dislike you to like you, eliminate infectious disease such as SARS in the country, etc etc

    Detailed instructions on how to cure illness or make those dislike you to like you is indicated in the sutra.
