    Hmmm ... whether one can or cannot chant certain sutra in house is rather subjective. In fact, my buddhist peers did pinpoint to her Shifu that Earth Store Bodhisattvas promise in his sutra that he will protect the place from spirits disturbance. However, the problem lie not the Bodhisattvas promise but the layperson own merits and vows. Example when a spirit tries to get the layperson attention through disturbance or sighting to request for help. The layperson may freak out as he may not be able to discern the  spirit request. That person may in turn blame the Bodhisattvas for not being able to fufill his promise. However, have the layperson forgotten since the moment he wanted to recite this sutra, he has begin on a Earth Store Bodhisattvas journey to be like him.

    Famous vows

    1)  If I do not go to hell to help them there, who else will go?

    2)  if the hell is not yet empty, I vow not to become a Buddha.

    Welll, according to her, it is ok to recite in the temple during the mass recitation due to the vast triple gem merits. 

    On the other hand, I have another friend who really like and chant Earth Store Bodhisattva Sutra on a regular basis at his room even when in Australia. Nothing has happened to him. So it is a matter of individualistic choice. I think proper Buddhist foundation is the key. Right understanding. Perhaps my buddhist peer's foundation was not strong therefore her shifu advise in that manner. As for Dawn and others who has been doing fine in one home, KEEP UP THE GOOD WORK .

    DEDICATION OF MERITS is skillful means of sharing of one joyful acts with our loved ones. in fact, I have this experience with my dead brother. During his earthly life, he is quite bad temper once he even used his shoe to throw at our home Buddhist altar. Last year he passed away in an accident, our family did a number of merits for him such as copying Lotus Sutra and placing with his ash; recitation; writing Namu Myo Ho Renge Kyo on paper; offering incense and offer lamp in the temple.

    In Jan this year, I dreamt about him. I went to this place when half naked people are hangup in pole like manner. They are suffering being link up like a chain. When the nite fall, they are queuing to get a portion of the food. Some bowls were empty, half full or quarterly filled. My brother appear and his bowl was full. He told me that there was this friend who will always bring food for him to eat. Subsequently, he said that from certain days onwards we can prepare a  dizhu -åœ°ä¸»ç¥žaltar in a particular spot in the kitchen area to make offering to him which we did as per instructed.

    I was very taken back by the dream. After much recollection, I realised that the so called friend might have been a manifestation of bodhisattva. During each recitation we dedicate the merits with this Lotus Sutra Chpter 25, Kuan Yin verse to him.

    He is endowed with transcendental powers
    and widely practices the expedient means of wisdom.
    Throughout the lands in the ten directions
    there is no region where he does not manifest himself.
    In many different kinds of evil circumstances,
    in the realms of hell, hungry spirits or beasts,

    We always pray for the Buddha and holy ones to manifest themselves, to preach the Dharma to him to relieve his suffering.

    Eversince the dream, I realised that the Buddhist teaching of dedication of merits is so profound that though we see not with our eyes yet its effect in the invisible spiritual realm is so vast.

    Gassho

    Namu Myo Ho Renge Kyo
