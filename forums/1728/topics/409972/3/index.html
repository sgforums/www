    I think Platform Sutra has a mixture of Prajnaparamita Sutra and Shurangama Sutra even though Hui-neng did not study Shurangama as far as I know. If Prajnaparamita Sutras are considered the second turning, Shurangama Sutra is like the third turning sutra.

    Prajnaparamita Sutra emphasis is on emptiness. Shurangama is on the union of luminosity and emptiness. The brilliant luminous clarity of mind and the non-duality of mind and appearances. Lankavatara sutra also falls under the third turning category. Platform Sutra talks about both luminosity and emptiness, so it is a third turning sutra.
