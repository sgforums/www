    Evolution and History of Root (seed ) Word

    The Siddam Script is commonly adopted and used in the Tang Dynasty , particular with the Coming of the 3 Patriach of Eastern Esoteric from India

    Vajrabodhi ,and two others

    Siddam script is an ancient writing language sanskrit which have its a very old history since the beginning of the Uses of the Rig Veda in Hinduism

    During Sung Dynasty in China , Many Esoteric and Reveal Esoteric School use the Commonly accepted Lanja Script .Its origin in the Indian culture was unknown , as I exhaustively tried to find answer to the origin of this script
    without success from MSN ,google or yahoo!search .

    After the Fall of the Sung Dynasty , the Chinese came under the control of the Mongolian which set up the Yuan Dynasty under the Leadership of Kublai Khan

    This brought the Mongolian to impose the use of Tibetan script in root word
    and thus this have an lasting effect on the Buddha Statue of the Esoteric Styles

    today ,with the coming of modern technology like internet , we have a good fortune to view root word at the ease of our home and in workplace

    We must be grateful as all this comes not by coincidence ,but tedious travel to India to obtain the original scriptures by Ven Hsuan Tsang , the Coming of Esoteric Patriach and Various expedition to bring Indian Buddhist Scripture to China
