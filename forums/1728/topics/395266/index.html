Shangpa Rinpoche Milarepa Guruyoga Teaching Apr 4-28

    Jetsun Milarepa is a famous yogi and poet of Tibet. He was a student of Marpa Lotsawa and a major master of our Kagyu lineage. Shangpa Rinpoche will be teaching Milarepa Guru Yoga. You will learn the essence of Milarepa Guru Yoga, the making of the torma, the chanting along with the ritual practices using instruments such as the damaru (drum). Following are dates for teaching the Milarepa Guru Yoga in the month of April at 8pm. Do join us in this important and auspicious teaching.
