    Prayer beads or Mala or Rosaries are used by various religions such as the Roman Catholic, Orthodox Christian, Muslim, Hindu, Buddhist, and Sikh to count the repetitions of prayers, chants or devotions. They may also be used for meditation, protection from negative energy, or for relaxation to help a person to relax; to attain a state of increased calmness; or otherwise reduce levels of anxiety, stress or anger. In Buddhism it is a traditional tool used to count while meditating when mantras are used by the Mahayanist.
