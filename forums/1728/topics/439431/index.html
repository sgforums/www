          Kampung Senang is holding its Flag Day on 12 Nov 2011, Saturday,
          island-wide. We need Volunteers to bless every passing by to inspire giving.

          ... This Flag Day is Critical to our Foundation’s projects community services which require S$1.3 million to sustain. We are targeting to raise over $50,000 for our beneficiaries on this Flag Day.

          Donations will help to support our following projects:
          · Holistic Support for People Affected by Cancer.
          · Mobility Aids Services & Training Centre, for physically disabled of all age groups
          · Free Medical Clinic and Sudsidised Natural Therapy Program & Organic Farming
          · Youth Volunteering Program
          · Child Care
          · Student Care
            · Elderly Day Care           

            • Sign up as a volunteer by calling us at 67852568/ 67498509. Or you may send us an email via volunteer@kg-senang.org.sg.
        • Details of Flag Day:
          Volunteers needed: 500
          Minimum age of volunteer: 16 and above
          Shift: 4 - 8 hours at your options
          Location: Island-wide
          ... Collection Centres / MRT stations:
          Tampines, Kovan Hub, Waterloo Street, Aljunied & Other Selected MRT stations

          It will be appreciated if you could helps us to make this appeal to your friends, members and volunteers. You may also choose to give by adopting our Donation Tins from now onwards.

          Please contact us at 6749 8509 or email: fund@kg-senang.org.sg
