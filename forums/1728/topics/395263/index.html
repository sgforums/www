Kyabje Penor Rinpoche Parinibbana 1st Anniversary 2-4 Apr

  • RU45@(
    2 Apr 10, 01:15

    To commemorate His Holiness Pema Norbu Rinpoche's Parinirvana 1st Year Anniversary Ceremony, our centre will conduct a 3 days puja from 2nd to 4th April 2010 at the open land beside Singapore Post Centre, near Paya Lebar MRT station. His Holiness Karma Kuchen Rinpoche (the 12th Throne Holder of Palyul Lineage) will lead this anniversary ceremony puja together with the lamas. There will 2 days Guru Yoga and Vajrasattva Purification Puja, Vajrasattva Empowerment and teaching follow by Vajrasattva Fire Puja on the 3rd day to pacify any negativities and obstacles.

    On 4th April evening, there will be a Grand Vegetarian Dinner together with H.H. Karma Kuchen Rinpoche. Dinner tickets are already available in our centre for purchase at S$50 per ticket, based on first come first served basis. Those interested are advised to purchase the tickets early to avoid disappointment.

    All students and devotees who have directly or indirectly received teachings, empowerments, refuge and etc. from the late His Holiness Pema Norbu Rinpoche to take this opportunity to participate in this 1st Year Anniversary Ceremony Puja.

    Location : Open space opposite Singapore Post Center
