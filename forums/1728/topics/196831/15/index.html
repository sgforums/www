    A Phrase from the Dhammapada to encourage all on the Bodhi Path

    taken from Newbuddhist.com the America Buddhist Portal

    Focus,
    not on the rudenesses of others,
    not on what they've done
    or left undone,
    but on what you
    have & haven't done
    yourself.

    -Dhammapada, 4, translation by Thanissaro Bhikkhu.
