    Hi this is just a gentle reminder to forummers in the Buddhist forum.

    I have noticed that over the months some very enthusiastic Buddhists have been putting in effort in posting some very good dharma resources and information about events and talks by dharma teachers throughout the world. I truly appreciate these forummers in taking the time and effort to post such great Dharma resources.

    However since our Buddhist forum does not have any subsections/subforums, I would appreciate if forummers consolidate your posts into one or a few topics rather than make many separate topics.

    I don't mean you have to consolidate all your posts under one topic, but rather under a few general headings, e.g. 'Mahamudra Advice', 'Dharma Events', and so on. This will also help other forummers in referencing them more easily rather than having to click each individual link one by one. I highly doubt forummers will actually click so many times to open different threads. It is better to put them into a single topic, while having headings in each post so that readers can know in summary what the post is about.
