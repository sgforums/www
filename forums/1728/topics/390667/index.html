    What Guanyin say is

     

    Shi ren dor si yu may de,  zi you xue hui kuan rong he kuan su,  cai hui de dao jie tuo."

    Worldly people is all with their Virtue , only by learning Tolerance ,Forbearance and Forgiving , will one be liberated from such afflictions (emotional entanglements ) of the mind

    the verse "Worldly people is all with their Virtue " is further explained as

    All people have their good points ,so no one is a totally bad guy

     

     

    posted here to share with others

     

    Namo Dabei Guanshiyin Pusa
