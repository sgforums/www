    The annual 3 day Summer Krodikali Retreat is a very wonderful and auspicious retreat for everyone of us. It is a comprehensive practice which embodies the entire path to awakening. We are very blessed to have our glorious Root Guru, Garab Rinpoche to conduct this retreat, to guide and help us, and to lead us in the genuine dharma path.

    This annual retreat is very special to all of us. It provides us a platform to practice toget...her with Rinpoche. In this retreat, even if one does not grasp the profundity of the teachings or the deity's qualities, yet we are able to receive tremendous blessings through the direct transmission of wisdom, power and compassion of Rinpoche who has held this practice lineage. This retreat is always done discreetly with the vast intention of Rinpoche to bring benefit to others in whatever way possible, in accordance with the aspiration of Rinpoche to propagate Dudjom lineage to the world. During retreat, Krodikali pith instructions will be given by Rinpoche.

    This retreat is not open to public. It is compulsory for all Rinpoche disciples (strictly ONLY for all Rinpoche's disciples and Dudjom lineage followers.) Should anyone of you wish to participate in this retreat but is not Rinpoche's disciples, kindly email Rinpoche's personal assistant, Mr Koncho Nyenda at garab_dorje@garabdorje.com for permission.
