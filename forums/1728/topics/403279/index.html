Buddhist Pilgrimage with , Geshe Lobsang Jamyang Dec 1-21

    Pigrimage with Geshe Jamyang to Buddhist sites in India and attend teachings with His Holiness the 17th Karmapa at Kagyu Monlam from 1 December – 21 December 2010 of 20 Days

    The Buddhist pilgrimage, from December 1st - 21st will take in  Rajgir, Sharvasti, Lumbini, Kushinagar, Vashali, and includes attending teachings with His Holiness the 17th Karmapa at Kagyu Monlam in Bodhgaya and with Geshe Jamyang along the way and at Root Institute.

    Website : http://www.eknoexperience.com/travel/chenrezig.html

    Itinerary

    1 December 10– Day 01: Arrive Delhi

    Transfer from International Airport to hotel in Delhi.
    Overnight Delhi

    2 December 10– Day 02: Full day of sightseeing

    New Delhi – India Gate, Rajpath and Secretariat Buildings, Indira Gandhi Memorial Museum. Old Delhi – Humayun’s Tomb, Red Fort & spice market.
    Overnight Delhi

    3 December 10– Day 03: Agra Leh

    Take early morning train/taxi to Agra. Check into hotel. Afternoon visit Taj Mahal and Red Fort and Baby Taj.
    Overnight Agra

    4 Dec 10 – Day 04: Agra – Varanasi

    Early morning visit to Taj Mahal, catch train at 06:45 to Varanasi. Arrive approx 5pm in Varanasi.
    Overnight Varanasi

    5 Dec 10 – Day 05: Varanasi

    Sunrise boat ride on the Ganges Sightseeing trip in Main Ghat area and Vishwanath Temple. Evening puja on the Ganges.
    Overnight Varanasi

    6 Dec 10 – Day 06:. Sarnath - Sharvasti

    In the morning visit Sarnath – Dhamekh Stupa and Buddhist ruins, Chaukhandi Stupa, Mulganda Kuti Vihar and drive to Shravasti.
    Overnight Shravasti

    7 Dec 10 – Day 07: Sharvasti - Lumbini

    Shravasti is an important pilgrimage location as he spent 26 monsoons and this where gave most of his teachings. Afternoon drive to Lumbini.
    Overnight Lumbini

    8 Dec 10 – Day 8: Morning Sightseeing around Lumbini

    Visit the Sacred Garden (the birthplace of Buddha) the Peace Stupa, the Japanese, Thai and Myanmar Temple, Overnight
    Overnight Lumbini

    9 Dec 10 – Day 9: Kushinagar

    After breakfast drive to Kushinagar. Kushinagar is an important pilgrimage location as that is where Buddha passed away. Visit the ruins of the Makutbandhan Stupa and the statue of the reclining Buddha.
    Overnight Monastery in Kushinagar

    10 Dec 10 – Day 10 Patna. Drive to Vashali

    Visit the place where the Buddha gave his last sermon before he reached perinirvana. Drive to Patna.
    Overnight Patna

    11 Dec 10 – Day 11: Depart for Rajgir

     Leave early morning to visit Rajgir where we take the chairlift to visit Vultures Peak, Nalanda University, and the Japanese Stupa, Vultures Peak & Nalanda University, return to Bodhgaya. 
    Overnight Bodhgaya

    12 Dec 10 – Day 12: Day in Bodhgaya

    Visit the Mahabodhi Temple, Japanese, Thai and Myanmar Temples and at sunset watch Buddhist monks chanting.
    Overnight Bodhgaya

    13 Dec 10 – Day 13: Day in Bodhgaya

    Start of Kagyu Monlam, Tergar Monastery, Bodhgaya.
    Overnight Bodhgaya

    14 -19 Dec – Day 14-19 : Attend Kagyu Monlam
    Overnight Bodhgaya

    20 Dec 10 – Attend last day of Kagyu Monlam
    Overnight train to Delhi

    21 Dec 10 - Arrive early morning, prepare for international flight home.
