  • An Eternal Now
    1 Apr 10, 13:54

    The only historical Buddha in our times is Shakyamuni Buddha.

    All other Buddhas you hear are either ancient (long forgotten times) Buddha or other universes' Buddhas.

    This doesn't mean there aren't anyone who is enlightened and liberated, for in Buddha's times alone he had thousands of students who are enlightened and liberated, and there continue to be such people today, even in this forum.

    However being 'enlightened and liberated' as an arhant or bodhisattva is still not the same as Buddhahood, as Buddhahood confers things like omniscience, all powerful, expedient means, perfection of all virtues, etc.
