    Maybe, other Buddhist practitioners can share their experience and knowledge about the cons of drinking alcohol. Also I'm interested to see how your practice is affected by alcohol. (i.e. is your insight and samadhi being affected, and how?) For instance Ken Wilber says half a cup of wine is enough to make his non dual experience disappear, though our moderator Thusness says that reports that while the body is drunk and out of control, cannot walk straight, but awareness is still there and very clear... and non-dual is not impaired. That is my experience also, though I wasn't in nondual state at that time -- but I was still pretty alert. Thusness also say mindfulness may be impaired depending on the level of drunkedness.

    Also, because of improved blood circulation, one becomes more daring, etc. (that's why can sometimes lead to stupid acts)
