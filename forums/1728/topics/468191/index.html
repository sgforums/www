    Question : When one died and entering the bardo state, their loved ones and friends performing buddhism liberation for 49 days of buddha name chanting on behalf. Can all these be helpful in liberating the dead during bardo state for rebirth to Pureland Amitabha. Or the dead in such bardo state has to chant buddha name and vow for Pureland in order to be liberated by Buddha? Would the dead in bardo state be able to chant Buddha name themselves and vow for Pureland?

    Answer : Although it is extremely rare for rebirth to Pureland in the bardo state, it did have such evidence. The mother of a laybuddhist here, Mr Hu was an example, she truly rebirth to Pureland during the bardo state within 49 days. She did not practice Buddhism in her life, only acquired Buddhism after her death, and how to understand from it?  During the 49 days periods, everyone was chanting Buddha Name, recitation of Sutra on her behalf. As she did not left off after death and still in the bardo state, she was touched and grateful. She requested to listen dharma lecture and embarked on Buddha name meditation for vow to Pureland Amitabha. In her last day, she resided on a living person to inform everyone that Amitabha Buddha has appeared to receive her and stated to them that her grade is low and middle level attainment. Henceforth, after the last breath of a person, assisting the death during the 49 days of Buddha name chanting is important, very important. According to our understanding, she was having deep rooted in Pureland Buddhism in her past lives, and because of her wealthy lifestyle, she was not having the opportunity to contact with Pureland Amitabha method, and only at her death she met such kind affinity with more than 300 people assisting in Buddha name recitation. This form of dharma affinity is not easy to be in meeting with.
