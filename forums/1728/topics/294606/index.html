    The Thai monk is a muay thai boxer last time and join the Thai Military ,it was before the international Muay Thai Competition he give up and practise Buddhism

    His muay thai training helps the poor thai border children to train up and get away from the frustration of prevailing poverty

    In Mahayana Concept ,I see him adopting a skilful mean by asking pre-ordainee to train stubborn horses .

    "if the horse is stubborn , it would be a headache to the child, so what about the child if he is stubborn to the teacher ?"

    The Thai tradition of blessing and tatooing culture is prevalent in Thai rural area ,where He also encounters the black magic of brahmanical origin trying to hurt him and Buddhist establishment

    its becos of his several narrow death escape, that many devotee revered him as possessing some form of magical power

    Though i dun really respect blessing teaching ,but it does offer a consolation to the rural people who are desperate ,and prevent mental breakdown

    Buddha have come to give us teaching to liberate our mental suffering ,not physical ones

    unorthodox tradition like kumantong and phra ngan still persist in rural thai

    This gives an idea how fortunate singapore is when the material here is abundance and we simply complain its not enough and we want more

    May I transfer all merits to this people and be forever free from borders fighting , opium smuggling and prostitution
