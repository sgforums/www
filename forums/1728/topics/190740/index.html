A Buddha's Drawing

  • coolbluewater
    14 May 06, 20:10
    Here's a picture of Buddha drawn by Barry, an inmate at Kaki Bukit Centre. Gotta know him when I started dharma sharing at KBC, and realised that he is a very talented person at heart. In fact, there are many inmates like Barry who have accepted punishment, and repented. I firmly believe that all beings have the heart of buddha within them, but we are clouded by greed, anger and ignorance. However, always repent and make sure we'll never do it again, forgive ourselves and move on. That's something I've learnt from the inmates who have made it to the society and became a better person.
