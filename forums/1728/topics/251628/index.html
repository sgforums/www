    Dharm Talk: The Similarities & Differences Between the 3 Vehicles/Traditions

    Date: 1 May 2007 / Tuesday/ Labour Day
    Time: 10am-11.30am
    Venue: Phor Kark See, 2nd floor of Ten Thousand Buddha Pagoda.
    Speaker: Prof Cheng Chen Huang

    For more details, go to http://www.kmspks.org/ch/events/index.htm#Pro_Cheng

    Biography of Prof Cheng

    Professor Zheng, a renowned practitioner, at present both the Standing
    Director & Chief Editor of Torch of Wisdom Buddhist Association, Taiwan,
    is actively involved in Dharma propagation activities in Taiwan, different
    states of America and South East Asia. These activities included of Dharma
    Talks, Workshops and Meditation Retreat.

    Not only has Professor Zheng been renowned in Buddhism philosophies and
    practices, he had published more than 500 news articles and 20 books. Some
    of these included the translated Chinese versions such as Tibetan Book of
    Living and Dying, by H.H. the Dalai Lama at Harvard; Consciousness at
    Crossroads; Anapanasati; Zen in the Martial Arts; Beginning to See and
    Mind Like Fire Unbound.
