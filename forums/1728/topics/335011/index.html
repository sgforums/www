    These gifts are the results of cultivation of the mind along the Samatha (concentration) meditation, most likely in his previous life, or he could be a former being from the higher heavens. You may want to read about the 6 types of psychics powers of the mind, 5 of them attainable by people in any religion, but the 6th one only attained by Buddha/ Arahants - destruction of mental intoxicants. Generally the psychic powers arises when one attains the 4th Jhana (mental absorption) and above (or 5th Jhana in Ahbidhamma) , when the mind is extremely refine , calm and in the state of equanimity. Warning though, attaining such feats without purity of heart, can be a source of danger for greed and delusion to arise.
