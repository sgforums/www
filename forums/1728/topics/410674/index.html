    When we let go of "I', of tribalism and see sentient beings as they are; fellow sufferers.

    As long as cling to "I" and identify I, Ourselves to  Others, sure intolerance will ensue.

    As long as we see it as a struggle between Us(be it religion , race or creed or class  etc.) and Them, Other, a rift exist. Too often, moi included, are guilty of tribalism, as we attach identity label on ourselves(I am a Singaporean, Buddhist, Christian, Muslim, Taoist,etc.) and then; others.

    It is useful if we wish to preserve our society ,our values and way of life, if we are engaged in some sort an inevitable violent struggle to have such an identity, but why let it get to that stage in the first place?!  

    Sometimes it all seem so natural that we are not even aware of it, as we fail to see the individual in the person, and lump them together with others in the same label as others: evil,radical, fundamentalist.
