    tot i post this here instead, as most rebirth topic had expired.

    'The Elucidation of Consciousness' provides very good answer to the subject of karma & consciousness.

    QUOTE
    "As an illustration when the wind passes over exquisite flowers, the flower remain where they are, while their fragrance spreads far and wide. The substance of the wind does not take in a fragrance of the exquisite flowers. The substances of the fragrance, the wind, and the organ of touch' have neither shape nor color, but the fragrance cannot spread far away without the power of the wind. Wise Protector, in the same way, after a sentient being dies, his consciousness will take birth again together with the elements of feeling, awareness, and dharmas. Accompanied by the elements of feeling, awareness, and dharmas, the consciousness is reincarnated through [the union of] its parents, who are the conditions of its rebirth.
