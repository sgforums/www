    9 Sep 06, 18:09
    You are already an extraordinary individual.

    Just simply chanting may not serve any purpose. A certain level of sincerity, mindfullness and a still-mind are needed. It will be very effective, if you can do these. It will be also very helpful, if more family members can participate. They are all equally great, whether it is the Great Compassion Mantra, or the Earth Store Sutra, or .....
    What is more important, which I suggest, is to choose one which you have the affinity with. A good choice will probably be just recitation of "NaMoAMiTuoFo" or "NaMoDiZhangWangPuSa" or "NaMoGuanShiYinPuSa". Remember, the most important thing still is about the state of your mind, during the recitation.

    The most immediate action which you can consider doing, to mitigate bad karma is by going vegetarian and to dedicate merits to your father, the more family members the merrier. Avoid all unwholesome deeds and cultivate all good deeds using the name of your father. Volunteer for something etc.

    If you come from a Chinese background, you may be interested to get the "DiZhangPuSa BenYuanJing" - 101 VCDs by Master Chin Kung. It will be very helpful.

    NaMo AMiTuoFo.
