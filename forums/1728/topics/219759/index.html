I closed my eyes, opened again, the hand has still there but very quickly it sort of get sucked away from me. I think it does not have harmful intentions and maybe want us to help, so we dedicated merits to that spirit.

    In buddhism there is not only one kind of petas but generally thirty six kind, each with different looks and characteristics.

    As for devas, i hadnt met one. But my dharma teacher did met one years ago. That time she almost died and was sent to the hospital and she could feel her blood stopped flowing and i think her heart stopped beating. The deva was very very tall... And it wore costumes that look like military kind. It spoke in a language my dharma teacher couldnt understand. But anyway my teacher was revived. My dharma teacher didnt knew that she encountered a deva, it was only later that she realised it and confirmed with another teacher from taiwan. As for what exactly happened i would rather not discuss about it here. But anyway there are many different types of devas and they also looked differently. There are devas from the sensual planes and the higher ones are the brahmas from the jhana planes. Devas and brahmas have much lighter bodies than our dense physical bodies. And the highest kinds are residing in formless planes so they have no body and are shapeless. Their consciousness are absorbed in jhanas.

    That was quite long ago so I don't remember the details. She din say whether male or female but i believe it was male. And yes, devas do wear costumes, but are called 'celestial clothings'. I don't think people asked her that much about how the deva looked like. But she was quite surprised by the tallness and I think she even had to looked up to see the whole figure.

    But Guan Yin and Amitabha is even taller, especially in Sukhavati (Western Pure Land). Of course they can appear short to people but in Sukhavati especially, they appear extremely tall. My master saw Amitabha before and my Taiwanese teacher saw Guan Yin before.

    My Taiwanese teacher was able to enter deeply into samadhi even before he knew about the teachings of Buddhism or attained enlightenment, and attained lots of supernatural powers. At his earlier days, he was saved by Guan Yin, when he foolishly used his powers to fight a few vengeful spirits but in return was attacked by a whole bunch of them. He fought the spirits to protect someone he knew and was reprimanded by my master for using spiritual powers in this way. My Taiwanese teacher later set a rule for his students not to seek any spiritual powers before gaining wisdom.

    Anyway so my Taiwanese teacher died in the battle, and his consciousness left his body. He was very lost and didn't know what to do. Then when Guan Yin appeared to him, Guan Yin commented that he was pitiful and 'resurrected' him. When he looked around, all he could see is enormous toes... the toes itself is like the height of a human. Guan Yin was so tall that he didn't see the whole figure, and therefore couldn't recognise who was the one who saved him. It was only later that he confirmed with my master that it was indeed Guan Yin who appeared and saved him.

    This reminds me that in Chapter 25 of the Lotus Sutra, the sutra states that Guan Yin will save those people who are in danger. It further states many many dangerous situations where through contemplating Guan Yin's power alone, one will be saved. That is why Guan Yin is known as 'Jiu4 Ku3 Jiu4 Nan4 Guan Shi Yin'. One of the things it stated is that when evil spirits of the entire universe is aiming at attacking you, you will be saved from them and they will not harm you if you would just contemplate the powers of Guan Yin, chant Guan yin.

    Another 'big toes' story is about my master. He visited Sukhavati/Western Pure Land while he was alive. I'm going to post on that.

    Disciple: Master, how do we witness Sukhavathi?

    Master Shen Kai: That's easy. But Sukhavathi is very far away. How far? It's passing through hundreds of thousands of millions of Buddhalands. In between this hundreds of thousands of millions of Buddhalands are not Buddhalands but Hui Tu. Buddhalands are pure where Buddhas dwell. Sukhavathi is hence very very far away. Far does not mean we don't believe.

    In the sutras, Buddha used to explain that it's very very near. So near that nobody believe it's true. There's no choice as sentient beings are too greedy, hence say it's very very far away and not easy to go or reach. So He expound the Amitabha Sutra, where Buddha spoke without people asking. If a person is not greedy, then Dharmakaya is very near as it encompass everything.
    Since it's entire existence, it's so near to reach like space everywhere exist Dharmakaya. Without Dharmakaya, Earth cannot be in the space. Cars cannot works. Every thing is benefited by Dharmakaya.

    When people do business, they need an initial funding, now Buddha say you don't need that initial funding, you believe in Amitabha and recite His name.
    When one see people wearing a gold necklace, but it's just a piece of metal chain like the one you see in jail to chain prisioners. Then Buddha told sentient beings not to be obsess with gold, you just need to recite Amitabha and don't need any initial funding and you can reborn in a place where gold is use for building constructions.
    So does Sukhavathi really exist? Yes. Don't think Buddha is lying. You can GO NOW and witness yourself and come back to tell people. Master tell you I been there before, you all might disbelieve. So I will describe it to you.
    Sentient beings on Earth are very small, Bodhisattvas of Sukhavathi are really Big and tall. A person standing next to Amitabha only reach the height of Amitabha's toe! When I reached Sukhavathi, only wish to pay homage to Amitabha, and adore Amitabha.
    Amitabha Buddha reply: What do you seek here in Sukhavathi?
    Shen Kai: Honored One, i came to seek Dharma.
    Amitabha Buddha ask: What kind of Dharma?
    Shen Kai: i'm not sure. Seeing Amitabha's forehead is adorned with a curled hair. i wisk to seek that Dharma, Honorable One.
    (Back to reality.) That's how I cultivate and got my curled hair, but this Dharma method cannot anyhow expound to people. It's is because you people have doubt in Sukhavathi. Thinking it doesn't exist. Hence I tell you my experience. Due to the busy schedule, the white hair is curled. When it grew longer to 3 inch, it will extend when i'm washing the face. That's the proof.

    How did Ocean of suffering (ku hai wu bian) come about? Because beings of samsara cannot sever emotion, love and greed. Sentinet beings cry a lot. Separation from friend, family, love ones, people cry. Son is leaving, mother crys, so does the son. Mother is dying, sons also crys. When people want's to eat, the mouth starts to water. This is greed. People need to work, they swear. In a life them, people generate so many water. One person, say one bucket of water, 100 pax, 100 bucket form a pool of water. If millions of millions of people cannot sever emotion, love and greed, form an Ocean of water.

    In our world there's male and female. Those who goes to jail, 99% are involve with female. But in Sukhavathi, there's not male nor female form.
