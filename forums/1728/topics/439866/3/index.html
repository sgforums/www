    If the view of inherency is still present, then when you realize awareness, it will certainly be reified due to the inherent view.

    The only way it can be dissolved is by arising the insight into anatta and emptiness. You can do this by contemplating on Bahiya Sutta, or the verses Thusness said in his article On Anatta (No-Self), Emptiness, Maha and Ordinariness, and Spontaneous Perfection, etc - many ways but comes down to contemplating on anatta.
