    Those who are interested to view the relics of our supreme teacher Lord Buddha, some of his major disciples and arahants may like to pay a visit to FA HUA MONASTARY, which is located at 381 Upper Paya Lebar Road.

    The relics was invited yesterday and will be open for public view from 10am - 9pm daily till this coming sunday on 27 Feb 2005, when the relics will be send off on 11am.

    For your information, this temple is located very near the Wat Kancanarama Thai Temple at Lor Ong Lye, along the main road (upper paya lebar road).

    I've just been there this evening, there's alot of friendly and helpful helpers in the temple to explain the exhibits to visitors.
