Homage to Lord Sakyamuni Buddha

    Wishing everyone a fulfilling and happy Vesak Day =)

    May everyone be reminded the compassion of Lord Sakyamuni Buddha whom had appeared on this little planet for about 25 centuries ago.

    May everyone be determined to follow Lord Sakyamuni's path for not only purifying oneself but to family and friends.

    May everyone be inspired to comprehend Lord Sakyamuni's discourses indepth and apply for the benefit of oneself and others.

    May everyone be firm and resolute in staying as a true child of Lord Sakyamuni and not going astray into pseudo teachings.

    May Vesak Day not be the only day in the year where Lord Sakyamuni's name is praised.

    Homage to the Teacher of the Threefold World, the Benevolent Father of the Four Kinds of Sentient Beings, Leader of the Saha World, our Fundamental Teacher, Lord Sakyamuni Buddha.
