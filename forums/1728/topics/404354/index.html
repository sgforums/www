    Our life is like riding on a roller coaster: a moment high with happiness, and the next drowning with sorrow and anger. While we are actively seeking happiness, yet happiness seems to be so elusive. Why are we experiencing such ups and downs? What causes our moodiness? Can we have a better control of these emotions? Is it possible to develop a more stable and peaceful state of mind? Come join us to explore these issues together from the Buddhist perspective.
