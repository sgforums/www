28th Kagyu Monlam 2010

  • RU45@(
    17 Jun 10, 16:51

    The 28th Kagyu Monlam will be held  from 15th – 22nd December 2010 in Bodh Gaya.

    The 17th Gyalwang Karmapa and other high Rinpoches will preside over the assembly  of  thousands of monks, nuns and lay devotees who will pray for the well-being of the world and that all sentient beings  may be touched by the wisdom, compassion and power of the buddhas and bodhisattvas.

    Gyalwang Karmapa will give The Public Teaching on The Aspiration Prayer of Samantabhadra.  His Holiness will give oral instructions on different meditations, and will give a commentary each day on one of the specific prayers for that day.

    In addition there will be different prayers recited for the well-being of Tibet.

    The Medicine Buddha  Ritual, Tara and Guru Rinpoche  prayers  and so forth will be recited  for the benefit of the living, and the  Akshobhya  Ritual  and Fire Ritual will be offered for those who have passed away.  For the benefit of both the living and the deceased, there will be a complete reading of the Kangyur, the Sixteen Arhats Ritual, the Alms Procession, the Sutric tradition Guru Puja with distribution of tsog, and the Lamp Prayer.
