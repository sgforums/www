    Programme :

    July 19-21
    Tara Retreat

    July 23 at 2:30 pm
    Dorje Phurpa Empowerment

    July 30
    Festivities on the occasion of Venearble Tenga Rinpoche’s 80th birthday

    July 31 at 2:30 pm   
    Hayagriva Empowerment in the tradition of Kyergangpa; a transmission of the Shangpa Kagyü school, for the removal of outer and inner negative influences, a self-generation of which is practiced with the extensive Gönkar practice, will be transferred by Tenga Rinpoche.

    August 6 at 2:30 pm       

    "Thugdrub Lama Norlha" Empowerment; a wealth aspect of Guru Padmasambhava from the Thugdrub Barche Künsel cycle of termas, particularly for the alleviation of economical difficulties, will be transferred by Tenga Rinpoche.

    August 07-08
    Guruyoga and Tsok offering to the Mahasiddha Milarepa

    August 08-10
    Gönkar Retreat

    August 13 at 2:30 pm       
    Guru Rinpoche Empowerment; Terma by Rigdzin Jatshon Nyingpo “Tsedrup Chagkyi Riwo”, long life practice “Iron Mountain”, will be transferred by Sangye Nyenpa Rinpoche.                                                
     
    Daily schedule
