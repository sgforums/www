    thanks for the classification.

    Tantric Power Spots include places in Nepal (Parping, Godhavari, Sankhu, Maritika etc)

    In India, Bodhgaya is of course a blessed place. But many people there.

    Orissa is one too.  There is Mount Kailash. Padma Ko, Sikkhim, Tso Pema, Lapchi mountains, Chimphur near Samye Monastery. 

    I'm just giving the general region. One has to know the specific location.  It may be a small area or a cave etc. 

    I have not been to Borobodur but it seems to be a energetic spot too from what i've heard.

    The actual very highly blessed places will tend to be quite uninviting and only renunciates with full dedication to practice can stay long and bear the living conditions&hardships... not to mention that sometimes the guardians seeing that you are not suitable will create obstacles.  But sincere practitioners will benefit alot.

    Hope and wish that you will have the chance to do practice at such places.

    In the meanwhile, wherever u practice, u can make it more conducive by fixing a place of practice, so that spiritual momentum builds up and anchor the force-field with prayers, intention, crystals, holy objects, relics etc.  It will help alot over time.  Having a realised being stay at that place before will also help. 

    In Tibetan tradition, the geomancy of the place for doing retreat is regarded as being very important.  It can decide the difference between success or failure.  Those who think that they can succeed against all odds with just their inner qualities while disregarding the external factors which will highly facilitate one's practice or otherwise, will often fail to harvest the fruits of their practice.
