    A very good movie imo my dharma friend recommended me.

    It's about how a man who survived a plane crash and undergone a mystical transformation that ended his fear and identification with a self.

    http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fearless_%281993_film%29

    A post by our moderator longchen in 1 Jan 2006 in another forum came to mind:

    There is no going toward the Source... we are the Source. The fact that you do not understand this... show that to me... you have not awaken yet...

    We are both the source and the creative expression of it simultaneously. Creative expression and return to essense happens all the time, at each and every moment. It is the temporary 'not being able to return to essense' that is as per karma. To me, source is constantly seeking to complete the cycle... just like fluid dynamics. Every single one of us follows a divine order... seeking to balance the whole all the time... it is balanced and being done ALL the time... it is just that the individual parts cannot see the TOTALITY. The indivdual part are us... the individual selfs. Therefore, i say, we as the individual self/personality' is not the 'doer of actions'... by being thoughts and action...we are the 'being done'.

    Yes, healing is important... but the healing only leads to greater and greater understanding that we are the source and that nothing is really that important. Things are displays... even death is an illusion... so whats' so scary about not ascending?

    Constantly...we are being fooled otherwise... by the concepts, conditionings that influences us... even if the perpertuator says 'WE ALONE HAVE THE TRUTH'. However, even the perpetuators are a part of the dynamics of balancing.

    Fearlessly, we enter the NOW moment, and realise that all along we have been fooled by the thoughts that is us... Nevertheless, it is also the thoughts that is us that returns us back to the understanding of us as the vast Source.

    When we realise that we are the source... less and less we are bothered by events. More and more fearless and happy we become... This is what is happening to me. I have healed my fear of death.
