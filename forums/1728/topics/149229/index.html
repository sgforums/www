    As long as the sentient mind exist,

    Democracy with no law = anarchy.

    Freedom with no law = chaos.

    But true 'Wu Wei', is without any sentient thoughts, no dharma/law to obtain. Xin1 Sheng1 Wan4 Fa3 Sheng1, Xin1 Mie4 Wan4 Fa3 Mie4:
    Ten thousand dharma/laws arise out of the mind, ten thousand dharma/laws cease with the mind. All dharmas, all laws, are made in accordance to our sentient mind.

    If everyone on Earth is a Buddha, and the world is a Pure Land, everywhere are Pure Minds, then there would be no need for laws. It would be like Amitabha's pure land, for that to happen in our world it would be a very long time later, when Maitreya Bodhisattva attains Buddhahood. But the current age is the age of the 5 defilements, and laws are important so that our actions are beneficial to both Ourselves and Others, and not the opposite.
