    A lot of People thought 4 Noble Truth is so simple

    but when Buddha spoke to the 5 bhikkhu of the Sangha after He attained Enlightenment ,the Four Noble Truth is more sophiscated than one seen

    He said to the five Bhikkhu , Thus is the Truth of Suffering , thus one have know , thus one should sever ,thus is the Cause of Suffering , thus one have know , thus one should sever,thus is the Truth of the End of Suffering , ,thus one have know ,thus one should sever, thus is the Truth to the cause of end of suffering ,thus have know ,thus one should practise

    that is the 3 turnings of the Wheel

    Suffering - the Fruition to the Worldly Existence
    Cause of Suffering - the Cause of our Worldly Existence
    End of Suffering - the Fruition to Out of the Worldly Existence
    Truth of End of Suffering -the Cause of out of the Worldly Existence
