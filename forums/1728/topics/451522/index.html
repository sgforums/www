    [Q]: Rinpoche I have a question and that concerns the dying bardo and organ donations. Some tantric teachers say when one is dying if you give away your organs it sort of interferes with the dying bardo. Is that true?
    [A]: If you are a good tantric practitioner, you have got good instructions, and you have been practising for some time, then I would say, better not because you have quite a good chance to to reach enlightenment there. Your organs will only help one or two people. Your getting enlightenment will help billions. Having said that, if you are not a good tantric practitioner but you are an aspiring Mahayana practitioner, a bodhisattva, you can’t really do these deity practices then by all means I would encourage you to give. An act of compassion is priceless. Even managing to help one sentient being is worth it.

     

    oh, this goes for purelanders too.

    ps: in italic: that does not mean purelanders are not aspiring Mahayana practitioner, a bodhisattva, or uncompassionate.

    Relationship you dont have much choice. When it comes it comes!! The only people who have the choice is those sublime beings, those who have no more karma, no more emotions, then they can choose. Oh okay today I am going to have relationship, tomorrow I am going to pause the relationship and start day after tomorrow. For people like you and me we have no control. Sometimes you even think that I have enough of relationship or I am not going to have any relationship or even you think like I am not good or nobody is going to like me. Even if you think like that when your karmic blows relationship doesn't come out of nowhere. If there is no circumstances like that even though you are the most beautiful or good looking and you have everything and you are very confident it will be dry like a Sahara desert, nothing, no relationship. So, important about relationship is not to have too much expectations, little bit of expectation we have as human beings but thats alright. We have too strong expectations both negative and positive. Oh I am going to have or I am never going to have it. Too strong decisons and expectations always ruins the relationship. If you are a couple I think its good to think like...your attitude should be like you have checked in a hotel for few days togather and soon the guests are going to go away separate. Its a temporary thing. Like I might never see her again tomorrow, this may be our last goodbye, may be it help. It will bring preciousness of the relationship. If it comes you should not be afraid and you should have relationship. If it doesnt happen then again you should not be too hard on this.

    ~ Dzongsar Khyentse Rinpoche.
