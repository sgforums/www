    KATHINA (ROBE OFFERING) CEREMONY_SUNDAY, 23 OCTOBER

     

    Dear Devotees and Dhamma friends,

    We are happy to remind all that this year's Kathina Celebration will
    be held on Sunday, 23 October. Due to the enthusiastic and good
    response of last year's event, this year's Kathina will be similar,
    i.e. Communal Kathina. Everyone is welcome to be a sponsor. You can
    either choose to be an individual sponsor or get a few friends
    together and form a group sponsor. Each sponsorship is S$500.
    You will soon receive a copy of our Kathina leaflets which provide
    details of the event and a detailed explanation of the meaning of
    Kathina and the benefits of participation. Leaflets are also available
    at the temple.

    If you wish to participate and be a sponsor, please see any of the
    following to register your interests:
    Brother Johnson (Treasurer)
    Sister Rocana (Asst Treasurer)
    Sister Sin Gek
    Sister Elsie

    May your merits be the conditions for attaining the Knowledge of
    Awakening. May you always be well, happy and peaceful
    With metta,
    Rocana

    Sunday/25 Sep 2011 - "Song Pret" - Sending Off - Alms Offering and Dhamma Speaker Ven. Devinda

    Monthly (Alms Offering) Every 1st & 3rd Sunday 9 am - 11 am

    Dear Dhamma friends,

    We are happy to invite all to come join us complete the "Pret"
    Festival this Sunday, which is the sending off. Two weeks ago, we
    conducted "Rap Pret" which was the receiving/welcoming of our
    ancestors and departed friends and relatives to share our merits
    accrued through meritorious deeds. This Sunday is the completion of
    the ceremony and the sending off. The program includes alms offering
    and Abhidhamma chanting. Our Dhamma speaker is Ven. Devinda from the
    Oxford Buddha Vihara with the topic of "How to Cope with the
    vicissitudes of life'. Complete program below:

    9.00 a.m. Chanting with the Sangha
    9.30 a.m. Taking of 3 Refuges and Observance of the 5 Precepts,
    followed by puja offerings of light, incense, flowers, water and
    fruits to the Triple Gem.
    9.45 a.m. Dhamma Talk "How to Cope with the Vicissitudes of Life"
    10.45 a.m. Abhidhamma Chanting
    11.00 a.m. Alms Offering, followed by Sanghadana (offering of
    robes/requisites to the Sangha members). transference of merits
    11.30 a.m. Lunch Dana

    We look forward to your participation and sharing the job of Dhamma with you.
    May you be well, happy and peaceful and may your merits be the
    conditions for the attaining of the Knowledge of Awakening.

    With metta,
    Rocana

    Think Wisely, Act Harmoniously, Live Peacefully
    Visit us at www.uttamayanmuni.org
