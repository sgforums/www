Lerab Ling All Mandala Retreat, July 14-30

  • RU45@(

    The All Mandala Retreat is the retreat that Sogyal Rinpoche most looks forward to every year. It is the one time when the whole Rigpa sangha can meet at Lerab Ling, the heart of Rigpa’s activity, practise together, and receive the many important teachings that Sogyal Rinpoche only shares when the entire mandala has gathered. This year’s All Mandala Retreat will be particularly significant, as it will include the visit of Dilgo Khyentse Yangsi Rinpoche.

    Event : Lerab Ling All Mandala Retreat

    Dates and fees

    Entire Retreat:
    Wednesday 14 July – Friday 30 July: 16 days — 784 euros (benefactor), 560 euros (standard price) or 448 euros (concession:  who is eligible ?)

    Short Option, including the visit of Dilgo Khyentse Yangsi Rinpoche:
    Wednesday 14 July – Thursday 22 July: 8 days — 392 euros (benefactor), 280 euros (standard price) or 224 euros (concession)

    To ensure that your arrival is relaxed, we advise you to come near the beginning of the orientation time. Please note that if you arrive outside of these hours, the orientation team will probably not be available and you may not be able to move into your accommodation.

    End of the Retreat: Friday 30 July, early afternoon (or Thursday 22 July, early afternoon for the short option)
