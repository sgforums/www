    The benefits of animal liberation practice are immense. Not only do the creatures being liberated receive the benefit and blessing, of which they will rebirth in the higher realm, animal liberation practice is also strongly encouraged for those with illnesses or heavy life obstacles, as by saving the lives of others,one creates the strong karmic cause for long life.

    During the event, Tsok offering will be performed on the Kelong. Milk and flowers will also be offered to the Nagas. All these inconceivable merits accumulated will then be dedicated to the long life of His Eminence Garchen Rinpoche and all glorious lineage masters.
