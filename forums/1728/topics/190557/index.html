    Buddha taught for 45 years after his enlightenment.

    You are right that there are simply so many sutras - that it is impossible to finish reading them. What you should do is to follow an enlightened teacher who can guide you and practise what he/she taught. That will be a lot faster - otherwise you can read all the sutras and yet not become enlightened.

    If you happen to be located in Singapore, you can also try Buddha Dhamma Mandala Society (BDMS) where their website is http://www.bdms.org.sg/ .

    Their spiritual director is Ven. S. Dhammika who conducts Sutta Discussions on Friday evenings & "All About Buddhism" course on Sunday mornings.

    So for further details, check out the above website. Btw, they also conduct 3 Refuges for Buddhists-to-be.
