    Dear Friends

    We welcome you to Awakening Upon Dying a talk by Elio Guarisco translator of The Tibetan Book of the Dead.

    In this lecture Elio will highlight the instructions of the Tibetan Book of the Dead according to the teachings of Dzogchen or the Great Perfection as a mean to attain spiritual realization at the time of death and the postmortem stages.
    Elio is a student of Namkhai Norbu Rinpoche under whose supervision he translated The Tibetan Book of the Dead written in the 8th century by Padmasambhava.

    Please see the detailed information in the attached booklet.

    The lecture will take place 7 PM Friday 22nd of March at 48 Frankel Ave, Singapore Ean Keng Si Buddhist Temple

    Organizer: Namdrolling, Dzogchen Community in Singapore. http://atiyoga.org/#events
