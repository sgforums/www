    He is also looking for and gathering testimonies from Buddhists, I have posted mine.. I hope other Buddhists from BWB wld post their testimonies too Bcos Buddhists generally lack testimonies, usually they chit chat among other buddhist friends about their own experiences, but others would not know if we just keep to ourselves.

    Below are some pointers to guide you in your writing, feel free to write down your feelings as you wish.

    * How have you benefited from Buddha's teachings?
    * Did it changed my life in any way? If yes, how?
    * Are you grateful to Buddha's compassion for teaching the precious Dharma? How would you express it in words? What would you say to Him?
    * Is there any mentor or someone that helped you greatly to tide over great difficulties or inspired you and helped strengthen your faith and practice? How would you like to express your gratefulness here?
    * What words of encouragement or well-wishes would you give to others in the path of cultivation and facing life's challenges?

    * Please take care to avoid Buddhist terms that beginners may not understand, abusive words; and words that may be deemed as disrespect to other races and religions. Also, please keep your content non-sectarian and positive in order to benefit people from other Buddhist traditions, non-Buddhists and free thinkers as well. Thank you!
