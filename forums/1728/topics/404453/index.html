    Gampopa Sonam Rinchen (1079-1153), a physician from Dagpo, was the foremost student of the Milarepa. Gampopa was renowned for the clarity of his perception and his knowledge of both Kadampa and Mahamudra methods. He integrated Atisha’s Kadampa teaching and Tilopa's Mahamudra teaching to establish the Kagyu school.

    Our centre is conducting a one full day Gampopa’s Anniversary to commemorate and celebrate one of our most important and foremost teacher. We encourage everyone to participate in this event, particularly students of Kagyu school, to learn more about this great master omniscience and to receive his ever present blessings.
