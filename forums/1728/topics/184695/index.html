    Five Eyes

    There are five kinds of eyes or vision

    human eye - it is our flesh eye, an organ to see an object with limitation, for instance, in darkness, with obstruction.

    divine eye - it can see in darkness and in distance, attainable by men in dhyana (concentration/meditation).

    wisdom eye - the eye of Arhat and Two Vehicles i.e. the sound-hearers (Sravaka) and the Enlightened to Conditions (Praetyka-Buddha). It can see the false and empty nature of all phenomena.

    dharma eye - the eye of Bodhisattva. It can see all the dharmas in the world and beyond the world.

    buddha eye - the eye of Buddha or omniscience. It can see all that four previous eyes can see.
    to add,

    The Divine Eyes cannot be obstructed by anything it see. The human/meat eyes are however obstruct by even a piece of paper.
    The Wisdom eyes sees the Real meaning of Sunyata, but not at all the Conventional Truth.
    The Dharma Eyes (of Bodhisattva) can see through the Conventional Truth.
    The Buddha Eye Is like a Thousand Suns, where each sun can shine the different mundane worlds. However different the worlds are, the knowing of the sun is also Infinity coming from One source light of Buddha Eye.
    Moreover,
    Divine eyes (of arahats) can see 1 Great chiliocosm.
    Dharma eyes (of bodhisattvas) can see 100 great chiliocosm to 1000 great chiliocosm to 1000000 great chiliocosm...depending on which level.
    Buddha eyes has not limit.
