    Event : Chod with Lama Wangdu Rinpoche
    Date : November 12-14
    Venue : Nalanda West, 3902 Woodland Park Avenue North,Seattle, WA 98103
    Website : http://www.betsybergstrom.com/classes/class.php?classID=12


    Programme :
    Friday night:
    Lama Tsering Wangdu Norbu Rinpoche speaks about the Chod lineage and practice and demonstrates the practice.

    Saturday:
    Wangdu Rinpoche offers the Chod empowerments, \"The Bellowing Laugh of the Dakini\" by Rig-Dzin Jigme Ling-Pa and
    \"The Method of Accomplishment on One Seat\" by Karma Chagmed, followed by a feast offering known as Tsok.

    Sunday:
    Wangdu Rinpoche offers the teachings for the Chod Practice and instruction in Damaru (double sided drum)
