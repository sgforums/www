    Dear Dhamma Friends,

    Palelai Buddhist Temple (PBT) is pleased to announce that an adult Dhamma class which has been conducted by Mr Kanya Teo for more than 20 years is on-going for year 2008. This class conducted every Sunday, 9.50am to 10.50am at classroom 2, level 2, Chedi Dhammasathit building, PBT. This Dhamma class is free of charge for anyone who may be interested.

    Please kindly refer to the table below for the 2008 syllabus of the class.

    Palelai Buddhist Temple
    Adult Dhamma Class Syllabus 2008

    Duration Topic

    January to February 2008 - 31 Planes of Existence
    March to April 2008 - Wheel of Life
    May to June 2008 - Rebirth

    This Dhamma class is also afflilated to the Young Men Buddhist Association based in Sri Lanka. As such, Dhamma class students may choose to sit for the annual YMBA examinations conducted at PBT on the last Sunday of the month of June. Examinations are optional. Revision would be provided for students interested in the examinations around 3 months prior to the examinations.

    This Dhamma class is on-going and Dhamma friends who are interested may complete the form enclosed and submit the duly signed form by hand or post to Palelai Buddhist Temple, 49 Bedok Walk, Singapore 469 145, by fax to 6443 9381 or email to watpalelai@yahoo.com.sg for our administration purposes. Please attention all submissions to Mr. Kanya Yeo. Dhamma friends may start attending the class with immediate effect and no applications to learn Dhamma would be rejected.

    Please do not hesitate to contact PBT administrative staff, Mr Keith at 6443 3324 or myself at watpalelai@yahoo.com.sg should you require further clarifications with regards to this matter.

    Please kindly assist to disseminate this information for the benefit of many.

    May all be well and happy always!

    With Metta
