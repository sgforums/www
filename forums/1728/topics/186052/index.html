    it is indeed good to see the promotion of Buddhist material .but there is a good reason to explain the mandala picture ,because Buddhism is an education ,not a blind faith

    this featured here is the Great Compassion Mantra in Siddam script , the original sanskrit text of Great Compassion Mantra

    The vast wordings will tend to scare beginner off .
    In Simplicity , in accordance to Tientai Buddhism Esoteric teaching

    Just Visualise the Centre word which is pronounced as Hrih(Har Ree)

    The seed word of Avalokiteshvara

    The Seed word of Amitabha is Also Hrih for your info

    then the seed word of Da shi zhi is Dsar

    Visualise the word on your forehead shine onto your whole body
    and remember the virtue and the great compassion of Avalokiteshvara

    every Buddha and Bodhisattva have a seed word
    in accordance to huayen and tientai teaching

    seed word is important in the practise of Esoteric Buddhism

    Print it and distribute to other , remember in mind the Power of Avalokiteshvara is inconceivable in this picture
