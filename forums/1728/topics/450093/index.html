    Dzogchen Teachings by Chögyal Namkhai Norbu Rinpoche in Singapore
    The teaching for this retreat is “Kuntu Zangpo Monlam”, essential instruction of Ati Dzogchen teaching.

    DATE: 4 - 8 MAY 2012
    VENUE:CHUI HUAY LIM CLUB (Level 3) 190 Keng Lee Road, Singapore 308409

    Retreat program
    4th May 4-6pm.
    Introduction of this teaching related with the transmission of Ati Guru Yoga and tridlung of Short Thun. 
    5th May 10-12am.
    Introduction about the Base and Tridlung of the Gana Pujas.
    6th May 10-12am.
    Introduction about the illusory visions related with Five Emotions. And giving Tridlung of Medium Thun.
    12.45am – 3.00pm
    Gana Puja for the Full Moon.
    7th May 10-12am.
    Introduction continue about the illusory visions related with Five Emotions. And giving Tridlung of Longer Thun Practice.
    8th May 10-12am.
    Giving some advices for the daily life practices and tridlungs of collective practices and so on. We finish this retreat with a practice of Ati Guru Yoga altogether.
    4.00 - 6.00pm
    Gana Puja for the end of this retreat.
