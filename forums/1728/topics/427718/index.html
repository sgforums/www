    Lust is like any form of attachment which can be very difficult to look after. “Combating” lust that accompanys with aversion  may not be the most correct way. Contemplating on 32 parts of the body helps in dealing with attachment of form. But I need is diligence in practicing it and building on my reserve of strength & wisdom

    When lust arises very strongly, sometime it can be very difficult to be mindful of. As of these few days, I come across some dharma materials which resonate with me that most of us run after TV / Music / games , any form of external pleasure for comfort. It just temporarily covers up the suffering which has always been inside us. 

    As accordance to 4 noble truth, there is a way out of suffering and so we shall not despair when we have despair...

    Anyway, I found this when I was browsing for some inspiration online:

    “ The advice is always the same: watch the emotion arise, notice that there was a time when it was not and know that there is a time when it will no longer be. Let it flow through you as you notice your breath as it comes and goes. Let it become gentle.

    Of course there is a lot said and written about lust, anger, greed, etc., but they are all the same, in the end: transitory events.

    As ever, the choice is yours whether to give in and wallow in the feeling or to stay balanced and gently amused by the ingenuity of our body/mind to distract us from equanimity.”

    And

    “ Lust is managed with right view regarding sexuality. Right view regarding sexuality is based in the perception of what his harmful sexual conduct and what is not. If we do not have a clear perception of what is harmful and not harmful in regards to sexuality, it will be difficult to manage lust.”

    And

    “In accordance to Venerable Hsuan Hua, it is important to cut off lust if one is sincere in getting out cycle of birth and death.”

  • An Eternal Now
    23 Apr 11, 18:52

    you must understand that whatever you practice, you cant expect results like removal of lust just overnight. it takes gradual practice and attainment. if you are able to overcome lust, that means you no longer have the cause to be reborn in the sensual plane. it means you have attained the stage of anagami. if overcoming cravings were that easy, everyone would have accomplished the four noble truths by now and become liberated. but thusness has spoken from experience that it is totally possible to overcome lust. but you need to have deep insights into anatta and impermanence until dispassion (lack of passionate feelings) arises.

    the attainment of insight and samadhi is able to permanently burn away fetters

    that is why insight meditation is so important

    before that, we can only apply measures to temporarily treat arising lust. whatever ways you try to lessen lust is merely a skillful measure to treat the problem, suppress the symptoms, but it does not uproot the source of craving. chanting is one way as mentioned by people.

    chanting is like putting a stone on the grass so it stops sprouting. like i said, you cant expect results just overnight. but when your chanting builds a momentum, you will be able to go on auto chanting mode throughout your waking and sleep states. even if you have nightmares in dreams, your chanting is able to serve as a positive force and momentum for you to overcome problems and remain calm. and of course the same goes for waking life and facing lust.

    that said chanting does not uproot the problem, like i said chanting is like a stone put on grass/weed to stop it from going. but to uproot the fetters from its roots, you need realization, experience, right view, and samadhi. at that point you can attain the stage of overcoming the fetters and be freed from samsara.
