    Tsikey Chokling Rinpoche's Vision

    His Eminence Tsikey Chokling Rinpoche, was enthroned as the 4th authentic reincarnation of Chokgyur Lingpa and as a tertön by His Holiness the 16th Karmapa. In accordance with his own prophetical dream and the heart advice of his root guru, His Holiness Dilgo Khyentse Rinpoche, Chokling Rinpoche is now building an outer representation of Zangdok Palri in Vajravarahi, outside Kathmandu, Nepal. As Zangdok Palri is the focal point of Guru Rinpoche’s realisation, it would be of great benefi t for the peace and happiness of the world, for the long lives and activities of all the great Tibetan Buddhist masters and the flourishing of the precious teachings of the Buddha. It is a supreme place of liberation through seeing, and accumulation of merit through offerings. In the future, this Zangdok Palri will be a dedicated tantric school to uphold the sacred teachings of the Secret Mantrayana.

    "For the benefit of preserving and propagating the precious Buddhadharma, liberating each and every sentient being in this age of degenerations, and ensuring the good health, long lives and bountiful activities of all the venerated Masters, I hope that all of you will strive to create a powerful link with this undertaking."
    - 4th Tsikey Chokling Rinpoche

    The Programs in Singapore

    This August, we are very fortunate to have His Eminence Chokling Rinpoche with us to celebrate the Ullambana Festival (commonly known as the Chinese 7th Month Festival). The program is as follows:

    4th Sept (10am): Korwa Dongtruk Changchok Puja
    4th Sept (7.30pm): Eight Manifestation of Guru Rinpoche Empowerment
    5th Sept (7.30pm): Eight Manifestation of Guru Rinpoche Fire Puja
    6th Sept (7.30pm): A View of Dzogchen (Public teaching)

    These pujas were specially choosen by Rinpoche himself to help us clear obstacles and create the cause for good conditions to arise quickly. It is very rare that Rinpoche visits Singapore during the 7th month. And it is especially auspicious and beneficial to have Rinpoche conduct the very special Korwa Dongtruk Changchok Puja that will benefit both the living and those who has passed on.


    In addition to Rinpoche’s Singapore program, Rangjung Yeshe Oddiyana will be organizing a host of Ullambana Festival activities starting from 10th Aug 2010. Our resident Lama, Tulku Migmar will be conducting a month long Changchok prayers and offerings at our centre.

    * All Changchok participants for the month long Changchok prayers at Rangjung Yeshe Oddiyana will be included in the special Korwa Dongtruk Changchok Puja.

    For more information on H.E. Chokling Rinpoche’s Singapore Program and our Ullambana Festival activities, please feel free to email us at ryo.enq@gmail.com

    We look forward to your participation in these special events. May all be auspicious!
