It is where Shakyamuni Buddha historically attained Enlightenment, which makes it one of the four important holy places in Buddhist culture. It history was also recorded by great Chinese masters such as Venerable Faxian and Venerable Xuanzang. The Mahabodhi Monastery in Bodhgaya was also gazetted as a World Heritage Site by UNESCO in 2002. Bodhgaya will also be the place where the Maitreya, the next Buddha, will attain Enlightenment.
