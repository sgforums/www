Library re-opened

  • neutral_onliner
    17 Oct 06, 23:46
    The Kong Meng San Phor Kark See Monastery Resource Library will be re-opened on the 8th of October in 2006 (1pm) , after major revamping of its facilities.





    Some of its new features include -

    - English and Chinese Buddhist reading materials for general interests
    - Academic materials on all Buddhist traditions for research purposes
    - AV materials, such as CDs, DVDs and VCDs, of Dharma talks, chants,
    documentaries and music
    - Wide variety of periodicals
    - Children room with children reading materials
    - Automated borrowing and returning system, with book-drop service
    - Online catalogue for library item reservation and renewal
    - Multimedia stations for AV items and internet access
    - Wireless internet connectivity
    - Meeting room for general purposes
    - Self-service cafe

    The library will be open daily (including public holidays, other than CNY) -

    Monday to Friday - 11.00 am to 7.30 pm
    Saturday & Sunday - 10.00 am to 5.00 pm

    For enquiries: Tel. 6849-5337 or Email: library@kmspks.org
