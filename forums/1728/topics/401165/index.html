    How karma is being 'calculated' and 'repaid' is extremely complex, and our every action of every minute can add/reduce our overall 'karma balance'. Personally, I don't feel it's necessary to find out every single case, unless for very exceptional and important cases. Even if someone's life was originally planned to end early, ie. he/she was planned to die young from an accident or something, because of previous deeds, he/she can still avoid that outcome in this life.

    One's life will be much more fulfilling if we focus on improving our present and future, rather than on the past. We can change our present and future to be better, but we can't change the past.
