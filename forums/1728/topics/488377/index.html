    We live in a world troubled by an ideology

    May 18, 2015  JC Wilcox The Nation

    Rohingya are claiming persecution in Myanmar. Across North Africa and the Middle East millions are suffering the violence of bigotry. Throughout Europe indigenous cultures are under threat due to mass illegal immigration and there is fear worldwide of further atrocities like 9/11 and 7/7 being committed.

    The people of the south of Thailand are not alone in their resistance to setting up camps for Muslim migrants. The UK is officially refusing to accept further migrants from Islamic countries. Australia is resisting criticism from rights organisations in rejecting illegal immigration. 


    All this fear and unrest and alleged persecution has one single cause, and that is Islam. The media and politicians evade the truth by speaking and writing of terrorism, of extremism, of radicalism and fundamentalism. The last noun is close but as an adjective it tells the truth: fundamental Islam.

    Islam was invented in the 7th century by a warlord in Saudi Arabia who claimed his ideas to be sanctioned by god. It was born of the sword and has grown by instilling fear in both its followers and those who oppose it. Nothing has changed as we see from its activities around the globe.

    There is a genuine, realistic reason for Muslims to be attacked in Myanmar; Muslims are seen to represent Islam. Most of the atrocities worldwide are perpetrated in the name of Islam. The most murderous atrocities are carried out by the Islamic State, ISIS. Israel is under permanent threat from Islam. Europe is under severe threat of Islamisation with mosques appearing like mushrooms. Christians in Egypt were murdered only recently by Islamists because of their religion. Almost all the terror, misery and demographic revolution is due to Islam. Every day this is more apparent with the abduction of school children and the desecration of historic Buddhist images, car bombs in Iraq and inter-Islamic war between Sunnis and Shias in Yemen.

    This is not opinion, it is a matter of undeniable fact. Judeo-Christian democracies and Buddhist countries are completely incompatible with Islam, which is a totalitarian political ideology. The two cannot co-exist any more than Sunni Muslims and Shia Muslims can accept each other on equal terms.

    Such a root cause of this troubled world must be confronted. Failure to do so will only result in even greater misery for both Muslims and non-Muslims alike. This rapidly growing major global threat can no longer be ignored.

    Migrating Muslims, due to their faith, are unable to integrate into Judeo-Christian democratic societies. They should better be absorbed into Islamic countries who share their ideology. This would avoid civil conflict.

    There is no point in Muslims seeking asylum in countries whose paramount law is freedom of expression and whose laws take precedent over Islamic law, Sharia.

    Successful integration can only take place when migrants fully accept the ways and laws of their host country. Muslims have shown they cannot do that. They seek to impose their ideology on countries where they have been received.
