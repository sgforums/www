    How to deal with difficult people?

    Firstly, accept the fact that difficult people exists and are inevitable

    Secondly, recongise that you have the power to choose how you feel about these difficult people.

    Thirdly, Difficult people presents Wonderful opportunities for self-cultivation.
    For Eg. curbing Anger.. by being mindful of how anger arise and disappear, own thought and reactions when dealing with this people. These people can be Valuable teacher in learning how to manage our anger. =)

    Fourthly, Practise compassion. Try to understand their shoes and causes of their behavour. We all err in some phrases of our life. It is important to learn to be more forgiving and don't to take trifle matters seriously.

    Lastly, we can learn to develop wisdom i.E EQ in dealing with them.

    There are many complicated situations in life, we can face in workplace or in school or home ETC. Thus i think it important to learn how to prevent this undesirable Situation by learning how to cope with it...

    Afterall, things don't always go your way. What important is @ the end of the day, you are happy and not letting these afflictive emotions get into you; making you a hateful and unhappy person. We all know when you think happy thoughts, you are happy. When you think unhappy thoughts, you are unhappy.

    it's obvious having hateful thought makes you a unhappy person... right ?
    It's important to start learning how to be mindful then can we learn to curb our anger and prevent bad impulsive behavour .. Learning to have compassion and loving-kindness for others prevent the arising of anger..

    Some of you might have though it unrealistic to behave NICE to someone who is being very Nasty to you. Yeap, you don't have to if you don't feel like it.
    being too nice to them will encourage more of their nasty behaviour but don't downgrade yourself to their standard. The best thing you can do is learn not to have bad feelings for them and try to be gracious to them as much as you can.

    or learn not to get attached to their remarks or behaviour. Learn to see things lightly. For all things are fleeting and impermanent, what the point of holding grudges towards these people? For these people are creating future bad karma ( future sufferings ) for themselves if they continue to behave so to others. We, as buddhist should wish them to repent and have compassion for them instead.

    our TRUE enemies are not the people but the afflictions in our Mind which lead to undesirable behaviour etc. The three posions known as greed, hatred and delusions.These are three evil roots primarily source of all evil deeds. These are our true enemies! we should strive to cultivate to unroot them.

    If you investigate deeper, the cause of his negative actions are caused by the 'three posions' arising also from ignorance. The cause of his actions are not him but the 'three posions'.. the three afflictive Qualities. Our true enemies are not the person but the three posions.. Not ourselves, not themselves but the three posion.

    we strive to unroot the three posions.. cos all of us have the tendency to perform negative actions due to three posions ( the PRIMARY cause of all bad karma ).
