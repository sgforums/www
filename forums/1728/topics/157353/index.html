    Originally posted by An Eternal Now:
    So far I have heard Hua Yen's and Tientai's classification of Buddhist sutra according to the 49 year Timeline of Buddha's dharma discourses.
    okie ,let me explain

    the 49 years timeline is not really timeline
    Buddha spoke to many people on many occasion

    to the Higher faculties - the Bodhisattvas
    the average faulties - arhats and srvakas and lay community and the royalties
    the lower faculties - zhou li pan tie qie

    the main 5 periods of teaching

    He attained Enlightenment on the by deep samadhi and after His struggle with Mara thousand army of desire, temptation and despair .

    at first in the first 49days ,He feels that the teaching He awakened was too deep for any to understand ,and wish to enter Parinirvana with Mara's request

    The lord Brahma ,together with his retinue ,request Buddha Sakyamuni to turn the wheel of Dharma for the sake of those who is still suffering in the 6 realms of existence

    the first period -Flower Adornment
    on the first 3 - 7 days ,Buddha was in the Heavens talking to the nagar(dragons) and Dieties

    He preached the Assembly of Buddhalands which in chinese known as Flower Adornment Sutra

    the Adornment of Various Flowers symbolise all Mahasattvas and Bodhisattvas and Buddhas specialising in their areas of Attainment to periodical appear in the Saha world and other world to help sentient beings

    Next , Buddha realised that His few disciple are Sound-hearer (sravaka ) and
    renounce-affinity ( yuan jue ) are not able to understand the Mahayana teaching of Greatness , so He start from the 4 Noble Truth , the Noble Eightfold path , the 12 dependent originations

    thus the turning of the wheel in Deer park(sarnath)
    that is the Agama Period

    then when the arhat have inspired the great path and many royalties came along with Mahasattvas in the assembly , Buddha initiated the period of teaching of Vaipulya Period (fang guang) means the Magnamious and Greatness of the Great Vehicle in the path of Bodhisattva to Buddhahood

    third period ,Buddha teaches the Prajna period ,which He specifically taken 22 years for the assembly on 10 occasion to understand the whole concept of Emptiness

    on the fourth period , which tientai also refers to akin to a Sun at the 4pm-5pm , He teach the teachings of Dharma Flower Period also known as Lotus Sutra

    Finally as He about to enter Parinirvana,the final few years ,He gives the teachings of nirvana Period ,in this period ,He reiterates the teachings of the Great Vehicles ,the coming of Buddha Maitreya ,the decline of Buddhism in future aeons exhort the assembly to carry on the practice

    His famous Last words is
    "Subject to change are all things"
    "Strive on with Dilligence"
