    Originally posted by extra one:

    shallow people


    When I first read your answer, I reacted, "how dare he say something like that about me, who does he think he is... blah, blah, blah.."

     

    But immediately I was aware of the reaction I was also aware of the mechanism behind it.

     

    It is the same mechanism behind his answer.

     

    I think I know what he is saying, and he thinks he knows what I am saying.

     

    And so we each have an "idea" of what the other person is saying and we respond according to our own idea, our own interpretation.

     

    So really, we can never know for certain what the other person means, we can only guess according to our own experience.

     

    So we never really react to other people, what we react to is our idea of other people. 

     

    If this is seen, then one can laugh!!!
