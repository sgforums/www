    Copying and chanting sutra is not as good as understanding the sutra. Buddha taught the sutra for the purpose of enlightening sentient beings... if we fail to understand and benefit from his teachings then the teaching has lost its purpose.

    Understanding the sutra is not as good as practicing to realize the sutra... and practicing is not as good as realizing the essence... realizing the essence is not as good as actualizing the wisdom. Of course, one should first understand, then practice, then realize, and then actualize it in our life.
