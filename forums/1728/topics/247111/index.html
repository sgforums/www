    The 3 moving house of mencius
    Mencius is the sage who followed Confucianism

    When he was young ,his mum moved house 3 times
    the first move ,landed them in a neighbourhood of operatic people

    Mencius learnt abt opera singing and the ways how to act on stage
    His mother seeing such thing is unsuitable for his future career , decided to move house

    the second move exposed mencius marketplace .Mencius become well-versed abt animal slaughtering and ways to earn in a marketplace .Seeing it is unsuitable for his growth in scholastic future , his mother moved house

    the final move landed in a neighbourhood of school.This give influence to mencius to his future philosophical approach when he read Analect , Great Studes and Zhong Yong
