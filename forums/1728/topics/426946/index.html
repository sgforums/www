    Mahabodhi Monastery is one of the temple I frequently visit. The old building was very old and run down. They had just demolished and is in the progress of building a new one. The new building will be equipped with 21st century technologies and architecture outlook (see the link below for the outlook of the new building).

    They need our financial support for the new building. Support them if we can.

    Click onto the below link and if you wish to donate, click on "Support Us" on the left hand side for the form to fill in.

    There are 2 young well educated local monks in their 30s and a few nuns in this temple. It is Mahayana tradition. It is located at Bukit Timah Road, opposite Beauty World Shopping Centre. There will be a MRT station near the temple in the near future.

    http://www.putige.org/building/support.html

    The Merits of Offering

    1. One’s past light karmic misgivings will dissolve while heavy ones lighten.
    2. One will be protected by devas and will be unharmed by natural and man-made disasters.
    3. One will always be free from the suffering of hatred and vengeance.
    4. One will be unharmed by yaksas, evil spirits and wild beasts.
    5. One’s mind will be at peace, free from harm and nightmares. One’s complexion will be radiant and full of auspicious energy.
    6. One who practices the Dharma wholeheartedly, will have adequate living necessities. One’s family will be harmonious and be blessed will fortune and longevity.
    7. One who practices what one preaches will be respected and loved by all.
    8. One who is dullminded will gain wisdom, one who is ill will gain health and onewho is poor will gain wealth. One who is female will be born as male in the future live.
    9. One will be free from being reborn in the negative realms. One’s look will be decent. One will be respected and blessed with good fortune.
    10. One will be able to plant a good seed and create a good cause for all sentient beings. For the benefit of all sentient beings in mind, one will have a big merit field and be able to gain immeasurable merits. One will also able to learn the Dharma, till one’s wisdom and spiritual penetrations are fully grown and one becomes a Buddha.
