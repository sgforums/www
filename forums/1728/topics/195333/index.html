  • bohiruci

    Feel kind of sad ,but i just wish to share

    I was on the way back home on bus

    When one person alight from the bus something fly into the bus by the rear exit door and jumping up and down

    it is near at the side of the seat before me .
    Then I look carefully ,it is a small fish

    and I was quite shocked ,and I have no container with water to sustain it.I pray hard to Guanyin to save this fish life , and hope it will not die ,no matter how futile it is and nobody to trampled on it.

    just one stop before my home ,i reach the river and when i hold the fish up
    it trembled badly , nearing its death

    something i fail ,was the employment of wisdom because I was at my wits end to save it

    and forget i should just drop it into the river nearby

    for 5 mins i looking for any shop selling water , but i couldnt find

    i saw a drain full of flowing water to the nearest river , and I let it go,

    I just hoped it will have a better rebirth and I feel very sorry for my limited karma to save it

    Raise this up so many out there will have a chance to make the most correct decision to a dying fish in a more faster way
