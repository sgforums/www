With Faith Prayers will be Answered

  • prayer42
    12 Dec 17, 13:45

    2017 was a challenging year. My business which paid rental for the office unit could not continue the rental payments due to partnership issues. I prayed to Guan Yin Bodhisattva to lease or sell my office unit at a good price. After 6 months, I became increasing worried as there was no potential tenants or buyers. I began to question my faith. When I despaired, the Dharma Door chat group sent a discourse on praying to Guan Yin Bodhisattva to lessen our burdens. Not once, but twice. I strenghtened my resolve and continued. After another 3 months, there was still no potential tenant or buyer. Then the Dharma Door chat group sent a discourse on karmic obstacles. I then realised it was my own karmic obstacles that was causing this situation. I then prayed to Guan Yin Bodhisattva that I will accept whatever outcomes that is deemed correct. I made new vows. My heart felt lighter even though my husband continuously pressured me to solve the rental issue fast. I carried on my recitations and little houses. Finally after another 2 months, a tenant is confirmed for 2018. My deepest gratitude to the Greatly Merciful and Greatly Compassionate Guan Yin Bodhisattva, Master Lu and all volunteers of Guan Yin Citta Dharma Door who faithfully send selected discourses through the chat groups.
