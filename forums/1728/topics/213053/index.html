    I heard that bones that were coloured is a good sign, but I am not too sure about whether they are relics.. Yes within this 49 days it is crucial to accumulate merits and transfer to him. Also if possible get a venerable or someone to speak the dharma to your father. Very often your father's antarabhava, or consciousness/intermediate body is still residing around within the 49 day period. You can attend pujas (fa3 hui4) etc and dedicate merits to your father.
