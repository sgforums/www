    Dear Members and Devotees


    You are cordially invited to join us in performing meritorious deeds during our 3rd Kathina Robe Offering
    Ceremony on Sun, 23 Oct 2011. This is an essential monastic event which marks the end of a three-month
    rain retreat. The ceremony is organized through the dedicated and generous support of our members,
    devotees and well-wishers.
    There will also be alms-round (Pindapata), in which devotees will have the opportunity to offer food and
    other requisites to nine Venerable Monks including Ven Dr Pannyawamsa, gracing the ceremony.
    If you would like to contribute towards the ceremony, the items available are sets of Eight Requisites ($50
    per set), Kathina Robes ($20 per set) and other requisites. You may wish to contact us at Tel : 6689-3359
    for more information.
    This year, we will be inviting 40 elderly folks from Shan You Counseling Center for lunch and we will share
    with them some Buddhist teaching.
    The Shan Community in Singapore will be pleased to present their cultural dance and songs as well as their
    cuisine for all on that day.
    On that day, volunteers from Shan You Counseling Center will be selling “ Sasana Soap@ $10 per piece.
    Devotees may offer these soap to the Sangha during our alms round.
    We look forward to your participation in our Kathina Robe offering ceremony and share in the merits
    accrued .
    PROGRAMME
    09:15 am: Welcoming Guests
    09:30 am: Welcoming the Sangha and Launching Kathina Ceremony
    09:45 am: Paying Homage to the Triple Gem, Observing 5 Precepts and Chanting of 30 Parami
    10:15 am: Offering Kathina Robes and Requisites to the Sangha
    10:45 am: Dhamma Talk on the benefits of Kathina Robe offering by Ven. Dr. Pannyavamsa
    11:15 am: Alms-Round (Pindapata) and Offering of “Sasana Soap” to the Sangha
    11:30 am: Offering Lunch to the Sangha
    Lunch for all Devotees
    01:00 pm: Dhamma teaching in Mandarin for the elderly guests
    01:30 pm: Procession of Money Tree (Padaythar Pin)
    02:00 pm: Sharing Merits
    2:30 pm: Shan Cultural Performance
    4:00 pm: End of Programme
    Sadhu! Sadhu! Sadhu!
