    I wonder why u think this topic is not suitable for discussion...

    Anyway, we usually associate the term "retribution" as a negative attribute which we hope that the person will have misfortunate befalls onto him as it is right and so we think. However, by karma, if you have aversion, hatred, selfish thought/feeling for him or delighting in his misfortunate, you are also creating bad karma for yourself.

    Well if you ask me about retribution. By karma, there person will reap what he has sowed. If he sowed bad seeds, he will reap bad harvest but it also depends on whether he knew his mistakes and repented. The anology is akin of planting a seeds but if he hasn't provide the condition for the seeds to grow. He will not reap the bad harvest or lessen the negative effects.

    we may have create wrong action in the past, but we can still stop the conditions, in order to stop the effect.
    like u plant a seed on the ground, but by blocking the sunlight, and by not applying fertilizer, water to the seed, the seed would not grow into a tree.

    to "reduce" retribution, try avoid doing evil actions/karma, do good, and keep transfering the merits to others, especially our "deptors" rather than keep it for oneself. it will help a lot.
    for me, i transfer the merit of being a vegetarian to all sentient beings.
    non-killing or saving lifes creates the the most amount of merits. moreover you are transfering to ALL sentient beings, instead of just few deptors. Such selflessness multiples the amount of meirts/virtues.

    so avoid doing evil is like blocking the sunlight/soil/water to the seed. and doing good is like applying concrete onto the seed!
