    Talk " Dying Peacefully"

    by Venerable Tenzin Palzang

    All of us have to face death, not only that of our relatives and friends, but also our own death. What are the preparations we should make now in order to have a good death?

    What kind of experiences will we encounter during the time of dying ? If we are properly prepared, then when the time comes, we will be able to leave this life peacefully.
