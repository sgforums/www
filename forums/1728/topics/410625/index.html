    Kurukulle — Empowerment & Teachings

    Into the Heart of Deity Yoga

    Among the most skillful means of Secret Mantra Vajrayana is the path of meditation known as deity yoga. As soon as you engage with deity yoga, your mind is in union with enlightenment as you begin to experience the enlightened nature of the universe and all beings.

     

    Although the teachings on deity yoga are vast and sometimes complex, the beloved and illustrious 19thcentury master, Patrul Rinpoche, simplified them in a short text bearing the name Key Points for Meditation: Four Stakes that Secure the Life-Force of Deity Yoga. These precise instructions are believed to have been originally transmitted by Guru Rinpoche himself.

     

    With his characteristic style that combines deep understanding with personal experience, Lama Tharchin Rinpoche will present these teachings as a basis for beginning a practice of deity yoga.

    Kurukulle — Empowerment & Teachings

    In Secret Mantra Vajrayana, the feminine deity or dakini is both the wisdom to be realized and the means for realizing it.

    In the Dudjom Terma tradition, the wisdom dakini Kurukulle, is the inner aspect of Yeshe Tsogyal, the consort of Guru Padmasambhava.

    Known as Rikjayma in Tibetan, her name means dakini that causes one to realize nondual awareness. From  point of view of enlightened activity that benefits beings, Kurukulle is associated with magnetizing enchantment, which draws all beings towards  enlightenment.

     

    By receiving this empowerment and instructions from Lama Tharchin Rinpoche, even as beginners we are empowered to become Kurukulle in body, speech, and mind.
