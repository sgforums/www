    Non buddhist who practice basic tranquility meditation will not achieve enlightenment or liberation which requires right view and contemplative investigation and awareness. There is therefore a difference between tranquility meditation such as that done to silence and tranquilize the mind, and the kind of meditation done to gain insight and awareness. The prior is known as shamatha while the latter is vipassana or insight meditation. Nonetheless even gaining calmness is something good and beneficial to one's mind
