    The Pa-Auk Meditation Centre in S'pore finds a New Home -- .

    Visuddha Meditation Centre has recently been relocated.

    Founded by Pa-Auk Sayadaw’s disciple, Venerable Visuddhacara, in 19 Mar 2003, VMC has been offering meditation classes based on Pa-Auk Sayadaw's method of Samatha and Vipassana Meditation for almost 5 years.

    Last October, with the help of Shwedagon Society (S), VMC has invited Burmese scholar, Dr. Mehm Tin Mon, to run Abhidhamma courses and it plans to invite him for a Visuddhimaga course (The Path of Purification) this coming April.

    For those who are interested in this method of meditation, check out its website for comprehensive information on Pa-Auk Tawya Sayadaw's monastery in Myanmar, as well as download free Dhamma talks by Pa-Auk Sayadaw and Dr Mehm Tin Mon. The centre offers sponsorship letters for obtaining meditation visas to Pa-Auk Forest Monastery. For those who wish to learn this method of meditation or are looking for a place to meditate, the centre offers free meditation classes and meditation space to all.
