    Many people who gained the ability to see ghosts are mistaken that their cultivation is very strong. In reality, most cases of being able to see ghosts are due to 1) Other ghosts aiding them in the ability to see ghosts. 2) Past lives, the are ghosts. (not uncommon) Both of them are 'spirit eyes'. Some people become very attached to these abilities and obstruct their own dharma practise. They may become attached to say, communicating with the other realm. Of course those who have deep samadhi, attained high jhanas, these people can see other realms.. but this is actually relatively very rare and does not constitute most of the cases of being able to see ghosts.

    Please read this article about the Five Eyes, which differentiates normal eye, spirit eye, deva eye, and other enlightened/wisdom eyes: http://www.chan1.org/ddp/channews/10-1987.html
