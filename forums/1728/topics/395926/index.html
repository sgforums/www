    Dear Dharma Brothers & Sisters,


    The Tibetan Buddhist Centre is honoured to have this opportunity to invite His Eminence The 6th Dorshi Rinpoche, Abbot of Tiantang Monastery, a visiting professor, doctoral tutor of regional universities and a well-learned Buddhist scholar to conduct a series of dharma activities for the month of April.     Please kindly take note of the following dharma activities, all dharma friends are welcome.
     
    His Eminence is one of the very few Tibetan Rinpoches within china who is proficient in both the Tibetan and Chinese languages. Dorshi Rinpoche had been taught by many great masters like Lakho Rinpoche and more than 10 other great masters respectively from Duochen Monastery, Shimen Monastery, TianTang Monastery, Laboleng (Labang) Monastery and etc. His Eminence, a great achiever has also been highly praised by 14th His Holiness Dalai Lama, 10th Panchen Lama and Kungtang Lama.


    Dorshi Rinpoche strives to preserve and promote Tibetan culture and Buddhism’s ideas of harmony with his endeavours to assist in the provision of education, medicine, poverty relief and other charitable works in Tibetan-inhabited regions.
