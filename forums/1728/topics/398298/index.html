    Teaching on Practical Methods of Developing Bodhicitta (Continuation) May 2010

    Shangpa Rinpoche will continue teachings on the Practical Methods of Developing Bodhicitta in the month of May every Tuesday and Sunday night 8pm to 10pm wherever possible. Do give us a call prior to coming to our centre for the teachings as the dates may change due to Rinpoche’s traveling schedule. Rinpoche will begin with an overview (revision) of Relative Bodhicitta; Ultimate Bodhicitta; Kye Rim (Creation Stage); and Dzog Rim (Completion Stage); followed by a detailed explanation of the 21 Homages to Green Tara.
