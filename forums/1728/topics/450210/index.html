    Kunsangar South retreat

    August 2nd - 9th, 2012

    “Man-ngag Tag-drol Gyud”

    a precious Terma teaching of Rigdzin Jangchub Dorje

    OPEN WEBCAST -  (Singapore time in red)

    2nd Aug.  (9-10pm)

    After a short introduction about this retreat teaching and giving tridlung of Short Gana Puja, then we do the practice of Short Gana Puja altogether,  because this day is full moon.

    3rd Aug. (3pm - 5pm)

    Giving a Donwang of Man-ngag Tagdrol Gyud,   this is representing the essential application for this teaching.
                               
    4th Aug. (3pm - 5pm)

    Giving the transmission about the Vajra Songs which are representing the States of Samanta Bhadra Yab and Yum.  Giving Tridlung of Short Thun practice.

    5rd Aug. (3pm - 5pm)

    Giving the transmission about the 25 spaces of Samanta Bhadra and giving the tridelung of Medium Thun practice.

    6th Aug. (3pm - 5pm)

    Giving a short instruction for the Way of doing practice the 25 Spaces of Samanta Bhadra,  and then we do this practice altogether.

    7rd Aug. (3pm - 5pm)

    Giving the transmission of Six Spaces of Samanta Bhadra and giving tridlung of Medium Gana Puja.

    8th Aug. (3pm - 5pm)

    Giving the introduction of self perfected “Dampa Roggya” (Zhitro) and giving the tridlung of Longer Thun practice.

    9pm

    We do Gana Puja for the end of this retreat and so on.

    9th Aug. (3pm - 5pm)

    Giving some advices for the daily life practices and tridlungs of collective practices and so on. We also do an Ati Guru Yoga altogether for finishing our retreat.
