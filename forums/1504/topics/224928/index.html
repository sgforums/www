    I've flown with Indonesia AirAsia on the Batam-Jakarta vv sector earlier this year. The cabin crew leader could be a bit better in serving her passengers inflight. My first flight ever on a B737, and wasn't expecting much out of it. More so since the plane was bought 2nd hand and hearing stories about less-than-desired ambience onboard AirAsia's 737s. Overall I rate Indonesia AirAsia 5.5/10.

    Last September, I flew with Jetstar Asia on the Singapore-Bangkok vv sector. Contrary to my anticipation to fly with Indonesia AirAsia, I was anxious and so looking forward to my first flight ever from Changi Airport and on the A320. Friendly and smiling crews, and a comfy cabin greeted me. I rate Jetstar 7.5/10.

    I've yet to try Tiger Airways and Thai AirAsia. But until Thai AirAsia deploys the A320 on the SIN-BKK route, I will stay off course for the time being.
