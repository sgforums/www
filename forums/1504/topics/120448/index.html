    Tip for dealing with LCC (Low cost carrier)

    #1 - make sure the trip is FIRM including travel date. LCC lowest fare typically do NOT allow ANY changes and when they do, it comes with a penalty.

    #2 - In the event that a trip may be changed, it may be a better idea to take the loss then to keep making changes which may incur additional charges for each change.

    #3 - If you feel you like the luxury of making changes, pay the higher fare which gives you more flexibility or don't travel on LCC.

    P.S. Read the fare condition before you book.
