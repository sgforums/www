    Jetstar Asia's reservation system to shut down from Fri till Mon

    Low cost carrier Jetstar Asia's entire reservation system will close from 2pm tomorrow till the morning of Monday, the 9th of February.

    This, while the airline undergoes its biggest reservation system replacement in its four year history.

    During the process, customers will not be able to make any bookings or amendments with the company's telephone reservation centre, and at Jetstar.com.

    But the company says that passengers travelling during the transition will not be affected unless they require changes to be made to their flight bookings during this time.

    It adds that passengers making new bookings or changes to their flights during the transition period should call their local call centers for further assistance.

    The Singapore Call Centre Hotline is 800-61-61-977.
