    Do you know what kind of duties they have as part of their job description? Does it involve working at the airport, or at head offices, etc?
    There is no job description. You are recruited on the basis that you are able to learn and can be posted to ANY position they post you too, including airport, head office, oversea posting (though this is always after X years of services), cargo, sales etc. etc.

    Most staff works at the head office in airline house behind the airport.
