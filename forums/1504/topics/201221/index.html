SQ orders more A380s and ...

  • Superbus
    21 Jul 06, 21:05
    ... the all-new Airbus A350XWBs!

    Singapore Airlines announced today at Farnborough Airshow in UK, their intention to purchase 20 A350XWB-900s, 9 A380-800s and leasing 19 A330-300s.

    This is a follow-up order on the 20 Boeing 787-9 Dreamliners ordered a few weeks ago.

    The airline is expected to receive the first A350XWB-900 in 2012, while the A330s will be an interim solution before the arrival of the A350s. The A330s, leased directly from Airbus, will enter service in 2009.

    With the new order for 9 A380s, SQ has now an outstanding order for 19 A380-800s, with options for 6 aircrafts.
