    Agreed. This is because Cambodia is still a third world country.

    Royal Air Cambodge ( which i dun noe whether it flies any more, i doubt it does , can someone confirm? ) to Phnom Penh and then transfer internla flight to Siem Reap.     There are other ways to go to Angkor Wat , which is near Siem Reap, like going from Vitenam Hanoi. I recommend searching the travel agents in People's Park Complex/centre for the lowest airfare.
