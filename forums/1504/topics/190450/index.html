    Well I tried using the waypoints (same as the real fl pln) and did a pretty slow step climb (all A340s have a pain in a A$$ climb rate) cruising at 0.74 but i ran out of fuel and had to divert to toronto just like everybody else. Its a pity that you can't fly polar routes in FS2004.

    After a check online with fellow simmers, i discovered that the product makers had missed out this fuel bug. So i just edited the aircraft.cfg file and now fuel flow seems more reasonable.

    My graphic card definately didn't do justice to the A345.

    The flight dynamics and controls for this aircraft is excellent, has a heavy feel when taxiing on the ground unlike certain freeware.

    The ILS and autoland feature has been re-edited by CLS and the aircraft now lands more gently, compared to the default autoland, where I would rather take the plane down manually.

    Overall an excellent and value for money payware, of course the top payware would have to be PMDG B747 queen of the skies.
