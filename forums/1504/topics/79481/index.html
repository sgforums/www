    What are your interesting encounters on the flights you've taken so far?

    During my flight to Helsinki on Finnair AY98/QF3195 (MD-11) late last year, I was waiting for my wife to return from the loo when I noticed her path was blocked by a Finn who had decided to get some exercise.

    He was standing on the aisle, facing the passengers, legs apart, doing some half-squat exercise. He was like lowering his body such that he was almost in a sitting position, then raising it, and down and up again. Apparently he wasn't aware that my wife had repeatedly said "Excuse me...". Finally he realised what was happening, said sorry and gave way. She told me after that that while she was out there, she had noticed some passengers giggling.
