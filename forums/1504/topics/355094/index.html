Mobile check in available for all SIA flights

  • QX179R
    7 Apr 09, 21:27

    Checking in for a Singapore Airlines flight has become even more convenient.

    SIA say passengers can do so anywhere in the world using their Singapore-registered mobile phones.

    They can also check in for connecting flights operated by any of the airlines which SIA check-in agreements with, if the connection is from a Singapore Airlines operated flight.

    Through the mobile check-in facility, customers are able to select their seats on SIA operated flights, as they would do on the Internet.

    The facility is made possible by the Airline’s new “SIA Mobile” application software.

    SIA says the software will also be enhanced later this year to include features such as booking of tickets and accessing of selected KrisFlyer services.

    To lead customers through each step of the downloading and check-in process, easy-to-follow instructions have been made available in a specially created link on the Airline’s website.
