  • Dalforce
    15 Apr 05, 01:28
    Originally posted by Boyzz_to_man:
    OBS.. that's interesting... u mean there is further selection during OBS?
    From what I know, no...

    The first interview will be the one where they screen you for your attitude and knowledge (not a lot on that).
    They will select 10 peeps out of maybe 2 days of interviewees for the second round.

    The second one will be a 3 days affair. (No quota here, if lucky all 10 may pass. Or maybe all 10 flunk)
    1st day - Administration and aptitude test
    2nd day - Situational test, panel interview (3 pilots, 2 managers, 1 psychiatrist) and tea party (also a test)
    3rd day - Call HR for result
    4th day - If got selected, proceed for Class 1 medical screening

    After that, another round of waiting game for your medical result and also if you are successfully accepted.

    If so, they will then invited you down to STC for briefing...

    For more information, go to
    http://www.pprune.org/forums/forumdisplay.php?s=b59f03cb69776f1e116cf299659d510b&forumid=45

    Enjoy
