SIA offers visitors US$1 deal for first night stay

  • QX179R
    14 Apr 09, 14:19

    Singapore Airlines is doing its bit to boost visitor arrivals in Singapore.

    Together with the Singapore Tourism Board and Changi Airport, it’ll offer all its customers the Fabulous Singapore Stopover Package for just one US dollar for the first night stay in Singapore.

    The Stopover package covers exclusive deals on hotel accommodation, airport transfers and entry to some of Singapore’s leading tourist attractions.

    Other goodies in the package include a 50 per cent dining discount at designated food outlets at selected hotels

    STB’s Director of Destinationa Marketing, Chew Tiong Heng, said the Package is a key example of how travelers can benefit from the 2009 Reasons to enjoy Singapore campaign, which is taking place till end of the year.
