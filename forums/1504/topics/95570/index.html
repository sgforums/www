    Airbus chief hints at announcement of major new order

    PARIS : Airbus chief executive Noel Forgeard said the European aircraft manufacturer expects to make an announcement regarding a major new order.

    "We've obtained a big part of the market with Jet Blue, Independence Air, Spirit Airlines, Frontier, America West and Virgin America in the United States and easyJet in Europe and another important one which we are thinking of announcing in the future," Forgeard told the French business daily Les Echos.

    Regarding orders, he said the industry should reach the same level as last year, or even perhaps better, in his first comments since executives at rival Boeing disputed Airbus's rosy forecasts.

    Airbus is to deliver this year "more than 305 planes and more than Boeing," said Forgeard.

    "For the coming years the recovery is more than just emerging, it's on our order book," he said.
