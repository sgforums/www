SIA cuts fuel surcharge

  • QX179R
    4 Feb 09, 16:01

    Singapore Airlines will reduce fuel surcharges on short and medium-haul sectors, for tickets issued from today.

    The reduction ranges from around 5 to 21 per cent, with the highest reduction for short-haul sectors in economy class.

    This is the third cut since fuel prices began easing.

    The new surcharges will apply to regional routes as well as between Singapore and countries in Europe, South Africa and North America.

    SIA says it will keep a constant review on jet fuel surcharges as prices remain volatile.
