    Hi guys, here's my report for my recent Taipei trip. I had taken advantage of a Cathay promotion (S$388 for SIN-TPE return via HKG).

    Fri, 1 Oct 2004: CX710 (SIN 0805 - HKG 1200), operated with A330 (Reg: B-HLT)
    Fri, 1 Oct 2004: CX564 (HKG 1310 - TPE 1455), operated with A330 (Reg: B-HLT!!!!!)
    Wed, 6 Oct 2004: CX405 (TPE 1615 - HKG 1800), operated with A330 (Reg: B-HLD)
    Wed, 6 Oct 2004: CX715 (HKG 2000 - SIN 2340), operated with A330 (Reg: B-HLI)

    As you guys can see, the same aircraft was used for both CX710 and CX564. No wonder we passengers bound for that connecting flight were asked to return to the same gate! This is the same time I ever encountered such a coincidence. By the way, CX564 is a daily HKG-TPE-KIX flight. I had Airbus all the way throughout my trip.
