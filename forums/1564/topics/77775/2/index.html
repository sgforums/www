    Certain questions, such as "Does god exist?" are essentially not resolvable.
    (In logical terms, they are non-falsifiable.)

    Hence it is better to focus one's efforts on searching and benefitting from issues relating to true knowledge rather than cracking one's head over something over which one can never know.
