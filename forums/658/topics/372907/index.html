    F1 Rocks Singapore with LG

    F1 ROCKS™ Singapore with LG is bringing some of the biggest names in music to the island city state for the only officially F1 endorsed concerts during the Grand Prix week.

    Thursday 24 SEPTEMBER

    Opening this run of exclusive shows will be a headline performance from multi-million selling Chinese pop legend JACKY CHEUNG, who for the very first time will be performing on the same bill as Taiwanese pop diva, A-MEI. Also appearing will be SODAGREEN and DA MOUTH.

    Friday 25 SEPTEMBER

    The night of legends will see full live sets from Texan rock titans ZZ TOP and Californian ska/pop giants NO DOUBT. Also appearing are platinum selling Scottish stadium rockers SIMPLE MINDS and contemporary urban production wizards N*E*R*D.

    Saturday 26 SEPTEMBER

    Having produced some of the sexiest soul music of the past decade, BEYONCÉ will be playing her first ever solo show in the city. Also taking to the Fort Canning Park stage will be THE BLACK EYED PEAS, whose dominance over the charts has seen the LA four-piece become one of the planet’s biggest selling acts.
