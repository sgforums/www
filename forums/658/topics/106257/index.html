    WKRZ Dancember is back again!! If you are 18 and above and have a passion for dance, then get ready for Dancember!

    Here's a chance to dance and win great prizes worth more than $5000 in the Dancember dance competition. Finals will be held on Saturday December 18th at Zouk.

    Come as a solo dancer or grab 3 friends to join the group category.

    Walk-in auditions take place at the Asian Civilisations Museum, 1 Empress Place Singapore 179555, on Saturday December 11th from 2pm - 5pm. Registration starts from 1pm - 2pm. Dancers to present a one-minute piece of their own choreography (freestyle) and choice of music in CD format.

    A copy of the registration form is available as follows:
    For solo,
    http://www.wkrz913.com/pdf/dancember_solo_form.doc
    For group,
    http://www.wkrz913.com/pdf/dancember_group_form.doc

    For enquiry, email dance@wkrz913.com or call Tasmin at 6353 6100.

    JUDGING CRITERIA is as follows:

    SOLO
    1) Creativity - 30pts
    2) Entertainment value - 30pts
    3) Rhythm - 30pts
    4) Costume - 10pts

    GROUP
    1) Creativity - 25pts
    2) Entertainment value - 20pts
    3) Rhythm - 20pts
    4) Synchronisation - 25pts
    5) Costume - 10pts

    Each contestant (solo or group) can only use 1-min worth of music (can be a montage of songs but must be linked together and not more than 1-min).

    The Dancember dance competition is brought to you by WKRZ, in collaboration with NUS Centre for the Arts and Project M.
