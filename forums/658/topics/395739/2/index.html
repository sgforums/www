    Artist:   John The Whistler
    Album: It's Crazy

    Now, another debut album. This time from Danish project JOHN THE WHISTLER released in ... Asia. My understanding is that there is European release but I am not sure where it can be obtained. This album was sent to me by long time visitor Sandip from Kuala Lumpur in Malaysia. JOHN THE WHISTLER is a project of African born UMBERTO CARLI who also written all the featured songs in this album. From his early teen-years he liked to whistle (after all this is the sound he heard in African jungle). Whistling is a leading motiv of this album produced by him but also by such a prominent EURO statue as AXEL BREITUNG. First single "I'm In Love" spread fast and became very popular (e.g. high position in dance charts in Norway, Denmark, Germany, Israel to name the few). I am sure you heard it already, you probably didn't know who was singing it. A very happy song, with nice rhythm and music line and of course whistling. So catchy, you just want to sing along. So are also other titles from this album such as "Tell Me" just released as a single for instance. Almost entire album is recorded inEURO-POP/DANCE style. Very good dance release from this newcomer to dance scene. The music is also quite original, so you should enjoy it very much so. I will be closely observing how the career of JOHN THE WHISTLER develops.
