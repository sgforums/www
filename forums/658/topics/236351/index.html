    Good Vibrations is an annual Australian music festival that takes place every February. Reputed for bringing the world's freshest and critically acclaimed music acts Down Under, the festival travels to four different Australian cities each year. Each event is staged in botanical gardens, parklands and natural amphitheatres.

    For the first time ever in 2007, music lovers in Singapore will be able to experience the sensations of this music festival as Good Vibrations is introduced to our island. Set to take place on Monday 19 February 2007, the 2nd day of Lunar New Year, expect live acts and DJs from eclectic genres - ranging from rock to electro, dance, hip hop and even world music. With a strong international line-up that includes Beastie Boys, Jurassic 5, Cut Copy, Ursula 1000, Cicada, Mad Mats and home-grown talents such as Electrico and Wicked Aura Batucada, music fans are in for a treat.

    DATE
    19 Feb 2007, 4pm

    VENUE

    DURATION
