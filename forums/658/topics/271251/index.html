    Every guitar will be able to do that. But please don't do it yourself if you're inexperienced as that might cause dead note or buzz if the action is lowered too much.

    The space between the strings and the fretboard is called action.

    So, lowering the action will be able to make you press the strings easier. Good luck on your guitar!
