    K , (born as Kang Yoon-sung) on November 16, 1983 in Goyang) is a South Korean singer. Althogh he made his debut in South Korea in 2004, he is more popular in Japan than in Korea, and has released more material there.

    Discography

    Albums

    1. Beyond the Sea (January 18, 2006)
    2. Music in My Life (December 13, 2006)

    Singles

    1. over... (March 2, 2005) Theme song of drama version's "H2"
    2. Dakishimetai (抱きしめたい, Dakishimetai?) (May 11, 2005)
    3. Girlfriend (July 27, 2005)
    4. Only Human (November 23, 2005) Theme song of television drama "1 Litre of Tears"
    5. The Day (April 19, 2006) Theme song of film "Typhoon"
    6. Brand New Map (August 23, 2006) Ending theme song of anime "Blood+"
    7. First Christmas (ファースト・クリスマス, First Christmas?) (November 1, 2006)

    DVD

    1. film K ~a voice from the heaven~ (February 15, 2006)
