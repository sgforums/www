    Kaiser Chiefs, the multi-million selling English indie rock band from Leeds will perform at Fort Canning Park on April 7th 2009! Kaiser Chiefs come to Singapore after headlining Australia’s V Festival.

    Comprised of vocalist Ricky Wilson, guitarist Andrew White, bassist Simon Rix, keyboardist Nick ‘Peanut’ Baines and drummer Nick Hodgson, Kaiser Chiefs are touring in support of their latest album Off With Their Heads.

    Don’t miss out on the chance to see five individuals, five Kaiser Chiefs rock out Fort Canning Park for the very first time!
