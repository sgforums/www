    I'm old school too, and "Evergreen" is one of my favourites!

    MIO also sang an English version of Evergreen, called Starbright, which was also on the 0083 soundtrack CD (although I don't think it was actually used in the series). Have you heard it? The music and arrangement are identical, but the English lyrics aren't a direct translation of those in the Japanese version.

    However, my all-time favourite anime song is Tenohira no Uchuu ('The Universe in the Palm of Your Hand') which is the ending theme heard only in the final episode of "Key the Metal Idol." It's an incredibly powerful and moving piece sung beautifully by Iwao Junko (who voices the main character Key in the OVA).

    You can download an MP3 of Tenohira no Uchuuj here and listen for yourself.
