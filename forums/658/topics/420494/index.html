Impresario 2011 for all who love music!

  • music-lover2011

    Missed the registration for the Vocal Solo and Vocal Duet/Group categories? FEAR NOT!

    You can still be a part of the 19th installation of Impresario, a Nationwide Talent-Search
    competition organised by Nanyang Technological University (NTU) Cultural Activities Club (CAC)
    since 1992.
    Join us at the Impresario 2011 Walk-in Auditions held in our Official KTV: Party World KTV or
    register for our Dance category and stand a chance to win up to $10,000 worth of prizes!

    Walk-in Auditions (Vocal Categories)
    Date: 22 (Sat) and 23 (Sun) January 2011
    Time: 2pm – 5pm
    Venue: Party World KTV (Orchard)
    International Building (Beside Shaw)
    #03-00

    Save the trouble of bringing your own music by choosing your song from the KTV system, or
    simply do it the A Cappella way! Just bring along your mesmerising voice and your passion
    for singing to IMPRESS the judges from our Official Music School, School of Music and the Arts
    (SOMA).

    Dance
    Registration deadline: 23 January 2011
    Dance Heats: 30 January 2011
    Venue: Danz People @ POMO

    Form up your group of 4 to 8 and groove to any dance styles and IMPRESS our judges from our
    Official Dance School, Danz People.

    Who knows, you could even join the Impresario’s alumnus in taking on bigger stages like Project
    Superstar (Kelly Poon), Singapore Idol (Candice Foo & Maia Lee) and even Taiwan’s highly rated
    One Million Star (Tay Kewei & Huang Jing Lun)!

    What are you waiting for? For more information and registration, visit http://
    www.cacimpresario.com/, follow us on Twitter or like us on our Facebook fanpage!

    Impresario 2011, it’s time to ignite the passion, impress all and inspire others!
