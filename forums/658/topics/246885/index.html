    Taiwan pop star Jay Chou has established his own company JVR Music. He will collaborate with his partner lyricist Vincent Fong and music producer JR Yang. JVR comes from the initial letters of their names.



    JVR Music only has one star: Jay Chou now.

    Located on the top floor in a new office building on Changchun Street in Taipei, the company's office is 850 square meters and costs 130,000 RMB a month in rent.
