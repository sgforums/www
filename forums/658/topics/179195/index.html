    Performing LIVE in concert for the first time in Singapore, Kosheen is set to raise the roof with the sound of singer Sian Evans' melancholic vocals mixed with the smooth electronica of Darren 'Decoder' Beale and Markee 'Substance' Morrison.

    Considered one of the UK's most exciting and experimental bands, their debut release Hide U blazed up the charts in 2000 and was voted Best Single at the 2001 Drum 'n' Bass Awards. Kosheen's subsequent singles Catch and Slip + Slide also earned them widespread mainstream recognition, with likes of Ferry Corsten, Way Out West, Sister Bliss (Faithless) and Tiesto featuring remixes of their singles in various album releases.

    Acclaimed for their fusion of breakbeats, drum 'n' bass and downtempo grooves, Kosheen has propelled into the circuit as a band that possesses a keen interest to push genre boundaries with their inimitable individuality and hypnotic collection of songs from the soul.
