    Scoot: K-Pop Star Hunt 3 Singapore Auditions
    Date: 15 September 2013
    Time: 10am - 5pm
    Venue: Bugis+ (201 Victoria Street Singapore 188067)

     

    Scoot K-Pop Star Hunt is making its triumphant return with Season 3!             

    Organized by channel M, FOX International Channels’ leading Korean entertainment channel, with Singapore’s fun and friendly low-cost airline, Scoot, coming on board as the title sponsor, Scoot: K-Pop Star Hunt 3 will create the new audition fever with full training support from FNC Entertainment, one of the top-tier music labels in Korea!

     

    Aspiring hopefuls with outstanding singing and dancing abilities from five countries, including Singapore, Taiwan, Hong Kong, Malaysia and Thailand, will have the once-in-a-lifetime chance to be in the spotlight of Asia, realize their K-pop dreams and vie for the coveted title as winner of Scoot: K-Pop Star Hunt 3!

     

    To get started on your idol dream, simply upload your best K-pop song-and-dance routines to the official website at www.kpopstarhunt.com as video submissions open on August 1st. K-pop fans between 15 and 29 years of age in the aforementioned countries will have more than 6 weeks (submission deadline varies in different countries) to upload their video submissions. Don’t forget to check the website regularly for the latest updates and exciting fresh entries, as well as guidelines and rules to enter the competition!

     

    Scoot: K-pop Star Hunt 3 will also be conducting a public audition in Singapore on 15 September 2013 at Bugis+

     

    For more information, pleae visit: www.kpopstarhunt.com
