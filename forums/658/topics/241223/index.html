    I puchased a copy of their album after hearing their song being played in a record store during my cd trawling. I heard one of their songs and i found it really catchy. I thought, well, i would just get their cd and try them out, at least there is 1 song i would really enjoy. (I'm a music freak, 1 song is enough for me to warrant a purchase, as dumb as it sounds.)

    So, i went ahead and paid for the cd. Popped the cd in, but frankly, i wasn't expecting much. I thought, oh well, let's hear the first track and if anything bores me, straight to the nice track. Heard their first track, well, nothing really much but a nice sounding "Intro".

    Trust me, every song right here is catchy, starting from track 2! So, if there is a chinese music cd you have to get, this takes the cake!

    Go out now, purchase this album. Your ears will thank you for it.
