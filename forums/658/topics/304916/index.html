    Due to Angela Chang’s ill health, her concert Angela’s Live @ Singapore scheduled to take place on March 29, 2008 at the Singapore Expo has been postponed to May 2008. Her work schedule has been put on hold to allow her time to rest and recuperate and exact details on the postponed concert will be released shortly.

    Concert ticket sales remain unaffected and will continue at SISTIC. As there are no changes to the seating arrangements for the new concert date, ticket holders will be admitted to the concert using the same ticket stubs, and there is no need for exchange of tickets.

    Patrons who prefer a refund can, from Mar 15, 2008 to Mar 29, go to the only SISTIC refund centre at The Singapore Indoor Stadium SISTIC Box Office with their original tickets to obtain a refund. The refund will be made through the original mode of payment. Refund hours are from Monday to Saturday, from 12 noon to 8pm, and on Sundays and Public Holidays, from 1pm to 7pm. For more information, please visit www.sistic.com.sg or call the SISTIC hotline at 6348 5555.
