    LAMC Productions is proud to announce that UK duo The Ting Tings will perform their first show of 2009 in Singapore on January 13th before headlining Big Day Out 2009 in Australia.

    The Ting Tings were voted #3 on the BBC's "Top 10 To Watch In 2008," the much-watched list of emerging acts in the annual BBC 6 Music poll Sound Of 2008, which observed: "The pair have now…become one of the most credible and critically-acclaimed groups on the indie scene."

    “One of this year’s hottest new bands” - The Sun

    “The best new band in Britain” - Sunday Times Style 

    “100% pop dynamite. 5 stars” - Heat 

    “Sure to be one of 2008’s must hears. 4 stars” - Glamour

    “Britain’s hottest new band” - News of the World

    “The official sound of the summer” - Guardian Guide 

    The Ting Tings will perform hits from their critically acclaimed debut album “We Started Nothing”.

    Early bird tickets for The Ting Tings are $85 from now till December 15th and $95 from December 16th – January 13th. Tickets are $105 at the door.

    For fans wanting to catch both The Ting Tings & The Prodigy, The Double Shot Package is available for only $150 till Dec 15, $160 from Dec 16 – Jan 12th 2009 and $170 on Jan 13.

    Tickets go on sale at all Sistic outlets on Monday 1st December 2008. Call the Sistic hotline at (65) 6348-5555 or book online at www.sistic.com.sg
