    The Ghana international, currently in China on the club's pre-season tour, is therefore contracted to the club until 2013. Essien's commitment follows yesterday's confirmation that goalkeeper Petr Cech had signed a new five-year deal.

'I have always admired him and it is good news for me and for Chelsea that he signs for so long.'

    Left-back Wayne Bridge also signed a new deal earlier this week.
