    Piglet Quest (lol)
    Reward: A heart element piglet thing joins you
    Recommended lvl: Any
    When you start out and head for the first town, the south star lady should give you a ticket. Warp to the main town and go south on that map to find soul dealer. Give her the ticket and he's yours. This might be a limited time thing tho, i think its an event.
    Has the skills: Shout (damages enemy in a line), Gaurd (raises defense of all allies), and Protect Master (takes damage instead of your character)

    Divorce quest
    Reward: Water element lady joins you
    Recommended level: 3+
    From the map u start out on in the main city, goto the house on the top left, she has blue on. Talk to her. Then go 2 maps right and find a house with an evil waiter walking infront of it. Go inside talk to the guy and fight him and his gaurds. Go back to the girl. She now joins you.
    Hs skills: Ice Attack (does ice damage to target)

    Excavation Quest
    Reward: Excavation skill
    Recommended level: Any
    From the map you start out on, go up into the in and talk to the old man walking around. He wants 15g for lunch. Pay him and learn the skill.

    Alchemy Quest
    Reward: Alchemy Skill
    Required level: 15+
    Havent completed this, but on the map u start out on, go in the house on the right. The man says come back @ lvl 15 for Alchemy skill.

    Scan Quest
    Reward: Scan Skill + Yellow Turban Headgear + Yellow Turban Fame(?)
    Recommended level: Any
    From the map you start out on, go right one map and go into the 2nd house. Take the mans quiz, answers are: bottom, bottom, middle. You then get the Scan skill, and if gotten all the questions right on first try, yellow turban.

    Lost Horse Quest
    Reward: +HP potion + Adventurer Fame(?)
    Recommended lvl: Any
    From the map you start out on, go 2 maps right, and go up to where you see an old lady. Talk to her and she says she's missing her horse. Goto bottom right corner of the map and talk to the horse named White. It joins you, now take it back to her.

    Delivery Boy Quest
    Reward: 5g + Adventurer Fame(?)
    Recommended lvl: Any
    From the map you start out on, go 2 maps right, and go up to where you see an old man behind a booth. He wants you to deliver something to the inn keeper at the inn on the same map. Take it to him and he gives you an item to take back to the old man. Take it back and get your reward.

    Training Quest
    Reward: Ability to level up "Training" under Job menu.
    Required Lvl: 10+
    Leave the first town to the world map, head east till u see a zone with a house on top of a mountain. Go 1 map up from there till you see an old man. He tells you to use the sit action under emotions to level your training skill, if your lvl 10 or over. Must be sitting on this map to level it. The higher the level, the more rares you find.

    Summoning Quest
    Reward: Summoning pendant of your element(?) + Summoning Skill
    Required lvl: 30+
    Dont know where or how to get it but i read it on the official site. Yes, you get to summon icon_smile.gif.Earth gets a golem, which lowers the enemys Dex and Agi. Water gets Shiva, which cures status effects and heals party. Fire gets pheonix, which deals damage. Wind gets Wood Dragon which limits the enemys movements/turns and the ability to train their SP to recover your HP.

    Bounty Quest - Quest is repeatable

(i forgot the npc's name) wants you to go out and capture a Robber and a Mt. Robber and bring them to him. Goto the worl map, go north up into the trees along the mountains, run around there and you'll end up fighting Robbers, Mt.Robbers, and Mt.King. Capture Robber and Mt.Robber and bring them to him

    Required lvl : 2 and above

    've discovered a couple of quests that you can only do if you're lvl 15+.

    They're in the village north of the town you start on.

    Liu Bei's Mother:
    Required lvl: 15
    Recommended lvl: ? (Very High)

    Talk to the old lady in the house behind the vendor. She'll ask you to find her son (Liu Bei). If you've completed the other Liu Bei quest, then you should know where he is (Most Eastern map, upper left in the Flower Garden). Be warned, he is a level 110 NPC. I don't know the exact level, or level range, but you'll have to be at a higher level than 15 to beat him. Much higher.

    Silkweaver Quest
    Required lvl: 15
    Recommended level: ? (High)

    Do you know that girl, near the pier? The one you can't talk to? Well, when you become level 15 and approach her, around 8 other guys pop up, and after you talk to her, you'll be pulled into a battle with guys ranging from level 52-53. Once you beat them, I assume you'll recieve some sort of reward.
