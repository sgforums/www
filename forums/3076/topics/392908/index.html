    Six double-decker buses caught fire at Ang Mo Kio Bus Depot at about 3.30 am today (Mar 7).

    The SCDF dispatched its resources within 3 minutes of being informed.

    Said the SCDF:

    "At 0333hrs, SCDF received a call alerting us of buses on fire at Ang Mo Kio, Bus Depot, Ang Mo Kio St 63.

    "Upon receiving the notification, 2 Fire Engines, 1 Red Rhino and 1 supporting vehicle, with approximately 20 personnel resources, were dispatched.

    "SCDF arrived at the location within 3 minutes. Fire was brought under control within 30 minutes of the arrival of SCDF.

    "Fire was confined to the six double decked buses in the open area. The cause of the fire is being investigated.

    "There were no casualties in this incident.
