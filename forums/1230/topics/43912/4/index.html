    Diarina,

    Nothing much is happening in my life right now... Just wanted to update you... Try s'thing different... If you remembered, I told you before, I had always been lonely... Thinking now to try describe it to you, coz I've always treated loneliness as a place, an event, a sound, more than a feeling. Guess that is what happen when you get so used to the feeling that you forget it is even there... It starts to manifest itself to you in different ways...

    You often hear that it is cold and black or dark... To me... It is greys and browns. Colors of autumn. True loneliness is scary, is emotionless. It is when you hold a blade so that it relects it's frosty glint back into your eyes. When that is no longer enough, you press it to your skin. It hurts, but you welcome the pain... For even though it is torture, at least you felt something... Then you see the blood, vivid and flowing... So beautiful, life-giving... Then you feel the sting, you know you exist, that you are real... That is why you keep cutting yourself... The only time when you are not alone, when youdo not feel alone. If you don't cut too deep, the blood stops fast and the scars heals quickly. Then it becomes an addiction.

    Imagine autumn, when you are alone. You know you are alone, fenced in, in another part of your world. There is no desire, no hope, no emotions. Just you, just walking on this abandoned highway. Trees weeping, their sun-kissed leaves having lost the former glory just drips down from the seemingly shrunken branches. You walk and walk and walk, still nothing's changed. So sure that you can never break out of this world, so you stood still and give up trying... One second you are surrounded by your form teacher pressing you for assignment, the next second, you screamed at her to leave you alone and find yourself in that world again, on that unused highway- just walking... and you don't care anymore...

    There is always winter evenings. Cold- you can feel the cold, even in sunny singapore. One moment talking to your friend, the next in a snow-clad winter wonderland, where you are alone... Always alone. You do not even mind being alone, do not mind the cold. In fact you welcome it, so that you can feel something. So used to being lonely it does not matter anymore, so tired ot the numbling drugery of reality that you even begin to crave for your world.

    Loneliness is like living on top of a jazz bar. You hear the music everynight. Hear the musicians practising even before the bar open for business. Lives with the neon light, cramped quarters, and all that smoke. Jazz is the music for those lonely in their soul, for it is the only music that can reach deep down inside, the only music that actually penetrates the silence of loneliness, that reverberates through the very core of your being...

    It is the rain that keeps you feeling, the true sense of being. It is the moonlight, frosty brillance, cold, untoucheable yet always there, in the darkness of the night. It is in the fallen petal of a once glorious rose, it is in a cup of steaming coffee poorly made. When you enter a greasy eatery and find yourself alone. When you see a family or enter a happy home. Simply when you see a butterfly that ceased to flutter its wings and fall- such simple moments, yet you never felt more alone...

    How do you describe your loneliness, diarina?

    Alone'
