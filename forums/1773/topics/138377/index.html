    Taoist seal and crossing the bridge ceremonies

    In Taoist temples, they have seals for the respective deities. For example, Guan Yin temple will have a guan yin seal, cheng huang temple(temple of the City deity) will have a cheng huang seal.

    Seals are symbol of power and authority.

    Taoist priests, when they write petitions, talismans etc, will have their seals chopped on it. Different sects the priests belongs to, will have different seals.

    The deities' seals represent the power and blessings from the deity. Chopping onto the shirt symbolizes the divine bestowing blessings on the person. It is out of goodwill, and nother sinister at all.

    Just like every Emperor, magistrates, officials, they have seals. Just like every companies, govt bodies, they have seals. It is a symbol of authority and authentication, trust and power.

    It wil be less superstitous and logical if we think like this.

    'bridge crossing' ceremonies, are when temples are having celebrations, they wil engage taoist priests or medium(in tranced) to lead devotees and the public in crossing this wooden bridge. It is a ritual to avert disasters and bring blessings. Symbolizing the blessings from divines will help us cross over every obstacles in life. It certainly boast the morale of the devotees and give them comfort in life.
