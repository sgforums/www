    Ubi Ave 2( off upper paya lebar rd) Lor Koo Chye Cheng Huang(city deity) temple honour Lord Tai Yang deity too. You may visit and pray there. He is been honoured together with 60 tai sui and Goddess Dou Mu, Goddess Tai Ying.

    You can take mrt to paya lebar stn and take bus 24, 70, 76.

    Famous Tian Hock Keng temple houses Lord Tai Yang too. He is been honoured together with Guan Yin, Goddess Tai Ying in the rear hall.
    But do take note, their explaination note on Him is wrong. He is not a Buddhist divine.

    Bao Chi Temple behind tong ji medicial hall(old) near Chinatown behind ministry of manpower also got Tai Yang deity. It is a temple that worship Kan Zhang sheng Wang(Lord Kai Zhang).
