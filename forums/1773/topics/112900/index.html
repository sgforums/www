    Taoism, similar to other faith, has its own set of rites and rituals for believers, devotees to go through, if they wish to regard Taoism as their lifelong religion.

    Similar to the concept of 'gui yi' in Buddhism and baptism in Christianity.

    Initiated Taoists seek refuge in the highest divine in Taoism, the San Qing(three pure ones) - Yuanshi tianzun (Heavenly lord of primival), Lingbao tianzun(heavenly lord of numinous treasures) and Daode tianzun aka tai shang lao jun(Heavenly lord of dao and virtue).

    They are the divine form of Tao, Taoism's main belief and they are regards as the Grandmaster of Taoism, creator of the cosmic and the three treasures in Taoism - the tao, the teachings and the spiritual masters.

    5 precepts will also be uphold, to improve our values and to a better person.

    Taoist mission(Singapore) is conducting this ritual on the 14th of the 2nd lunar month, eve of Taoist day. Around in march.
    interested can contact Taoist mission (Singapore).
