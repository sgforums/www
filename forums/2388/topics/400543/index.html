    Mudhalvan (1999) is a Tamil film directed by Shankar, later re-made in Hindi as Nayak: The Real Hero. The film stars Arjun, Manisha Koirala and Sushmita Sen.This story was inspired by the Frost/Nixon Interviews that were broadcasted in the U.S. in 1977. The film's score and soundtrack are composed by A. R. Rahman. A reporter critically interviews a minister on a talk show one fine morning and gets challenged to accept the role for one day, he accepts it and soon finds himself embroiled in political intrigue. This film was a tremendous success at the box office and continued Shankar's dream run. It won the Filmfare Best Tamil Film Award in the year of its release.. The hit song Shakalakka Baby by A.R.Rahman went on to gain international acclaim appearing in Andrew Loyd Webber's Bombay Dreams and Kelly Poon's Soundtracks. Former Miss Universe Sushmita Sen also makes a cameo appearance for the song.
