    Originally posted by MohamedF:

    Erm no... apparently you don't understand the concept behind Rare Candy...

    Each Pokemon has 510 Effort Values Points to be gained in the game.

    Each 4 Effort Value Points gives you + 1 in 1 of the 6 stats, HP/ATTACK/DEFENSE/SPEED/SP. ATTACK/SP. DEFENSE

    In order to get Effort Value Points, you battle other Pokemon. Example, Pidgey gives you 1 point in Speed, which means if you battle 4 Pidgeys, your Speed will + 1.

    In the case of RARE Candy, you do not gain any EVs, so your Pokemon is significantly weaker.
