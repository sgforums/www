    Review for Gangs Of New York

    Definitely not for the faint-hearted!! The opening gang-fight-scene was enough to ruin your appetite even for the best nasi lemak in town.

    Yes, ladies and gentleboys, its another one of Martin Scorsese's almost-classics. (will be a full-blown-without-a-doubt-classic only if Cameron Diaz bared her assets. Yes, boys. a moment of silence.)

    we have a rojak of emotional + physical roller coaster ride.
    vengeance + betrayal + loyalty + politics + integrity + racism + rampant sex + rampant sex + rampant.. oops.. + gang fights + and oh yes, a little something for the ladies too---> the oh-so-vulgar 4-letter word L-O-V-E.

    One of the rare movies where the bad guy up-stages the suspiciously-feminine hero. Daniel Day Lewis' character will give Dr Hannibal Lecter a run for his Turkish Lira.

    The racial and social divide were portrayed graphically towards the end.

    Tonnes of message was what the creators attempted to cramp into a 3-hour show. Surprisingly, tho, it was 3 hours more tolerable than the equally-lenghty Lord Of The Rings 2.

    One gripe. Prepared to be disappointed if u expect a grand finale between the good and the bad. No Crouching Tiger Hidden Dragon-like fighting scenes. But this is Hollywood!

    Personal Rating: 8/10

    Review for 8 Mile

    Movies with singers/rappers acting as main characters have never really appealed to me. (unless there're tonnes of nudity and wild beastly sex involved. but that's not the point...)

    Never really a big fan of i-hate-the-world-and-i-hate-the-way-the-world-hates-me Eminem. Have always associated that certain white rapper with skate-boarding little kids wearing over-sized clothings and baseball caps.

    As for the movie, the plot was typical. Bollywood-style. Poor youth from dodgy neighbourhood going against all odds. In this case, racial discrimination as well as family dysfunction. (check out his mom, though!!)
    Poor WHITE youth gets bullied by Afro-Americans (to be politically correct) and finally gets sweet revenge via some cool ego-boosting, reputation-stamping, foul-mouthed, mama-vandalising moves during the finale.

    My favourite part of the entire otherwise-draggy movie was the Battle scene. Never has rhymin n rappin sounded so damn shiok! perhaps we can organize one of these battles among ourselves. In cantonese/hokkien/malay. Rasa sayang, eh rasa sayang......

    Ok lah... not being able to provide much nonsense this time. been rusty especially after such a long hiatus.

    Review for Daredevil

    Another comic superhero gets a Hollywood make-over.

    J-Lo's what's-his-name-other-half plays a blind lawyer by day and a (surprise surprise) justice vigilante with bat-like supersonic-detecting capabilities by night. If u ask me, its almost like Batman, only that he's blind. And wears a different-coloured body tights. With the same cool how-on-earth-did-he-get-or-made-those-when-he's-blind ghizmos/gadgets.

    Anyway, the movie was not exactly your typical slash'em whoop-their-asses superhero serving. But then again, so was Spiderman, Batman, Superman, etc.... thank god this superhero's name doesnt end with Man. imagine watching Dareman. eeeuurrghh......

    The show was pretty generous on action/fighting scenes. A word of caution, tho. Those scenes can get pretty Blair-Witch-Project-like at times. I had to cling tightly onto the popcorn box and close my eyes and start counting sheeps to prevent sharing with the rest of the audience what i had for last night's dinner.

    This is one of 'em shows u dont really need to sacrifice much brain cells for. Watch it for the sheer entertainment value of it. For those who desire arty-farty intellectual type of movies, go watch Barney And Friends instead.

    Rating : 7/10
