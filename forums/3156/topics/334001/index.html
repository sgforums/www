    The MOE Approved Calculator Casio Fx 95 MS has the ability to solve simultaneous equations, cubic equation and quadratic equation. This will help the students to check their answers.

    Quadratic Equation

    Solve the equation

    2x^2 +1x - 12 = 0

    Steps Using MOE Approved Calculator Casio Fx 95 MS to solve for x

    Press ON

    Press Mode twice

    Press 1 for Eqn

    Press right arrow once

    Press 2 for degree 2 ie quadratic equation ax^2 + bx + c = 0

    Press 2 for a

    Press 1 for b

    Press -12 for c

    Press =

    x1 = 2.21,

    Press =

    x2 = - 2.71

    Answer

    x = 2.21, - 2.71

    Simultaneous Equations

    Solve the simultaneous equations

    2x - 3y = 15

    3x - 7y = 27 1/2

    Steps Using MOE Approved Calculator Casio Fx 95 MS to solve for x and y

    Press ON

    Press Mode twice

    Press 1 for Eqn

    Press 2 for 2 unknowns ie simultaneous equations

                                           a1 x + b1 y = c1

                                           a2 x + b2 y = c2

    Press 2 for a1

    Press - 3 for b1

    Press 15 for c1

    Press 3 for a2

    Press - 7 for b2

    Press 27 1/2 for c2

    Press =

    x = 4.5,

    Press =

    y = - 2

    Answer

    x = 4.5 , y = - 2
