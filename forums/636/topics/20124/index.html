    SunSpot 2001 - A Scouts and Guides Carnival -

    Date: 30 June 2001
    Day: Sat
    Time: 10am to 10pm
    Venue: Sentosa, Tanjong Beach
    Tickets: $20 (inclusive of entry and goodie Bag)

    uPCOMING Next Saturday, Dunearn Unicorn Scouts' Group are organising a big scale carnival for scouts, guides, ex scouts, ex guides and interested public.

    The Objective of the carnival are;

    1) Bring as many scouts and guides to one single event (noone had try this before)
    2) Reunion the ex scouts and guides to the carnival
    3) celebrate Dunearn Secondary 40th Annivesary
    4) Promote oudoor sports and games
    5) Raise some money for children society (We really hope we can do that!)

    HighLights of the day;

    1) Beach Soccer Competition
    2) Beach Captain's Ball Competition
    3) FunStalls
    4) Rockwall
    5) Talent Time - Performances
    6) Campfire Party - 6pm to 10pm

    Tickets are still selling now. You might be thinking that maybe $20 is a bit expensive. Try thinking of the budget a small scouts' group like ours doing something like this and to top it all donating excess to the charity.

    We need your support to make it happen; if you are current scout, guides, ventures, rovers, scouter, guider or ex scouts or guides or you are interested to look see the carnival.

    Tickets available at 90012641- Central pick up location

    Look forward to see you there.
