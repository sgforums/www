My Review of Anarchy-Online

  • tango

    www.anarchy-online.com

    For those who had played MMORPGs, anarchy online is the first and only online mmorpg to be set in the future world.

    PvP is allowed at certain areas.
    3 main factions(not clans), Omni-Tek, Neutral and Rebels.

    The gameplay can be rather frustrating if you do not know how to play initially. Learning curve on basics is about an hour without reading manual since they provide me with no manual book on beta.

    Apartments aka houses are provided for youto store your equipments unlike Asheron's Call.
    Lag issue is only problematic during peak hours. Graphic is the best of mmorpgs that I have played.

    Out of 100 points for MMORPG, I give AO 90 points.

    AO is subscription based like all MMORPG that have gone gold. Price is USD 12.95 monthly.

    The game will not be available to Asia on 27th June, it will only be available to Scandivinia, and America. If you wish to buy the game, only way is to buy through online-gaming networks and ship to Singapore.

    If anyone wish to buy together, it would be best to buy together and the shipping cost will not be so costly as all the game will come together.
