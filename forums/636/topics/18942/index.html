    Next, I do not really like the way MC is run. Things are a little bit erm......too serious. I would prefer to be in a 'fun' clan than a clan which takes things pretty seriously.     Lastly, I feel that I cannot contribute much to MC. The only thing which I'm good enough to contribute would be graphical and HTML service, but its awkward for me to join a clan just to contribute in that sector

    Well, I would not be leaving the CS community, so I would still be seeing you guys around. I wouldn't want to remain clanless either, probably I would join another clan, in which I can make a difference in, or maybe even create one (I'm less lightly to do the latter). Well, I guess thats it....so cya around guys.
