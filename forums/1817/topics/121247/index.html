    Pangu aka known as the Lord of the Primival Origin (Yuan shi tianzun/yuan shi tian wang) is often considered the 'One'. He is the divinity of the Tao, He reprsent the ultimate Wuji, nothingless. From Him, came the 'taiji' period, from His vital breath came anoter divinity of Tao - Lord of the Numinous Treasure(Lingbao Tian zun). He holds a Jade ornament or a taiji symbol, He represent the two major forces in the cosmic -Yin and Yang.

    Finally, the Three came, from the Two divinity, they split into Three - come another and final divinity of the Great Tao - Lord of Tao and Virtue aka Supreme Lord Lao Tze (Tai shang laojun/Daode Tian Zun). From him, all beings rises and the world is set. He is also the avatar of Tao.

    So the diagram goes like these -

    Tao(Tai shang laojun) -> One(Yuan Shi Tian zun) -> Two(Yuan Shi Tian zun, Lingbao Tian zun) Three ->(Yuan Shi tian zun, Lingbao Tian Zun and Tai Shang Lao Jun(yes, he again))
