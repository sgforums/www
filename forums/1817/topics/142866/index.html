    What is Blasphemy?

    Most countries have blasphemy laws. England has an old, mostly disused blasphemy law which is rarely evoked. Pakistan (1986) and Sa'udi Arabia are examples of countries with very strict blasphemy laws. I am not sure about the consistency of blasphemy law in the USA, although one example can be found here.

    Blasphemy is defined differently in different areas. In Pakistan the law is used to prevent any criticism of Muhammed, including literary and historical criticism.
