    30 Oct 03, 07:32
    Hello everyone, will like to recommend all of you to buy Final Fantasy Tactics Advance for GameBoy Advance. Basically this game is sort of a mini-version of the pioneer Final Fantasy Tactics that was on Playstation.

    Features of the game includes:
    1) A huge variety of classes and jobs including white mage, black mage, summoner, warrior, ninja, thief and many more. The list is almost endless.

    2) A new addition to the original FF tactics is the Law rule. In every battle, there is an 'umpire' who monitors the match. If u are not careful, u can get 'yellow' or 'red' cards for breaking a rule during battle. For eg. if the rule stated 'no magic' and u casted one, then u will get a yellow card.

    3) Items are plentiful. In fact, u will find the shops stocking new weapons, armours and etc after u complete a few missions every now and then.

    4) Great graphics for a GameBoy Advance game. While the graphics are nothing to shout about compared to the PS2 or even PS1, the colours are brilliant to say the least and the characters are well drawn (although in an anime manner). The graphics will not appeal to everyone though, esp those accustomed to playing 3D games.

    Overall, FF Tactics Advance is one of the best RPG for the Gameboy Advance, noting close contention from Golden Sun and Advance Wars. Lastly, if anyone has FF Tactics Advance, maybe we can link up and exchange some stuff (characters, weapons or items).
