    Gaming tournaments in the month of May

     

    For all you gamers out there, 2 tournaments during the month of May to quench your gaming thirst.

     

    1) Compaq AMD Professional League 2008/2009 season

                CAPL is into its 3rd consecutive season. This season it will be held from 30th May to 1st June. It is the perfect DOTA tournament where amateur teams can join the Open Category and professional teams fight it out in the Elite Category.

     

    For more information, log onto the official website at:

    http://capl.com.sg/

    Registration details are announced both at the official website and also at:

    http://www.esportsc.com/

     

    2) WCG 2008 Campus Gaming Festival (Secondary and ITE only)

                This 2nd installment of WCG Campus will be held at Republic Poly from 15th to 17th May. Secondary schools and ITE Institutes will battle it out to represent their schools for Esports glory. Games that will be played includes the popular DOTA and Guitar Hero 3 (Xbox).

     

    For more information, log onto the official website at:

    http://sg.worldcybergames.com/index.php?option=com_content&task=view&id=38

    Registration details are announced both at the official website and also at:

    http://www.esportsc.com/

     

    Slots are running out so book your tickets fast!
