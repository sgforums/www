               -----------------------------
               A) What are Treasure Troves ?
               -----------------------------
              
               Majority of the stages have hidden areas and will only reveal
               themselves when you have captured the area beside them. These
               hidden areas are called Treasure Troves in the game. When you
               defeat the general in the Treasure Trove, you will unlocked that
               general as your officer. You can bring them to battle once you've
               unlocked them.
              
              
              
              
               ---------------------
               B) How this FAQ works
               ---------------------

               1) Map name 
              
                  Location: Where is the treasure trove located on the map.
              
                  How to reveal it: Tells you which square to capture to
                                      reveal the Treasure Trove.

               -------------
               D) SHU Forces
               -------------
              
                  a) Invasion of Yi
              
                     Location: West of Liu Zhang's main camp
              
                     How to reveal it: Capture the square above the
                                       NorthWestern Fortress.
              
                     Officer Unlocked: Ieyasu
                      
              
                  b) Showndown at Chi Bi
              
                     Location: Southwest of Wu's main camp
               
                     How to reveal it: Capture the square that is at 
                                       the right of the 2 ships that 
                                       are side by side.
              
                     Officer Unlocked: Qiong Ying
              
              
                  c) Siege of Chang An
              
                     Location: South of Wei's main camp
              
                     How to reveal it: Capture the square below of the
                                       Western Defense Base.
              
                     Officer Unlocked: Lin Chong
              
              
                  d) Escape from Ru Nan
               
                     Location: Southwest of Wei's main camp
              
                     How to reveal it: Capture the square below of
                                       Wei's main camp.
              
                     Officer Unlocked: Kenshin
              
              
                  e) Battle of Yi Ling
              
                     Location: Northwest of map
              
                     How to reveal it: Capture the square above the
                                       NorthWestern Defense Base.
              
                     Officer Unlocked: Hua Rong
              
              
                  f) The Yellow Turban Rebellion
              
                     Location: Northwest of map
              
                     How to reveal it: Capture the square that is 3 squares
                                       to the left of the Northern Attack Base.
              
                     Officer Unlocked: Xiang Yu
              
              
                  g) Battle at Hu Lao Gate
              
                     Location: Southeast of map
              
                     How to reveal it: Capture the *Southeast Defense Base
                       
                     *Note: Game displays SW Defense Base but it's actually
                            in the Southeast direction.
              
                     Officer Unlocked: H.S.Niang
              
              
                  h) Battle at Si Shui Gate
              
                     Location: Northwest of map
              
                     How to reveal it: Capture the square above the Western
                                       Fortress.
              
                     Officer Unlocked: Huyuan Zhou
              
              
                  i) Sixth Northern Campaign
              
                     Location: Northwest of map
              
                     How to reveal it: Capture the Northwest Defense Base.
              
                     Officer Unlocked: Tadakatsu
              
                     Location: Northeast of map
              
                     How to reveal it: Capture the square that is on the
                                       right of the Northern Fortress.
              
                     Officer Unlocked: Nene
              
              
                  j) Nan Zhong Campaign
              
                     Location: North of map
              
                     How to reveal it: Capture the square that is on the
                                       left side of the Northern Fortress.
              
                     Officer Unlocked: Keiji
              
              
                  k) Battle at Mt. Ding Jun
              
                     Location: Northeast of map
              
                     How to reveal it: Capture the Northeastern Fortress.
              
                     Officer Unlocked: Okuni
              
              
                  l) Battle of Wu Zhang Plains
              
                     Location: Southeast of map
              
                     How to reveal it: Capture the square below of the Eastern 
                                       Attack Base.
              
                     Officer Unlocked: Guan Sheng
              
                     Location: Above the Central Attack Base
              
                     How to reveal it: Capture the square above the Central Depot.
              
                     Officer Unlocked: J.Taigong
              
              
                  m) Campaign in Xu 
              
                     Location: North of map
              
                     How to reveal it: Capture the square that is 2 squares left
                                       of the Northwestern Attack Base. 
              
                     Officer Unlocked: Liu Bang
              
              
                   n) Battle of Chang Ban
              
                     Location: North of map.
              
                     How to reveal it: Capture the Northern Fortress
              
                     Officer Unlocked: Nobunaga
