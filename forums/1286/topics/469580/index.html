    Hi any of you guys familiar with Chaos Heroes Online? Was reading up some articles on this game and found that Sega had picked it up over in Japan in Feb 2013.

    Chaos Online is developed by Neoact, who originally did the DotA Chaos map back in the old WC3 Dota days. And was very popular in Korea during those days as DotA AllStars did not support Korean text back then.Â 

    So far the game has been launched in Korea, China, Japan and Indonesia. On top of that, there seems to be an English service opening up soon for it.

    The game's character designs is very much similar to that of DOTA 1 & 2 (some may call them straight off ripoffs), but after trying the China CBT last time realise its actually very much different in terms of skill combination as well as a governing system behind the MOBA gameplay section.

    In addition, there's a crafting system in games that allows players to craft and upgrade special items in game. Which are obtainable as players level through the game doing both daily or even one time off quest. These items will then appear inside a player's game shop and useable to them just as any other in-game items are at a cost of gold earned through killing creeps, tower and other heroes.

    Map seems to be bigger then both Dota and LoL Map, there are portals gateways located in 2 areas of the map which allows players to travel quickly from one end of the map to another but is only use it twice during a game.

    Unlike Dota or LoL where by there is always an ultimate PvE type guardian in the middle of the map e.g. Roshan. Chaos replaced it with a special roaming Guardian which patrols the base of both teams (a Phoenix or a Dragon) depending on which team you're on.
