    The Warrior Profession

    Warrior Attributes:

    Strength: Every rank gives an additional 1% armor penetration on attack skills and increases the effectiveness of Strength-based skills.

    Swordsmanship: Increases damage and effectiveness of sword strikes and sword-based skills.

    Axe Mastery: Increases damage and effectiveness of axe strikes and axe-based skills.

    Hammer Mastery: Increases damage and effectiveness of hammer strikes and hammer-based skills.

    Tactics: Increases the duration and effectiveness of tactics based skills, which include many shouts and stances.

    The Ranger Profession

    Ranger Attributes:

    Expertise: For each rank of Expertise, the Energy cost of all of your attacks, Rituals, touch skills, and Rangers skills are decreased by 4%. Several skills, especially those related to Energy costs and skill recharge times, become more effective with higher Expertise.

    Marksmanship: Increases the effectiveness of bow-related attacks and skills

    Wilderness Survival: Affects the power of all linked skills, such as preparations, traps and rituals.

    Beast Mastery: Improves all skills linked to capturing and containing your pets, as well as a few non-pet-related boosts to help in combat. This attribute also increases the damage your pet does in combat when it makes a normal attack.

    The Monk Profession

    Monk Attributes:

    Divine Favour: For each attribute level in Divine Favor, an additional 3.2 health (rounded) is added to an ally each time the Monk casts a Monk spell upon that ally.

    Healing Prayers: Increases duration and effectiveness of Healing Prayers, allowing the Monk to heal their allies.

    Protection Prayers: Increases duration and effectiveness of Protection Prayers, allowing the Monk to protect their allies from harm.

    Smiting Prayers: Increases damage and effectiveness of Smiting Prayers, which penetrate armor. Extremely effective against the undead.

    The Elementalist Profession

    Elementalist Attributes:

    Energy Storage: For each rank your maximum Energy increases by 3. Several skills related to gaining health and energy becomes more effective with higher energy storage.

    Air Magic: Spells that control lightning and the wind. Primarily offensive spells that target single enemies and deal lightning damage with armour penetration.

    Fire Magic: Spells that control fire and meteors. Primarily offensive spells, many that deals fire damage to groups of enemies and cause burning.

    Water Magic: Spells that control water and ice. Many spells that slow enemy movement and deal cold damage.

    Earth Magic: Spells that control the earth, sand and rock. Many defensive spells such as wards to protect allies. Offensive spells can deal earth damage, cause blindness to enemy.

    The Necromancer Profession
