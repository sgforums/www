    Selling land won't work. The price of land increses for each unit, as your amount of land increases.

    Selling tech doesn't require citizens.

    Go to CN forum, or your forum, look for people who is willing to buy tech.

    The person will usually pay 1.2 mill for tech deal.
    Then, you will look for middleman. Middleman will receive 100k or 200k for being middleman.

    Here is how tech trading works.

    Person A is a high level player. As per unit of tech increases with the increase in amount of tech a person has, for person A, buying 50 units of tech will cost him $3 million.

    Person B is a complete newbie like you. As such, person B only needs to spend 500k to buy 50 units of tech.

    Person A offers to pay 1.2 million for tech. He saves the cost of 1.8 million if he buys it from person B, rather than buying it from CN.

    Person B agrees to buy tech and sells to person A since he will gain more than 100k of profits from tech selling. That is a huge sum for a newbie.

    A middleman, person C, offers to be the middleman for 100k.

    Person A, B, C agree to tech-trade.

    Here is how tech-trading works:
    1) Person A, the expert sends 1.3 million to the middle-man, person C.
    2) Person C keeps 100k for himself for being middle-man and passes on 1.2 million to person B, the newbie.
    3) Person B uses the 1.2 million person C sends to him to buy 50 units of tech.
    50 units of tech for person B will cost less than 1.2 million.
    4) Person B sends the 50 tech, which he uses person A's money to buy, to person A, the expert. Person B keeps the profits and uses it to buy infra.

    In this way, person A, the expert makes 1.8 million since he saves that amount of money.
    Person B, the newbie, makes (1.2million-800k to buy 50 tech=400k) of money.
    Person C, the middleman makes 100k.

    You don't have to need the money to buy tech to sell it. They money will be sent to you by person A to a middle man, person C, and person C will send the money to you.

    Use the money person A send to you to buy the tech. Send person A the tech, and keep the profits for yourself. Even if you have no money, or you are a newbie, you can still do it. Hope that helps.

    When I do tech-trading, I don't have the money to buy the tech either. I use the money the person who wants to buy tech sends to me to buy the tech and sends back the tech to him, while I keep the profits to myself.

    One Word of Danger: Sometimes, someone attacks you before you can buy the tech, and you ends up losing the money to the somone in a war, and ends up unable to have the money to buy tech, and unable to pay person A the money he sends to you.
