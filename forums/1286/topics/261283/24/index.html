    Originally posted by herbs:
    y my efficiency keep lowering?
    Efficiency is calculatted by the following:

    Nation Strength DIVIDED by the Number of days played.

    And since Nation Strength is based on:

    Land Purchased * 1.5 + Tanks Deployed * .75 + Tanks Defending * 1 + Cruise Missiles * 1.5 + Nuclear Purchased * 50 + Technology Purchased * 20 + Infrastructure Purchased * 3 + Actual Military * .10+ (aircraft rating totals * 5)

    The ones in bold are the ones you will like to concentrate on. So pump up your NS, and you efficiency will increase.

    Hint: Efficiency does not affect anything at all, so don't worry about it being low.. As you get stronger, definitely it'll go down..
