    if you can get aluminum, iron, lumber, and rubber in addition to what you have now you can get some nice price reductions and happiness increase as well as bonus resources (beer, construction, asphalt, and scholar when your literacy rate is 90%).

    use the Cybernations Calculator:
    http://www.cse.sc.edu/%7Etemlyaka/CNCalc/cncalc.html

    add in the resources you get now and experiment by adding various other resources and see what you think is the best for you....when you find the 4 best resources use the Advanced Trade Finder:
    http://www.cybernations.net/search_advanced_trades.asp

    tick 2 of your needed resources and be sure you pick the 'require two' option in the Search Criteria box, then click 'Search Nations'....you probably should set a Nationl Strength range for your search so you don't bother searching nations with a 90,000 NS....0(low) - 10,000(high) is a good starting point

    shuffle resources in your search,,,aluminum/iron, aluminum/lumber, etc...send out lots of PMs asking ppl to trade with you

    ask ppl to send YOU a trade agreement, that way you can keep looking instead of stopping while your agreements are waiting for approval. If somebody agrees to trade then send an agreement.

    i've sent out 100s of messages asking ppl to trade....i'll take some work, don't give up!
