STGCC 2009 Conference Sessions with industry experts!

  • dith

    If you’re an industry professional (animation, design, gaming, digital media, etc.) or a student in any of the fields mentioned, these sessions were designed just for you!

    Scheduled for Friday, 14 August 2009, the first-of-its-kind STGCC Conference Sessions have been designed to stir you, enlighten you and inspire you. Featuring key players from the global creative industries, the sessions will cover topics ranging from making it big in the designer toy industry, to graphic novels, film-making and digital artistry.

    All attendees will receive a special goodie bag with gifts sponsored by our valued speakers!

    Speakers | Topics
    Eric Khoo (SIN) | “From Comics and Action Figures to Movie-Making and Graphic Novels”
    Imaginary Friends Studio (SIN) | “Be a Successful Digital Artist with Style”
    Kidrobot (USA) | “Kidrobot: The Toy Creative Process”
    LEGO Concept Lab (JPN) | "Beyond the Brick: Design and User-Led Innovation at LEGO"
    FZD School of Design (USA) | Drawing from Design"
    Imagi (HKG) | “Astro Boy: Hollywood Storytelling, Eastern Animation”
    Animax Asia (SIN) | “Animax’s LaMB: The Production Experience”
