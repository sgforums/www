    RAPPELZ             

      ...WHERE MASTER AND CREATURES FIGHT SIDE BY SIDE

      

     

    Get your battle gears ready as Eaglegame International Ltd., delivers its latest MMORPG, Rappelz!

     

    Rappelz, now on its Epic VI: Resurrection, is a one-of-a-kind, free-to-play MMO that charges to greater heights of action as the most sinister and fearsome villain of the gameplay makes her comeback. The tale of the Witch's return begins. To win over her, three races are bound for the mission- the Gaians, the Asuras and the Devas.

     

     

    CHOOSE FROM THREE DIVERSE RACES 

     

    Gamers will surely love the three distinct yet exceptional characters in the game; the fearless Gaians - oldest inhabitants of the land who base their skills from the four forces of nature such as Earth, Wind, Fire and Water; the righteous Devas - holy-based race who specialize in healing magic and defensive buffs and the cunning Asuras - masters of the dark magic with distinct offensive skills and high evasion.

    MAXIMIZE PET CAPABILITY

     

    RAPPELZ' trademark is its in-house patterned pet system which allows regular pets to be significantly useful to their master. In the early stages of the character’s training, pets are already introduced. Also known as Creatures, these special allies grow, train and eventually fight along with their master.

     

    Creatures and pets in Rappelz also manage to level up! They go through the challenge of a 3-stage evolution. Along the way, the pets learn of their unique battle style and acquire more job levels. A character, depending on his class, can have to the maximum, three pets.  It is then fair to say that an avatar reincarnating himself through his pets can be three times more powerful and efficient than  a regular warrior.

     

     

    HIGH-END CUSTOMIZATION OF CHARACTERS AND CLASS STRUCTURES

    Players of Rappelz are sure to enjoy the variety of characters and skill customization. Upon starting, he can already decide on which race (Deva, Asura, Gaia) to use and what physical features he wants his avatar to possess. Aesthetically customizable at the beginning of the game are face structure and markings, hair, and skin tone. Also, among the most admired venture of this game is the player’s ability to decide his in-game profession. He can be a Guide, Stepper or Rouge depending on his chosen race. As the player reaches level 50, these three professions can be further upgraded into more specific class structures.

    OWNABLE, ADVENTURE-SEEKING DUNGEONS

    Rappelz offers challenging dungeon sieges, sieges that can only be won by closely coordinating with a guild. There are two levels in the dungeon siege. First level is the raid and the second level is the siege.

     

    All interested guilds are allowed to join the raid. The challenge initially rises as the participating guilds are tasked to kill two great bosses at the shortest time possible. The winning guild advances to the second level, the dungeon siege itself.

     

    Conversely, the dungeon siege requires more tactical and strategic planning as the winning guild engages in purely PVP battle. It is like going through the eye of a needle. The current guild owner battles with the recent raid winner. The best fame and wealth awaits the top-rank, unbeatable guild. 

     

    THREE-FOLD QUESTS

    It is an acknowledged fact that quests exist in all MMOs, but what makes Rappelz extra special? Rappelz allows a three-fold quest. These includes the storyline quests, repeatable quests and quests given by Sayah in the hidden village (requires hidden village pass). All quests are sure to give rewards, however, if one wishes to get maximum rewards he may opt to take all the quests simultaneously. With the myriad of quests offered by Rappelz, boredom is never an option.
