    This is a game created by a singapore company, Tyler Projects. Been addicted to this game for 1 year 3 months and counting. Do join me in this facebook game (I must say the standard is pretty good for a facebook game and best of all it is free!!!).

    Some feature of the game:
    - 4 Different Class for you to choose from
    - PvP system (Get to fight other players for the honour of having the best ship in the air)
    - Fully customisable ship (Choose the ship, weapons and parts you want to have)
    - Market system (Fully funtional auction and shops)
    - Quest System (Storyline based quest)
    - Clan War (Get to fight other clan fort for the honour of being the best clan. There are also some singapore based clans like Hwa Chong Zi Di (HCZD), SMU Battle Stations (SMU), Anglo-Chinese School Internal Alliance Network (ACSIAN))


    This is one of the games that the developers give almost daily updates on the game.

    Furthermore, to enhance your gaming experience, they also have weekend event every single week with bonus like +30% exp/gold or even some super boss monster for you to fight which will allow you to win some cool super rare weapons.


    To play the game:
    http://battlestations.notlong.com (This link will give you free resource to kick start the game)

    To get a glimpse of what you can expect:
    http://battlestations.mobileweapon.net/history_view.php?hid=230082

    Support Singapore Games!!!
