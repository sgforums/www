    Heroes of Might and Magic Online (HoMM Online) is an online game that combines the classic elements of HoMM III, with the updated graphics of HoMM V, to present a unique gameplay experience. HoMM Online is a turn-based online game that puts the emphasis on strategic planning and tactical skill, while allowing players to socialize and create their own legends of glory!

    You have 8 playable factions to choose from, with 16 classes and 56 creatures to use. After choosing a hero, you can learn more than 20 special hero skills, cast hundreds of spells, and partake in thousands of quests as you advance your path as a soldier for hire. The fact that it is online also offers you a wider opportunity to PK other players! We've built a special Arena to hold all kinds of PK Tournaments, to allow the bravest warriors to test their mettle. You can also ask your friend to play together, create your very own Legion, and learn craft skills to create your own unique Legion buildings!
