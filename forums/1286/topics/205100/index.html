    BC has been annouced to be postponed to Jan '07.

    I am currently in the closed beta, so I'll answer what queries anyone might have for in-game. Currently, my paladin is level 65 and I have a level 14 draenei warrior, level 10 draenei shammie and numerous other BE below level 10.

    At this time, not every region is open and level up progression is still limited to 67. It is said that the flying mount will cost 5000g. Although it sounds like hell of alot, it is easier to accumulate gold in game.

    With regards to epics and tier sets, I have understood that T1 would last a player until ~62, T2 until ~64 and T3 until 66-67 before feeling the pinch on the gear requirements. So if you haven't completed your set, fret not, for you will be finiding green/blue gear that is better than you ep1x not far in game.

    The greatest change is the PvP mechanics. The honor ranking system has been completely revamped. You no longer suffer from honor decay and typically accumulate 'honor tokens' or 'BG tokens' to purchase PvP reward armour and weapons.
