    There were some great moments and fun maps played:
    * letgodowl was the classic dustbowl map in lego textures with some minor changes which makes it seem easier for the invaders.
    * murderball_forever was a great update of a classic map with ramps that boost you and sound effects to boot. The objective is simple, kill the person carrying the ball and then grab it yourself to score points for both yourself and your team. 4 teams, 1 ball and a lot of fun.
    * navarone has a really great start, you jump out of a plane to land in the water below and then storm the beach on the way to the 1st cap point. After the 2nd cap point has been capped the defenders have a button they can push to close the doors momentarily to give the defenders some time to regroup in preparation of the next assault wave.
    * escape_b is a fun I&D map set in an Indiana Jones theme. tN|Indiana_Jones christened part of the map "canyon of the crescent moon":

    * hwguyz2 is an Axl CTF map. The only allowed classes are HWGUY and medic and its the first team to score 10 caps. If you die you have to spectate until the round is over.
    * junob7d has the same starting and cap pt1 as navarone as they were both done by the same mapper and this is where the similarities end. Its a 4 cap pt A&D map with a great alternative route for a demo assisted jump point for the final cap point.
    * baconbowl_r(I&D map) both teams finished on a tie with [CA]asdf managing to cap the 3rd and final cap point right on the bell:

    * sc5x_bonus_beta2 was a hide and seek map with cpz showing off his seeking prowess.
    * civsawc pitted two HWGUYs against everyone else as a cilivian. Its a small map and the objective for the cilivians is to cap the flag, when they do this the civilians are given quad for a limited time to seek their revenge on the HWGUYs.
    * axldrop7 is another murderball map with only 2 teams and is another Axl map. Half the fun in this map is surviving the initial drop, shooting the enemy team while falling and then landing safely on the platform below. Most people don't seem to know that you can air strafe to help you land safely. There is a quad powerup in the centre of the map and a resup room as well.
    * rasen is another great I&D map with a detpackable moat at the 3rd and final cap point. y0ink* had a spray that looks like a ladder and as I hadn't played this map in ages I fell for it as I tried to climb a hut(on top of the hut would have been a perfect place for an SG) that was located near the chokepoint next to the gates. It turns out I wasn't the only one tricked by y0ink*'s spray:

    * tc_soldierball is another murderball map, perfect for small amounts of people. The only allowable class is soldier and there's 4 teams all competing for that one ball.
    * nyroball is muderball map, great when tc_soldierball is too small but murderball_forever is too big. Its smaller size and no class restrictions allow for some great gameplay.
    * high_flag is a fun CTF map to play. In order to reach the flag you you need to either conc, rocket, pipe, or gren jump your way to the flag. IF you have time you could try climbing the ladder but that is made next to impossible when you have enemy defenders shooting at you.
    * area49_2 is a co-op escape map. It took us just over 2 hours to complete. There was about 10 of us playing.

    New Map Vote Plugin Installed
    To help people play on the TFC server when no admin is around a new map vote plugin has been installed.


    You can now change the map at any time by typing in either:
    rocktheboat
    rock the boat
    rtb
    rockthevote
    rock the vote
    rtv


    You can suggest maps to be played next by typing in:
    nominate mapname
    vote mapname


    You are shown the list to be voted on for 10 seconds and then you can vote. Once voting has started you have 20 seconds to vote by pressing either 1 to 0 on your keyboard.
    The format of the vote is:
    slot 1 - a&d(avanti style)
    slot 2 - ctf
    slot 3 - i&d(dustbowl style)
    slot 4 - valve maps
    slot 5 to 8 - nominated maps
    slot 9 - extend
    slot 0 - donkey vote
