    Oh. This question. Very frequently asked by my friends.

    It's better to get PSP 1000/2000 (I own a 2000 myself) cos' you can modify and play *ahem* pirated/downloaded games from third-party sources. HOWEVER, if you don't plan to do that (which means you have to waste around 30 to 60 bucks on a UMD) you may get a PSP 3000 with much better graphics/sounds. But what's most important is that you look long-term. It is literally impossible to carry around 20 UMDs without getting them spoiled one day (maybe it is possible, but yeah, kinda exaggerated) and if you're like, a hardcore gamer, it is recommended to download games. Like you know the websites where you can download PSP games like PSP blender and etc, you need to pay the website (some are trustworthy, some are SO NOT) then you can download unlimited games for most websites. For me, sure I own a PSP, but I don't have the guts to try out the website thing. UMDs are best for me, if I can afford them.

    The price of a PSP (I got it at FunZCenter in AMKHub, I think this is the shop name o.o) with the freebies and such was 400-500 when I got it. But yeah, PSP 3000 is out. So if you're getting PSP 2000, I think the price has gone down. But if you're getting PSP 3000, it's pretty much sadded (to me).
