    The game has 3 servers : Columbus, Magellan, Da Gama.

    I am currently playing in Columbus server, during weekdays, there is not much lag. As for weekends, well... stay away from crowded cities like Athens you should be fine.

    For beginners, I recommend that you build a merchant ship first and take up some tasks from the Merchant Guild Master to earn some cash. Sell all your cannonballs since they take up space in your ship. Avoid pirates and thunderstorm while at the sea. Once you have some money, learn timber-felling and mining. You will be chopping tree or mining ore most of the time until you can afford to build a new ship.

    When playing the game, you can go, system -> operation and change the mouse controls.

    Character Choose Control -> Left Click
    Camera Control -> Right Click
    Move Control -> Left Click

    It is easier for me this way, you can try this settings too.

    Athens is like a marketplace full of player stalls. Thus much more laggy than other cities. However, you can buy most of the materials and equipment there. Player crafted items are usually of better quality.

    To build level 2 ships, sail to Genoa. (West wing of Italy)
    To build level 3 or 4 ships, sail to Algiers (Capital City of Algeria, north Africa)

    Having a real atlas of the world really helps in this game.
