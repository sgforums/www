SPACE WOLVES

  • KumSioJui
    21 Mar 05, 13:10
    This is a new SPACE RPG, RTS Games been spending the whole of weekend playing it. Highly addictive.

    In the aftermath of your ship being hijacked by space pirates. You hook up with another pilot and both decided to become mercenary bounty hunters. Both of you manage to purchase an old derelect cargo ship and convert into sort of a military mobile base, like a mini aircraft carrier. In this game you start out with 2 pilots and eventually end up with a total of 6 pilots, and as you gain experience and skills with the completion of each mission more advance technology available to you.

    Technology upgrades includes
    fighters:light recon to heavy fighters or missile gunships
    weaponry:simple autocannons to heavy lasers to alien blasters to adv missiles and torpedos.
    systems: counter-measures, engine boosters, shield enhancers, cloaking devices, auto-repair bots.

    Technology can be purchased from trade depots, black markets or as booty after a dogfight which is tractored into the mothership.

    There are many factions in this game:
    The USS: Government Patrol Service
    InoCo : Powerful Corrupt Corporation
    TRIAD: Powerful Corrupt Corporation
    Berserkers: AI fighters that attack human fighters
    Aliens: Unknown

    The game centers around flying 40 main missions and many other sub-missions for various factions which may include recon, raids, assasinations, smuggling, ambush/robbery, blockade busting missions etc ...
