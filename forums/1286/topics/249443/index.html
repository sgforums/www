Freeware game review- Vantage Master Online

    Vantage master online is a strategy turn based game by falcom that's similiar to final fantasy tactics. Although its name has online in it, it's not really a online game although there's a multiplayer mode. There's also a scenario mode that's like a rpg element.

    Basically all u have to do o finish each stage, you have to reduce hit point (HP) of your opponent to 0. You will lose the game if your HP were reduced to 0 by your opponent. When the battle begins, the first thing you do is to summon Natial. Then move your master and Natials as you move the chess, to attack the enemies.

    This is really one of the best freeware strategy games out there. Good anime style graphic, good sounds and with good details too...there's day time, night time and the terrain elements. The menus are all in english but the story in scenario mode is probably still not translated into english.

    there's also a nice credit role when u finish the scenario mode, overall i would give it 8/10
