    Prey is a first-person shooter which tells the story of Tommy, a Cherokee garage mechanic who denies his past and is uncertain about his future. His world comes to a halt when he and his people are abducted to a menacing mothership orbiting Earth. Tommy taps his latent Cherokee spiritual powers to save himself, his girlfriend, and the planet.

    Built on a heavily modded version of the Doom 3 engine, features include wall walking, gravity flipping, and intense single and multi-player gameplay.
