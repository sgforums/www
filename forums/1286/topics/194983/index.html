    Perfect Dark Zero is a first-person shooter video game developed by Rare and published by Microsoft exclusively for the Xbox 360 video game console. It was released as a launch title in North America, Europe, and Japan. It received lukewarm reviews from the gaming press, as well as underwhleming sales at retail. It has been critically panned by some for an unusual aiming system, as well as poor storyline and voice acting.

    With respect to storyline, Perfect Dark Zero is a prequel to the successful Nintendo 64 game Perfect Dark, made in 2000. It takes place 3 years prior to the original game, in the year 2020.
