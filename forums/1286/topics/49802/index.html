    Hmmm.... you know, when you want games that are girl-friendly, you should really look at the GameCube. Afterall, Nintendo's main policy on games is that 'they are for EVERYONE.'

    Here are my recommendations of GameCube games that are good to go with your sisters, mothers, girlfriends, and the open-minded, non-chauvinistic guys.

    Multiplayer games:

    Super Smash Brothers Melee
    4 people whack each other using special moves, martial arts, and any items they can grab hold off. Throw a Pokeball at a friend, and watch him/her cries as Legendary Pokemon Zapdos appears, and then laugh as his/her character gets zapped by Zapdos's repeated lightning. Oh yeah, did I mention that the animation is all cute? And, and that it stars classic Nintendo characters, so you can watch Mario beat the hack out of Pikachu? Official site: http://www.smashbros.com/battle/index.html

    Super Monkey Ball 2
    a bunch of monkeys trapped in balls, getting from point A to point B, travelling across dangerous platforms. This simple-to-play game has you tilting the level floors, rolling your monkey ball into the goals without falling off. If you get tired in the main game, there are a plethora of party games that will keep the whole family entertained. Some people even like the party games more than the main game.

    Mario Party 4
    Basically a board game that has all 4 players participating in a team minigame every turn. I've even saw 4 big grown men laughing their butts off while playing this game, once. Serious.
