    Rules

    - Bypassing Crescendos is illegal

    - Subtitles are not allowed

    - Map exploits is illegal if it’s not made for the original intent of the game or mapmaker. 

    - Blocking the path of a survivor is legal, unless it prevents them for advancing in the game then it is illegal

    - Using any cheats, exploits or hacks not made for the original intent of the game is illegal and might result in immediate disqualification depending on the situation. 

    - Ghosting is not allowed

    - The organizers reserves the rights to change or modify this section at any time

    Regulations

    - All matches will be played by and according to the official published tournament schedule

    - Teams are required to be fully present 15 minutes before scheduled match to check in with the organizers and to set their settings and configurations.

    - Teams with a minimum of 3 players are allowed to play but AI will be removed

    - Teams are required to send in all 4 players registration forms at one go to avoid confusion or any inconveniences 

    - Your team is only allowed to use players who have registered for the tournament. 

    - Registration will be ongoing until the last week of the tournament. Teams will be randomly picked and there will be at least a minimum of 16 teams allowed for this two month long tournament. 

    - No in-game spectators other than those authorized by the tournament committee are allowed in the matches.

    - Abusing of chat function will be punished according to the level of abuse.

    - Teams that have lost will be allowed to reapply and play again, however priority will be given to the newer teams, Champions are not allowed to reapply.
