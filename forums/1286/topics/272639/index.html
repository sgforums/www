    3.Before you throw your precious flashbang lookout to see if there are any teammates around. I realized on server a Hongkong farktard deliberately throw at teammates..

    4.Try not to block your AWPing teammates and it could annoy them.

    5.Footsteps. You can listen to footstep e.g. tunnel in de_dust2 and it may earn you some extra frags

    6.Some people has a unique way of shooting, that is after they open fire they use DUCK key to squat. I am one of them, but this results in low accuracy,unless you are talking about targeting their head, open fire and squad..

    7.For AK you could consider this - Far range use a bullet at a time, don't hold but pressing repeadly. This is very accurate as every bullet hits. For close range you can use 2shot per click. For Very near range do your best to spray !

    8.Locating your crosshair above chest level helps better for headshot.

    9.Solitude kills. What if the other party whole gang chiong same place? Good luck then.. This type of scenerio often happens.

    10.Signals. When your teammate use C2 enermy spotted or c3 Need backup you locate where he is from radar and sapport him, or else inevitably you are the one losing out.

    11.Many people do not use radar. Please make it a point to check radar often like you go dentist every 6 months ! I am not suggesting you check your radar every 6 months but try to look at it when your teammates die
