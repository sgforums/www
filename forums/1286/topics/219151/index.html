    Hilary Duff will appear in the game, joined by her beloved Chihuahua Lola.



    Hilary will be downloadable for the PC version of the Sims 2 Pets from October 17 through December 31 only. To download the Hilary Sim, players will visit the official Sims website.

    Once she is downloaded to the game, Hilary will be a fully playable character in the Sims. This means that players can customize and control Hilary as they do other Sims.

    To access Lola, players will type in a code that will "unlock" Lola, introducing her to the game. Players can indirectly control Lola through other Sims, including Hilary herself.
