    Depending on what map you go...you will be offered a free machine gun..
    and 2 grenades and 1 handgun and G-GUN..

    Ok Lets start with basic tips..

    1. Magnum is the most effective 1 hit kill weapon in the whole game ,
    better then crossbow

    2. Always pick up large objects to throw..and aim at the spot and getting closer to the person always help

    3. Move around..Always do that..if u fail to move around..your body will become full of bullets..

    4. Take medipaks and the HEV suit energy power up only when area is safe..

    5. Using the Super "Rifle" with the right click special plasma power can kill enemies spreaded out inside a room

    6. Throwing is only effective at short ranges..

    7. The Grenade and Bazooka missile "splash" range has been reduced..
    do hit accurate..

    8. Using right click to grab the weapon and throw the weapon at someone
    is much more effective then using 1 to fire all the way

    9. Always go servers with lag-free...

    10. Work with your teammates to help secure a area..

    11. In HL 2 DeathMatch, skills doesn't matter much anymore, weapon does

    12. When throwing explosive barrels around, do ensure no one shoots at it

    13. Check your team..and know how to identify your members at long ranges..

    14. Remember not to play too long =p

    15. Stock up enough ammo for emergencies..conquer sector by sector..
    always watch your back
