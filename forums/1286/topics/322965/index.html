    [B]Registration for World Cyber Games 2008 @ Suntec City is now open! [/B]

     

    Date: 7th – 10th August 2008 (Thursday to Sunday)

    Time: 10am to 10pm

    Venue: Suntec Convention Hall 402-403

     

    Open Category Games (all nationality):

    •     DotA - $75 per team

    •     Call of Duty 4 - $25 per team

    •     Halo 3 (4v4) - $20 per team

     

    Singapore National Finals Games (only Singaporeans):

    •     Counterstrike 1.6 - $75 per team

    •     StarCraft: Bloodwars - $5 per person

    •     Warcraft III: The Frozen Throne - $5 per person

    •     Age of Empire III: The War Chiefs - $10 per person

    •     Need for Speed: Pro-street (PC) - $5 per person

    •     Command & Conquer 3: Kane’s Wrath - $5 per person

    •     Halo 3 (4v4) - $40 per team

    •     Guitar Heroes III - $10 per person

    •     Project Gotham Racing 4  - $10 per person

    •     Fifa 08 (PC) - $5 per person

     

    Participants are entitled to a goodie bag and a WCG 2008 T-shirt each!

     

    For more information on prizes, game rules, schedules and structure, go to [url]http://sg.worldcybergames.com[/url] 

     

    Participants are to note that there is an age-limit for Singapore National Finals Halo 3. Anyone born after Nov.6th, 1990 will not be allowed to participate.

     

    Slots are limited, so join now! Register at [url]http://www.esportsc.com/index.php?option=com_tournament&Itemid=5[/url]

     

    Closing date for registration:  1st August 2008 (Friday)

    •     All payment of registration fees MUST be made by this date.

     

    Registration fees may be paid via online payment as well as manual payment. Venues for manual payment are as follows:

    •     Funan DigitaLife Mall: 3rd – 6th July (12noon – 6pm)

    •     SAFRA Mount Faber: 18th – 19th July (12noon – 6pm)

     

    Steps to make manual payment?

    1.    Sign up for the tournament you intend to join on eSportsC [url]http://www.esportsc.com/index.php?option=com_tournament&Itemid=5[/url] , under the different categories.

     

    2.    Register yourself or your team under the manual payment section

     

    3.    Make payment at the above venues before the registration closing

    dates of the tournament

    4.    Look for Andrei at the registration counter to make payment

     

    5.    Manual payment can also be made at our office at 41Jalan Pemimpin, #02-03A, Kong Beng Industrial Building S(577186). For more info about how to get to our office, please go to [url]http://www.rapturegaming.com/contacts_main.php[/url]

     

    Note:

    •     There will be no reservation of slots via phone or email

     

    •     Registration is only complete upon receipt of full registration fees either by cash or credit card (for online payment)

     

    •     For manual payment, we advise participants to make payment as soon as you have sign up for the tournament either at our office or through the venues available above. Rapture Gaming will not be liable for any discrepancies of UNPAID registrations slots incurred during this period.
