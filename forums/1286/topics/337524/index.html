    Really now.

    It just struck me that the majority of Singaporeans, say 60%, (now, I'm just pulling figures out of thin air with no research what-so-ever) have never really experienced the true gaming experience.

    A recent visit to a cybercafe yielded unsurprising result. Many of them were playing DotA, MapleStory and CounterStrike 1.6.

    Is this the average gaming diet of a Singaporean? If so, this is poor cruisine and another fine dining experience ought to be catered.

    I get scoffed at for purchasing hits like Fallout 3 or even Left 4 Dead. I cried myself to sleep, rocking in my dark corner, hugging my darling Fallout 3.

    What I really would like to get for Christmas is that Singaporeans move out of the gaming  hits of 1998 and move on to 2008.
