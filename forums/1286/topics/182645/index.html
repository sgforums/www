    1. Teamwork - Play for your platoon and not for yourself (sometimes you have to make a sacrifice)

    2. Maturity - You don't bully, pinch, kick, annoy, hassle or heckle other people in the gaming community

    3. Enthusiasm - There's nothing wrong with being keen!

    4. Discipline - Know when to behave in public

    1. Must be available for mobilisation 7 days a week,available every night, and make your saturdays and sundays free for trainings and matches (be it online or LAN)

    2. Headset with Mic for voice communication (Teamspeak)

    3. The use of trainers or any sort of cheating is not tolerated.

    4. Contribute a fee of $5 for the clan server monthly.

    5. Good attitude.

    6. Commitment

    Your application will be analysed and upon acceptance you shall be placed on trial
    with us for a certain period of time. At anytime during the trial period we will decide whether
    to promote or not to promote you to a full member.
