    Kojima Confirms MGS Movie



    Speculation has been flying about a silver screen Metal Gear Solid adaptation, but series creator Hideo Kojima has been tightlipped. In a recent broadcast of Hide-chan Radio, Kojima announced that MGS was indeed getting made into a feature film. "We're going to do it as a movie," he confirmed. "A Hollywood movie." When? The director was reluctant to give specifics, saying "I can't say when yet." He added that the film was going to be in English. A Hollywood movie based on MGS... And this is a good thing?
