    What is Universe & Allies?

    “Universe & Allies” is a browser based RPG which doesn’t require downloads to play (Web-Based Game). Each player starts with a galaxy in universe. Player needs to continuously develop and extend the development to other planets. Eventually player will encounter other’s player which they can choose to ally or against with an objective to conquer the universe.

     

    It is very similar to all the games of the same genre, except it is in futuristic space warfare setting (Star Wars/Star Trek/Battlestar Galatica) against medieval (3 kingdom/Three Kingdom, Utopia), WW 2 (War 2).
