    Anyone played before?

    One my favourites.

    The gameplay is like tycoon styled economic strategy game where you play an evil mastermind trying to take over the earth.

    You can play as 3 avatars, a Dr Evil lookalike or Maximmilian, Alexis a fur wearing female with serious attitude or Shen Yu, a Chinese conspirator with a mystical air.

    Basically you keep and build your base inside a mountain from scratch and have minions and henchmens keeping your base from the good side. To gain money and noteoriety which you need in the game, you send your minions to embezzle money and steal artifacts and icons needed to win a scenario.

    Great game. Great graphics.
