    I recommend you to get the package with no "2 Games + NDS Lite" etc.. Or to cut it short, try not to get any package that comes with games.

    Choosing between exported set or non exported is totally up to you. Local set comes in all the colours cept for white and black. Exported set is white and black. Local set has warranty (official one) whilst exported set doesn't.

    Many tend to fret over the "exported no warranty, I buy later got prob jialat liao" situation. My advise to you is to ask the shop uncle do they provide warranty for the NDS Lite, like if you find anything wrong, you can come back for help. Not many shops will do this, but just try your luck.

    To buy at Sim Lim is err... complicated. Make sure you know what is in the set that you're buying, and is anything wrong. Know the range of price and know when to haggle, when not to. If you know what you're doing, you can get great prices for your item.

    All the best.

    There is black and white for local sets too, in fact all colours are available for export and local sets. But if you are getting those limited edition sets, like the Brain Age 2 Crimson / Black set etc, there is only export sets

    The only diff in local and export sets are the warranty, which is 3 months provide for Maxsoft. Export sets are are only $10 cheaper to local sets, so paying $10 for 3 months warranty, why not?

    In fact, if you bought an export set, it will be a 2 pin charger as most export sets come from US or Japan. If the shop is good, they will do a 1 to 1 exchange on the charger from a 2 pin to 3 pin, at no extra cost.

    I do know a place which sell a local set with r4, 1gd micro sd, screen protecter and casing @ less then $350. PM me if you want the address.

    Buying seperately from all over sg might be cheaper on the surface, but by how much? The savings will be only about $20 - $30. And if you take into the consideration of transport charges, the saving might not be high. So you might as well get everything done at a shop, and if you got any problems, you can look for them for help too.
