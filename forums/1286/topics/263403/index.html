    Most of Lara Croft Tomb Raider series are good games. But the gameplay has always been pretty complex, only advance/mature gamers will be able to complete one of these game.

    Player Requirements:
    1. Resistance to epilepsy/seizure. If you feel like fainting/vomiting when playing FPS game... then you may not be suitable to play this game.

    2. Good Perception. You need to explore and observe your environment thoroughly to look for secret exits/locations/paths. This is the most difficult part since most of us gamers will get frustrated and quit the game when we can not find/reach the exit.

    3. Good Reflexes and Timing with the controls. Seriously speaking, playing any tomb raider game with the keyboard is a very bad idea, do use a hand-held controller. Pressing the jump button one second too early or one second too late can make a big difference.

    4. Good Memory and Intelligence. You need to remember places where you have been to or you will find yourself running in circles. Use pen and paper to draw maps, record clues/findings and also give you a better picture while solving puzzles.

    5. Good Patience and Determination. If you need to jump from one cliff to another cliff, you may not succeed at first, but keep trying. You may need to try 10 times, maybe 20 or 30 times before you can finally succeed, you just need keep trying. Practice makes perfect.
