5 Essential Functions of Mounts in MMO

  • Junoyes
    17 Aug 09, 11:21

    The Mount system has become an essential feature for most MMOs in today's market.

    Conquer Online, a renowned Chinese style MMO, has also introduced its

    own mount system in the latest expansion - Raiding Clans. Players can breed various types of mounts and

    ride them headlong through the vast continents of mythological ancient China. So what can these trusty

    mounts do for you in this virtual MMO universe? Below I would like to list a few instances.


    1. Allow you to visit some rural places

    Travelling in the vast world of an MMO, it is tiring to just depend on your bare feet. However, with a

    vigorous steed, you can cruise on the continent and access some truly splendid landscapes and uncharted

    dungeons that few people have seen before.


     


    2. Help you escape from vicious enemies

    Encountering various monsters is part of everyday life in an MMO. When you are trapped by a flock of

    frantic apes during an adventure, don't be scared. Just summon your trusty steed! They will help their

    master retreat safely from the dangerous region.


    3. Create romantic scenarios for masters

    When you are chasing a girl (or guy, for you ladies), a gorgeous mount could be equal to a brand new

    Ferrari in real life. Imagine you and you lover riding to the summit of a mountain together under the

    shiny moonlight. How romantic!


    4. The symbol of your statue

    In a fantasy MMO, mounts are not merely a transportation tool. On the contrary, mounts are the symbols

    of a players' status in game. From mounts, you can easily distinguish low-ranked newbies and experienced

    veterans.


    5. Show off the power of your guild
    Compared to a lone rider, a whole cavalry of warriors is much more menacing! Through a horse riding

    parade, a guild can demonstrate its dominative power to the whole player community!
