    Alright, this is basically the Chinese equivalent of WoW.
    Many things are similar here, but this game has really good graphics, where u can fight on land, fly in the sky, or swim.
    There's als a huge huge world map for u to explore and the siege system is quite interesting as well.
    For those of you that likes to find love in game, u can even fly to the sky together.
    This game has tons of customization, no 2 people will look the same unless you're too lazy to customize and use the default.
    I tested this game about 2 months back, in chinese and i would rate it 9/10.
    International version coming out soon.
    If you think this game is a bimbo game, you'll be surprised to find that it is quite content heavy as well.
    Only drawback seems to be that there's only 6 classes to choose from.
