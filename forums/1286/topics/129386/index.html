    In his latest adventure, the Prince of Persia returns to Babylon with his love Kaileena. Instead of returning to his tranquil home, he finds his homelands torn apart by war and the kingdom turned against him. Thie prince is captured and Kaileena sacrifices herself and unleashes the Sands of Time in order to save him. Now hunted as a fugitive, the Prince discovers that past battles have stirred a deadly Dark Prince, whose spirit gradually possesses him.

"By combining two playable characters in a masterly balanced gameplay, with the perfect mix of acrobatic moves and a compelling storyline, the Prince of Persia will appeal to both Sands of Time and Warrior Within fans alike.     Game features include the ability to play as two distinct characters and choose your own style of fighting thanks to the Free-Form Fighting system; battle freely through Babylon - on rooftops, streets and underground passageways; a dramatic story as you fight your way to the truth of the sands of time; and of course, manipulate time to battle your enemies.
