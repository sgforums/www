    GO Kart or "GK", is a fun and free-to-play massive multiplayer online racing game published by Garena. Race across the colourful maps while throwing items at your opponents to slow them down. Here is the good news about GO Kart, it has a plethora of useful props that can be used to leave your opponent behind the dust or give yourself a speed boost. GO Kart has a large selection of items available in its Shop, so the game certainly has a lot of playability. The game controls are clear and concise, all you need are 4 control keys to get started with Go Kart.

    Though the race begins with different maps to choose from, a lot of fancy driving lies between the player and the entire selection of maps. Some parts of some maps require the use of speed boost to jump over to the other side, by doing so, it leads you to the shortcut. All this seems so exciting, just wait till you are in the race.

    In GO Kart R&R, you get to rest after a marathon of racing, whether it is to wallow on your defeat or celebrate your victory, this is where you can unwind and meet new friends. If you feel an affinity towards someone you just met in GO Kart, you can even add he/she as friend, with a friend it makes the experience so much better and gives yourself some competition in the race, which will improve your skills or even show off your driving skills to others.

    [IMG]http://i375.photobucket.com/albums/oo195/leehengleon88/2.jpg[/IMG]

    In GO Kart R&R, players can gather to share their enthusiasm for GO Kart, when you start to realise that by sharing your enthusiasm with others, you will be giving others the opportunity to feel the same way as you do, especially when you can talk about how amusing GO Kart can be, its development possibilities and what yet to be discovered. R&R is also a place of romance, who knows you might meet he/she that is the one for you. You can talk all you want, show off your highly fashionable avatar and gain experience from time to time. Isn’t this a great place or what?

    What do you know about avatar? What we wear and how and when we wear it, depicts our personality for changing situations, if you feel like going punk or hip hop or sporty, GO Kart avatar has it all. In GO Kart, the avatar is important because it’s a means of self-expression, it’s from the clothes a person wears that we get our first impression of personality.

    GO Kart avatar ranges from eyewear, expression, top, gloves, tattoo, bottom and headwear. Wow! That’s a lot isn’t it? So don’t worry about not having enough avatar to choose from. If you have no idea what to wear, you may always ask for your friends idea or play mix and match with your own creativity.
