    Priston Tale 2 SEA - The Rebirth of an Old Tale

    Hello players,

    As you may have already knew, Asiasoft will be opening its latest MMORPG, Priston Tale 2 SEA this coming September.

    As the community is concerned with regards how updated the SEA server will be, Asiasoft is seeking players' opinions in what they like or dislike about other servers' version.

    To have your opinions heard, please visit the Asiasoft Priston Tale 2 forums. Nothing can be promised at this stage, but all concerns and suggestions seriously. Thank you!

    Official PT2 SEA forums: http://forums.asiasoftsea.net/forumdisplay.php?f=751
    Official PT2 SEA website: http://www.PT2SEA.com/
    Official PT2 SEA FaceBook page: http://www.facebook.com/PT2SEA


    Game introduction:

    Priston Tale 2 is a free-to-play, 3D Massively Multiplayer Online Role-Playing Game (MMORPG) with action-based, role-playing elements and also features
    the award-winning Unreal graphics technology.

    Set in the beautiful world of Priston, this highly anticipated sequel to the popular Priston Tale offers exciting PVE (Player Versus Environment) raiding
    action with newly designed dungeons and instances.

    Choose from three main races and six classes, including Fighter and Huntress from Tempskron, Knight and Disciple from Morion, Rogue and Pilgrim
    from Sophetio and embark on a grand journey in search of the 2nd Enigma. The future of the Priston continents lies in your hands.
