    American Jonathan Wendel wins Singapore leg of Cyberathletes Professional League



    Quick fingers and sharp eyes have won American Jonathan Wendel US$15,000.

    Wendel won the Singapore leg of the Cyberathletes Professional League World Tour.

    Held at the Padang, the Singapore leg is the seventh and only Asian stop among 10 countries.

    Thirty-two top professional gamers battled it out on their computers and Wendel emerged the winner.

    Wendel also gets a chance to compete in the World Tour Championship Finals in New York in November.

    The huge turnout at the event shows the growing popularity of cyber-gaming in Singapore.
