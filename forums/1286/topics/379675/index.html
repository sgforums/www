Conquer Online Martial Arsenal System Preview

  • Junoyes
    23 Oct 09, 18:03

    After the release of the Raiding Clans expansion in July, Conquer Online will have another major update on October 28, which will bring players more intense conflicts in the world of mythological ancient China.

     

     

    The most critical update will be the brand-new Martial Arsenal feature for the guild system. Each guild will have 8 Martial Arsenals which can be updated by the registration of the high ranking gear from the guild members. When one Martial Arsenal has received enough through the Arsenal Donation, its sealed prowess will be unleashed and all the members of the guild will receive different levels of privilege according to their positions in the guild hierarchy.

     

    Along with the Martial Depot, the Conquer Arena and Couples PK Tournament will be launched, in which you can engage in a gladiatorial duel anytime, or challenge other couples with your beloved.

     

    Amass legendary weapons, Unseal the prowess of the Martial Arsenal!
