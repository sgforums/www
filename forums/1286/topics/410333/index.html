    In news that will surely shake you to the core and inspire you to question your existence as a software geek, it looks like Duke Nukem Forever -- the most fabled of fables -- will finally be released in 2011. You will be able to play it on your PS3 (woo, finally a game!), PC and Xbox 360.

    After being dropped by its original developer 3D Realms in 2009 it was quickly picked up by Gearbox Software, a developer that obviously felt the fly-ridden carcass had not been flogged enough. This past weekend, however, Gearbox showed off some fairly impressive gameplay at PAX. It seems like DNF may actually be released this time. Really!
