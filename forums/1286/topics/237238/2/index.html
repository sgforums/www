    I just played it for a few hours and I must say that this game reminds me a lot about Dungeon Siege 2, and I do not like to play Dungeon Siege.

    This game has good sound effects, graphics and music which is definitely better than World of Warcraft.

    However, there are some gameplay issues which I would like to comment:

    1. High respawn rate of monsters in hostile areas. This will create 3 big problems:
    A) Increase the tendency of players to just camp at a spot and go AFK.
    B) Traveling backwards or retreating from an area is a hassle, since even areas that you have cleared will be full of monsters in a several seconds.
    C) Makes combat very "mechanic" and predictable. "Mechanic" means doing the same thing over and over again.

    2. Limitation in skillsets.
    I not completely sure about this but from what I can see so far, if you use a certain Skill Stance, the character can only use skills that belong to that particular stance. For this part, they can learn to be more flexible like Guild Wars, where a character can use any skill that belongs to any class of any level.

    3. Feels like a RTS game.
    Just like in Warcraft 3 and many real-time strategy games, you can just give your units commands like hold ground, attack move and patrol to have your characters automatically fight for you.

    At this moment, it is a free-to-play-game, so there will be players.
    But, once it becomes a pay-to-play-game, most players will be gone.

    Fortunately for them, this is just Beta so they still can do some major improvements to the game before it is officially launched.
