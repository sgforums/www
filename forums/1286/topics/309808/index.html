    Originally posted by aiglosicicle:

    nice a not? better than maplestory? i need something to pass my time before ns.

    It's closer to existing 3D games like MU Online and GE rather than Maple. In fact, the equipment system and inventory system can be said to be copied from MU Online. What's interesting and unique in this game are its battle system (real-time comboing - where you need to time your button presses, which makes it seem a little Audition-like, to give you bonus exp, and other features like auras, battle styles and skills) and endless (and I really mean endless) number of quests, some with in-depth stories which are far from those "Please get me the mirror from Sera who lives next door." kind of crap.

    And no, you don't grind monsters to level your character up. You grind quests. And beside all these, you can have a female character dressed up scantily and do a dance in the middle of a town for you and other players to ogle at while gaining Spirit Points (SP) required for combos and auras.

    If you were to compare some of its features against Maple's, then I would tell you, the Chaos Lamp in the game is like a Gachapon ticket except that it is found randomly among all monsters and it's free. And shouting in the entire channel is free too, with a 10 second per shout limit (I think they may remove this if people started to spam though). Party members have 30 - 50% EXP bonus as long as you are within close proximity with another member (no, you don't need a priest for that).
