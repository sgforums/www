    This game needs a very powerful gaming computer to run smoothly. What i did was lower my display resolution from my usual 1024 X 768 to 800 X 600. Disable shadows, short-range sighting..e.t.c.

    I played this game about 1 year ago. This is the kick-ass build I used.

    Magic Specialization.
    Major Skills:
    Athletics (For fast running speed)
    Hand-to-Hand (For lightning fast attacks)
    Destruction (For cooking your enemies when you don't feel like beating them up)
    Conjuration (For summoning a powerful monster to fight for you)
    Alteration (For opening locks and giving yourself a LOT of damage resistance)
    Sneak (For playing hide-and-seek with your victims)
    Alchemy (This is a must-have for any build you use. Potions are mainly use for fast recovery during combat and curing abnormal status.)

    Pros:
    High Speed - (You can run faster than a horse, which means you can easily out-run every opponent you encounter)

    High-damage resistance (With enchanted clothes and max-out Alteration
    magics, your armour-rating will be stronger than a warrior that is wearing heavy armour.)

    You do not fight alone - (By summoning a creature to fight for you, you are creating a diversion which can give you a lot of advantage in combat. You can even beat up your own summoned creature like a punching bag when you need some self-training.)

    And many more advantages for you to find out...

    Cons:
    Magicka(Mana) is essential for this build. Once you run out of it, you cannot do much.

    Low health. This is not really a big problem most of the time but can be fatal will you trigger some nasty traps that do a lot of damage.

    No blocking! Using your arms to a block a axe or sword attack is a very bad idea.
