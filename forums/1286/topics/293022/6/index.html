    I got my Wii set from SC Games at Jalan Besar Road. Was a tad more expensive than I expected, but they seem to know their stuff quite well. Quite a reliable place. Also, all those WiiSports add ons and extra Wii-motes/Nunchucks were cheaper than other places.

    Yeah, before modification, a Wii can only play games from the region it is from, either PAL or NTSC, and original games.

    With modification, (Ie. Soldering a chip into the Wii Motherboard), you can play nearly all games from all regions, and pirated/burnt games as well

    Some shops sell readily modified sets, but if you like a challenge, can buy the chip and go home to solder yourself. No guarantees, though. ^^;
