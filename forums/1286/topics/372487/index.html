    You have dedicated sound card? Like Creative X-Fi or something? If so, go for the Carcharias, since the Carcharias would be able to take advantage of the dedicated sound card. I have tried the Carcharias with integrated sound, and it just sounds a lot better with a dedicated sound card. If you haven't gotten a discrete sound card, go for the Megalodon, since it comes with its own audio synthesizer. I haven't really tried the Megalodon though, so don't take my word for it. I have no idea how comfortable it is, but the Carcharias definitely is.
