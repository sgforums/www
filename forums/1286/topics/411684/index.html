    Rappelz is a unique, free-to-play MMO game based on MIGHT and PRIDE.  Known in over 33 countries worldwide, a new Singapore and Malaysia server was recently launched for local players.

     

    Rappelz follows the tale of the return of the Witch - the most fearsome and sinister villain of the game. To stop her comeback, three distinct races are bound for the mission – Gaia, Deva and Asura, to bring back peace to the world of Navislamia.

     

     

    Although the storyline seems quite simple in the beginning, it gets deeper as you play along, with the gameplay being complex enough to provide experienced players with suspense and challenge. Compliments on famous MMO portals include “one of the most unique F2P fantasy games” on Ten Ton Hammer, and “a must-play MMO” on ONRPG.

     

    It was said on Game Ogre that Rappelz has “the best pet system ever”. Pets and creatures in Rappelz level up, go through a unique three-stage evolution and become useful allies to their master. A character can have up to three pets at once fighting alongside him, making him three times more powerful and efficient than a regular warrior, something that is unheard of in other games.

    Rappelz is also about having fun together with your friends. The game has been praised for its dungeon siege system, which includes the timed monster attacks (raids) and the live PVP action (sieges). This electrifying shared action will surely keep players right at the edge of their seats, with the best fame and wealth awaiting the top-rank, unbeatable guild.

Yet, boredom is never an option in Rappelz with the myriad of quests in the game.
