    Luna Online greets all Malaysians!
    OMG ! It’s really coming to Malaysia ! I can’t wait to play it ! I’ve been waiting for this game for so loooooooong

    Run Up Game Officially announced they are going to launch “LUNA online” for Malaysia and Singapore territory, after 9 months testing, preparation of marketing and localization, Run Up Game finally schedule to launch the first ENGLISH version of LUNA online service in the mid of December 2008.


    Luna online is a MMORPG where Elfs and Humans must bond together to fight the evil that invades their Blue land. Game characters are super cute style, with very manga-like (cartoon-like) facial expression.

    The game also provides the unique “Friendship system” and “Family system”, and it has a very convenient trading, dating, family and community system for everyone. You can also build up your farm, plant, business, train and feed your own lovely mounts and pets.

    There is a "Match making system" which will find someone else in the server for you to match your character builds, such as if you are a mage with low armor. It will find a good tanker to be your "match partner", once found your perfect match you will get some additional powers...if you rate other users "good" and have good personality with them you can match up with them again or even bond into the family system".

    The "Family system" is a party of 5 people that you have good "match" or good "relationship" with, you'll be able to grand name of father, mother etc. etc.(party leader, sub-leader etc. etc.) to make your own family in game. If you are good with your family, you'll be able to gain more power and will be able to build things, like vehicles etc. etc. you'll also get a farm , but you will have to fight with monsters or defeat other players to defend your farm . When fighting monsters, if a "match" or "family" member is under attack and you help them, it will strengthen your relationships which will make you stronger, or level up or even more...
    For further information, please visit http://luna.runup.com.my/
