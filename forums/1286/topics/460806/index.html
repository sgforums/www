    Game Intro:
    The Battle of 3 Kingdoms takes place on the Three Kingdoms era of Ancient China which is from the period of the Yellow Turban Rebellion to the fall of the Eastern Han Dynasty. Players get to experience first-hand events during the time of strife and turmoil that eventually lead to supremacy and domination.     Features:
    1.Historical Characters
    The faithful recreation of the historical accounts engages players to experience the heroism and valor of well-known figures from the Three Kingdoms period. The virtuous Liu Bei, the villainous Cao Cao and the mighty Guan Yu are just some of the heroes from the Battle of Three Kingdoms that rise to the call of allegiance for you. Other than the infamous warlords, renowned beauties Daqiao and Xiaoqiao are not being left out too.

    2.The Art of War
    The Tactical deployment and war strategy are one of the interesting features of Battle of Three Kingdoms. Such strategy and formation was derived from ancient Chinese military books. Battle of Three Kingdoms provides players to execute a wide range of strategic and tactical methods of deployment which is the key of claiming victory on various enemies.

    3.Legendary Horses
    Explore the world of “Battle of Three Kingdoms” with your Generals riding alongside their loyal steed. A spirited and awe-inspiring fleet horse is always the faithful companion of a hero. Players can find almost all the legendary horses in the games such as Red Hare, Shadowless and Hex Mark.
