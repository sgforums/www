    Sorry for the "a bit late" post. Busy trying out this joystick yesterday.

    Joystick Features:

    Pros:

    1. Extremely stable base. Even with violent stick movement, the joystick stays in place.

    2. User-friendly installation software. That same "Wingman" software can be used with older Logitech peripherals so there's no need to uninstall and stuff...

    3. Neat looking throttle level.

    4. Accurate joystick when it is calibrated right.

    5. Precise twist handle.

    6. Cool looking design. Yup I know it looks dull in the picture but wait till you see it "live" you'll go saying "WOW"!

    7. Tons of buttons, even tho I seldom use them... the top buttons are also made of rubber. Very nice to touch.

    Cons:

    1. Slightly poor egonomics... first of all, after using this stick for over 30 mins, my whole arm etc: triceps, biceps wrists and fingers starts to swell! Not the most comfortable joystick in my opinion.
