    Final Fantasy, DoA babes heat up in Dead Fantasy 2 fanvid showdown

    Posted Mar 25, 2008 at 02:52PM by Gino D. Listed in: Off Topic, Videos Tags: Square Enix, Kasumi, CGI

    32 QJ

    Here's something nice to start the week with! Remember that CGI tr from a few months ago where hot babes from Final Fantasy and Dead or Alive were pitted against each other in one super kickass death match? Hellzyeah. We sure can't forget it indeed.

    Now, here's the sequel to that: animator Monty Oum gives us Dead Fantasy 2.

    The video picks up right where the previous clip ended, and from that point on, it's 11 full minutes of orgasmic action. More punches and kicks are thrown around, including the occasional magic too, with Rikku, Yuna, and Tifa representing the FF fandom and Kasumi, Ayane, and Hitomi for the DoA crowd.

    Here's the catch though. Three new characters are added into the Brawl - err, brawl, rather. We won't spoil you - our own jaws dropped when the new challengers appeared in the arena. And by the time you reach the end, you're treated - and teased - with one heck of a cliff hanger.

    Man, when's the next vid coming out? Monty Oum mentioned that there are at least five more installments to his little project and he promises that we're in for quite a ride in the coming episodes of Dead Fantasy.
