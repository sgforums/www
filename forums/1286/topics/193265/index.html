    Taken from HWZ

    Online shops in SG

    Red Hot Gamers
    I bought my PSP Playgear Pocket from them, the prices shown are also very attractive. There is a S$4 charge for delivery to your doorstep but they didnt charge me when i ordered my PGP. They used to sell GTA LCS but dunno what happened.

    16by9 Online
    Delivery is free. There are however only a few pickup points unless you order 3 or more items, this way they'll deliver to your doorstep.

    Funz Centre
    This site has an awesome layout. But the funny thing is its *dead* or so i think... but they have a shop though. Here's the address ...
    Blk 710A Ang Mo Kio Central #01-2631
    Tel : 64566672

    Seow Choon
    Nice layout. Great range on games (they even sell MP3 playes and guidebooks). Delivery is S$5 for stuff below S$50 or u can just go down to their shops.

    Play Boxx
    The PlayGear Pocket is the cheapest here only S$29. They dont deliver, you have to go down to their shop to pick it up.

    Mobile Square
    Ah the well known Mobile Square. They sell HPs and games (Only PSP games). Theyhave reasonable prices for new games. What i like is that they actually buy used games and sell them. Clean layout.

    Mega Multimedia
    Nice prices for games on all consoles. There is a delivery charge of S$3 if the stuff you are buying are below S$80. There is also a reward system

    Gamescore
    They have an awesome range of games but they dont deliver. The layout is quite good too. They too have a reward system

    Gameshop
    They have almost anything you need here. Average prices and they also have 2nd hand games for sale. There is a reward system whereby u can sell your games for points/credits. The deliver charges are as follows ...
    "A flat door to door delivery fee of $2.00 is charged for all orders above a total value of $35.00 irregardless of the weight and size of the item. One of the lowest in town! If the total value of your order is less than $35.00 and $20.00, a low order delivery fee of $5.00 and $7.00 will be charged respectively."

    Wallgarden
    A unique site i must say. What attracts me is the Grand Theft Auto San Andreas they are selling for S$90. Very limited number of games.

    Tec-Drome *added on 31/10/05*
    A reputable shop located in Sim Lim Square (SLS). Someone on this forum said that the shop has been reduced to 1/2 the size due to an increase in rental. But that does'nt change the fact this shop still rocks. I'm gonna buy my Saitek Eclipse Keyboard and GuildWars from them after my 'O's (they gave me discount yay).

    The Software Botique *added on 02/11/05*
    Wide range of games. They even sell gaming mice and keyboards. They don't deliver, so you have to go down to their shop. Address ...
    109 North Bridge Road, #05-42 Funan DigitaLife Mall
    Tel: 6339-9793

    CyberActive *added on 28/11/05*
    This shop has a few branches around SG but does not do delivery. It only offers Xbox games and PC hardware. Prices look rather reasonable.

    Comics Connection *added on 29/11/05*
    Many console games. They even sell magazines, DVDs, TCG etc ... Sad thing is when u want to purchase an item u are required to fill up a quotation price to see how much the item is.
