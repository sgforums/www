Battlefield 2 to be included in GXL League!

  • Silent Scope
    10 Apr 06, 01:16
    Great news to the Battlefield community! Due to overwhelming support from the Battlefield community, GXL has decided to include Battlefield 2 in its upcoming GXL League!

    GXL Game Poll

    GXL Selection result

    We would like to inform all the gamers that the new season 2006 GXL Season 1 was rescheduled to begin with the registration opening on the Monday 1st of May 2006. The actual league rounds will begin on Tuesday 16th of May 2006.

    This is the new list of games to be included in the new season:
    PC Games
    Counter-Strike 1.6
    Warcraft III The Frozen Throne
    Warcraft III DOTA Allstars 6.27b
    FIFA
    Battlefields 2
    Age of Empires

    XBOX
    Winning Eleven 9
    Dead or Alive 4
    Project Gotham Racing 3

    Note: GXL Management reserves the right to remove or add titles of the games to the current list.

    The delay in the beginning of the league is caused by technical difficulties - we are looking to give the gamers a flawless experience in the new revamped GXL, inclusive the new website, new forum, etc. A lot of things will change, but the beauty of the game mechanics will remain intact with the players being able to schedule the online matches during their own free time.

    In the meantime all the gamers are welcome to post their ideas and suggestions for the new season in this forum - we are always glad to hear contructive ideas. (flaming will be relentlessly deleted)

    P.S. Anyone interested in doind admin/marshaling for GXL season kindly contact me on my email or msn.
    The inclusion of BF2 in the GXL League is the biggest mandate that this game is worth your time. If you have not tried your hands at BF2 and is an avid FPS gamer, how about giving it a try now? You may join one of the many BF2 clans or even form a clan of your own to join in the fun and excitement?
