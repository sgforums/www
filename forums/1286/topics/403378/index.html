Cyber Gaming Festival 2010 | 2 - 4 July

    *The CGF Team reserves all rights to the artwork shown above

    Next week, the NUS Students' Computing Club will be hosting the annual Cyber Gaming Festival 2010 (CGF '10). The CGF Organising Team consists of all student volunteers and this is the first time we are doing it under atrium mall instead of the usual lanshop. We are very excited about it and hope that you guys here can lend us your support by spreading this news around.

    Like us now! and share it with your friends.

    Date

    Friday 2 July - Sunday 4 July

    Venue

    Square 2 Atrium (right above Novena MRT station)

    Time

    Friday (6pm - 10pm), Saturday and Sunday (10am - 10pm)

    Registration is OPEN !

    DotA - $50 per team, 5 vs. 5
    Blackshot - $50 per team 5 vs. 5 (open to all students only - Tertiary institutions, ITEs, JCs, Polys, Sec)
    Super Street Fighter 4 - $20 per team, 2 vs. 2
    Tekken 6 - $20 per team, 2 vs. 2

    *All participants are entitled to goodie bags + Dry Fit CGF T-shirt

    Prizes

    Too much to include here. Please visit Cyber Gaming Festival 2010 Prizes

    We are not done yet, aspiring game developers will have the opportunities to meet industry leaders and fellow NUS undergrads specialising in Game Development. Talks ranging from discovering opportunities in new social media (e.g. Facebook, twitter) by game business experts, to interesting pet projects that our fellow schoolmates are building will be showcase live on stage. Seize the chance to network with like-minded game developers and you might one day be part of making a billion dollar game!

    Don't play any of the games? Just be there to "GUESS THE CHAMPION" and you stand a chance to bring home over $100 worth of goodies.


    Cheers,
    The CGF Team
