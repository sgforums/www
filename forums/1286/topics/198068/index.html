    LAN shop started multiplying in 1999 due to the success of CS. that time it was still beta 5. since it's a multiplayer game and broadband wasn't that common that time, children and teens flooded the LAN shops to play their favourite game.

    standard rate then was 5 dollars per hour. the most expensive i have came across is $6 per hour at the one at Suntec City. The biggest LAN shop at that time.

    as the years goes by, with broadband becoming more and more common, gamers switched from spending money at the LAN shops to playing in the comfort of their own house. with no one to chase them away when their time is up.
