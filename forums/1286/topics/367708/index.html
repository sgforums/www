    Conquer Online will release Raiding Clans Expansion on the date of July 16th, 2009! The Raiding Clans will bring several major new features to this renowned MMO, including mounts, a Clan System, a new map...It’s the time to saddle up for the journey to the uncharted realm.

     

    Mount System

    The loyal steeds will accompany their masters as they journey across the vast continent of the Middle Kingdom. A distinguished steed will not only glorify your profile, but more importantly, will help you with the rare attributes to dominate the world of Conquer Online.

     

     

    New Map – Frozen Grotto

    There will also be a new region emerging in the Raiding Clans expansion – the Frozen Grotto. In this dark and frozen underground world, ancient demons are waking up from the thousand-year sleep. These hungry monsters are longing for fresh blood to warm their frozen bodies. Meanwhile, this dangerous dungeon is also an ideal place for the top adventurers to enhance their power and chase down some epic treasures.

     

     

    Clan System

    The Clan System will allow adventurers to set up their exclusive inner circles and share the privileges of clan membership. Clan members will have an exclusive chat channel to share their secrets and strategies. During the conflict, clan members can receive bonus battle power and defense which can grant them an overwhelming advantage.
