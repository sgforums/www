Luna Online Singapore

    Hi,

    Has anyone played this cutesy 3D mmorpg called Luna Online? It is a free-to-play mmorpg and hosted in Singapore by a company named RunUp Game Distribution and Development Pte Ltd. The game has nice vibrant graphics and does not require a high-end machine to play.

    You can visit Luna Online SG website at http://luna.runup.com.sg/en/top/top.php

    You have two races to choose from: Human and Elves, each with its own unique characteristics for example, Humans are strong in offensive abilities with high damage while Elves are defensive and dwell on protective powers. It has a wide variety of jobs and each class has access to all paths within the class. For instance, a Fighter can start out as a Warrior or Guard at level 20, followed by any of the 3 at level 40; Infantryman, Mercenary or Swordsman. Each with its own unique abilities and effectiveness in a party or PvP. Other basic classes to choose from are Mage and Rogue and they each expand to various other branches like Fighters. 

    Besides partying and questing, the game also has an interesting Dating function that basically notifies your character if another players has similar likes and dislikes as you. Then you can go about playing Dating quests with that player and earn wonderful rewards. It also has fishing for rare items as well as farming. In farming, players can form families to toil the fields together, planting seeds and rearing mounts for special add-ons in future versions. E.g. pets and rides. Since the company has a local base, you can purchase RunUp point cards (not compulsory) at your nearest Comics Connection store. No credit cards or PayPal accounts required. 

    Simply register an account and download the game. Only about 500Mb in size. 

    If you decide to try the game, you can pm me in game by typing "/LordVenus Your msg" in the chatbox to say hi. I am also the leader of an active guild in Luna Online and if your welcomed to join us if you are interested. We have our very own guild forums too!

    Thanks for reading! Hope to see ya in Luna Online!
