    There's an Indian Fine-Dining restaurant i know of (previously situated at Lv 2, Excelsior Shopping Centre) but is currently located at Chinatown Point Basement. It's called Annalakshmi.

    It's more of a mixture of North as well as South Indian cuisine. Closed on Sundays and opened only for lunch on saturdays. There're certain days they serve Ala-Carte and on other days, it's buffett.

    The food really rocks. It's vegetarian though, but trust me, it's very nice.....a vast variety of dishes to choose from.

    As for the price, don't worry. You pay how much you want. Yep.
