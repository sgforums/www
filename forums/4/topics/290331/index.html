    turn on the lowest flame..

    pour in the seeds (don't pour in too many), put in butter and salt, must cover with a cover (if not later pop corn all over the kitchen ) ,

    after some times u'll hear and see new pop corns popping in the pot (if the pot cover is glass)..

    (rmb to use lowest flame) and quickly off the flame when u see the rdy pop corns (if continue will become chao dao liao)..
