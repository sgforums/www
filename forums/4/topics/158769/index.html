  • BillyBong
    15 Nov 05, 08:34
    Food court at level 4 has a large variety of food.

    Personally i would go for the Lam's noodles, hot-plate stall or homemade noodle stall.

    The fishball stall is also pretty good with huge fishballs.

    Forget Loy Kee (not so) Best chicken rice and screw the jap food (too ex and not worth it)

    For restaurants, try soup restaurant (samsui chicken is always a draw) and their herbal soup array (yummy ) or the Crystal Jade La Mian Xiao Long Bao.

    Avoid the basement food court unless you want a change in environment. Billy Bombers suck and so does that cafe at the far end of the 2nd level neat the escalator.

    Oh yeah, there's a seoul garden for those looking for a cheap bbq buffet.

    Lai Lai's roasted meat next to Popular book store is too pricy and not worth the portion they serve.

    Other than that, it's fair game. (expecially for the fast food franchises)
