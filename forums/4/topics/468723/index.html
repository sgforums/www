    Tim Ho Wan Singapore’s first charity event:

    • To be held on 28 April 2013, in conjunction with the official opening of the restaurant
    • 100% of proceeds will go to The Straits Times School Pocket Fund
    This one-day donation drive will begin at 10am and last till 10pm. And as a form of thank you for their donations, Tim Ho Wan will treat all Samaritans to its award-winning BBQ pork buns. 

    For a $2 donation, the donator will receive one bun. 
    The tier continues as follows:

    $2 - 1 Bao(s)
    $5 - 2 Bao(s)
    $10 - 3 Bao(s)
    $20 - 4 Bao(s)
    $30 - 5 Bao(s)
    $40 - 6 Bao(s)
    $50 - 7 Bao(s)
    $60 - 8 Bao(s)
    $80 - 9 Bao(s)
    $100 - 10 Bao(s)

    100% of the proceeds will go to The Straits Times School Pocket Fund and aid underprivileged kids in Singapore. So come on down and join us for a good cause!
    On top of all the proceeds donated by the public, for every $1 donation, Tim Ho Wan will also 'add' (æ·») $1 (on a 1:1 ratio) to the proceeds, capped at $8,888.

    The Chairman of Straits Times School Pocket Money Fund, Mr. Han Fook Kwang, will also be officiating the event, together with chef Mak and the other shareholders of Tim Ho Wan.
    The public is advised to take note that on 28 April 2013, due to the charity event, the dine-in area will only be open for business from 1.00pm onwards.
