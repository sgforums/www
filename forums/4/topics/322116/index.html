    Personally, i dont really like having steamboats indoors, its not that serious, but a cut off for me.

    Hmmm, as for the steamboat/ala-carte/fondue buffet, i found it overly expensive as the food (be it non-cook/cooked/fondue items) is rather little in variety. And you have to keep ordering for the cooked food as its super small in servings.Beverages are not included though.

    Its S$24.80Pax during weekends and hols. and i think S$19.80 during weekdays.

    For 2 adults will be likely S$65++ including drinks and other stuffs thats not included in the buffet.

    If you are not going for the buffet, the pricing range are superbly far.Its below S$10 for a single steamboat with fixed/ready prepared steamboat "food". Which is so little that you can hardly feel anything. Lol.. Hmmm.. And if your going for the fondue, i suggest you go Fullerton and pamper yourself with the Chocolate Buffet!!!!

    So, i would suggest you going HotPot Culture only if you can sit there for HOURS and dont mind eating the same few stuffs over and over again. You can try out all the available ala-carte tho. I believe its around 30+++ dishes. But the servings are small.

    For me, wont be likely to return unless its weekdays and with a group of non stop chit chatss....=DD..
