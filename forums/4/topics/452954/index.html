    why buy pesto? Why don't you make it yourself. Very easy. Here's the recipe:

    ·         45g (1/4 cup) pine nuts

    ·         1 1/2 cups fresh basil leaves

    ·         2 small garlic cloves, halved

    ·         60g (3/4 cup) shredded parmesan

    ·         5 tbs olive oil

    if i  if you can't find pine nuts, you can replace it with almond or walnuts. Make sure you toasted all your nuts, and dump all the ingredients to the food processor or blender. You'll get the best pesto. Hope this would help you.
