    Don't know got people post this place before here , anyway .....

    Location at Boon lay Hawker centre , a corner store just besides main road .

    The Nasi lemak store is name "Power" Nasi Lemak ..... really power !!!!

    Chilli sause is nice , chicken wings is freshly fried , hot and crispy , tender inside also .

    Rice is fragrance ...... cheap also , just $3.00 , filling !

    P/S : Must have patience and be ready to Q-up for at least behind 10 people at all time , 15 minutes wait . If you don't like Q-ing for nice food , don't go ! You'll be very angry .

    Full marks for their service . Always friendly & patient . Never crack under pressure .
