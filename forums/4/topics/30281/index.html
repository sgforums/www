    One fullerton has a variety of places to have a romantic dinner but it does get pricey.

    However since u have a generous budget of 150 bucks, that can cover a lotta places.

    One fullerton restaurants are mainly western cuisine so unless u enjoy such food, dun bother going. The view's nice though ard eveing time when the sun sets.

    Mandarin hotel has a couple of places that have good food under ur budget. Top of the M has a great view and some fantastic food while triple 3 is another buffet (value for money if u consider the choice of foods u get) on the 7th floor.

    Reservations are a must if u wanna go.

    Alternatively, u can go for a simple dinner and after that spend the evening at a jazz bar. Equinox springs to mind with it's wide variety of drinks, not to mention a city view with live music.
