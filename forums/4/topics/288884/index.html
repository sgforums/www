    Well, you can ask the waiter to recommend or just anyhow point at the menu.

    There was a French stall at Upp Serangoon Road. I went there once.

    The service was horrible. The waiter/waitress refuse to serve you. They were there for show only. You asked them for spoons, they asked that you to take it yourself.

    You must go and take your forks and spoons and whatever you want. No customer service at all.

    After the makan, you must go queue to make payment at the counter. The staff are just not friendly at all. They behaved like they are doing you a service by selling their food to you.

    Food wise, very small portion.

    It was my first and last time to eat at the French Stall.

    patgoh
