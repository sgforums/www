    AVA says chicken essence products here safe for consumption

    SINGAPORE: The Agri-Food and Veterinary Authority (AVA) said essence of chicken products in Singapore are safe for consumption.

    It said it conducts routine checks on imports of these products, which come mainly from Thailand and Malaysia.

    AVA was responding to queries from Channel NewsAsia after news reports of a recall in the US.

    The US distributor for Brand’s Essence of Chicken had recalled products there as the US Department of Agriculture’s Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) could not determine the source of the ingredients.

    Responding to the recall, Cerebos Singapore said the US recall was due to import compliance issues and not quality or safety.

    It said the products, which were made in its own manufacturing plants, were safe and conformed to international safety standards.

    Cerebos, which has been importing the product into the US for over 20 years since 1986, is working with authorities to resolve the compliance issues.
