    POH CHEU - SOON KUEH



    ADDRESS : Block 127 #01-230 Bukit Merah Lane 1
    OPENING HOURS : 8am - 6pm, closed on Sundays and public holidays
    PRICE : 50 cents (turnip, yam or chives filling), 60 cents (bamboo-shoot filling)

    The simply divine filling is practically bursting out of the fat soon kueh but what stands out is the ku chye (chives) kueh with paperthin springy skin. As you sink your teeth into it, take the time to enjoy the crunchy vegetables which look green and fresh. If you like yam, go for the yam kueh. This stall at the corner of a big coffeeshop in the Alexandra Village vicinity is also well known for its variety of ang ku kueh. Everything is fresh and handmade. The family of 10, including cousins, chip in to help out in this labour-intensive operation. At the helm is the matronly looking boss who started the business 20 years ago with her own recipe.
