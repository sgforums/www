    A listing of where to get your fix of chicken rice.

    Singapore, August 7, 2009 - Hainanese chicken rice is steamed white chicken served cold on warm white rice with a light soy sauce, sesame oil, and garlic-chilli dip, accompanied by a broth of chicken stock.

    The original Hainanese chicken rice was served with ground green chilli dip.

    Over time, lime was added to the chilli sauce.

    Singapore chefs also use only chicken stock unlike the Hainanese, who use both pork and chicken.

    Pre-fried in chicken fat and cooked in chicken broth, the rice has a rich flavour.

    The dish is said to have benn established in Middle Road, Purvis Street and Koek Road more than 60 years ago.

    CHUEN CHUEN CAFE
    WHERE:    17Upper East Coast Rd, Crescendo Building
    OPEN:9amto11pm
    CONTACT:6445 3164

    The Bugis St Chuen Chuen Chicken Rice was reputedly the first to add beansprouts and crystal chicken feet as sides to Hainanese chicken rice.

    Now,30 years later, they are still as popular as the main dish.

    Its trademark? Hot, zesty homemadegarlic-chilli sauce.
