    Adam Food Centre to be closed for 6 weeks for cyclical repairs

    SINGAPORE: The Adam Food Centre will be closed for cyclical repairs and redecoration works for six weeks from 18 February.

    The National Environment Agency said works carried out include repainting, cleaning the exhaust fans and ducts as well as repairing the toilet and light fittings.

    The repairs should be completed by 29 March.
