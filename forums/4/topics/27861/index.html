    Very Sorry To Say - but Jalan Kayu Prata is so over-rated, and is nothing more then a pain in the pocket with its shrinking size - and this cheating trend is growing from the number of copy cats following the same miserable size at a hefty price of 60 cents per piece. Everyone is being taken for a ride by the Jalan Kayu hype, and they expect everyone to be "kayu" to be thumped like the prata dough.

    The good old traditional Prata that tastes as it should, and of the size that its has always been can be found at places where the REAL Indians eat them.

    Corner of Geyland Lorong 5 and Sims Avenue - open 24 hours.

    Corner of Syed Alwi and Verdun Road - open 24 hours

    Around this area, you will find some real good and truly authentic northern Indian naan, chapati, prata, tosei, etc. etc. with curries, keema, etc. etc. etc. to go along with.
