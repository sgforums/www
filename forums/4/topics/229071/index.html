    Went there on Sat for both lunch and dinner...yummy food

    For lunch had sliced fish beehoon soup...this stall soup is not the creamy kind, so not that nice...but the fish quite fresh...

    Also tasted the supposedly very famous Chicken Chop Hor Fun..find that the chicken is the nicest ingredient for that dish...dun mind going there for another try...

    Also ordered Oyster Omelette...the oysters like a bit raw and bitter, but after you get used to the taste, it started to taste nice...

    For dinner had bbq stingray with sambal, bbq sotongs with sambal and also kangkong with sambal from the shop beside Xiang Xiang...also ordered Fried Hokkien Mee and Mee Goreng from xiang xiang...the hokkien mee is very nice Actually now craving for it

    Then for dessert...had this red tea jelly mixed with condensed milk and lychee and grounded ice...tasted like heaven esp after all the spicy stuff we had eaten...

    Then went back and downed nearly 2 bottles of beer...perfect ending to the last day of New Year...

    I think once I move into Spore I might put on a lot of weight...

    P/S: Wanted to order the famous nasi lemak from Sri Sujana? But were told they only take order
