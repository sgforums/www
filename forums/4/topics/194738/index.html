    Secret Recipe is one of the best cafes, the lamb stew set + cheesecake ($17) is really nice. if you like cheesecakes go here.

    Godiva at citylink mall: The chocolixer - dark chocolate - is $8 and goood. its a sweet icy thick chocolate drink with fine crushed dark chocolate and ice and even the whipped cream laced with chocolate has taste.

    SPANISH: the best place is The Tapas Tree at clarke quay riverside, all their food tastes really nice, try the spicy beef stew, ox tail stew, squid rings etc n c0cktails. Nice ambience too

    Bistro Delifrance: They have nice new desserts, like ice cream in warm whole cherries sauce, ice cream on waffle with strawberry sauce etc

    order pizza if yall are lazy to go out, or order pizza to be sent to whereever you are including the zoo: pizza hut's best tasting pizza imo is BBQ chicken supreme, and also get their 10 chicken drumlets

    VIETNAM: their beef noodles are the best tasting beef noodles imo. from a cafe opposite the road from bugis parco, opposite direction from bugis village. or another viet restaurant at the basement from a office building at 6 battery road.

    THAI: nothing is great to me except maybe the Phad Thai Noodles from Thai Express and nothing else from Thai Express.

    KOREAN: is what ive been eating and liking at food courts. tho i hardly eat at food courts or hawker

    the Taiwan Shan Chi Lin snack shop's XL crispy chicken and all their other food taste quite nice

    if you feel like having a hand in cooking yourself.. visit pepper lunch at Ngee Ann City lowest floor. if you go marina bay there, zheng fa huo hai xian is the best. the rest have a smaller spread and many have UNfresh seafood and sulfury crabs.

    If you want snack food in your home.. get some NTUC's marinated chicken wings and lasagne and scallop sushi and nissin seafood cup noodles

    and all the LADIES who like drinking alcohol, on Wed nights go to Este The Club (supposedly 25+ but actually 18+) for free freeflow of strong housepours. theres good entertainment like dancers/bands.
