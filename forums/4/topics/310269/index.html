    HILL STREET FRIED KWAY TEOW

    ADDRESS : Block 16 Bedok South Road #01-187 Bedok New Town & Food Centre
    OPENING HOURS 11am - 7pm, closed on Mondays
    PRICE $2, $2.50, $3


    Take just a bite of this char kway teow and you will understand why this stall gets so many accolades from newspapers and television shows. Robust and full of flavour, each plate of noodles comes with lots of egg, beansprouts, chives and, yes, lard bits. The dish may not be very healthy but the brother-and-sister team does try to keep the oil to a minimum. In business for over 40 years, their recipe and cooking method were passed down from their father. They say the secret lies in the strength of the flame. The lard bits and the chives chip in extra flavour too. Avoid going on weekends if you don't like to wait – the queue is always long.
