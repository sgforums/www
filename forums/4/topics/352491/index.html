    Actually, I did a bit more searching online the other day. Hakka soon kueh is more accurately known as "soon ban", and unlike normal soon kueh, which predominantly contains turnip, Hakka soon ban has other ingredients like mushroom, shrimp, tau kwa, black fungus etc. And also, the skin is yam-based and not made with rice flour.

    There's a Beef House along Syed Alwi Road that sells (supposedly) very good Hakka soon ban. I wonder if it's the place my ex-colleague bought it from (cos she mentioned Jalan Berseh, which is in the area).
