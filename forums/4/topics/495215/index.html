molten Chocolate Pie from KR McD is coming to SG

    The raving molten Chocolate Pie from McDonald’s Korea is coming to Singapore on March 1

     

    For those who have been keeping track of McDonald’s limited food offers around the world, then you will know there’s such a thing as the Chocolate Pie.

    First released in Korea, Bangkok and Japan, the caramel-crusted pie filled with hot molten chocolate is finally coming to Singapore on 1 March 2018! McDonald’s has also teased about the Chocolate Pie on their Facebook page earlier this morning so it’s definitely true.

    Kicking off the Chocolate Pie frenzy, McDonald’s will also be launching two new food items this Thursday, namely the Fish & Fries as well as Sweet Chilli Fish Burger.
