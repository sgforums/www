    Just finished my series on Tiong Bahru Market.

    The new market is missing some of the old favourites. So I was a little dissappointed. But still managed to shoot some of the recommended stalls there:

    1. Wanton Mee (Bryan Wong's show)
    2. Shark's meat Lor Mee (Also Bryan Wong's show)
    3. Chwee Kuey - still the same old stall
    4. Liang Liang Dessert stall - Ice Kachang Dinosaur!
    5. Lee Hong Kee Roasted Meat
    6. Tiong Bahru Boneless Hainanese Chicken Rice

    Missed the Mee Jian Kueh which is quite famous.

    If there are any favs there please highlight!
