    Food Tips > Dried Chicken Meat
    Recipe by: Recipe Team

    Dried Chicken Meat
    Yummy chicken "bak kwa" that will surely be everyone's favourite snack.
    Ingredients:
    500 gram chicken meat
    500 gram pork
    For Gravy: 1 1/4 tblsp.fish gravy
    175-200 gram sugar
    1/4 tsp.five spice powder
    1/2 tsp.saltpetre
    2 tblsp.water
    1 1/2 tblsp.soy sauce
    1/4 tsp.pepper
    2 tblsp.lard
    1/2 tblsp.rose wine

    Non Halal
    Category: Snack
    Preparation:

    Chop chicken meat and pork.
    Season the meat mixture with gravy. Arrange thin layer of meat onto a big flat plate with small holes (for ventilation) such that they become a big, thin layer of meat. Leave it to dry. When dry, cut it into squares (about 10 cm in length).
    Barbecue the square meat layers with burning charcoal until both sides are golden in colour. The barbecued slices can be stored in the freezer for up to several weeks.
