    Wisma Atria, I have not tried cos I don't frequent that place.
    My family loves Crystal Jade for the dim sum esp the liver & kidney congee,
    lotus rice, roast pork, siew mai. My favourite is the braised beef noodle & fresh carp congee. We prefer their dim sum to Tung Lok.

    So far I've been to Suntec, Plaza Sing, Great World City, Taka, Bugis, Scotts.
    The best is Taka cos they have HK goose noodle & specialty dishes not found in the other outlets. U have to book early cos the place is fully packed.

    Also tried their portugese buns at Bugis, La Mien & Korean outlets at Taka.
    - also not bad.
