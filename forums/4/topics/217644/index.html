    One of my favourite - Hao3 Zhou1 Dao4 at AMK Central - level 3 above 4 Season's Dept store. (Zhou1 as in porridge).

    About $13.90 for dinner on weekends. Weekday lunch is much cheaper.

    Got choice of porridge soup base, normal chicken or tomyam soup, and cooked snack, bee hoon, curry chicken, curry puff, etc. Raw food got fish, sotong, beef, pork, etc.

    Those who don't want to eat steamboat can order other a la carte food.
