    In the East, there's the infamous Fei Fei Wanton Mee at Joo Chiat Place, just beside Kim Choo dumpling store. Everyone says it's nice nice nice but personally I feel that it's nowhere near nice. There's no fixed standard e.g. sometimes the soup can be too bland or too salty and sometimes the number of wantons the stall owner gives is not fixed. She can give like only TWO wantons per bowl..

    Another one would be at Dunman Foodcourt. It's newly renovated, bright and still warm But the wanton mee there is really nice, and served in those traditional chicken bowls. But you have to wait for up to an hour for your order to be served, cos it's so damn popular!
