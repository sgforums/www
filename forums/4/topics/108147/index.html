    There was a clicky in my Yahoo logged-in page which always gives the "pointless information of the day" thing.

    Today's was a "is McD's fish sandwich made of fish?"

    Well it is... but here's something people don't know... this:

    The caloric ratio of the sandwich is 46% fat, 39% carbs, and just 15% protein. In addition to the "foaming agent" dimethylpolysiloxane, several partially hydrogenated oils, which produce trans fat.

    Took me by surprise how much fat was in the "fish"... 46% fat!

    This made me wonder what the heck is in other stuff.. did a search and I got this:

    Chicken McNuggets - 54% fat
    Quarter Pounder - 45% fat
    Sausage McMuffin - 53% fat
    Cheese hamburger - 40% fat

    scary isn't it?

    So, remember... when you're chowing down on seemingly "healthier chicken nuggets", it's 54% fat you're eating
