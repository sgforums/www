    Anyone eaten any meals from the new KFC breakfast meals? I just tried the "a.m Twister Meal (Original Recipe)

     

    a.m Twister Meal (Original Recipe)

    Ingredients:     Diced Original Recipe Chicken fillet, scrambled eggs, chopped tomatoes, cheddar cheese and mayonnaise all wrapped in a tortilla and toasted to perfection. Served with a delectable a.m Honey Biscuit and hot coffee/tea.

    My review: I bought the ala carte one and did not manage to try the Honey Biscuit.. The twister meal tastes like the Cheesy BBQ Meltz in stores for lunchtime.. The chicken, diced tomatoes are all the same of what you see in the Meltz. The only difference is that this one is added with Mayo and Scrambled eggs. The mayo is not so thick and the scrambled eggs is just an addition to the softness and does not taste much but is still not bad. Improvements can be done if more mayo would be added. And the tortilla  tastes like original prata so there ain't much. Similarities to the Meltz are 65% alike. Next up I will be trying the a.m Twister Meal (American) differences will be that the chicken, tomatoes and mayo will be replaced by turkey bacon, hashbrown and cheese sauce.

    Overall: 8/10
    Worth a try eating.
    it is approx. 20cm long just fyi.
