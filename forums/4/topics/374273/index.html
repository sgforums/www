    International buffet at The Line, Shangri-la hotel

    This hip and modish mega-restaurant serves what must be the biggest, richest and most diverse buffet in Singapore.

    Huge dinning area, featuring very stylish - contemporary décor, with clean lines,  light wood parquet, charismatic fire-colored panels and an open-kitchen that allows you to see the chefs in action... there is also an 'alfresco' dinning area.

    16 serving stations are scattered across the large restaurant (which means that none of them gets too packed...), serving an unbelievable variety of fresh, luscious fare from all over the world:  from the must-have seafood station, with a very decent range of fresh crustaceans, oysters and mussels to cold appetizers, sushi and salads,  and from fresh pastas and pizzas at the Italian corner to delicate Indian tandoori meats... everything is fresh and delicious (what's more... there are also some 'cook on the spot' stations).

    Prices are really "reasonable" for such a splurge
    Daily for lunch and dinner

    Shangri-la hotel, 22 Orange Grove Road
    Phone: 6213 4275
