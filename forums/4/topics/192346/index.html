    There is only one tepenyaki worth trying ... Shima at Goodwood Park Hotel. Go for the set lunch/buffet whcih also include unlimited salmon sushimi, it's about $40. Dinner is not worth it as it's overly pricey. After the lunch, you adjourned to another section, away from the smoke for your coffee and ice cream(included in the price), and may sit there all afternoon long admiring the views of Scotts/Orchard - nice embience.

    Another place is at Jurong Hilltop(besides the Jurong Bird Park), and you need a car to get to the top. Quality wise, I wld go for Shima anytime. The place is also dimmer and older.
