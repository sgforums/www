    Here's my review on menotti:

    Just had dinner at Menotti tonight, had the following:
    - Deep Fried Calamari with spicy sauce ($11.50)
    - Seafood Stew ($22)
    - Veal Scappalone ($22)
    - Mango Gelato Milk Shake ($6)

    Calamari was good. Just right not rubbery
    Seafood Stew was great, but its not enough for a main course
    Veal with Mash Potato tasted like pork chop, but more tender. Not too bad, but not that good either
    Mango Gelato Milk Shake was normal

    Service a bit rushed, placed a bit overcrowded (they really know how to make use of space)

    Food: 7/10
    Service: 6.5/10 (Not too attentive)
    Ambience: 6.5/10(Too crowded, squeezed too many tables in one small place)
