Dining At La Cantina in Venezia

  • Blackswan
    18 May 09, 20:54

    If you're looking for a an idyllic hideaway, off-the-city dining experience, check out the chic La Cantina in Venezia Italian restaurant situated at the Changi Village Hotel. Located at the hotel’s top floor, be greeted by the outdoor swimming pool & deck chairs amidst the lush tropical greenery of laid back Changi Village. Upon entering the restaurant, the charming décor & simple floor-to-ceiling glass panels transport you into a gracious haven of Venetian living. A must try is the romantic balcony dining that overlooks our island’s Eastern shoreline & the Changi’s golf course, which is a perfect venue for sunset cocktails & candle-lit dinners.
