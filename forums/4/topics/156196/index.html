    The pasta one goes something like this, or what I can remember:

    Ingredients:
    1) cooked pasta (cockscrew design)
    2) mixed vege (peas, carrots, sweet corn)
    3) milk or milk powder and water (?)

    (salt and pepper needed??)

    Method:
    - Cook the above in a pan, mix well.
    - Pour contents into an oven-safe dish, top with strips of kraft cheese (looks like cheddar)
    - Bake at 180 deg C for 20 mins until golden brown

    The end result is like gratin. Good luck!
