    For god's sake you don't need to go to a class to learn how to cook bloody carbonara!!
    I can teach u right here!
    Ingredients: black pepper
    cheddar cheese by the block (grate it)
    parmasan cheese (however u spell it)
    paresely (however u spell it)
    streaky/back bacon (chopped up into bits)
    salt
    1 pack of white button mushrooms (campbells)
    pasta (anything u want; preferably spagehtti or fettcine; however the heck u spell it)
    extra virgin olive oil (borges)
    1 litre of milk
    corn flour (IMPORTANT)

    Ok grate the cheese, u need about 2 or 3 handfuls of cheese all grated, chop the bacon, mash the parsely, cut the mushrooms.

    Cook your pasta according to directions on packet... using pot and water and salty water with some olive oil added, boil pasta until al dente.

    In frying pan, fry the bacon and mushrooms until done, set aside on plate.

    ok in 1 small bowl... add 2 or 3 teaspoons of corn flour, add some milk,,,, stir and stir and stir until got no lumps... set aside...

    ok in 1 pot, add 4 or 5 cups of milk, bring to boil, stir and stir,,,, then add your grated cheese... stir some more.... then add the corn flour milk mix, (SPECIAL NOTE: stir the bowl of corn flour mix 1 final time before u add in) then continue stirring... the sauce will then thicken....keep stirring for abt another 3 mins on small heat... until the sauce bubbles slightly....then finally add salt and pepper and your friend mushrooms and bacon....

    Then put noodle on plate....add the sauce and sprinkle parsely and pepper to make it look POWER...

    CAN ALREADY!
