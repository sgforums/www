    The Altivo

    I've been there on several occasions.

    Say if you're going with a date - a good idea would be cable car ride from Harbour Front to Mount Faber - then walk a few steps right up to the place (Call in advance to make a reservation at the cushion seat if possible)

    Price wise - it depends on whether you're planning to dine or just going for drinks. If you're dining and not having any wine, you'd be looking at around 50 - 80 per person, top it up with a few beer and cocktails to wash down the food... (I don't know bout you, but I'm quite a big drinker) Total bill can hit up to 200.

    But if you're conventional-no-nonsense-not-too-many-drinks kinda person, then a safe estimate would be around $100.

     

    P.S. Place is pretty inaccessible, you really don't want to be stucked at the top of the hill with no vehicle... So 1) Either have your own ride 2) Borrow your folks' ride 3) Be in time for the last cable car ride <-- Can't remember what time though
