    United Nations Students' Association (UNSA - SMU) is a student organization whose primary aim is to promote the objectives of the United Nations. Established in 2002 with the support of its parent organization, United Nations Association of Singapore (UNAS), UNSA-SMU operates by educating members on various societal issues of concern, while promoting peace, human rights, respect for international law, social progress and larger freedom. To give relevance and substance to its activities as well as to promote the ideals and the potentials of the United Nations, the Organization works frequently with youths, NGOs and other civic organizations in Singapore.
