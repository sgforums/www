    recipe for scrambled egg.

    2 eggs.
    beat it.
    heat pan.
    pour oil on pan.
    let oil heat up.
    pour eggs in.
    after 5 secs, flip the outer edge and fold the egg into half, (some uncooked egg will spill, ignore), after another 5 secs, flip it into half again, leave for another 5 secs. done. a quarter raw scrambled egg. should look yellowy inside and slightly brown inside.
