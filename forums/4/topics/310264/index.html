Lobster noodles for just $10?

    Celebrating its 10th anniversary, Rendezvous Hotel Singapore has cooked up two exciting creations to whet your appetite - lobster noodles and dragon cocktail.

    The first is Crispy Baby Lobster served with Wok-Fried Curry Glass Noodles. Both the lobster and noodles are synonymous with longevity and good fortune in Chinese traditions.

    This special dish will be served throughout the year at the hotel's Straits Cafe at a too-good-to-miss S$10++. Usually such a dish could cost as much as S$36.

    Prices are subject to 10 per cent service charge and prevailing government taxes.

    Dance of the Dragon (Dragon Cocktail)

    As a strong supporter of the arts, Rendezvous Hotel Singapore has worked with the Singapore Dance Theatre in giving F & B perks to their members and dancers in the past two years.

    Special discounts offered to them have enticed them to meet and bond at the hotel over food and drinks. So Rendezvous Hotel has concocted a Dance of the Dragon cocktail to promote its 10th Anniversary as well as Singapore Dance Theatre's 20th Anniversary.

    It comprises mango juice, topped with champagne and a dash of creme de casis. Both parties hope the drink will reflect their efforts in promoting arts and culture here.

    For S$16.80 ++ per drink, complete with dragon fruit and gooseberry pearl garnishes, Dance of the Dragon cocktail is available at three of the hotel's F & B outlets - Straits Cafe, Courtyard and Palong Bar.

    Hope you enjoy yourselves at this special bargain.
