MA MAISON @ BUGIS HAS GREAT SET LUNCHES GO EAT THERE

  • neverendingtrauma
    1 Jul 06, 00:34
    located at 2nd floor of bugis parco. ma maison which means my home in french serves mostly jap-french fusion food.

    cheap prices of $15 per person for a tasty set lunch. nice homely dim interior with much decor on walls.

    their set lunches come with jap dressing salad, soup, 5 choices of main dish (i chose curry rice with chicken and my gf chose mushroom white wine cream sauce chicken chop with rice), then drinks or ice cream.

    i had a good filling lunch, tasty chicken curry rice, i rate it 9/10. she also liked her meal and we got a extra bowl of the mushroom cream sauce.

    they have a varied ala cart menu too including seafood, steak, spaghetti, paella, desserts etc.

    but i'd recommend you to go for their set lunches i pass by bugis every other day and i finally know a good cafe to have our lunches.

    other places i like are secret recipe's lamb stew n cheesecake, via veneto at clarke quay's pasta calamari n tiramisu, the tapas tree's spicy beef stew n ox tail stew, for chocolate menotti and godiva, and for buffet dragons gate at habourfront tower lvl 4. thats another great place!
