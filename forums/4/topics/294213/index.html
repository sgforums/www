    The brown sauce I make which my husband loves
    I'm not sure if your wife will like it though

    Sauteed Yellow Onion(Sliced thinly) in butter till brown
    Pour in Sherry (Use Red Wine if you don't have sherry)
    Mix in about 1/4 cognac of what you use for the sherry
    Pour in Worcestershire Sauce
    Dijion Mustard
    Some Soy Sauce
    Add a little cream or more butter for a richer flavor

    Add a little cornstarch if you want for it to be thicker

    Btw I don't have a recipe coz I cook by feel >.<
    He always drinks the sauce up like soup >.< or use the bread to soak it up
    Oh and the sauce taste better if you add in the steak juices to it
