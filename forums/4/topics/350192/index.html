    Another fascinating Japanese product! :D

    This is comfort food! Even before you sink your teeth into it!

    Introducing... NISSIN CHICKEN RAMEN!

    This is one of my favorite instant noodles. I like the cute chicky, and I like the bouncy texture of the noodles. I like how you just cook the noodles without having to add any seasoning (it's all in the noodles, so you can even eat it like a mamee snack!). I also like how you can choose whether you want to soak it in hot water for 3 minutes or cook it in boiling water for 1 min (look at bottom picture). And, there's an indentation (?) in the middle of the noodle block for your egg yolk to settle nicely (once again, bottom picture)!

    You can get individual packs (from Meidi-Ya) and bundled packs from most supermarkets, and Meidi-Ya (not sure about other supermarkets) also carry the mini-sized version (think Maggi Mee-in-My-Mug)!

    I even have a tee shirt for this! xD
