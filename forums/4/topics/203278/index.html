    I'll start off!

    Erm, actually I'm far from a connoisseur of coffee.

    But I do love my cuppa!

    My favorite is actually kopitiam coffee, because I have a lousy palate and need something as strong as Asian coffee to stimulate those buddies on my tongue. I love brewed coffee! I haven't found any coffee places serving brewed coffee that don't taste like ditchwater. So save me if anyone knows of any place with good, strong flavors!

    At home... at home... I have a variety of coffee ranging from instant 3-in-1s to flavored ground coffee from the Coffee Bean & Tea Leaf and coffee beans from Indonesia.

    Usually I make myself a cup of low-sugar instant coffee with half a teaspoon of Blue Mountain coffee granules (that don't taste anything like good Blue Mountain).

    When I feel like I've got nothing better to do, I'll whip out the good ol' coffee sock and brew some myself.

    I think coffee is best drunk "raw" without sugar, or with as little sugar as possible.

    But you know, when I started out with Starbucks, I used to order Iced Caffe Americano with loads of syrup.
