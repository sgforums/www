    Tuna/shredded chicken sandwich.

    Ingredients:

    Sunshine high fibre bread or burger.
    MMI tuna chunks in water (can't seem to get it anymore in NTUC)
    or
    shredded chicken
    tomatoes
    lettuce
    cheese slice
    Meadow Lee Margarine

    Steps:

    1) Mix shredded chicken/tuna with Best Food or Kraft Mayonnaise and pepper.
    2) Toast 2 pieces of bread
    3) Take out bread when done, spread margarine
    4) spread shredded chicken/tune on bread
    5) add lettuce
    6) add tomatoes
    7) add cheese
    serve
