    Egg sandwich

    Ingredients: eggs, mayonaise, bread, salt and pepper.

    Method:

    1) Boil eggs for 15-20 mins.

    2) Remove egg shells and smash eggs.

    3) Add mayonaise, salt and pepper and stir.

    4) Put mixture on bread and serve.

    Merry Meet Dear FireIce,

    This is my Special Classic Sandwich. To show my support for your thread... Hahaha

    You will need:

    1 ~ Luncheon Meat/ hotdogs
    2 ~ Cheese Slice
    3 ~ Oregano Herb( fresh or those Powdered ones is fine)
    4 ~ Grinded Black Pepper ( chuncky not those fine ones)
    5 ~ Scrambled Egg
    6 ~ Tomatoes, cucumbers & Lettuce (Whatever you like)
    7 ~ Mushroom ( can ones will be fine)
    8 ~ Wholemeal Bread (personal preference but will got well with any bread, from Bagel, ciabatta to simply white bread)

    Instruction:

    Prepare Scrambled eggs. Put a piece of Cheese on a slice of Wholemeal Bread, sliced Luncheon Meat/ hotdogs on the cheese, a dash of Oregano & Black Pepper to gether with sliced mushrooms cover with another piece of Wholemeal Bread. Put into Oven @ 150 degrees for 3 min or set mid heat in Microwave for 3 min, what we want is the bread to be abit crisp & melt the cheese only. Take off the top slice of bread put scrambled eggs, Tomatoes, cucumbers & Lettuce (Whatever you like ~ optional: Salad sauce or mayo)! Delicious sandwich is done. Feel free to invent your own recipes. Have fun!



    Blessed Be,
    Oceanos
