COTTAGE waffle place @ suntec city

  • jos_hua
    10 Jul 07, 00:54


    Was with my girlfriend last week at Suntec and chanced upon this recently opened small outlet selling waffles and dryers ice cream. Its run by 2 young people and they are very friendly & have great service.
    They serve waffles with fillings such as tuna, durian, chocolate, or just plain. I remember there are more than 15 different choices.
    The waffle texture is crispy on the outside & soft in the inside. I got 3 waffles at only $6 nett (maple honey, hazel nut chocolate and durian) which I find is a pretty good deal. The servings are large enough and the fillings are plentiful enough to let you taste them in every bite.

    They also serve coffee & drinks, and the waffle combo comes with drinks plus ice cream. They have also eeked out a small cough sitting area & 2 small tables for you to dine in.

    They are located at the 3rd floor of Suntec. After Toys R Us, you walk towards the carrefour side and you will pass a toys model shop on your left. Then you can turn inside the toys model shop and you can see them. The name is called Cottage Place Waffle.

    Please get a chance and check them out. I always try to support our local young people in striking it out on their own. Cheers
