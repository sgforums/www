    The Japanese Restaurant "Fiesta" offers some quality and "value for money" Japanese Food.

    Choice of dining at the counter, and watch all the various sushi and sashimi dishes going pass you as you dine, as an ingeniously designed mini conveyor belt is continuously replenished with new and fresh preparations.

    One is at the ground floor of HDB Hub at Toa Payoh (next to the McDonald's), and the other is at Jurong Point Basement.

    Great tasting fat noodles, wonderful rice, and fantastic salmon and tuna sushi and sashimi.

    Eat till you are stuffed, and the price for two averages between $42 to $52 - (depending on what you order).
