    Wonder how many love to eat this oily dish. I love the chai tao kway at pek kio/cambridge food centre. Nice and cheap. During weekdays it is the old man frying the chai tao kway, the son is helping him over the weekends. There are both black and white chai tao kway. The black one is more popular.

    Black one really tastes yummy! It is cheap from $1`, $1.50 to $3.00. $1 is with one egg, $1.50 with 2 eggs. This tastes great! It was featured in one of the food shows on TV.

    Queue is usually long.
