    Hi BillyBong, feeling hungry so looking into this section for food tips.

    Tung Luk serves authentic Hongkong style and Hongkong quality cuisines, as every one of their many kitchens are manned by Hongkong chefs.

    The Group operates with a Central Office that has a Chief Chef, who will ensure all the Tung Luk Restaurants anywhere will serve the same high quality food, and according to the same cooking standards set by the boss - who is from Hongkong.

    Very high quality food, at slightly below the price of similar restaurants operated at 5-star Hotels.
