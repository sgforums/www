    Just curious why Marche is so popular.
    I have been there about 3x myself and gave up going since due to the following reasons:

    (1) Food
    Nothing fantastic, overly priced. Whatever they have there can be found outside at better prices and with better quality. The food used to be cheaper, over the years, I found the prices were adjusted upwards discreetly.

    (2) Queuing
    This is something I don't quite understand. Singaporean's love queuing is it? In Marche, literally have to queue 3 times just for a meal - Queue outside, Queue for food, Queue for payment. And we still go?

    (3) Ambience
    Not much ambience to speak of, usually noisy like a marketplace, unless you mean you love going to the market?
