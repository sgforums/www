    2 Jan 05, 00:27
    Vienna is over-rated.

    Sampled the food there recently and found it very normal.

    Admittedly that while they have a generous serving of crayfish, crabs, sashimi & lovely soups, their teppanyakki is poor by comparisons, their desert variety is very limited and never sufficient.

    Ice cream was finished and the captain said there 'wasn't anymore refills'. Same for the blueberry cheesecake. Funny i tot this was a buffet style dine-in.

    They twice got my teppanyakki order wrong even with the labelling system in place and for an 'internationally-named' restaurant, had great difficulty understanding my England.

    Also noticed that almost all the waiters and cooks were Chinese nationals. Makes you wonder why use the name 'Vienna'.

    Overall, out of 10, i rate it a 5.5
