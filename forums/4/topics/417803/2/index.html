    there is this restaurant in Esplanade... Which sells really good beef. Its known as Barossa.

    You can actually enjoy wonderfully cooked beef, buffet style for only 40++ per pax. The buffet is only available on certain days, and its really worth a try.

    Angus beef, wagyu beef, T-bone steak... damn delicious.

    However, you get quite full after 2 serving, so imagine the size of the beef. Go with several friends and each of you order a type of beef and share.. that way you get to taste more.

    Its really worth it, and definitely not to be missed.
