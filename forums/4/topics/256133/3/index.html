    Food republic is beginning to relate quality food with higher prices. Unfortunately, after the initial euphoria that accompanied their Wisma outlet, their subsequent VIVO and Suntec branches churned out less than satisfied reviews.

    Of the food court chains, they are currently the priciest, with half a chicken at 'Sergeant Chicken Rice' going for $14/-, compared to the average $10/- at a standard hawker stall and $12/- at a branded chicken rice restaurant. Furthermore, their supposed 'boneless' chicken still leaves some leftovers you have to personally remove. Not at all satisfying, given that the chicken is quite ordinary (but still better than Loy Kee best).

    Maybe the 'General' should stop by and give them a good hidding.
