    I find the mango/strawberry Ice a rip off....just some toppings, a miserly ice top, evaporated milk, flavouring and a WHOLE LOT OF ICE!

    The pork chop rice was ok, taste was good, but again rather pricy for just some egg, tomatoes, cucumber, baby cailan and one porkchop. I expect it will be the same for the rest.

    The noodles really looks like (or maybe IS) instant noodles standard, with luncheon meat, egg and some veg. And all that cost $5+.

    Haven't tried their stir-fried dishes like beef noodles and fried rice, probably those will be better, but they are also higher priced at $7+ .

    The Shrimp Paste chicken was good, a lot of tenderiser though...

    All in all, an expensive place to eat for the portion you get. Also they should strike out Service Charge since they hardly do anything. (water is 40 cents by the way and no refills)
