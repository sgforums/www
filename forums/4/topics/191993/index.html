    Well, I'd like to say something.
    I came from Shanghai, have eaten Chinese food since my birth
    Actually, Crystal Jade and Ding Tai Feng are different style.
    CJ serve Hong Kong style food, from Ding Sum to main courses. Hong Kong is famous for its various range of food, also great taste.
    Ding Tai Feng is a Taiwanese restaurant. Their style is typical Taiwan, simple and light.
    That's the difference.
    Ding Tai Feng in Shanghai is full of Taiwan business people. Shanghai people are also not big of fan of Ding Tai Feng.
    Comparatively, Hong Kong restaurant in Shanghai is much more popular.
