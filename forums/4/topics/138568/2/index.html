    Originally posted by thinger:
    how does one go about doing butter or teriyaki?
    Butter Chicken:

    1. Thaw chicken
    2. Marinate with margarine or hard butter.
    3. Garnish with salt and pepper
    4. Stir fry with garlic and ginger (alternatively u can also cook in an oven, use aluminium foil and turn the chicken every once in a while(do not wrap) )

    Terriyakki Style

    1. Buy T-sauce
    2. Thaw and marinate chicken
    3. Stir-fry with ginger and garlic, add water and corn flour
