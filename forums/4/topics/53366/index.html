    Fantastic value and quality "Teochew Muay" can be found at Toa Payoh Lorong 5, Block 75 Food Court - which can be seen from Lorong 5.

    This stall only operate during lunch hour, and be prepared for a long waiting line of self-service customers, as only an elderly husband and wife team operate this stall.

    Waiting time about 15 to 20 minutes in the line, so if you are in a hurry or only have a short lunch break with long travelling time, you may as well try it during non-working days.

    A meal for 4, with separate dishes portioned for 4, consisting of - large pig intestines, lean pork meat (kong bak), fish meat cake, braised chicken feet, salted vegetables, dried bean curd (tau pok), 5 bowls of porridge and 2 bowls of nice fluffy rice - all cost only $9.70.

    Remember to call the lady to collect payment AFTER your meal, as they are so short-handed, they allow you to enjoy your meal first, and hand you with a perfect sur-price after a high quality meal.

    No hassle parking.
