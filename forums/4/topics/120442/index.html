    Have just revisited this cafe on #4 Stanley Street (road between telok Ayer

    street and cecil street)

    Tried a tuna sandwich set which comes with a freshly brewed cup of coffee for

    $7.50. The sandwich is very substantial and tasty with fresh vegetables on a

    toasted ciabatta bread.

    They also have salmon, chicken, turkey, ham and egg sandwiches. Muesli and

    banana yoghurt etc etc.

    Good bunch of young staff always smiling and joking. They also offer breakfast

    sets for $3.90 - egg sandwich with coffee , where to find ????

    But what really stands out is their coffee, imported from holland and made to

    perfection.. Their cappucino Rocks man..... must try

    They also are the official coffee of the Ajax football club served in their

    stadium.
