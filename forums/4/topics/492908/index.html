Nespresso lowers prices of coffee capsules

  • FireIce
    2 Nov 16, 16:13

    When a new Nespresso boutique opens in Raffles City on Wednesday (Nov 2), the Grand Cru coffee capsules on the menu will be cheaper.

    The brand is adjusting prices of its coffee ranges downwards in Singapore, in its boutiques and on its website, it said in a press release.

    For instance, Kazaar and Dharkan capsules that were $1.07 each are now 76 cents each, Espresso capsules that were 91 cents each are now 68 cents each, and Pure Origin capsules that were $1.07 each are now 76 cents each, it said.

    The Lungo range will be priced at 72 cents per capsule, instead of 99 cents; the Variations range will be priced at 82 cents, instead of $1.15; and the limited edition range will be priced at 88 cents, instead of $1.20.
