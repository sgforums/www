    Buses: 4N, 17

    • MRT: Simei MRT
    • - You need to take 2 different services (bus no: 9 & 17) to reach your destination.

    • - Alight at Simei Ave, opp Blk 3012, which is the 3rd stop to change bus
    • - You need to walk to Simei Ave, opp Blk 3012 and take bus no. 17
