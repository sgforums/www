    my friends told me that without trying the sashimi there in brunei, it's as if you hadnt set foot in brunei at all! couldnt believe until i set foot there..


    operating hours as above (with 2 lizards as cameos in my photo lol)


    train express loaded with dishes!


    one of the private rooms available. (too bad cant take photo of the surrounding cos i only took it before i left and its pretty untidy lol)


    top left - sashimi. it's so thick that you cant resist the sight of it and you just wanna put it into your mouth! $15 is damn worth it!
    top right - unagi tofu. tastes damn godly and a must for unagi lovers!
    bottom left - cheese salmon sushi. ummm... anything to do with sashimi and i just cant resist!
    bottom right - japanese mushroom. it's addictive!


    this is how thick the sashimi pieces are if you wanna have a good look.


    sashimi with fish egg sushi. mmm


    fried salmon skin - super addictive! simply love this.


    tempura prawn - crispy and juicy!


    softshell crab - freaking awesome especially with the sauce!


    beef stick - for those who want a craving for beef.


    phoenix dish - new dish but simply owns! looks small on picture but...


    i cant even eat the whole sushi into the mouth! requires 3 bites at least to finish it all. and theres LOBSTER MEAT in the head! $10 for this dish! CHEAP AND NICE! last but not least...


    TOTAL OWNAGE! i dare to say you have never set foot into brunei before if you hadnt tried this! servings there are more generous as compared to restaurants in sg!

    spent about $25 a person, and it's fricking worth it!
