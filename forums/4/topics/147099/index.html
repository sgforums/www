    Chicken Nagi Cheese

    Chicken drumstick meat is seasoned with light soya sauce , sake , ginger juice

    seasoned meat is then coated with tempura flour and deepfried

    when cooked , the meat is arranged on a plate topped with cheese and put in a microwave for a min or so to melt the cheese .. when its done , mayo is drizzle like a net shape over the cheese and chicken ... seaweed is then topped as garnish.

    The cheese and mayo is guess shld be western element and the chickin is actually Tori Karage but fried with a diff flour and the seaweed both which is Jap culture..

    Natto Omellette

    3 eggs and a pack of Natto ( Jap fermetlted beans ) is beat together.. this is then cooked on a pan while flipping . knocking constanly to achive a nice oval shape... it is serve on a plate and mayo and BBQ sauce is drizzle over it in a net pattern and finally topped with dry bonito fish flakes

    western - BBQ sauce / Mayo
    Jap - Natto / bonito fish flakes
