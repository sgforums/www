    I had ate many chicken rice around Singapore.

    My top chicken rice list:

    1) Ghim Moh - Dong Feng Fa (CHicken is smooth, tranasparent fat, Gravy nice)

    2) East Coast Road - Xing Ho Hainanese Chicken

    3) Blk 273 Bukit Batok East - Always close very early around 8pm - long que

    4) Selegie - Xing Ho Hainanese Chicken

    5) Boon Tong Ji - Balestier

    6) Bishan Central Chicken RIce - The chicken is very smooth and soup sour which i like. I dislike cos it is rather cold cos they keep the chicken inside the cold water. But the fat is lock and become transparent, Chilli so so. Chicken taste left a bit. BUt one thing is very long quece

    I ever eat hougang south there before. Their design look delicious but the chilli failed.

    If you want super hot chili.

    Five Star Chicken Rice - Upper BUkit Timah (Opposite Beauty World)
