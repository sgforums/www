    Every ONE Can Make Poverty History! Every ONE Can feeds someone in need. Each and every ONE of us can make a difference!”

    Join us on Saturday 17 October for an “Every ONE Can” warehouse sale at food distributer Food Xervices, located across from the railway station on Keppel Road. Buy food items at discounted prices for donation to the campaign (and pick up a few things for yourself at wholesale prices too). Proceeds will be distributed by Food From The Heart.

    Plus there will be music by Jack N Rai and The Pervy Boys, celebrity appearances and balloon sculptures and popcorn for the kids (and young at heart;)

    We'll also have Stand Up moments during the day.

    Free shuttle service from Harbourfront MRT (near VIVO city) to the warehouse sale every 30mins, on the hour and half-hour, from 10am - 5:30 pm. Return shuttles on the quarter-hour. The pick up point at Harbourfront is the BUS STOP outside the Harbour Front MRT station OPPOSITE VIVO CITY.

    Event ends at 6pm today!
