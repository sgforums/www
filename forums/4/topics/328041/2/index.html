    OK, OK, Jerry.   Jerry, jerry yan has new song, nice!   

    Get ready 12 chicken wings.

    Preparation A:

    1) Ginger 4 slices

    2) Water 500 ml

    3) Salt - 1 and half teaspoon

    4) Sugar - 1 teaspoon

    5) Chicken powder - 1 teaspoon

    6)  Hua tiao wine - 150 ml  (you can also add rice wine if you like)

    7)  Pour all above ingredients into the 500 ml water and boil.

    8)  After boiling, leave this aside for cooling

    Preparation B:

    Marinate chicken wings with:

    1) Sugar, chicken powder and salt for at least 15 mins.

    After marinating:

    1) Boil water

    2) Pour chicken wings for cooking.

    3) After cooking, place chicken wings aside for cooling

    ---------------------------------------

    After cooling the chicken wings, pour them into Preparation A (must be cool by now).   Leave this in the fridge for 6 hours or more if you like.   Thereafter, you have yummy yummy chicken wings to makan.   Hope you and family like this.

    *  Please note that you are feel to add the amount of ingredients as you deem fit.
