    There are a few that I use to frequent.

    1) Blk 226A Ang Mo Kio Ave 3 Market. Ru Lai Vegetarian Food. Cheap and good.

    2) Blk 356 Hougang. Along Upp Serangoon Road. Da Zhi Ran. Roti Prata. (Tokkong)

    3) Blk 501 Hougang. La Mian. Curry flavour. Shiok!

    4) Along Eu Tong Sen Street. Just after Keong Siak Street. Just Green. Good deco and ambient. Tim Sum, wah lao, Best!

    5) Sin Min Estate, Blk ?? Opp ROV. Ru Lai Vegetarian Cheap & Good.

    6) Campass Point. Ba Xian. Yong Tau Foo.

    7) Blk 128 Ang Mo Kio Ave 3. Shop house. "Duck rice", noodles, etc mo tuck teng.

    Ang Mo Kio Ave 10, Hawker Centre. Kang Lau Mian, Lu Mian. Good gravy.
