    This is a "steamboat only" coffeshop /restaurant that has a long histroy since the 1970s , located @ the basement in Golden Mile plaza along beach rd ( if can't locate ask the folks around there they sure to be able to tell u the directions - can't really see the rest from outside the bldg - quite old bld & @ nite quite dim)

    Pls be sure to be there @ 6 pm or b/4 esp. during weekend if not hv to Q quite long , a quite different experience - not the usual coffeshop steamboat u get . They are very famous among " Old Birds " asked yr Uncle or anuties they may know this place.

    They serve beef, cockles, eggs, Tang Oh, chicken rice, Hainese chicken, pig "spares", pork, prawns , satay.

    The coffee is quite good - Hainese coffee & Chicken rice are the best here. Of course steamboat is good too with a flavour of the good old days - check out the dinning atmosphere there then u 'll understand what I meant --- seems to be in a total different world.

    I think average per person is abt $10 - $12 max.

    I can't remember the name but there;s only one there .

    Happy dinning !
