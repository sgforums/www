    Hi guys!

    No authentic Oktoberfest party is complete without the world's most popular German wheatbeer! With celebrations that are bigger and better than last year, there is an Erdinger Oktoberfest celebration to suit every taste and palate!

    The celebrations kick-off at the Swiss Club on 30 September where Erdinger is the Official Beer. The Swiss Club Oktoberfest is one of the highlights of the local festive calendar, renowned for its unique atmosphere, sheer scale and fresh Erdinger Draft beer!

    Meanwhile, Erdinger Oktoberfest enthusiasts can also enjoy celebrations at over 200 bars and pubs around Singapore. The likes of Timbre, Durty Nelly's, Muddy Murphy's and Beach Cabana have special celebrations in store, so head down, today!

    As an added treat, the Erdinger Oktoberfest celebrations can be enjoyed at home, too! An exclusive Oktoberfest pack consisting of bottles of Erdinger Weissbier, Erdinger Weissbier Dunkel and Erdinger drinkware is available at leading supermarkets and hypermarts till 15 October!

    Happy Oktoberfest celebrations with Erdinger! Prost!
