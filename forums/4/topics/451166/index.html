    Why so expensive? $35 for just 2 plates of duck rice and 2 plates of wanton mee

     

     

    STOMPer Serene was appalled that a foreign cashier at a roasted duck stall in Food Republic foodcourt in Suntec City charged her $35 when she ordered only two plates of duck rice and two plates of wanton noodles.

     

    Said the STOMPer:

     

    "We ordered two plates of duck rice and two plates of wanton noodle, but were charged for two plates of roast duck, two bowls of rice, two plates of char siew noodle and two bowls of wanton soup.

     

    "We weren't even informed that the wanton noodles came with char siew.

     

    "When we asked the staff about the bill, the cashier said, 'It's like that.'

     

    "We couldn't do anything about it as the cashier told us that the food had already been prepared.

     

    "Everything happened so fast!

     

    "The staff had served us the food in a different manner (separate portions) so that they could earn more.

     

    "We were ridiculously overcharged!
