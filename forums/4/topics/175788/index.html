  • the Bear
    26 Jan 06, 22:05
    try this:

    shell a lot of cooked cockles (not overcooked)

    get a jam jar
    put all the cockles in the jar (should fill about 2/3 of the jar)
    smash about 3 or 4 cloves of garlic and throw inside...
    cut up a chilli into very small pieces and throw inside..
    fill nearly to full with dark soy sauce
    stir it around a little so the dark sauce and spices are mixed up with the shellfish
    throw into fridge for about 3 days..

    take out and eat cold with porridge..

    heavenly!
