    ~Yoghurt Salad~

    Ingredients:
    - Marigold Strawberry Yoghurt (recommended or choose your own yoghurt)
    - Baby honey tomatoes (from thailand..think it's sweeter)
    - Lettuce
    - Chicken Breast Meat
    - Carrots

    Steps:

    1. Rinse the vegetables & chicken! {duh}
    2. Pour a decent amount of water to a pot and boil it.
    3. Cut the chicken breast meat(dice) while waiting for the water to be boiled.
    4. Throw in the chicken breast meat into the pot when the water is boiled.
    5. Cut the lettuce and carrots to a size whereby you can eat it within a bite, while waiting for the chicken breast meat to be cooked.
    6. Once the chicken breast meat is cooked, put it into the fridge and wait for it to cool down.
    7. Take a container and empty the yoghurt into the container.
    8. Place all the lettuce, tomatoes and carrots inside the container, stir well.
    9. Take out the chicken breast meat, put it into the container and stir them altogether again.
    10. Put the container inside the fridge and cool it.

    Better served when chilled.
