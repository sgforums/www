    Jollibee Singapore opens two new outlets at Changi City Point & Novena Square 2

     

    Previously you can only get a taste of Philippines-based fast-food chain Jollibee from Lucky Plaza and if you have always found it’s rather out of the way to travel downtown just to satisfy your craving, good news for fans because they have added a couple more stores in Singapore.

    The two new Jollibee stores are located at Square 2 above Novena MRT station and Changi City Point, both located at level one in the respective malls and are now open for business. Jollibee is the largest fast food chain in Philippines with over 750 stores in the country known for its burgers, fried chicken, spaghetti & Filipino dishes.
