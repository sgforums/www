    some gelato is no added sugar. I am diabetic myself and i would recommend that when you go for food, do not go for SUGAR FREE, normally that means they use artificial sugar which is not good for your health in the long run anyway, NO ADDED SUGAR just means that the whatever sugar is found comes from the fruit or food itself.... which is actually healthier and better for diabetics.

    Anyway I ate at MOF, a japanese cafe in Bugis Junction the other night, I saw they sell Gelatos that have no added sugar. you can try there. Not many such ice creams or gelatoes. You can try Cold Storage, they have a good range of health foods that are suitable for diabetics but not sure about ice creams
