    Sacrilegeous!!! WHAT'S SO SPECIAL ABOUT THE RAMLY BURGER?!?! Lemme tell u... I quote from a diary entry....

    "The man, nodded at me in a businesslike manner, and briskly took out a slim patty from his icebox. I stood by his stall and waited. His actions were practised as he flipped the patty and began to butter the bun. I can hear the faint sizzle as he pressed the bun down gently to warm it.

    His fingers sprinkle a generous portion of vegetables and onions over the bun. I tried to see what are the various sauces that he squeezed liberally from his bottle, but the yellow light rendered the colour of the sauces all shades of grey.

    He wrapped the chicken patty up with the omelette dexterously, flipping it int a square. Then he assembled the entire burger on the plastic wrap and wrapped it up. He gently popped the burger into a crisp paperbag, and folded once across the top of the bag neatly - and he handed it to me with both hands and murmured "Dua Dua"

    At that moment, I had a revelation. This man is giving me a RAMLY BURGER. Its not a characterless, mass-produced Big M Bugger.

    It is a RAMLY burger. Every single burger is different, made with love -- with YOU in mind. You can stand next to him, and tell him if u wanted less salt, less sauce, more onions. It has the cook's personal touch that is not standardised by franchise. It is - in short - a work of art.

    You get to see it, smell it, feel it, and eat it, all for a mere 2.20 ringgit. At that moment, when I first unwrapped it, and sink my teeth into that juicy savoury burger, I know I felt at peace with the world and I am CONTENTED.
