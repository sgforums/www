    Went to baby fair at Sg Expo yesterday and had early dinner there at the food court. What a joke!

    I ordered a duck rice set meal and it was pathetic.  Serving small...all the rice and stuff huddled in one sad corner of the plate. soup was watered down. it sure looked much better in the picture

    my wife got it worse, she ordered ban mian and the serving even smaller.i have lived in sg my whole life of >30yrs and i have had my fair share of ban mian but this one looked like what i would have left behind if i couldnt finish a normal ban mian. 1 veg leaf, 1 veg stalk, maybe 10-13 pieces of the bee hoon guay and the rest soup which my wife said taste like water plus salt..and she paid $4

    and the common thing?both stalls run by mainland chinese. not that i want to create tension or sterotype or whatever  and maybe its just me but  everytime i see a stall run by mainland chinese, i feel quite wary for this reason...

    we were in a rush cos need to head back to make my baby's dinner so just eat what we can tahan and run off

    so anyway i am not asking people to belive me and not to eat there but to share my experience and also i realise that sg hawker/food court food has to have minimum standards which i have taken for granted until recently.
