    Originally posted by FireIce:
    FI's famous potato and tuna salad

    Ingredients:
    5 medium-sized potatoes (red-skin the best, if not, get australian potatoes)
    3 eggs
    200g picnic ham
    miracle whip
    bacon bits
    tuna mayo
    ground pepper
    lettuce

    Instructions:
    peel the potatoes.
    prepare a big pot of water. add salt to the water. boil potatoes until soft.
    leave it to dry. when cooled, cut into dices.
    hard-boil the 3 eggs. mash them. (me use a fork and press)
    chop the ham
    mix potatoes, ham, mashed eggs, tuna mayo, miracle whip, sprinkle pepper while you mix. (u control the amount of tuna mayo, miracle whip and pepper according to ur liking)
    chill overnight for best taste.
    serve in a bed of lettuce, sprinkle bacon bits all over.

    Anyway.. What are the ingredients for coleslaw and how do I go about preparing one..?
    8 cups finely diced cabbage (about 1 head)
    1/4 cup diced carrots
    2 tablespoons minced onions
    1/3 cup granulated sugar
    1/2 teaspoon salt
    1/8 teaspoon pepper
    1/4 cup milk
    1/2 cup mayonnaise
    1/4 cup buttermilk
    1 1/2 tablespoons white vinegar
    2 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice

    Cabbage and carrots must be finely diced.
    (I use fine shredder disc on food processor.) Pour cabbage and carrot mixture into large bowl and stir in minced onions.
    Using regular blade on food processor process remaining ingredients until smooth.
    Pour over vegetable mixture and mix thoroughly.
    Cover bowl and refrigerate several hours or overnight before serving
