    yumy yumi, me like pumpkin, too!! Just bought pumpkin for 50 cents from Sheng Siong. I usually fried pumpkin with dried shriumps, it tastes real good!

    Recipe :

    1) Soak dried shriumps for about 15 mins
    2) Cut pumpkin into pieces and wash it.
    3) Prepare garlic 3 to 4 pieces, cut into small pieces.
    4) Remove dried shriumps from the water. Retain water for use later. Fry dried shriumps. Add garlic. Fry until frangrant.
    5) Add pumpkin, fry for a while then add dried shriump water.
    6) Thereafter cover the wok for a couple of minutes.
    7) Check if the pumpkin is cooked. If it is then it's ready to eat.

    Yumi, yumi!! Hope you like the recipe!
