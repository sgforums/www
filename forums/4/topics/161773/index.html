    Northern Thai Tom Yam Soup
    1-5 Tyrwhitt Road
    Opening Hours 10.am to 4.30pm, Mon - Sat
    Closed on Sundays and Public Holidays

    They have Tom Yam or Clear soup. You can choose from Mixed Seafood, Fresh fish slices or fried fish slices or mixed. One bowl is $3. The cooks are Thai.

    They also serve really good Pad Thai and Fried Red Tilapia with a really good Chilli Sauce. One whole fish (enough for 2 people) at $6.

    So far, the only coffee shop tom yam that I really like is at a Clementi coffeeshop, behind Fairprice, and facing the old cinema/KFC - also where the famous Brothers Rojak is located.

    The tom yam is from the handmade noodle stall, and does not come from bottled tom yam paste. The clear soup has added lemon leaves, pineapple pcs and chili padi to give it the hot and sour zing! Very refreshing. But I've moved out so hardly get to eat it now.
