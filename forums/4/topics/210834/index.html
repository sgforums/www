    Sugee cake
    Chin Mee Chin Confectionery
    204 East Coast Road
    Opens: 8.30am - 5pm; closed on Mondays

    This 63-year-old kopitiam is famous for its kaya. Its cream horns, custard puffs and swiss rolls are popular too. But the real gem is its sugee cake.

    Made in true Katong tradition with ground almond and cashew nuts, the cake is soft and full of nutty flavour.

    Don't scream if you drive all the way from Jurong and find that they have run out - the baker makes only a few batches a day.
