    Shopping Mall with a pretty good foodcourt (FC) in the East:
    - Parkway Parade (B1) - Pretty good stuff there, it's generally packed. I personally like the Mince Pork noodles, Yong tao foo etc. Def one of the better FCs in the east.

    Other FCs in the east which are great:
    - Changi Airport T1 and T2 (L3) => Wouldn't say that all the food's great, but they would basically be the ones i would go often enough, up to 2 weekends in a row. They are definitely 2 of the better foodcourts to go for. I personally like the Indian Rojak and the Hokkien Prawn mee in T2.
