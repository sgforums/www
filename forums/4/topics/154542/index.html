    Mien by First Pasta
    Cuisine: Italian
    Location: Bukit Timah Hawker Centre (Near the Junction of Bukit Timah Rd and Jalan Jurong Kechil)

    For Italian food lovers, here's the place where you can have pasta at a hawker centre price. The exact address of the store is printed on the name card on the right.

    Went to this stall a few times. Very nice pasta food.
    It's at a hawker centre in Bukit Timah, so of course you will only pay hawker centre food kind of price. But the quality is as good as or even better than some expensive pasta restaurants in town or holland v.
