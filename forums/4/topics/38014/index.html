    The average price of Boon Tong Kee chicken is quite standard:

    1. 12 bucks for half a chicken
    2. 22 bucks for a whole chicken
    3. 6-8 bucks for vegetables or inerts and intestines

    Customers who patronise the place aren't usually there for a drumstick meal...they usually whack whole chickens.

    By the way, the price list above is for the main branch at balastier road. The outlets at upper bukit timah and river valley are a little more costly (add 1-2 bucks to the price list above) However, the service is generally ok and food quality so far is gauranteed

    However, another franchise - Wee Nam Kee, is not bad either. Price range is comparable. Got one opposite the novena church that opens until quite late...past 1130pm and another outlet along thomson road near the sweet secrets shop.

    If u're looking for exotic taste, u can try the kampong chicken rice opposite the wee nam kee at thomson. Affectionately called "Chicken House", the meat is lean and tasty, with very little oil used. They serve cold crabs too...huge crowds ard 6-730pm daily.

    Oh by the way, "loy kee best" chicken rice is very ordinary ...quite a common sight in food courts.
