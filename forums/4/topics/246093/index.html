    Used to patronise this shop very often around 10 years ago. I was working at a construction site at Woodland Link Road. Used to bring C.O.W to eat there. Then, they were still at the basement. There was another stall that sell pineapple rice but they served on a pinepapple itself. Business were fierce & this stall, to lure customer, always charge you higher on the reciept & collect round figure. This brings in many customer to bring their client there to eat & claim from their company. For eg, the bill is $87 & the will collect only $80 from you.

    But ever since they moved to 4th floor, the standard drop. Most of the dishes they served were cold. More like a mass production cos they have quite a few popular dish like the fried fish, fried sotong, pineapple rice & etc so they cook in very large portion & leave it there to be served.
