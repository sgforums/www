    17 Jun 07, 21:47
    Was at Oosh this evening. The ambience is nice, but spoilt by the loud and unsuitable music (Indie) which did not gel with the surroundings at all.

    The service standards are much to be desired. The staff are not trained to serve - gruff grunts were the main with respect to anything they were asked e.g. topping up of water (which never came despite 3 separate requests. Empty plates were left uncleared for a good 15 minutes and we never got asked if we wanted dessert. We left after the main course.

    The kitchen is located too far away from the dining area. Our main courses were served cold. Cold beef is not, at the best of times, palatable.

    I've read some reviews which say that it is not child-friendly. For a non-child-friendly place, there were several screaming children running around. That's probably not the restaurant's fault but it is definitely not a quiet place.
