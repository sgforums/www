    oh.. talking about fried fish..

    take a flattish type fish.. shaped like a pomfret...

    score the flesh deeply so that you get squares...

    get some chilli, garlic, onion, vinegar, sugar, salt.. pound it and turn it into a paste...

    stuff the paste into all parts of the fish, in the deep cuts you make.. let it sit for a few minutes..

    deep fry...
