    Originally posted by choco B:
    Any specific eateries in Fortune Ctr to recommend? Name & guide to location would be good. Thanks in advance!
    For vegetarian + organic food, there are shops on 2nd and 4th level, which are quite good. Some daily specials they have inc bak kut teh, Korean bibimbab, Hakka lei cha, and 8-treasure rice.

    Vegetarian - there are 2 stalls on level 2, one directly opp. the organic food shop and the other to the side of it (this one has quite tasty zhi zha noodles, lor mee, fish beehoon, and Heng Hwa lor mee & Heng Hwa beehoon, etc.). There's also a more "gourmet-ish" (in presentation) vegetarian foodcourt on the ground level facing the main road which has nice food, but maybe has a tad more MSG (as commented by some).

    Other the the foodcourt, the rest are small shops, so don't expect much ambience, and sorry I didn't take note of the shop names.
