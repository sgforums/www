Hougang Pt's food in kpt is getting worse to worse!!!

  • cyberr1981
    17 May 11, 23:35

    Here's my review and opinions, let's go 1 round and start from chicken rice stall:

    chicken rice stall = when hougang pt first opened in 1998, it is very good. In recent years, the chicken and rice tasted stale and overnight.

    duck rice stall = it was only average in the past. A few years back, I actually bought and tasted stale and bitter duck meat from them. Ever since then, I boycotted them totally and have not bought from them for years

    western stall = was really good. In recent years, standards dropped. Now chicken chop is pre-cooked. rice and fries tasted stale and hard. mushroom soup tasted stale also.

    fried rice, fried kway tiao = from past till now, ok only. Not exceptionally good but not bad.

    porridge stall = ok only

    zi char = previous vendor's egg is pre-cooked and always tasted stale. Only their meat is freshly cooked but still average only. Current vendor, I don't dare to try...

    mixed veg rice = used to be quite good but in recent years, standards really dropped.

    boiled noodles stall = average only. Recently, they become stingy on ingredients like give smaller prawns, fewer tau pok when ordering laska/prawn noodles.

    hot metal plate stall = Was really good. In recent years, they pre-cooked their meat and it tasted stale and overnight. Their noodles is never good. Tasted like plastic.

    economic bee hoon stall = looks stale and overnight, don't dare to try

    fish porridge stall = don't look nice to me

    VERDICT: Not worth eating unless got famine. During lunch time, I always make the effort to go Sheng Siong/North Star for better food.
