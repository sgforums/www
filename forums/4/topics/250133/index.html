Changi Village Hotel Buffet

    Review on Changi Village Hotel buffet we had on 23 Mar 2007.

    Cost was $38+++/head.

    Types of food they had:
    Salads
    Soups (1 chinese soup, 1 western)
    Salmon (smoked)
    Warm food (roasted chicken, cheese baked mussels, steamed fish fillets, etc)
    Satay
    BBQ (Prawns, Fish, Chicken, Steak, Lamb, Mussels)
    Dessert (cakes, ice cream, stir-fry fruits with alcohol

    Verdict:
    BBQ Prawns was nice coz the prawn was huge. Lamb is nice. Chicken, Fish, Steak normal)
    Satay is nice (beef, mutton, chicken) and the sauce is nice coz of the pineapple which was grinded really fine
    Warm food ok, but not good
    Dessert ok

    Food Rating would be about 6.5/10, coz I expected more from the cost
    Service Rating would be about 8/10, the staff are attentive, and they gave free parking upon request
    Ambience would be about 8/10. Nice, cosy, quiet ambience, with a rustic feel.
