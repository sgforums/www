    This outlet is opened by the former Maru Maru Sushi at Marina Square (yes, they have relocated). However, it is now more upmarket i.e. no belt sushi and instead, they serve you over the counter when u make orders. You can order sushi by pieces (even one piece) and there is a wide range of toppings to choose from including ikura, nama hotate, hamachi and puffer fish. For unagi and anago, they actually heat up the sushi before serving. Only one serving of assorted sashimi will be served. Most importantly, the rice for the sushi is freshly moulded by the chef upon order. Not those machine made ones. The size is smaller (bite-size) and taste much better. It is not cheap but the quality is certainly better than any of those $1.90, belt-type sushi with machine made rice balls. Try it during weekday lunch where it is the cheapest to taste the difference!
