    These are the various stalls where I will "swear" by if I really want a nice CKT meal and yes I am ranking them BUT it is personally my opinion. Try it when you have the time:

    1. Zion Road - Zion Riverside Food Centre/ Stall 17 No.18 Zion Road Fried Kway Teow (landmark- Great World City) Starts from 6pm onwards. Close on alternate Mondays.

    2. Bedok South Road Market & Food Centre/ Stall 01-187 Hill Street Char Kway Teow (landmark Bedok MRT station) Starts 10.30am to 6.30pm. Close on Mondays.

    3. Hong Lim Food Centre/ Stall 02-18 Outram Park Fried Kway Teow (landmark Chinatown Point) Starts 7a.m. to 4.30p.m. only. Closed on Sundays and P.H.

    4. Serangoon Gardens Market and Food Centre/ Stall 46 302 Newton Circus Chao Guo Tiao (landmark Serangoon Neighbourhood Police Post - NOTE: NOT CHOMP CHOMP!!) Starts 1pm. to 9pm. Close on Mondays.

    5. Golden Mile Food Centre/ Stall 01-91 91 Fried Kway Teow (landmark Golden Mile Shopping Complex) Open Daily except on Mondays until 2p.m. only. NB> This stall does not use pork lard!!

    I hope you will be able to enjoy to your heart's contend. Bon Appetit my friends
