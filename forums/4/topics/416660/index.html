    Well GM Foods has some advantages:

    • Pest resistance – Farmers do not have to use pesticideson their crops anymore.
    • Herbicide tolerance – farmers could spray these crops with herbicide and kill the weeds, without affecting the crop.
    • Disease resistance – Crops Resistance to virus or bacteria that can cause plant disease.
    • Drought tolerance – Crops are able to withstand long periods of drought and still survive, this helps people who are living at dry land area to grow crops for the well living.
    • improved storage and nutritional quality
    • pest and disease resistance
    • selective herbicide tolerance
    • tolerance of water, temperature and saline extremes
    • improved animal welfare
    • higher yields and quality

    But it is also argued that some of them causes health risk to human consumption and its effects on humans in the long run is still unknown, an effect may be allergy reaction within a consumer.

    In Singapore, the AVA (Agri-Food & Veterinary Authority) ensures GM foods sold in Singapore are safe for consumption before they are sold in our markets.
