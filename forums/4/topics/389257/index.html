    If you have chance, dont buy white rice from supermarket....

    Buy red rice....

    Red rice is better and more superior....

    Red rice has more nutrition....and also has a low Glycemic Index.....meaning the sugar will be absorbed more gradually by the body.....

    If you eat white rice......you feel full fast.....and then you will also feel hungry earlier...

    Red rice will be more gradual.....

    So this is good and suitable for everybody, including diabetic people.

    But even if you are not diabetic, it is also good for u.

    Especially if you tend to feel sleepy after eating lunch / dinner, try red rice. You will not feel sleepy anymore.

    Of course, even if you dont care about all this, red rice has another advantage of having more nutrition, because white rice is actually a kind of processed / polished rice.

    Taste is also good and unique, it is a kind of nutty.

    So if you go to supermarket.....dont forget...buy red rice.
