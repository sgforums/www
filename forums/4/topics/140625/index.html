  • mochou
    8 Jul 05, 01:16
    Originally posted by dragg:
    try the 'put chai gou' and 'por lor bao'

    the food there is fantastic.

    avoid occupying big tables unless you are with a big group. their sitting culture is different from ours.

    go see a concert too.

    Por Lor Bao(BoLuoBao) and Egg Tarts are sold at those Cha Chan Tang(Cha Can Ting), those famous ones will have long queue. ppl there like to buy at the moment they have just been taken out from the oven.

    For street stalls(used to be trolleys,but govt catch them until they rent shops, mostly found in MongKok area), Curry fish ball, got normal/spicy, for singaporeans may prefer the spicy type. Many others stuffs but personally i like curry fishball most.

    Someone mentioned goose, the best goose is in Sum Cheung(Shen Jin,"deep well"), a place quite far from the city tough.

    For Roast pigeon, the best is in Long hua hotel(it's call JiuDian but not really a hotel)in Shatin, also far from city area.

    One more type of birds, it's call Wor Fa Cheuk(HeHuaQue), it's a very small bird, very crispy, one bite can eat the whole bird.

    For Moon cakes/LaoPoBing, most famous is (Hang Heung)HengXiang, which in YeungLeong, evem further from city than Shatin. But dun worry, they got small shops in MongKok area nowdays, cos the HQ in YeungLeong is really too far.

    If u happen to go to see big Budda in Lantau Island, there's a small shop near the busstop. Try the Tofu hua there.

    many many more.... lazy to mention all.....
