    Usually busy working people like us just buy any breakfast and eat on the go. Sometimes i find it too oily. And sometimes we get bored with Peanut butter or margarine on our bread.

    Here's a Tuna salad Sandwiches. (Healthy style)

    1) 1 Can Tuna Chunks. Remove liquid.
    2) 1 Onion dice/chopped
    3) 1 Capsicum dice/chopped
    4) Low Fat Mayonaise
    5) WholeMeal grain bread
    6) Cucumber slice
    7) Tomato slice
    1 Egg. HardBoiled. removed shell and mashed it.

    Put the dice up vege. mashed egg and Tuna into a mixing bowl. Add in Mayonaise 3-4 spoon. Add in pepper. Stir and mix well.

    Spread on bread. Add in cucumber and tomato slice

    Done.
