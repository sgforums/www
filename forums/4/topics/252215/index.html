    Most restaurants in country clubs are open to the public. Some may restrict the hours that non-members can patronize. Exclusive ones like Singapore Island Country Club, I am not sure.

    But I can go to the restaurant at Laguna Country Club and have a meal while enjoying the beautiful view of the golf course. If you go there, I highly recommend the Bitter Gourd Beef Hor Fun - yummy!

    The restaurants at Raffles Marina at Tuas are also open to the public. You can catch a great view of the Tuas Link from there.

    I'm a member of Serangoon Gardens Country club. All the restaurants are open to the public, including the popular Jumbo seafood restaurant.
