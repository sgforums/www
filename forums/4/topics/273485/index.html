    The green thingy is either parsley or maybe pesto. Some restaurant use pesto to have a better flavour.

    Recipe for garlic bread is simple.

    Butter 100gm
    Chopped Garlic 25gm or (1tbl spoon)
    Chopped parsley 5gm
    Salt & pepper to adjust by you own taste.

    *Butter must be in room temperature so that you can mixed all of the ingredients.

    *After you mixed throughly, using a butter knife & spread on to the bread.
    *Bake your garlic bread till golden brown in colour.
