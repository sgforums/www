    20,000 pints of free beer to be had in Singapore in March 

    St. Patrick's Day (Mar 17) may be more of an Irish affair, but that's not stopping us from joining in the festivities here—and what better way to kick off three weeks of revelry than with free booze?

     
    From Mar 1-20, participating watering holes around Boat Quay will be giving out  20,000 complimentary pints of Guinness Draught. All you have to do is to register by sending an SMS with your Name<SPACE>NRIC to 8151-5555, and successful applications will receive an SMS notification for redemption. Here's hoping you have the luck of the Irish.
     
    We heard they are still rounding up the participating bars so for more information, visit the Guinness Facebook page.
