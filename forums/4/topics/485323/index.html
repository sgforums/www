IndoChine Waterfront closes on 30 June 2014

    Along the Singapore River across the Fullerton Hotel, sits an iconic alfresco restaurant stretched just around the corner of the Asian Civilisations Museum. Spacious and welcoming, IndoChine Waterfront has offered respite to the weary, brought joy and forged many friendships over good food and drink, during the past 13 years. Unfortunately, due to a pending reconstruction and revamp of the site, this beautiful site will be closed for good, on Monday, 30th of June 2014- apparently, the site will be reconstructed and built into a new entrance for the Museum.

    Firstly, the Musuem is a ghost town- not many people visit it, you never see it crowded. Secondly, Waterfront's closing will mean 100 employees losing their jobs. what are your thoughts on this?
