    Ocean serve one of the best cai png. It is located at Toa Payoh near a market. You can see the market when you come from CTE exit Bradell. The market is those very old type & the cai png stall is at one coffee shop opposite the market. Many people go there to eat curry fish head (which is very famous) but i think their dishes also very good as the dishes is more like those cze char type. The price is a bit on the high side as the average 2 meat 1 veggie cost around $3+ to $4+.
