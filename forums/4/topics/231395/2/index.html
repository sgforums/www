    ok, let's list out the different types of cai png:

    1). Normal standard cai png (Economic Rice) (in kopitiam, food court) where they put and you choose the dishes

    2). Malay cai png (same as seen in kopitiam, food court)

    3). Curry rice, or jian dao jian (think its a dialect form of cai png)

    4). Nasi Lemak (cai png also, except the rice is coconut rice)

    5). Economic mee/bee hoong (Same, except is noodles)

    6). High Class buffet cai png (those available in hotel, like chinese lunch buffet)



    Now, what are all your favourite dishes?

    For me, its: 1). egg tofu, 2). Bittergoud with abit of egg, 3). Minced meat with kiam chye.
