    Last night, I tried the Che Jian fried hokkien mee at Chomp Chomp, stall #11 for the second time. This time, I ta pao, and was pleasantly surprised that it was wrapped in opei leaf, for just $3 dollars total! Opened the packet 10 minutes later, and after tasting it, I'm convinced it's pretty good. The moistness is perfect, the texture and color just right (only thin beehoon is used, much more flavourful) and the taste is rich and well balanced. Well, it makes up for the disappointment of losing my all time favourite Ah Huat there when the old man retired. The young man who took over the stall was such a disappointment.
