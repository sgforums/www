    5 Feb 07, 00:07
    Yhing Thai Palace Restaurant @ 36 Purvis St, #01-04

    I always go there for good thai food. The prices are not cheap but the quality and taste more than justifies it.

    If you order the tom yam kang, please get your drink ready. My partner says the pineapple rice is fantastic. I find the pandan wrapped chicken very tender, cooked just right. Ask for the otak, I think its fishcakes on the menu, but its really otak. The stuffed chicken wings is best, another of my favourite is the claypot vermicelli with prawns. And I never leave without the tapioca dessert.

    I 2 people go and eat, always set me back close to 60 bucks one. By the way, here's their number 63379429. If you going for dinner, try to make reservations. Otherwise you can sit outside wait long long.

    Aroi Thai at Upper East Coast Road between SPC Petrol Station & Siglap NPP. This is a family run restaurant. All the waiteress inside this restaurant are all Thai aunties. They serve authentic thai dishes which is as good as those you eat in thailand. Be warn that their food are quite spicy to some local standard cos they do not cater to local taste & stick to the original. Price wise quite on the high side.

    Previous visits for 2 person cost $60+. Ordered a tom yam steam fish, tom yam soup with beef & basil leave fried with mince pork (very very small portion) & two rice.

    Their price for all the dishes start from $11.90 for small serving.

    Quite expensive but good food.
