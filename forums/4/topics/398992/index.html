    Basically eh... 3 type I think...

    Nigiri - The most common type where U got the sushi rice on the bottom and the main item on top... May come with or without seaweed... Common nigiri are: Salmon Sushi, Tamago Sushi, Kani Sushi etc etc...

    Maki - A circular roll which consist of sushi rice outer, then main item in the inner... May or may not be wrap with seaweed... Common maki are: California maki, Kani Maki, Tamago Maki etc etc...

    Temaki - A handroll... More in a cone shap with the rice and main item inside...

     

    There are of coz a few more types but those are more uncommon... such as oshisushi, chirashi sushi(though U still can find this type in places like sakea sushi)...
