    Bedok, Changi food centres reopen on Jan 1 after upgrading works

     

    The markets and food centres at Block 85 Bedok North Street 4, and Blocks 2 and 3 Changi Village Road will re-open for business on Tuesday after 10 months of upgrading works.

    Business will resume progressively as stalls complete their renovation works.

    Both centres underwent ten and a half months and ten months of upgrading respectively under the Hawker Centres Upgrading Programme (HUP) by the National Environment Agency (NEA).

    Patrons can now look forward to a more spacious, pleasant and hygienic dining environment at the upgraded centres. There are also more seats at the refreshment areas of both centres. The seating capacity at the Bedok North Street 4 centre has increased by approximately 32 per cent, whereas the seating capacity at Changi Village Road has increased by 6.6 per cent (for Block 2) and 106.6 per cent (for Block 3).
