    There's this nice hainanese chicken rice stall at chinatown hawker centre. The name is Tian Yuan Su Shi Tan 天源熟食摊 .. at one corner, facing the telok ayer pap branch. The cheapest is $2. The normal serving $2.50 comes with huge servings of juicy chicken meat. The rice is tasty as well. The stall is managed by an old couple.

    Go try! :-)
