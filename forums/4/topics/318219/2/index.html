    It's not salt that makes a fry crispy

    I always eat my fries without salt.

    How long a fry stay crispy is not because of the salt...of course if you coat the fry with lots of salt till you can feel the crunch beneath your teeth I have nothing to say.

     

    How long a fry stay crispy is due to :

    1. Solid content of the fry

    2. Moisture of the fry

    3. Frying Method (Must always be fried Deep Frozen)

    4. Temp of Oil

    Also if it's flour coated fries, it will stay crispy longer or taste more crispy. Nothing to do with salt at all.
