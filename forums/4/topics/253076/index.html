    Originally posted by mhcampboy:
    duuh.. where is it?amk?? woodlands?? bedok?? near where...
    General location off Cambridge Rd at Farrer Park (can approach from Farrer Park MRT). I lived at Dorset Road for a few years in the late 80s.

    My favourite food at the Cambridge Rd Food Centre is the Cambridge Yong Tau Foo. It just has the right balance to tinkle me. Great sauce, great chilli, and Yong Tau pieces that don't look as if they come from a factory. Very under-rated, but what a joy for me to know and eat it.

    And yes, there is also a great Chwee Kuay stall there, chunky chye poh and really great chilli to go with it. Understandably, I'm not a fan of Tiong Bahru style Chwee Kuay - its chye poh tastes funny and its chilli is no where near the Cambridge standard. Many people like to down play the importance of using the right chilli. Not me
