    Another good outlet would be Hisatomo. this is another good place to dine at but the price here is slightly more to the high end cost. Typical jap food nth special but sashimi are fresh too.

    If u guys really have good money to spend and wont cry after spending a few hundreds on jap food, i would recommend the 1 at Lido cinema there. its on the 7th floor if i nvr remember wrongly. have to go by the lift from mac there 1.
