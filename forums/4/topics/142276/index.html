    Inspired by someone's comments on whether the Big Mac burgers shrink during the $2 promotion, I bought 3 Big Mac's yesterday night.

    I put the 3 burgers side by side and compared them. Burger 1 and 2 had no noticeable difference, but the bun size of burger 3 was definitely smaller.

    Next I lifted the bun and inspected the size of the meat patties, I found that the meat patties of 3 burgers were different!

    The meat patty of burger 1 is approximately 7mm thick and 69mm in diameter.

    The meat patty of burger 2 is roughly 8mm thick and 75mm in diameter.

    The meat patty of burger 3 is roughly 6mm thick and 70mm in diameter.

    I havent bought the Big mac during the non-promotional period, but will do so later this week when the promotion ends.
