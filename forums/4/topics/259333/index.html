    Last week i just cooked "tang yuan"... So nice, cheap & good... what for go hawker centre? cooked yourself much more better....

    Ingredients
    1 pkt "Chinatown" brand Tang Yuan....
    (Available in Peanut w White Sesame & Black Sesame)

    3 Cups of Water

    Pot

    Pandan Leaves(washed)

    Rock Sugar

    Extracter/Drainers

    Instruction
    - Boil the water & when the water boils, put in pandan leaves & rock sugar

    - Taste the soup whether is it sweet enough, if not, add more rock sugar

    - Once the rock sugar is fully melted, drain the soup in another bowl... as there is lots of remaining leaves at the soup

    - Wash the pot and pour in the soup & boil it again....

    - once boil, put in the "tang yuan"... do not defrost the tang yuan....

    - once the "tang yuan" floated, prepare to serve....
