The Shrinking Drumstick

Next
  • ^tamago^

    Have anyone realised that the drumsticks from chicken rice stalls are getting smaller and smaller?

    Not too long ago, chicken drumstick rice would costs 50 cents more than the normal chicken rice. A common workaround is to order chicken backside which costs the same as the normal ones, but the meat is almost, or just as tender as that of the drumstick.

    However, nowadays even though the prices of chicken drumstick rice have not gone up, the size of the drumstick has become smaller and smaller, sometimes taking up only half the plate for meats, leaving much empty space that it can hurt.

    And while the price of the normal rice may have risen to match that of the drumstick rice's, the price of chicken backside now commands a premium instead, since they'd have to fill up the plate with meat, instead of just doing their best with a small drumstick.

    Sigh.
