    I am from Singapore. Born in Singapore, live in Singapore, drink Singapore water and eat Singapore food.

    I go to JB quite a lot of times. Last time I use to take taxi in JB, but since I have know their buses, nowadays I took bus in JB.

    One note here, drive only at the main roads at night (after 7pm). Avoid the following roads at all costs:

    Jalan Tampoi (near Plaza Angsana and the whole stretch ... no road lamp and the stretch between Bandar Baru Uda and Jalan Kempas, no road lamp and build halfway... a lot of small stones on the road)

    Jalan Abad (somewhere near Century Garden, a bit of road lamps but super steep slope)

    Taman Johor Jaya (Jalan Johor Jaya and Jalan Dedap (near the Golf Driving Range especially because my dad got a minor accident there)

    Jalan Masai Baru (the junction in front of Carrefour until Makro) no street lamps at certain stretch

    Tebrau Highway (after Carrefour and at the junction of Pasir Gudang Highway) due to the roadworks of building a flyover, there has been jams and more heavy jams in the peak hours. Drivers driving to Kota Tinggi, Ulu Tiram, Desaru, Mersing and east coast must aware of it

    the road in front of Plentong Giant (interchange of Pasir Gudang Highway, similar as the junction of AMK Ave 1 and CTE) but their's is one junction got traffic light and the other one no traffic light, beware. After 7pm total darkness
