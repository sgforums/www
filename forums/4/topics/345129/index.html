    I have an industrial-grade Oster 12-speed blender, so pulverizing ice is no problem.

    My favourite smoothie concoction comprises the following ingredients:

    • 1/2 cup of peach slices (drained of syrup)
    • 1/2 ripe banana
    • 2/3 cup of ice cream (usually vanilla or chocolate chip)
    • 2/3 cup of pina colada mixer
    • About 6 "standard size" ice cubes

    Throw everything into the blender, hit "pulverize" and watch the lights in the house dim. Blend until everything is nice and smooth, pour it into a tall glass and enjoy!
