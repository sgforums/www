    yorkshire pudding is easy to make.. make sure you use bacon fat to make it. better for flavour. 

  • ben1406
    11 Mar 09, 03:22

    all you need is 2 eggs, 2 cups of 2% milk, 2 cups of all purpose flour, fat (bacon works best), salt and pepper. mix all of it execpt the fat in a bowl and wisk till its a batter.

    oil your baking trays with fat around. pour in mix and bake at 375F for about 10 - 15 mins or when it puffs up.. easy money !
