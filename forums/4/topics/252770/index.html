  • BillyBong
    7 May 07, 10:10
    Xiao long bao at DTF is poor copmpared to the crystal jade and imperial treasure, so avoid it.

    Prawn fritters are nice, so is the yang zhou fried rice. But everything there is pricy. If you're there for the la mian and you've previously tasted those at CJ and IT, then it's more or less the same standard.

    Their hua tiao jiu chicken is not as 'toxic' as the other two: they marinate with less wine, so it preserves the original taste of the chicken.

    Other than that, cost-wise for 5, assuming one la mian for each and 4-5 side dishes will be ard 110-120 bucks, budget.
