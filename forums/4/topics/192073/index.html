    I tried the "Hao3 cho1 dao4" (cho1=porridge) steamboat buffet at AMK Central (at 3rd floor, above Season's dept store) which gave a choice of chicken soup and/or tom yam soup, or rice porridge soup (in a clay pot).

    Dinner costs $13.90 net with nice soup stock and a good variety of fresh ingredients, plus a free drink. There was also a small buffet of cooked snacks, including chicken drumlets, chicken curry, bee hoon, etc., fruit and potong ice cream.

    Most of the seats are located outdoor in a relaxing setting and there's also a small aircon area. Not a posh kind of place, but recommended for the value-for-money and tasty food. It gets quite a full house during dinner time on the weekends. Will go again soon.
