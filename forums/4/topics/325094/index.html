    from Bugis Junction's Mos Burger, walk out.. cross the road directly, that stretch got nice desserts and I heard there's a nice porridge steamboat.

    near that main road that you crossed, somewhere near the bus stop where the cheers is, there's a nice wanton mee stall inside this mini air-conditioned shop. quite nice also.

    Inside bugis junction, you can always eat at the basement there got those mini snacks and stuff. on the first storey got this Ajisen Jap restaurant.. okay okay. beside it got Sketches Pasta, not bad. second storey got some other restaurants like Waraku, or Sakae all these. and last but not least their food court.

    If you can take the heat, you can go into Bugis Village inside got a food court style place got nice ban mian I heard. Further down near Sim lim Square the hawker now having renovation so no choice liao.
