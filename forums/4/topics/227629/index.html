    Singapore Cable Car - Sky Dining

    If you want to feel on top of the world, and i mean it literally, you have to experience sky dining atop one of Sentosa's quaint cable cars. Dining at 70m above the sea for a duration of 1.5 to 2 hours, this will be one unfogettable experience for you to share with your loved ones. Choose from two distinctive but equally tempting set dinner menus prepared by Altivo's masterful chefs. At $88 nett per couple, the first set (Set A) is affordable without compromising on quality. For starters, enjoy Cream of Tomato Soup served with Garlic Crostini. Then, choose from either the well-seasoned Pan-fried Fish Fillet in Cajun Spice or the succulent Grilled Chicken with Prommery Mustard Jus, both served with sauteed garden vegetables and caramelised potato. After the gratifying main course, tuck into the sweet apple crumble served with strawberry coulis. The other set (Set B) is served with a glass of house wine. Priced at $158 nett per couple, this set guarantees you satisfaction starting with the first round of Cream of Tomato Soup served with Garlic Crostini and Smoked Salmon Salad with mascarpone cheese. Next, decide between the juicy Grilled Beef Tenderloin with Rosemary Jus and the flavorsome Pan-fried fillet of Salmon with Honey Miso, both served with sauteed garden vegetables and caramelised potato. End your meal savoring the divine Chocolate Brownie served with chocolate sauce and strawberry coulis. Lean back and revel in the spectacular sight of the city and its habour.
