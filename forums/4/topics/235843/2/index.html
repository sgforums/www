    7 Feb 07, 21:38
    1) Put the duck on the bigger plate, pour hot water, to clean the dust, remove some hair if u want, release some oil (this procedure take less than 5 mins, remember dun dip into water too long)

    2) Throw away the water.

    3) Dun chop the duck yet, steam 10-15 mins than cut into small piece, can eat liao.

    Must eat with white rice, otherwise u will feel very salty.
    Some ppl eat with orange sauce, but I never try.

    p/s: Dun throw the skin, the most tasty part is the skin itself.

    I just went to NTUC just now, there are selling a kind of "alcohol preserved duck", the look very similiar to "wax duck", but the taste is not, dun buy wrongly, it's "wax duck" not "alcohol preserved duck".
