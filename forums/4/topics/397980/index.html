  • Lubb-Shhh-Dubb
    2 May 10, 10:19

    I want 2 votes, because I cant decide!

    KAP (King albert park) for the win! Many years ago, there used to be a toy railway track that ran around the circumference of the ceiling. Always thought it was really cute to see the train racing around. Furthermore, it used to have a huge and i do mean huge playing area for kids to play in. Still remember the times i spent with my childhood friends (many many many years ago) just sitting and eating ice creams in that place.  

    When secondary school came around, KAP came to the rescue again. Where else could ACS boys meet MGS/SCGS/Nanyang secondary gals? Well then again there were Chinese High guys there as well ... more than ACS boys.

    Nowadays, I spend my time at NUS macdonalds.  Its cheaper than anywhere else. Furthermore they're open till really really late, so even if you're mugging into the wee hours of the morning, you still have an airconditioned place to chillax.

    Sadly, KAP has lost much of its old charm.  The train no longer runs and the kiddy play area has gone to make way for more tables. So thanks for the memories KAP, but times have changed and now i'm a cash strapped uni student.  So NUS macs for the win!
