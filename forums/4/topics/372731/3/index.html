    I'm taking orders for home-made mooncakes. Both baked and snowskin are available.

    They will be sold in minis (60g each) and 8 in a box. The more popular flavours are white lotus, durian lotus and green tea.

    Baked mini white lotus with melon seeds @ $15.00

    Baked mini white lotus with melon seeds and single yolk @ $19.00 (each mooncake has 1 whole yolk each i.e. total 8 yolks in one box)

    Baked mini green tea lotus with melon seeds @ $15.00

    Baked mini green tea lotus with melon seeds and single yolk @ $19.00 (each mooncake has 1 whole yolk each i.e. total 8 yolks in one box)

    Baked mini durian lotus with melon seeds @ $17.00

    Baked mini durian lotus with melon seeds and single yolk @ $21.00 (each mooncake has 1 whole yolk each i.e. total 8 yolks in one box)

    Many other flavours are available like red wine berry for snowskin. Email lovingbaking@gmail.com for more information or complete price list. Or visit http://sweetscouture.blogspot.com/2009/09/festive-specials.html for pictures and information.
