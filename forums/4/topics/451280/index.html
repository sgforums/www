Subway (BIPL) change of management

  • FireIce
    25 Apr 12, 21:18
    Dear Valued Customers, This is to inform you that we have sold our Subway business to one of our former directors, James Kodrowski (Be Our Guest Pte. Ltd) with effect from April 1, 2012. We have been transitioning over the last several weeks and have concluded the transition with this email today. We apologize for any inconvenience that the transition may have caused you. For any queries, concerns or issues pertaining to Subway, you may get in touch with Mr. Kodrowski or alternatively, Ms. Michelle Lim , Catering Officer at 6270-9267. We have been a proud franchisee of Subway for the past 13 years and we appreciate the loyalty and support that you have provided to us over the years we have been in business. Our company will continue to operate other brands in Singapore, with Chili's Grill & Bar recently opening its 3rd location at JCube and our 4th restaurant opens at The Central Clark Quay in October 2012. We have a delivery and catering program via www.chilistogo.sg and we will be offering some introductory offers shortly. In addition, we have the rights to Morelli's Gelato which is opening soon, and are purchasing a well known pizza chain which will in turn be led by our team, with Ms. Ann Cheng a critical component of our system and she will remain with our company. Thank you once again and we look forward to your continued patronage of our brands. Your Sincerely, Greg Blakney CEO Belgarath Investments Pte Ltd.
