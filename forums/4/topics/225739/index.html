    Recently i have started work in the airport T2...Wow, so much food 2 choose from ar Let me start by intro the food in the 2 canteens. One in T1, the other in T2

    T1 Canteen
    Fish soup stall- The salmon teriyaki is the BEST i have eaten 4 a long time. Very Crispy on the outside, soft inside..Yum..Served with fluffy jap rice and salad.Only $4.50(The serving quite adequate.. No fishy taste at all! But beware if you go during lunchtime,long queue..Most ppl will order the salmon teriyaki even though got sell other fishy dishes
    **You can also choose BBQ sauce instead of teriyaki**

    T2 Canteen
    The pasta/western food store. The cream spagetti is the most delicious i had. They dun show that on the menu/board..But They sell sausage/ham spagetti with tomato sauce, juz tell them u wan change 2 mushroom cream sauce.Simply delicious, its the 1st time i finish a whole plate of cream pasta, cos very 'ni'. The one they sell does not have any 'ni' feeling, very amazing. The pasta is super Q, and guss what, a plate only $3.50 I have not tried their western food, but when i see ppl ordering i nearly drool. They have chicken chop/cutlet,catfish...All presented restaurant style with sauteed potato cubes and salad..Looks real good

    Marrybrown Chicken T2
    What can i say? After trying, i dun think i will go back to KFC..After work i ta pau home..45 min later, its was still sooooo crispy! The inside was juicy and tender. Oily percentage only 50%The mash potato was good. Oh, and they sell vegetable salad, the type KFC years ago sell(peas/carrots/corn).Yummy, i prefer that to coleslaw

    Still got so many place to eat
