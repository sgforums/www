    Is there one at Upper Serangoon Shopping Centre, or are you referring to the one at the corner of Serangoon Road and Sturdee Road ?

    !!!!! !!!!! !!!!!! - "The French Stall"

    Great French Cuisine at double the price of some western grill found in a regular S-11 or Kopi Tiam Centre, but with the quality found in some 4-star hotel restaurants.

    You get the real stuff "Goose Liver", "French Onion Soup", "Pork Medallions", "Wines", and "Desserts" with better prices then at the hotels.

    All the items on the Dessert List are a must try.

    Finish your dinner with the best coffee in town, or linger on with the fine list of wine as you chat the night away under the open sky.

    Eating is done along the '5 foot way' or on the pavement, as the entire show kitchen is occupying the shop, and you will see the French man cooking in a non-airconditioned environment.
