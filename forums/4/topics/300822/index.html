    Not sure how many of you have heard of this website www.dabao.com.sg.

    When i heard of dabao.com, i thought it was an ingenious concept as they deliver some of the best hawker food from Geylang to your home. But to my great disappointment, these folks are obviously not serious about the business.

    I've called many times and even SMS to place my order but nobody bothered to reply! I even called the SMS number which was a M1 mobile number.

    The website or number i called, doesnt say that they are closed for business on 1 Jan so I had to assume that it's business as usual but no one was interested to deliver my order. In their FAQ, they even state no surcharge for delivery on public holidays & weekends!

    It's really disappointing that such a simple & brilliant delivery concept cant even live up to it's own name of Dabao.
