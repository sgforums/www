    I just went to this Seafood BBQ restaurant at Marina Bay for dinner last night. I find the pricing was quite alright at $12 NETT (Lunch - Dinner)

    Free Soft-Drinks
    Free Potong Ice Cream
    Free 2 sticks of Satay (Choice of Chicken, Pork)
    To & Fro Transport from Marina Bay Bus Stop

    The seafood was pretty fresh and it's free flow too. There's cooked food available, around 10 selection, similar to menu of a typical zhi char store. The chicken stock is quite tasty, doesn't get thirsty after that and hence I reckon there's no MSG or very little being used

    Anyone is there before too? I think it's a pretty good deal and thus sharing with the rest here.
