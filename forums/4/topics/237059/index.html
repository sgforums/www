  • rainee
    12 Feb 07, 12:24
    Went to this ice cream parlor yesterday for dessert after dinner...

    Very nice ice cream served, I ordered the Big Apple ice cream and it comes with 3 very nice to munch apple chips The ice cream has a cinnamon flavor to it too (Price: $6.90)

    Also ordered waffles with scoops of ice cream on top of it...yummy as well. I prefer the waffles which I can get in a shop in City Square, but the strawberry ice cream is really delightful. Very rich in taste

    The price a bit steep, but the cafe has a very nice ambience and is currently nicely decorated to commemorate V-Day. A recommended place to go for dessert on V-Day

    Address:

    11 Kensington Park Road
    Serangoon Garden Estate
    Singapore 557263

    The shop is located at the opposite of the infamous Chomp Chomp, along the same row of 7 Eleven.
