Review of Botak Jones (AMK Branch)

  • soul_rage
    3 May 07, 10:14
    Just had lunch there yesterday

    Here's what I had

    Fish and Chips ($6)
    - A deep-fried dory fillet
    - Spicy fries
    - Coleslaw
    with generous amts of mayo

    Review:
    The fillet is normal size, not really huge. Their menu says they use japanese bread crumbs, which did not make any difference to me. The fillet is tender, not too bad, but other than that, its normal fish fare not unlike normal western food stalls

    Spicy fries were not spicy, rather they had several types of spices on it, so overall the fries were pretty nice-tasting, and quite interesting to eat. Portion of fries was GENEROUS (read the caps)

    Coleslaw was nice, better than KFCs, and portion was quite large too

    NOTE: If you are having lunch, don't order the large portion (just take the regular), I don't think you can finish the large portion, given I was really bloated after my lunch.

    Overall my rating for their fish&chips:
    Value-for-money: 8/10
    Taste: 7/10 (normal fish fillet, but fries were good)
    Overall: 7.5/10

    Not really "damn good food at damn good price", but rather, "quite good food, at quite good price".
