    27 Mar 05, 23:34
    Foodcourt 1: Maybe you can try the chicken chop there? Last time in SP My friends and I usually don't mind queueing for the chicken chop... One best thing in foodcourt 1 is that the food is cheap... $1.50 you can eat a plate of wan ton mee...

    Foodcourt 2: During my 3 years stay in SP, I only tried Mcdonald's in Foodcourt 2...

    Foodcourt 3: I remembered when I just entered SP, the mixed rice stall hawkers are very mean in attitude but since the foodcourt have been renovated, they have been disappeared...

    I came from DECC so foodcourt 3 I patronise regularly... You may try the western food there, also the stewed pork hand-made noodles and the grilled/fried chicken rice at the waffle stall...

    Foodcourt 4 is another one that I patronise during my 3 years memorable stay in SP... The nasi lemak there is not recommended as for example, the potatoes in the curry chickens are damn hard... You may try the Japanese and Indonesian food stalls there... Quite nice... Also the fried rice stall seems not bad also... Top recommendation: The chicken katsu don in the Japanese stall in which my friends and I patronise regularly... Also, the pork chop noodle in Foodcourt 4 is a total turn off...

    Foodcourt 5: Nothing to say as only KFC and Pizza Hut are there...

    Foodcourt 6: Seldom goes there as it is to far for us EEE students... However their fried chicken rice is nice...
