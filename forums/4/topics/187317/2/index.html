    I just wish to share that... err.....this insight

    U know when u dip kaya toast into ur coffee or tea?

    The 'grain' of the bread is actually very important...

    if u dip the bread in with the grain 'perpendicular' to the drink... it doesnt soak as well and have tendency to break into the tea and coffee and end up soggy...

    So better to dip it... grain parallel to side of coffee cup... it holds the liquid better.. and u can suck the coffee and tea.. and bread won't break
