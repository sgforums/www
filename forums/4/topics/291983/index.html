    After that incident with her, I SWEAR i will never ever patronise her restaurant again... if you ask me abt the fish.... objectively? The portion is small - expensive $15-17 for 2 tiny pieces of cod and SOGGY chips.... my hubby's halibut was MISHY.... and our oyster poppers? Not exactly the freshest.... so there, one customer and her friends and relatives lost FOREVER... these ppl should do self-reflection and take a course in CUSTOMER SERVICE!
