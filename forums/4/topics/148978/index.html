    FREE SCRUB WITH PASTRIES

    It's that time of the year again when mooncakes appear in practically every Chinese restaurant in town.

    To stand out from the crowd, Wah Lok Cantonese Restaurant at Carlton Hotel has come up with a rather unusual promotion this year.

    Buy any of its green tea mooncakes or walnut pastries, and you get a complimentary back massage and a choice of a green tea or walnut back scrub at Elements Spa.

    The spa at Orchard Hotel Shopping Arcade is opened by swimmer Leslie Kwok. Presumably, the scrub will be done with spa products and not the mooncake fillings.

    The green tea mooncakes sell for $28 for a box of eight while the walnut pastries go for $24. The latter is delicious, even if a trifle sweet.

    Wah Lok Cantonese Restaurant, Carlton Hotel, 76 Bras Basah Road, Tel: 6311-8188
