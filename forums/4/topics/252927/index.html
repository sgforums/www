Review of Melben Seafood at Toa Payoh Lor 8

  • soul_rage
    7 May 07, 10:02
    Had dinner last night to celebrate my dad's birthday.

    Here's what we have:

    Chilli Crab ($38.50)
    Golden Sand Crab ($38.50)
    Deep Fried Yam Paste wrapped around Seafood salad ($20)
    Stir-Fry Chinese Spinach ($8 )
    Mongolian Pork Ribs ($15)
    Tomato Sauce Baed Tofu ($10)

    Verdict:
    The crabs were nice and huge, but its $35/kg, which is really expensive. In particular the golden sand crab was essentially butter milk crab, which was something special, and the taste is unique.

    The Yam Paste wasn't really nice, coz in the centre, the seafood salad, was really, tastes something like Delifrance crabmeat mayo filling

    Chinese Spinach ok, nothing special

    Mongolian Pork Ribs was nice, but portion was not huge

    Tomato Sauced Based Tofu was nice

    Review:
    Food: 7.5/10 (Crabs were good, but the rest of the food was so-so)
    Value-for-Money: 6/10 (It was really too expensive at $35/kg for crabs. Their price was somewhere in the region of No Signboard, etc, which I just think they are not up to that level to charge such prices)
    Service: 4/10 (Sucks big-time, they just can't wait to kick us out so that the next family can come in to eat)

    Total Avg Rating: 5.8/10

    Won't be going back there again. Can find other places with good crabs at a better price
