    before i forget the tips...i will post it here to share...

    1) Add more oil to the wok. Pour the beated egg in. This is to prevent the egg from sticking to the wok. Another purpose is so that you need no add much oil when u cook the rice.
    2)Cook the egg. Fire must not be too high, if not the egg will become chao tak. Throw in the spring onion next. Make sure the egg is not overcooked.
    3) Cooked rice should be left overnight. Actually, the wetness of the rice is the most important. Keep it as dry as possible with little bit moisture.
    4) After cooking the egg, throw in the rice. Toss it around so that the egg aroma and rice can come together.
    5) Add some seasoning.
    6) Add in the soy sauce. Important thing to remember is that soy sauce should be added around the wok, not onto the rice itself.
    7) Stir fry till aroma comes. Serve.

    SAMBAL FRIED RICE

    this is my recipe for sambal fried rice.... very nice... try it!!! This recipes quantity enough for about 2 person.

    Ingredient:
    2-3 garlic (chopped)
    6 crabmeat (slices)
    mixed vegetable
    hotdog (slices) *Or you can use char siew, chicken...
    Sambal Chilli *see hw spicy you want then you put... more spicy more nicer
    Cooking Oil
    Light Soya Sauce
    3-4 Egg
    Rice *Rice cannot be hot

    Instruction:
    1. Beaten the egg and warm the wok with oil and cooking the egg... make it into smaller pieces.

    2. Scoop the cooked egg on a bowl. Pour oil on the wok and fried the garlic, Don't fried until it is overcooked.

    3. Add mixed vegetable, hotdog and crabmeat into the wok and add soya sauce.

    4. Make sure the mixed vegetable, hotdog and crabmeat is not overcooked. Add rice into the wok and stir-fry it.

    5. Add sambal chilli and soya sauce. Make sure the chilli and soya sauce is spread evenly in the rice.

    6. Beat 1 egg and add into the rice. Make it evenly and pour the cooked egg inside.

    7. You may Garnish and serve hot...

    *** This fried rice is very popular... When i cooked in my chalet... Everytime ppl eat finish....
