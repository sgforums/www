    Asia Pacific Food Expo 2014 (APFE 2014) returns for another year as one of Asia’s largest scale food event with both local & overseas exhibitors exhibiting their quality produce to you, the discerning customer. APFE 2014 aims to bring the best food from the Asian Region into Singapore and lead the charge in grooming Singapore into becoming the food hub of Asia. Expect a host of exciting activities during the Food Expo with daily appearances by popular radio DJs, TV artistes as well as entertaining local and foreign acts at the Central Stage.

    Running from 21 - 25 November 2014 at the Singapore Expo Hall 4, it’s going to be the highlight of any food lover’s calendar. Admission is FREE! So, come on down with your family and friends.
