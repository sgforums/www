Singapore Coffee Festival 2016

  • FireIce
    29 Apr 16, 13:01

    The Singapore Coffee Festival (SCF) 2016 will be Singapore’s first coffee festival, a showcase of Singapore’s speciality coffee, local kopi and cafe lifestyle.

    Held at the F1 Pit building with over 100 exhibitors from the coffee and café industry, the SCF will be an exciting festival first for coffee lovers and drinkers who want to learn more about what goes into making a good cup of coffee.

    Ever curious about how to make a decent cup of coffee? This festival is for you! Want to sample top coffee roasts from around the world? SCF is your one stop shop! Feel like sampling food and specialty coffee from cafes and restaurants all over our lovely little island? Right here! Experience live on-site home barista challenge showcases, educational talk sessions from 

    experts in the coffee industry, a local kopi showcase and more…? Goodness overflows!

    The festival promises to be an interactive multi-sensory experience and a platform to showcase local craftsmanship in speciality coffee, without forgetting Singapore’s unique and rich kopi heritage. Riding on the surging coffee culture in Singapore, SCF will be a fantastic opportunity for local coffee players and Baristas to showcase the Singapore coffee industry. The Singapore Coffee Festival looks to create conversation amongst cafe owners, wholesale retailers and the man on the street – come for a cuppa and stay for the chatter.

     

    Media / Trade Days

    Please note that the first day of the festival (9 June 2016, Thursday) is reserved for members of the media and trade participants only. 

    Timings are as follows:
    11.00am – 9.00pm

    General Public Days

    The festival will be opened to the public from 10 June 2016, Friday to 12 June 2016, Sunday.

    Timings are as follows:
    11.00am – 9.00pm

    Festival goers are reminded to bring along their printed ticket as proof of purchase to be exchanged for a spiffy non-transferrable wrist band at the venue entrance.
