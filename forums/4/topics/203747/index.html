    Most traditional teochew restaurants serve cold crabs, e.g. Mong Hing Teochew Restaurant at KeyPoint - their teochew food is good, and my family likes to eat there.

This shop is interesting bec it looks like a western joint but is actually situated in an old local kopitiam, right behind the bus stop. I see a good crowd on weekends.
