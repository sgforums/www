Bad experience at Dover Road

  • Augustin
    3 Apr 03, 02:43
    I went to this new hawker center along commonwealth ave under a block of new flats just after Singapore Poly...... I can say I had one of the WORST lunches i ever had in my life!! It started with this lady from the thai food stall approaching me as I walked past..... she said her Pineapple Rice is nice... blah blah blah.. all the good talk..... so i fell for it and ordered with my friend.... well.... the pineapple rice turned out to be pukeable!! it was sooooo oily, dry, and full of flosses.....

    that was ok then...... a few minutes later i struggled to finish my meal, my throat had one of the most horrible encounters...... bad acids from the rice went up..... it wasnt one of those where u eat chili then u burp the stuff goes to ur throat..... this was WORSE!!!! to the extent I immediately lost my voice!!!

    The next few days was followed by bad bad flu (not related to SARs) along with lotsa yellow mucus........ lots and lots of them!!!! This is double confirmed when my other friend got it too....

    I will never ever ever eat Pineapple rice again..... its not the dish's fault though.... and... someone call the Ministry of Environment!!

    Gus
