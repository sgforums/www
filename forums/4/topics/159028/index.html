    So Good Coffee, Sally Roast Duck, Rongji Seafood, and the prawn noodle stall moved together to the cactus road area. the Coffeeshop name is Wang Feng Guan coffeeshop.
    The sheng mian stall (orange bowl) shares the same place as the prawn noodle stall (green bowl) and only operates in the morning, but had since closed down due to poor business.
    The Rongji Seafood food quality has deterioted sharply! once i ate the thai style sweet and sour fried fish.. 5mins already come out!! also no business.. remember this seafood stall close on sundays and mondays but now bo pian!

    There is 2 other Zhi Char stalls along the same blocks, as well as a Pub (May Pub), Western Food (Uncle David), banana supplier etc etc.

    the coffee stall,sally duck rice and noodle stall only opens until 2pm last time.. At the old market, stalls opened at night was Harmony Hot & Ccld Drinks (Indian owner), Malay Mee Goreng Stall, Rongji Seafood, the BBQ fish, Turtle soup, sugar cane, hokkien mee, dessert and a few more.

    The 2 soya bean stalls ended business.. one is due to retirement, the other is cannot find a suitable stall space. F&M Muslim Food (good nasi lemak!) moved to Blk 254? at Pasir Ris. The rest of the stalls not sure where they went!

    4 stalls from the wet market merged and moved to neighbouring Jalan Selaseh (fish, chicken, pork, vegetable)
