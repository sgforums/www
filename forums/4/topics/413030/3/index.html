    Alamak .. if you can get my gender wrong, i am quite sure you will not be able to find your way Hahaha. Let me try to give you a rough route you can take. At city square, exit at level 1 through the McDonald dessert counter. Then walk towards Kota Raya shopping mall. You will see the indian temple. Walk along the road inbetween Kota Raya and the indian temple (in the direction of the traffic). To go to Ya Wang, take the right lane into Jalan Segget (there is a huge duck the size of big bird, hanging outside the shop). To go to Hua Mui, walk straight, further down the road. It is at the corner of the roll of shop houses. Hope it helps.
