    my fren buy de plate and takoyaki sauce from dat jap supermarket at liang courts one, i think. den the flour, fish flakes can buy from daiso. mayo, seaweed, and other filling can get from normal supermarket except tako..

    if can, i'd add in pickled ginger oso.

    quite simple to cook one, even i oso can. juz mix in flour, water and egg until its liquid mixture. one egg, 1 part flour 2.5 part water.

    den heat up the plate, oil it, den i pour mixture into the holes, den when its abit dry le, den i join 2 halves takoyaki into one bigger ball. den i juz roll around until i sian.

    take out.. put sauce, mayo, seaweed and fish flakes over it can le.
