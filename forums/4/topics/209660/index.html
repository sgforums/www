Good Morning Nanyang Cafe

  • MooKu
    11 Sep 06, 13:35
    This place is on the ground floor of the old Majestic theatre, located in Chinatown. It's basically a kopitiam without a real tiam.

    The special item on the menu is ciabatta toast and orange ciabatta toast.

    I think the ciabatta toast is just ciabatta with kaya and butter.

    I just bought the orange ciabatta today (there's still Â¾ of it left in front of me). I can't really tell what they smeared on it except butter. There's something else that looks like kaya but doesn't taste like kaya and doesn't really look like kaya either. I wonder if that's where the orange part comes from.

    *takes a bite*

    Slight citrus flavor only.

    Ciabatta bread is suspicious! I think it's an imposter. It looks like ciabatta, with the shape and the bread surface texture and powdery covering. But I don't remember ciabatta tasting completely like normal bread. At least not the one from SwissBake.

    Price

    Slightly costlier than your usual kopitiam grub (probably because it's indoors and air-conditioned?).

    Kaya toast : $1.20 each.
    Ciabatta toast : $1.80 each
    Orange Ciabatta : $2.20

    Forgot the kopi and teh costs.

    But the iced kopi costs $1.10, and is too sweet for my liking, although it tastes really milky.

    Has anyone else tried the ciabatta toast before?
