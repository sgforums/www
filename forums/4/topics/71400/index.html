    Strongly recommend a new cze char restaurant in woodlands at 354A Woodlands Ave 1. It is between Rosewood condo and causeway point.

    Its crabs are fabulous. Even better than Still Road and No Signboard.

    Its steam fish head hong kong style and hong kong style yee mee which is famous in hotels and high end restaurants are also very good.

    Cereal prawns, fried rice and steamed fish are also better than most other places. The taste is also unique as the chef has created his own variances and really taste good.
