    Ok i took tour bus from genting, so stop off point at boon lay village.
    So went up 2nd level found a Tze Char stall called Hong Kong street. Ordered Har Gao, Shark fin ( fake one la i poor kid), Horfun, Prawn paste chicky and Crab.

    Har Gao ==> all Tim Sum $2 only ( Siew mai also comes with fresh prawn not cheapo pork)

    Shark fin ==> $3 bucks ( shark fin although fake but the crab meat inside is real!!)

    Horfun ==> $3 (forgot liao, but alot of liao4, fish slice vege and prawn )

    Prawn paste chicky ==> $8 ( Beri big plate and the chicken is juicy and fragrant)

    Crab ==> $28/kg ( Veri worth it in my opinion)

    Total damage $42 for 4 pax. IMHO, Very worth it, the prawns in the Har gao is those u eat liao will "tiong tiong" in ur mouth one. They also serve Xiao long Bao at $5 bucks per basket although i dunoe how many inside.

    So if got time go try!! Very nice!!

    -------------------------------- Directions -----------------------------------------------------

    Take MRT to Lake side and take bus 240.
    Drop at Boon lay village.
    There are 2 buildings if i nt wrong, with 1 with the 7-11 store.
    Climb to lvl 2 (the building w 7-11 de) den try to find lo..
    Outside design nuthing must see inside den got write " Hong Kong Street".

    Ya dazzit! Got time go ok!! If not if u go like 3 yrs later the portion giu jui or
    price increase den veri sad liao.
