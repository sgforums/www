    anyone tried preserved cockles? yummy

    best with teochew porridge

    get a jam jar, fill about 1/4 with dark soy sauce and minced garlic and about a couple of sliced chilli

    shell the cooked cockles and make sure they're clean.. pop them into the jam jar...

    continue until jar is almost full.. top up with more dark soy sauce and more smashed garlic...

    cover, put in the fridge...

    good for about a week...

    great stuff when eating porridge
