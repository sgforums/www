    Seafood is usually avoided not because it is poisonous, but mainly because the risk of internal infection from eating it after an op is higher than other consumables.

    Especially if you are taking antibiotics after an op involving your kidneys, liver or intestines. (Digestive system related)

    The doctor should be able to properly advise you on what to avoid and what to eat. For one, i can recommend you take more liver in your diet.
