    italian food, especially pizza is totally different from the american crap we have aka pizza hut. if you go to italy you will realise that the pizza is somewhat like our hawker food, except that its almost 3 or 4 times the price. just plain and simple: crispy crust, some tomato paste, optional cheese mostly mozerella, some herbs and maybe vegetables to go along. and they do have lots of other foods depending on what region. the food in the tuscan region is very different from lets say the southern regions like sicily. same thing with chinese food, french food, spanish food. i guess the only "standard" food is american junk food, and we all love our burgers, not macdonalds, i mean a good grilled beef patty done to your liking be it medium or medium well etc, generous slices of real cheddar and fatty strips of bacon..ahh..decadence..
