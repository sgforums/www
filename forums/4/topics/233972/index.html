    Nowadays with so microwavable, instant food like items from Q-bites... do you gals still cook at home?? Or does your hubby cook?

    Although I am a guy, I find that cooking is relatively simple and interesting.

    One simple thing to cook is like spaghetti. All you need first boil the pasta in a pot, this normally takes 7-10 mins.
    While waiting, you can dice up your fav items to add to your pasta. For example, onions and garlic to add fragrant to your cooking. Ham, prawns, cuttlefish and maybe diced some tomatoes and peppers.
    Once you get those ready, heat up some oil and fry up the garlic and onion first, then add the prawns ,cuttlefish, ham, tomatoes and peppers. By the time all this had been prepared, your pasta should be soften and ready to add to your pan. Add the pasta and Ketchup/ pasta sauce. If ketchup is use, pls remember to add sugar, water and some starch.
    Pinch of grounded pepper to finshed.

    I can do all these within 15 mins.
