    Depends on whether it's from an Indian stall, Malay stall or Chinese stall...

    For Malay and Chinese stalls, majority of them serve roti john with normal chilli sauce... As for Indian roti john, you can request for curry instead of chilli sauce...

    As for the fillings, you can ask for a plain roti john with egg and onion only...

    And as for the bite size chunks, you can ask the stall holder to help you to cut lah...
