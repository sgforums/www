    i attended my workplace gathering just last weekend and savoured one of the best tiramisu and cheesecakes ever found right here in Singapore.

    EKAC (Everyone Kneads A Cake)
    www.ekac.com.sg
    No. 7 One North Gateway, #01-14, Singapore 138642.
