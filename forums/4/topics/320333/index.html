    Just to share something that i accidently found today after deciding to find a new place to eat dinner.

     

    If anyone of you remember this stall in Shaw Tower that sells very good chicken chop/cutlet, san bei ji and also the very nice noodles, i think you will be very interested to find out where they have shifted to since the food court closed.

     

    I loved their food very much and just today while at this food court i found the lady stall helper very familar and upon looking at the chef working inside the kitchen, it suddenly dawn on me that they have now shifted here.

     

    Tried a chicken chop fried rice,thou the portion is abit smaller now, the standard is still there, cooked by that same one and only male chef.

     

    So anyway their new location is at the bras basah complex's first floor food court, right at one corner, you can't miss it esp since there is a queue waiting for their food.
