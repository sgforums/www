    Well, i have went to some hawker centres and coffee shops to try out the different food stalls, recently i went to 2 western food stalls one is a sole owned and the other is a chain company.

Before that there is only one western food stall at the Blk 43 coffee shop which is directly opp the CC. So i saw that their business was abit quiet thus i decided to ask the stall holder abit. They say since the bar open, business went down and affected the whole coffee shop but still many ppl say bar is not that nice their westen food just sucks. Thus i would like to recommend food lovers to the western food stall at Blk 43.

    Blk 43 Cambridge Rd, just opp Pek Kio CC, you can take bus 131 from Novena Sq bus stop, then alight at the 3rd stop (nt counting the boarding stop at Novena Sq)
