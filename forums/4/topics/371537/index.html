    Food Nation
    Singapore, August 7, 2009 - Derived from a cooking method where the rice is soaked in coconut cream, nasi lemak literally means “rice in cream”.

    It is usually wrapped in banana leaf, together with cucumber slices, ikan bilis, roasted peanuts, an egg and sambal chilli. Chicken wings and otah otah are often added to it. The sambal chilli is usually the make-or-break factor for nasi lemak.

    NAM SAN (top)
    WHERE: 267 Joo Chiat Rd
    OPEN: 24-hours
    CONTACT: 6345 5542

    The nasi lemak at Nam San is made from a Nonya recipe. It is fragrant, rich and the mackerel banana leaf otah is the main draw – get the nasi lemak combo meal.

    CHONG PANG NASI LEMAK
