    For good value for your hard earn money ...Aston is highly recommended. They got many outlets all over the lion city. Really like the grill fish fillet with herbal sauce, black pepper chicken cutlet, cheese sauage, baked potato, cheesey fries and all time fav bbq baked beans (most places serve tomato kepchat).

    http://www.astons.com.sg/

    price reasonable, average about $6.50 for a hearty meal. One main + 2 side dishes.

    For fat juicy chicken wings go to chomp chomp hawker centre. Don't go during the weekend, very very crowded.
