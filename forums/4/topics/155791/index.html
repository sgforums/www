    the food at Poison Ivy (the restaurant) (the farm is Bollywood Veggies) is good, not too greasy.

    A couple of things worth trying -

    kueh pie ti - fresh and crunchy.
    banana curry - sweet and slightly spicy
    fig tea - refreshing... especially if you've spend the morning walking around the farms there.
    they also have banana cakes for sale... they taste good when you toast them before eating...

    they are not opened for dinner and also not opened Mondays.

    there is a bus service 925C that goes past on weekends from Woodlands Regional Centre... about 25min freq...then walk a bit.

    been there a few times already.

    price wise... middle range restaurant prices.
