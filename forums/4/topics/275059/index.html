    You can try the following Chicken rice outlets:

    Non-Franchise:
    1. Chien Chien (bugis St) at Longhouse, Thomson Rd
    2. Nam Kee Chicken Rice [along the same stretch of Thomson road as (1)]
    3. Chicken House - serves Kumpung Chicken Rice, also along thomson road stretch as (1)
    4. Chicken rice at Upper Thomson road at the same place as the Famous Ampung Yong Tow Foo Stall (Near the shell station, close to the T-junction of Mandai Road, Sembawang road and upper thomson road)
    5. Margaret Drive Xin Ji Chicken Rice (2nd floor)
    5. Tian Tian Chicken Rice (Maxwell Food Centre)

    Franchised:

    1. 5 Star CHicken Rice (Thomson Road and various other locations)
    2. Boon Tong Kee (Bukit Timah, River Valley and Balestier Road)
    3. Sergeant Chicken Rice (Food republic outlets, so-so to me lah...)
    4. Loy Kee Best (Food court outlets, also so-so lah)

    For other locations, you can check out Leslie Tay's blog
