    Originally posted by 4sg:

    I love Hor Fun, never get tired of it. Anyone know what is the name the scauce used in Hor Fun?

    Name your favourite Hor Fun place here.

    Sauce : 1st -Cook ingredient (prawns, sotong, vege), 2nd - Pour water (slightly more than the hor fun sauce you eat every time) 3rd- Add hor fun (let it boil and cook horfun). 4th- Add thicker (Do not just pour the powder, stir it in a different small dish making it milky then pour into horfun). 5th - Cook/boil it is ready.

    Favourite Hor Fun place : The food court opp. Lasalle/Sim Lim Sq. The foodcourt is just under Sheng Shiong. You should try. It is from a Thai stall. Near the western food stall. Try it! Different way of cooking Horfun!
