    The fish head curry in Geylang: To suit his Chinese customers, one Indian man re-created this dish in his restaurant in Tank Road about 30 years back.

    Food Nation
    Singapore, August 7, 2009 - It combines tradtional South Indian fish curry and Chinese delicacy, the fish head.

    Fish head curry is available in both Indian and Chinese restaurants and is often served bubbling in a the large clay pot.

    37 HENG KEE HOUSE OF STEAM FISH (top)
    WHERE: 671 Geylang Rd
    OPEN: 11.30am to 2.30pm, 5pm to 1am
    CONTACT: 6747 4840

    You can pick your fish head of choice here.

    Customers can select fish such as Golden Snapper, Lion Head Snapper and Red Grouper fish head from the eatery’s ice box.

    The chef prepares each dish by his own hand, and his signature style is to remove the eggplant skin so it absorbs more curry.

    Thick and creamy, the curry also packs an appetizing sour punch.
