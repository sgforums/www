    Originally posted by mhcampboy:
    got simple recipe how to make smoothies??
    They're simple but you'll need one of those blenders that can blend ice cubes.

    The method is basically blend:
    - a handful of ice cubes or more (up to individual taste)
    - peeled & fresh fruits (cut into small pcs)
    - half to 3/4 cup of fresh/low fat milk or plain yoghurt
    - 2 teaspoons of honey (optional)

    Top with crunchy cereals or muesli or chopped fruit (optional) - don't blend.

    You can try mixing:
    - banana + strawberries
    - more mango + milk
    - banana + mixed berries

    Just make sure to use fresh fruits and fresh milk. Enjoy!
