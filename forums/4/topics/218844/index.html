    this rice got coke added in as ingredient

    ARROZ CON POLLO

    2 cup rice (not instant)
    2 cup cooked chicken, cut in 1 inch strips
    1 med. onion, chopped
    1 green pepper
    1 sliced tomato
    1/2 sliced red onion
    1/2 cup peas
    1/2 cup carrots
    1 small box raisins
    1 can Coca Cola or Pepsi
    Garlic powder
    Cumin
    Salt and pepper
    Oil
    Butter

    1. Boil chicken with chopped onions, 1/2 green pepper, 1 tablespoon garlic powder, 1/2 tablespoon cumin, 1 teaspoon salt, and pepper or poultry seasoning (if desired). Boil slowly until chicken is cooked. Reserve broth for cooking rice.
    2. Fry red onion, 1/2 green pepper, tomato with 2 tablespoons oil or butter. When braised, add raisins, peas, carrots, and chicken. Cook 2 to 3 minutes on low heat.

    3. Mix rice with 3 cups chicken broth and Coca Cola. Cook until rice is done.

    4. Mix ingredients in Steps 2 and 3. Add 2 tablespoons oil, 1 tablespoon butter; add pepper to taste. Cook 30 minutes on low heat. Makes 4 servings.
