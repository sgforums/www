    B and B
    -------------

    4-6 cracked ice cubes
    1 measure brandy
    1 measure benedictine

    1/ Put the ice cubes into a mixing glass.
    2/ Pour the brandy and Benedictine over the ice and stir to mix.
    3/ Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.

    Zombie
    -----------

    4-6 crused ice cubes
    2 measures dark rum
    2 measures white rum
    1 measure golden rum
    1 measure triple sec
    1 measure lime juice
    1 measure orange juice
    1 measure pineapple juice
    1 measure guava juice
    1 tbsp grenadine
    1 tbsp orgeat
    1 tbsp pernod

    1/ Put the crushed ice cubes into a blender.
    2/ Add the three rums, triple sec, lime juice, orange juice, pineapple juice, guava huice, grenadine, orgeat and pernod.
    3/ Blend until smooth.
    4/ Pour without straining into a tall chilled glass.

    Singapore Sling

    * 1 1/2 ounce gin
    * 1/2 ounce Cherry Herring brandy
    * 1/4 ounce Cointreau
    * 1/4 ounce Benedictine
    * 4 ounces pineapple juice
    * 1/2 ounce lime juice
    * 1/3 ounce grenadine
    * dash bitters

    Shake with ice. Strain into an ice filled collins glass. Garnish with cherry and slice of pineapple.

    Tequila Sunrise
    ---------------------

    4-6 cracked ice cubes
    2 parts white tequila
    orange juice
    1 measure grenadine

    1/ Put the cracked ice cubes into a chilled tumbler.
    2/ Pour the tequila over the ice and top up with orange juice.
    3/ Stir well to mix.
    4/ Slowly pour in the grenadine and serve.

    What the Hell
    ---------------------

    4-6 cracked ice cubes
    dash of lime juice
    1 measure gin
    1 measure apricot brandy
    1 measure dry vermouth

    1/ Put the cracked ice cubes into a mixing glass.
    2/ Dash the lime juice all over the ice.
    3/ Pour in the gin, apricot brandy and vermouth.
    4/ Stir well to mix.
    5/ Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.

    Diablo
    -----------

    6-8 cracked ice cubes
    2-3 strips of lime peel
    1 measure lime juice
    3 measures white tequila
    1 measure creme de cassis

    1/ Fill a small, chilled tumbler with cracked ice cubes.
    2/ Add the lime peel.
    3/ Pour the lime juice over the ice.
    4/ Add the tequila and creme de cassis.
    5/ Serve.
