NZ Ice Cream Buffet @ Mingles

  • TehJarVu
    23 Sep 07, 21:09
    Mingles (at Velocity)
    238 Thomsom Road #02-78/79
    Singapore 307685
    Tel: 6253-4650



    What They Have:-

    (1) NZ Natural Ice Cream Free Flow, with Free flow Waffles, and 1 tea/coffee
    3pm to 6pm, everyday
    adult $11.80
    children $9.80

    (no promotion period... it's everyday.......)



    (2) Or Whole Day Deal (Special Diner's Deal or something like that....)
    you can order any main dish, and you get 1 compliment, plus 1 tea/coffee

    the 1 compliment, you can either choose

    a) soup + roll
    b) waffle + 1 scoop of ice cream, or
    c) 2 scoops of ice cream with fresh cream

    And if you add 1 more dollar, you can replace your tea/coffee with a ROOT BEER FLOAT!!!!!!!!!



    i had the above chicken salad
    only $8.90,

    so i paid another dollar, and i get the chicken salad, 2 scoops of NZ ice cream (i chose macadamia premium and rum and raisin) with fresh cream and a root beer float!!!

    (sorry forgot to take picture of the 2 scoops of ice cream!!!)

    they serve many types of sausages too....

    i don't remember the menu... you have to go and see for yourself...
