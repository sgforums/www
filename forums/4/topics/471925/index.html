    Attention fellow foodies:

    If you don't already know, the Annual Singapore Food Festival begins tomorrow, 12 July, right outside Bayfront MRT station! 

    Admission is free so come on down and eat till your hearts' content with up to 40 different stalls and 120 dishes including all of Singapore’s Top 20 Favourites such as chilli crab, rojak, oyster omelette and many more!

    Prices start from as cheap as $2.00!! 

    Details are as follows
    Date: 12th July - 21st July 2013
    Time: 11am - 11pm
    Venue: Bayfront Avenue @ Marina Bay (Bayfront Station exit A, Raffles Place exit J)
    Admission: Free

    For more information please head to:
    http://singaporefavouritefood.com/2013/index.html
