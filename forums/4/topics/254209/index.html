    Anybody went to Indulgz before? It's located at Bugis area and I'm just back from there for Mother's day celebration.

    I think the food is nice, however the waiting time is quite long. Hmm, apparently, there was only 1 waiter and 2 chef that I saw. Not much customers and the place is small but nice. The ambience there is suitable for any celebration I guess....

    We tried the butterfish steak, ribeye steak (medium well), tomato basil, chocolate fondue, chicken bites in tangy sauce, strawberry yoghurt smoothie, banana smoothie, coffee. Cost $150+ for all the above mentioned, because it's mother's day today, they gave my mother a stalk of flower and took an instant photo for us and gave us on the spot.
