Singapore's first Hello Kitty cafe is opening in May

  • FireIce
    25 Mar 16, 13:57

    It's official---a cafe dedicated to the world-famous mouthless cat will be opening at Changi Airport Terminal 3 in May this year. 

    Taking inspiration from the orchid, which is Singapore's national flower, the cafe features a "garden city" theme with plenty of greenery and whimsical blooms. While details of the menu have yet to be released for now, those who have been to Hello Kitty cafes overseas will know to expect food and drinks emblazoned with the cartoon character. Hello Kitty Orchid Garden will operate 24 hours and offer all-day breakfasts and desserts like gelato sundaes, waffles, pastries and tea cakes. 
     
    The cafe will also have a retail space selling Hello Kitty merchandise such as tea blends and grow-it-yourself plant kits, all of which are exclusive to Singapore.
     
    For updates, head to the cafe's Instagram and Facebook page.
