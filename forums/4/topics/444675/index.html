    Fourre Cookies by Hellema are a truly special Dutch treat. They are 2 biscuits, just lightly sweet, with a cream filling, making them a classic sandwich cookie. Very under rated but with a strong following. The biscuit aren't chewy but just melt in your mouth. Come with chocolate, vanilla and strawberry cream flavours. Start your new year with a pack of this! Can get it at cold storage or ntuc.. around $3.50 cents each.
