Singapore Craft Beer Week 2016

  • FireIce
    12 Aug 16, 13:08

    It’s hard work keeping track of all the craft beer events going on in Singapore these days, and now there’s another one on the horizon you need to know about. Singapore Craft Beer Week returns for its third edition from Aug 20 to 27.

    The event kicks off on Aug 20 with a tour of Singapore’s oldest microbrewery, Paulaner Brauhaus, an institution celebrating its 20th anniversary this year. Expect $20 deals on food and drinks. The party moves to Kult Kafe on Aug 21 for Sunday Beers on the Hill featuring stalls from Archipelago, Eastern Craft, The Mad Tapper and more with live music and DJs. Other highlights include an opportunity to meet Singapore’s first female brewmaster, Crystalla Huang, discountsat all Brewerkz outlets and a mini music festival at Molly Malone’s Irish Pub.
     
    For more information, head to the Singapore Craft Beer Week website.
