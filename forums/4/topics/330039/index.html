    Olive Tree Restaurant
    Popular in Singapore for its light, healthy cooking style with an emphasis on fresh herbs and spices, Olive Tree Restaurant has been winning awards for years now. Located in the InterContinental Singapore hotel, Olive Tree serves up a buffet lunch and dinner daily as well its popular Sunday Brunch.

    For the Buffet Lunch and dinner on weekdays there’s a selection of appetizers, salads, meat carvings, hot mains and a live pasta station.

    On Fridays and Saturdays the Gourmet Mediterrranean Seafood Buffet Dinner takes over, serving fresh seafood from the Mediterranean, including fresh oysters, clams, prawns, mussels, slipper lobsters and many more.

    Then on Sundays look out for the Sunday Brunch at Olive Tree, which offers a sumptuous spread of fresh lobsters, king prawns, freshly-shucked oysters, tiger prawns, mussels and smoked Norwegian salmon alongside pasta, tapas, meats and desserts all for one fixed price.

    Address: InterContinental Singapore, 80 Middle Road, Singapore 188966.
    Tel: (65) 6825 1061.
    Lunch: 12pm-2.30pm Mon-Fri.
    Dinner: 6pm-10.30pm Sun-Thu; 6pm-10.30pm Fri-Sat.
    Sunday Brunch: 11.30am-3.30pm
