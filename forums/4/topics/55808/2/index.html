    lived in clementi all my life, know all about the food here.
    in clementi central
    1) Boon Kee noodles- Central market, nice wantan noodles
    2) You tiao etc- beside Boon Kee
    3) Fried carrot cake--also in the market, old man with straw hat
    4) Nam kee fired noodles--fried prawn noodles, but have to wit damn long
    5) Tanglin western food--give the chicken chop a try!
    6) Satay--next to Tanglin western food
    7) Fried oyster omelette--next to satay stall
    Curry puffs---nearer to the toilets
    9) Chwee Kway--next to the curry puff stall
    10) Tiong Bahru roast pig---this is a new stall, try it out!!
    11) Kway Chap--S11 @NTUC, excellent stuff
    12) Tenderfresh Western Food--S11 @NTUC
    13) Peanut pancake---S11@NTUC
    14)Tiong bahru porridge--Teck Hin coffee shop@interchange
    15) Clementi Bros rojak
    16) BBQ stringray--Kopitiam
