    Today:
    Hehe.. here's a bit of a quick lunch, 5 minutes to pluck the herbs, 5 minutes to wash and prepare the ingredients, 20 minutes to marinate, 30minutes to cook and plate. Do forgive the ugly plating (didn't want to waste a piping bag for the little bit of sauce)

    Ingredients

    1. sprigs of fresh rosemary
    2. fresh basil leaves
    3. chopped garlic
    4. olive oil
    5. lingonberry concentrate
    6. strawberry vodka
    7. fresh cherries
    8. chicken thigh
    9. juice from half a lemon
    10. pasta of your choice

    Pan-fried Rosemary Chicken Thigh with Ligonberry Marinate and Strawberry Vodka


    1. Marinate chicken thigh with some salt, lemon juice, rosemary, ligonberry concentrate and strawberry vodka
    2. Pan fry it until cooked, add a spash of vodka to char-flame it for a bit
    3. Serve with fresh cherries and lingonberry sauce

    Aglio Olio


    1. Boil water with a pinch of salt (pun intended)
    2. Cook pasta in boiling water until al dente
    3. Drain the pasta and blanche with ice cold water
    4. lighly pan fry with garlic and olive oil
    5. add in crushed (use fingers to tear it) fresh basil leaves 1 minute before it's done
    6. Tuck in~
