    dont particular like meekong...but lots of celebrities did visit there.
    there's other nice vietnam resturants too...do try the crispy chicken rice...i find it really nice...

    along swanston street..there this pizza paulor for take away going at $1. can try this satay stick too at $3.

    Along Chinatown...there a few good resturant. try Orchid Garden (Singaporean owner). also can try shanghai food there diag opp. of Orchid Garden (forgot the name). There's this Chairman Mao resturant too..but i find it normal.
    Do visit Red silk and dessert house (try lemon grass chicken for both) @ Midcity (not sure if remember the name correctly,-but is one of the major building next to village Cinema [Chinatwon is just behind these building])....u go in, u will see lots of Asian and got those "hello kitty", "toy shops" and this 'sour plums' shop.

    St kilda is a must to try out those chocolate cakes. Also can try the gelato there

    Lygon as mention...are nice italian food and gelato. Yes go for Durian , mango. There's this pizza joint at lygon..that is going half price for all pizza and pasta on every tuesday or thursday...(i got bad memory liao)

    Russel street...got a hk resturant...not bad...

    Else...if u see any noodle box house...go in and try...i like those alot...noodles in the box.

    Fast Food...just go for the wraps...is nice....to me especially Hungry Jacks aka BK. KFC nothing special...not worth to go. McDonald....err they put beetroot in McOZ burger.
    Ali Baba also can go try.....

    U find lots of Fish and Chips there and i think they are much better than SG standard. (Smith Street got a fish n chip resturant opp crumpler shop that is good)

    (Go bridge road (richmond), there this jap resturant, or Swan street (richmond) there is a vietnam owned resturant that sell ncie sausage rolls)

    Else just go crown casino and try $2 fish n chips..but small...inside crown there is this cafe this is famous for cheese cakes...do try.

    Finally..really want to save $...go to Target Supermarket to get ur food and cook urself....oh yeah above the level of Target, there r nice jap food too.

    Go to melbourne central too...there are nice resturants and food courts.....can try those "wraps".....

    be prepare to pay $6 onwards but...the portion is big...think can share between 2 ppl....and one more thing..i personally find it very salty.

    cant recall anything...brain too dead...and failing memory.
    but soon u get used to it....
