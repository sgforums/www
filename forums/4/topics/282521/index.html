    quote :

    Bahan-Bahan:
    600 gm ubi kayu
    1 cawan gula
    Â½ biji kelapa parut(dibuang kulit ari)
    Garam secukup rasa

    Ingredients:
    600gm tapioca
    1 cup sugar
    Â½ a skinned coconut, grated
    Salt to taste

    Cara:
    Kupas kulit ubi kayu dan bersihkan. Kemudian rebus dengan sedikit garam hingga empuk. Setelah itu, angkat dan toskan airnya. Getuk ubi panas-panas hingga gebu. Masukkan gula dan kelapa. Getuk lagi hingga ubi kelihatan melekat-lekat. Sediakan sebuah loyang. Masukkan ubi tadi dan tekan sehingga rata dan padat. Bila telah sejuk, bolehlah dipotong dan dihidangkan.

    Skin the tapioca and clean thoroughly. Then boil the skinned tapioca with a pinch of salt until cooked. Drain the tapioca. Mash the cooked tapioca while it's still very hot until smooth. Add in sugar and the grated coconut. Mash the mixture again until it is somewhat sticky in texture. Prepare a baking tray (preferably those aluminium tray where muah chee is prepared). Flatten the tapioca mixture on the baking tray. Allow it to cool before cutting it into the desired shapes, then serve.
