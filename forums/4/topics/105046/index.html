    consists of this (dunno what u call the main cake material, sponge?) with a chocolate core that u can heat it up and it spills out the moment u cut it.

    the base of it is a biscuit-like crust that appears to splinters easily

    taste review:

    generally this chocolate (cake?) suffers from a prob that plagues most chocolates. i noticed that if u eat this cake too fast, there is gross feeling that lingers in the mouth.

    the 'sponge' is well-done, not too moist not too dry, just nice

    the sore point comes from the bottom crust which doesnt seem to go well with this cake and the icing doesnt seem to fit in(taste and appearance wise)

    8.5/10
