  • zx6r

    hi all.....

    just wanted to warn all those who like Japanese food and sushi to avoid patronising the Edo Sushi at the Thompson Plaza...

    the service was horrible and the staff on duty were an utter embarrassment to service staff in Singapore...

    on the 3rd of May, i patronised the branch of Edo Sushi at Thompson Plaza, thinking that it would provide the same service standard that i got from other branches.

    Little did i know that the waiters and waitresses would choose to sit at the table across from mine and pay NO ATTENTION to my constant waving for service.

    they would stare at my table and then start singing to the music playing over the system in the restaurant... and worse was that the guy and girl singing.... were obviously tone-deaf...

    another table that had left after i arrived, was not cleared of it's plates and rubbish all over the table for 20mins, and the only person to serve and clean up the entire place was the Duty Manager... thus, i had to even walk up to the manager to ask her to let me pay the bill....

    in hindsight, maybe i should have just ;eft without paying the service charge and the bill.....
