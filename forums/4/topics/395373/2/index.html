    First Post! =) *Fanfare to welcome the newbie*

    Now thats done... A&W was always a fav of mine when it was here in singapore. Who wouldnt like chopped onions and minced meat on a hot dog wrapped in a soft warm bun, or 2 scoops of vanilla ice cream in a huge ice cold mug filled to the brim with root beer?

    Nowadays, the only way to get that A&W fix is to take a drive up north, way north into Malaysia, where A&W outlets thrive along side the likes of Krispy Kreme and Wendy's, another 2 fantastic places that have yet to grace our shores.  

    Then again for the foodies that DO drive up to Johore or KL along the NS highway, do stop at the Ayer Keroh R&R. Its home to a nice A&W outlet, nothing is as refreshing as a root beer float after a 3 hour drive.
