    I went there last week for lunch buffet ($19++ =$22). I was expecting it to be something like those in other countries, with alot of different kind of meat to taste. The one i had in San Fransico has 60+ types of meat and cooking. I wasnt expect that many in this restaurant, hoping for at least 1/2 of it.

    I was shock that for the whole 1 1/2 hr there, they only serve beef, chicken, sausages, ham and pineapple. And there is only 1 type of cooking for each meat. They keep repeating those few until we decided not to take anymore. Although there is a small section with soup, salad and some dishes, still it is very disappointing to me.

    After 2 hr, we decided to leave then we saw them start serving some bbq fish and buns. We gave them a pass because the buns look hard and the fish looks very dry.

    I wouldnt be disappointed if they would just brand themselves as a buffet restaurant, somehow with the Name they have, i just expect a little more.
