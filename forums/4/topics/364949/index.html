    this is the URL, http://www.soshiok.com/articles/12895 Singapore, May 22, 2009 - The "Ultimate" Crab Buffet will be featured from June 25 to July 12, at Ellenborough Market Cafe, Swissotel Merchant Court. Begin your meal with appetisers such as Thai Style Black Pepper Crab with Glass Vermicelli and Coral Crab Bisque with Sago Pearls. Then move on to indulge in the big spread of dishes, ranging from Flower Crab with Sweet & Sour Dip to Black Pepper Sri Lanka Crab to Wok-fried Sri Lanka Chilli Crab with Man Tou. Dishes in the buffet also include others, besides crab. They are Paper Wrapped Chicken with Chilli Dip, Braised Beef Brisket with Jade Turnip Vegetables Curry, Ikan Chuan Chuan and many more. End your meal with desserts like New York Cheese Cake, Chocolate Fountain or Banana Sago Soup. This buffet will only be available for dinner from Monday to Sunday at $46++ per adult and $23++ per child between the ages of 4 and 11 years. Ellenborough Market Cafe Swissotel Merchant Court 20 Merchant Road Tel: 6239 1848 Nearest MRT station: Clarke Quay
