    Rojak (chinese style with shriump paste)

    Where is your favourite rojak?   Where to find the best rojak?

    My favourite are the ones from Whampoa Food Centre and HDB Hub Food Court.

    The one from Whampoa tastes real nice and different from others but rather pricey.     The rojak from HDB Hub Food Court is value for money - more ingredients compared to others.  Add ingredients like kangkong, beansprout, cuttlefish and price is real cheap from $2 onwards.

    The rojak at Kopi Tiam at Swissotel, Stamford Road, is nice, too.
