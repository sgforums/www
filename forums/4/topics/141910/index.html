    urm, u guys need to find out more abt the word halal

    It doesnt mean no pork only. Generally, Muslims can eat only food that are prepared according to the Muslim rites, especially on meat.

    Plus not all kind of food can be consumed, such as certain type of seafood, which is one of Japanese main food type.

    Besides, some food are not made from pork, but may contain stuff from pork. Remember there is one big hooha in Indonesia when they found out that the MSG Aji-no-moto contains certain extracts from pigs? We wouldnt noe.

    As to which type of food is considered halal also vary from country to country. SG's Muis has different definition of halal from Malaysia's one.

    Unless your Muslim fren is a liberal Muslim who eat anything that is non pork, otherwise, very hard.
