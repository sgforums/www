    for a starter, why don't you just go try those "beef noodles" stalls at food courts...

    try kway teow with either the "raw" (sliced beef) or the "cooked" (stewed beef) and with soup.. this is the basic and gives you most of the good taste of the beef and broth...

    my favourite beef noodle stall is at the basement of the Army Market at Beach Road.. not to be confused with the one on the 1st floor (which some prefer)

    the broth at the basement stall is more subtle and light.. the one upstairs is a bit overpowering and the stallkeeper seems to be very rude
