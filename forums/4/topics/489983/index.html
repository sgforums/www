    Looking for good food and snacks this weekend? There’s no better place to be than World Food Fair 2015 organised by Exhibits Inc (SPH) happening at the hall 5 in Singapore Expo this weekend from 10 – 13 September starting from as early as 11am till 10pm supper time.

    Get to sample the world’s cuisines from over 100+ F&B establishments at this finger-licking event and among the exhibitors present includes leading food and beverage manufacturers, distributors and suppliers of meat, poultry and seafod, rice products, instant foods, soy bean products, edible oils, ice-cream, caterers, health products and supplements plus many more.

    Visit the official World Food Fair website for more information or find out the special deals and promotions available during the event.
