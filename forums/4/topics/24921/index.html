    I know of chicken sandwich, egg sandwich, egg+luncheon meat sandwich, egg+tuna sandwich, ham+cheese sandwich etc.

    Chicken sandwich:

    1) Boil some chicken meat... from the drumsticks or breasts also can... then SHRED 'em! Mwahaha. oops!

    2) Mix with mayonnaise and salt + pepper to taste.

    Egg sandwich:

    SImply boil some eggs and add mayo and salt and pepper.

    Egg + Tuna:

    Add tuna to the egg sandwich mixture.

    Egg + Luncheon Meat:

    1) Fry an egg and lightly fry some luncheon meat. Be careful not to let the luncheon meat dry up. Just make the surface a bit crispy.

    2) Kiap the egg and luncheon meat in the middle of two pieces of bread.
