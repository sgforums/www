    there is this highly recommended one at MS....

    REAL FOOD DAILY
    5 Mohamed Sultan Road, #01-01
    Tel: 6733-8231

    Try their tofu stack.
    Up to 80 per cent of this casual-cool diner's menu is prepared with organic ingredients.

    It is famous for its breakfast spread, chunky burgers and health-conscious desserts like organic warm apple pie and flourless chocolate cake.

    What's more, you can ask for low-fat alternatives like rice mayonnaise and soya smoothies.

    Try the tofu stack (below, $18.95), tandoori chicken burger ($24.75) and organic melon chicken salad ($23.55).

    This organic eatery isn't just about eating healthily for lunch and dinner. You can have your organic cake and eat it too, and it's good. The marble cake, for one, is made with organic cocoa, sugar, eggs and flour and the texture is rich, dense and moist. At $5.50 a slice, it's not too premium a price to pay for a relatively healthy sweet.

    Check out too, its homemade apple pie which is the old-fashioned version stuffed with lots of organic apples topped with a crust that is in between shortcrust and puff pastry that gives it a nice crunch with just that little bit of softness. Served with soya ice cream, one serving sets you back $12.50.
