    The chain that serves "Whampoa Rojak" that are found around town - from Whampoa Hawker market, to People's Park Food Court.

    The original guy who started this, and worked at Whampoa, seems to be operating a stall at the Jackson's Food Court - at the corner of Macpherson Road and Tai Thong Crescent.

    The stall occupy a shared space with a porridge stall.

    Great mix of fresh items, especially with crunchy jelly like strips, and nice tasting lotus flower, mango too with great prawn paste, and generous spread of crushed peanuts.
