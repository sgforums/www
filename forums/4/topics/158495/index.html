    Originally posted by Hele1668:
    But how 2 make pancakes? Wat is d recipe?
    simple.
    buy pancake mix. u can get this in the baking goods aisle at most supermarkets.
    mix an egg with milk, pour in pancake mix and whisk well
    add water till u get the consistancy of the mix the way u want it
    get a small non-stick frying pan, lightly coat it with oil(i use butter for this)
    use a paper towel to soak up excess oil. u shouldn't be able to see large droplets of oil in the pan
    pour mixture into the center of the pan and let it spread out in a circle by itself
    use very low heat, so that your pancake won't burn on one side while remaining sticky in the middle.
    use a spatula and test the edges to see if they r sticking to the pan. if they are, let it cook for a bit more b4 testing again.
    if they dun stick, slowly try to slide spatula under the pancake to see if the underside has been cooked. if it is, use spatula to life pancake up n flip it over.
    cook for another 30 sec or so, and your pancake is done
