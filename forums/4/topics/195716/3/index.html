    Kovan hawker centre.

    order not in merit.
    1.Wan ton Mee ( facing the old bus interchange, 2nd level of the hawker centre)

    2.Kwey chup ( facing the carpark)

    3. Ba chor mee ( 2 stalls, as mentioned, one old uncle & one middle aged man & his wife. 2 stalls side by side. near the stairways up to 2nd level. next to Selegie soya bean)

    4. Carrot cake (facing car park)

    5. Selegie soya bean (cheap & rich)

    Simon road
    got one old shophouse selling teo chew porridge. there's parallel parking in front of the shop.

    another is at the entrance of simon road. The char kuay tiao is good.
