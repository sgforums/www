Toa Payoh food centre to undergo upgrading

  • QX179R
    31 May 12, 12:59

    SINGAPORE: The food centre at Block 75 Toa Payoh Lorong 5 will be upgraded under the National Environment Agency's (NEA) Hawker Centres Upgrading Programme (HUP).

    Work will start on June 1.

    The food centre will re-open with a fresh and modern look in January 2013.

    The NEA said on Thursday that the upgraded food centre would offer a more pleasant and hygienic environment.

    There will be more tables and seats, with the number of tables to increase from 58 to 112, while the number of seats will increase from 342 to 460.

    Cooked food stalls will be equipped with new mechanical exhaust systems to improve ventilation.

    Public toilet facilities will be upgraded and will have child-friendly features.

    An extension canopy will also be installed to provide better shelter when it rains heavily.

    The NEA, which manages 107 hawker centres, has upgraded 98 centres under the HUP.
