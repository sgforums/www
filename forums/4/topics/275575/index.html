    But alas nothing is forever, went back there last week after a year, even though i live pretty near, was dissapointed to see all my stalls from my childhood gone

Or you might wanna try the hor fun stall (the one that packs in paper boxes) both pretty decent.

    But imo, thats Ghim Moh for you, a few decent stalls, but nothing fantastic.

    There are 2 carrot cake stalls - I like the white carrot cake from the stall next chwee kueh. For black carrot cake, go to the one selling satay and rojak. The satay also quite good but i think they only start selling in the evening.

    For Fish Slice Bee Hoon, you've got to try the one with orange bowl.

    There is a new stall selling chee cheong fan with fresh prawns etc, next to fishball noodle (orange bowl). They have a TV screen at their stall, so not too difficult to find.

    There are about 5 chicken rice stalls in the hawker, my fav is the corner stall nearest to AMT.

    Also, I love the teh peng from Amos Coffee (next to Soy Bean drink stall) near ATM.
