    Heh Guys, an update of the place because i just went to JB.
    For those people who are still not sure of this place, its actually near the golf range in Pelangi, travelling around the road, after the golf range u will see the soccer field that i talked about. Its not the restaurant in the golf club though, its a food court in another sports club, that has badminton courts and a swimming pool.
    Anyway, there are now lots more new stalls around, and the dim sum there is also not bad.
    They also operate till around midnight, but by then alot of stalls might have closed.
    There is also a big car park, with free parking, so you guys do not have to worry about parking
    It is also very safe there as there are security guards around.
