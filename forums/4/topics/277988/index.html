    okay so last night, the 10 of us went to pizza hut at siglap for dinner. half way through the meal, my friend noticed there was a small insect in one of our pizzas. i didn't think much of it as the really was really small and insignificant, but my friend raised the matter with a waiter.

    soon, the manager came to apologise to us and told us a new pizza would be given to us. alright cool, we thought and continued with the meal.

    after everyone was done we asked for the bill and started collecting the cash. at this point of time, the manager went over to my friend and took away the bill.

    we were wondering why he did that when he said, it's okay the meal is on the house. we were all stunned for a moment. the bill was $130 and the manager decided to waive it off.

    i'm really impressed with the attitude of the manager, unfortunately i didn't take down his name. he was a malay guy maybe in the 30s.

    Kudos to the manager of Pizza Hut @ Siglap! you've definitely earned my respect !
