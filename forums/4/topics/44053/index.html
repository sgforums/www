    Ingredient :

    Ladies fingers (abt. 250gr)
    Coffee mix --> coffee + water + sugar + rhum / kahlua / tia maria (as desired) put it in a bowl
    Cocoa powder + coffee --> for dusting

    Tiramisu mix :

    a. 2 egg whites + 25gr caster sugar --> beat well
    b. 2 egg yolks + 75gr caster sugar--> beat well
    c. 250gr mascarpone cheese --> beat 1 minute only
    d. 200gr whipped powder + 400 cc iced water (can substitute with whipped cream) --> beat until creamy (if using whipped cream do not beat too long, only 1-2 minutes)

    Direction :

    1. Beat a-d separately. Then mix them together in a large bowl, mix it with large spatula. Can add rhum / kahlua / tia maria if you like. Put it in refrigerator while youÂ’re doing no. 2.
    2. Dip the ladies fingers (only in secs, if too long itÂ’ll be mushy) in the coffee mix. Arrange them orderly in the 20 x 20cm pyrex then pour the tiramisu mix upon it. Dust it with cocoa powder. Do it until the ladies fingers and tiramisu mix finish. Make abt. 2-3 layers. Dust it with cocoa powder and garnish it with strawberry.

    Note :
    - Always put it in the fridge as itÂ’ll last abt. 1 week.
    - Can add black cherry inside the layers if you like. Or garnish it with chocolate block.
    - Can put it into small cups also, if you do not wish to make it in a large pyrex.

    Well .. good luck. I hope you like this recipe. Tell me if you try it.
