    Just a few more details:

    Use pork collar meat other cuts won't be nice
    Marinade the meat in sesame, ginger, soy, msg leave for few hours
    Make batter from flour and water
    Deep fry till brown

    Sauce,

    Impt to caramelise the sugar first. Heat oil, add sugar about half cup, caramelise. Then add the rest of the stuff, tomato sauce, soy to taste, dash of worchestershire sauce pineapples and tomatoes. Cornflour to thicken the sauce
