    Dun Use Bottled sauces...

    it ll taste all the same .. those pastas...

    try using fresh tomatos..
    1. u score a "X" tru its skin
    2. Put it in hot water till skin wrinkles
    3. wash over cold water to remove skin
    4. Remove the insides: seeds, etc...
    5. then u dice all the tomatoes u have..

    now...

    all u gotta do is to
    sautee all the spices like onion and garlic with olive oil...
    Throw in the tomatoes...
    add pepper and salt to taste...
    most importantly add sugar to neutralize the acidity of tomato...
    and cook till boil first
    den let it simmer under low fire to break it down into a paste...

    u ll like it..
    gd luck
