    I will like to recommend the Fried Hokkien Mee stall at Bukit Timah Wet Market.The stall is quite prominent due to the long queue during meal time.

    I have tried the one at Old Airport Road but still prefer the Bukit Timah Wet Market. The Hokkien Mee at Bukit Timah is not too wet or too dry. The portion is also just enough to make you feel full for a simply $2 a plate. It's very hard to find another place that sell fried Hokkien Mee at such a price and considering that it is very delicious.
