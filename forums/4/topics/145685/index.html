    Tau Suan

    Ingredients
    300g split green beans, skinned
    200g rock sugar or castor sugar
    2 liters water
    2 pandan leaves, knotted
    4 tbsp potato starch water, mixed with 3 tbsp water into a solution
    Yu Tiao, sliced thinly

    Method
    Soak the beans in water for 30 minutes.
    Drain and steam on high heat for 20 minutes.
    Bring the rock sugar, castor sugar, water and pandan leaves to a boil.
    Add the beans, boil for 5 - 10 minutes, and add in starch solution.
    Cook until the soup thickens slightly and has a translucent look.
    Serve hot with yu tiao slices.
