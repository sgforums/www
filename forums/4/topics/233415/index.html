    Pizza:

    If u know how to make the dough then make, much more tastier and can adjust the dough according to your liking, but its going to take quite some time, skill, practice and patience to do it, not for the busy. No time just go buy the instant ready-made pizza dough.

    Method:

    1) Preheat oven. Set it to 250 degrees celcius and set timer to max if u doing for 1st time. Time taken for pizza to cook will vary depending on your oven, your oven cook fast, 10mins enough liao. Slow then 30mins still not ready. If using oven toaster, set to only the bottom cooking element enough liao. Dun on the top cooking element.

    2) Spread instant pizza tomato sauce(NOT the maggi type) or speghetti sauce(traditional flavour) on surface of dough. Then add Mozerrella(forget how to spell) cheese onto the surface of the sauce. DUN PUT CHEESE ON THE CRUST AND DUN PUT TOO MUCH CHEESE. Because cheese on the crust will cause a mess in the oven and too much cheese will make the pizza taste "rubbery"

    3) Place pizza into oven. Cook until the edges of the cheese near the crust turns golden brown and dough is soft and look like "cooked bread". During the process, Make sure u switch sides if u suspect that your oven is cooking unevenly. If your oven performs even cooking, then no need worry.

    4) The dough and cheese should be 3/4 cooked when u finished step 3. Take out the dough and then add in whatever toppings u like. Ham, pepperoni, anchovies, olives, anything u like. Sprinkle some mozerrella cheese on top of the toppings.

    5) Place pizza back into oven to cook toppings. Set both top and bottom elements in operation if using oven toaster. When toppings are cooked, cut pizza into pieces and serve.

    How long your pizza takes to cook depends on the oven. Mine usually takes 20mins for the whole process, including preparation and stuff.

    Enjoy!
