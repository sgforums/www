    These days where to get $2 duck rice? I am surprise that I found 1 stall selling duck rice at $2. The popiah is nice, too! Cost $1.20, much tastier compared to most of the hawker centres and coffee shops popiahs. It is fragrant, a little salty and sweet. lupchiong is one of the ingredients.     Worth the try!!!
