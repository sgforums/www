    yeah, i've been there recently on hari raya.
    the selection there is international. one gets a good selection of seafood. eg sashimi, fresh oysters......
    however, that is as good is it gets. the buffet spread has a more chinese feel, with dishes like kailan , braised duck and stuff. Nice to eat but not fantastic. Variety needs some work here.
    Desserts are good! Rich with chocolate mousse and the likes...again there could have been more variety.
    Interestingly the dinner buffet is cheaper than the lunch(perhaps because of the working crowd), parking is aplenty for the dinner buffet.
    ambience is alright, service can be better though commendable.
