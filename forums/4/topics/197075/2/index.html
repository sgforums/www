    i made my own...

    chopped parsley, chopped garlic, cinnamon powder (for slight flavor but mostly color), butter (salted or to ur preference...SCS or something?) and some sugar (small pinch will do)

    - optional > "stir" fry the garlic first b4 adding it to the soft butter.
    - melt the butter in a frying pan or microwave. once its slightly soft, bring down the heat and add in ingredients. first the chopped garlic, sugar, cinnamon powder and lastly chopped parsley.
    - mix it well and place the garlic spread into a container for usage at a later time. u might like to let it cool (solidfy) b4 spreading it over bread or french loaves.

    for ur eating pleasure.
