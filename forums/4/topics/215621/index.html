    Almost everyone likes a cold drink on a hot hazy day, but does anyone else here enjoy cold meals?

    I actually like certain food items that are cold, and probably can have a cold meal a day, instead of a hot meal that most people enjoy.

    Some of the meals that I like to eat cold are the following:

    -- Cold Vietnamese vermicelli, the one with the cold sauce poured over the cold bee-hoon with meat or spring roll inside.

    -- Cold Korean noodles with their spicy red pepper sauce, has anyone here tried it and like it?

    -- Cold sandwiches

    -- Cold tow huay (been curd)

    -- Salads

    -- Cold rice with chicken and assorted vegetables and pickles
