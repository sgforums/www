Club Chinois

  • OCEANOS
    28 Nov 04, 03:00
    This evening i went for dinner with my Family at Club Chinois suggested by my brother. The Ambience is quite nice, service is wonderful as for the food its quite refreshing. Price & charges is very close to Fine Dining Standard! Club Chinois opened around 5 or 6 years ago when Fusion Food was in fad, serving Fusion Food with the concept of East Meet West! Today is my 2nd visit after its overhaul, was a pleasant small Family gathering for 5 of us! I am particularly impressed by its Dessert "Green Tea Egg Custard with Caramel & Lemongrass" which i had 2 servings...maybe i got a sweet tooth...hahaha Rich, creamy baked Green Tea Egg Custard flavoured by Lemongrass & Caramel served with platter of cut Fruits consisting of strawberries, blue berries, kiwi, watermelon & honeydew melon was simply divine! We went for the "Tasting Menu" & a bottle of Red Wine. Well the shark's fin is quite nice, rich stock highly recommended for shark's fin Lovers. Service & Ambience is definitely great for a nice evening with your love ones or a corporate dinner with your clients! Those who don't mind the price can probably pop by to have a nice dinner or lunch. I saw in the Menu there is a "Dim Sum Lunch Menu" which i might want to go down & try...I love Dim Sum...hehehe

    Yours Truly,
    Oceanos
