    I had dinner with my family at Alexandra Village just now.

    Very full wor..

    orderd from Rong Guang Tie Ban Shao Yu stall.. the zi char stall la..

    For 4 pax

    1) Cereal Prawns
    2) Prawn Paste Chicken
    3) Stingray
    4) Samabl Kangkong
    5) Fried Rice

    Very big portion wor.. last week we went HongKong Street @Bukit Merah Central and ordered 5 dishes and 4 plain rice, $50.20.

    Tonight I went AV ordered 4 dishes and one $6 fried rice, total $47.

    My siblings din really feel like eating prawns tonight, so i bobian ate 6 cereal prawns.. big, soft, crispy, fragrant and sweet. My mum ate 4, sis ate 2. 12 prawns for $16. I kept scooping the cereals to eat.. Yes, very generous serving of cereals.

    The fried rice was yum yum, many ple ordered that. The Prawn Paste Chicken aka Har Jiong Gai was like normal chicken wings, juz that it tasted a bit different, but was served hot and crispy. More than 10 wings. But I duno how much it cost, coz din ask. Have to queue at the stall and make ur order and make payment before they will deliver the food.

    Other than zi char, I also ordered Satay from the Old Punggol Satay Stall.. first time i eat satay see the sauce got put pineapple de. At first I tot it was garlic, until I dipped the satay into the sauce and eat.. sweet, sour and spicy de. The ketupat also not bad. Sedap~
