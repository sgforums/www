    The market at Lor Ah Soo, hougang Ave 1 has a lot of good food and cheap...

    Kueh Chap (2 stalls, both have their strengths)
    Loh mee (Very Long Queue)
    Hum Chee Peng and You Cha Kueh
    Mee Rebus at the Malay Stall
    Chwee Kueh (as good as the Tiong Bahru one)

    You will be spoilt for choice.

    At the Corner Coffee Shop diagonally opp. Heartland Mall Hawker,
    got good Fried Oyster (Oh Lua). The fried hokkien mee is nice too but standard a bit drop already.

    Oh yes, my favourite, but must queue for sometimes an hour. I always bring my Readers' Digest to read while in queue....is the
    Beef Noodles (get the $4 serving one....sure to satify).
    It is at the Old Hougang South Interchange Coffee Shop, near the DBS bank. I yearn to eat it almost every weekend, but now got 2 babies, very difficult to queue and wait. The Laksa at the hawker center at the other end also very good....but now I prefer to make my own ...can put lots of cockles and also put eggs..
