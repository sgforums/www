    chicken rice outlets at thomson rd (chicken house and wee nam kee), novena MRT (wee nam kee) and balestier (boon tong kee)

    Whampoa hawker center has some nice duck rice and cold desserts.

    Sembawang SC's basement has some nice and inexpensive thai fd at the corner of the fd court.

    Upper thomson has a kowloon roasted meat and tim sum stall just opened (near the Han's bakery). Yummy king rated. Further down, there is a famous Ampung Yong Tow Fu stall (the one closer to the shell kiosk)

    Chong pang city has a chicken rice stall (yummy king rated) but i forgot the name. It's at the hawker center near the carpark.
