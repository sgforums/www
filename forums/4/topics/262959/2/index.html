    FC2

    MACS = After i started schooling at SP, I seldom eat the MAC outside the school. $4.80 EVM Vs $6.++ Meals outside the school. What do you think?

    Chicken Rice Stall
    Taste ok. All the chicken they serve are abit oily.

    Cai Png
    Cheapest food stall. Every morning those China maritime students would go there and order CHAO FAN JIA DAN ($1) . The types of food there never change. Nice stuff are minced meat, KongPow Diced Chicken, Fried Fish with curry leafs. The sunny egg is sub-standard. The uncle will give alot of rice, so if you cant finish so much just stop him from giving so much.

    Noodles stall
    Forget about the noodles stall in FC2. I would rate this the worst stall in FC2 and perhaps in whole of SP. The previous stall holder and the current one both cannot make it.

    Vegetarian Food
    Closed down due to lack of business-

    Muslim Food
    The best stall in FC2. Try the fried chicken, mutton and beef rendang and the curry. Its simply the best.

    Drinks stall
    I would suggest you not to take the cups of ice when buying can drinks. Watch out for small particles inside it.

    Bakery
    Nice but expensive.

    Thats all folks!
