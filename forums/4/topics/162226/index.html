    After spending almost half a year in Bangkok, the only local Thai food that I feel is authenthic are those I found in Gloden Mile Complex.

    There used to be a small store that sits only a few tables outside the building facing the main road (Beach rd), The cooks and service staff are Thai but the boss is local. IMO, they serve the best Tom Yam Khoong !!

    There are couple more stores within the complex. They are good but not as good. Somehow I felt their taste is already localized.

    Another store that's godd was in the hawker centre next to OG, Albert Complex. Also has a Thai cook .. but too bad, this store is no more there.
