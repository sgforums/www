  • ceecookie
    10 Feb 07, 10:46
    Hi, would like to warn those who wanna go to Sakura at clementi to stay clear of the oysters.Not fresh one.

    My cousin organised a lunch for his bd there on sundat.About 12 people went.

    Half of the whole group ate the oysters,5 got diarrhoea the following day and tuesday.

    One of my aunt and uncle who ate the oysters said that when they go doctor,latter said stomach flu.

    It dont appear to be for that particular day's batch of oysters,as my mom's fren's mother who ate the oysters months ago also got diarrhoea for days after.

    The other raw food there like salmon sashami was fine,as i didnt get diarrhoea after eating them(but i dunno if a mild stomachache later is related to it)

    Be warned.
