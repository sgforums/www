    same row of shops - katong laksa
    opposite side - tiong bahru pao

    along Lor 4 - near the library facing the TP Central CC - coffee shop
    rojak - lots of peanut
    goreng pisang...

    out to Lor 5 - hawker centre
    bak chor mee - soup one best...
    wan tan mee...
    fish ball mee
    clay pot rice...
    satay...

    jump to Lor 8 market
    BBQ chicken wing
    duck rice
    Ocean fish head curry
    braised pork knuckle

    on to Lor 7
    fried oyster.
    fish porridge

    Lor 1
    market -
    roast pork and char siew at the coffee shop
    come daily hokkien mee at the market (upstairs)
    chey sua chye tow kuay

    coffee shop (Blk 111) chye tow kuay, lor mee, ban mian
    next to MRT - shop selling fish and chips
    Blk 116 - yong tau foo
    Blk 205? - Hong Sheng Zhi Cha... 40 mins - 1 hour wait for the hor fun
