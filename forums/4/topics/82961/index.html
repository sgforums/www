    26 Jun 04, 03:36
    Gelato is not like your regular ice cream. Many people actually confuse Gelato with Gelare Cafe.

    Ice-cream is usually made with milk, cream, butter and sugar.
    Gelato is made with water, sugar and milk that is usually low-fat or skimmed

    The lower sugar content of the preservative-free sweet treat means that some gelato flavours are almost 99 per cent fat-free.

    And also because Gelato are made with the freshest ingredients, the shelf life of gelato is only about one week. (compared to regular ice-cream, which can keep for up to a year)

    Gelare sells 100% pure ice cream though it does sell low fat yogurt and non fat sorbet.
