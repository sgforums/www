    Upper circular st has quite a few zi char stalls and one famous bak chor mee sua. Sin Ho '5 Star' Chicken rice has an outlet there as well.

    Along the riverside, there is ricciotti, Jumbo, North Indian Tandoori cuisine and Just Steak (2nd floor, take the escalator up next to Jumbo).

    All quite pricy dining-in restaurants.
