  • soul_rage

    Had the following on Friday 4 May 2007:

    - Chicken Wings ($1.20 per piece, min 3 pieces per order)
    - Carrot Cake ($4 portion, alternative is $6)
    - Happy Pot Earthern Pot Steamboat ($8.90)

    The chicken wings were... normal... nothing special, better off paying for $1 chicken wings

    Carrot Cake was nice, but at the price of $4, was really overpriced

    Happy Pot, we had Tom Yam Soup, comes with some veg, tung hoon, chicken meat, 2 fishballs, and 2 prawns. Interesting taste, and earthern pot was something new. Unfortunately, portion was small, and again, the price was on the costly side

    Din try other things, saw Prata Set at $4. Nasi Goreng set at $6. Malay Chicken Rice at $4.

    Overall verdict:
    Food Rating: 7/10 (Carrot Cake is good, Happy Pot was not bad, Chicken wings, soso)
    Value-for-money: 5/10 (I feel portions were small, and prices too costly. Its overhyped by Makansutra)

    Won't be going back there again with that sort of price. We can find better food at cheaper prices elsewhere.
