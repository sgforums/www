  • browniebaobao
    28 Apr 07, 11:36
    Juz went to Zi Yean (Zi Rang Hai Xian Zhou Mian) at Lengkok Bahru for dim sum breakfast..

    very worth it! We ordered 13 items, $39.40 (drinks included) for 4 persons.. average one person less than $10.

    I love their

    1. Crispy Shredded Yam Cake (went specially to try that coz i saw ieat recommended that in his blog)

    2. Steamed Scallops Dumplings (very fresh prawn and scallop)

    3. Steamed Glutinous Rice with lotus leaf (the nice taste really very good~)

    4. Steamed Siew Mai (best with their hae bee hiam)

    5. Chicken Chop La Mian (looks small, but my brother said it's very filling, nice la mian)

    6. Guo Tie (dip with vinegar and ginger.. sis said it's nice)

    7. Century Egg Porridge (juz the porridge itself is very yummy already)

    We also ordered..

    Tianjin Meat Buns "Gou bu li bao zi" (taste like rou bao, but with a ginger taste)

    Steamed BBQ Pork Buns "mi zhi cha sha bao"

    Steamed Egg Rice Roll with Char Siew "Cha shao zhu chang fen"

    Rice with Sausage and Chicken "La chang zhen hua ji fan"

    Deep Fried Prawn Dumplings with Mayonnaise (x2)
