    Me normally would prepare the dish in such manner:

    Dice up the pork
    Marinate with soy sauce with a pinch of salt
    Coat them with corn flour
    Deep fry the meat in boiling oil over high heat for five minutes
    Drain the oil from the wok and turn the heat to medium heat
    Pour in tomato sauce into the wok
    Add 50ml of corn starch
    Add in three tablespoon of sugar
    Simmer the sauce at low heat for one minute
    Pour in the meat and stir mix for two minutes at high heat

    Vola! Sweet & sour pork...
