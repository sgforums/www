    6 Jul 04, 12:33
    yup... gotta keep a stock of the smallest fish around where the piranha will go "CHOMP!" and eat the little fish... but the piranhas, like true-x says, drop diamonds....

    and for those who are stumped as to what to do with the laser-proof monsters, you keep feeding it fish pellets until it explodes...

    later in the game, upgrading of weapons is pretty necessary as you'll need firepower to whack the missile-monster...

    as for a stage, get the big fish to turn ember or yellow by feeding the big fish with chemicals... do not let the smaller fish eat the chemicals as they will die... once the big fish become ember, they'll drop stars... that's when you buy a star-catcher... have a ratio of minimum of 3 ember fish to 2 star-catchers... coz the star-catchers will also starve to death if not enough stars.. you'll be rewarded with diamonds..
