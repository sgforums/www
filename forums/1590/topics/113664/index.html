    For those who were asking, here's the marinate recipes for the BBQ food! Enjoy!

    MARINATED FLANK STEAKS OR CHICKEN

    45 pcs mid-joint wings
    1/2 c. soy sauce
    3 tbsp. honey
    1 lemon (juiced)
    1 tbsp. garlic powder
    1 tbsp. ginger finely chopped
    1/2 c. vegetable oil
    3 tbsp. minced onion

    Mix all the ingredients together. Pour over flank steaks or chicken. Let marinate 24 hours. Then BBQ or broil. It's best if you BBQ on grill!

    -------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Peppered Pork with Orange

    1 kg pork chops
    1 tbsp clear honey
    1 tbsp raw sugar
    1 tbs oil
    4 tsp soy sauce
    1/4 tsp salt
    2 tbsp crushed black pepper
    Zest and juice of one orange

    Place the honey, raw sugar, oil, soy sauce, salt, pepper, orange juice and rind in a bowl and stir until smooth.

    Cook the chops on the grill for 15-20 minutes, turning occasionally and brushing with the honey mixture throughout the cooking.

    -------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Tequila Beef

    1kg beef steaks sliced
    6 tbsp tequila

    Marinate for 4-6 hours & grill to desired tenderness. Flavor accordingly since genera; comments were that it was a tad too bland!
