    A night of unadulterated decadence is beckoning round the corner yet again come [B]14 November[/B], which continues the monthly succession of [B]Dark Friday [/B]organized by the [B]Singapore Dark Alternative Movement[/B]. Like the previous two installments, this night of mayhem and misadventure will include the riveting DJing talents of internationally renowned personalities in the music scene.

    Headlining is [B]DJ Stefan P[/B], who has been active in the scene since 1988. He spins as resident DJ at the [B]Factory[/B] in Magdeburg and popular German clubs of the genre. This promoter and organizer of concerts has a stellar 18-year track record, and will surely extend the local scene frontiers with his impeccable mixing.

    As always, supporting deck surgeons [B]Saito Nagasaki, Mentor [/B]and [B]Murderfreak[/B] will unleash their dark charms with familiar alternative anthems amidst new releases--14 Nov marks the[B] local Record Release party [/B]for [B]Autodafeh[/B]'s debut album [B]“Hunt for Glory”.[/B] These veterans of EBM deliver hard pumping old school electronic sounds that will revive nostalgia for fans of Nitzer Ebb and Front 242. [B]Watch out for CD giveaways[/B] courtesy of [B]NMMV[/B]([url]http://www.nmmv.de/[/url]).

    So with only a [B]cover of $10 [/B]and [B]absinthe shots at $15 [/B]at [B]Legends@Orchard[/B], let those bats in your dark little hearts be set aflutter by mindblowing and electrifying music at the [B]only Goth night in Singapore[/B].
