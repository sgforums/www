    ANTHRAX: SCOTT IAN Action Figure Unveiled - Apr. 21, 2008
    An action figure of ANTHRAX guitarist Scott Ian is currently being developed for an estimated August 2008 release.

    Bif Bang Pow! brings Scott Ian to the world of collectible figures for the very first time with this rockin' resin wobbler that stands seven inches tall and comes in window-box packaging. If you're a faithful fan of this talented man and his music, you just gotta get this!

    As previously reported, all five members of ANTHRAX — Scott Ian, Charlie Benante, Frank Bello, Rob Caggiano and Dan Nelson — will take part in a meet-and-greet at the grand reopening of Sam Ash in Huntington, New York on Thursday, April 24 at 7 p.m.
