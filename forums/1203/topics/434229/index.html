    If you love music and song writing, why not write a theme song for the Speak Mandarin Campaign!

    Demonstrate how learning Mandarin can be a fun and enjoyable experience and you just might win cash and an opportunity to learn from Singapore’s most successful lyricists and composers!

     

    Student Category

    1st Prize:

    • Cash $500
    • Song Composing Course
    • Personalized Coaching sessions

     

    2nd Prize:

    • Cash $300
    • Song Composing Course

     

    3rd Prize:

    • Cash $200
    • Song Composing Course

     

     

    Open Category

    1st Prize:

    • Cash $5000
    • Song Composing Course
    • Personalized Coaching sessions

     

    2nd Prize:

    • Cash $3000
    • Song Composing Course

     

    3rd Prize:

    • Cash $2000
    • Song Composing Course
    •  

    Simply write and record an original Chinese song less than two (2) minutes in length and send it to:

    Speak Mandarin Campaign
    c/o National Library Board
    Marine Parade Community Building
    278 Marine Parade Road, #B1-01
    Singapore 449282

     

    Submissions should include:
    1)    A CD with the song and song lyrics in a word document
    2)    A printed copy of your song lyrics
    3)    Your particulars (full name, NRIC, phone number, address)

     

    Submissions start on 4 July 2011 and ends on 5 September 2011, 5pm.
