    Israeli Metallica Fans Boycott The Band's Concert At The Ramat Gan Stadium

    The previously announced Metallica concert at the Ramat Gan stadium in Israel has been met with an angered response from the country's metal fans, who feel that the ticket prices are ludicrously overpriced. Producer Marcel Avram is charging fans 600 shekel (around $159) for the cheapest seats and twice that amount for floor seats. Fans have expressed their outrage over social networking sites such as Facebook and many are refusing to buy tickets unless the price is dropped
