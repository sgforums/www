    SINGAPORE: Judas Priest and Lamb of God, two of the biggest names in heavy metal, will be descending on Singapore in 2012 for a double bill at Fort Canning Park on February 20!

    Judas Priest's Singapore stop is the UK heavy metal band's only gig in South East Asia on their EPITAPH: Farewell World Tour - the group's last major concert tour.

    Apart from Judas Priest, the February 20 concert will also feature US heavy metal band Lamb of God, who will be releasing their latest album "Resolution" in January.

    "All of us in Lamb of God are extremely honoured to return to Singapore in 2012.

    "Our show in 2010 was a highlight of our world tour and we can't wait to share our new material and energy with our fans," said Lamb of God drummer Chris Adler.

    "Oh, did I mention Judas Priest is playing? Are you kidding me? Worlds collide as we join our heroes on stage to bring an unforgettable night of heavy metal!"

    Tickets to the heavy metal double bill will go on sale from November 28.
