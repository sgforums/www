    'Sickest Music Video Of All Time' Gets Re-Released In 'Radium Vol. 2' - Mar. 15, 2008

    With the upcoming release of "Radium Vol. 2", the DECEMBER WOLVES video for "Porn Again Christian" will finally get a proper release. The clip was directed by Matt Zane and was the "sickest video of all time," according to Kerrang! magazine. Unfortunately, due the pornographic elements and the time when the video was released, viewing the piece became next to impossible. All music-related web servers went into a frenzied panic because of the images that were being depicted and were shut down.

    Now the "Porn Again Christian" video will be released in "Radium Vol. 2". The perfect vehicle for hardcore sex and porn.

    "When we heard the idea about a video," commented DECEMBER WOLVES guitarist Brian O'Blivion, "our first thought was no, but when Earache suggested it would be a video for 'Porn Again Christian' involving depraved XXX filth created by porn legend Matt Zane, we quickly changed our minds. Fucking genius!"

    "It just one of those perks that comes with a series like this," says Zane. "Now everyone in the metal community that have just just heard stories over the years about this video will actually be able to see it in its entirety."
