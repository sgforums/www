    Bakteria Posts New Music Video Online For "Defecate! Suffocate! Mutilate! Masturbate!"

    Mexican metallers Bakteria have posted a music video for the title track of their upcoming debut album "Defecate! Suffocate! Mutilate! Masturbate!" The video clip can be viewed below. Bakteria also issued the following statement about the video:

    "We are sick of all these nice and polished videos with poser-whimps. So we made it in our rehearsal room in Guadalajara. We cover our ugly faces to spare you the urge to puke...that's why! We are NOT ashamed of our lyrics!"

    "Defecate! Suffocate! Mutilate! Masturbate!" is set to be released on November 6, 2009 via Anstalt Records.
