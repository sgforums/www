    Cavalera Conspiracy

     

    Cavalera Conspiracy is a heavy metal band featuring members from Brazil, France, and the United States. Cavalera Conspiracy marked the reunion of the Cavalera brothers after 12 years separation and is the first time they have made music or shared a stage together since the infamous Sepultura break up on December 16th, 1996 in London.

    Current members

    • Max Cavalera - lead vocals, rhythm guitar
    • Igor Cavalera - drums
    • Marc Rizzo - lead guitar
    • Joe Duplantier - bass, backing vocals

    Fans of Igor and Max and Sepultura..rejoice..they are back...

    Order of Ennead

    ORDER OF ENNEAD (Tampa, FL) is a death metal band that blends elements of death and black metal together, with emphasis on songwriting and structure. Order of Ennead separates itself from other bands with its lyrical content, which explores themes such as existence, abolishment of negativity and that which makes up the self.

    Featuring Steve Asheim (Deicide, Council of the Fallen), Kevin Quirion and Scott Patrick of Council of the Fallen and an asian dude on the guitars
