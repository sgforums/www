OUTBREED (Singapore) - The Biggest Dark Alternative Event

    This will be the largest scale SDAM event ever held we are doing it in a warehouse-esque location featuring a collection of top local live acts from our beloved genres alongside DJ talents

    [b]First 100 RSVP on our event thread at [url=http://www.sdam.sg]SDAM.sg[/url] will receive a $10 Wrangler Jeans voucher* upon entry![/b]

    Singapore Dark Alternative Movement brings you Outbreed, the first ever large-scale alternative party this 26th of June. Gathered in one mindblowing live gig are the biggest alternative musical talents in the local scene as well as our resident DJs.

    This is, bar none, one hell of an opportunity to appreciate genres of music spanning the entire spectrum of dark-alternative, all in one wild night!
    Located at Mandai Orchid Gardens, you can--nay, we command you to mosh/party as hard as you like--there's literally no one around to hear you.

    We're sweetening the deal by giving away $1000 of WRANGLER JEANS vouchers to the first 100 people who sign up for Outbreed at [url]http://www.sdam.sg[/url].

    [b]Tickets are priced at $25 each, with group discounted prices at $60 for three[/b].

    Live acts:
    - Aesgrade
    - Meza Virs
    - Cosmic Armchair
    - Dualtone
    - House of Anonymous

    DJs:
    - Mentor (aka X'Ho)
    - Murderfreak
