    DAVE MUSTAINE's Coffee Raises Funds To Buy Toys For Kids

    MEGADETH mainman Dave Mustaine has issued the following update:

    "Due to the tremendous success of Legends Cup Coffee already, I was able to get a whole bunch of toys with the help of one of our own Droogies here, Elaine Hinton, who works at Hasbro Toys.

    "Well, with your support over the last record, tour, and the launch of my family's new venture, I bought 9 1/2 pallets of toys and am donating them to the children who have lost their homes and ultimately their toys and their little worlds; the church I go to that has 300-400 military children whose parents are deployed; the Toys For Tots with the Marines, who always have given toys to kids, and lastly our firefighters down here; whereever they want this donation made on their behalf.

    "A representative from my management at Azoff Artists, called our local station down here in San Diego, and sadly, I think that they are gone for the holidays, but let it be known that we are trying to determine who to contact in the Fallbrook area from the Firefighters Association that protected this area down here, that has a charity we can send some of these goods to.

    "NONE OF THIS WOULD BE POSSIBLE WITHOUT YOUR SUPPORT OF LEGENDS CUP COFFEE.

    "Thank you guys, I really, really mean it, and so will the children. Thank you Elaine; thank you Hasbro!!

    "I love you and God Bless you all for making this happen."
