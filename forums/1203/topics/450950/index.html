    Synthetic Scar is all about combining different styles of music, from electronic to hardcore. In it's own core it is a metal band, but also a synthesis of modern electro music and old school metal. In other words, a combination where everything is alowed, there are no boundaries and Nu Metal is the meeting point of the band's expression. Band recorded debut album called "Single Casualty" from which you can hear 4 songs on Myspace and Facebook page along with extensive visual material from the band (music video, photos, behind the scenes etc...). Band also wrote and recorded four songs for the hit FPS game "Serious Sam 3: BFE" soundtrack and recieved very positive reviews by the gamer community and prominent gamer websites. Visit Synthetic Scar's online material and enjoy!
