    Chthonic Frontman, Freddy Lim, Comments on Plane Nightmares

    Chthonic frontman, Freddy Lim, has checked in with the following blog:

    "Our flight just went thru a horrifying turbulent flow that shook the whole plane. In the cabin it's full of the cracking sound . I opened the curtain, looked out the window and all I saw is the wing flipping like the bird's. I closed the curtain right away because I know it doesn't help to see this kind of thing. Dani was worried that the wing is gonna be broken and so are the equipments on the plane, and probably a lot of passengers share his concern. I found out that none of on-seat screens on my row was working as the left side of the screen was shaking and the middle part only had noise. Some screens to my right worked but the headphone jack did not. The head pillow on my seat lost a screw so didn't work either. Nobody expected this plane to survive this turbulent flow. Jesse was writing spell on his palm and I was saying goodbye to my family and friends in my mind already... 10+ minutes later, turbulent flow was finally over and our plane was still in one piece and flying us from London to New York lol. Why didn't we just fly back to Taipei directly? Itinerary constantly changes as the tour schedule got more and more complicated. I really wished I could've flown form Scotland to Taipei directly.
    So our flight ended up like this: Glasgow -> London -> New York -> stay overnight -> Tokyo -> Taipei
    * I fell asleep afterward on that flight and woke up - twice! - from nightmares about crash landing..."
