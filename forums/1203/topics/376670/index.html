    CMB Pictures and DKM Studios proudly present "ARISE"; the first ever Sri Lankan anthological metal music documentary showcases the music, the lives and the reasons behind some of the best raw underground talents from the Sri Lankan metal music scene.

    The film which revolves around several underground metal bands from the local music scene takes on an investigative journey in to their art as well as their ideologies and life ambitions.

    "ARISE" is currently passing its production stage and the directors are hastily putting up the material which suggest that the film will be ready for release before the middle of this year. The documentary features in depths interviews with bands such as Funeral In Heaven, Fallen Grace and Cannabis.

    "ARISE: The Music. The Life. The Reason" DVD is set to be distributed through Hela Distributions (Sri Lanka), later this year.
