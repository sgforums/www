Disabled AC/DC Fan Impaled At Brisbane Concert

  • sand king
    1 Mar 10, 18:43
    Disabled AC/DC Fan Impaled At Brisbane Concert - Feb. 28, 2010
    News.com.au reports: A quadriplegic AC/DC fan suffered serious facial injuries when a mate accidentally hit the joystick of his motorised wheelchair and catapulted him into the mosh pit during a concert at the Queensland Sport and Athletic Centre on Saturday night.

    Witnesses said a metal pin used by the 31-year-old Morningside man to manoeuvre objects became a spear and imbedded in his eye as his chair crashed more than a metre to the centre's floor.
