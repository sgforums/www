    Music in Cognition is a music gig brought together by a group of young individuals, who simply felt that song lyrics, deserve much much much much and much more attention =).

    As much as a tune perfects the song, the lyrics complete it.

    Were your favorite songs ever singing out to you clearly and loudly?

    ———————————–

    Take THIS opportunity to enjoy live music from a 4 piece group consisting of a singer, guitarist, bassist, and percussionist. They are forming together just for this!

    Be inspired. Or ponder upon. Or simply, just enjoy the words of songs like Imagine by John Lennon, Across the Universe by The Beatles, Dreams by The Cranberries, and many more from others like Queen, R.E.M., Housemartins.

    Also, use this chance to convey a special message for your friends. Make it a special performance with your friends!
