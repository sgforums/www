    my fren `pass' 8 of the songs last nite and i have been listening it for a while since, including this morning's 6.30am run and the train to work and back. my verdict? i like death magnetic a lot after repeated listening.

    i had said earlier that i was non-committal initially but i think metallica won me over.

    i like the long and complex arrangement that reminds me of metallica of old. many of the songs have nice hooks and riffs, and there is a lot of energy in the songs only metallica knows how to kerrang out.

    suicide and redemption is a solid instrumental. broken beat and scarred, judas kiss and cyanide got some really good solos.

    my only grouse is rick rubin made this sound all too `clean'. this is his trademark, his ability to commercialize almost any song or artiste he touches.

    bottomline, i will definitely buy their CD. they are back!
