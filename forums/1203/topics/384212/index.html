    MILEY CYRUS Recording Cover Of POISON's 'Every Rose Has Its Thorn' - Dec. 9, 2009 According to People.com, American singer, songwriter, and actress Miley Cyrus has recorded a cover version of POISON's '80s rock ballad "Every Rose Has Its Thorn". The track was recorded in New York and is rumored to feature a guest appearance by POISON singer Bret Michaels, who was said to be working in the studio with Miley.

    "'Every Rose' is one of her all-time favorite songs and she is really excited about cutting it," Miley's mom Tish Cyrus tells People.com.

    Miley's first concert experience — other than seeing dad Billy Ray Cyrus onstage — was watching POISON perform in Nashville.

    Cyrus, 17, is best known for starring as the title character in the Disney Channel series "Hannah Montana".

    Cyrus's solo music career began with the release of her debut album, "Meet Miley Cyrus", on June 23, 2007, which included her first top ten single, "See You Again". She has sold more than fourteen million albums worldwide.
