    It goes all the way back to 1985 when Helloween release their first full length album titled 'Walls Of Jericho'. Heavy Metal music in Singapore has never been the same since then. Many has turn ardent fans to the Helloween's fever and has proclaimed the band to be one of the most influential musicians all over the continent. Helloween fans grew in tremendous length after the release of Keepers Of the Seven Keys I and Keepers Of the Seven Keys II.

    The band have been touring for decades but their round trip end furthest always in Japan. It has dissapointed many in the sunny island of Singapore. Hellish Rock Tour made it possible for Helloween to extend their step into South East Asia. Starting in Japan, Helloween will meet their fans in Korea, Thailand, Indonesia, Singapore and Australia but it does not stop there.

    The concert will happen here in Singapore @ Fort Canning Park on the 24th February 2008. Official concert ticket launch on the 7th of January 2008. Early bird discounted ticket available from www.tdc.sg
