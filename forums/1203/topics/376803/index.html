Megadeth Album "Endgame" Hits #1 on Canadian Hard Rock Chart

  • NeverSayGoodBye
    25 Sep 09, 17:01

    Megadeth drummer Shawn Drover has checked in with the Canadian chart position for the band's latest album, "Endgame."

    "I just received the official word on the chart position for My homeland, Canada, to which 'Endgame' is #4 on the billboard charts, and #1 on the Canadian 'Hard Rock' billboard charts!!!!!!!!!!!! To say that this thrills me, is a huge understatement, believe me!! I am very greateful to all who made the effort to buy the c.d. and make a difference. To be #1 on any chart is a huge thrill, and it is something we do not take for granted.

    "My day just became a little bit better."
