    Celestial Bloodshed Frontman Steingrim Torson Shot Dead.

    The frontman of Norwegian rockers Celestial Bloodshed has died after suffering a gunshot wound to the stomach.

    Steingrim Torson, 25, was fatally injured in an incident at his best pal's apartment in the city of Trondheim last Thursday (30Apr09).

    The unnamed friend has been arrested by police for the shooting, but the 20-year-old "has no explanation of how the gun went off", according to his attorney Morten Aalberg.

    Police inspector Alf Rune Nilsen says, "Witness accounts and technical findings at the scene suggest that a murder was committed." (c) WENN
