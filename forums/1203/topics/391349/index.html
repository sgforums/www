Elexorien Recruits New Vocalist Lotte

  • NeverSayGoodBye
    19 Feb 10, 17:33

    Elexorien has issued the following update about recruiting a new vocalist:

    "New vocalist found! Hearken, proud Legions of the Blade, and hear our words, for we have been quiet for too long, now. Allow us to share something with you. Something out of the ordinary, really. A thing which fills us with great pride and honour, and which has changed our perspective for the better, from a reminiscent gaze at what we have achieved so far, to boldly looking ahead at a bright and splendorous future for Elexorien.

    "My friends, we have found our new singer! Lotte - Vocals. Nineteen years of age, she goes by the name of Lotte, and she will manifest baffling new definitions of sounding the siren call. Numerous events are being planned as we speak, so have no fear of missing out on an opportunity to behold us in full glory once more in the near future. Exact dates and locations of said conquerings will be announced soon. We expect our Legion to be at its full strength, and present at all skirmishes. As always, we are looking forward to rush into the fray side by side with you."
