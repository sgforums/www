Alice In Chains Scores A Number One Single "Check My Brain"

  • NeverSayGoodBye

    Alice In Chains has managed to top both the Billboard Rock Songs Chart and the R&R Rock Chart with their latest single, "Check My Brain." The song is the second single from the bands forthcoming new studio album, "Black Gives Way To Blue" which will be released on September 28th in Europe and a day later in North America.
