    Marry a man who loves you more than you love him

    Dear TR, Temasek Review
     
    I was browsing the NECDC newsletter “Spring” Mar/Apr 2010 when I came across an article “United for Life” about Mdm Cynthia Phua’s view on marriage, family and love.
     
    I have no problem with her views on marriage and family but what struck me as unusual is her parting advice to unmarried women, to quote   “Marry a man who is crazily in love with you and who loves you more than you love him”.
     
    I don’t care if Mdm Cynthia Phua loves her husband less than he loves her, but coming from a Member of Parliament and a woman role model to boot, her advice is indeed unbecoming and imparting wrong values to younger generation.

    As  a married woman myself, I love my husband as much as he loves me, and that to me  is the very essence of a happy married life. Anything short of this may lead a man to stray out of wedlock, and we have only ourselves to blame.
     
    In this new generation of feminine rights and equality of the sexes, we must give our love  as much as we receive.  I hope that her spouse will not be disappointed with her or have any regrets.
     

    Yours sincerely,
     
     
    Wendy Tan
    Happily Married for the past 10 years
