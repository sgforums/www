    When you say having dinner, is the place somewhere you are familiar with or is it a fine dining restaurant where you will feel intense stress and nervousness?

    The advice i have is, first, be comfortable with your surroundings, if you are comfortable in a coffeeshop, then go there, if you are comfortable in fast food outlets, then go there. The surroundings can really affect your outlook and how nervous you already are. If you are feeling extreme nervousness, take deep breathes and loosen up yourself.

    Now, ask yourself this simple question. If you are a girl, and you have plans to have dinner with a guy, do you expect it to be fun or boring?

    Henshin, be fun and spontaneous! Move away from boring topics like what type of guys she like and what she expect of her future family will like or what her childhood was like.
