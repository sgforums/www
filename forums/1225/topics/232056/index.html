    It is always a good thing to have someone whom you can talk to freely.

    The only obstacle to every good friendship is always the question of 'line drawing'

    What most people did was, they delibrately avoid the other party so to prevent 'liking' or affection from happening. I call it a unnatural process.
    What people suffer will be prematurely killing off a potientally good friendship which will last and mantain for many years. Because of the fear of falling into the forbidden 'zone'.

    The other category, is - when two parties know very well why are they close and will want to continually work onto strengthening the friendship.
    The agenda is clear. The motive is clean. The relationship is honest.
    This 'soulmate' friend is a rare gem.

    What happens IF, one falls for the other?
    This is life, and when these happens. We never know the result without trying. Anyway this is another topic altogether.

    Do you wish to end it prematurely and miss the potientally good bond you might build? You never know how much she could bless you in the future you know?

    You have to ask yourself, what do you wish to achieve from this friendship. Your honesty in this answer will determine your friendship.
