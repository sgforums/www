    Originally posted by Michelleleeth:

    Hi all,

    I'm Malaysian girl currently work in Singapore. Any advice how can I married to Singaporean guy? Thanks.


    If you are a foreigner in Singapore holding a work permit / SPass / EPass / Dependant Pass, you need to seek a permission from MOM - Marriage Department to marry a Singaporean. Failing to do so means getting your work permit cancelled and you will be banned from entering Singapore. Take not that even if previously you had worked in Singapore 10 year ago and was a work permit / SPass/ EPass / Dependant Pass holder, you still need to seek permission from MOM-Marriage Department. This is a mandatory procedure. If you are not a work permit holder then no need for you to seek permission from MOM - Marriage Department to marry a SIngaporean. Then after your marriage inform your company management about it. Your company will gain benefit from your marriage because they do not have to pay the monthly levy to MOM anymore. And you can apply Long Term Visit Pass from ICA should you resign from your company.
