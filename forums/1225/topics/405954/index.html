Being honest (with women)

  • SgLoveCop@blogspot
    21 Jul 10, 23:35

    When it comes to romantic relationships and dating, the word honesty seems to have taken on a different meaning. A lot of women equate being honest with being open. When a man is not open to a woman, she considers him dishonest. But being honest and being open is two separate issues.

    Everyone has their share of positive and negative experiences in life. It is entirely up to you to decide which experiences you wish to talk about in the presence of your date/partner.

    If a man only chooses to talk about all his positive experiences to his date, she can only have a positive image about him simply because she doesn't know anything negative about him. Despite what they claim, women want to enjoy their date by having a positive and uplifting time with a guy. Anything that is negative will only have a negative effect on her interest towards him. Which also means that anything positive will only have a positive effect on her interest towards him.

    You can tell her how you used to represent your school for athletic/sport competitions. Tell her you have a great family and enjoy spending time with your family members on weekends/holidays. Tell her you have a great job where you get along very well with all your colleagues and you enjoy the work that you do. But never ever brag though. Basically everything that comes out from your mouth has to be positive and avoid anything negative at all costs.

    Woman like men who are mysterious and they like the fact that they have to work to find out more about you. They like to play detective and find out everything about you. So it is of utmost importance for the man not to give the store away too easily. If you tell her your whole life story on the first date, then there is nothing interesting for her to find out anymore. You have to open up to her very slowly. Spoon feed her bit by bit.

    In fact, it is highly recommended for the man to do only 30% of the talking by asking her questions about her and let her do 70% of the talking. Listen attentively and ask her questions on her answers. Women love to talk (about themselves).
