    Brad attends a friend's wedding and chances upon Angelina. Brad goes over to initiate small talk with Angelina. After several minutes of conversation, Brad asks Angelina for her phone number. Angelina gives her phone number to Brad and he can't wait to get her out for the first date.

    Within 48 hours, Brad gives a call to Angelina asking her out for a date. Angelina agrees to meet Brad for a date. In Brad's mind, the only reason Angelina has agreed to meet him for a date is because she likes him or she has romantic interest in him.

    But in reality, there are various possible reasons why Angelina agreed to meet Brad for a first date. Let's explore them.

    1. Angelina's boyfriend is out of town for the weekend and she wants Brad to fill in.

    2. Brad's brother is a handyman, and Angelina needs someone to fix her faulty lights.

    3. Angelina doesn't want to stay home and stare at the four walls.

    4. Angelina wants to go to her favorite band's concert (which Brad will be paying for)

    5. Brad is a director and Angelina is an aspiring actress.

    6. Angelina loves clubbing, drinking, dancing and Brad is taking her to a party.

    7. Brad drove a Lamborghini to the wedding.

    8. Angelina might meet someone she really likes while hanging out with Brad.

    9. Angelina wants to make her boyfriend jealous.

    10. Angelina can't stand her boyfriend anymore and wants him to break up with her.

    Brad has no idea what he is up against. Do you?
