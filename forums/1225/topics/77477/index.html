    The game play.

    Always end conversations, meetings and phone calls first.

    Girls are usually the ones playing this game.

    Two reasons to end conversations FIRST.

    I) It says, 'I'm not a wimp, needy guy and i have better things to do. I'm busy, if you want me, you're going to have to work to get me.'

    II) You'll be in a situation where YOU are in control.

    If the women you're seeing starts getting agtitated and ask 'But why??? Can't we spend awhile more?' or 'when can we meet again?'
    You know you're in control buddy.

    When women starts to say 'I have fun today, but i'm busy and i have a few things to do, so call me tomorrow and maybe i can get together with you at the end of this week again.'

    Do not take the bait.

    Tips

    Do not say caring and affectionate things directly, say them INDIRECTLY.'
    Women have a love for metaphors, demostrations, stories and gifts, etc.

    Eg. Saying 'Your beautiful' 12 times a day, isn't attractive. Writing a poem and leaving it somewhere as a surprise is very attractive.

    If i'm appreciating something about a women. I point to a specific part or detail. They enjoy it when Guys notice them.
