Great places to chill or bring your significant other

  • kivichio
    7 Sep 11, 04:22

    Please feel free to add to the list. I'll start with mine:

    1. Henderson Wave Bridge late at night to avoid the crowd.
    2. Introbar at Swissotel Ground floor to watch the walking crowds
    3. Axis Bar at Mandarin Oriental to look at the Bay
    4. Halo on top of Wangz Hotel. The Duke/NUH Neon light some may consider garish but I find a nice break in the open vista.
    5. Boathouse with the fantastic deck area, great to watch fireworks over the bay
    6. New Asia Bar at the top of Swissotel (free entry with Fairmont or Swissotel Keycard) - Fridays and Saturdays they become a club with guest DJs rather than a lounge.
    7. Sentosa beaches at night (park at Sentosa Resort & Spa and go to the beach closest there as it is usually deserted. Make sure to bring one of those inaudible electronic mosquito repellent in your pocket to discretely prevent being overrunned by bloodsuckers. Also don't forget a towel to sit on the sand)

    These places are usually deserted most of the time, and its great to just relax in the company of the person you're with.

    I'd love to hear what places you like.

You don't even have to be a member although if you are, just grab a coke or lemon tea from drinks area inside and get a couple of towels.
