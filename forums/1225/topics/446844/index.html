How much time is too much time spent together?

  • Pritchard983
    8 Feb 12, 01:40

    New relationships are exciting and couples often spend a lot of time together getting to know each other. How much is too much time to spend with someone during a new relationship?

    Every couple different. Some couples spend hours on the phone while others are at each other’s homes most nights of the week. Discuss expectations when it comes to spending time together. If one partner feels smothered if may be time to pull back a bit. Couples can have petty arguments when each person needs some space. Quality time may win out over the quantity of time you spend together.

    You can tell you’re likely spending too much time together if it affects other areas of your life. You should still be able to meet your work obligations, take care of your home and spend time with other important ion your life. Your focus is understandably on your love life, but other areas shouldn’t suffer.
