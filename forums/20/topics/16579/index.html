    The shows:
    20th Feb (Wed) - Mononoke Hime,
    Metropolis
    21st Feb (Thu) - Ah! My Goddess Movie,
    One Piece The Movie 2

    Cost:
    $2 for both days (public)
    Free for VAS members

    Isn't very often for us to see anime on screens bigger than 29-34 inches, so come on down, especially for those living nearby!!
