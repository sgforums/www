    NTU CAC Joint Dance Concert Poster Design Competition

    If you think that you have the artistic creativity in poster designing, this is the competition for you! NTU CAC Joint Dance Concert is looking for artistic talents to express their theme of the year..

    Themed "Mélange- Celebration of Life", the concert promises to delight the audience with the stirring showcase of NTU's dance talents and the splendid mix of dance styles. The concert is presented by the seven dance clubs under the NTU Cultural Activities Club (CAC) namely:

    ·                   Breakdancing

     ·                   Chinese Dance

     ·                   Contemporary Dance

     ·                   DanceSport Academy

     ·                   Modern Jazz

     ·                   Kinetics

     ·                   Salsa En Sync

    Like a jigsaw puzzle, the series of performances will piece together seamlessly to narrate the exciting life story of a dancer.

    Application form and competition details have been attached. For more information, please email to designcomp@cac-jdc.com

    Deadline for submission of design is 22 December 2008! So do hurry up!
