    Just a thought that came: ~

    Today I went to play pool with a few friends. It initially wasn't an activity I suggested but since somebody suggested about it, I just tagged along.

    Within the group there's a boy who's in his 16. And I was playing a few games of pool with him. I ain't very good in pool and so is he. But we played nevertheless and he did better than me. Soon, he beat me 3-2 in the game and I was like pretty much disappointed with myself being rusty and all.

    But right then, he said to me. "It's not a matter of whether we win or lose."

    I looked up and something just striked my mind and spirit of calmness filled me. I was feeling relieved and very comforted at that very point in time.

    Suddenly, I continued his phrase, "... what matters is that we enjoy the game together."

    I excused myself for a while and thought of the whole thing again.

    "It doesn't matter whether we win or lose, what matters is that we enjoy the game together."

    Same goes for happiness...
