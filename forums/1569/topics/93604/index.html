There Was A Time...

  • Joshua1975
    13 Sep 04, 22:41
    There was a time when...
    My life was so simple.
    I made the rules as I went along
    and my worries were few.

    I have learned many lessons.
    Some mistakes can be corrected
    and some can only be forgiven.

    To just exist from day to day is basic survival.
    To really live and seize life's lessons
    is the greatest gift you give to yourself and to others.

    Your strengths and weaknesses will be constantly tested.
    The tests you give yourself are the hardest
    and yet you will feel the most rewarded in passing.

    Compassion and a sense of fairness should govern your life.
    Without either quality, you cannot grow
    in mind or in spirit.

    My life is no longer simple.
    There is much that I still have to learn
    and regardless of the rules...

    I am still in the game.

    By A. L. Franklin
