    3 eggs, beat... pinch of salt and ground pepper.. beat...

    handful of grated mozarella cheese, handful of grated cheddar

    some sliced up mushrooms..

    butter..

    frying pan... melt some butter... stirfry mushrooms until cooked.. remove...

    heat frying pan until unreasonably hot...

    add butter to grease pan.. add eggs and spread it out... add mushrooms and cheese.. fold over.. decrease heat and slowly cook... and cheese melts inside the folded omelette

    serves one gluttonous person
