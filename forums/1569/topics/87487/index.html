    Sometimes you think about a person, but you won't call him.
    Cos even if you called him, you wouldn't know what to say to him, so you might as well not call.

    You miss you parents, so you call them, to chat with them.
    But everytime without fail, you will end up quarrelling.
    The next time you feel like calling, you hesitate, and put down the phone.

    You call a long lost friend, you used to have a lot to talk about and you can talk about everything;
    Now you can only talk about unimportant matters, and that don't feel good.
    From then on when you miss him, you won't call him.

    You feel like calling your ex, but he already has a new love, what can you say to him? You've lost contact for so long, it's best not to keep in contact.
    When you broke up, everything went so well, it was actually miraculous. Why spoil the mood left from that day because you miss him?
    If you miss someone, you don't naturally have to hear his voice. It might be a different feeling after hearing his voice.

    Everything seems to be better in you dreams rather than reality. The person you are thinking of is also gentler than in reality. Dreams might seem far away, but sometimes it seems closer than reality. Dreams might seem near, but on the other side of the phone, it might seem distant, so might as well not call.

    Loving someone is simple. But to let the person you love understand you is very difficult. Henceforth a person who understands you is more important than a person who loves you. So find someone who understands you to dote on you. Let someone who understands you love you.

    Whenever you feel happy, the first person you think of if your lover. When you feel sad, the first people you think of are your friends. Instead of saying the people you find when you are sad are the people you love, why not say that people who are willing to share your pain are the ones who really love you. Because it's easy to share joy. If I share my problems with 2 person who loves me, the person who comforts me and rides over the problem with me, is the one who will spend the rest of his life with me.

    But men's feelings are usually the opposite. If he is not willing to share his problems with that person, that person is the one he loves. Because he can't bear to see his lover worried. So women would love to tell their lovers to share their problems and hope to share it with them.

    Let someone who understands you love you. Choose a man which is worthy of your feelings.

    LOVE ~ requires just a bit of rashness
    but UNDERSTANDING ~ can't do without even a little bit less of chemistry.
    Love is not about walking into each others' world, it's about being able to walk into each other's lives.
