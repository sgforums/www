    One Day(In Memory of XXX)

    One day
    when I have lost count of the days passed by
    Since we last talked
    One day
    when the morning ray stream into the room
    I would wake up with no thoughts of you
    One day
    when my smiles would once again be genuinely happy
    Where would you be?

    One day
    when we walk past each other
    I would not have to turn my head away
    I would not have to hide my eyes
    One day when sad songs fill my ears
    My eyes would not be stung with tears
    Would the sweet memories always remain?

    One day
    when I no longer feel lonely and lost
    One day
    when I finally find a new love
    One day
    when I have truly gained the courage
    to look back and not regret
    You would have already forgotten...

    One day
    when I can walk on the road
    And not think of the times
    we have walked through it before...

    i.c.y
