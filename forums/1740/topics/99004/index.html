    Got this little joke from my email...

    A man and his wife were having some problems at home and

    were giving each other the silent treatment.

    The next week the man realized that he would need his wife

    to wake him at 5:00 AM for an early morning business flight

    to Chicago. Not wanting to be the first to break the silence

    (AND LOSE), he wrote on a piece of paper,

    "Please wake me at 5:00 AM."

    The next morning the man woke up, only to discover it was

    7:00 AM and that he had missed his flight. Furious, he was

    about to go and see why his wife hadn't woken him when he

    noticed a piece of paper by the bed. The paper said,

    "It is 5:00 AM. Wake up."

    Men are not equipped for these kinds of contests.
