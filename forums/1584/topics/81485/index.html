    Physics SPA Practical

    Skills Tested:
    B - Implementation

    Apparatus/Materials Given:
    2 500ml Beaker
    2 Test Tubes
    Bunsen Burner
    Electronic Weighing Scale
    Kitchen Towel
    2 Thermometers
    Boiling Water
    600g of Lead Shots
    Measuring Cylinder

    Procedure:
    Place 100g of Lead Shots into test tube.
    Do the same for the other test tube.
    Place the test tubes into the beaker of boiling water.
    After approx. 5 minutes take temperature of the lead shots in the tubes. Record this as T1.
    Ready a beaker* of 100ml of water and take its temperature. Record this as T2.
    Empty all the Lead Shots into the beaker. Record the temperature change as T3.
    Remove water from beaker, and dry Lead Shots on Kitchen towel provided.
    Repeat experiment for varying volumes of water in beaker*.
    Calculate the value of T3 - T2/T1 as T4.
    Plot T4 against mass of water in beaker*.
    Discuss any anamolous data.
