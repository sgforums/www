    SET-UP:

    you're provided with 2 metal rods, 2 metre rules, 2 retord stands, 1 piece of thread (at least 10cm) and weights ranging from 50 - 550 g.
    clip the 2 metal rods onto the clamps of the retord and suspend the ruler onto the 2 metal rods horizontally at 5.0 cm mark and 95.0 cm mark.
    at the 50.0 cm mark tie a loop and hang a hanger onto it.
    then put weights on in, be mindful of 6 readings.
    then take the height of the bottom of the ruler to the bench (labelled depression) for the respective weights
    remember to tape the loop at 50.0 cm if you're using a whole big loop (those who choose to tie 2 small loops can dun use tape)
    enjoy!

    TABLE:

    remember to include weigh, 2 seperate columns for loading and unloading and initial height and depression. then it will be the log of both datas
