    1. cut down e number of ciggies per day. e.g one pack to ten sticks, or only buy ciggies on every third day.

    2. lower e content as u go. e.g. menthol to mentol lights, but maintain e same number of ciggies per day. (some pple think going for a lighter version helps, but actually smoke even more, cos they neglected the number of ciggies they smoke per day...)

    3. set a date on e calendar. give urself a few weeks or 1-3mths to cut down e ciggies, then reduce e number till u totally quit as marked on e calendar.

    4. determination...determination...note that there are cues everywhere. e.g even on tv or real life where u see pple lighting up a ciggie...u remind urself not to smoke worz..

    5. pick up a new habit. e.g. sports, to divert ur attention away from smoking. or some small tics like whenever u feel like smoking, go for a sweet.

    6. good luck =)

    EDIT: one is considered quitted smokin only when they stop it for at least 6mths.
