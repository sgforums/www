    hmmm, just ghosted today.

    1) Use Norton Ghost to create ghost diskette so u can use it to load the ghost images to your HDD at dos

    2) Use the diskette to boot, then choose Partion to Disc, it will create a ghost image OF the partition you choose ON another partition which must be big enough to save the .gho file.

    3) To split the .gho into 650 meg, u need the ghost wizard which comes installed with Norton Ghost, split the .gho into parts of 650 meg so u can save them to cds.

    dis is the barebone lah...its not difficult and its very power....format and re-install in under 6mins....
