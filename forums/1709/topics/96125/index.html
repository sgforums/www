    Here is a super short cut guide on how to do it:

    1) Download the .torrent file to your pc and open it from there.
    2) Make sure that you pick a file that is seeded.
    3) Select a place on your harddrive where you want to download the file to and click save.
    4) Firstly, the bittorrent client you use will allocate enough space on your hard disk for the file you want to download, this can take a couple of minutes and of course make sure you have enough hard disk space.
    5) When finished allocating, your client will try to connect to the tracker.
    6) Only worry if you haven't started downloading after 20 minutes or so and you still get error messages.
    7) If you decide to stop and shut off your pc for the day, then just close your client.
    You can resume a partially downloaded file, just save to the same folder.
    9) Make sure your client checks your existing data before it starts to download (if you download a single file, save to the file on your HD. If you download a folder with several files, save to the folder that the folder with the files is in on your HD).

    I stop here, I don't want to add on the burden of details. Need more info, can post here and ask me.
