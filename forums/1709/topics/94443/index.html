    what i did to clean my "corrupted" IE.

    1. Open Windows Explorer > Tools > Folder Options > View, choose "Show hidden files and folders.
    2. Browse to your profile C:\Documents & Settings\Ur Profile\Local Settings\Temp.. delete all the files in there.. If your computer having a few profiles, please delete all the files in their temp folders.

    3. Delete files in C:\Windows\temp

    Some ads remain as exe file in your temp folders and Ad-aware unable to detect the...

    4. Next run Ad-aware, perform 2 scans as "smart scan" and then C:..

    This should be able to clean your IE.. please DO not open your IE while Ad-aware scanning...

    I used the above methods to clean a few computers liow..
