    ABOUT JAY PARK

     

    The former leader of South Korean boyband 2PM, and part of Seattle-based b-boy crew Art of Movement (AOM) has been confirmed for a fan meeting convention in Singapore on 25th September as part of his Fan Meets Tour 2010.

    Not only will Jay be performing songs and choreographed dance moves in this 90-minutes fan meeting, there will also be game segments to interact with fans and AOM will battle the winning local b-boy crew in a friendly exchange. Fans would be happy to hear that exclusive Jay Park and AOM memorabilia will be given away during the fan interaction.

    “We have been overwhelmed by the numerous requests by both the media and fans to bring Jay Park in ever since his return and we are glad to be able to bring his fans closer to him.” says Heather Riley, Director of Diva Production.

    Since the news of his comeback broke, Jay has been the top trending topic on Twitter and over 2000 fans showed up at the airport during his recent return to Korea. His cover of “Nothin’ on You” spawned over 5 million views on Youtube and was #1 in Korea on Cyworld Music charts and was #2 on iTunes sales.
