    Much has been made of the transformative powers of the Internet in less-than-free societies. But before you get all gooey over Google in China, take a look at Baidu's lists of the questions most often typed into their search engine in 2006. They're divided into "how," "why," "what," and "should I" lists. Most of the top searches are either time-honored snoozers ("What is love?" ) or predictably materialistic ("How to get plastic surgery?" ).
