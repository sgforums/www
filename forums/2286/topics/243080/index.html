    WHAT IS THE 1 SECOND FILM?

    THE 1 SECOND FILM is a 70mm non-profit collaborative film project attempting to bring thousands of diverse people together to make film history. Anyone can produce The 1 Second Film by donating $1 or more. Once finished, all profits raised by the film will be donated to the Global Fund for Women. The 1 Second Film will have 90-minutes of end credits; a feature-length 'making of' documentary will play during the credits, showing how people around the world came together for one moment.
