    MacTalk Magazine is a new print magazine from the founders of the MacTalk Australia forums & community. It's first release has a print run of 10,000 copies which have been distributed nationally via select Apple resellers as well as a team of people who have volunteered to place it in their places of work and in schools, as well as pass the magazine onto family, friends and colleagues who they think are interested in the magazine.

    The magazine is a collection of articles that are not only relevant to running and using your Mac, but also the associated tasks you may do with it. There are tutorials, audio/visual related topics, product selection advice and Mac culture.
    It is the magazine for Australian Mac users, by Australian Mac users.
