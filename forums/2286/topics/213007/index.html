    The Concerto Table is designed by Nicholas Lovegrove & Demian Repucci. With a shared love for food and dining they decided to create a piece of furniture for dining that was as integral to the dinner party experience as the food itself.

    Inspiration
    The Concerto Table seeks to transform the experience of the modern dinner party by combining the use of a dining table with the elegant curvature of a grand piano. It has the sensuous look of an old, well-seasoned, grand piano while also providing the necessary multipurpose use required of so many furnishings today.

    The table cleverly accommodates the issue of the many forgotten piano lessons of youth by providing a docking slot for your iPod. Simply download your favorite music onto your iPod, plug it in and instantly the Concerto Table becomes both dining surface and dinner party background music at once. Lift the sound lid slightly to give the speakers concealed below a volume boost.

    Also, the shallow keyboard drawer along the front side opens to reveal your cutlery sets. lined up side-by-side. This item is perfect as a modern work surface. With generous dimensions, you can spread out your work to make sense of it all at once. Finished using your laptop? Store it securely under the speaker lid.
