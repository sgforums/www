    It's Rad To Pick Your Nose

    This is David Choi.


    David Choi is a singer/songwriter/remixer/producer/multi instrumentalist from the Los Angeles area. He hails from a musical family and has spent his formative years honing his craft.

    In 2004, he was the GRAND PRIZE WINNER for USA Weekend Magazine's JOHN LENNON SONGWRITING CONTEST FOR TEENS. He appeared in USA Weekend Magazine with Usher.

    Following that, he became the GRAND PRIZE WINNER for DAVID BOWIE'S MASH UP CONTEST which was a mashup of both "Shake it" and "She'll Drive a Big Car" (from Bowie's latest album) entitled "Big Shaken Car".

    After getting accepted into ASCAP's Lester Sill Workshop, it has led him to sign with Warner Chappell Publishing as a staff songwriter/producer as of April, 2006.
