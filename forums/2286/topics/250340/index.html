    From the website:

    The Birthday Massacre is an audio/visual project that has been in development since 1999. The sights and sounds of the birthday massacre branch into a wide range of creative themes and blend together a colorful palette of music genres and contrasting imagery.

    By combining imagery, sound, fashion and performance, we aspire to create an experience for our audience that is both unique and multi-faceted. Through the use of creative media, we've developed a world inspired by emotion, memories, satire, horror, romance, fantasy, nostalgia, and tragedy; a world we hope to evolve and explore for a long time to come.
    The Birthday Massacre is a band formerly known as Imagica. They're from Toronto, Canada. I'm not sure what genre they belong to, but they look kinda gothic. But they have this website, which, together with the background music, has a surreal feel to it. If you click on some things in the menu, small, special effects will precede a pop-out within the flash display. And you can preview their album music too!
