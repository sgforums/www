    The Interim

    Date: 4 December 2005, Sunday

    Programme @ the Padang:

    0600 - Start of full marathon
    0630 - Start of half marathon
    0700 - start of 10 km, Team, CEO Challenge
    0730 - Estimated finishing time of top 10km (Men)
    0735 - Estimated finishing time of top half-marathon (men) & 10km (women)
    0745 - Estimated finishing time of top half marathon runner (women)
    0815 - Estimated finishing time of top marathon runner (men)
    0840 - Estimated finishing time of top marathon runner (women)
    0930 - Prize presentation for to 3 marathon open winners (men & women)
    1000 - Start 7-Eleven's Kids Dash (750m)
    1030 - Start of Wheelies 10km race
    1100 - 1200 - Prize presentation

    Contest details:
    As part of our Runspiration programme, photo enthusiasts will get a chance to show off their creative skills in this year's inaugural photography contest for the Standard Chartered Singapore Marathon.

    Come down with your cameras on Sunday, 4 December 2005, and capture:

    - Elite runners
    - Family and friends running together
    - Children in the 7-Eleven Kids Dash
    - Supporters and Cheerleaders
    - Carnival @ the Padang

    This year's theme is "Passion and Enthusiasm", and the winning photos will be judged based on originality, creativity, and relevancy.

    Carnival @ Padang map. Please note there will be limited access at the end point, especially when the elite runners come in. (Kindly note that the Carnival map is subjected to changes)
