    Man Utd boss Ferguson has ref fears over Chelsea clash
    tribalfooball.com - November 20, 2006

    Manchester United boss Sir Alex Ferguson is demanding a strong referee for this week's massive Premiership showdown with Chelsea.

    Fergie has already claimed the champions are being given 'special treatment' after referees' supremo Keith Hackett agreed to meet Mourinho following the Chelsea manager's scathing attack on match official Graham Poll following their 2-1 defeat at Tottenham 15 days ago.

    Ferguson, whose side are three points clear, said: "We need a strong referee after everything that's gone on in the last few weeks. There's going to be a lot of focus on him and I hope he's not swayed by recent events."

    Ferguson fears favouritism for Chelsea from the FA when the appointment is made and admits that the referee will be his chief concern.

    He added: "The Chelsea game is obviously gathering arms and legs. It will be built up and built up until kick-off time, so it's a good thing we have this really important match on Tuesday against Celtic."

    Man Utd boss Ferguson has dinner with Chelsea's Mourinho
    tribalfooball.com - November 19, 2006

    They may talk it up through the press, but Manchester United boss Sir Alex Ferguson and Chelsea's Jose Mourinho remain the best of friends ahead of this week's big showdown.

    The pair dined out in London on Friday night, served up a feast by celebrity chef Gary Rhodes, at his London restaurant Rhodes 24.

    Ferguson is a patron of Shine, a charity which helps people with learning difficulties and their families. And he had been hoping to arrange a dinner with his Chelsea counterpart to auction off for some time.

    Shine chief executive Stephen Shields told the People: "Sir Alex phoned our chair man Jim O'Neill some months ago to tell him he was hoping to invite Jose to dinner but was waiting for the right time.

    "He asked him towards the end of last season and Jose agreed.

    "We auctioned the night, which we called 'A Dinner With Genius', in June - Friday was the night we got everyone together.

    "We are thrilled at the success and were additionally thrilled that Jose, on top of the work Alex does for us, was able to give us some of his time."

    Webb appointed for Man Utd v Chelsea clash
    tribalfooball.com - November 21, 2006

    Ref Howard Webb has been appointed in charge for this week's massive Premiership crunch match between Manchester United and Chelsea.

    Referees are currently in the spotlight with Phil Dowd the latest to be strongly criticised after a number of controversial decisions in Blackburn's draw with Tottenham at Ewood Park.
