    since i am an instructor myself i can better explain how you need to do this ..

    first you need to find a company you want to work with .. in my opinion DO NOT join skate sports, urban inline and singapore inline ..

     

    my recommandation is INLINECULTURE .. i'm working there actually .. just go their website and look for the number .. when you get their number call and ask when they are having their next instructor course.. they are not open on mondays so dont bother ..

    when you've got a date on when they have their instructor course just keep in contact with them cos it is usually not confirmed until the last minute

    and also if possible go down to the shops and try to make friends with them .. they have 2 shops in ECP(east coast park) 1 shop in katong 1 shop in WCP

    i suggest just go to the ECP one .. (it's nearer to the lagoon in the park itself)

    names you should take note is me(xiao ben), waiyin, steven, Ang, Alloy360
