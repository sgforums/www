    Asean Football Championship 2007

    Sa Wa Dee Krab

    The almost final showdown is here. Thailand meets Singapore at the National Stadium, for the very last time tonight. Thailand has been filling the press with mind games, to throw Singapore off their guard. Singapore should not fall into this trap and have our own strategy, in place. Thailand is known for their attack and not so solid defending. Singapore should capitalize on this, by hitting Thailand ferociously with counter attacks. Each time Thailand attacks and fails, we should be giving them a high tempo counter attack, if this does work, Thailand will be set on defending. If they do, than Singapore should play a role of, attacking and counter attacking. What Singapore should not be doing is defending, even If they are a goal down. Winning here is a must, if Singapore does not want to feel the pressure on the second leg in Bangkok.

    Tickets for the game can be bought at the National Stadium itself on match day.

    Good luck, Singapore! We are all watching.
