    It was the first ever Mizuno Run in Singapore and it shows at their amatuer approach to some of the aspect in organising such an event.

The run starts late(bad) , the traffic police not doing justice by stop me a couple of times from dashing across the roads at the traffic lights. Runners have a scolding match with the TP (rarely seem).... Water point not well manned.
