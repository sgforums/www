    Originally posted by Master -_-:
    wat about the 1/2 ball and 1 ball thingy? can explain? i dun understand?
    usually when 2 teams meet, there will b a team regarded as stronger n the other is deem weaker. so to b fair in the market, the stronger team will give handicap to the weaker team. lets say if Alpha FC gives a 1/2 ball handicap to Bravo FC, the scoreline at the start of the match will b

    Alpha FC 0
    Bravo FC 0.5

    u can decide whether u wanna take Alpha FC to give 1/2 ball or take Bravo FC eat 1/2 ball.

    if u take Alpha FC give 1/2 ball:
    u need to win the match. u lose the bet even if the match ends up in a draw game. eg1. if Alpha FC score 1 goal, scoreline will b:

    Alpha FC 1
    Bravo FC 0.5
    (u win the bet)

    eg2. Alpha FC n Bravo FC score 1 goal each, scoreline will b:

    Alpha FC 1
    Bravo FC 1.5
    (u lose the bet)

    if u take Bravo FC eat ball, u need a draw or Bravo FC win the match to win the bet.

    the odds r decided by the bookies. there r "level", "level-half", "1/2 ball", "1 ball" n so on.. the odds may change depending on the market.
