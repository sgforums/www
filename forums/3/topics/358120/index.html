    Excerpts from the article :

    Singapore Table Tennis Association President Lee Bee Wah yesterday announced that "four bous and four girls - all Singaporeans" have been shortlisted for the Youth Olympic Games(YOG).

    Noting that all the table-tennis players fielded at last year's Olympics were foreign-born,she said,"In the past,a lot of them were FTS(products of the Foreign Talent Scheme)."We'd like to give local talent a chance."

    The eight who have been shortlisted are : Clarence Chew,13 of the Singapore Sports School(SSP) ; Oscar Chau,12 of Raffles Instituition(RI) : Tay Jit Kiat,13 of SSP ; Kerry Tan,14 of RI ; Isabelle Li,14 of SSP and Mandy Wong,12 ; Lam Teng Si,12 ; Chau Hai Qing,14 all of Raffles Girls School.
