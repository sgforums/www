    SAMBA FUTEBOL CAMP

    www.theRcard.com

    VENUE: The Cage, Singapore's First Indoor Stadium (38 Jalan Benaan Kapal)
    DATES: JUNE 2006 ( 5-7, 12-14, 19-21, 26-28 )

    The 3-day football camp, tailored for boys and girls age 8 - 14 years old includes discovering football, character building and developing social skills.

    Limited to 20 participants per age group/ camp, it is open to those interested to learn the correct playing techniques of football whilst having fun playing the game.

    Adopting the Brazilian football methodology, we will impart the skills similar to the method of training used by the 5 times world champions to aspiring football players.

    Participants will each receive a FREE Football Kit (Jersey, Shorts, Socks and Ball), a Training Evaluation by our Brazilian Coaches and Certificate of Achievement at the end of the camp. Participants will also get the chance to meet a prominent National Team player for an autograph and photo-taking session.

    The Red Card will present the 'Best Samba Player' award for each age group per camp. The winner gets to select 3 friends for an Exclusive Football Clinic with our Brazilian coaches.

    Registration fee is $220 per participant (refreshments are provided). Interested participants can register via fax: 6438 0235 or email: info@theRcard.com by Thursdays (JUNE 1, 8, 15, 22) respectively, the week before the camp.
