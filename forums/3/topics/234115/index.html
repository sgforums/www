    The battle comes to our own backyard

    Tonight, Singapore takes on Malaysia for the second leg, of the semis at the National Stadium. All the fans want from this game is a win, forget about scoring as many goals, as possible or trying to beat Malaysia by a high margin. What Singaporeans want, is a never die attitude from the players, a fight to the end. Qualifying for the finals should be their main, objective and about 45,000 tickets had been snapped up, so far. For those of you who are attending, the match tonight at the stadium, do not forget to stand up, when our National anthem is playing. All the best Singapore!
