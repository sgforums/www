Run For Hope 17 Nov 2013

  • FireIce
    25 Sep 13, 22:24

    Run For Hope (RFH) is an annual run organised by Four Seasons Hotel Singapore, Regent Singapore and the National Cancer Centre Singapore (NCCS) to raise much-needed awareness and support for cancer research.

    For more than 30 years, the Four Seasons group of hotel & resorts across the world have been deeply committed to the cause of advancing cancer research.

     

    Date: 17 November 2013, Sunday

    Time: 7.00am

    Venue to gather: The Promontory @ Marina Bay

    Run Categories: 10km / 3.5km
