    Presenting 'Legs and Paddles 2009'!

    Legs and Paddles is a biathlon combining running and kayaking together for an exciting mix! There are various categories to cater to all! Regardless of male, female, young or old! Here are some brief details:

    Date and Time : 23rd May 2009 (Saturday)
    Venue : East Coast Park
    Price : Team - $140 for team of 4 ($5 off for each NUS student!)
    Open - $30 (NUS), $33 (other students), $35 (public)
    Challenge - $35 (NUS), $40 (others)

    Visit our website at nussportsclub.org/legsnpaddles.html or nuscanoeing.wordpress.com for the latest info with regards to LNP 09 as well as to sign up.

    NUS Legs & Paddles 2009

    23 August 2009 @ Marina Basin (Kallang)

    1. LNP CHALLENGE

    Distance: 8-km run and 5-km kayak (singles only)
    Categories: ‘Men’ and ‘Women’
    Price: $23 for NUS members and $25 for public participants

    2. OPEN

    Distance: 5-km run and 3-km kayak (singles/doubles)
    Categories: ‘Men Singles’, ‘Women Singles’, 'Doubles'
    Price: $20 for NUS members and $22 for public participants

    3. TEAM

    1st Leg: 3-km run
    2nd Leg: 3-km kayak (doubles)
    3rd Leg: 3-km run

    You can participate in teams of 3 or 4. For teams of 3, the runner would complete both running legs of the race. The other two team members would complete the kayaking leg in a doubles kayak.

    For teams of 4, the first runner would complete the first running leg, while the second runner would complete the third leg of the race.

    Price:

    4. LNP RUN

    Distance: 8-km run
    Price: $12 for NUS members and $14 for public participants
