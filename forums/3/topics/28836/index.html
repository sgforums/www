    Singa World Sports started out with the vision to cultivate lifelong interest, skills and positive attitude that lead to an active and healthy lifestyle in youth. By reaching out to the wider participation with the help of proper guidance, we hope to help youth in their development in sports.
    At Singa World Sports, our mission is to assist athletes realize their fullest potential with professional guidance and by doing so, perform at their peak achieving personal best.
    We realize that there are many people out there who want to play sports but might not have the opportunity. We believe that with a structured and all rounded program that covers not only the technical aspect of each sport but also the participants physical fitness and mental skills, participants will be able to have fun and at the same time play at a competitive level.
