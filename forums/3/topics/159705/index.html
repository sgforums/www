    Well.. there are no MMA type matches here in Sg but there are 2 gyms that I know of.

    Mixed Martial Arts Academy - Run by Darren Da Silva. Alot of training partners and a good mix between hardcore and leisure students. The only club with someone who has fought MMA in Indonesia before. Located at 41A North Canal Road, opp Speakers Corner. You can call Darren at 91852887. They recently had an advert in the New Paper. Darren also came out in an article there recently as well.

    Contact Kinetics - Run by Chris De Wett, one of the top Kyokushin Full-Contact Karate fighters in Singapore. They do MMA too. Not sure of the standard though, but I heard it's not bad. Gym is located at the boxing gym in Farrer Park. Website is www.contactkinetics.com

    You ask me, I'm a little biased cause I used to train at Darren's. But I suggest you check out both before making a decision. =)
