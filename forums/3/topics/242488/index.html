    When I was in BMT, there was this anti Man-U fan from the next bunk that went on and on the whole day about Man U. He always made fun of how Dwight Yorkie reminded him of Darkie toothpaste (now known as Darlie).

    He had his own favourite club, but quite honestly I don't think anyone remembered. Because he never spoke about them. He was always going on and on about Man U this and Man U that. He was obssessed with Man U, macham like his whole life revolved around hating Man U. Even when people weren't interested in listening to him, he'd go on and on. People would just stand up and leave him talking to himself, but he'd just go on to the next person and keep on talking and talking. He was so ostricised that he resorted to coming our bunk as well as going other bunks to whine about Man U.

    Towards the end of our BMT, some people got so pissed off by him that they decided to give him one heck of a blanket party one night. Kenna punched in the head, kicked but no weapons (not so inhuman lah). Some of those beating him didn't even follow soccer, they just couldn't stand him. And his own bunkmates? They were helping to keep watch outside in case the company specialists or officers came around.

    After that he tried to complain to Platoon Officer and Platoon Sergeant, nobody really cared given the reputation he gave himself. He even wanted to go see OO. Can't say I pity him, but we all had peace from him for the rest of BMT. Maybe should have done it sooner. Anyway, this was years ago.
