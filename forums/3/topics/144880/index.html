    It's time again for SPCA's biggest fundraising event of the year --
    our Fun Run at Macritchie Reservoir! Last year, we raised $19,000 to
    help the dogs, cats, and small animals that come through the SPCA's
    facility.

    This year, Cheryl Tay, Miss Singapore Universe 2005, will be flagging
    off this 4.8km half-trail, half-bitumen fun run as our guest-of-honor.
    There will be prizes for the top competitive runners, as well as
    lucky draw prizes for noncompetitive runners and walkers. Plus a free
    Sony Music CD for all race-day attendees (while stocks last).

    WHEN: Sunday, 21st August, 2005 -- registration begins at 6a.m. --
    race starts at 7a.m. SHARP

    WHERE: Macritchie Reservoir Park (Adam Rd entrance)

    REGISTRATION FEE: $25, including a specially-designed t-shirt {while
    sizes and stocks last}

    TOP PRIZE: Tudor Monarch Quartz Gents Watch

    WAYS TO REGISTER:
    *download a registration form from www.spca.org.sg & mail a completed
    form in with your cheque payment
    *visit SPCA Reception @ 31 Mount Vernon Road from 15th July to 20th
    August, 8a-4p weekdays, 10a-3:30p weekends
    *visit BigO Cafe-Restaurant @ 2nd level of Wheelock Place from 15th
    July to 20th August, 11:30a-7p daily
    *show up on raceday itself from 6a.m. in the 2nd carpark of Macritchie
    Reservoir Park (look for the white tents)

    PLEDGE CARDS: For those interested in going the extra mile for the
    animals, pledge cards can be signed out at the SPCA Reception Desk for
    you to have friends and family help raise funds without having to
    attend the race itself.

    For any enquiries, please email spca_fundraising@yahoo.com.sg or call
    the SPCA at 6287-5355 during the hours mentioned above.

    The SPCA would like to thank the following sponsors for their gracious
    generosity in this 3rd year of SPCA Fun Run: Tudor, Keppel Offshore &
    Marine, BigO Cafe-Restaurant, Stacey Foo, Richard & Amanda Copping,
    and Forest Prints Design.

    Hope to see you there! Please feel free to pass this email on to
    anyone you think might be interested!
