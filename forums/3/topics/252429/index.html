Chelsea Signs The Next "Cech"

  • stellazio
    4 May 07, 22:47



    Czech teenager Jan Sebek claims to have agreed a contract to join Premiership champions Chelsea.

    Dubbed 'the new Petr Cech' (pictured), Sebek trained with Chelsea in January and appears to have shown enough to warrant a deal.

    The 16-year-old goalkeeper has been catching the eye on loan at Viktoria Plzen from parent club FC Tachov in recent months.

    He had also been wanted by several of Czech Republic's top clubs, including Sparta and Slavia Prague, and Slovan Liberec.

    But Sebek has accepted the chance to move to Stamford Bridge and is determined to make the most of his opportunity.

    "It is a step towards big football," Sebek told MF Dnes.

    "But I know I still have a long way to go.

    "I hope I will not disappoint anyone."
