    A better head to head record just means which team wins more when both teams meet in the league.

    For example, Real Madrid won Barcelona 2-0 at the Bernabeu and drew 3-3 at Nou Camp this season. This means that Real Madrid had a better head to head record this season.

    So when both teams have the same points, the team with the better head to head record would be on the top, inregardless of goal differences.
