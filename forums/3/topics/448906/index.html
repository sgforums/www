    After a 2 years absence, Triathlon Association of Singapore if proud to announce that, the Singapore Aquathlon is back! This year’s edition combines both the National Championships with the Inter Schools and Collegiate Challenge. Come 19 May 2012, the School’s individual and Division Champions, and the National Champions will be known!

     

    To celebrate our return, and to show our appreciation for the continuous support from Multi-Sports Enthusiasts in Singapore, TAS is giving away 8 Fabulous prizes* worth up to $500, in a lucky draw!

     

    How to Qualify?

    Register for our Singapore Aquathlon 2012 before 31st March 2012 to qualify for the Lucky Draw! Winners will be announced and notify by email on 2nd April 2012.

     

    *(Prizes on offer: Rudy Project Freeon Sports Sunglasses, OSIM UCrown head Massager, Power Breath Trainer, TAS 1 year Memberships)

     

    Are you the fastest aquathlete at school level or even at national level? Come and take the Challenge, and contest yourself or your school with the best in Singapore!

     

    For more information, please visit our website: www.singaporeaquathlon.com
