    Benni issues Rovers vow
    By Alex Livie - Created on 14 Oct 2006

    South African striker Benni McCarthy has assured Blackburn Rovers fans that he will not turn his back on the club.

    The forward was brought to Ewood Park in the summer, as Mark Hughes endeavoured to fill the void left by Craig Bellamy's departure to Liverpool.

    McCarthy has made a seamless transition to life in The Premiership and does not have any intention of moving on.

    "I love being here," stated McCarthy. "Everyone says it rains every day, but it's sunshine sometimes and it's fine by me.

    "Blackburn were persistent and it was always my dream to play in England.

    "If I did well and a big club came in for me, it would be up to Blackburn. But if they want me to stay, I'll stay.

    "Blackburn's wish is my command. I want to stay loyal to them, they gave me my chance."
