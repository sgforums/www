    A statement from the CEO of 'MP & SILVA' (Media rights holder of Serie A) to all Serie A fans , as well as the Press in S'pore - “It has been brought to our attention that the large community of Serie A supporters in Singapore has expressed their disappointment with the cessation of Serie A broadcasts, and the lack of opportunity to support their favourite teams on television. We would like to highlight that since early this year, every possible effort has been taken to provide all broadcasters with reasonable packages in bid to secure television coverage for Serie A to cater to local football fans. However, we regret to inform that in spite of our best efforts, none of the broadcasters have indicated their interest to broadcast Serie A as yet,” said Andrea Radrizzani, Group CEO, MP & Silva. “We have been able to offer Serie A fans to football fans across Asia, with Singapore being one of the few exceptions. As the international rights-holder for Serie A, we would also like to offer our sincere apologies to fans and supporters in Singapore, and we will put in our best efforts to work with various parties to bring the Italian league back on local television screens.”
