    Del Nido: Rijkaard only good enough for Sevilla B
    tribalfooball.com - December 19, 2006

    Sevilla president Jose Maria del Nido has dismissed the credentials of Barcelona and their coach Frank Rijkaard.
    Del Nido's club sits atop the Primera Liga standings and he declared: "For me, everything that is part of my club is the best. And if you ask me about Rijkaard, I would say that he would be the trainer of Sevilla B because for me Juande (Ramos) is the best there is. If Rijkaard is bothered by this, that's his problem.

    "If we qualify for the Champions League, we will be the team that everyone is frightened of."
