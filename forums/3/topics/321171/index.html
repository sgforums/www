    Champions Amatuer Football League (CAFL)

    Expected to commence on early August.

    3 Leagues on different timing during weekends.
    8 to 10 teams per league.
    1.) CAFL Sat evening - 5pm
    2.) CAFL Sun morning - 9am/11am (Majority 9am)
    3.) CAFL Sun evening - 3pm/5pm (Majority 5pm)

    Prizes
    Champions - $1000 Cash with a team trophy and 18 individual medal
    Runners up - $500 Cash
    3rd Placing - $200 Cash
    Best Fair play award $100 with a team trophy.

    Fees
    Registration - $100
    Deposit - $100 (will be refunded upon completion of the league)
    Total: $200

    Per game fee
    SSC School Fields - $100/game
    Artifical Fields - $140/game


    Tournament Age Group: Open. (Present Prime League & S-League players are not eligible)    Strongly encourage only social footballers to take part.
        
    Rules
    All FIFA rules apply. Except for :    
            1) Substitution-. Only 9 rolling subs @ any period of time    
            2) Yellow Card- Will not be carried forward for the next game    
            3) Red Card – Player will be suspended for 1 game

    Fairplay     1) Yellow Card- 1pts, Red Card- 2pts will be awarded against the team.    
                       2) The team with the least points will win the Fair Play Award.    
               
    Duration    40 minutes per half with 10 minutes half time interval
