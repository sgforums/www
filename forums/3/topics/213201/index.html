    Merseyside Police have already confirmed they have terminated their inquiries into the Manchester City midfielder's actions at Goodison Park on Saturday, following consultation with the FA.

    But the FA would not be rushed into making a decision on whether to punish Barton, and are expected to finally issue a statement on the matter this morning.
