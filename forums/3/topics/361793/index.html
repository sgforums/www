    Hi all!

    NUS is orgainising an annual running event called RunNUS.

    Organized by the NUS Students' Sports Club, the highly anticipated RunNUS is once again back in action this year. We welcome all avid runners to join us, as we bring to you RunNUS 09.

    Date: 30 August 2009
    Venue: National University of Singapore
    Categories: Competitive (12 km), Mass Run (5 km), Team (Group of 4)

    RunNUS 09 Registration will open on 1st June 2009!

    Enjoy early bird discounts by submitting your registration and payment before 30th June 2009.

    First Walk-In Registration will be held from 26th to 27th June 2009    .

    It will be the first major sporting event in the new academic year, drawing a considerable number of participants. In addition, it serves as an opportunity for the freshmen to learn more about NUS.

    This year, with the success of past year experiences, we are expecting a large turnout of approximately 1000 to 1500 participants on the whole.

    Do check out the official website http://runnus09.com for more details!
