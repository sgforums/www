  • ClubM_sg

    Hi folks.... Football in S'pore has become overwhelmingly popular during the last 2 to 3 years thanks to the EPL. However, the standards have not improved at all. I believe if Singapore is to make it BIG in football, many barriers need to be obliterated.

    First is the accessibility of football fields. In order to get access to almost every football field, there is a sum of money to be paid. 'FOC' fields are extremely rare in Singapore.

    Secondly, there are too many rules everywhere. Just look at the third world countries like Nigeria, Cameroun, Brazil, etc. Kids play soccer (and maybe any other sport) in the streets, on any empty fields, etc. Here, the moment you start doing these sort of things, you will be chased out or complaints will come from everywhere.

    Thirdly, Singapore teams definitely need more support from their own people.

    Well, there are many more reasons why S'pore can't make it to the highest level in Football presently, unless they look at the root of the issue and buid up from there.

    Cheers!

    Club M Singapore
