    Mancini green light for Inter Milan's Beckham offer
    tribalfooball.com - December 11, 2006

    Inter Milan coach Roberto Mancini wants to sign Real Madrid midfielder David Beckham.
    Beckham is coming off contract at the end of this season and is a target for Inter president Massimo Moratti, who hopes to snap him up on a Bosman.

    Mancini has spoken to Sven Goran Eriksson about the former England captain and is convinced Becks can add something extra to his table-topping team

    AC Milan discuss swap for Fiorentina ace Toni
    tribalfooball.com - December 12, 2006

    AC Milan have begun talks with Fiorentina over an end-of-season swap deal involving Luca Toni and Alberto Gilardino.

    The two clubs have been discussing several of eachother's players and Milan are pushing to have a deal setup which would see Gilardino reunited with his old Parma coach Cesare Prandelli in Florence in exchange for Toni. While insisting he's happy at Fiorentina, Toni has reportedly told close friends his dream is play for one of the big Milan clubs before calling time on his career.

    Milan have also asked about a January deal for Fiorentina defender Tomas Ujfalusi and are prepared to offer Daniele Bonera, Marek Jankolovsky plus cash for the Czech Republic international.

    Juventus continue Real Madrid talks for Robinho
    tribalfooball.com - December 13, 2006

    Juventus are continuing talks with Real Madrid for their wing ace Robinho.
    Corriere dello Sport says the Brazilian youngster has been identified by Juve powerbrokers as a major cornerstone of their squad rebuilding next summer.

    A loan deal has been mooted, though Real are now reluctant to part with Robinho after his sudden form reversal following a difficult start under coach Fabio Capello.

    "He has all the qualities required to become an even better player than myself," admitted Pele recently.
