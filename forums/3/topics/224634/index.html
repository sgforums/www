    David Beckham is set to return to Old Trafford to play for a Europe XI against Manchester United.
    The charity game, on 13 March 2007, will mark the 50th anniversaries of United's European Cup debut and the signing of the Treaty of Rome.

    The Real Madrid midfielder will be part of a squad coached by Italy's World Cup-winning boss Marcello Lippi.

    "I'm delighted by the prospect of such a game and am looking forward to taking on my friend Sir Alex," said Lippi.
