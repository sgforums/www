    Catch world-class basketball at the Singapore Indoor Stadium from August 11 to 13, 2006 when the Singapore Cup comes back for a second year running.

    Basketball fans here and in the region can look forward to three days of brilliant play by a number of NBA stars from four top national teams.

    Argentina, Gold medalists at the 2004 Olympics in Athens.
    Defending World Champions Serbia and Montenegro.
    Slovenia - Ranked 22th in the world.
    Spain - Ranked 5th in the world.

    Also featuring: Singapore All-Stars, Malaysia All-Stars, Melbourne Tigers (Australia) & Yulong Club (Chinese Taipei)

    Singapore Indoor Stadium
