    I'm not so sure, actually - if history is anything to go by, the odds are stacked against Nadal to defend his crown. Kuerten was the last player to defend his title back in 2001, but even he failed to defend his maiden triumph when he won it as a teenager back in 1997. Then again, neither did Michael Chang( '89 ) or Mats Wilander( '88 ) who both won their maiden French Open crowns in their youth.

Here's hoping Federer will become the first player since Rod Laver to hold all 4 Grand Slams at once too, but more importantly, set up an epic final with Nadal on June 11th.
