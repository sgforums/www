Mourinho, in contrast, cared more about his own team, than bothering to respond to them. Arsenal and Man U spent too much team worrying about Chelsea, that they forgot about other teams. It affected the players, and that's why Man U/Arsenal are now losing to teams like Blackburn, Boro, West Brom.

But next season, if they can learn not to let chelsea affect them, and sign a few new players, things may change.
