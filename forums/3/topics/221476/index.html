    AC Milan risk losing Brazilian foursome
    tribalfooball.com - November 20, 2006

    AC Milan are set to lose at least three of their Brazilian stars at the end of this season.
    Milan directors have genuine fear they will be forced to sell Kaka to Real Madrid next June, particularly if they fail to qualify for the Champions League this season, says La Repubblica.

    Also on their way are goalkeeper Dida, who has refused to sign a new contract, fullback Cafu and veteran attacker Serginho, who is facing retirement due to a back complaint.

    Inter Milan president Moratti draws up Beckham offer
    tribalfooball.com - November 21, 2006

    Inter Milan president Massimo Moratti is preparing a pre-contract offer for Real Madrid midfielder David Beckham.
    Luis Figo is set to leave Inter at the end of this season, says Corriere dello Sport, and Moratti wants Beckham as a direct replacement. The former England captain is off contract in the summer and has baulked at discussing new terms while he remains on the outer of coach Fabio Capello's plans.

    Moratti knows the difficulty some players have with Capello and is confident Beckham's arrival can add to his squad for next season
