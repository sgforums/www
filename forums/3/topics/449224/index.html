    Run a lap within 1 min 15 s, then rest for 2 minutes (3 if you can't run the next lap within that time, you can shorten it in the future). Do this 6 times.

    When you get used to it, shorten the rest period, or try running 2 laps within 2:30 (thrice), or 3 within 3:40 (twice).

    Don't underestimate the importance of recovery between trainings.
