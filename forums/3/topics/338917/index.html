PLK PADDLERS Dragonboat Recruitment 21 Dec 08

  • dragonboatplk
    30 Nov 08, 09:05

    PLK PADDLERS (Paya Lebar Kovan Youth Group Dragonboat Team) is starting her recruitment exercise for the new season. New or seasoned rowers, both guys and gals, are welcomed to join us!

    Venue: Kallang Water Sports Centre

    Date: 21 December 2008

    Time: 0900 - 1300hrs

    Registration is free!

    For more information, check out our team blog dragonboatplk.blogspot.com.

    Alternatively you can drop us a mail at dragonboatplk@gmail.com.

    Here's looking forward to seeing you guys on the boat!

    Experience is not a pre-requisite to join us in our Open House. You must register with us so that we can confirm your place on the boat. Equipment will be provided. To register, drop us a mail with your name and contact number.
