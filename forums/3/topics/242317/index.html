    ESPZEN is, by far, the largest football league in Singapore (and we dare say Asia) - we have a total of nine Sunday divisions and a midweek league totaling up to 78 football clubs enjoying weekly matches.
    The Sunday league has a promotion and relegation system which entices the more competitive teams in the league. The more social teams come together every week to play football, talk about football and go drinking after to discuss more football!
    The match lasts them a little less than 2 hours but they will go on throughout the night discussing about the match for 5 hours on beer
    ESPZEN is not only a league... it is a CULTURE filled with people of different races, nationalities, religions - everyone who love FOOTBALL.
