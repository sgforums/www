    The Community Games will be back from March to May 2014!

    Are you a sports lover? Do you want to know more friends? Are you looking for competitions where you and your family and friends can participate together as a team? If you answered 'YES' to any of the above questions, come join us in the Community Games!

    The Community Games is a multi-sports competition held across the island, bringing various exciting sports to the heart of the community and connecting residents of different ages, races and living across estates. The sports featured in the Games are Badminton, Basketball, Bowling, Football, Netball, Sepaktakraw and Table Tennis!

    Community Games not only provides you a chance to play your favourite sports with your family and friends, it is also a platform for you to make new friends from all walks of life who share the same sports interests as you!

    So what are you waiting for? Registration is now open! Spread this piece of news to your sports kakis, family members and friends now! Send your competed forms to pa_community_games@pa.gov.sg.

    Do also like us at www.facebook.com/CommunitySportsClub for more updates and adrenaline-pumping moments of the Games!

    Community Games, more than just a game, is brought to you by People's Association.
