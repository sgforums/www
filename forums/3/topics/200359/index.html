    Ok let me explain the game to make it easier for you guys.

    You are given 100 credits to build your team. Any players as long as long you keep to this budget.

    You earn points according to the real game. For example : Arsenal 5-0 Man Utd .
    Henry scores all 5 goals and you have Henry in the team, you earn lots of points.If your team's goalkeeper keep a clean sheet , you earn points too especially if it's a victory. There are plus and minus points. Plus = Score goals , create goals . Minus points = Yellow or Red card, own goal . Your objective is to score as many points as u can . Choose your best 11 team . The game starts from the start of
    EPL to the end of EPL season. If an EPL game is postponed, you miss the chance of winning pts. Do keep watch of injuried players. You can transfer players any time of the season but max 1 per week , if you want to transfer 2 or more , you have to deduct some pts from your team. (not worth it )

    Hope this helps.
