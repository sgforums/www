    The silver goal rule has been in effect since the last world cup to encourage attacking soccer. Golden goal simply nullified the game-play of both sides since neither were prepared to come out of their shell, fearful of conceding the goal that might end their dreams.

    Given the silver goal rule, one can still attack, and if they concede a goal can still go in search for an equalizer up to half time.
