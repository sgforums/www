    Mancini future splits Inter Milan board, squad
    tribalfooball.com - September 19, 2006

    The future of Inter Milan coach Roberto Mancini is causing rifts at board level and also amongst the playing group, it has been revealed.

    Mancini's future is the subject of intense speculation after Inter's disappointing start to the season.

    La Stampa says amongst the players, Luis Figo and Dejan Stankovic are loyal to Mancino, however he is offside with Javier Zanetti, Adriano, Marco Materazzi, Santiago Solari and Julio Cruz. The new arrivals, such as Patrick Vieira, Maicon and Hernan Crespo, are staying on the fringes of the debate inside the dressing room.

    Meanwhile, at board level, Lele Oriali is fully supportive of the coach. However, Marco Branca and Rinaldi Ghelfi are the most critical, while Ernesto Paolillo, the general executive manager, is neutral.

    Roma's Pizarro has dig at Inter Milan tifosi
    tribalfooball.com - September 19, 2006

    Roma midfielder David Pizarro has had a dig at Inter Milan fans ahead of tomorrow's big match.

    The Chilean moved to Roma in the summer from Inter and said today: "The tifosi of Roma are exceptional, they get very involved.

    "There is no comparison with those from Inter, they are calmer.

    "At Rieti, during my first day with the Giallorosso, it has been beautiful. Sometimes in this trade the truth gets lost, especially what the fans can do for a player."

    Pizarro added: "Roma are the equal of Inter. We're now above them on the table and suddenly the team everyone wants to beat. This game will have a bearing on the championship, for sure."
