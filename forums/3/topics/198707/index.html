    Juventus to be relegated.Also AC Milan, Lazio, Fiorentina

    By Myles Palmer

    THE CHIEF PROSECUTOR, Stefano Palazzi, has just said that Juventus should be relegated to Serie C for match-fixing.

    AC Milan, Lazio and Fiorentina should be relegated to Serie B.

    Juventus's shares have been suspended on the stock exchange.

    THIS IS AN ATOMIC BOMB in European football.

    If you've backed Italy to beat Germany tonight, lay off that bet NOW !

    Torino were promoted in a play-off, so they will be Turin's club in Serie A.

    Last season Genoa were promoted but bought two matches, so they went down again.

    What a culture ! What a country ! What a shambles !

    Uefa need to know today which clubs will play in the Champions League and the Uefa Cup.

    But Uefa have given the Italian FA another 20 days to supply the names of the clubs who will participate.

    THIS COULD FREEZE all big transfer deals till managers see who will be sold by those corrupt clubs.

    Kaka, anybody?

    Zambrotta going cheap?

    Buffon brings his gloves to London?

    The entire Juventus squad should sue fixer-in-chief Luciano Moggi.
