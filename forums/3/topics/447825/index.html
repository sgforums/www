NUS RowRunRace '12 - A Kayaking and Running Biathlon

  • NUSRowRunRace12
    24 Feb 12, 18:25

    Hello everyone, there's an exciting event coming up!

    NUS RowRunRace is a kayaking and running biathlon organised by NUS Canoeing Club. Formerly known as Legs and Paddles, RowRunRace offers a unique racing experience as the only biathlon of its kind in Singapore.

    This year's biathlon promises to be better than before, with the race being held at the iconic Marina Bay Sands.

    Enjoy the scenic views of the Singapore Flyer and the Merlion whilst kayaking in the Marina reservoir. The race track also takes you past famous locations like Marina Bay Sands and the Helix Bridge. You can also get a glimpse of the Gardens by the Bay when it officially opens in June!

    Don’t worry if you don’t have any kayaking certification, as the newly-introduced Open Deck Doubles category removes that restriction. Try out kayaking with your partner in the heart of Marina Bay!

    Brave the wave, beat the heat. Join us for a fun-filled experience and cheer on your friends!

    ---------------------------------------

    Date of Event: 03rd June 2012 (Sunday)
    Time: 7.00 am to 6.00 pm

    Venue: Marina Bay Sands, Beside the Convention Centre

    Registration Date: Begins 20th February 2012

    Distance: 5km kayak followed by 5km run
