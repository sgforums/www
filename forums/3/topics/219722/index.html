    Remembrance Day silence at the Bridge
    Friday, Nov 10, 2006
    Remembrance Day will be marked at Stamford Bridge on Saturday by a period of silence prior to kick-off between Chelsea and Watford.

    The 11th day of November silence will remember those who fought for freedom during the two World Wars and also the brave men and women who have lost their lives serving their country in more recent conflicts.
