    The Ultimate Beach Soccer Challenge is a platform for the launch of the National Beach Soccer League (NBSL).

    The Red Card is organizing a 5-a-side Beach Soccer tournament on the 10th and 11th of June 2006 (2 weeks prior to the NBSL) at Sentosa, Tanjong Beach.

    The 2-day event will consist of 32 teams each from the Youth (Under-16) and Adults Open category. Each team will play a minimum of 2 matches to make the last 8 for the qualifying rounds where teams will play 5 minutes each half.

    With a total of 64 expected teams playing on 2 pitches, the tournament will run from 9am to 6pm on both days.

    The winning team from the Adults Open category will play in the NBSL for free. And winning teams from both categories will walk-away with attractive prizes up to $500 cash.

    Entry fee of $50 for Youth (Under-16) and $70 for Adults Open is applicable per team. Teams can register up to 10 players with a minimum of 7. Interested teams can register before the 2nd of June 2006 via email: feza@theRcard.com or fax: 6438 0031.

    DATES
    10 & 11 June 2006

    VENUE
    Sentosa, Tanjong Beach

    CATEGORIES
    Youth (Under-16) &
    Adults Open
