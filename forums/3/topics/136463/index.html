2005 Beach Volleyball Open

    The Championship will be held on 9, 10 and 16 July 2005. Venue is at Siloso Beach, Sentosa.

    This event is open to all Singaporeans, PRs of Singapore, and foreigners with valid student passes, employment passes or work permits.

    Total Number of Teams
    Men: 32 teams
    Women: 24 teams

    Check the website for more information on the tournament.

    Contact:
    Angelina Scanlon
    Director of Beach Volleyball Development
    Tel: 9479 9110
    Fax: -
    Email: vaslist@vas.org.sg

    Registration Information:

    The registration is based on first-come-first-serve basis.
    The registration fee for affiliated members is $60.00 per team (both players of the team must be affiliated with VAS.
    The registration fee for non-affiliated members is $80 per team.
    Registration fee to be paid in cash or cheque (payable to Volleyball Association of Singapore).
    The registration form, TOGETHER WITH the registration fee, shall be submitted at the VAS office on the CLOSING DATE, FRIDAY, 17 June 2005, 5.00 pm.
    Late registration WILL NOT be rejected, however a late fee will be charged. Registrations submitted after 17 June 2005, 5.00pm will be charged at $120 per team (for affiliated and non-affiliated members).
    No more registrations will be accepted after Friday, 1 July 2005, 5.00pm.
