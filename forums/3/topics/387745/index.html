    Hello guys!

    Surf n Sweat, organized by NTU Sports Club, is back in its 15th year! It will be held at Siloso beach, Sentosa on Sunday, 7 February 2010.

    Look out for exciting games and attractive prizes to be won!

    New attractions include a main event Surf n Hunt (in addition to the traditional Surf n Run), more side games such as Tchoukball, other interesting games such as Zorb ball and Sumo Wrestling competition, not excluding a hotdog eating competition and even a photography competition.

    Check out http://surfnsweat.ntusportsclub.sg for more details. See ya there!
