    Attention to all sports enthusiasts, Surf n Sweat is back for the 14th year!

    NTU Sports Club will be holding Surf n Sweat 2009 on 1st March 2009, Sunday at Tanjong Beach, Sentosa.

    There will be 12 different categories for the main event -Surf n Run.
    And not forgetting the all time favourite Side Games!

    Beach Soccer - (4+1) all guys
    Ultimate Frisbee - (5+1) min. 1 gal on court any pt of time
    Beach Volleyball - (4+1) min. 1 gal on court any pt of time
    Beach Captain's Ball - (5+1) max. 2 guys on court any pt of time
    Touch Rugby - (3 on court when attacking, 2 on court when defending) min. 1 gal on court any pt of time

    We'll also be introducing Bossaball, a new game for trying out all day, as well as various stage games and performances!

    There bound to be something for everyone!! Come enjoy the sun, sand and sea with us!

    Find out more and sign up NOW at http://surfnsweat.ntusportsclub.sg/
    Registration closes on 16th February.

    Hurry! Before all the spots are taken up!
