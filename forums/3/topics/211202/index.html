Middlesbrough kid Walker handed England U18 captaincy

  • ghimpheng
    20 Sep 06, 16:49
    tribalfooball.com - September 20, 2006

    Middlesbrough are convinced they have potential star on their hands in new England U18 captain Josh Walker.
    Walker has already made his debut for Boro and has actually captained England teams at U16 and U17 level.

    The Boro midfielder will lead England in tomorrow night's match against France and told the Evening Gazette: "It is a great feeling, it's what you dream of as a young boy.

    "You dream of captaining England, I have been lucky enough to have done it since I was 16. The feeling when you pull on the shirt is class. It's different to playing in the Premier League - I'm lucky enough to have done both."
