Juventus skipper Del Piero: I'll play on for eight years

  • ghimpheng

    Juventus ace Alessandro del Piero says thoughts of retirement are far from his mind.
    "I have a message for those who have massacred me in the press," snapped Del Piero. "I intend to play for another eight years."

    Del Piero also said: "I'm like (Gigi) Buffon, I want to see Juve put together a winning team in the close season. I was happy Mauro Camoranesi and David Trezeguet chose to stay this season, if only for selfish reasons. Their decision has made us stronger."
