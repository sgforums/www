    A Belgian football team has conceded 58 goals in just two games.

    FC Wijtschale is said to have become the nation's favourite joke by their performances.

    The team's B side lost to 28-0 to Zillebeke last weekend. The week before, they conceded 30 goals.

    Local newspaper Het Laatste Nieuws calculated that the team lets in a goal on average every three minutes and six seconds.

    But despite scoring just four goals and conceding 139 in their first eight matches, the players are not ready to give up yet.

    Club Spokesman Mars Elslanger said: "They really love football and all the other teams credit them for their spirit."

    FC Wijtschale's A team has only a slightly better record. They lost 11-1 at the weekend.
