    National Beach Soccer League (NBSL)

    www.beachsoccer.com.sg

    VENUE: Sentosa, Tanjong Beach
    DATE: Kicks-off 25 June 2006

    Each team will play 12 minutes each quarter with a total of 36 minutes per match for 7 consecutively weeks to determine the champion for the season.

    The criteria for teams selected to play in the various divisions will depend on the team composition. Teams will be evaluated by NBSL officials and award their playing status accordingly.

    Winning teams in each division will stand a chance to win prizes up to $2000 cash and be selected to play in the International Brazilian Beach Soccer event in December 2006.

    Each team will play a total of 7 games in a division with a participation fee of $80 per game/ team. Teams can register up to 10 players with a minimum of 7. Interested teams can register before the 16th of June 2006 via email: feza@theRcard.com or fax: 6438 0031.
