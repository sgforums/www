    Hi everybody! Are you tired from work, and wishing you were on an adventure, or doing something fun? Come down to Asia Xpedition 2016, 15 to 17 Apr 2016, and get started on your adventure!

    If you dare, you can try out our rock-climbing wall after the competitions, or simply participate in our exciting daily lucky draws.

    If you have a crazy travel experience, share it with us in our pre-event Vlog or Travel Writing contests! Simply submit a 30sec video to us or write between 250 and 300 words, and stand a chance to win attractive prizes!
