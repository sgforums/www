    well i havent made it to the run at the reservoir on Sunday yet -
    something to do with my saturday nights i guess but i did find a couple
    of groups that do run regularly so i thought that i`d share what i found:

    1) Run 246 meets Tues and Thurs at 6:30 out the front of Caltex House at Raffles Place in the city. I found this through:

    sportskaki.com

    and clicked under their runners section. there you`ll see the banner advertising this group. Its mixed ability running/walking either 3 or 5 km up along the esplanade. Let by a trainer the group has warm ups and warm down sessions. FREE

    2) House Harriers see for example: www.pachome1.pacific.net.sg/~ken/lchhh.html

    This group meets on different nights usually at 6pm (bit early waaaa) and sometimes on weekends at different locations each week all across SG. Usually 5-10km with slow joggers/walkers together with some very fast runners. Something like orienteering. Seems to be a mix of jungle and road running with beer drinking fun afterwards. $10-20 depending on whether u drink beer or not.

    Please let me know any others.
