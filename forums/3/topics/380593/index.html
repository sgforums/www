    Basketball Action 2010

    Since 2005, BBAXN basketball leagues have changed the face of Singapore Basketball. All teams are provided a well organized amateur basketball leagues all year round. From Basketball leagues, Cup tournaments, and the BBAXN All Star Team, BBAXN now manages over 35 basketball teams, with hundreds of players (both expatriates and locals) representing over 20 different nationalities. Our plans for 2010 are well underway and we expect the league to grow even stronger.

    BBAXN is conducting player trails for TWO new basketball teams…

    In line with our objective to revolutionize amateur basketball in Singapore, BBAXN is managing the formation of TWO Men’s Basketball teams, to participate in the BBAXN Men’s Weekend League Season 10. Using the BBAXN “Player Looking for a Team” player list in the website, BBAXN is looking to put together like minded individuals who all enjoy the beautiful sport of Basketball, and to assist these players in building their own team. BBAXN has a rich history of forming clubs whether it be at the Men, Women or Junior Level.

    BBAXN will host TWO basketball training sessions in October and November wherein players will meet potential team mates and start organizing the team.
