    Officially they are known as Compressed air, Pre-compressed, or CO2 types.

    The ones with detatchable cylinders that can be charged for several shots are precompressed. Compressed air guns have a lever to "pump" for every shot. CO2 is similar to precompressed, but charged with CO2 gas instead of normal air.
