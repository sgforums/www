    Hi guys, I chanced upon this forum via Google search.

     

    There is a newly opened adventure centre located at Pengerang, Malaysia. The journey is 45 mins bumboat ride from Changi Point Ferry Terminal in Singapore. They offer outdoor activities such as dirtbiking and pitbiking, wakeboarding, mountain biking and ATV rides. =)

     

    Training is provided for dirtbiking and wakeboarding activities. For wakeboarding, you only have to pay for the time spent on riding. Travelling time from the park to the riding spot is not counted. Riders only pay for the actual riding time. While cycling from Pengerang to Sungai Rengit, you can enjoy the nice greenery and cheap and fresh seafood in Sungai Rengit.

     

    Come join in the fun and see ya guys there~~
