    SMUX Volar! 2007 is an adventure carnival organised by SMUXtremists, the outdoor adventure club of SMU. This event aims to promote outdoor adventure activities to the SMU community, youths and the public, by providing them with an avenue to try out these activities.

    Come join in the exciting activities such as cable-skiing, tyrolean traverse, rappelling, banana boat, underwater hockey, skating, biking, Adventure Race and many more!

    Event: SMUX Volar! 2007
    Date: 15 April 2007 (Sun)
    Time: 9am to 5pm
    Venue: East Coast Lagoon, East Coast Park (nearest carpark at E1)

    Ticket: $8 (includes goodie bag, free singlet, chance to win fabulous prizes at our grand lucky draw and coupons to participate in the activities) No tickets required to enter site.
