What happened to Roger Federer

  • Darkknighthuang

    For the past 4-5 years, Roger has been so dominating and invincible in his game. Beginning of this year, he started to show signs that he is vulnerable, lost to some unfancied players, lost on his favourite surface and even lost his no. 1 ranking. Most recently, he lost to G. Simon in the ATP masters cup in Shanghai. I saw that match and can't help thinking that he is past his best and may decide to retire within 1-2 years. I can think of a few likely reasons for his mediocre performance this year:

    1. His illness early this year.

    2. Too much competitive tennis.

    3. He has lost his passion for the game.

    4. The competition have read his game and are getting better.

    Any others?

     

    Anyway, in my opinion, he is still the greatest player of all time and I admire him for his off-court image too.
