    Reasons for other leagues being second favourites among majority of the Singaporeans:

    - Not clear enough transmission
    - Boring commentators
    - Late night games
    - Crowds from some other leagues arent as vocal as the english supporters. Sometime its the singing and chanting from the english fans that makes the game more exciting than it really is.
    - Too many EPL matches being broadcast
    - Player names and club names easier to pronouce
    - Lack of exposure to other leagues right from the start
    - Peer influences, your friends all like EPL teams, you like other teams not much to talk about.

    But to sum it all up, EPL being the most popular league doesnt mean they are the best league in terms of quality. I felt people should broaden their football horizon.

    Well just my humble opinion.
