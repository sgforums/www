Cold Storage Singapore International Triathlon 2013

  • Triathlon Singapore
    9 Jul 13, 15:59

    Date: 28 & 29 September 2013

    Website: www.triathlon.sg

    Sign up now for the only International Triathlon event in Singapore! Sanctioned by the International Triathlon Union (ITU) & Proudly brought to you by Cold Storage!

    Cold Storage is proud to reassume its role as title sponsor of the biggest and most prestigious triathlon in Singapore which aspires to encourage the community and families to live better through sports.

     

    In line with Cold Storage philosophy, the Cold Storage Singapore Triathlon is her further involvement after the highly popular Cold Storage Kids Run. This triathlon caters to the whole community, offering a wide spectrum of race categories and distances to cater to individuals and teams with different proficiencies and targets. The aim is to encourage everybody to try – whether you are a seasoned triathlete or simply want to enjoy family bonding through sports and exercise.    

     

    This year, the Cold Storage Singapore International Triathlon, organized by the Triathlon Association of Singapore, will regain its international Sanctioning from ITU and ASTC for 2013. The installment will comprise three main race highlights, plus an all-new ‘Family Relay Challenge’ category where families can compete for the honour of ‘Best Tri-athlete Family’, or simply embrace the exciting challenge of a triathlon together.

     

    2013 ITU Triathlon Asian Cup: Are you up to the rigours of the only international triathlon event in Singapore? Will you wear your nation’s colours with pride? As part of the 2013 ITU Triathlon Asian Cup Series, the Cold Storage Singapore International Triathlon will see world class athletes from Singapore and the region competing in the Elite and Junior Elite Categories, not only to gain points for their ITU World ranking, but also to win money amounting to USD $10,000. Come down to support our national tri-athletes as they compete with the best tri-athletes in the region!

     

    National Triathlon Championships: Are you the best tri-athlete at national level? Challenge yourself with the best in Singapore in the National Triathlon Championships Category today! Mingle with the best age-groupers in Singapore, in the Junior Sprint Category (16 to 19 Years) or the Standard Category (18 years and above). Stand a chance to be lauded as the best tri-athlete in Singapore!    

     

    Tri-Club Challenge (Open for all Schools, Clubs, Corporate Companies, Statutory Boards etc.): Do you have a triathlon team in your organization? Want to create a sporting culture in your organization? Join in the Inter-Club Challenge! Sign up as a team in the individual or relay categories, and share a competitive moment with your colleagues and mates! A token of recognition will be given to the 5 organizations with the most number of participants in this category. Stand a chance to be crowned the “Tri-Club” champion of Singapore!

     

    (NEW!) Family Fun Relay: The family that exercises together, stays together. Embark on a regime of health and self-improvement as you spend quality time with your loved ones by participating in the Family Fun Relay! Race clinics and training sessions will be organized to ensure asafe and enjoyable experience for all families.

     

    Cold Storage is also proud to support VISION 2030. Live better by training for the Singapore International Triathlon proudly supported by the SINGAPORE SPORTS COUNCIL. Embody VISION 2030 with Cold Storage by living better through sports.
