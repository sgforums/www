    Arsenal captain Thierry Henry is ready to pull a prank on manager Arsene Wenger to celebrate his ten years at the club.

    Wenger completes a decade with the Gunners next week and Henry said: "Just winning our next game would be enough to make him happy but we need to do something special to mark the occasion.

    "So maybe we'll put him under the shower after the game!

    "Thankfully, I don't think Arsene can live without football. It's his life and it would be very difficult for him to stop.

    "To be a great boss you need to be able to manage people - that's what makes Arsene so special.

    "Dealing with me for all these years is enough of a pain for him but he has to deal with all those other characters in the dressing room. He always manages to stay calm and get the right reaction and I really admire that about him."

    Henry was speaking at the launch of the Willow Foundation as Arsenal's official charity of the year.
