    hi everyone! Kampong Chai Chee Community Centre's Youth Executive Committee(YEC) is organising their annual Leisure Nite Cycling event yet again! So, every bikers do join us is u can make it ok?

    Date of Event: May12 2006 (It's Vesak Day and it's a Friday so people can definately rest on the upcoming weekend!)

    Reporting time: 9pm at Kampong Chai Chee CC! The CC itself would be both the starting and the ending points!

    End time: As from previous years' experience, it might end at around 5.30am the next morning.

    Fees: Members $10 , Non-Members $15 (Bike Rental is also available @ $8!)

    Route: We will start at CC as mentioned earlier, then to east coast park, up to changi and all the way back to the CC. Estimated 9-12 rest points will be set. Many attrations could be seen along the way too!

    Distance: Estimated 40-70km.

    Where to obtain Registration forms? Interested individuals might wanna go to Kampong Chai Chee CC office to get one!

    Closing Date: 28 April 2006

    For further enquires, please call Kampong Chai Chee CC at 62419878.

    See u there! =)
