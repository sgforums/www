  • Deportivo
    23 Jun 07, 19:14
    You'd be silly looking for a Henry replacement because their quite clearly isn't one.

    My 2cents is that Henry leaving and the timing is perfect for you to kick on. Im talking about Henry's time being up at Arsenal and the pretty obvious evolving of your football over the last 2 yrs.

    Henry is a counter attacking teams dream. His pace, ability to beat not just one player but 2-3 players with absolute ease, his finishing and intelligence in his game made him stand out to be your talisman and undoubtedly one of the best players on the planet over 2-3yr time span.

    But your game has changed, gone are the days of counter attacking and scoring goals within 15seconds of the oppositions corner. You dont have the pace in the side any more and as good as Cescs passing is it's not a patch on Bergkamps.

    You've become a more patient European styled football team, who'll keep possession and control the game whilst always looking for those clever triangles and through balls. What you'll now get IMO is a more conventional striker, someone who'll play on the shoulder ala Crespo making clever runs across the pitch etc. Henry just doesn't fit that mould of player.

    You'll be stronger next season no doubt but not because of any supposed galvanising effect on the team but more because you'll have the right player who'll be perfectly suited to the new style of play.

    Something that you gunners have to worry about is whether Arsene is staying.
