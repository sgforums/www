Trikking

    Just been to East Coast Park recently and I saw quite a number of pple on a new 3 wheel bike. (managed to find a picture)

    it looks like fun.... and it was something fresher than the usual cycling.

    so I went a head to try it. It looks simple but I was struggling for about 15 mins.

    The shop owner Andrew showed me some tips and once I got the momentum. I was off alone on this 3 wheel vehicle.  

    The ride was really fun and comfortable. I usually get saddle sore from riding a bicycle.

    I traveled a distant from shop (next to East Coast Burger King) to just before the Seafood center, then the shop owner Andrew chased up to me to see how I was doing. He wanted to make sure I have no problems with the "Carving Vehicle" and that I enjoyed the ride. The ride feels like skiing on the streets.

    the movement looks simple and relax but after the 1 hour ride I could feel my muscles. Andrew said this was a low impact full body workout equipment and I really feel the muscle ache this morning.

    It was a damn good workout ! i think I will go back for more.

    Thought of getting 1 unit for myself but it is a little pricey. Then again sames goes to good bicycles.

    Rental is going for $8 an hour onwards. I will need to rent about 100 hours to get myself a good model.

    hmmm...  maybe I will wait for my year end bonus. '

    for those khakis who wished to give it a try..... the Shop name is My 3 Wheels

    The shop is a very small shop space within the Bicycle Kiosk next to East Coast Burger King (Near East Coast Mcdonalds)
