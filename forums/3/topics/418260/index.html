    Do you recall the first time you raced? All those long hours spent on practice? The trials and tribulations you overcame? Do you have a truly inspiring sporting story to tell? If so, you might just be the person I’m looking for.

    Inspiring Singapore

    Hi, I’m Daniel Swee, a freelance Sports Photographer based right here in Singapore. Having been heavily involved in shooting sports, I have been a witness countless of times to the triumph of the human spirit over the conditions. Now, I would like to turn my attention to the many stories of athletes.

     

    I’m cordially inviting all athletes with a tale to tell to step forward and share your story with me. It doesn’t matter if you’re 12 or 62, kick a ball or swing a racquet, I want to know the story behind your perseverance, motivation and inspiration.

     

    Selected athletes would be invited to share more of their journey in sports with me and also attend a photo session by me, the photos and stories acquired would be featured in my promotional collateral. Athletes would be able to keep the selected images for personal usage only.

     

    Deadline for submitted entries would be the 26th of December.

     

    So if you would want to share your story, head down over to my blog at http://hooky3659.wordpress.com/inspiring-singapore/ to view the terms and conditions and submit your story.
