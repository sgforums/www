    Singapore ref for T&T's World Cup debut
    Sunday, June 4th 2006

    A referee from Singapore will officiate when the Trinidad and Tobago Soca Warriors take to the field in their first game against Sweden at the 2006 World Cup in Dortmund, Germany on June 10.

    Shamsul Maidin of Singapore will carry the whistle for T&T's debut game.

    Prachya Permpanich from Thailand and Eisa Ghuloum of the United Arab Emirates (UAE) will serve as assistant referees for the match, while Oscar Ruiz from Colombia and Fernando Tamayo of Ecuador have been named the fourth and fifth officials, respectively.
