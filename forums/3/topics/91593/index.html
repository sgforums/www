    I think it needs to be in a central location but not necessary to be near any particular place such as CC or NSC etc Why? Bec whether its school or CC or whatever, the number of times that particular customer will order from you is limited to a few times per year at most. So unless there is high repeat orders, it makes no business sense to locate your business near any particular customer unless rent there super cheap etc. Also if it is bulk sales and not 1 off walk in customers, then the location again is not so critical. Why? Bec people looking to buy in bulk will be willing to travel some distance to obtain it bec of the quality/design/price advantage. But if its a 1 off for walk in customers, then location becomes important... then it willhave to be somewhere where there is high pedestrian traffic of the right mix who will be interested in your product.
