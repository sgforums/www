    Singapore junior table tennis team led by Isabelle won 6 gold, 2 silver and 7 bronze medals at the 16th South East Asian Junior Table Tennis Championship.

    All 12 players are locally born and bred.

    Isabelle is ranked 17th in the world in the women's under 18 category.

    She won the gold medals in the girl's singles (under 18), girls' team event (under 18) and the mixed doubles (under 18) and a silver medal in the girls' double (under 18).

    Congratulations.
