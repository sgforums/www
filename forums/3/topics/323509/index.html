"Let Me Run With You" Event held on 20th July

  • ~Vincent~
    8 Jul 08, 16:08

     

    Hi,

    I am representing Asia Pacific Volunteering Group from Barclays.

     

    This year, Barclays will be partnering with Special Olympics Singapore to organise a relay marathon & Sports Carnival at Bishan area.

     

    Special Olympics is a a non-profit organization dedicated to providing sports training and competitions for persons with intellectual disabilities, giving them opportunities to develop physical fitness, self-esteem and confidence, and become productive and respected members of society. The objectives of this event are to fund-raise for their training cost annually and create awareness. Below is the event information:-

     

    Event: Let Me Run With You

    Date: 20th July (Sunday)

    Event Type: Relay Marathon & Sports Carnival

    Location: Bishan Park II & Raffles Instituiton

    GOH: Minister Teo Chee Hean

    Website:

     

     

    http://www.specialolympics.org.sg/letmerunwithyou/main.html

     

    We are hoping for more participants especially if your family or colleagues would like to join in the fun.

     

    Feel free to contact me through

     

    vincent.saw@barcap.com if you are interested in joining.

     

    Regards,

    Vincent
