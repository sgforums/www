    how much? - tao rai
    cost/price - raka
    how much is the cost/price? raka tao rai
    can lower the cost/price? - lot raka mai
    expensive - paeng bai
    expensive or not? - paeng mai
    thanks - khob khun khrab/kha or *kob jai na khrab / kha
    where got sell Thai food? - mee arhan Thai kai tii nai (<--word by word: have food Thai sell where)
    have - mee
    don't have - mai mee
    sell - kai
    at where? - tii nai
    can I have? - gor.... mai
    can I have a pencil? - gor dinso mai
    can I have the English newspaper? - gor nam seu pim pasa Angrit mai
    cannot/don't want - mai dai
    *cannot/don't want lah - mai dai na khrab / kha
    yes - chai/khrab (m) /kha (f)
    yes "liao" - chai laew
    no - plao
    what are you doing? - tam arai you
    too big - yai bai
    too small - lek bai
    bigger - bai gwar
    smaller - lek gwar
    don't know - mai roo
    know or not? - roo mai
    *know or not har? - roo mai na khrab/kha
    know or not where is the Chatuchak market? - Roo mai wa talad chatuchak you tii nai na khrab/kha
    don't understand - mai kao chai
    understand - chai
    understand or not? - chai mai
    *understand or not har? - chai mai na khrab/kha
    you(f) - ter
    you(m and respectful) - khun
    i(f) - chan / dii chan
    i(m) - pom
    he said that - kao bok wa
    him/her - kao
    said - bok
    said that - bok wa
    speak - poot
    Can't speak Thai - poot Thai mai dai
    *Can't speak Thai de wor - poot Thai mai dai na khrab / kha
    correct - took dong
    corect liao - took dong laew
    wrong - pit
    wrong liao - pit laew
    and then? - laew kor
    we - rao
    photograph - roop
    have photograph? - mee roop mai
    milk - som
    water - nam
    orange juice - som
    beer - biar
    coke - "cork"
    pepsi - pepsi
    sprite - sa pite (read "3 times" in Hokkien)
    fanta - fanta

    colour - see
    white - see kao
    red - see daeng
    yellow - see leuang
    orange - see som
    blue - see nam ngern
    light blue - fa
    green - see kiaw
    black - see darm
    brown - see nam tan
    grey - see tao
    purple - see muang
    gold - see tong
    silver - see ngern
    pink - see chom poo
    light - aorn
    dark - gae
    light pink - see chom poo aorn
    dark pink - see chom poo gae
