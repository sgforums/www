    What I will take note :
    1- It's NOT ONLY on local market, not how many countries it has gone into as it'll means that there are lesser overseas opportunities. Market saturation?
    2- How huge is the market? Are you bonded by some hidden rules?
    If you want to go to an undeveloped country, are you allow to do it?

    5- Boss's mindset.
    6- Comparing on similar products, How unique and competitive? Or there are too many options?
