    Preparing For A Part Time Degree Course in Singapore

    1) Do Your Research: http://www.cpe.edu.sg provides a list of recognized Private Education Institutes in Singapore.

    2) Select Institutes of good record and with good academic partners: A 4-Year Edutrust Certified Institute such as SMF Institute of Higher Learning will be a good choice, as it has met the stringent requirement of the Council of Private Education.

    3) Select a UK or Australian University qualification. The curriculum is more easily "digestable" for Singaporeans used to British Education System such as GCE.

    4) Universities such as Monash University, Murdoch University, University of South Australia, University of London or University of Bradford are good choices.

    5) Find a study buddy and get support from your friends! It is not going to be easy to exercise self-discipline to attend. Getting friends or family members to push you may help.

    6) Ask questions. The only way to learn FAST is to ask questions in class; the other way is to read the whole chapter at home by yourself!

    7) For every hour of lecture, allocate 3 hours to read and practise. Allocate at least one hour a day after work for your assignments.

    8) Stay focus and always enjoy the learning experience.
