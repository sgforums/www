    for maths i think most impt is practice... so give the students exercises to do, but dun overwork them lah..

    then after that go thru the questions with them, make sure they understand how to derive the answer.... ask them to explain how they got a certain answer...

    then make them re-do some of the questions again, esp the harder ones that they got wrong... cos from experience after u explain and u ask if they understand, they will say yes... but usually won't know how to do it all over again by themselves...

    for the weaker students i think it's impt to sit by them while they do their exercises, and prompt them when necessary...
