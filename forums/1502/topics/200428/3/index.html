    1. Stadium Boulevard
    2. Stadium Walk
    3. Stadium Road
    4. Stadium Crescent
    5. Stadium Drive
    6. Stadium Link
    7. Jalan Benaan Kepal
    8. Geylang Drive

    1. Mountbatten Road
    2. Nicoll Highway

    Access to the road closure area will only be granted to police, military, emergency and labelled vehicles.
