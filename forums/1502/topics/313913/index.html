    Youth Eco Concert 2008

    Join us at the Clean & Green Singapore Youth Eco Concert 2008! Singapore's only live concert organised with our environment in mind, the event will be made carbon-neutral as we plant tree saplings to offset the carbon emissions and promote the use of recycling bins along Orchard. Let your favourite stars: Asian Idol Hady Mirza, Anugerah winner Aliff Aziz, Vasantham Star winner Ebi Shankara, Kelly Poon and Daren Tan of Project Superstar fame as well as Shawn Tok, male champion, Campus Superstar Season 2, share with you how a little effort can make a big difference to our environment.

    With youth and enthusiasm on their side, these artistes will show you why they and each of us have the ability to make a difference. They will share how we can do our bit to protect and sustain the environment. So join in the fun and party, do your part and make a difference on :

    Date: 19 Apr 2008 (Saturday)
    Time: 6pm to 9pm
    Venue: Ngee Ann City Civic Plaza (nearest MRT station: Orchard)

    Entry is free, simply bring a recyclable (e.g. cans, plastic bottles) or make a pledge at the entrance. Choose to take public transport (MRT or bus) to Ngee Ann City Civic Plaza outside Takashimaya Department Store - and take your first step to befriending our earth.
