    Who moved my soap by Andy Borowitz

    This book is essentially a guide written by ex-Ceo about surviving in U.S prisons and adapting to life inside. A light-hearted view and very often hilarious. One of those books that gets u into fits of laughter with every page u turn. Included is a list of must-haves in the prison, prison slang, the art of picking up soaps and others.

    One can easily finish this rather thin book within a week. Thus, i will recommend everyone to grab this book in any bookstore and read it during the lunch hour. Probably can finish it within a week if u are a quick reader.
