Change in You

  • Hocklez
    24 Jul 04, 17:38
    I noticed something this week

    Never thought it was big deal

    Place the thing behind my mind

    Never had this cross my mind

    I know your faith and trust in

    And now u gave up

    Started your second

    That is a shock

    Hope, you are still the one

    I have always known

    Unknown, yes I am

    Puzzled, yes u should

    Question in my mind

    Answer me

    To show my existance

    I am always who i am

    To who u are
