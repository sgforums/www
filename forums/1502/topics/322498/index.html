    • Format of video: .mp4, .wmv, .avi. QuickTime.

      School of Digital Media and Infocomm Technology
      Singapore Polytechnic
      500 Dover Road
      Singapore 139651
      Attn: Video Competition
    • Submit video by: 18 July 2008 4:30 p.m..

    • The winner's school will be notified of the result.
    • Prize-award ceremony will be held at SP on 13 Sep 2008.
