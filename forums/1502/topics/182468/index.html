    In line with characteristics of people in this age group, UNCUT is differentiated by three distinct elements - Fresh, Young and Raw.

    Before the telecast of UNCUT, the search is now on for the best logo design.

    Requirements

    This competition is open to all students of Nanyang, Temasek, Ngee Ann, Republic and Singapore Polytechnics.

    Students are to submit a creative logo design for UNCUT which will be launched in April 2006. This logo will be used in all official print, video, web and promotional materials for UNCUT and will also be included in ALL interstitials submitted to MediaCorp TV for broadcast.

    Submission Date: March 8 to April 8, 2006
    Format: CD (jpeg, tiff or psd format)

    CDs containing the logo designs are to be sent to:

    Channel U Promotions
    Caldecott Broadcast Centre
    Andrew Road
    Singapore 299939

    Please include your name, NRIC and contact numbers in a separate file in the CD.

    * All CD entries are non-returnable hence please ensure that you keep a personal copy of your original design.

    The winners stand to win more than $1000 worth of prizes that include the stylish Canon Ixus i zoom, Apple iPod Nano and iPod Shuffle!
