    Alright lah hahaha.

    I'm not that good with Chinatown, but I could help a little by telling you the nice things I've tried.

    Famous food... I know of this stall selling roast meat (they sell no chicken!) and some dim sum items (such as char siew buns, yam balls, chicken wings, lor mai kai etc). It's called "Dao Ji", as in, the dao in my signature and ji4 de2 (remember)'s ji4. It's well-known for its roast duck rice. Personally I like the chicken wings. It's located behind People Park's Complex, more specifically, behind OG.
  • MooKu
    7 Sep 04, 12:04
    There is also a HUGE food centre in some gray building. I'm not sure what the name of that building is, but if you stand opposite Lucky Chinatown, it's to the right of Lucky Chinatown, behind a bus stop. There is a CK Department Store there.

    The food centre is very big - I keep getting lost in it. But they do have good food that's also cheap at the same time.

    My favorite stall is Er Gu's dessert stall. The stall sells yummy almond/peanut/sesame paste that costs $1.60 per bowl. It's located around tables #200 something. The stall faces a wall.

    edit: It's called NEW BRIDGE CENTRE.
