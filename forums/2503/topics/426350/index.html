    I see the Thai fever rising after Thailand got talent contestant Bell video made wave around Singapore and in Facebook. So maybe there are people out interested to learn. So here are the basics: Hello/goodmorning/good afternoon/bye bye -- sa wat dii What are you doing? -- Tham arai yu na Watching tv -- du ti wi yu Talking on the phone -- kui Toh ra sap yu Where are you? -- yuu tii nai na What time? -- gi morng na How much? -- thao rai na Have you eaten? -- Kin kao rue yang Not yet eaten -- yang mai kin No chance to eat -- yang Mai dai kin Eaten -- kin leaw Very full -- im mak Very hungry -- hiu mak What did you eat? -- kin kao gap arai na Just add 'khap' to the back if you are a guy and 'ka' at the back if you're a lady for politeness. Remember it is very important to be polite very meeting new people, so don't forget to speak politely too by adding khap or ka.
