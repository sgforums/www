  • AndrewPKYap
    13 Jun 08, 03:52

     

     

    RCA - is very far from the MRT stations, and is much much nearer to Param 9/Thai cultural Center stations.

     

    Ratchadaphisek station is already near to Lat Phrao - very far away from RCA

     

    ==============

     

     

    Rachada is short for Thanon ("Thanon" is "road" in Thai, "Soi" is "lane") Ratchadaphisek

     

     

    ===============

     

     


    Don't go to Pat Pong, if you want to visit a-go-go bars, go to Soi Cowboy (Soi 23, Sukhumvit, between BTS (skytrain) stations Asoke and Phrom Phong or Nana Plaza (Soi 4, Sukhumvit, BTS (skytrain) station Nana.)

     

    But to get to Pat Pong, you stop at MRT Silom  station or BTS Sala Daeng (interchange at Siam station, change to BTS line going towards Saphan Thaksin Station, if you are on the Sukhumvit line)

     

    ==================

     

    Yes the bars close early since Thaksin came to power and that is why he had to be kicked out - he was becoming like LKY, intolerable)

     

    ==================

     

    Chinatown is not "near" Yaowarat Road... Yaowarat Road and trhe surrounding roads make up Chinatown

     

    ==================

     

    To watch football, you should go to Gulliver's Tavern, Sukhumvit Soi 5 Sukhumvit, BTS (skytrain) station Nana.) It is about 5 minutes walk, on the right from the BTS station along Soi 5

     

    ==================

     

    as for sightseeing, there is a solid gold Buddha at the temple just outside the main Chinatown area...

     

     

    Wat Traimit  stop at MRT Sam Yan station
