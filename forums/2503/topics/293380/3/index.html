    Part of the reasons I love BKK is cos of the food there hor...

    You haven't tried Baan Khun Mae right? Should go and try it, esp the steamed snapper dish. Their service is excellent as well.

    Another recommendation is the Tom Yum Goong in the Coffee Sky Restaurant in Baiyoke Sky Hotel (it is located on the 18th floor). But you have to ask them to make it a little bit spicy. Cos the first time we had it we asked for that, and it tasted awesome. Best Tom Yum I have ever had. Their Som Tham was excellent too. But the last day we went there again to order the Tom Yum Goong and this time it was really mild and it didn't taste as nice as the one we had the first time. So remember to ask for the spiciness if you are ordering this dish.
