    Pattaya Beach got topless Caucasian women in bikini....

    The Pattaya Tiffany show is world famous...

    The beach according to Singapore standards is very clean but for Thai standards is very dirty...

    If you can, go to Seacon square (far away from down town Bangkok but was once the biggest mall in SEA (before Mid Valley Mall in KL took over the title). Best part is tourist don't know and so the prices are aimed at locals...

    You can go to Chaktuchak Weekend market by both BTS (Skytrain) and MRT (Underground) but MRT stops inside the market (stop at Kampang Phek station, not Chaktuchal park station..)... BTS stop at Mo Chit terminal station...

    spelling not accurate... see BTS and MRT maps online....
