    Tyre Compound

    Since the introduction of a sole-tyre suplier, the FIA has introduced rule for Bridgestone to supply two different compounds during each weekend. In the last two races, you might have seen white strip on tyres. This denotes the softer compound.

    Spain: medium, hard
    Monaco: super soft, soft
    Canada: super soft, soft
    USA: soft, medium
    France: soft, medium
    Britain: medium, hard
