    Racing drivers start out by racing in go-karts when they are as young as 5 years old.

    With luck, once they prove successful in a senior kart championship, they get hired by a team from one of the junior motor-racing formulae (e.g. Formula Ford, Formula Renault, AF2000)

    The competition is very stiff at every level, you always need luck and you may end up racing in another top series like ChampCars, the German Touring Car Championships (DTM), the FIA GT Championship or A1 Grand Prix.
