    They're likely to get Mercedes powertrains next season (engine, gearbox, KERS).but the announcement will only be made on Monday. Mike Gascoyne has left the team along with Colin Kolles in a "reshuffle" however. There goes one experienced and highly-rated designer.

    Interestingly there appear to be no rookies for next season so far. Contesting for the remaining slots at STR and Honda are Senna, Di Grassi, Bourdais, Sato, Button (touted to stay) and Barrichello.

    I haven't read anything about the new track but given the current economic climate, I don't think it'll be finished that early, if ever.
