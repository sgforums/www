    Venue: Istabul Motor Park
    Laps: 58
    Race start: 8 p.m. Singapore time

    The Turkish Grand Prix was an instant hit with the drivers and fans when it was introduced in 2005. There have been only two winners here - Kimi Raikkonen (2005) and Felipe Massa (2006 - 2008).

    Technical: Fast and flowing with many long high-speed corners, including the 4-apex Turn 8. Aerodynamic efficiency is key to setting a good lap time. Some of them are blind, due to the many elevation changes, and bravery helps. Soft and hard compound tyres will be used this weekend.

    Going into the weekend: With all the current teams signed up for 2010 at least conditionally, the focus returns to the track. Can anyone really stop Jenson Button and Brawn? Ferrari and Massa will be looking to use their recent upturn in form to defend their victory. Red Bull have said that a win here is key to their championship hopes, and they have a double diffuser to aid them. BMW-Sauber are the last team to introduce their trick diffuser and they have brought it to Istanbul.
