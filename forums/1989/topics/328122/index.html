  • Kuali Baba

    Venue: Valencia street circuit, Spain
    Circuit length: 5.440 km
    Laps: 57
    Race start: 2000 HRS SIN

    The European Grand Prix returns to Spain after a 10-year spell at the Nuerburgring, at the first new track since the 2005 Turkish Grand Prix. Thus it is an unknown quantity. Downforce settings estimated through simulations are expected to be similar to Melbourne and Hockenheim. However it is wider than Monaco and like Singapore, it features a water crossing. Hot weather is predicted for the weekend.

    Before the summer break, Kimi Raikkonen narrowed the gap to championship leader Lewis Hamilton to 5 points. Felipe Massa is another 3 points adrift. A minute of silence is expected to be observed before the start of today's opening practice session. Be sure though to get familiar with the track tomorrow during qualifying and also on StarSports Raceday on Sunday!
