    Here are the notable changes:

      - The Australian race will take place later in the afternoon
      - Turkey's event is moved from August to May
      - Valencia becomes the new European Grand Prix venue, taking Turkey's spot in August
      - No United States Grand Prix, while the French leg has been reinstated
      - Suzuka does not return as speculated
      - Hockenheim takes Nurburgring's place as the venue of the German Grand Prix
      - Singapore and Valencia's dates are subject to final approval once the respective tracks are complete
