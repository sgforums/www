    Teams

    Jordan will be rebranded as Midland F1 team

    Minardi F1 will be taken over by Red Bull Racing, and renamed Squadra Toro Rosso

    Team Sauber will be taken over by BMW

    BAR will be fully owned by Honda

    Williams will use Cosworth engines

    Super Aguri, headed by ex-F1 driver Aguri Suzuki, planned to join the grid using Honda engines and Bridgestone tyres. However they have missed the deadline to pay the US$48m bond. They do not appear on next year entry list.

    Tyres

    Toyota and Williams switch to Bridgestone tyres

    Tyre changes in race will be allowed
