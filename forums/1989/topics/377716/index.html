    Suzuka returns to the calendar after a 3-year absence, where Fuji Speedway took its place. Almost half of the drivers on the grid have never driven on it before, including Hamilton, Kovalainen, Sutil, Vettel, Grosjean, Buemi, Alguersuari, Nakajima and Glock. It is famous for its narrow, technically demanding and dangerous nature - the wrong racing line or the smallest mistake can result in a very slow lap time.
