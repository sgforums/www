  • Kuali Baba

    Track: Istabul Park, Istanbul, Turkey
    Lap count: 58
    Race start: 1500 hrs local time (2000 hrs SIN)

    Istanbul was introduced to the calendar in 2005. F1 fans do not normally take to new
    circuits well but this has been a hit with them, as it has a fast and generally flowing
    nature and it features severe elevation changes throughout.

    This is the first of three races run anti-clockwise in the 2008 season, as the event has
    been moved from its previous late August spot. Medium levels of downforce will be
    used, and overtaking opportunities abound at the first corner and the hairpin at the end
    of the back straight. Another corner to look out for is the 4-apex Turn 8, which is one of
    the most breathtaking on the calendar.

    There have been only 2 winners at this event (Massa and Raikkonen), and both of
    them are now driving for Ferrari. The scarlet cars are widely expected to dominate
    once again. At the other end of the grid, Super Aguri has withdrawn from the circus,
    leaving just 20 cars on the starting grid for the first time since 2005.
