    Venue: Silverstone Circuit, England
    Laps: 60
    Race start: 8pm Singapore time

    This is the last British Grand Prix that will be held at Silverstone as Donington Park, previously used in 1993 for the European GP, becomes the new host. Silverstone was created from a former airfield and hosted the first-ever round of the F1 world championship. Ironically,.almost 60 years later, it's also the venue of the announcement of a breakaway series that could change the look of the sport forever.

    The open nature of the track makes it susceptible to strong winds which can change a car's handling from lap to lap. A stable car that can avoid fluctuations in aerodynamic grip in such conditions is crucial. Long straights, fast curves such as Copse, Stowe (where Michael Schumacher broke his leg 10 years ago) and Becketts dominate the course, punctuated by a few fiddly complexes like Luffield and Club. The race can be done on a single pitstop but two pitstops will be the norm on Sunday.
