    N.B. This thread is for news related to the race and the practice sessions before that. Other banter goes into the existing thread.

    Track: Marina Bay street circuit
    Laps: 61
    Race start: 2000 HRS SIN

    This is the second new race venue on the 2008 calendar, also a street circuit but the only one that is run anti-clockwise (against the flow of normal traffic). It is also the first ever F1 race to be held after sunset.

    It comprises of 23 mostly-sharp turns that punctuate short straights. This means only Monaco is slower, and if it rains, the 2-hour limit may elapse. Overtaking will likely be very difficult, with the best chances coming at the end of the pit straight and outside the Esplanade Theatres.

    Bridgestone have brought the 2 softest compounds to tackle the low-grip conditions. Off the racing line, leaf litter and rubber marbles will compound the difficulty of passing unless it rains before or during the race. A high downforce set-up similar to Monaco and Australia will be used, and the brakes will take a huge battering due to the frequency of usage.

    McLaren's appeal to reinstate Lewis Hamilton's race win was deemed inadmissable as it given in-lieu of a drive-through penalty, which cannot be protested. Therefore, he goes into the weekend with a single-point gap over Ferrari's Felipe Massa. BMW's Robert Kubica is a further 13 points adrift in 3rd

    Qualifying begins at 10pm on Saturday and the race at 8pm on Sunday. Enjoy the 2 hours of capturing the attention of the world while it lasts!
