    The Dubai Autodrome & Business park is the first fully integrated automotive and motor sports facility in the Middle East region, incorporating a world class FIA sanctioned 5.39 km motor racing track, international standard pit lane complex with a "state-of-the-art" grandstand accommodating over 7,000 spectators as well as providing modern hospitality suites for corporate customers.
    Providing a "real home" for the automotive industry in the Gulf with a comprehensive business park being developed around the racetrack. Modern units have been designed to provide offices, workshops, showrooms, warehouses and conference facilities for all types of activities in the automotive and associated industries creating a cohesive working environment from which a motor racing culture will develop and prosper. An international and local racing calendar will be established on the Autodrome to include Single Seater Racing, Touring Cars, Motor Bikes, Sports Cars and Karting.

    The design philosophy for the track on the project led by Edara and HOK, when given the luxury of a clean sheet approach by the client, has been to coordinate the thoughts of Union Properties, racing car drivers, motorcycle racing riders, motor sport teams and the governing bodies of motor sport, the FIA and FIM, into a sequence of corners and straights over undulating ground that should satisfy all levels of motor sport whilst also providing a memorable spectator environment.
