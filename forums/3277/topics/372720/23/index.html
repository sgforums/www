    One late night, the eerie clouds were hovering over the ominous sky and the crows were making yakking noises near the alley. Mr Chips, a mortician, also a detective was on his way to collect a new corpse.

    Upon reaching one of the houses where the dead body was, Mr Chips went to talk to the dead man's family.

    "He's being murdered by someone. That someone is still on the run and police is still investigating who's the real murderer. I can't believe such a nice man like my son is killed just like that" said the man's dad. Standing beside the dad was a slutty looking young woman in her early 20s. She was wrapping her arm around the old man's and by assumption, Mr Chips believed that she was his mistress.

    The woman gave a subtle wink at Mr Chips, and Mr Chips was almost swooned over by her slutty eyelashes. When asked for her identity, the skimpily dressed lady smiled and said "I'm Ris Low, likes red and loud and something that shouts me and also something that goes BOOMZ! I also like leopard and zebra preens." Mr Chips was astonished by her bimbotic reply.

    Suddenly, Mr Chips caught a slight glimpse at Ris's pocket. There were some blood stains and he found it quite suspicious, but his thoughts were dismissed when Ris invited him to sit down for a cup of tea.

    After awhile, Mr Chips got some helpers and together, they carried the dead body away. He then placed the body inside a coffin and shut it tight.

    Suddenly, the coffin made a creaking noise that woke Mr Chips up in the middle of the night.
