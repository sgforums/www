    Public Forum in English: “Healthy Mind, Healthy Heart: Mental Health & Chronic Illness”, 12 February 2011, 2pm to 5pm at IRAS Auditorium, IRAS Centre, Level 5

     

    The Singapore Association for Mental Health (SAMH), supported by Health Promotion Board (HPB) will be organising a Public Forum in English on“Healthy Mind, Healthy Heart: Mental Health & Chronic Illness”, 12 February 2011, 2pm to 5pm at IRAS Auditorium, IRAS Centre, Level 5.

     

    The Public Forum is free of charge and details of the forum and registration form are enclosed. The closing date for pre-registration is on 10 February 2011 or when seating capacity is reached, whichever is earlier.

     

    Please help to disseminate the information to your friends and others who may be interested in the forum.  Feel free to make photocopies of the attached flyer should you need more.  Seats are limited, so please register early and send all registration forms to samhcomed@gmail.com.
