    For the first time in my life, I made a cheese cake today which I first ate 12 years ago. It was then made by a neighbour. It was delicious, and still is, today.

    Here's the recipe:

    Non Bake pineapple cheesecake

    200g digestive biscuits
    125g butter
    1 tin pineapple
    2 blocks Philadelphia Cream cheese
    200ml light sour cream
    6 tsp gelatine
    6 tbsp fine raw sugar

    1) Pound biscuit in a plastic bag til fine and mix with butter. Then press into baking tin and put in the fridge.

    2) Beat cream cheese til smooth and gradually add the sugar and continue beating.

    3) Add in light sour cream into mixture and beat lightly.

    4) Cut pineapple into small pieces and together with juice, boil it.

    5) Put in gelatine and mix in (2) & (3).

    6) When slightly cool, pour over the biscuit and cool.

    7) Chill overnight for better results and taste.

    Enjoy it. We did.
