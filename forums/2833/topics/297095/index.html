    You tarik-ed the tea too much? :"D
    1. Mengku tea is not generally known for its bitterness
    2. Big Snow Mountain is especially not known for bitter note

    Try this:
    1. Use a porcelain gaiwan
    2. Use 3-5g of leaves for a 150ml capacity gaiwan
    3. is the Snow Mountain 2006? If so, boil the water, rinse and warm the gaiwan with the hot water. Pour off the water and throw in the leaves, place the cover and set aside for about a minute. Then pour the water over the leaves and rinse off, replace the cover and set aside for another minute.
    4. Pour the water, not over the leaves but by the side of the gaiwan.
    5. Pour out the tea almost immediately and drink.
    6. It should taste better than the first time you tried it.
    7. If not, the tea is probably bad, give us it.
