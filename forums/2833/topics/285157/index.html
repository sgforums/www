    Hi Pierre,

    There are many types of tea. The ones that we are drinking are known as pu-erh. This particular genre of tea ages like wine and develops very complex flavours that are as intriguing as the best wines available. The current craze will be tea cakes that are thirty to fifty years old and the 50 year old cakes can cost as much as SGD18k. Another attractive side effect that this tea has is weight loss. Hence it is also known as a slimming tea.

    As for the other teas like ti kuan yin, dong ding ooling, long jin, etc.. these you have to drink within a year or so when they are still fresh. Else they will not taste and smell good after that.

    Hope this clarifies.
