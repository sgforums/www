    GAME: Football Club House Prediction League

    This game is easy to play. We'll select a few matches, ranging from 1-10 matches per week for guessing and predictions. The rules are as follows:

    Each player to post only once for the predictions. Any last min changes must be done 15 mins before kickoff. If edited time is more than 15 mins, the prediction will all be deemed void. Once scores have been awarded, no discrepancies will be entertained unless prove is provided. Editing 15 mins after kickoff will deem all the remaining predictions void.

    When you post scorelines, please copy the color code that the mod used in the fixtures for the week and then add in your predictions and make your predictions stand out before you post them. This will ensure smooth checking of scores and faster updating of league table.

    Do not chat in this thread, to chat, please proceed to:
    http://www.sgforums.com/?action=thread_display&thread_id=94081

    More information about the SCORING SYSTEM:
    http://www.sgforums.com/?action=thread_display&thread_id=94432

    Point System:

    Exact Score (Correct Selection + Score + GD) +3 pts
    Correct GD (Correct Selection + GD) +2pts
    Correct Score (Correct Selection only) +1 pt
    Wrong Score +0 pt

    *Please note that correct score for draws is +3 and correct draw but wrong score is only a +1. Sorry for last min notice.

    1st - League Title Holder and Champions League honours
    2nd - League Runner-Ups and Champions League honours
    3rd - Champions League honours
    4th - Champions League honours
    Last 3 - Relegation shame

    Players with inactiveness (of participation for more than 5 gameweeks) or guilty of cloning nicks to have multiple tries may be banned from the game without notice. Please play with ethics.

    Have fun! Moderator will post games for you all to predict here. Any questions, please ask here. Thanks.
