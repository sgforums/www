    Middlesbrough's UEFA Cup adventure continued with an easy 2-0 victory over a very poor Lazio side at the Riverside. Two goals from Bolo Zenden, either side of half-time, were the finer points to a victory which was fully deserved and which firmly announces Middlesbrough's arrival on the continental stage. Elsewhere, Alan Shearer and Craig Bellamy teamed up to fire Newcastle United past Dinamo Tbilisi with a 2-0 win at St James' Park. Graeme Souness' side dominated throughout and were never really troubled by the visitors.
