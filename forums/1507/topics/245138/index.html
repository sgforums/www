    Women Make A Difference (WMD) was formed to celebrate, educate, entertain, inspire and activate women, WMD seeks to successfully make a powerful difference in the lives of Singaporean women, so that they can impact the lives of women and children globally.

    Our objectives are twofold:
    i) to galvanise women in developed countries like Singapore to live, learn and become better people with a mission to help those in need, through a range of interactive activities that bring value to the participant;
    ii) to support, financially and otherwise, women and children in countries that are in need of help in the areas of social injustice, health, education and economy.

    The WMD team want to be known as a power team that creates a positive sisterhood among women, a sisterhood that births new ideas and relationships. We are united by a common desire to promote the physical, spiritual, financial, emotional, mental well-being of women. We exercise mutual respect and support, driven by Truth and Love.

    WMD serves as a platform each year for women to come together via entertainment, education seminars, events, media, sports and the arts.
