    Cocco Latte @ The Gallery Hotel

    The cosy two floor Cocco Latte boasts an intimate lounge are on the first floor and a DJ console on the second.

    The Latte Lounge -
    Located on the ground floor is The Latte Lounge, a Cafe bar with low modular seatings in rather fetching groovy urban shapes and colors - this is a space right for music, meetings of minds and general creative inspiration.

    Bedecked with funky artwork featuring artists from all over the galaxy, the art on the walls changes regularly.

    Tuesday to Thursday & Sundays:Singles Nite
    Highlights include a killer "human jukebox" featuring everything from White Stripes to Chicks on Speed to Dusty Springfield.

    The Cocco Cocoon -
    The artery of energy flows through the Cocco cocoon on the second floor. It is an intimate space with a DJ console that changes decorative theme bimonthly. As the cosmos shifts, different settings and time-warp theme parties meet here for a swirling good time.

    Friday:HouseCall
    Gorgeous arms-in-the-air house is very much the emphasis for this room. Jump around to chunky-funky uplifting house stormers.

    Saturday:Bump
    Hitting the Hip Hop, classic soul and r&b bullseye
