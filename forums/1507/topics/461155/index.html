Upcoming Skincare brand seeking participants for feedback!

  • Stevejf

    Would you be or know anyone who may be interested to be part of the development process of a new French skincare brand coming to Singapore?

    We are currently seeking women aged between 20 years old to 40 years, of different skin types and age groups for feedback on our new skincare products that are specially formulated to suit local women’s needs.

    Every participant would be able to bring home a sample set of skincare products to use for 3 weeks, at the end which participants would be required to return after 3 weeks to share their feedback with us.

     

    Overview of our products:
    The products you would be sampling have been researched, developed and manufactured by a leading skincare R&D team in France and have met the standards and quality tests prior to distribution at the sessions. All products have been wholly made in France and are mainly made up of ingredients derived from fresh flowers and plants. All our products are free of parabens, alcohols, silicones, sulfates and oil.

     

    Who We Are Looking For:
    Singaporean women aged between 20-40 of different skin types and age groups,

     

    What you get:
    In additional to the complimentary set of products, participants will receive $50 at the end of the first focus group session, and an additional $100 upon the 2nd session 3 weeks later. An easy $150!

     

    Duration and procedure of the study:
    Duration: 3 weeks

    Group 1: First Meeting: 17 Nov (Sat), 10am to 12pm
                      Second Meeting:   8 Dec (Sat), 10am to 1pm

    Group 2: First Meeting: 17 Nov (Sat), 1pm to 3pm
                      Second Meeting:   8 Dec (Sat), 1pm to 3pm

    Group 3: First Meeting: 18 Nov (Sun), 10am to 12pm
                      Second Meeting:   9 Dec (Sun), 10am to 12pm

     

    First Meeting
     A focus group session to be held on 17 Nov (Sat) or 18 Nov (Sun) where participants will receive their sample products and feedback forms and provide feedback on their first impressions on the products.

    After One Week (26 Nov):
    Arrangement will be made (via courier) to collect first part of the survey at a time and place of your convenience.

    Second Meeting:
    Participants will meet again to submit the remaining feedback forms and evaluate the products.

     

    Venue:
    Kandahar Street (close to Raffles Hospital, nearest MRT: Bugis)
    (Further details will be released to selected participants)
