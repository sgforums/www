    I like cleansing oils because they clean really well.

    I'm using the Shu Uemura cleansing oil for oily skin now. Bought the small bottle for $50+. (May be different size from Honeybunz's description?) Cleans thoroughly. Has a mild peppermint smell. Hurts the eyes if it gets in contact with eyes.

    Before this I used Ipsa cleansing oil. There's only 1 oil for all types of skin. Cleans well too. No scent. Gentle on the eyes. Small bottle costs $40+, for more quantity than Shu (I think 10ml more) .

    Comparatively, I think performance wise these two brands are on par. Perhaps Shu feels less oily after you wash off with water.... but they're almost the same. Ipsa is more economical.

    I didn't know about the bubbles around the eyes from using oil cleanser, but these two products don't give me this problem. After using a cleansing oil, I use a normal cleanser.

    Oil cleansers may be pricey but they go a long way, at least 2-3 months worth of product in a small bottle. So I think they're still worth it.
