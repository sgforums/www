    Seriously...i have only been hearing horror tales about Eyelash extension...

     

    1.) The "glue" used may be sensitive to the eyelid, may cause irritation and inflammation.

     

    2.) You need to be disciplined to not rub your eyes or wash your eyes often as it will cause the eyelashes to drop off easily or irritation to your eyes.

     

    3.) Your natural eyelashes may actually drop off together with the fake ones and it may cause a slower growth of your natural eyelashes.

     

    4.) Thinning of natural eyelashes.

     

    5.) Visible and obvious pores (i dun really know how to describe it) on your eyelids which looks pimply.
