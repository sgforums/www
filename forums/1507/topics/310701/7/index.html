  • FireIce
    19 Jun 09, 20:42

    from heavy to light coverage:
    liquid -> 2way cake -> pressed powder -> loose powder

    if u are going to wear makeup for long hours, liquid would be most lasting..... touch up at midday or later part of the day with loose powder.....
    but can be most problematic too as it can clog pores.....

    2way cake would be the most common.....
    no need use hand to apply.....
    usually gives a smooth matte finish....

    pressed powder is pressed loose powder....
    for the days u need medium-to-light coverage.... or for touching up......as it's handy to bring out....

    loose powder can be used on its own when u need sheer coverage
    or use to mattify liquid foundation
    some LP comes with shimmer
