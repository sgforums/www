    How to buy blushers?
    Make sure the formula suits your skin type and lifestyle

    * Powder Blush

    Powder blusher suits all skin types, but is best for oily skin and anyone looking for long-lasting colour (it is the densest of the blushers)

    * Cream Blush

    This type of blusher is best applied with the fingers. It's also dense, but lets your skin show through. Cream blush is particularly suited for dry skin because it contains moisturing ingredients

    * Gel Blush

    Gel blushers provide a sheer glow. They work best on normal to oily skin and are hard to spread onto dry skin since they are fast drying. TIPS: Always remember, less is more.

    Be sure to do the "blusher test". Once you have applied your makeup, stand back and look in the mirror. You should have on just enough blusher to make you look as though you are blushing. Any more than that and you need to tone it down.
