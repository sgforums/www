    twenty47

    You want a shawl to keep warm, go for pashmina or cashmere, or wool, which is cheaper.

    Department stores like Takashimaya and Isetan stocks these. The higher percentage of pashmina/cashmere, the warmer and pricier it is. Also note higher percentage of pashmina/cashmere means the fabric gets "fuzzier" more easily, may cause problems when using.

    Another good source for shawls is Holland Village Shopping Center second floor. You can browse around those ethnic looking boutiques. One shop there called Rustic Living has wide range and reasonable prices. They've got an outlet at Centerpoint too.
