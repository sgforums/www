    here's a short guide to eyebrow shaping (BY ANASTASIA BH):

    Step 1
    Apply the Pre-Tweeze Gel to the brow area in preparation for tweezing.

    Step 2
    Arch and shape by applying one of the Classic Stencils to the brow. Fill in the brow area using the Angled Cut Brush in Small 15 to the brow with Brow Pomade.

    Step 3
    Remove hairs that are outside the stenciled area with the Tweezers.

    Step 4
    Comb the brow hairs up with the spooley end of the Brow Pencil or Mini Duo Angled/Spooley 7 Brush and clip any lengthy or unwanted hairs with the pink handled Scissors.

    Step 5
    Apply the After Tweeze Cream to calm and soothe any redness or irritated areas.

    Step 6
    Apply the Eye Lights Shimmer or Eye Lights Matte pencil just below the arch of the brow. Gently smooth and blend into the brow line to create a highlight that opens up the eye. The Eye Lights Shimmer can be used all over the lid, cheekbones, and on the lips.

    Step 7
    Apply the Brow Gel to condition and hold the shape of brow all day.
