    Hmmm....dream shoes.....takes a while to really know what is perfect for myself.

    Love Boots, plain plain design with a side zip and the leather reach till the lower calf type.

    Love Strappy heels, with heels the height of at least 2 inches.  With short front soles, cos me feet is wide, not long, so sometimes wheres strappy sandals heels, the front portion like a lot of space or the toes will spill over the side of the soles.

    Also Strappy pumps,  dont like pumps alone  cos the back of the feet will get blisters.  And strappy pumps means that the shoe is secure to the body even if I have to spread my legs and run.  Pumps with straps are more secure to me.

    And I cant wear open toe heels or throng sandals heels.  Open toes squash my toes together and the open toe front can see the toes.  Throng sandals heels, my little toes always spill over the sides.  So I always like those mary janes heels that wrap the toes up

    Then is Running sports shoes with springs at the back, the crocs mary janes, hydro & cayman designs.  Tactical boots & motorcycle riding boots.
