    SPH Magazines launches new women's website herworldPLUS

     

    Women looking for content on every aspect of their lives can now turn to herworldPLUS, a comprehensive new website launched by SPH Magazines.

    Replacing the former Her World website, herworldPLUS offers content on diverse topics including shopping, beauty and fashion, dating, relationships, sex and men, work, money, health and travel, food, gadgets, weddings, decor and parenting. Group-sharing and community-building are other key features of the website.

    In addition, readers will enjoy content from other SPH Magazines, such as Home&Decor, Young Parents and Simply Her. Besides offering more varied content, the new intuitive website is easier to navigate with video and photo tools.

    herworldPLUS editor Niki Bruce said: 'The idea behind herworldPLUS is to offer our readers online content that will resonate with every part of their lives. To help women stay on top of things, key sections are updated daily and regularly. Our readers can also share their latest fashion buys and ideas via our online community.'
