    tips to prevent smudging of eyeliner and mascara:

    apply eye primer or a layer of loose powder on the lids, close to the lashes
    eyeliner shd be applied after eyeshadow, before mascara
    heat the eyelash curler with hair dryer before kiaping the lashes
    (kiap before applying mascara)
    use waterproof mascara...
    use eyelash brush to separate the lashes properly after applying ur first coat of mascara

    carry a small bottle of eye makeup remover in ur makeup pouch and some cotton buds (i find the maybelline eye and lip mu remover small and handy and effective) so u can dab away the stains/smudges then touch-up makeup
