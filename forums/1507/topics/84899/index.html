    The Apsara Spa(Javanese Style)

    Apsara Spa was created from a very deep affection towards the beauty and romanticism.

    Apsara Spa treatment uses special recipes that contain various herbals, traditional spices and local exotic flowers.It brings together the total hair, facial and body aromatherapy into one exotic treatment meant to be for the Kings and Queens of Java in the old days.

    This extraordinary experience combines six steps of treatment :

    Welcoming Treatments (15 minutes)

    It will start with washing your feet in a big silver bowl filled with warm water, tropical flowers and special aromatic oils; it be continue with washing hands in a smaller bowl, which later will be used for inhaling seven deep breaths to relax and calm the mind.

    Total hair, facial and body aroma therapy massage (2 hours)

    The treatment begins with a gentle message all over the body, continues with a tender message for the face, neck and ends with the scalp, using special recipes that contain many herbals, traditional spices and local exotic flowers for the aromatherapy. During this treatment you would be pampered with the romantic rhythm of the Apsara Dancers performance.

    Esalen

    Esalen is a marriage between Swedish massage, with its precise manner of working with muscles and circulatory system, and the deeply personal sensing work brought by the individual therapist and has become a physical/emotional/spiritual human emergence to be sung to through touch. The recipient is gently urged to become an active participant through heightened awareness and surrendering to a deep sensing place. Long, lengthening strokes with gentle rocking and stretching, passive joint movement, sculpting of deep musculature, delicate cranial balancing, all point to a subtle neural reeducation of the body. Personal attention takes precedence over technique; thus each massage session is unique in itself. Esalen massage then becomes a meditation of receiving touch from another as part of a larger picture of richness which puts you firmly in touch with your more personal self. Warm scented oils or lotions are used to deepen the sensual experience through smell and texture.

    Hot Stone Massage

    Hot Stones have been used for healing by Native Americans, Aborginies, and many ancient European & Middle Eastern cultures for centuries. This massage uses flat bastalt river stones, beach pebbles, & volcanic stones heated in water to a temperature of 125 degrees. The stones are applied to the body, along with a warm aromatic oil, and are sometimes left on areas such as the sacrum on a protective towel for extra heat infusion. Most of the massage is a combination of Swedish and Esalen, using the stones as a tool for a therapeutic, deep muscle HEAT penetration.
