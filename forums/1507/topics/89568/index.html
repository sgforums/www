    Baked Apples

    A delicious fall treat, perfect as an easy, low-fat dessert.

    Serving: 6
    Prep Time: 20 minutes minutes
    Cook Time: 60 minutes
    Total Time: 80 minutes

    INGREDIENTS:
    1/2 cup dried Chinese dates or raisins
    6 Rome apple
    1 lemon cut in half
    3 tbsp honey
    1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
    2/3 cup apple cider

    DIRECTIONS:
    1. Rinse the dates and soften in boiling hot water for one hour, changing the water twice. Drain, and remove the pits with a paring knife. Cut into rings.

    2. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F.

    3. Cut a slice 1/2-inch thick off the top of each apple and set aside. Trim the bottom of each apple so that it will sit upright. Core and seed the apples.

    4. Squeeze a little lemon juice inside each ahttp://www.sgforums.com/?action=post&thread_id=89568pple. Arrange the apples in a baking dish.

    5. Mix the honey, cinnamon and sliced dates. Spoon some of the mixture into each apple. Cover the apple with the sliced-off top.

    6. Pour the apple cider in the baking dish around the apples.

    7. Cover the top of the baking dish with aluminum foil, sealing the edges. Place on the middle shelf of the oven. Bake the apples in the preheated oven for 50 to 60 minutes, or until very tender when pierced with a knife. Uncover and serve warm.

    NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION:
    Based on individual serving.

    Calories: 190
    Total Fat: 1 g
    Carbohydrates: 44 g
    Protein: 1 g

    Clam-and-Corn Chowder

    INGREDIENTS:
    Serving: 5

    4 slices bacon, coarsely chopped
    1 medium onion, chopped
    1 pound russet potatoes, peeled and cubed
    1/2 teaspoon salt
    1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
    three 6 1/2-ounce cans chopped clams, drained, with juice reserved
    2 cups milk
    1/4 cup all-purpose flour
    1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen corn kernels
    1 cup half-and-half
    1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley leaves

    DIRECTIONS:
    1. In a 5-quart Dutch oven or heavy kettle, cook bacon over medium heat until crisp and browned. Using a slotted spoon, transfer bacon to a paper towel; drain. Pour fat into a small heatproof bowl. Return 1 tablespoon fat to Dutch oven; discard or freeze remaining fat. Add onion to fat; cook, stirring occasionally, until softened -- 2 to 3 minutes.

    2. Add 2 cups water to Dutch oven, stirring to loosen browned bits on bottom. Add potatoes, salt, pepper, and reserved clam juice; stir and cover. Bring to a boil over high heat; reduce heat to low and cook until potatoes are fork-tender -- about 10 minutes.

    3. In a small bowl, combine milk and flour. Add to Dutch oven and stir. Return to a boil, stirring constantly, until chowder has thickened. Add corn and half-and-half and cook 5 minutes. Add reserved clams and cook just until clams are heated through. Stir in parsley and reserved bacon. Divide among 6 soup bowls and serve.

    NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION:
    Based on individual serving.

    Calories: 354
    Total Fat: 11.0 g
    Cholesterol: 62.0 mg
    Sodium: 461 mg
    Fiber: 4.00 g
    Protein: 22.0 g

    Pasta with Turkey Sausage and Pepper Meatballs

    High in Vitamin C from the red pepper and tomatoes. A great-tasting dish!

    Serving: 6
    Prep Time: 15 minutes minutes
    Cook Time: 50 minutes
    Total Time: 65 minutes

    INGREDIENTS:
    8 ounce lean ground beef
    1/4 tsp pepper
    1 tsp vegetable oil
    1 lb rigatoni pasta
    see recipe for Basic Tomato Sauce tomato sauce
    8 casings removed ounce turkey sausage
    1/2 minced cup onion
    1/2 diced cup red bell pepper
    2 large egg white
    1 1/2 cup fresh bread crumbs
    2 minced tbsp fresh parsley
    1 tsp fennel seed
    1/2 tsp salt

    DIRECTIONS:
    1. Place meatball ingredients (everything except the pasta, tomato sauce and oil) in a medium bowl.
    2. Knead the mixture with your hands until it is smooth.
    3. To prevent sticking, dip your hands into cool water before forming each meatball.
    4. Form mixture into 30 1 1/2-inch balls.
    5. Pour the tomato sauce into a large, heavy-bottom saucepan and bring it to a simmer over low heat.
    6. Warm 1/2 teaspoon vegetable oil in a large non-stick skillet.
    7. Add half the meatballs to the skillet and brown them on all sides.
    8. Spoon the meatballs into the tomato sauce.
    9. Add the remaining 1/2 teaspoon vegetable oil to skillet and brown the rest of the meatballs, then add them to the sauce.
    10. Simmer, stirring, for 20 minutes.
    11. While sauce is simmering, prepare pasta according to package directions; drain. Transfer to a large serving bowl.
    12. Remove any bay leaves from sauce; pour sauce over pasta and serve.

    NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION:
    Based on individual serving.

    Calories: 562
    Total Fat: 11 g
    Carbohydrates: 83 g
    Protein: 30 g

    Deep-Fried Sweet Potato Balls

    Yields: 5 servings
    Prep Time: 20 Minutes
    Cook Time: 15 Minutes
    Ready In: 35 Minutes

    INGREDIENTS:
    3/4 pound sweet potatoes-- cooked, peeled and mashed
    4 teaspoons cornstarch, divided
    1 pinch salt
    1 tablespoon water
    1 cup shredded coconut
    1/2 cup brown sugar
    3/4 cup sesame seeds
    1 1/2 quarts oil for deep frying

    DIRECTIONS:
    1. In a mixing bowl, knead together potatoes, 2 teaspoons cornstarch, salt and water to form a stiff dough. In a separate bowl, combine coconut and brown sugar. Form potato dough into small balls; make a well in the center and fill with coconut mixture; close up hole and reshape balls. Roll in sesame seeds, then in remaining cornstarch.
    2. Heat oil in deep-fryer or deep, heavy-bottomed pan to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
    3. Fry balls in oil until golden. Drain on paper towels. Serve.

    NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION:

    French Toast

    Yields: 6 slices of French Toast = 3 servings

    Prep Time: 5 Minutes
    Cook Time: 15 Minutes
    Ready In: 20 Minutes

    INGREDIENTS:
    6 thick slices bread
    2 eggs
    2/3 cup milk
    1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)
    1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg (optional)
    1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
    salt to taste

    DIRECTIONS:
    1. Beat together egg, milk, salt, desired spices and vanilla.
    2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle of skillet over medium-high flame.
    3. Dunk each slice of bread in egg mixture, soaking both sides. Place in pan, and cook on both sides until golden. Serve hot.

    Deluxe Waffles

    Yields: 6 servings

    INGREDIENTS:
    2 cups all-purpose flour
    1 tablespoon baking powder
    1 teaspoon salt
    3 tablespoons white sugar
    4 egg yolks
    4 egg whites
    1 1/4 cups milk
    1/2 cup vegetable oil

    DIRECTIONS:
    1. In a large bowl sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar. Make a well in the center of the bowl.
    2. In a separate bowl, mix together the egg yolks, milk, and oil. Pour into well of flour mixture and stir well. Stir just until ingredients are moistened.
    3. In a small bowl beat egg whites until stiff. Fold into batter.
    4. Cook batter in waffle iron.

    CHOCOLATE ECLAIRS

    For all your sweet craving!!

    This require 3different preparation step.

    1st Step Eclair
    INGREDIENTS
    1 c. water
    1/2 c. butter
    1 c. flour
    4 eggs

    DIRECTIONS
    Bring to rolling boil water and butter. Take off heat. Add flour and stir with spoon until ball can be formed. Add eggs. Beat like crazy. Mix until dough is very stiff; otherwise it will run all over pan. Drop by full teaspoons onto a cookie sheet for individual serving; put in a circular ring or in a strip. Bake 45-50 minutes at 400 degrees. Cool out of draft.

    2nd Step Chocolate Sauce
    INGREDIENTS
    6 oz. semi-sweet chocolate chips
    1 1/3 c. canned milk
    1/2 c. butter
    2 c. powdered sugar
    1 tsp. vanilla

    DIRECTIONS
    Melt chocolate chips and butter together. Gradually add milk and powdered sugar. Bring to boil. Stir continuously. Boil for 8 minutes. Remove from heat and add vanilla. Makes 3/4 quart.

    3rd Step Filling
    INGREDIENTS
    2 pkg. instant French vanilla pudding
    2 1/2 c. milk
    1 (8 oz.) container LeCreme or Cool Whip
    1 tsp. vanilla

    DIRECTIONS
    Mix pudding and milk. Mix well. Fold in Cool Whip and vanilla. Slice eclair in half and fill generously with entire amount of filling. Replace top of eclair. Drizzle top of eclair with Chocolate Sauce.Put in a fridge for abt 2hrs wahla you have yourself a homemade CHOCOLATE ECLAIR.....yummi!

    Braised Duck

    ingredients

    1 duck
    1 packet of 5 spice powder
    salt equal to volume of 5 spice powder
    1 bottle of semi-premium grade dark soy sauce
    1 handful of sugar

    method

    marinating

    wash duck thoroughly making sure bits of the head is cleaned (if you want to keep head)
    mix spice powder and salt together
    smear mixture evenly inside and outside duck
    put duck in a plastic bag and tie up
    bung into fridge (not freezer) and leave for 3 to 4 days

    cooking

    rinse duck (not wash.. just rinse most of the marinate off) not too thorough
    melt sugar in wok until it starts to turn into a black treacly mess
    pour about Â¾ of the soy sauce and dissolve the sugar
    heat until sauce starts to simmer
    put duck in and keeps scooping the black sauce over the duck
    turn over every 15 minutes and keep basting the duck
    when the duck turns golden brown, fill wok with water until most of duck is covered
    keep turning over duck every 15 minutes, taking care not to burn it (best put a small steel rack to elevate the duck from contact with bottom of the wok)
    keep simmering until cooked (about 2 hours)

    voila! braised duck!

    Name of cake: Kueh Lapis

    Ingredients:

    (A)
    25 egg yolks
    4 egg whites
    350g castor sugar
    1 tbsp sponge quick

    (B)
    100g plain flour

    (C)
    250g butter, beaten
    2 tbsps condensed milk
    2 tbsps rum

    Methods:

    1) Beat (A) until fluffy, stir plain flour and mix well.

    2) Add in (C) and mix evenly. Pour a thin layer of batter in a 9" tray and grill until golden brown.

    3) Repeat until batter finished.

    Tip: Cover tin bottom with 2 pieces of baking paper before baking to avoid scorching. Each layer of the layered cake is about 1 cm thick.
