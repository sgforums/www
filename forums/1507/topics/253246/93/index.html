    In a surprise announcement, MUJI Singapore will be shutting down their e-commerce site this Christmas Day, and all orders placed online will be processed and fulfilled by 29 December.

    MUJI did not state its reasons for the online wind-up, except the move to close down the e-commerce operations of the site will enable them to develop a better and more comprehensive, all-inclusive shopping experience for their customers.

    The company, however, did not provide any date on when the new site will be ready and says details will be released nearer to launch date.

    With the closing down, MUJI will also be offering its final Clearance Sale on hundred over products with discounts up to 50%! What’s more, customers will enjoy a further 15% off with discount code MJFDCS15.

    Items on sale include fashion clothing for men, women and kids, accessories, shoes and bags, innerwear, fabrics, furniture, as well as health and beauty products.
