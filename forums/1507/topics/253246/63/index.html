    Attention to all SAFRA members!

    The original cast of Ah Boys to Men will reprise their roles – this time as recruits of Singapore’s Naval Diving Unit (NDU). The movie offers a peek into the intensive training regime of the NDU, which has never been revealed so far.

    Tickets are available from 7 – 15 Feb 2015 on a first-come-first-serve basis at SAFRA Mt Faber or SAFRA Toa Payoh, While stocks last.

    Promotion Details

    To start off SAFRA’s SG50 Celebrations – SAFRA is giving away a FREE pair of preview tickets to Ah Boy to Men 3 for every member.

    Simply present your valid SAFRA membership card and NRIC to redeem.

    Contact Information

    Only available at SAFRA Mt Faber and SAFRA Toa Payoh Customer Service Counters.

    Terms & Conditions

    • Tickets are available on a first-come-first-serve basis, while stocks last.
    • Limited to two tickets per SAFRA member upon presentation of a valid SAFRA membership card and NRIC.
    • The location and seat allocation of the movie cannot be chosen.
    • Movie preview is only valid on Sun, 15 Feb 2015, time and location will be stated on the actual tickets.
    • The tickets cannot be replaced if lost and are non-exchangeable, non-refundable and non-resalable.
    • Collection of tickets on behalf of another person will not be accepted
