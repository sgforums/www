    SINGAPORE - Australia's popular Mexican restaurant Guzman y Gomez Mexican Taqueria (GYG) will be setting up shop in Singapore, and to mark the occassion, it will be giving out free burritos on Oct 24.

    Free Burrito Day will take place from 11am to 4pm at the new outlet in Asia Square Tower 2 Food Garden. Customers are limited to one burrito or burrito bowl per person.

    Only Guzman y Gomez's signature variety of burritos and burrito bowls will be available on that day. No other menu items will be available for purchase until the day after.

    Subsequently, the full menu will include Mexican favourites such as quesadillas, tacos and nachos. Customers can also look forward to Guzman y Gomez's signature free salsa bars.

    Expect affordable prices as items like a mini burrito or a two-piece taco will cost about $7.90.
