    Here comes TANGS SG50 promotion and in celebration of the nation’s 50th birthday, they have dropped TANGS Card’s minimum qualifying amount to just $50. Previously you would have to spend $250 to sign up for one.

    TANGS cardmembers will enjoy 6% rebate for any purchase at their stores and a huge 12% rebate on special days which they will notify you through SMS or email. Other perks include birthday treats, invites to workshops and events, 10% rebate at Island Cafe, free delivery with minimum $100 purchase from their e-Store or $200 in -store.

    The new $50 spending qualifier promotion will last till the end of 2015 according to TANGS. Find more information and full T&Cs at TANGS official card page.
