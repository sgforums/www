    What brand(s) are you currently using? In the past till now, have you been using the same brand? If not, what other brand have you used before?

    My first skin care brand was Oil of Ulan (now called Ulay) -- The ordinary range (a bit pinkish in colour). That time I was still schooling, didn't have much pocket money. So I bought this which was quite affordable, from some supermarket.

    Then I started to have a few freckle spots. So I decided that I needed to use something with sun screen. That time Oil of Ulan didn't have this range.

    Then I realised that my sister and my mother were using Estee Lauder. One of the products they were using was the Night Repair which does have sun screen feature. So I started applying that every day before I went for my lecture. Then slowly, I used E L Fruition (also share with my mother) and E L Oil Control Moisturiser (given to me). My skin went very very well for a long long time and I was so proud of my skin. Until one day, I was tired of this brand and decided to switch brand. I switched to Clarins for a well, and then Loreal and then back to Clarins.

    In recent years, I went back to E L. Then I was intro to Nu Skin which I used for a few months. Recently I have just started on SK 2.

    What are the brands that you have used and have done wonders to your skin?
