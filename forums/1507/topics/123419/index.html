    getting a good concealer is not easy.
    alot of the brands didn't offer a good one.

    what's more important is choosing the correct shade to cover the darkness.
    remember. don't think that getting a lighter shade will cover the darkness.
    it's wrong. you'll only get a white patch with grayish shade.
    you'll have to get a shade nearest or slightly darker than your skin tone.

    actually, the easiest way to cover dark eye shadows is to use purple concealer.
