    1) To greatly reduce the chances of your Bicycle getting kopped, get a U-Shackle Lock instead of those chain /cable locks. Trust me, that time I used a THICK stainless steel chain lock and the next day, I saw it lying in 2 pieces next to my bike. Heng the U- shackle lock prevented the thief from running away with my bike! Ask the locksmith if u are not sure what a U- Shackle lock is..

    2) Don't just lock the frame to the railings/ bike parking lot, lock up the wheels to something secure or to the bike frame. Reason: If the thief cannot steal your frame, he can still remove your wheels and steal them, leaving the frame behind.

    3) Park the cheap bike outside. The bike I park outdoors is an old one... I will never park my Voodoo Sobo outdoors!

    4) If possible, park your bike at a place where there will be lots of people around, 24 hours a day.------ I parked mine outside the mac donalds at the main road, and I had no trouble with people attempting to steal my bike!

    Alternatively, u can park in front of a 24 hour Kopitiam or near the main road. Never park your bike at a secluded area! My friend more kiasu, he locked his bike at the lamp post right outside the POLICE POST!

    5) I think coding your bike at the police post is FOC..

    That's all the advices I can give!
