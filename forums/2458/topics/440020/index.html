  • alize
    21 Oct 11, 02:27

    TS, don't confuse East Coast Park (seaside park) with East Coast Parkway (the expressway). Both are parallel and along the sea.

    If you are coming from East Coast Park, follow the park all the way to Fort Road Flyover. You can cycle under East Coast Parkway here and cycle along Fort Road to...

    Mountbatten Road...

    Nicoll Highway (which is not an epressway despite the name. Cycling on road is permitted and there are pavements too)...

    [if cycling on pavement] Bras Basah Road... which becomes Orchard Road...

    [if cycling on road] Stamford Road... which becomes Penang Road... which becomes Somerset Road... which is next to Orchard Road.

    (There are also many underpasses to cross under East Coast Parkway.)
