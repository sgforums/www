    The MOE Approved Calculator Casio Fx 95 MS has the ability to solve simultaneous equations, cubic equation and quadratic equation. This will help the students to check their answers.

    Cubic Equation

    Solve the equation

    2x^3 + 7x^2 - 2x -1 = 0

    Steps Using MOE Approved Calculator Casio Fx 95 MS to solve for x

    Press ON

    Press Mode twice

    Press 1 for Eqn

    Press right arrow once

    Press 3 for degree 3 ie cubic equation ax^3 + bx^2 + cx + d = 0

    Press 2 for a

    Press 7 for b

    Press -2 for c

    Press -1 for d

    Press =

    x1 = 0.5,

    Press =

    x2 = - 3.73

    Press =

    x3 = - 0.268

    Answer

    x = 0.5, - 3.73 or - 0.268
