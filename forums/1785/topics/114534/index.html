    "Oh Considerate People"

    Oh considerate people
    Do not impose your thoughts on others
    The knowledge and morals you have gained in this world
    Do not necessarily apply
    At all times
    Or for all things
    Or for all people
    I would like you to know that your kind intentions
    Can sometimes injure
    The people whom you judge
    Can decide for themselves more than you think
    They do not need to rely on your gods
    Because they have their own gods

    I am not asking you to leave them alone
    I would like you to have the courage to watch over them
    Your children
    Your pupils
    Your juniors
    Even if they do not submit to your will
    Do not worry
    Do not be anxious
    If you maintain your tranquility
    And if you continue to show yourself
    There will surely be someone for you
    The people whom you think are below you
    Are really your teachers
    The people you are trying to teach
    Are your mentors in life
    If you become aware of this you can learn

    People at higher positions
    Have the duty to watch over others
    The duty to trust and watch over them
    This is your duty
    In choosing to become a parent
    In choosing to become a teacher
    In choosing to become a supervisor
    If you remove from your feelings
    Your criticism of others
    Even if you are not mature
    They will follow
    There is no need to use flattery
    The people whom you think are below you
    Have more wisdom
    Than you think
    It is just that they are not aware and cannot express it
    The courage to accept
    The courage to watch over them
    That courage
    Is what you need now

    People with titles of specialists
    Never forget the time when you were a novice
    Do you want to create dolls who listen to your words
    Or do you want to actualize the ideals you aim for
    I want you to think again
    Even if the methods are not your own
    Your ideals can be realized
    Methods are important
    But do not be attached to them
    People with attachments will always fail
    Even if it looks like a roundabout route
    Just rely on the gods that reside in people's hearts
    In the heart of every person

    Let me say it once more
    Continue to watch over them and to show them until they become aware
    Even if there are those who choose different methods from yours
    That Is all right
    Because even it the path they aim for is different from yours
    As long as they try to move on
    The path will always appear
    So do not ponder and be anxious
    Follow your own path
    Because as long as your mind heads in the direction you head towards
    Even if you sometimes get lost
    You will surely reach your destination

    Oh considerate people
    Do not impose your thoughts on others

    Luna

    April 11, 1998

    When people feel that they know more facts than others, they wish to convey them.

    Then when their trials do not go smoothly, they feel discontent.

    For the person conveying, it is self-evident, so sometimes they feel irritated about why people do not understand.

    The more you try to impose on people anything that is different from their common sense, the more strongly they tend to feel like denying it.

    So even if it is frustrating, something imposed on others cannot become the truth.

    If you want to share with others what you are aware of, please just show it and do not point out mistakes.

    Just take action and show it yourself. As long as you feel you have to correct people or to point out their mistakes, even the things you thought you had become aware of cease to be the truth.

    If you simply have the will to simply convey and to show yourself, and as long as the awareness is not something that comes from ego or attachment, then someday people will become aware.

    Luna
    Morioka City, Iwate
