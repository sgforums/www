    From an Indian current affairs show, every Saturday night...

    Once there was this guy Alpha. He was walking along the beach with his friend Bravo when Bravo gave Alpha one slap (dont ask me why). Alpha wrote on the sand 'Bravo slapped me today'

    Later, both friends decide to go for a swim. Alpha couldnt swim well and started to drown. Bravo swam over and saved him, pulling him to shore. After that, Alpha carved on the rock on the beach, 'Bravo saved m life today'

    Bravo was puzzled. He asked 'Why when i slap you, you write it on the sand. But when i saved you, you wrote in on the rock'

    Alpha replied 'Bad things, shall be forgotten with the passage of time, as words written on sand will be blown away as time goes by. Good things, shall always be remembered, just like words on the rock, which cannot be erased easily'
