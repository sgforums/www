    Actually the trick is not to use too much force. Break the egg hard at the part of the egg with air pocket. Some part of the shell will break and some will come sticking out. Peel from the edges of these first.

    Also, when peeling the rest of the egg, dont use ur fingers and press into the egg. Ur fingers should move along the contour of the egg.

    To summarise, dont use too much force....

    the trick..as taught to me by my SQ chef friend:

    just before full boil- take the eggs out and dip into a
    bowl of ice cubes + water for 2 minutes, this shrinks
    the body of the egg from the shell..

    than put the eggs back into the boiling water again for
    10 seconds, this expands the shell n warms up the egg again

    n add a bit of salt to the water when boiling
    this prevents the egg from cracking..

    it works good, try it..
