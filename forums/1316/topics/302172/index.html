    Gundam Musou worldwide sales
    An ITmedia News story on Koei that mentions sales figures for various games released by the company says the multiplatform Gundam Musou has sold 600,000 copies in Japan (880,000 total worldwide). Stateside, the PS3 version sold 100,000 copies while the XBox 360 version moved another 200,000. In February of last year, not long after the title was first announced for the PS3, Bandai Namco's Shin Unozawa stated the goal for Gundam Musou was to sell 1 million copies worldwide.
