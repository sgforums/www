    Dengeki 15th anniversary

    Mediaworks is celebrating the 15th anniversary of its Dengeki brand publications on November 24-25 at Chiba's Makuhari Messe convention center. The event will include talk shows for Gundam SEED Frame Astrays (guests: Koichi Tokita, Tomohiro Chiba) and Gundam Unicorn (guests: Harutoshi Fukui, Yasuyuki Tokumitsu). On sale at the event will be an HGUC Gundam TR-1 Hazel Kai Dengeki 15th Anniversary Special Set.

    Gundam.Info Dengeki 15th 00 pics

    Gundam.Info posted the first of seven upcoming photo galleries taken at the Dengeki 15th Anniversary event, featuring merchandise and scratch-built models. Just as the February issue of Dengeki Hobby (on sale Christmas) will come with Gundam Astraea modification parts for the HG Gundam Exia kit, there will also be red parts for the Gundam Astraea Type F mobile suit seen in Gundam Ace's Gundam 00F. It's not made clear what issue of what magazine the Type F parts will be enclosed with.
