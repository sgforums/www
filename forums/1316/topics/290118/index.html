    Gackt to release Gundam album

    Daily Sports Online reports that on December 19, Gackt will be releasing "0079-0088", a new album featuring 8 songs. Gackt, a huge Gundam freak, has previously provided the ending theme "dears" for Gundam: The Ride as well as theme songs for the Zeta Gundam theatrical trilogy. The 8 tracks consist of 5 of his own as well 3 others from the Mobile Suit Gundam movie trilogy: "Cross Of Sand", "Ai Senshi", and "Meguriai", his first cover songs. The project, announced on the same day Yoshiyuki Tomino celebrates his birthday, is said to be a present for the Gundam creator.

    0079-0080 album detail change

    Nippon Crown Music announced changes for Gackt's upcoming Gundam album "0079-0080". Release date remains the same, however 3 version will be released.
    1) "0079-0080" (general release) - 2,500 yen
    2) "0079-0080" feat.Amuro Ray - 2,700 yen
    3) "0079-0080" feat.Char Aznable - 2,700 yen

    Amuro Ray and Char Aznable version will be available in limited numbers and will feature special recording by Toru Furuya (for the Amuro Ray version) and Shuichi Ikeda (for the Char Aznable version). The special recordings were supervised by Yoshiyuki Tomino.
