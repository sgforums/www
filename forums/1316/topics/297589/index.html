    Akiba Blog MS bar report

    Akiba Blog posted a follow-up report on the Mobile Suit bars, which opened on the 15th. According to them, Zeon was mostly full. The interior had red walls decorated with the Zeon emblem, black tables (about 14 two-person tables and 10 bar counter seats), and First Gundam was being shown with a projector. Operators (female staff) wore mono-eye t-shirts with pleated miniskirts. A Char-custom cellphone with Zaku head charger was seen on the counter as well as a Gihren Zabi-cosplaying customer. The menu had some 48 drinks and 19 food items, including the "Mass-Production Zaku" (matcha oolong tea), "Dom" (Black Russian), "Asshimar" (Orange Blossom), "Gouf Heat Rods" (squid entrails), and Luna II (fried chicken).
