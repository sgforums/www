    Online store:

    V2 Hobby World
    A singapore online shop. He give very very good price for all his kits. But most of his stocks are based on orders only. So you may have to wait a while for your orders. May be able to help you search for certain model kits as well. Highly recommended.

    HobbyLink Japan
    An online store based in Japan. It has a very wide selection of gundam model kits and toys. But bare in mind the shipping cost.

    Toy Wave
    An online store based in Hong Kong. They accept payment in singapore dollars via local bank fund transfer. Dealt with them a few times, and so far so good.
