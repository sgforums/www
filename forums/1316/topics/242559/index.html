    G Gen Cross Drive update-o-rama
    This week's Famitsu reports SD Gundam G Generation Cross Drive will be released on August 9 in two editions: a regular for 5,040 yen and a limited edition for 21,840 yen which comes with a Nintendo DS Lite with a Nu Gundam-based visual design. Preorders of the game will also come with a Gundam game custom DS software case which can hold up to 3 games. First runs of the game will have an original Gundam War promotion card.

    Gundam SEED Astray and X Astray are newly confirmed appearing series, with more yet-to-be announced. Original characters Fio Crawford and Nikea are also revealed to appear.
