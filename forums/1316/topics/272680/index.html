    Chara Hobby 2007 limited edition goods

    Media Works' site shows a 1/144 resin kit of the TR-6 Woundwort will be on sale for 16,000 yen.

    Hobby Japan will have resin kits of the Gundam Mk-III (Chara Hobby specs, 22,000 yen) and the Knight Gundam, Spy Gundam, and Musha X from Musha Retsuden Zero.

    Cospa will be selling T-shirts and other merchandise based on Gundam Ace manga and popular quotes from First Gundam.
