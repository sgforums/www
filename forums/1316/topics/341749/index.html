    2 Jan 09, 16:20

    flat clear comes in many varieties.  It's basically a clear colored paint but with a dull finish (so that it's not glossy).

    There are a few varieties, mainly Tamiya, Topcoat or Mr. Hobby.

    Then there's the difference of type of paint (lacquer (quite poisonous - spray only in an open area) or acrylic (not as poisonous - but still spray in an open area).

    The choice of which one to use depends on what type of paint you're painting your models.  The solvent used in lacquer paints will dissolve acrylics.  So if you painted using acrylics, DO NOT spray a lacquer clear coat - otherwise your model will turn into a runny/melted mess.  However, if you painted using lacquer paints, both lacquer and acrylics work.
