     MG 1/100 SHIN MUSHA GUNDAM

    •JPN retail 5,000 JPY



    •Shin Musha Gundam joins the Master Grade series!

    •Based on the Ver. O.Y.W. 0079 frame for excellent articulation and action posing!

    •Front armor skirts are made of independent plates just like actual samurai armor, for realistic appearance when legs are posed.

    •The "Nichirin-maru" sword comes with a removable scabbard that can be attached to the left waist.

    •Backpack unit holds accessory weapons, including the Sankomaru spear, the Denkomaru halberd, and Tanegashima rifle.
