    Japan government: Don't Wiki from work

    Yomiuri Online reports the Ministry Of Agriculture, Forestry, And Fisheries announced today it verbally reprimanded 6 people for using their computers while at work to contribute to Wikipedia entries. One of these is a person who wrote more than 200 bits of Gundam-related material. Since 2003, 4008 contributions were made to Wikipedia from the Ministry, with 2/3 of those having no connection to their work duties. Changes were made in the system to prevent office computers from being used to write further such entries.
