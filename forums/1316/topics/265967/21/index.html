     1/100 TIEREN (LAND USE)

    •JPN retail 2,800 JPY



    •A 1/100 scale model of the Human Reform League's "Tieran Ground Type" mobile suit with unprecedented mechanical detail.

    •Parts feature rivet-like surfaces that replicate the feel of tank armor.

    •Abundant detail evokes the sense of it being a real weapon.

    •In addition to a rapid-fire cannon and carbon blade, includes a newly designed bazooka.

    •Includes one pilot and two foot soldier figures that are in scale with the mobile suit.

      1/100 GUNDAM ASTRAEA TYPE-F

    JPN retail 2,800 JPY



    •The first 1/100 scale kit of the "Gundam Astraea TYPE-F" from the side story "Mobile Suit Gundam 00F", which takes place in the same timeline as "Mobile Suit Gundam 00".

    •Includes translucent sensor mask parts for disguising the fact it is a Gundam.

    •Includes optional parts for converting to "TYPE-F Kai".

    •Includes GN Beam Rifle, GN Beam Saber, Shield, Proto GN Sword, and GN Launcher.

     1/144 HG SAACHEZ'S AEU ENACT CUSTOM AGRISSA TYPE

    •JPN retail 1,000 JPY



    •The first scale kit of Saachez's AEU Enact Custom from "Mobile Suit Gundam 00".

    •Utilizes ABS joints for maintaining delicate proportions and allowing transformation into flight mode.

    •Distinctive faceplate utilizes translucent parts.

    •Blade rifle, missile pod, defense rod, and plasma sword are included.

    •Blade rifle features sliding blade; missile pod can be attached.
