    1/100 Astrea Gundam

    •JPN retail 2,800 JPY

    •Release of the 1/100 scale kit of the “Gundam Astraea” featured in the “Mobile Suit Gundam 00P” series currently being serialized in Dengeki Hobby Magazine.

    •Includes new features such as shoulder joints that can extend outward, allowing dramatic poses as seen in the animated series.

    •Waist features axial cover parts for recreating off-center poses.

    •Weapons include GN beam rifle, GN beam sabers, shield, prototype GN sword, and GN launcher.

     

     1/144Hg Throne Drei Gundam

    •JPN retail 1,600 JPY

    •An HG version of the “Gundam Throne Drei” from “Mobile Suit Gundam 00.”

    •Backpack and GN shield pod feature a hatch-opening mechanism.

    •In addition to thigh joints with improved articulation, the torso can lean forward and back for angled poses.

    •The cord built into the GN hand launcher can be extended and connected to the HG Gundam Throne Eins (sold separately) to accurately portray the GN High Mega Launcher.


     1/144HG Nadleeh Gundam

    •JPN retail 1,200 JPY

    •An HG version of the “Gundam Nadlee” from “Mobile Suit Gundam 00”

    •Distinctive lens unit is molded in translucent plastic.

    •In addition to thigh joints with improved articulation, the torso can lean forward and back for angled poses.

    •Includes new GN beam rifle, GN beam saber, and shield.

    •Can also hold the GN cannons of the HG Gundam Virtue (sold separately) to replicate the Gundam Nadleeh’s armament in the animation.
