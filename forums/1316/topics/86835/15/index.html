    New translations from Animage GSD questions. Many mysteries explained!
    http://blog.yam.com/ekliptiko/archives/655916.html

    Another Chinese Traditional translation from the nice guy at that website, this time for "GSD 20 questions" on 2005 Nov Animage magazine.

    Don't have time to translate due to my exams, but we did get confirmation that each PLANT colony housed 500000 people, with about 100 Plants. This makes 50 million in total.

    Human characters are named by Fukuda's wife.

    The MS weapon naming rules are thus:
    ZAFT uses Latin and Italian.
    EA uses English and German
    European/Asian department of EA uses French.

    The fact that ZAFT forces have no clear ranks was an idea borrowed from the old Israel army...

    Messiah was built from a hollowed former mining asteroid.

    The Red Haro was imitated from the other Haros left at Lacus' old home. Lacus only took the Pink-chan with her.

    The armour of Akatsuki was too expensive to build more than one, and no, that's NOT gold foil.

    Rey is another clone of Al Da Fllaga, but his fetus was not "woken up" until much later. Rey has no genetic differences to Rau. (which means he wasn't an improved model, and likely have the same genetic illnesses that plagued Rau.)

    Neo had false memories implanted, though how the Mu memory went is unknown.

    The "Angel Bath" had the mineral salts of the bath water imported from ORB.

    SEED is mostly activated by extreme stress. Like sudden bursts of strength people get in emergencies. The script made it impossible for anyone but the younger soldiers to go SEED. So there is an age component as well symbolising "bright future".

    Confirmed importrant: Coordinators partners had genetic clashes with each other in their fetus/fertilised egg, lowering chances of a sucessful pregnancy. Genetic compatability has now become a requirement for marrying legally in PLANT, which means there is no such thing as true love in PLANT legal society anymore. Ironically, Patrick Zala was one of those people who believed that science will solve everything, and don't believe interbreeding with Naturals is a good solution. Gilbert was actually one of the Geneticists hired by Patrick Zala to help solve the genetic birth issues.

    What is Destiny Plan?
    A world where everyone had their life determined genetically. There will be no conflicts because there won't need to be arguments on how things should be done. Gilbert had in mind Destiny Plan working with the people following ir by choice. He didn't plan to force his plans on people.
    (My notes: THAT's the problem. Gilbert had wanted everyone to choice his plan, yet still free to choice what they wished. That doesn't make sense... It's like saying everyone can freely vote for the ruler, as long as they all vote for ME.)

    What happens to the PLANT government now?
    No offical agreement, but the production crew believe Yzak had a good chance to take over. Regadless of his personality, Yzak is respected in PLANT high society.

    (I don't translate everything, because some of it... Like info on Terminal... is common knowlege by now.
