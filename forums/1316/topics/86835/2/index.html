    DESTINY info from this month's anime mags
    According to reports on Animage posted on 2ch's ongoing SEED DESTINY netabare threads, the first episode will be titled "Okoreru Hitomi" ("Angry Eyes" or "Eyes That Get Angry"). Other staff credits...
    Production: Satoshi Toba
    Storyboards: Mitsuo Fukuda
    Character animation director: Hisashi Hirai
    Mecha animation director: Yousuke Kabashima

    The Junius Treaty was signed in the first half of C.E. 72 at the place where Junius Seven once stood onboard an Earth Alliance battleship. There, ZAFT built a monument to the treaty. The details of the treaty itself state that both sides were to ban the use of Neutron Jammer Cancellers and the Mirage Colloid. Both sides also agreed to set a quota restraint on the size of their MS forces. Despite that, the two continued to research and develop new weapons due to the lingering bitterness between them. Because of the MS force limit, an emphasis on quality rather than quantity came about, leading to more advanced MS.

    And this month's Newtype had the following descriptions of Destiny characters:

    Neo Roanoke - An Earth Alliance captain. He commands the special forces group that Stellar's team belongs to. He's deeply trusted by subordinates.

    Lord Jibril - Blue Cosmos' new leader. Through his power, the organization has made a comeback in might. He plans the extermination of Coordinators. Seiyuu: Hideyuki Hori (George Glen from Gundam SEED, Scwartz + Kyoji from G Gundam)

    Notable info from the Q&A portion:
    - What happened to Kira?
    Kira, Lacus, and Miriallia appear in the OP, but that doesn't necessarily mean they will appear in the series. (Similar to Amuro and Char appearing in the ZZ OP).

    - Dearka and Yzak?
    No comment.

    - Characteristics of Luna and Shin?
    The relationship between Luna and Shin is more like mother-in-law and son than sister and brother. For instance, Luna always gets on about Shin's dress code. Although Shin has a dark past, he doesn't get down on himself like Athrun.

    - About Rey Za Barrel?
    He's a leader and roommate of Shin's. Cool and meticulous as well.

    Lastly, DESTINY, GUNDAM, and ZAKU are all acronyms for this series. Â¶ 4:45 AM
