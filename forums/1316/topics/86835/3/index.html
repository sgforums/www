    Gundam Ace DESTINY info
    Some info from the latest issue of Gundam Ace is out there:

    Mobile weapons
    GAT-02L2 Dagger L - The Earth Alliance force's mass production MS. An improved version of the Dagger with an enhanced armament.

    ZGMF-601R GuAIZ R - ZAFT force's mass-produced MS. A GuAIZ with minor changes, the shield and extensional arrestors on the waist were replaced with rail guns.

    TFA-4DE GaZaOOT - ZAFT forces heavy MS, an improved ZuOOT. The firepower is strengthened with the beam cannons on both shoulders and the large missles, which replaced the 2-barrel auxiliary cannons.

    Neo Roanoke's mobile armor - The romaji for this is "eguzasu". Come up with your own fun English spellings, kids! Equipped with 2 rail guns and 4 all-range attack weapons.

    Characters
    Cagalli Yula Athha - After the battle of Jachin Due, the Orb Union once again gains independence. The daughter of the Union's leader, Uzumi, becomes the new representative head. In order to prevent any outbreak of future wars, she works hard by meeting various groups. However, some say she became the leader by being used as a political puppet.

    Neo Roanoke - Earth Alliance officer with rank of Colonel (Captain using Navy ranks). Leader of the special forces and loyal military person. Always wears the mask to hide his face.

    Arthur Trine - Commissioned officer of ZAFT. XO of Minerva and Shin's superior.

    Lord Jibril - The new leader of Blue Cosmos, an organization with the intent of purging all coordinators. The organization gains new life under his leadership. His goals are to eliminate the coordinators and setup a new ideal world.

    Ian Lee - Belongs to the Earth Alliance's special forces. He serves as the captain of the Gardi Ruu. He trusts Neo's command of the unit and serves as a tactical assistant. His rank is Major. Seiyuu: Tadahisa Saizen

    Mad Abes - The leader of the Minerva's MS staff. A middle-aged man with the spirit of a true artisan. Seiyuu: Taiten Kusunoki

    Vino Dupre - A 17-year-old boy with the ZAFT forces. Part of the mechanic crew of the new warship Minerva. Was busy preparing for the ceremonial launch of Minerva. Being rushed into combat, he couldn't hide his puzzlement from the Earth Alliance's raid. Usually, he has a cheerful personality and is the one to loosen others around.

    Friday, September 24, 2004
    Gundam Ace info

    DESTINY Astray
    The official DESTINY sidestory begins with this issue with the same cast in charge of the Astray manga returning. 5 months have passed since the Junius Seven Treaty. Matias hires Jess Rabble to take a scoop/pic of ZAFT's former secret weapon, Genesis Alpha (a smaller-sized Genesis). Once Jess reaches where the Genesis Alpha is located, he was captured by Gai and handed over to Lowe. Here, the Junkyard Guild was busy equipping Genesis Alpha onto ReHome as an engine. Meanwhile, ZAFT forces attacked Genesis Alpha in order to hide the evidence that it existed. The attacks were defended against by Gai and Canard's Dreadnaught Eta and ZAFT retreated after Jess took a pic of Genesis Alpha.

    Characters
    Jess Rabble - 20-year-old natural and photo journalist. He pilots Raysta, a civilian-use MS built by the Junkyard Guild. He uses the gun camera to take photos of the battlefield. Carries a goggle around all the time to protect his eyes.

    Kaite Madigan - 22-year-old Coordinator and a professional MS pilot. He is not associated with any faction and is more of a mercenary. He's always wearing a suit even while piloting a MS.

    Matias - Mysterious client. Natural. Age and real name are unknown. Owns a big mansion and hires Jess.

    Mecha
    ZGMF-1017AS GINN Assault - Equipped with Duel's Assault Shroud. Weapons, armor, and propulsion are all upgraded. However, not a lot were produced. Piloted by Madigan.

    MWF-JG71 Raysta - Serial number is short for "Main Works Figure Junkyard Guild Type 71". Raysta is derived from the word Astray and means "Civilian-use Astray". Raysta was built using data gathered from Astrays in the previous war. The MS is built from various junk parts. The Junkyard Guild has Raysta for sale or rental. However, it cannot be used for war purposes.

    Gundam Astray Out Frame - MS discovered by Lowe at ZAFT's Genesis Alpha. During its discovery, it was not equipped with armor. Therefore, Lowe named it Out Frame. No word on if this MS was built or stolen by ZAFT. Lowe modified Out Frame as a construction-use MS equipped with no weapons. Lowe then gave it to Jess.

    Dreadnaught Eta - Modified X Astray (aka Dreadnaught Gundam) piloted by Canard. The Dragoon System was taken out. The MS was designed by Lowe.

    Gundam Legacy - Midnight Fenrir/ Desert Rommel Edition
    Continuation of the Midnight Fenrir/Desert Rommel story. Rommel decides to attack the EFF, who are demanding the Zeon forces to surrender as the cease fire treaty was already signed. After the first skirmish, Rommel decides to retreat to reorganize his troops while Garret realizes the EFF are telling the truth. Garret agrees to act as decoy for Rommel. Meanwhile Lt. Agar (of EFF) agrees to become a negotiator in hopes to end this stand off.
