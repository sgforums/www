    The last link has a description of the MS IGLOO 2 promo video, taking pictures or videos of which was strictly prohibited: After the title logo, sounds of gunfire can be heard. One after the other, Zeon forces descend to the Earth. Wappas and Zakus emerge from the HLVs. Kycilia Zabi gives a speech. Earth Federation foot soldiers fire handguns & rifles. An Earth Fed battalion clashes with the Zeon.

    Commentary by the writer of the report says they got goosebumps watching it. The scale was different from the first IGLOO, with a considerably large battlefield in a vast plain area. You could get a sense of the hugeness of the Zakus as they were depicted being looked up at from below. Though they didn't stand out, Type 61 tanks could be seen.
