    Osamu Wakai to wed
    Sponichi Annex reports comedian Osamu Wakai, famous for his Amuro Ray impersonation, recently announced his upcoming marriage to a 28-year-old "Sayla Mass-esque" department store employee. The couple was planning to submit their marriage registration on July 7 but decided to hold off for one day (until 7/8) in homage to the enduring RX-78-2 Gundam to symbolize a long-lasting bond. Wakai's proposal was also inspired by the scene in Mobile Suit Gundam in which Sleggar Law gives Mirai Yashima his mother's ring.
