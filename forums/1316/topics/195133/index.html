    UPDATED C.E.73 Stargazer updated its mechanic page with GSX-401 Stargazer and character page with Mudie Holcroft and Shams Couza.

    GSX-401FW Stargazer
    "The GSX-401FW Stargazer is a mobile suit developed independently by DSSD. Holding a neutral status with regards to both the Earth Alliance & PLANT, DSSD created the unit as a non-combat mobile suit with the objective of exploring space beyond Mars. It's most notable features are the interplanetary propulsion system known as the Voiture Lumiere and its ability to be used largely unmanned thanks to its high degree of autonomy. The machine itself as well as its operation support system are collectively designated as 'Guider UNmanned Deployment Autonomic Manipulation'. The abbreviation for this is 'GUNDAM'."

    Mudie Holcroft
    "18-year-old female. Natural. Phantom Pain mobile suit pilot. Rank: 2nd Lieutenant. Belongs to the same MS platoon as Sven. Orphaned when she was very young and underwent complete training as a soldier for anti-Coordinator warfare at an institution for children. Her make-up is as eccentric as her personality. Always tense and even though she appears frank with others, that's only on the surface. She's missing an essential human sentiment and that mentality prevents her from understanding everyone else and, perhaps, herself as well. When in combat, she exterminates her enemies completely without mercy. Piloting the GAT-X1022 Blu Duel, she excels in close-combat."

    Shams Couza
    "19-year-old male. Natural. Phantom Pain mobile suit pilot. Rank: Lieutenant Junior Grade. Belongs to the same MS platoon as Sven. As with his comrades, orphaned when he was very young and underwent training as a soldier for anti-Coordinator warfare at an institution for children. A cynic who consistently views everything from a defiant standpoint. Flies the long-range artillery mobile suit GAT-X103AP Verde Buster but is an all-rounder who can also skillfully handle close combat. Wears glasses but these are just plano lenses for show as his eyesight seems to be fine."

    GAT-01A2R 105 Slaughter Dagger
    "The GAT-01A2R 105 Slaughter Dagger is the primary mobile suit of Phantom Pain, the Earth Alliance forces' informal 81st Independent Mobile Battalion. The prototype was the Alliance's mass-production model 105 Dagger. Phantom Pain, being under Logos' direct control within the Earth Alliance and not the regular military's, has its own distinct criteria regarding choice of weaponry. As a result, they employ both the standard military's GAT-04 Windam as well as the 105 Slaughter Dagger. The unit has Phantom Pain's own minor changes from the original 105 Dagger, such as the use of the AQM/E-X01 Aile Striker as a standard armament. Developed for mid-range combat mobility, the Aile Striker equipment was battle-proven by the exploits of the Alliance's first mobile suit ace pilot, Kira Yamato. With the combination of the 105 Dagger base unit and the Aile Striker, the resulting versatility & reliability can be said to be a highly aggressive system configuration."

    "16-years-old. Male. 1st generation Coordinator. GSX-401FW Stargazer test pilot. 2 years ago, he became orphaned when he lost his DSSD staff member parents in an accident in space.
