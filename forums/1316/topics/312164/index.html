    Gunpla Expo in Nagoya
    Nagoya Parco's website has details for the upcoming Gunpla Expo in Nagoya. From April 25 through May 11, the event will be held at Nagoya Parco's Parco Gallery. As in previous years, the Expo will be divided up into various zones and there'll be limited edition Gunpla (1/60 Gundam Exia Clear Color Version, MG Zaku II Ver. 2.0 Real Type Color Specs, HG Gundam Exia Metallic Version, BB Senshi Sangokuden Ryuubi Gundam Metallic Version, etc.).
