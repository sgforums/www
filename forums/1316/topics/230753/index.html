    Info from this week's Famitsu. By the number of titles in the top 100, Bandai Namco Games ranked #2 with 15 games (behind Nintendo, with 30). By sales numbers, Bandai Namco ranked #4 with 4,220,898 units sold. Rankings of Gundam-relevant titles:

    29) Mobile Suit Gundam SEED Destiny: Alliance Vs. ZAFT II Plus - 440,432 copies sold
    4 SD Gundam G Generation Portable - 271,425 copies sold
    53) Mobile Suit Gundam Climax U.C. - 241,597 copies sold
    7 Another Century's Episode 2 - 181,052 copies sold

    Additionally, SD Gundam G Generation Portable was the #2 selling game for the PSP in 2006 while Gundam: Target In Sight was the #2 selling game for the PS3, with 96,714 copies sold.
