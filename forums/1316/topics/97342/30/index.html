     1/100 MG Launcher/Strike Gundam

    •JPN retail 5,000 JPY



    •The highly anticipated MG version of the popular “Strike Gundam” with “Launcher Striker” and “Sword Striker” equipment.

    •Each Striker pack can be attached to the Strike Gundam to create Launcher Strike Gundam and Sword Strike Gundam variations.

    •In Sword Strike Gundam mode, the “Midas Messer” weapon is detachable from the left arm, and the Piercer Lock on the tip of the “Panzer Eisen” weapon can be mounted on a wire to re-create it being launched.

    •In Launcher Striker Gundam mode, the Agni’s support arm is flexible, allowing it to be posed in scenes from the animation.

    •Includes two “Armor Schneider” and a shield.
