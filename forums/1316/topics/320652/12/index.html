     1/100 Cherudim Gundam

    • JPN retail 2,600 JPY

    • The Cherudim Gundam joins the lineup of 1/100 scale precision models of Mobile Suits from Gundam 00.
    • Shoulders incorporate a swing mechanism for enhanced mobility forward, back, up, and down, allowing for dramatic "aim and shoot" poses.
    • Forehead-mounted sensor covers open for sniper mode, and the "follow screen" is molded in translucent plastic.
    • GN shield bits can be removed and placed on a stand to re-enact scenes from the show.
    • Includes GN sniper rifle II, and two GN beam pistol II guns.

     1/144 HG O-RAISER

    • JPN retail 800 JPY

    •  An HG series kit of the "0-Raiser" support unit from Gundam 00.
    • Can be combined with the HG Gundam 00 (sold separately) to form the 00 Raiser.
    • Included base can be used with other Gundam 00 HG kits as well. Can also be connected to the Action Base 2 (sold separately).
    • Includes GN Sword II connector parts and two GN Shield II.

     1/144 HG GN ARCHER

    • JPN retail 1,200 JPY

    • The transforming and combining GN Archer support mecha joins the HG series.
    • Includes special parts to allow it to be connected to the HG Arios Gundam (sold separately) to form the Archer Arios.
    • Transforms from support vehicle to Mobile Suit mode.
    • Includes two GN rifles.
    • Includes an all-original feature: the ability to combine with 00 Gundam and the 00 Raiser!

     1/144 HG GADESSA

    • JPN retail 1,500 JPY

    • The Gadessa from "Mobile Suit Gundam" 00 joins the HG 1/144 scale lineup.
    • The Gadessa's trademark giant cannon can be mounted or removed for firing, and opens to replicate "full burst" mode.
    • Utilizes new miniaturized polycaps for enhanced posability, allowing it to take poses just like in the show!
    • Ankle units open/transform for standing on the ground.
    • Can be displayed on special base.

     1/144 HG GUNDAM EXIA (TRANS-AM MODE)
    GLOSS INJECTION Ver.

    • JPN retail 1,200 JPY

    • An HG series kit of the Gundam Exia in "Trans-Am Mode," as seen in the first season of "Mobile Suit Gundam 00."
    • Characteristic lens parts and shoulder belt units replicated with separate materials.
    • Upper thigh joints utilize a three-ball joint system! Enhanced vertical and horizontal movement for smooth posing!
    • Weapons include a GN sword, GN longblade, GN shortblade, GN shield, two GN beam sabers, and two GN beam daggers.
    • Foil stickers are printed in appropriate colors for Trans-Am mode.

     1/144 HG GUNDAM DYNAMES (TRANS-AM MODE)
    GLOSS INJECTION Ver.

    • JPN retail 1,200 JPY

    • An HG series version of Gundam Dynames in "Trans-Am" colors.
    • Head antenna units slide to replicate sniper mode.
    • GN full shield can break down into three parts; even when oriented to cover the front of the Mobile Suit, a portion can be opened for equipping of weapons.
    • Includes a GN sniper rifle, two GN full shields, two GN shields, and two beam sabers (hilt only)
    • Foil stickers are printed in appropriate colors for Trans-Am mode.

     1/144 HG GUNDAM KYRIOS (TRANS-AM MODE)
    GLOSS INJECTION Ver.

    • JPN retail 1,500 JPY

    • An HG series kit of the Gundam Kyrios in "Trans-Am Mode," as seen in the first season of "Mobile Suit Gundam 00."
    • Faithfully transforms into flight mode.
    • Shield tips can be opened horizontally.
    • Includes GN submachine gun, shield, anti-MS rifle, and two beam sabers (hilts only).
    • Foil stickers are printed in appropriate colors for Trans-Am mode.

     1/144 HG GUNDAM VIRTUE (TRANS-AM MODE)
    GLOSS INJECTION Ver.

    • JPN retail 1,500 JPY

    • An HG series kit of the Gundam Virtue in "Trans-Am Mode," as seen in the first season of "Mobile Suit Gundam 00."
    • GN cannon barrel can extend, and the connector arm is posable, allowing for dramatic shooting poses from the show.
    • GN cannon and leg unit sides slide to open into GN field mode.
    • GN bazooka barrel can slide to replicate "burst mode."
    • Foil stickers are printed in appropriate colors for Trans-Am mode.
