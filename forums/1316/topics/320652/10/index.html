     1/100 OO RAISER

    • JPN retail 5,000 JPY

    • A special Gundam 00 set that can be enjoyed in combined form or displayed separately.
    • Set includes a 1/100 Gundam 00, a 1/100 0-Raiser, and a Celestial Being action base.
    • Shoulder-mounted GN Drive units light up.
    • Gundam 00 and O-Raiser can be combined.
    • Included action base is molded in an exclusive color.

     

     1/100 O-Raiser

    • JPN retail 1,600 JPY

    • A 1/100 scale kit of the "0-Raiser" support unit from Gundam 00!
    • Can be combined with the 1/100 Gundam 00 (sold separately)
    • Features realistic aerodynamic proportions in flight mode.
    • Utilizes translucent parts for the canopy and other areas.

     1/144 HG ARIOS GUNDAM

    • JPN retail 1,500 JPY

    • The Arios Gundam from Mobile Suit Gundam 00 joins the HG series lineup!
    • Transforms from Mobile Suit mode into flight mode.
    • In flight mode, the leading edge can spread apart.
    • Weapons include two GN Beam Sabers, one GN Rifle, and Gatling Gun.

     

     1/144 HG Mr.BUSHIDO'S AHEAD

    • JPN retail 1,200 JPY

    • A highly anticipated scale model of Mr. Bushido's Ahead!
    • A faithful rendition of this masked man's unique Mobile Suit.
    • Includes two GN Beam Sabers and a shield.

     

    ACTION BASE 1 CELESTIAL BEING Ver.

    • JPN retail 700 JPY

    • An Action Base designed to display 1/100 scale Gundam 00 series kits.
    • Base features the Celestial Being emblem.
    • Arm unit can be adjusted for angle and height.
