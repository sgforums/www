     1/100 OO GUNDAM

    •JPN retail 2,800 JPY

    •A 1/100 scale model of the "00 Gundam." GN Drives on both shoulders light up!
    •GN Drives incorporate integrated light units that require no wiring work, and they light up at the flip of a switch.
    •Features even easier to build construction that combines both style and posability.
    •Upper thigh joint system uses a swing-action construction that, in combination with rotating thigh units and posable GN Drive units, allows the kit to take famous poses from the show.
    •Weapons include two GN swords and two GN shields.

     

     1/144 HG SERAVEE GUNDAM

    •JPN retail 1,600 JPY

    •A Gundam with a face on its back! The "Seravee Gundam" from "Mobile Suit Gundam 00" joins the HG series!
    •Back unit can open to replicate "full drive" mode to reveal a movable face.
    •Included GN Bazooka II can be combined with GN Cannon to replicate Burst Mode.

     

     1/144 HG AHEAD

    •JPN retail 1,200 JPY

    •The Ahead, cutting-edge Mobile Suit of the "Arrows," joins the HG Series!
    •Distinctive chest pattern utilizes a two-layer construction of translucent parts covered by opaque parts.
    •All joints and back thrusters are flexible allowing for a variety of dramatic poses.
    •GN Rifle can be converted into a long-barrel type.
    •Includes GN Rifle, GN Shield, GN Beam Saber, and two anti-personnel "automatons" with container unit.
