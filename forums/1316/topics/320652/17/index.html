    1/144 HG TRANS-AM RAISER GLOSS INJECTION Ver.

    • JPN retail 2,000 JPY

    • A Gloss Injection Version of the powerful Trans-Am Raiser with special parts joins the Mobile Suit Gundam 00 series!
    • A set including an 00 Gundam and O-Raiser molded in gloss injection and using holographic stickers to replicate the color during Trans-Am mode.
    • 00 Gundam and O-Raiser can be connected or disconnected.
    • The newly designed GN Sword III which can transform from sword to rifle mode features a translucent plastic blade that can also be covered with beam effect parts.
    • Also includes 2 GN Sword II, 2 effect parts for the GN Sword II units, GN Sword II connector parts, 2 GN Shield II units, and a specially designed stand.

     

    1/144 HG AHEAD SMULTRON

    • JPN retail 1,200 JPY

    • A custom Ahead variation designed for those who can harness Quantum Brainwaves: the Ahead Smultron.
    • Distinctive back-mounted thruster unit can be swiveled.
    • Head sensor unit and facemask are molded in translucent plastic.
    • GN short rifle includes conversion parts for turning it into a GN rifle.
    • Includes GN rifle, shield, and GN beam saber.

     

    BB SENSHI No.322 00 RAISER

    • JPN retail 1,000 JPY

    • A BB Senshi version of the 00 Raiser with original features for added play value!
    • Not only can the O-Raiser and 00 Gundam be docked - they can also be formed into all-new BB Senshi original modes, including Bird Mode, Fighter Mode, and Rider Mode!
    • GN Sword II includes effect parts for beam saber mode.
    • Includes 2 GN Swords and 2 GN Shield II units.
