    1/100 SERAVEE GUNDAM

    • JPN retail 3,600 JPY

    • A 1/100 scale kit of the Seravee Gundam from "Mobile Suit Gundam 00."
    • The Seravee Gundam's distinctive block construction is replicated in perfect detail, allowing disassembly and transformation!
    • Shoulder and knee mounted GN cannons can be posed and accessory hands can be attached to replicate the distinctive hidden arms.
    • Accessories include a GN Bazooka II and GN beam saber.
    • GN Bazooka II can be placed in Burst Mode, Twin Rifle, or Twin Buster Cannon modes.
    • Includes a "joint base" that is compatible with those included with Seravee Gundam and Seraphim Gundam.

     

    1/144 HG GARAZZO

    • JPN retail 1,500 JPY

    • The Garazzo, piloted by the Innovators, joins the HG series lineup!
    • Shield opens to replicate GN Field generation.
    • Accessory hands include gripping, flat palm, and beam-saber-holding versions.
    • Includes a display stand.
