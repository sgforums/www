     1/144 HG 00GUNDAM

    •JPN retail 1,000 JPY

    •The main Gundam 00 unit from the second season of Mobile Suit Gundam 00 joins the HG series.
    •Utilizes new polycap technology for enhanced style and super posability while maintaining ideal proportions.
    •Shoulder-mounted GN drives are linked to the frame to allow lateral movement along with the backpack.
    •Features clear parts in specific areas for the best visual effect.
    •Includes two GN sword IIs, and expressive open hand parts.
    •GN Sword II can be transformed into sword/rifle form by swapping parts.

     1/144 HG GN ARMS TYPE D + GUNDAM DYNAMES

    •JPN retail 6,000 JPY

    •Highly anticipated by fans! A kit of the heavy weapons system GN Arms Type D and a newly rearmed Gundam Dynames.
    •The giant cannon on the right side of the GN Arms system GN can be folded away; the missile pod on the left side features a hatch that can slide open.
    •Dynames includes a handgun that can be placed in a leg-mounted holster.
    •Features an in-scale Lockon Stratos figure that can be placed atop the GN cannon to replicate the dramatic "last shooting" scene from the series.

     1/144 HG SAACHEZ'S AEU ENACT CUSTOM (MORALIA TYPE)

    •JPN retail 1,000 JPY

    •Saachez's AEU Enact Custom (Moralia Type), the sword-carrying foe of Gundam Exia, joins the HG series lineup!
    •Color-molded plastic replicates the suit's distinctive blue coloring right out of the box.
    •Utilizes ABS joints throughout, allowing transformation to flight mode while maintaining accurate proportions in Mobile Suit mode.
    •Includes blade rifle, missile pods, defense rod, and plasma sword.
