    Gundam Crisis opening ceremonies info, press preview

    Gundam Crisis official site posted info for the grand opening of the attraction on July 21. Guests Yoshiyuki Tomino, Toru Furuya, and Shuuichi Ikeda will all be on hand for two appearances during the day, including a talk show and lottery drawings for signed Gundam goods. Sunrise Gundam Division producer Shigeru Horiguchi will also appear.

    OCN Anime Special Effects blog also has coverage of a press preview for Gundam Crisis. 200 members of the press and Sotsu Agency & Sunrise representatives were in attendance. Participants in other Fuji-Q attractions, such as AKB48, appeared as well as Nagano Olympic bronze medalist Tomomi Okazaki who, dressed in Earth Federation military garb, escorted press members.
