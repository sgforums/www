    Some bits from an interview with Mizushima on the latest NT issue:

    Other interesting notes from that Mizushima Interview:

    1. 00 was a project that starts with the mechs being the main focus.
    2. They also has plans to make it into a historical drama.
    3. The events of 911 was taken into consideration and thus turn the spotlight onto the topic on "effects of war on the world"
    4. Mizushima only watched 0079, 0083 and Turn A before. He wanted to do a continuation of the UC timeline, but was shot down and thus the AD Timeline.
    5. If everyone had noticed, all the protagonists suddenly turn silent of late. If they had caught themselves in a sticky suitation, their survival instinct will the main point of view.
    6. There was talk of romance in the production stage, but after being bog down by other parts of the scenarior, that aspect was left on the cutting room's floor. So don't expect any.
    7. The combination of Isaku Okabe (A Military Critic) Tomohiro Chiba (Good Friend Of Kuroda) and Kenji Teraoka (A Realist Animator) had some stunning results on the script. But almost 90% of the parts arn't finalised for animation due to a variety of factors.
    8. The OP animations has hidden a lot of hints about what's coming from the start.
    9. As to why Kuroda was picked as the screenplay writter, it's because of people's recommendation. However, confilct between Mizushima and him later. So what Mizushima revealed is that Kuroda is solely responsible for the Screenplay for the first quater to half of the series.
    10. Mizushima consider himself as more of a hands-on director. As in instead of being a director who solely follows a script that is written, he's open to changing it later on based on the suitation.
