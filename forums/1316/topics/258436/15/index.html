    Gundam 00 prequel drama CD announced
    Sunrise gives news of a Gundam 00 drama CD called "Mission-2306 -Mobile Suit Gundam 00 Another Story-" to be released on July 23. Scripted by Yousuke Kuroda and supervised by Seiji Mizushima, the story as given on the site:

    "Celestial Being...a private armed organization that conducts military operations without regard to race, country, or religion to rid the world of strife. A new mission is given to a Gundam Meister from Celestial Being, Setsuna F. Seiei: to prevent the assassination of Barry Halevy, the leader of a fossil fuel export regulation watchdog group. As part of the mission, he's responsible for guard & surveillance to ensure no harm comes to Halevy's beloved daughter, Louise, from terrorist organizations. But there are people in high school with one or two peculiarities beyond Setsuna's imagining. A love triangle?! And a perverted teacher?! Can Setsuna safely guard this important individual while dealing with these...??"

    Cast: Setsuna F. Seiei, Lockon Stratos, Allelujah/Hallelujah Haptism, Tieria Erde, Sumeragi Lee Noriega, Graham Aker, Billy Katagiri, Saji Crossroad, Louise Halevy, and narrator (Toru Furuya).

    Gundam 00 Vol. 7 special details
    Bandai Visual's site says the final volume of the first season of Gundam 00 (out July 25) will, in addition to episodes 23-25, contain 15-minutes of special video consisting of the following:

    - A new SD anime short
    - A special second season promo video (Seiji Mizushima interview + a second season newsflash)
    - Footage from the first episode preview screenings from September of last year

    Also included will be a digital gallery and audio commentary on episode #25 from Mamoru Miyano, Shinichiro Miki, Hiroyuki Yoshino, Hiroshi Kamiya, and Seiji Mizushima.

    Though the audio commentary for selected episodes is a regular feature for the Blu-ray edition, it only began being included on the DVD release as well starting with Vol. 5:

    Vol. 5, episode 17 - Katsuyuki Konishi, Daisuke Namikawa, Rie Kugimiya, and Seiji Mizushima
    Vol. 6, episode 21 - Youko Honna, Ayahi Takagaki, Arise Satou, and Seiji Mizushima
