    New "offical" GN Drive information

    1) Schenberg only define the "end goal" - how CB get there, he don't care about.

    2) Corner is from family of observers.

    3) Observers can override Veda decisions.

    4) Schenberg design various "fail-safe" systems, like the Trial system from

    Nadleeh.

    5) Observers can come from families that have been observers, or from "powerful"

    figures at the time.

    6) Corner find out the true goal of CB, then add in his own ambitions to it. The

    "normal" observers don't know the true goal of CB.

    7) Thrones and GN-X are produced under the orders of Corner. Corners have

    connections with business and political side. GN-X uses current production parts

    to build.

    8) GN-X is a general purpose MS (unlike the original 4 Gundams).

    9) The GN Tau Drives can produce GN particles if it's hooked up to a generator.

    It CANNOT produce GN particles when there is no "electric charge".

    10) The "real" GN Drive cannot stop producing GN particles once it start to

    produce them.

    11) "Real" GN Drive can produce infinite energy, but cannot produce large amount

    at one time. Various "round" shape on the Gundam's arm is supposed to be used to

    stored the "extra" energy generated. The "add-on" Dynames uses in episode 5 are

    round shaped, and it is used to store large amount of GN particles.

    12) The true goal of Schenberg and how it is proceeding currently will be a

    focal point later on.

    13) Veda is supposed to consider human race's long term survival as the goal -

    it is not restricted to the lifepsan of a normal human (probably means it can

    accept lots of death currently provided later on, human race survive).

    14) There is a reason for Sergei's scar.

    15) A lot of the unanswered question in series 1 will be answered in series 2,

    at least the important ones.

    16) GN Tau Drive will not produce GN particles anymore once the "electric

    charge" went out.

    17) GN particles and T GN particles have different properties and color, but how

    they are different is still a mystery.

    18) Nadleeh's trail system is to prevent a Gundam going berserk.

The GN Arms can use many kinds of different equipment suitable for the Gundam connected to it. To date, two units have been manufactured: the Exia-use Type-E and the Dynames-use Type D."
