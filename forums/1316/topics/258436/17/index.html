    Famitsu.com Gundam Meisters article

    Famitsu.com has another article on the PS2 game detailing various features.
    The 00 Mode, invoked when you fill up and activate your 0 Gauge, seems to be a renamed version of the previously described Action Movie system. You continue raising your level until the 00 Mode ends when a mistake is made in button input. When the Mode ends, if you've reached beyond a certain level, an Action Movie can be triggered. If you reach the maximum level, a two-unit Formation Attack is carried out.
    There are 4 kinds of Action Movies: Single Hit Certain Kill (push the button when a moving cursor reaches the center point of a vertical gauge), Timing (push the button in time to an extending, rounded bar on screen), Barrage (press the button as fast as you can in a certain amount of time), and Multiple Button Fast Push (press the indicated sequence of buttons as fast as possible).
    The game will also feature an original opening animation sequence set to music by the brilliant green though it's not clear if the song will be "Ash Like Snow" or something new.
