    This month's 00 side story mecha
    Note: the following is taken from 2ch so there's potential for false info but at the very least the names Sadalsuud and Gundam Dynames Torpedo are confirmed by content listings for Dengeki Hobby and Hobby Japan.

    00V
    GN-002/DG014 Gundam Dynames Torpedo - Has sensors on its left shoulder & face and anti-underwater equipment with a huge, larger-than-body-height torpedo launcher that conceals the right arm. Can shoot submerged enemies without entering the water. It appears to fire GN particle-charged GN torpedoes but as the torpedoes themselves remain undiscovered, the facts aren't certain.

    00P
    Prototype Gundam Unit No. 2, Gundam Sadalsuud - Scouting & command specialized prototype equipped with sensors all over its entire body. As it is a sensor-using test unit, it has a meager armament and an equal lack of armor. For the time being, it is the prototype of the Dynames.

    Character:
    Gundam Meister 874
    One of Celestal Being's four Gundam Meisters. Though Meisters generally use codenames, she's called by a number in her case. Doesn't appear in front of other members and apparently has a secret of some kind. Outwardly, she looks to be in her early teens and, if she is as old as she appears, as a mobile suit pilot so young, she is the best of the best.

    00F
    Gundam Sadalsuud F - This unit is reinforced with weaponry and armor to a degree that doesn't compromise the prototype Sadalsuud's sensor effectiveness. Nonetheless, it isn't suited mainly for combat.
