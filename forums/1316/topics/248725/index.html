  • Gordonator
    16 Apr 07, 23:17
    1. What equipments do you use when fixing a gundam model kit?
    Cutting parts from runner -> Pliers(or is there better alternative?)
    Ans: i use plier. it's the best choice for all modelers.

    Trimming the nubs -> Penknife not very effective when trimming hard nubs and left some unsightly marks
    Ans: i use penknife and i am very satistified with the result.

    Holding small parts -> Twizzers but can't seem to hold on to small parts properly
    Ans: i use my hands. just be extra careful.

    2. Do you put stickers on the model?
    Ans: i only put on decals and i try to aviod putting sticker cos as time past, dust will collect inside the sticker and make the model looks dirty and sticky.

    i always believe simple is beautiful. a few decals will do. over putting too much also not nice.

    3. Do you file or sand the nubs?What grit do you use?
    Ans: i don't do sanding. no time.

    4. Are the fingers strong enough to hold the weapons?
    Ans: Good question. MG hands are indeed not strong enough to hold the weapons, especially rifles. that's why i use double sided tape to take the weapons to the hands.

    5. What brand and model marker do you recommend for panelling?
    Ans: oil based extra fine Gundam marker from japan. $3.90. easy to use and erasable.

    6. Do you fill up the seams with putty?
    Ans: No. that's require lots of skills.

    7. Do you protect your model with spray finishing?
    Ans: No.
