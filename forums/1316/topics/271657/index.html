    Asahi.com's English site has a pic and brief news blurb about the Gundam Tachi-Neputa mentioned back in March.


    A gigantic paper replica of robotic armor from Mobile Suit Gundam, a popular TV anime series, is completed Tuesday in Goshogawara, Aomori Prefecture. The 11-meter-tall, 1-ton paper doll will be put up on a float for the Tachi-Neputa festival to be held in the city from Saturday through Wednesday. The festival was revived in 1998 after it was stopped in the Taisho Era (1912-1926). Nine craftsmen and workers created the Gundam based on drawings and a plastic model. It will be shown at a Goshogawara tourism promotion facility until the festival opens. (NOBORU TOMURA/ The Asahi Shimbun)
