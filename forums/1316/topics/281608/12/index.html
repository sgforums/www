    FInale Summary from Duckroll, animesuki

    - Setsuna and GN-Arms is fighting Corner's golden MA

    - Alle and Halle have a conversation in the cockpit while Kyrios is hiding

    - Alle decides he wants to go out and fight them, even if its a huge disadvantage, convincing Halle to be with him

    - Halle pulls back his hair and they become one

    - Kyrios goes nuts on Soma and Sergei, takes off one arm of Sergei's GN-X

    - Kyrios even TRANSFORMS to MA mode while broken (lol)

    - Cuts back to Setsuna fighting

    - They're doing pretty well, and then GN-Arms transforms into the GN-Armor and joins with Exia

    - Exia totally pwns the MA, cutting off one claw with the huge GN-Sword and blowing the other one up with the GN-Cannons

    - More guns and stuff pop up in the MA

    - Lasse gets badly injured by the overwhelming firepower

    - Exia charges anyway, and stabs the MA up with the GN-Armor's weapons as it gets destroyed

    - Lasse probably dies, but it's not shown

    - The next scene is Exia flying out from the smoke and going crazy on the MA, cutting up it in a slow single motion

    - Exia flies back and it explodes

    - Back to Kyrois

    - It takes out another of Sergei's GN-X's arms

    - It goes Transam to show Soma who's boss

    - Totally disables Soma's GN-X

    - Starts to kill Soma

    - Sergei jumps in the way and knocks Kyrios off

    - Soma fires and shoots Kyrios up

    - Alle's is missing Halle's eye, badly wounded

    - He sees Soma get out of her cockpit to go save Sergei

    - Sergei asks what she's doing, go back to fight

    - She says that without him she's all alone and she needs him to be with her

    - Alle recognizes Soma as "Mari" and wonders why she's there

    - Kyrios floats away

    - Back to Exia, the remains of the MA opens up revealing a GOLDEN MS, lolz

    - Corner talks shit about Aeolia's legacy being all washed up again and then he charges up and fires a direct hit of a super blast at Exia



    -BREAK-

    - When we return from the break Exia flies back in Transam mode, he's undefeated and he's ready to kick ass

    - He dodges anything Corner has and fights really well, but Corner has a really strong GN-Field

    - Setsuna then has a flashback and remembers what Lockon told him when he first joined CB as a pilot

    - Exia's ace in the hole is that it's melee capabilities allow it to pierce through a GN-Field in the event where a Gundam happens to fight a Gundam

    - Setsuna remembers Lockon's words and charges forward dodging the MS' shots

    - He stabs through the GN-Field with the GN-Sword and breaks it

    - Then while Corner is being a wuss, Setsuna shows him what a REAL Gundam pilot is made of and uses ALL SEVEN SWORDS on him. STAB STAB STAB STAB STAB

    - As Corner is dying, Ribbons sends a transmission to acknowledge that he has failed in his lameass attempt to take over the world

    - Ribbons was never interested in Corner's stupid plans, but merely using Corner to see how far he can go

    - Corner dies

    - Before Setsuna can recover much, another GN trail appears

    - The GN-Flag comes at Exia and Graham declares his intention to defeat the Gundam and mouths a whole bunch of propaganda and ideology crap at Setsuna

    - It's a pretty show but really well animated fight where they take off each other's arms, heads, etc before charging and stabbing right through each other and exploding

    - Tiera in the disabled Nadleeh decides to eject the GN Drive so they can recover it without him, he still wants to bang Lockon in heaven, how sweet

    - Kyrios is also floating in space, and Halle tells Alle goodbye and after which his reflection fades away

    - Exia's remains are floating in space, and there's a long message (transmission?) from Setsuna to Marina while they show the shots of the various surviving characters (Nena on a beach or something, might be a desert, Cathy at the bridge of the ship looking distaught, Soma holding Sergei's hand as he's rushed into medical care on a bed, etc) and it ends with Exia floating right over the Earth



    FOUR YEARS LATER..... 2312 AD



    Saji has made it into space, he seems to be some sort of contractor working in space now, and he tells Louise to just wait a little longer for him, he doesn't seem as vengeful or angry as he was when he was younger. He looks into space and sees a green GN trail and comments that it's not a Earth Federation GN Drive.... and he thinks it has to be a Gundam. There's a trace of anger there.



    The Earth Federation has further militarized itself with a brand new series of Mobile Suits that look like knights and have what look like lances. You can see Cathy among the soldiers in the big parade. The leader of the Earth Federation is a black man.



    Marina's assistant is leaving her, as she's watching the news on TV, telling her that the world has changed, and so have their position. Marina doesn't try to stop her. Ribbons is shown with a new group of people that look like him, I think he calls them Innovators. And they're preparing for the next phase. You see Graham in a mask.



    There might be a few scenes here I'm forgetting, but eventually the ending theme stops playing. And then there's one more epilogue. Somewhere on a CB asteroid (might be L1) you see the engineer ushering two new Gundam meisters into a room (I think one of them is the female Tiera), and he opens a hanger showing the O Gundam. The original suit. Then you see a shot of Nena somewhere looking pretty evil. Then they talk about the other suit, the Gundam Double O. The suit that will make the difference to the world. The camera zooms into the closed hanger and you see the Double O in the shadows.



    END
