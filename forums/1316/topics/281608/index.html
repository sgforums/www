    Gundam 00 Ep1 summary
    As the Gundam 00 Ep1 Private Showings draw near their conclusion, Gunota was lucky enough to attend one this weekend. SPOILER ALERT

    Ep1: Celestial Being

    It starts out with young Setsuna, a kid soldier, fighting against a MS. It appears to be a religious conflict as he keeps saying to himself that he does not believe in God's existence. As Setsuna desperately runs away from the enemy MS, a flash of beam fire from the sky shoots down on all of the MS. Setsuna looks up and sees a glowing Gundam (as seen in the latest trailer).

    OP credits

    The time is now A.D. 2307. In front of the public, the AEU reveals the brand new MS Enact in time for the 10th anniversary of the Human Reform League's orbital elevator opening. Celestial Being, still not known officially, initiates Phase One. The Gundam Exia appears from the skies. Setsuna disables the Enact and leaves the scene. Under hot pursuit, Setsuna is now engaged with several MS. Lockon Stratos, piloting the Gundam Dynames, helps from the ground and along with Setsuna takes out the MS. Now Phase Two is complete. Up in the Human Reform League's orbital elevator, another anniversary party is taking place. An attack is carried out by an unknown terrorist group. However, Gundam Kyrios and Gundam Virtue eliminates them. This concludes Phase Three. The news media are now reporting the failed terror attack on the elevator. Next, a news broadcast shows a video they received from an unknown group. On it is what appears to be the leader of Celestial Being. He declares they will use force to end all wars on Earth. Other world leaders see this as a major contradiction.
