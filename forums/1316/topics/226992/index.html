    Original sound MS Gundam movie trilogy to hit DVD

    Reports from people with early copies of this month's Gundam Ace say a Mobile Suit Gundam Movie Memorial Box is scheduled to come out December 21 for 18,900 yen. Unlike the previously released movie trilogy on DVD, this one will be the original sound version (as originally shown in theaters, other home video formats, and on the Bandai Channel) and not the already available Special Edition version, which featured re-recorded voices by the original cast in 5.1, revised sound effects, and some changes in the placement of the background music. The Memorial Box will be produced for a limited time (until June 30, 200 and feature an HD premium master.
