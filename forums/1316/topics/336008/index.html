    You mean those two rows of symbols?  I'm pretty sure most people ignore those anyway...  (they're not necessary most of the time since the parts only fit together a certain way). 

    Here are their meanings from left to right:

    1st row:

    1-1 - Glue together

    1-2 - Indicator for which marking seal to use (in Katakana)

    1-3 - Indicator for which decal to use (in Roman Alphabet)

    1-4 - Attach parts in the opposite direction on both sides

    1-5 - Attach part in the same direction both sides

    1-6 - Caution: note the direction of the part

    1-7 - Caution: do not over tighten the screw

    2nd row:

    2-1 - Cut this part

    2-2 - Make the same part the number of times indicated inside the box

    2-3 - Assemble this part first

    2-4 - Assmeble this part afterwards

    2-5 - Turn the part according to the degrees indicated in the box

    2-6 - Choose one of the parts to assemble first

    2-7 - Move both parts the same way in opposite directions
