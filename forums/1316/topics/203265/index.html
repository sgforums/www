    First Gundam ranks high with dads
    Oricon recently conducted an Internet survey of 600 parents (300 women & 300 men) in their 20's to 40's who had young kids and asked them to rank classic anime they'd like to tell their children about. Mobile Suit Gundam ranked #3 on the men's list and #5 on the overall rankings. Complete top 10 lists follow below:

    Overall ranking
    1) Heidi, A Girl Of The Alps
    2) Dragonball
    3) Doraemon
    4) Manga Nippon Mukashi Banashi
    5) Mobile Suit Gundam
    6) A Dog Of Flanders
    7) Candy Candy
    Galaxy Express 999
    9) Star Of The Giants
    10) Captain Tsubasa

    Dads' choices
    1) Dragonball
    2) Doraemon
    3) Mobile Suit Gundam
    4) Heidi, A Girl Of The Alps
    5) A Dog Of Flanders
    6) Star Of The Giants
    7) Galaxy Express 999
    Captain Tsubasa
    9) Slam Dunk
    10) Manga Nippon Mukashi Banashi

    Moms' choices
    1) Heidi, A Girl Of The Alps
    2) Manga Nippon Mukashi Banashi
    3) Candy Candy
    4) Dragonball
    5) Doraemon
    6) A Dog Of Flanders
    7) Dr. Slump & Arale-chan
    Galaxy Express 999
    9) Ikkyu-san
    10) My Neighbor Totoro & Captain Tsubasa (tie)
