    Plamo Radicon Show 2007 UPDATED
    Run Chicken S has the first report from this year's show:
    HGUC Re-GZ - January, 2,940 yen
    R3 Walker Galliar - Spring
    Jumbo Grade Zeta Gundam - Said to transform with part-swapping
    BB Senshi Koumei Re-GZ - Spring
    BB Senshi Kakouton Giros - Winter
    BB Senshi Rikuson Zeta Plus (exhibit only)
    BB Senshi Shuuyu Hyaku Shiki (exhibit only)
    BB Senshi Sonshoukou Gerbera (exhibit only)
    Mascot Robo Haro - 00 style, February, 4,800 yen
    GFF Metal Composite Casval's RX-78 Gundam
    EMSiA Zaku Real Type Color - January, 2,100 yen
    Zeonography Qubeley Set EX - December, 8,190 yen

    No new MG but metallic coating versions of the Sazabi & Nu Gundam were on display. Also shown were the usual 00 lineup. The February issue of Dengeki Hobby (on sale Christmas) will come with upgrade parts for the HG Gundam Exia to create the protagonist's MS from the official 00 side story (the design of which will be revealed in the December issue, on sale Oct. 25).
