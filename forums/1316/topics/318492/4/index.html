    MG SWORD IMPULSE GUNDAM

    • JPN retail 4,500 JPY


    • An MG kit of the Sword Impulse Gundam, which appeared in the first episode of "Mobile Suit Gundam SEED DESTINY" and many others!
    • The two "Excalibur Anti-Ship Swords" can be connected and the blades can be extended and retracted.
    • The "Flash Edge Beam Boomerangs" can be detached from the Sword Silhouette pack to form a large boomerang.
    • Impulse Gundam can be disassembled into the Core Splendor, Chest Flyer, and Leg Flyer units.
    • The Silhouette Flyer can be attached to the Sword Silhouette pack to replicate the launch scene, and can also be displayed on a specially designed stand.
    • Core Splendor can be swapped for a Multi-Pack, allowing the MG Strike Noir or MG Strike Gundam's Striker Packs to be attached in its place.
    • Includes Sword Silhouette pack (2 anti-ship swords, 2 beam boomerangs), anti-ship sword beam effect parts, beam boomerang effect parts, beam rifle, shield, and 2 assault knives.
