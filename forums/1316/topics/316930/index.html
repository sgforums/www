    Namco Bandai Holdings 2008 financial results
    Namco Bandai Holdings' English site posted their fiscal 2008 financial highlights (PDF). Mobile Suit Gundam was responsible for 50.9 billion yen in sales (Bandai Namco Group total), just ahead of what was forecast back in March, with 18.8 billion yen alone from Toys and Hobby. Bandai expects Gundam to bring in 45 billion yen (17 billion yen from Toys and Hobby) in the coming fiscal year.
