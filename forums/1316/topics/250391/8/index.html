    BB SENSHI SANGOKUDEN No.321 BACHO BLUE DESTINY

    • JPN retail 600 JPY

    • The "Blue Destiny," a.k.a "Shin-i no Kin-Bacho," rides forth from the new Sangokuden series!
    • Armor can be removed and hair parts can be reversed to transform from normal to berserker mode.
    • Knob on head can be turned to transform eyes from normal to berserker mode.
    • Includes the Shiryu Gokurenso weapon, which can be disassembled into multiple swords and spears.

     

    BB SENSHI SANGOKUDEN GIGATAIFU SHIBAI SAZABI

    • JPN retail 1,000 JPY

    • The Giga's Daitaifu, Shibai Sazabi joins the kit lineup along with the new series!
    • The Sazabi's distinctive red color is rendered in a metallic red.
    • Can be disassembled and recombined in Commander or Strategist mode.
    • Its weapons, the Silver Fang and the Crimson Fan, combine to form the Great Fang Fan Sword.

     

    BB SENSHI SANGOKUDEN
    SONKEN GUNDAM & SHUYU HYAKU SHIKI -SEKIHEKI NO GOHO-

    • JPN retail 1,600 JPY

    • A special set of Sonken Gundam and Shuyu Hyaku Shiki in combat during the Battle of Red Cliff!
    • Sonken Gundam is molded in translucent plastic, while Shuyu Hyaku Shiki is coated with gold chrome.
    • Shuyu and Sonken's weapons combine for their ultimate attack move.
    • Includes a reversible background image board that shows The Battle of Red Cliff and the Land of GO.

     

    BB SENSHI SANGOKUDEN
    KOMEI Re-GZ & SHUYU HYAKU SHIKI -RED CLIFF COLOR-

    • JPN retail 1200 JPY

    • A special Sangokuden item released in conjunction with the movie Red Cliff!
    • A set of Shuyu Hyaku Shiki and Komei Re-GZ molded in special colors that evoke the image of Shuyu and Komei in the Red Cliff film!
