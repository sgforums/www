     MG 1/100 RX-78-2 GUNDAM Ver.2.0

    •JPN retail 4,200 JPY


    •The long-anticipated Master Grade 2.0 version of the constantly evolving RX-78-2 Gundam design is here!
    •Includes accurate proportions and features that are faithful to the Gundam as it appeared in the classic animated TV series!
    •An all-new "full action inner frame" allows the Gundam's waist to twist and move forward and back even with the Core Fighter in place.
    •Three-part sole design enhances contact of feet with the ground.
    •Accurately sculpted Core Fighter features a pilot's seat that rotates to face forward during transformation into Core Block mode.
    •Weapons include beam rifle, "super napalm" canister, hyper bazooka, two beam sabers, a beam javelin, the Gundam hammer, and shield, each with appropriate action features.
