  • jetabt767400
    20 Mar 07, 00:42
    Hmm, the marker that you are using, is it oil or aryclic based? The spelling might be wrong but 'agar-agar' there are these two types. I find oil types more easier to smudge, so to avoid, its best to use a super fine aryclic marker to do the panel marking. But if you are using the oil based one, not to worry, just use a soft dry tissue or an eraser to rub off the excessive ink, and it should work out fine. Do not use a wet tissue or it will smudge even further.
    Enjoy.
