    I have finally finished my MG Ex-S Gundam after 1 month!

    see below for picture of finished kit and my review.



    Review

    It's a big kit, with many parts. i won't say it's very complicated but definitely not recommended for beginners. those who have modelled MG kits before shouldn't be a problem.

    quite a lot of joints requires screwing. this can be very troublesome as the screws are very hard to screw in. u will need a very small screwdriver to do the job. for me some of the parts are so hard to screw i used superglue or didn't screw them at all. it's up to individuals, if u find the joints tight enough, u can forget about screwing.

    the kit requires lots of panel lines drawing as well. took me a few days just to draw panel lines.

    overall i'll say this is one awesome kit. go for it if u have the money and time.
