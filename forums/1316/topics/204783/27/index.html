    regarding whether markers are sufficient to make the kit look real, i can't give u an answer. it depends on the techniques on controling the panels, marker painted parts, etc. u can see some hobby mags doing wonders with markers. but generally, plamodellers will prefer airbrushing and enamel paneling when they have a budget for airbrushing.

    im jus a casual collector, and due to personal reasons, i can't pick up airbrushing. it really depends on how u want ur kit to be. dont really have to do "full set" to make the kit look really nice. like me, i only sand away whole greasy surfaces of parts, clear sprue gates, panel and decal them, and im happy with it. ultimately, if u have the time and patience, be sure to try it.

    edited:

    @wcloudx: marker sets quite seldom seen in singapore. but hobby point has them sometimes. at least $22 per set. and ultra fine panel markers, can get at $3.90 la..
