    i'm currrently working on my MG F91 Harrasion Madin. here are some of my thoughts on th kit.

    the kits is smaller than regular MG kits. it feels like 1/144 as compared to 1/100. some parts are very small thus require careful handling.

    some small parts don't fit tightly, i have to use glue to secure them.

    MG F91 comes with water decals, but i find that they don't stick to the model when it dries. can come off if u rub against it.

    MG 91 comes with 2 interchangeable face plate as well. to do that, u need to take put the helmet and switch the face plate. but by doing so, the eye piece sticker will comes off easily.

    lastly i was surprised that MG91 doesn't comes with any rubber parts for the joints at all!

    water decals.... hmm u can try washing the whole runner with dilute soap water before starting any assembling and decal applying. tats wad i seen from other forums.

    regarding the faceplate in risk of removing its eye-sticker, u can try removing the sticker as a whole, place it on a doubleside tape, cut it out and put it back. thats wad i do when it almost tear apart on my F91.

    btw, no rubber parts also means that some poses will be more secure as rubber parts will lose its strength after some time. even if it loses its strength in posing, can use superglue to make the ball joints thicker by abit, so it'll hold on longer.
