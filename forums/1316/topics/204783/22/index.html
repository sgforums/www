    my personal thoughts on MG Destiny.

    i would say this got to be the MG released by Bandai so far. i see that they really made effort to improve their MG. the new shoulder joints are very flexible. very good for posing.



    this is also the first MG i see to have left and right feet. i don't know whether other MGs have this but most of my kits, both the feet are identical.

    the stand is also redesigned. instead of holding the gunpla at it's torso, it hold it's backpack. nice touch as now the stand will not hinder the posing of the legs. we can also easily remove the gunpla from the stand without using much force. (but note that the catch can be easily broken if u move the gunpla too much with too much force)

    so basically, i would strongly recommend MG Destiny. it's cool.
