    Summary:
    While ZAFT attacks the first relay cylinder of the Requiem weapon, the Minerva is sent on a bombing run of Djibril’s moon base. Before they set off, Shinn is quite worried about Lunamaria’s well being, but she says that she’ll be fine, asking Shinn to believe in her. The two share several moments together before setting out. As usual, the battle is intense, especially since the Minerva is the only ship assaulting the base. However, with ample use of the Tannhauser and good piloting by each of the gundam aces, they quickly bring down everything that’s thrown at them including several mobile armors and Destroy Gundams. Djibril gets desperate and wants to fire the Requiem prematurely, but that never happens. Instead, Rey destroys the ship he’s tries to escape on. Sitting on his throne, Gilbert thanks Djibril and then says goodbye. On Earth, Kira and Athrun get ready to launch the next day. They talk about everyone’s dreams and desires while looking at the peaceful sky. When the ArchAngel is about to launch, Cagalli delivers a speech to the crew. Athrun notices that she’s no longer wearing the ring that he gave her, but he never gets the opportunity to speak with her. When Kira and Lacus approach him concerned, he replies that it’s ok because their dreams are the same. Walking out, Cagalli runs into Meyrin and requests that she watch out for Athrun’s safety. Cagalli leaves with tears in her eyes, but salutes the ArchAngel off as it once again heads for space.

    I enjoyed this battle episode even if it felt rather one-sided at times. There is a lot less Yzak and Dearka and a lot more Shinn/Rey/Lunamaria than I would have guessed. But since the fight is more about the Minerva’s moon assault, that makes sense. I’m just happy that the battle didn’t seem too much of a redux of past fights.
    The ring is gone! I didn’t expect that to happen, but it makes sense. Cagalli probably wants to concentrate on her state duties, and realizes that when this is all over, she and Athrun will have all the time in the world (assuming he survives). But it still seems very painful for her not to be able to go with them. I have a firm belief that Athrun and Cagalli will still end up together, but for now they will pursue their dream in different ways.
    Neo/Mwu is now officially the pilot of the Akatsuki. They even changed the OP sequence to reflect this. It’s about time the man who makes the impossible possible gets a powerful mobile suit again. I’m rooting for the fact that he doesn’t die again, and hopefully the suit will serve him well in that respect.
    Rey’s quick reflexes catch Djibril trying to escape, resulting in a quick death. I thought the thank you, and then goodbye line from Gilbert was a nice touch. With Djibril’s purpose served, whatever that may be, Gilbert is now ready to move forth with his plans. He’s already sent Meer to the moon city Copernicus, and that also looks to be where the ArchAngel is headed. The preview shows Athrun, Meyrin, Kira, Lacus, and Meer under small arms fire (maybe from the bodyguard Gilbert gave her?). Maybe Meer will join the ArchAngel (oh dear, I can see the Meer and Lacus encounter now…. I’m very curious to see what her eventual role in all this will be.
    Closing Thought: Seems like we’re shifting girls around in Athrun’s harem again. (I say this in all jest)

    In depth episode summary
    The pre-OP starts with chaos in PLANT, with the civilians evacuating. Gilbert helps calm down some people organising that. Meanwhile, the ZAFT armada is heading for Requiem's first relay point. In the control center, they order Requiem's recharging to be hurried up. They want to fire the "second movement" ASAP, carrying on this reference for Requiem being a song. Djbriel is burning to hit Aprillus.

    Gilbert is still talking to people at PLANT (from the safety of Messiah's command center), and says that there's no way they can stall another strike since EA/LOGOS is a terrorist organisation bent on their destruction, not a country/federation. The EA forces launch all their fleets into space to protect Requiem's relay points. Yzak's group head to that battle too.

    OP: One change and it's not trivial - Mwu/Neo shows up in the poses instead of Cagalli for Akatsuki moving around in space. This is surprising since Akatsuki is Cagalli's father's "will" and ORB-01. I guess Cagalli put sentiment aside and decided the Archangel would need all the help it can get (more on this later).

    Phase-45 "Prelude to a Revolution" begins.

    In Orb, in the Archangel's hanger, Akatsuki is being moved in, and Athrun and Meyrin are helping at computer terminals. Kira comes over to help, since he's finished his work already - and is worried about Athrun overdoing it since he's still injured. Kira asks Athrun how far he had gotten and starts typing away at high speed, and Meyrin gives a small gasp. Athrun is worried about PLANT, and Kira thinks ZAFT will never allow a second strike, but it's annoying for them not to be able to do anything.

    The Minerva is curving around the Moon and gets an order from HQ, and Abby is surprised at the contents. As the orders are explained, Gilbert is shown in some kind of situation room, with many large projections and a number of personell working at computer terminals. The Minerva is ordered to strike Requiem itself and the command center directly while the rest of the fleet attacks the first relay point. Minerva has to do this alone as a surprise attack.

    Talia explains this to the pilots, and Shinn is surprised they have to do it by themselves. Rey seems to agree with the plans, since they're closer to Requiem than the relay point. Talia says the problem is how long until Requiem can fire again. With the main fight elsewhere, the Minerva might be able to slip in. Shinn seems a bit down at the "surprise attack" strategy and Talia says she knows it will be a hard fight but they have to do it.

    Talia has a brief flashback with a younger version of herself and a small boy - when she left Gilbert so she could have a child, perhaps her son and husband were killed (perhaps on J7), which lead to her joining the military, but this is simply a guess.

    The pilots agree to the plan and the Minerva then heads closer to the Moon's surface (to avoid radar detection presumably).

    A brief scene is shown of the Copernicus city on the Moon (actually a neutral city), and a depressed looking Meer is shown in a swimsuit near a pool. Her bodyguard looks on.

    Meanwhile, Requiem is about half-way to re-charging. At the first relay point an intense battle is going on between large numbers of ZAFT and EA ships. However, the EA have some new ships with beam shields at the front, and the shields are huge, enough to cover the ex-colony relay point. Yzak's team arrive and they immediately head out in the MS to join the attack.

    The Minerva flies close to the Moon's surface and Rey starts to explain the plan (did he discuss this with Talia?). Rey thinks that if Requiem can be fired again, Djbriel will do it even if it means crushing his own troops. However, if the first relay point is destroyed then Requiem is useless. Shinn agrees that LOGOS and Djbriel are the types to do that sort of thing, with various flashbacks of Destroy.

    Lunamaria regrets not being able to shoot down Djbriel's shuttle in Orb. Shinn says it's not her fault - it's Orb's for sheltering him. Rey rather coldly says that the event is already in the past so no need to think about it.

    Meanwhile, Yzak and Deaka show nice teamwork to take out some large MAs with beam shields pretty easily.

    Djbriel is keen to fire Requiem again and thinks when that happens, it'll be all over the Coordinators - finally he'll be able to do what nobody was able to and put the history of humanity back on the right path. Just a few hours to go.

    The Minerva is getting close to the Requiem base, and Talia orders condition red. In the pilot's room, Rey seems to have finished discussing the plan, and Lunamaria has a key role, and Rey says she better not get the timing wrong. Shinn seems a bit nervous about her part. Rey says he and Shinn will be busy providing a diversion for Lunamaria to slip through. Lunamaria says she understands and for them not to worry. Rey says any mistakes won't be tolerated, and leaves.

    Lunamaria wants to talk to Shinn privately, and Rey tells them to be quick as he leaves them. She hugs Shinn telling him to be careful and he hugs her back. Shinn still has reservations about Lunamaria having to attack the cannon's controls by herself - it's too dangerous. He wants to alter the plan, but she stops him saying Shinn will be in just as much danger. She says she'll be okay and asks him to trust her. Shinn then suddenly hugs her and they go spinning through the air.

    As the Minerva continues over the Moon, Lunamaria and Shinn head down in the elevator. Shinn says he'll protect her, the ship and PLANT, and she smiles. Lunamaria launches first in Blast Impulse.

    In the Requiem base, they detect the Minerva arriving and Djbriel is shocked.

    Eye-catch. Second half begins.

    Various EA MS and MA take off. But they don't seem to have that many (already commited most of them to first relay battle I guess). The Minerva prepares for battle. Talia orders Destiny and Legend to take off, and you can see the Moon's surface in the background as they move down the catapult.

    Shinn starts owning grunts and Rey uses's Legend's DRAGOONs to take out a large MA - side note, two of the DRAGOONs have a sort of beam saber tip, except somewhat wider and more like a firecracker. They fly right through the MA from underneath, bypassing the MA's beam shields.

    Meanwhile, Lunamaria is skimming over the moon's surface. Gilbert gets a message saying the Minerva has reached the EA moon base, and he seems quite happy. Rey is mowing down grunts, and so is Shinn, just not so fast. Minerva is shooting down missles and firing cannons, some of which hit part of the EA base defences. Talia orders the Tannhauser to prepare to fire. Meanwhile, Gilbert orders the battle to be displayed in PLANT.

    And a brief scene of Meer is shown sitting part-way into a pool, sadly looking at her red Haro. Murrue and Mwu/Neo look on from the Archangel's bridge. Cornelia looks up at the moon, sitting out in the open with her dog.

    Some Destroys prepare to launch - seems to be three. Minerva fires its bow cannon but it is blocked by a MA with beam shield. The force from the blast rocks the Requiem base. Shinn punches through a Destroy cockpit with the palm cannon. Rey uses the fire-tipped DRAGOONs to drill through another Destroy's beam shield, and through the cockpit. Meanwhile, Lunamaria heads in from the side, taking out some grunts on the way and Shinn spots her.

    Requiem is 61% recharged, and things are looking a bit bad for the EA and Djbriel orders the cannon to fire - even if it's not full power. The aim is to mow down the ZAFT forces. Yzak's group continue to attack and the first relay point takes some damage. Djbriel orders Requiem to fire regardless, but the base commander doesn't like it. Djbriel says he'll escape at the same time - "As long as I at least am alive, there are many possibilities". Djbriel creaps out the base commander a bit.

    Lunamaria is facing many grunts and a MA, and Shinn helps out a bit. Shinn tells her to carry on, and he'll make sure she isn't shot. She thanks him. The Minerva fires Tannhauser again, blowing up part of the base in general, and Rey finishes off the last Destroy. Shinn blows up a MA that was going for Impulse and she dives down into a large shaft in the base. Rey calls Shinn and says they'll attack the base command center.

    Requiem prepares to fire at 2/3rds power. Shinn notices this and gets angry (but still isn't in SEED mode). Meanwhile, Djbriel moves on to the Girty Lue (or similar class ship). Lunamaria reaches the base of Requiem's cannon, and blows up the command center before the cannon can fire. Rey watches from above. Shinn has gone inside the base proper and starts blowing things up. Rey notices the Girty Lue take off and moves in to attack the bridge. Djbriel gets melted down - and Gilbert is immediately shown smiling. Legend finishes off the Girty Lue.

    On his "throne", Gilbert says "Thank you, Djbriel, and, sayonara". ZAFT has won.

    On the Archangel's deck, Athrun is looking out onto the sea. He has an updated uniform, that of colonel. Kira walks over to him. Kira is worried about Athrun opening his wounds again since they'll be taking off tomorrow - Athrun seems to have taken his bandages off. Lacus watches from a doorway and then leaves.

    Athrun looks up at the moon and says it's so quiet down here. Kira has a unfiorm upgrade too - it's almost the same as Cagalli's (for the ranking indications at least), making Kira a general! Athrun wonders why the world can't always be like this. Kira asks Athrun if he has a dream, desire or even ambition about that. Meanwhile, scenes of Cagalli working at her desk are shown, and also the Minerva pilots returning to applause and to Shinn running over to hug Lunamaria. Kira thinks everyone has a similar desire for peace.

    Athrun says that in Gilbert's world, such things wouldn't exist. Kira says that since such things won't exist, war won't either, but neither Kira or Athrun like such a trade-off. Kira wonders if they're being selfish and Athrun says they might, but adds that's how humans have survived for so long. Athrun says it's difficult - maybe they shouldn't fight, or maybe they have to fight but isn't entirely sure which. Kira says it would be nice if everyone had the same dream. Athrun has some flashbacks of Shinn and thinks that probably everyone has the same dream, but they just don't realise it.

    The next day, the Archangel is still in its dry dock. In the hanger, Akatsuki is next to SF and IJ and its gold colouring really stands out. Cagalli is speaking. She is lined up with Murrue, Neo/Mwu, Kira, Lacus, with Kisaka behind her with some underlings. In front of her in the rest of the Archangel crew, including Athrun.

    She says that it is not clear what happened on the moon and so things are uncertain, but the Archangel is now officially part of Orb's military (as the 2nd space "fleet" I think). Cagalli says she will give them as much support as she can. Athrun notices that Cagalli has taken off her ring. Cagalli says that PLANT has taken a lot of damage, and that right now, Gilbert is the strongest leader in the world, and most people view him as being right. Cagalli says that Orb still has a lot of power and above all wants peace, but that has to be with freedom as well. Cagalli asks them all to help achieve that, and they all salute (including Lacus).

    Murrue then announces they'll be heading to the Copernicus city on the moon in 30 minutes, and the group disperse. Cagalli turns to Mwu/Neo and says "Colonel Leonork, please take care of Akatsuki" and he says "Leave it to me", and then Cagalli leaves with Kisaka.

    Kira and Lacus go to Athrun, effectively asking why he won't go talk to Cagalli. Athrun says it's okay like this, surprising Kira and Lacus. He adds that they don't need to rush their relationship, and that their dream is the same. Kira and Lacus smile.

    As Cagalli walks away, she sees Meyrin (in her ZAFT uniform with her hair up), and Meyrin seems a bit nervous. Cagalli turns to her smiling and says that it seems Meyrin will be going with them. Meyrin somewhat nervously says yes. Cagalli asks Meyrin to take care of "him" (ie Athrun) since she (Cagalli) can't go with them, surprising Meyrin. Cagalli says she'll pray for their safety and turns away, with Meyrin looking at her. However, despite being calm and collected the whole time, Cagalli has tears in her eyes as she walks away.

    The Archangel's dock pulls back and the Archangel goes through the pre-launch sequence. In the bridge, Lacus says to Murrue "let's be on our way then", and Murrue gives the order to launch. The ship takes off from the sea with Cagalli looking on, saluting.

    Next episode is "Song of Truth".
