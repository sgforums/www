    Depend on the paint use. Matt color is easier to obtain constant effect with hand brush. If u go for glossy effect, handbrush is impossible to obtain consistent color and result.

    Thin yr color too. Thick paint is difficult to work with handbrush!

     That means use thin paint and slowly layer by layer to achieve good result..
