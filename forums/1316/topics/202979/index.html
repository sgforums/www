    PSP Gundam Battle Royale?
    News from 2ch is that a game entitled "Gundam Battle Royale" is coming to the PSP on October 26. Word is it'll be another entry in Capcom's Vs. Series, this one being a Universal Century compilation featuring over 80 MS from MSG, 08th MS Team, 0080, 0083, "First Gaiden series", Z, and ZZ. The basics will be the same as the other UC Vs. Series games but it uses the Alliance Vs. ZAFT arms system. The awakening system will be largely modified, with five different awakening types. The 7 modes of the game are given as: CPU Battle, Universal Century, Survival, Time Attack, Training, Ad Hoc versus, and Infrastructure versus. Three appearing units will be the Dendrobium, Blue Destiny, and Zudah. Price is reported to be 4,980 yen.
