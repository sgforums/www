    This is a straight build with all parts sanded down and seams removed (for the PS parts, cement was used; for the ABS parts, cyanoacrylate glue (aka krazy glue) was used).  The eyes, the base and the little dude (Heero) inside the cockpit were painted.  The inner frame and gray parts were sprayed with flat clear.  The other parts were sprayed with pearl.  Decals were then applied and then covered with an additional coat of clear and panel lines were drawn.  Except for the abovementioned parts, the colours you see are what the kit had out of the box except with a clear pearl coat.  Enjoy!
