TGS 2007 Gundam Special Stage

  • duotiga83
    23 Sep 07, 19:33
    Famitsu.com and DengekiOnline.com covered the Gundam Special Stage at Tokyo Game Show 2007. Two new titles were formally announced:

    Gihren's Greed: The Menace Of Axis for the PSP. Though the 2008 date and price are yet to be fixed, the producer let it slip that it will cover up to Char's Counterattack.

    Emblem Of Gundam for the Nintendo DS was also mentioned but virtually nothing was revealed about it. The logo, depicting Axis smashing into A Baoa Qu, indicates a Zeta Gundam basis.

    Hiroko Moriguchi showed up as a guest and performed "Mizu No Hoshi Ni Ai Wo Komete", "Mou Hitotsu No Mirai ~Starry Spirits~" (G Generation Spirits OP theme), and "Eternal Wind".
