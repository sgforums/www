    Gundam cosplay bars to open

    Akiba Souken and Akiba Blog caught some signs advertising twin Gundam cosplay bars to open in Akihabara in mid-December. The "mobile suit bars", named "Zeon" and "Federal Forces", will be housed on the 4th and 5th floors, respectively, of the Zeniya building, also home to Iosys' Akihabara shop and Pash Cafe Nagomi. The bars will be centered on the worldviews of First, Z, and ZZ with mobile suit and character-based food & drinks. Applications for female staff members are currently being taken.
