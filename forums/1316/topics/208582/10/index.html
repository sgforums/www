     HGUC KAMPFER

    •JPN retail 1,800 JPY


    •Long requested by fans, the Kampfer from "Mobile Suit Gundam 0080: War in the Pocket" joins the HGUC series!
    •Designed to replicate the image of the mobile suit as seen in the series, the waist and arms feature enhanced posability.
    •Shoulders can be pulled out and rotated to allow the shotgun to be wielded in both hands.
    •Weapons can be mounted on racks on the back, waist, and legs.
    •Includes two Giant Bazooka, shotgun with stock, normal shotgun, two Sturm Faust, two beam sabers, and chain mine.
    •The long core wire used for the chain mine alllows it to be wrapped around enemy mobile suits.

     

     HGUC RICK DOM II COLONY COLOR

    •JPN retail 1,500 JPY


    •An HGUC kit of the Rick Dom II, which Zeon forces used to infiltrate the space colony in "Mobile Suit Gundam 0080: War in the Pocket."
    •The kit's plastic is molded in the suit's distinctive green coloration.
    •Weapons include two Sturm Faust, a Giant Bazooka, a 90mm machine gun, and beam saber.
