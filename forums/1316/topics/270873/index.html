    Gundam Expo Tokyo 2007
    Gundam.info gives details on Gundam Expo Tokyo 2007, to be held October 11 - 18. The event will have the following zones:



    Successive Gundams Zone - An introduction to each Gundam production through graphics & video.

    Gunpla History Zone - Gunpla displays and history.

    Character Zone - Featuring life-sized cutouts of characters and a quiz corner.

    Theater Zone - Screens a special video covering the Gundam series called "The Legend Of Gundam 2007".

    Item Zone - A variety of Gundam merchandise: Gunpla, figures, games, etc.

    Mobile Suit Gundam 00 Zone - With a large display and the latest promo video.

    Experience Zone - Assemble Gunpla and play the latest games.

    Sale Zone - Stuff to buy, including items commemorating the event.

    Gundam Expo report

    ITmedia +D has a report on what fans will encounter at this year's Gundam Expo 2007. Some of the featured items are:
    - 1/10 scale model of Zeong, Gundam, Zeta, Hyaku Shiki, and Strike Freedom (scale model for Strike Freedom is unknown)
    - wooden and platinum version of Gundam
    - quiz corner voiced by Gundam seiyuus
    - colony model and 1/1 Zaku head (previously displayed at the Bandai Musuem)
    - Wii MSG Sensen 0079, DS SD Gundam G Generation Crossdrive, and the upcoming PSP Battle Chronicle are playable at the Experience Zone.
