    Erm, I dun have a model kit book, never mind, but there is one price 59.00 Singapore dollars. Mobile Suit Illustrated 2006. ISBN 4-8402-3411-6 First released 15th May 2006. Very good book, I would say, as it illustrates with coloured pictures accompanied by specs, and schematics or blue prints of all the MS that are shown widely. Prototypes and MSV mobile suits are, sadly, not included in the schematics. Not to worry though.
    Some touching points are the cockpit drawings of selected MS, including Seed's star, the GAT-X105 Strike. Also another point, the cockpit hatches of many Seed/Seed Destiny MS.
    For someone like me who is interested in designs, this book indeed helps alot on many new MS concepts. It is availible in Books Kinokuniya.
