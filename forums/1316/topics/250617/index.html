    MSiA Gundam 00 Campaign Part II

    Following the clear version MSiA Gundam Exia promo campaign, Tamashii Web posted details on how to get the Clear Color Editions of the other three Gundams' MSiA figures. Each will be respectively available via required mail order forms in one of three magazines on sale this month: Gundam Ace (Dynames), Dengeki Hobby (Kyrios), and Hobby Japan (Virtue). The ordering deadline is February 29 with delivery starting in the middle of May. The Dynames & Kyrios go for 2,500 yen and the Virtue for 3,000.
