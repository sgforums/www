    Gundam Expo 2007 details

    From December 14 through January 1, Gundam Expo 2007 will take place at the Metropolis Mall, next to the Hung Hom KCR station. Highlights include:
    - A world premiere showcasing of Sangokuden - Sou Sou Gundam, Sonken Gundam and a top secret large-size display at the venue.
    - The famous Japanese Gundam designer, Mr. Hajime Katoki, is coming to the expo - a first outside of Japan.
    - Bandai Gunpla World Cup and BB Senshi Sangokuden Gunpla competitions.
    - The latest Gundam 00 Gunpla, MG kits, gashapon and candy will be showcased.
    - Free Gunpla construction class workshop, gashapon panel, Gundam War CCG competition & workshop.
    - Lots of fun and exciting minigames and stage events.
    - Limited edition Gunpla and newest Gundam goodies will be on sale, including the new BB Senshi Sangokuden Peach Orchard Oath Set.
    - Lots of other exciting events open to all. Admission is free.

    The official site can be seen here. Thanks to Hi Nu Gundam for the translation.
