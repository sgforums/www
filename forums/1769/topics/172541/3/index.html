    btw, if you guys are planning to buy Abalone for reunion, here are some tips when comparing prices.

    Nett Weight vs. Solid Weight
    e.g. New Moon abalone might be cheaper than Calmex and they have pretty much the same nett weight. but if you check the solid weight of the can (which is wat your want), calmex is much heavier. Therefore if you take that into consideration, the price of Calmex is not that expensive. In term of taste and texture, I think Calmex is the best.

    Fake Calmex
    I have seen a few Calmex look alike abalone before. Just take note, Calmex cans are printed, while fakes are using paper labels.
