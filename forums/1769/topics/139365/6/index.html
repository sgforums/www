    Originally posted by sbst275:
    But powerful woman rather be submissive at home... Anyone know why?
    Because of one word ... ... ... ... ... L-O-V-E!

    Being powerful and assertive at work is one thing, but the home is not a corporate battlefield. Rather, it's a place lovingly built by a couple and where both should trust and love each other enough to do things for each other in love. Love makes us choose to do things willingly for our special ones.

    That's why I take such a stand against the word obey. If I know that my husband loves eating his prawns peeled, I will gladly do so out of love, and he wouldn't even need to ask, and NOT out of obedience because he ordered me, 'Hey, peel my prawns for me, will ya?'. Things like that should be done with quiet love, not quiet obedience.

    To obey means that there has to be a 'command', an 'instruction' given which you have to follow and 'obey'. I'd rather things at home be done because you love him, because he loves you, no powerplay, no boardroom-like battles, no scheming and strategising or megalomaniac ego at work!
