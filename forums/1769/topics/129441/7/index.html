    I just tried this chillie crab recipe last night (its Violet Oons) and it was scrumptious!! Just like Long Beach's chillie crabs!!

    Sauce:
    1 cup water
    5 tablespoons tomato ketchup
    1 1/2 teaspoon sugar to taste
    1 1/2 tablespoon corn flour
    1/4 teaspoon salt

    Prawns:
    1lb large crabs cleaned and quartered(or prawns, crayfisf, lobster...all good!!)
    3 tablespoons vegetable oil
    8 cloves garlic, roughly chopped
    8 fresh chillies, roughly chopped
    1 egg
    1 teaspoon lime juice or lemon juice

    Make sauce by whisking together all ingredients.
    Heat oil, add garlic, stir fry for 1 min. Add chillies and stir fry another 1 min
    Add crabs and stir fry till shells turn slightly pink/red.
    Add the sauce and fry till shell are red. Break the egg and using a fork streak through the egg through the sauce. Simmer for few seconds and remove. Add in lime or lemon juice.(dont skip this part...its what makes it authentic!)

    Enjoy your crabs with deep fried man tou!!!
