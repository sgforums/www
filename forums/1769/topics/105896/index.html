    Some sit-down event, small and cozy, where we can all talk and get to know one another. Comfy seats and nice, cozy ambience a great plus! I think for a first outing, don't make it activity-filled cause the most important thing is to get to know the folks!

    A meal followed by drinks would be great! Also, would be good to avoid the crowded areas this time of year. Wouldn't relish hawk-eyed waiters trying to make you vacate the premises asap!

    I'd recommend Foster's at Holland V. Haven't been there myself but passed it so many nights when I'm off Evening duty and folks seem to enjoy themselves there. The place is romantically lit (ie. dimly lit) with candlelights (or votive lights) in their outdoor seating area and they have a resident guitarist who plays wonderful jazz / long songs / easy listening songs. Don't know about the food but they must be pretty decent cause they're still surviving when a lot of other restaurants have closed down in this area and on top of it, they've expanded their seating capacity.

    Food for thought... open to other ideas and suggestions.
