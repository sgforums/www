    You can always get a basic hat and add things like flowers, fur, feathers, jewels, beads, etc etc to jazz it up.  That gives it a unique touch.  If you want to go this craft-y way, you can easily get the things you'll need at Spotlight, which I believe is still the largest haberdashery in Singapore. 

    I don't think dressy hats are easy to find in Singapore simply because, well, I don't remember seeing anyone in Singapore walking around wearing hats! 

    BTW, are you thinking of wearing them in some special function?  Here in Melbourne, once a year during the races, women deck themselves in all manner of ridiculous hats and fascinators in their hair.  You can often get a good laugh from it.
