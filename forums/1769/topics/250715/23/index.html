    Originally posted by laurence82:
    i used to have migraines when i was young, and that was when nurse found that i have low blood pressure

    got relationship between blood pressure and migraine?

    Of course!!

    Cranial (Brain) blood vessels are very fine and very sensitive to every little dilation and constriction. Changes in your blood pressure either ways will cause changes to your blood vessels as they change their sizes to adapt to the extra / lessened pressure of the blood flowing through it, ie. high blood pressure, vessels dilate; low blood pressure, vessels constrict.
