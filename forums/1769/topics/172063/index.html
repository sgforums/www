    This evening, my parents and I drove down to my brother and his wife's new home.

    It was tastefully renovated! I liked the decor and they had a wonderful view of the koi pond from their master bedroom and living room bay window!

    I loved the bookshelves in their study area too! I want bookshelves just like that!

    That got me thinking... it must be a wonderful feeling when you get your first home! Be it a bachelor / bachelorette pad or a newly-married couples first home, I'm sure there's an unforgettable feeling when you first get the keys to your home!

    I know quite a few of you here are newlyweds or own your own home. Tell us how it felt and other interesting stuff like how you decided on the decor theme, etc.

    If you don't own your own home yet, share with us how your dream home would be like!
  • Rhonda
    1 Jan 06, 21:56
    Oh, and one thing mom and I observed and discussed ...

    Couples these days expect a larger living space than before. Meaning, it's common for them to combine two bedrooms to form one large master bedroom. Even the singles relish a huge bedroom cum study / walk-in-wardrobe kinda concept.

    It used to be a couple purchase a three-bedroom apt and one would be the bedroom, two would be the kids' rooms.

    Now, it's more like two bedrooms would be combined as one huge master bedroom or one room would be a bedroom, another would be his study, the remaining one would be her study!

    I guess there are higher expectations these days!
