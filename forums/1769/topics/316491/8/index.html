    Cane doesn't mean you have to physically hit the animal with the cane.  Just instill fear with the sound can liao!

    I used to condition my cats to fear the cane.  Whenever they are up to no good, or if they have damaged something, I will always do the following:

    - Yell out, "WEI!!!"

    - Purposely stomp to the 2nd bedroom and reach up the cupboard where I hid the cane;

    - Purposely draw out the cane with a lot of fanfare... sssssswwwwhack!!
