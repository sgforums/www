    This is just Day 3 of being connected and my life has been an utter waste.

    I spend my days sitting in front of the PC, surfing, chatting, posting, playing games. OK, I did do a little bit of ''work'' in MS Word and MS Powerpoint but that's about it.

    As a result, I've been sleep-deprived, and I've started to have bad eating habits for the first time since I came here. I ''forget'' my meals, then feel too lazy to cook / go out to get food, so I end up eating loads of junk food and snacks.

    I feel like that bloke who did that documentary, "Fast Food Nation" now except this is the computer habits version of it. I now suffer from shoulderache, backache, bum ache, a general feeling of being unwell, and bloatedness.

    Did you guys realise how damaging sitting in front of that PC for hours each day at a stretch can do to you?
