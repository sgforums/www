    ok, here's my review of the 2 pubs I went to on Friday with modgurl and her friend... watz his name.... aye, forget it.

    China One (Clarke Quay):
    Pretty good Live Band
    A couple of Pool Tables
    Moderately Crowded
    Too few seats for a big place
    Plenty of babes

    Other comments - Although itz free-sitting you really need to book a seat, or go there early just to grab a seat, cos by 10pm or so, there won't be any left. The music is very loud, so you'll have to yell it out loud just to talk to someone beside you normally. I have to say this but, the people there are pretty fake.

    Blue Bar Bristo (Central Mall):
    Very good 3 men band with amazing vocals
    Nice Atmosphere
    1 large bar table in the middle, served by gorgerous barmaids
    Plenty of seats
    Lesser moderately crowded

    Other comments - Itz DE perfect place, so far, to relax. To take your mind off horrible thoughts and to de-stress. The band have amazing vocals. How amazing you asked?? Well, if Taufake was to sing beside them, he's just a pee-squeak. No offence to Taufake fans, but you'll have to listen to it for yourself.

    Think I'll frequent Blue Bar more. It has a more relaxing atmosphere and friendly looking barmaids, got to know one of their names by the way, not much to brag about.... heh heh . So any kakis wanna go there every friday, just PM me and I'll give u my hp number to contact.

    I've also learnt something, itz no fun going to the pub/bar alone. I've experienced this on friday while waiting for modgurl... and yes obvious she's late.... ha ha !! She was driving and was waiting for a good spot to park. Anyway, while I was waiting for her, I just stand there in China One, listening to the band... maybe itz just me but, I can picture myself how I looked like a complete loser standing alone there.... ... ha ha!!
