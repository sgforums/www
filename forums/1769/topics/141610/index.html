    Another 'bo liao' topic by Elindra. Anyone can continue on with the different topics. Basically it is your observation on things going around in SG - Be it happy, sad, touching, hilarious etc.

    I'm writing on taxi drivers because in my new job, I get in contact with them lots. So far, I find that most taxi uncles are really nice people and they have really funny and hilarious views (really hit the nail on the head though) about the government and the current economic situation.

    Also, they tend to have alot of advise for you on life; Be it religion, law, ghost stories, accidents, latest headlines, holidays etc

    I have met the mean drivers before but I find most of them really nice. They usually get very happy and excited when you talk to them. I guess it can be pretty boring driving all day with most customers ignoring them or being unreasonable.

    I wonder is that why most of the uncles waive off the cents and round down the bill for me because I listen to them Most of them also tend to pour out their greviances and unhappiness. I have to say it is a pretty thankless job but alot of them really take pride in their work

    Anyway, these are my ramblings for the day
