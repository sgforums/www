    it really depends on how you see it
    a marriage of true minds is not about the certificate at all

    rituals are important in human society
    it can be as simple a ritual as saying 'i love you' and meaning it
    it formalises a vow you make to the other person, at that time,
    with the intention and belief that you want to keep that vow

    marriage for me symbolises a ritual where my husband and I
    inform the people we love and live with that we love each other,
    and we hope that they will love and respect our spouse too because of this relationship.

    It formalises the relationship between me and him
    and through this, I also transform from an individual in society
    (where I make individual decisions that impact mostly just me)
    to a duo-vidual - where now my decisions are made based on what is good
    for BOTH of us (which means I am sorry girlfriend when I can't join you for that
    tea and shopping date when he is sick)

    but yes, it can all change too. with or without the cert. But that at one point of my or his life, we had thought it could have/might have last forever, this love, makes all the difference.
