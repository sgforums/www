    1 small sweet potato (cut to cube)
    1/2 yam (cut to cube)
    1 tapioca (cut to cube)
    1/2 grated white coconut (extract 500ml santan)
    1/4 tsp salt
    1/2 teacup / cup or measuring cup of sugar
    1 tbsp sago
    2 pandan leaves

    method
    steam the sweet potato, tapioca and yam till cooked
    put santan, salt, pandan leaves in saucepan and boil
    add sugar
    put cooked sweet potato, yam, tapioca and sago
    boil gently till sago is cooked

    * sago must be last to put for cooking. if not afraid the other ingredients like yam etc will nt be cooked in balance...
