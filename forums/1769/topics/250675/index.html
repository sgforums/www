Learning something new...

  • jetta
    26 Apr 07, 01:01
    I learnt that almost every car has a cabin air filter located in the exterior of the glove compartment. This filter traps air coming into the car. Depending on where you live, you can find leaves, etc in the filter which should be changed annually. I'm surprised that I've gone on for this long without knowing about the existence of such a thing. My knee jerk reflex was to call and order the part for both of our cars. Ticket shock though: US$61.36 for my car and $80 for the hubster's.

    Why am I always the last one to find out?
