    check this out.. i got email from Sun with Moon (the one ferret could not go.. and had the squid stuffed with liver)

    For The Very First Time In Singapore, Japanese Dining SUN at CHIJMES and sister restaurants SUN with MOON Japanese Dining & Café, at both Wheelock Place and The Central, presents an exclusive Kyushu Gourmet Experience (from 15th April to 9th June 2008) featuring fresh seafood and ingredients specially brought in from Kyushu and local dishes inspired by the region.

     Highlights include:

     1. Prawn & Lettuce Sushi Roll

    This tasty original Miyazaki creation is made by rolling prawn, lettuce and mayonnaise in sushi rice and seaweed.

     2. Awabi (Abalone)

    Japanese abalone is prized for their delicate sweet flavours and subtle hint of the sea. Beautiful as sashimi (with its firm crunchy texture) and is also favoured for Teppanyaki.

     3. Amami Keihan (Chicken Rice with Broth)

    Hearty dish of shredded chicken, egg, spring onions on rice and served with savoury broth from the Amami district of Kagoshima.

     4. Karashi Renkon (Lotus Root with Mustard Miso)

    Sliced lotus root stuffed with a special paste of miso and mustard and deep fried. Crispy, spicy with a touch of sweet-savoury from the miso.

     5. Nagahama Ramen

    This Kyushu-styled Ramen from the Nagahama district has thin noodles in a tonkotsu broth made from pork bone stock and served with homemade char siew and a boiled egg.

     6. Ika (Squid)

    One of the specialties of Kyushu is fresh sashimi of squid. Thinly sliced, it looks like strips of white translucent noodles and is highly enjoyable with a light dip in Japanese soy sauce.

     7. Hakata Mentai (Spicy Cod Roe)

    From the Hakata district of Fukuoka city, this famous spicy cod roe is taken as an accompaniment and is delicious with rice, sushi or mixed into dishes for that “pop” in texture and flavour.

     And to enjoy this special dinner menu of over 40 items in true Kyushu fashion, SUN will also be serving some of Kyushu’s famous Shochus (a clear Japanese distilled spirit) made from a variety of ingredients, including imo (sweet potato), satsuma imo (purple sweet potato), goma (sesame), mugi (barley), kokuto (black sugar) and soba (buckwheat).

     The Kyushu Gourmet Experience menu is available from 15th April to 9th June 2008.
