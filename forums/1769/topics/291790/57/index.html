    Elindra, how do you select the designers? Can you ask them to do up proposals for you and must you pay for it?
    Well, if you ask them for their ideas and roughly how the things are laid out to the floor plan, you don't need to pay, unless you wanna pay them for the design to be done up in 3D artwork etc.

    I select them based on the following criterias:
    1. How they expand my initial idea
    Sometimes I give them something stupid and see if they will correct me or not
    I dun want a designer to give me everything I want even if it is fugly.

    2. The estimate for materials and the quality they give
    Got some the materials they give is lousier than what I have not but more expensive

    3. I also ask if they have pictures of their earlier works to see if I like the styles or not

    4. I also see how fast they get back to me lor
    Got some of them sibei xia lan one. You can chase them forever and they won't get back to you coz they think you will not increase your budget or you want something cheap. Ok lor, if you think I can't pay I won't use it. Simple as that
