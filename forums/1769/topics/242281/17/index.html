    good friend did her wedding at Raffles Hotel. Booking a ballroom and a separate break out room that is used as a cocktail waiting area as well a serve the tea ceremony rooms for relatives that was late and couldnt make it for the earlier tea ceremony at the homes.

    There was 2 dinners, one chinese and 2nd one was western.

    Her morning, was fetch the bride routine and tea ceremony routine ending at her matrimonial home.

    Her afternoon, she did the soloeminsation at the Raffles Hotel Garden at the Pavilion. Pavilion was decorated nicely with added touches of flowers and pretty angelic flower gals.

    1st dinner was chinese dinner, so she uses the break out room for cocktail waiting and some more tea ceremony. then its in for the dinner.

    The 2nd dinner, was done on the lower floor ballroom, with a big square dance floor.

    flower arrangements was huge and beautiful, with 2 different bottles of wine and the meal is western muslim.

    For her march in, guest lights sparklers, she walks in with her hubby and they start the first dance of waltz on the dancefloor as per angmoh style. to which, her hubby's friends gives toast to the couple. The guest list for the 2nd night is mostly folks that flew in as far away as switzerland, belgium & europe.

    We had a problem with the sparklers cos we didnt want to set off the fire alarm with the sparklers smoke. But it went on very well after discussion with the hotel people and they even dimm the lights for her entrance.
