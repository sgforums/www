    Has anyone been to a outdoor solmisation? How you feel about it, what was the wet weather contingency plan, dress code, location, afternoon or evening?

    depending on theme, usually outdoor solmisation is more casual and relax... afternoon gives u better photos but hot lor

    try asking fort canning or alkaff mansion... they have such events...

    Any other good deco ideas dinner venue more 'unique'? the table and chairs r white (cannot change color ) and i dun like white

    u have to decide on a color scheme and let the hotel knows.. usually they will cater their decor to ur needs... u want anything special, have to let them noe exactly wat u want.. if not u'll get the "standard" decor de...

    Anyone used wedding planners? Are they value for money or not necessary?

    wedding planners help u ease off the burden of all the nitty gitty stuffs a wedding will bring... not necessary unless u have the cash for it... u just need a couple of close gfs who's married or been thru enuff weddings to help u...

    for bridal studios, any good ones to recommend? and why?

    try look at the few at tanjong pagar... wedding news have a more "expensive" outlet along the same stretch... very nice gowns and each design is only one piece de..

    anyone knows of a good live band that will be good for wedding dinner?

    seriously hor... ppl are too bz eating or toasting with u to notice the live band de... unless u want it as a performing item, then again tat might drag ur dinner longer... hotel usually have time constrain...

    i was told hard liquor no corkage charge. if Jack, Jim and Johnny was served instead of Tiger, Merlot and Chardonnay, will it be very un-wedding like?

    i prefer martell than wine lor... but yah dun look so "weddingly".. but hey u're there in ur gown to remind them wat! plus i'm sure they won't forget since they have given u angbaos for it...

    the most important question: how to convince parents that having a wedding during chinese ghost festival is OK? (i dun wan to change date )

    this one hor.. seriously, it's difficult, esp. if ur parents are superstituous... unless u can tell them during tat period u got discounts for everything then u can afford the wedding otherwise they'll have to pay for it

    and if they agree and u get a free wedding, then change date lah! save ur own $$ for a fantastic honeymoon!
