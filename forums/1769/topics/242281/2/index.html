    Girlieee Bride-to-be!

    There are good, credible vendors around but alot depends on your budget . I notice no budget stated heheh.

    Has anyone been to a outdoor solmisation? How you feel about it, what was the wet weather contingency plan, dress code, location, afternoon or evening?

    Need to request for hotel to provide wet weather plan or marquee for your solemnization ceremony

    Anyone knows a good florist that does those floral arrangement for weddings? like decorating the chairs with flowers, etc.

    Ask from hotel if they have recommended florist, they usually do. Advantage - florist is familiar with venue, hotel trusts florist.

    Any other good deco ideas dinner venue more 'unique'? the table and chairs r white (cannot change color ) and i dun like white

    As above, ask the recommended florist what they can do. E.g. colored table cloths on top of the white ones and floral arrangements.

    Anyone used wedding planners? Are they value for money or not necessary?

    IMO, not necessary. You will need a team of friends or relatives to work with you, for pre-wedding preparation work as well as duties on the actual day. (need help just sms :^D)

    for bridal studios, any good ones to recommend? and why?

    Budget is key here. IMO you can shop around, start with bridal magazines, then visit a studio whose gowns you like. Then go back singaporebrides.com to check for reviews.

    What to look out for - a designer than is gam with you in personality , who has a sense of aesthetics that is gam with yours i.e. both have same concept of "nice / pretty". Also important is credibility - if shop has reputation of being late, last minute work, drop immediately.

    anyone knows of a good live band that will be good for wedding dinner?
