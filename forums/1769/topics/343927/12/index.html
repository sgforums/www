    Bacon Explosion


    Ingredients:

    2 pounds thick cut bacon
    2 pounds Italian sausage
    1 jar of your favorite barbeque sauce
    1 jar of your favorite barbeque rub

    Create a 5x5 bacon weave.. get it as tight as you can

     

    add barbeque seasoning

     


    add loose sausage about the same thickness as the bacon...


    add fried bacon (resist temptation to eat the bacon)

    add more barbeque seasoning...


    roll the sausage gently into a roll, careful to pinch the ends to seal in the bacon goodness...

     


    once the sausage roll is secure, roll up the bacon weave... sprinkle more barbeque seasoning.

     

    over about 165 degrees, 1 hour for every inch diameter.. slowly cook the log...


    there.. almost all done... after about 2 1/2 hours..


    Slather barbeque sauce on it...


    Slice it up, eat and have a heart attack with a smile on your face
