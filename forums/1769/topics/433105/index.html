    I went to Tarafuku @ 313 ytd for the first time... Ard $33 per pax on a weekday dinner...

    U get a small hotpot to do shabu shabu and u can choose a selection of cook food as well... Typical japanese buffet fare

    Food was okay, slightly above average... Sashimi was fresh, just that the cuts are abit wierd at certain pieces... The soft shell crab is super oily and soggy even if just fried -___- The miso fish and the braise pork and egg is excellent... Various desserts is good... The others like Teriyaki Ika is average... Sushi is not very well done, the rice falls off too easily... Yakitori meat was good, but they dun serve it with yakitori sauce, extremely wasted becoz it was just nicely done but without the sauce >___>

    spread abit little when compared to places like kushin bo...

    IMO, not worth the money... I would prefer suki sushi (the now Ishimura/nihonmura) at hougang plaza...

    Anyway, Ytd aborted my Ban Heng plans for this week. Instead I went over to ABC Market...
