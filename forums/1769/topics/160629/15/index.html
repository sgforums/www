    Hmm... I've been wearing a uniform now for the past 3 years +. I must say that there are pros and cons.

    Pros :
    - don't need to think about what to wear everyday
    - cheap - uniforms are provided free!
    - everyone sticks to a dress code and, in places like schools, it can save the students' parents a lotta money because kids in school won't be able to go around spending their parents' money buying designer wear, trying to do the one-up! (Can hear the moms and dads heaving a sigh now! haha!)

    Cons :
    - no creativity, no leeway to express your individuality
    - boring, staid!
    - if the style of your uniform doesn't suit you, you'll look like a walking pumpkin or whatever!
