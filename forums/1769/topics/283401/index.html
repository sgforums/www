    When we started to look at developing an online business model, we specifically wanted to come up with something that addressed the issue of spiralling house prices. It took us a while (over a year) but we were convinced that we could come up with a business that would help a few fortunate people own a home of their own.

    The property auctions on the Humraz website do just that.

    The auctions are self-funding, in that the Â£3/bid charge for bid status messages allows us to sell the properties at ludicrously low prices, as long as enough people participate by bidding online or by SMS.
