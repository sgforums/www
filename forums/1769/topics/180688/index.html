    I have plenty of childhood treasures and a few of them are still around, thanks to grandma, who keeps the toys 'for my kids'.



    Anyways, here are a few of them :

    - Long-necked giraffe on wheels with different-coloured rings around its neck. The rings get larger around the base. I loved that toy, for some reason or other! As I grew up, I didn't play with the rings around its neck as much but still, I loved to have it around.

    - Kiddy plastic stool with a red top and beige base. I upturned it and it became my 'stove' for imitating the porridge lady! I even imitated how she would knock her scoop against the side of the saucepan just before she pours the porridge into bowls!

    - Kiddy Dim Sum Trolley with fake food, a teapot set and a fake birthday cake. My absolute favourite toy!! We weren't rich and mom had to save up for a few weeks then before she could afford to purchase that for me.

    - Giant Panda Soft Toy from uncle. I loved it to bits cause when I was a kiddo, it was taller than me! However, grandma wanted to keep it in pristine condition, , so she kept it in a large transparent plastic cover and only let me play with it on my birthdays! A few years back, she wanted to show how well-kept it still was but when she removed the cover, fur flew all over! She should've jus tlet me play with it when I was a kiddy !

    - Red and Yellow 'Tractor' that my brother and I would ride around the house. We used to fight over whose turn it was to ride the tractor. It was discarded years later when the wheels got so worn-out smooth, we'd try to pedal as hard as we could but still remain on the same spot!

    Hmm... can't remember the other toys for now. Will add another post in here as and when I can recall.
