    Advice? Eh... Tie the tourniquet real tight and clench and unclench your fists for a while so that your veins will become more prominent, then it'll be easier for your partner to get the IV cannula in.

    Before inserting the needle, also make sure you use your thumb of your other hand to 'hold down' or 'anchor' the vein first.

    And, when you're ready to insert the needle, do it fast. The more you hesitate, the harder it gets. Plus, some people have veins that will 'move' or 'contract' once the needle is in so you have to do it quick.
