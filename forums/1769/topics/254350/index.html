    Thought I'd start a series of questionnaires and discussions for us in Club 30 to get to know each other better. Instead of asking for personal particulars like filling up some form, there'll be a few questions on different aspects of our lives. Sometimes, you can get to know a person well just from the way they live their lives, their habits, etc.

    (I know a few twisted individuals here would like to have the information to keep as fodder for criticisms and assaults in future, but nevermind, we'll just let the twisted go their own twisted ways! )

    Let's start with Part I where we shall look at our habitat! Answer them and give as many details and comments as you can!

    Questions :

    1. What type of place do you live in?

    2. Who does most of the housework? Why?

    3. How would you describe the condition of your room?

    4. If budget is not a problem, how would you decorate your house? What kind of style?

    5. In your opinion, which is the most important room in the house? Why?

    6. Is your house always in a clean, organised condition and ready to receive guest without prior notice? Or do you feel uncomfortable having guests over without prior notice?

    7. Dust Bunnies Test - Do you think someone with a dust allergy will be alright in your house?

    8. How often do you invite friends / guests over? Why or why not?

    Alrighty... answer away!
