    Receipe for TungHoon Salad

    Ingredients: -

    500gm prawns, shelled and steamed
    80gm tunghoon, soaked in boiling water and drained
    100gm shallots, sliced
    3 stalks serai, sliced finely
    3 limau perut leaves, shredded finely
    4 red chillies, sliced finely
    a few springs of coriander leaves, chopped
    200 gm roasted groundnuts, pounded coarsely
    75ml lemon juice
    3 tbsps fish sauce
    1 tbsp sugar

    Method

    1. Dissolve sugar in the lemon juice and fish sauce
    2. Combine all the other ingredients in a salad bowl and mix well
    3. Pour in the lemon juice and mix well.
    4. Serve
