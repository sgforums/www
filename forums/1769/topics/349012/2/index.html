    Hmm... how I eat my sandwich depends on my mood.  Often, I'll just chew through it.  Sometimes, I'll sit in front of the TV, peel off the crust to chew on it first, then nibble through the sandwich.  Othertimes, I'll cut it in half, either diagonally or otherwise, and nibble half at a time.  On other occasions, I'll wrap the half in a paper napkin so I don't dirty my hands, before eating. Sometimes, I hold the sandwich vertically, esp if it's cut diagonally, and chew from one tip upwards. 

    I used to finish up the plain noodles first before eating the good stuff like the char siew, wanton, fishballs, etc last.  However, when I was dating, too often, my dates would say, "Oh, you don't want those izzit?  I finish them for you!" and then proceeded to plunder from my plate!!    I save the best for last, only to have other people steal them!!    Therefore, due to competition for the good bits, I now eat the noodles and the good stuff at the same time.
