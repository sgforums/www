Anyway, we filled a small plastic bottle with said powder, then fabricated an electrical detonator by soldering wires to a small flashlight bulb, breaking the glass to expose the filament, inserting the bulb into the neck of the bottle, then sealing the neck with melted candle wax that was allowed to harden, with the wires sticking out of the wax and connected to another set of electrical wires long enough to allow detonation from a safe distance.

    We detonated a few of the smaller bottles at various remote locations in Singapore--the blasts weren't all that spectacular, but they were fun. The bigger ones, we blew up at a remote location on the northern part of Pulau Ubin. Fortunately, we were never caught.
