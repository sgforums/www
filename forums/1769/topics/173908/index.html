    Here's an interesting article in the Jul 2001 issue of Redbook mag :

    The #1 Cause of Work Stress : UNCONTROLLABLE NOISE

    ''Feeling unproductive and stressed out at work?

    Everyday background noise - the copier whirring, phones ringing, coworkers chatting - may be to blame, according to a recent study at Cornell University.

    ''You may not even be aware of these sounds, but they make it more difficult to concentrate, which wrecks havoc on your motivation and stress level,'' says study coauthor, Gary Evans, PhD.

    To keep all that racket from ruining your day, restructure your schedule : If your office is quieter in the morning, use that time for tasks that require concentration (reading, writing) and save mindless duties (opening mail, filing) for noisier periods.''
