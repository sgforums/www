(I'm blatantly disregarding the ah-peks and the chee hongs running rampant out there ) Somehow having nice, well kept and clean fingernails would give the impression of hygiene. And if you wear nail polish, there's nothing that irks me more than chipped polish! Imagine seeing this lady... well groomed, beautiful and poised. You are intrigued. You think to yourself... hmmm.... attractive.

    and then you see the chipped red polish.



    So much for that.

    All we actually need for upkeep and maintenance is a nail file, cuticle scissors, and a buffer. Relatively cheap items.
