    Ben & Jerry's ice cream festival @ Marina Barrage on September 12th, who's going?

    http://www.facebook.com/home.php?ref=logo#/event.php?eid=108974276887

    ==============================================

    This year we’ve created a moosic extravaganza where yesteryear’s music bursts alive wih Jack & Rai, Elusion featuring Melissa, and 5 other local bands. Expect mind-blowingly fun games like Giant Twisters, Guess What?, Spot-The-Difference and the i-cone-nic Vermonster Challenge. Come dressed in Hippies style and get exclusive access to a priority queue for when the craving comes!

    There’s also a free shuttle bus service to take you to Chunk Fest from Marina Bay MRT. So get your retro clothes and your stomach ready for the Chunkiest ice-cream festival ever!


    Vermonster Challenge:
    Take up the challenge to slay the Vermonster, a monster sundae that contains twenty scoops of Ben & Jerry's ice-cream, along with other delicious toppings.

    Go to selected Ben & Jerry's Scoop Shops during Mondays to Fridays before 31st August to accept the challenge. The top 2 teams from each shop will be in the epic final showdown at Chunk Fest 2009! Be prepared to come face to face with the defending champion, Team Monster Truckers!

    Fastest team win $500 worth of Ben & Jerry's ice-cream and merchandise & FREE Vermonsters at VivoCity Scoop Shop for a period of 1-year!


    Chunk Tunes:
    Here's your chance to share your moosic with the world. We are seeking bands regardless of sizes to jam with Jack & Rai and Elusion featuring Melissa at Chunk Fest 2009!

    Sign up for the audition now and show us your wicked tunes.


    Visit our website, www.benjerry.com.sg, to find out more!
