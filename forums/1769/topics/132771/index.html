    Actually, you don't have to have expensive tastes to deplete your $$.

    Just hang out A LOT for quite a long time in any cafe and that will do you in. For instance, if you meet your friends 3-4 times a week and you spend hours in a cafe, say, Coffee Bean. You each order a beverage, then you order a slice of cake each. You talk talk talk, then you order another beverage each... and that's already what... more than $20. For the sake of the discussion, let's say, $25 after tax.

    One outing $25 X 4 times in a week = $100 (per person $50) X 4 weeks in a month = $400!!

    Of course, that's just guesstimate. Sometimes, the bill is more, esp if you eat a meal first before going to the cafe. Sometimes, the bill is less.

    It's fun to be with friends and all... but going out sure is costly!
