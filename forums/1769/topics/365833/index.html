How to enlarge a ring?

  • Honeybunz
    27 Jun 09, 21:52

    I bought a ring years ago.  Over the years, my fingers become fatter.  Now I want to put on that ring of mine but it's too tight. 

    Where should I bring to to enlarge it?

  • nightzip
    27 Jun 09, 21:57

    Ah....this I know.

    You just bring it to a jeweller's shop. They will recommend a few ways, but will take your finger measurements again to know how much more to enlarge.

    1st way: Very small enlargement, they will grind/polish internal diameter of ring, to enlarge by at most 0.1 to 0.2mm increase in inner diameter.

    2nd way: Small enlargement, they will heat up the ring to slightly molten state, then use of something like a thong/rod and push it to make it with larger inner diameter.

    3rd way: Large enlargement, then they will charge more, as require more gold or silver, they will heat up the ring, then localized make a cut, and then heat up the spare gold or silver, then mold it to fill the cut gap, then overall heat up the ring again, then put over rod and then enlarge it.
