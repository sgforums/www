    but, they say, relocating to johore is definitely not their first choice, why ? cos it's not just a function of costs in Singapore. Most of their clients, especially the civil contractors, are locals. secondly, it's logistics, logistics is not just about getting things you've made to your clients, but also getting your raw materials to your factory, put a country border in between these two points, and you'll add duties and taxes on top of your costs. if you're shipping your partial components from singapore to johore and then exporting out the finished products again, paying duties and taxes both ways, then it's just as cost uneffective, if not more, with the only effect of enriching our neighbours and not ourselves ... plus you'd factor in the effeciency, or rather the lack of, of our neighbours, as pinky pointed out, the bumi monopolized transport industry up north is not cheap ! ... so imagine the steel sewage pipes you need for new buildings like the HDB flats and sky scrappers would end up costing more, driving up our costs even further ....
