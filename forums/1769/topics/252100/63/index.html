    Everything was alright until I looked back from the departure lounge and saw my friends and family there... all standing near the glass doors, a few waving, a few talking... Tears started to well up...

    I've come to realise this - there's never a perfect place on earth to settle in and sink your roots in. Rather, it's more a matter of which place appeals to you more and you can put up with the shortcomings more. Basically, humans are flexible survivors and I think the cowboys / Red Indians used to say something like wherever I lay my head, shall be my home.
