    Narnia vs LOTR
    (pls note comments are in relative to the other movie and purely personal opinion)

    Storyline:
    Narnia = somewhat simplistic, I think owing to fact that this is a children's book
    LOTR = more complex story

    Movie enjoyment factor:
    Narnia = 7/10, still worth the movie dollar
    LOTR = 8/10

    CGI eye poppers:
    LOTR = good
    Narnia = slightly below good, but still good. I like the Minotaurs.

    Hunk factor:
    Narnia = zero!
    LOTR = wins hands down

    Cheem factor (can I understand the movie without reading the book?) :
    Narnia = definitely
    LOTR = yes, but must pay attention when watching @.@

    Encore! Can't wait for next movie!! factor:
    Narnia: not really. Perhaps because ending was not left hanging.
    LOTR = yes

    Wet wanton count T___T :
    Narna = 2 . First few minutes of movie was definitely heart-wrenching & tear inducing
    LOTR = uuh not much. Maybe 0.5.
