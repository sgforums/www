    Oh I tried Pasta Inc behind my office on Friday

    Had a strong craving for Carbonara so I went there

    The place was packed and you needed a reservation!

    Anyway they only had a table outside by the road and I took it anyway.

     

    I had Seafood Soup and Carbonara.

    Seafood Soup was good. I tend not to like tomato soup but this was full of flavor and slightly tart without being overwhelmingly tomato-ish. There was seafood (mussel, prawn & squid) with every mouthful but I thought the soup portion was really small.

     

    Carbonara was of reasonable portions. It is good (not overwhelmingly creamy and you can taste the egg and it’s not overpowering but was a tad salty for me). I drank like 2 glasses of iced water. Not tried the other stuff but I figure there has to be some standard for it to be packed

     

    Anyway, what impressed me was the service. Although I was cut out by being the only table outside, they were very prompt and checked on me frequently, even updating on the status of my food. At the end of it, they gave me a 10% discount (they didn’t have to) for not being able to seat me inside. I will definitely be going back again.
