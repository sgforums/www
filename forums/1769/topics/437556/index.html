    Chop Mui Lee Bakery, a traditional teochew bakery which as been in business making traditional pastries for over 70 years will be closing shop this year as the owners retire.

    They told me with a tinge of regret that they could not find anyone who would apprentice with them and take over the shop. Since they were getting on in age, they decided to close shop this year and retire.

    They also lamented that the other traditional teochew bakery in Singapore was also closing shop this year for the same reason.

    Singapore will be much poorer for the loss of these traditional bakeries
