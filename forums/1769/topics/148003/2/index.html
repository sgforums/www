    There are unlimited ways u can cook instant noodles. Just be creative and see wat's available in kitchen or go supermarket to get something.

    - i have tried using those "Rou Gu Cha" bags in supermarket, the type u put the bag to make Rou Gu Cha soup. I just boil the bag and add some other stuffs like pepper, garlic, dark soy sauce etc. Until i try the soup is tasty enuff, i add in the noodle that i boil with another pot.

    - i often fry instant noodles, it's nice, but more work has to be done, and need to wash the wok after that.

    - u can also buy curry from prata shop, just the curry only. then cook the instant noodle with plain water, drained all the water away then mix the curry with the noodle. U can do this with pasta sauce or anything similar that u like.

    - adding corn in soup(those in packs that u can buy in supermarket) will make the soup like paste, is another different feeling.

    But these method makes instand noodles not "instant" anymore.
