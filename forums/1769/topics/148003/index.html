    Originally posted by hisoka:
    looks good but if you do that then its like cooking normal mee mah not really instant noodles le
    It's still faster than cooking normal mee wor...

    Everything's microwaved.

    I poured 'enough' water into my microwaveable container, cooked it on High for 2 mins to make it hot, rinsed and dumped my dou miao inside, then cooked it again on High for another 1-2 mins, together with my Abalone mushrooms.

    Next, remove the vegetables into your bowl BUT don't discard the water! Use it to boil your Lobster balls on High for 1.5mins. Next, put in your instant Mee Sua and cook for another 1.5 mins on High. Remove from microwave, pour in your seasonings, and voila!
