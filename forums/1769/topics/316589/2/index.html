    I bought a few other bottles of pre-mixed drinks with much lower alcohol content.  There's a XuXu 50% strawberry drink that I wanted to buy but the expiry date is in June 08, so no-go. 

    Discovered a newly-opened authentic Japanese restaurant along Bridge Road!  Spanking new and the menu looks awesome!   I bought takeaway but I think I shall return again soon with friends to dine-in! 

    Returned home, drank one glass of Pina Colada with just 17% alcohol, but whoooosh!!!  I think I'm a bit tipsy now!    I can feel the hot flush in my cheeks, and my head isn't spinning yet but I'm getting there!    I feel funny when I walk around and almost walked smack into the bedroom door, so I think my alcohol tolerance is extremely low, and darnit, I got myself tipsy! 

    Sigh... think I'd better forget about Cachacas!
