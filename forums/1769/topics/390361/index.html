    8 Feb 10, 07:52

    I took this pic because they eat cucumber and pineapple with belachan. It's really good though

     

    My fav veggie in Kuching, the 'Bilin'! It's actually a wild fern and it is the veggie I order all the time. There is another type called the 'Pacu' but no luck finding it.

     

    My fav cooking method for the 'Bilin' is stir-frying it with the Kuching Home-Made Red Wine. Really very fragrant! It smells a bit like Hua Tiao Jiu but much more fragrant.

     

    I did eat a normal veg dish too. Fried Long Beans with Minced Pork and Mushrooms! Very Yummy too!
