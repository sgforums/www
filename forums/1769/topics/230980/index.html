    We have a separate fridge and a separate freezer at home so we usually do a big meat shopping and store them in the freezer which can last the 2 of us for 4 - 6 weeks.

    As for toilet papers, detergents, soap, shampoo, toothpaste, etc, they usually last for a bit so we don't need to get them very often.

    So our weekly grocery shopping usually consists mostly perishables like vegies, fruits, milk, yoghurt, bread (even that we usually buy a few loaves if it's on special to freeze them in the freezer) etc. However, we tend to buy other stuffs too like chips, cookies.... whatever we are craving for at that time . So my piece of advice - NEVER GO SHOPPING WHEN YOU'RE HUNGRY!!!

    Still, our weekly grocery bill can add up to at least Aus$50 or sometimes under Aus$100 depending on what we need or get on that week. It's never fixed.

    Yes, things are getting really expensive nowadays!!
