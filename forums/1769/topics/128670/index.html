    Heres a review from E! entertainment....
    "Danny Boyle has found God. The director may have always had the faith, but the hollow-eyed junkies careening through Trainspotting and the murderous zombies of 28 Days Later gave us little indication of a religious streak. Millions, however, is a snappy advertisement for Christianity, a morality play complete with saintly visitations and a blessed innocent. Alexander Nathan Etel is the youngest son of a recently widowed father, struggling to do the right thing sans the guidance of his beloved mum. When a miracle arrives in the form of a bag full of crisp English pounds, Etel and his brother must decide what to do the with money before England goes over to the Euro. Dry Brit humor, fine acting and propulsive direction keep things from getting too syrupy. In the end, the movie's moral message is refreshing--even if Boyle does beat us over the head with it.
