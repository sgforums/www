    13 Apr 06, 00:00
    Went to the Members Preview this afternoon!

    It's the same like all Times' previous Books WArehouse Sales at the Expo - boxes of books placed on top of makeshift tables, neatly categorised according to areas - Children, Fiction, Self-Help, Food and Cooking, Computing, Stationery, Sports, Health, etc. Archie comics and other comics were available too!

    Outdated mags as recent as Jan 2006 were available at just $3 each! I saw Australian Women's Weekly, Vogue, Vogue Entertaining, etc. Not sure if they'll have other mags available on other days.

    Most of the fiction weren't new ones, although I spotted four copies of Robin Cook's latest fiction there. I saw two copies of 'Out' for just $5.

    Go take a look if you can!
