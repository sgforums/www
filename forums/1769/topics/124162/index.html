    ... does not seem to be that common, which is a sad, sad thing.

    I've been increasingly frustrated in my interactions with folks who just can't seem to think coherently. They keep missing the point, misinterpreting facts or what was said, or they'll get distracted and go off-tangent altogether and then when you try to draw them back to the main topic at hand again, they just don't or won't or can't get it.

    The shocking thing is, even university graduates can be guilty of this!

    I'm starting to think if there's something seriously wrong with our education system here.

    Also, I think something should be done to keep students awake during English lessons from Primary school onwards. Apparently, most just don't pay due attention to their English classes because if you would just look around, the grammar can be pretty atrocious. Could it be a case of us sitting on our laurels? It's amazing that we boast of English being our first language and the main language used in conducting business and yet, our mastery of the language leaves much to be desired!

    I wonder if anyone else here feels the same way about the two points I brought up. Sure hope I'm not alone in this!
