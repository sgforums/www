    hey.. who wants to make cheesy melba melts which are like the ones at Muddy's?

    i give a cheat recipe

    can of Campbell's cream of mushroom
    chopped up champignons from a can
    toast
    cheddar, mozzarella, monterey jack cheeses
    cayenne pepper

    method:

    add champignons to undiluted soup from the can.. heat up gently so that everything becomes a thick gooey mess.. careful not to burn it...

    toast 1 side bread... add a dollop of the stuff onto the toasted side of bread... cover with cheese (best to use the pre-sliced cheddar) and add the other grated cheeses on it...

    toast gently until cheeses melt over

    dust the top with cayenne pepper

    cut up into triangles and serve...
