    I'm never impressed by the brand, but by the design.  Sometimes, I walk past a shop window and see a handbag I love, I'll just go in and if the price is reasonable, I'll buy it, doesn't matter if it's non-branded, as most of my bags are. 

    The thing about a branded handbag being able to last longer, well that's not a selling point for me.  Why would you want to carry the same bag for 5 years when you can have different style bags to mix-and-match with your outfit? 

    Also, if you're paying for real leather, good workmanship, durability, and a classic design for a branded handbag, fine.  BUT, if it's just a PVC bag with a fashionable design that costs a bomb just because it's up-to-date fashion and it's branded, forget it! 

    Mom loves branded handbags and she has a huge collection.    There's this Chanel one that's taken out and used only once a year - during Chinese New Year, simply it's too precious to use on a daily basis!    She treats it like bao bei and wipes it down lovingly, admires it, uses leather polish on it, etc...  amusing to watch! 

    I feel like a vulture sometimes... patiently waiting by the sidelines for her to tire of any one bag, and then, it becomes MINE!!  My Precioussssss!!!
