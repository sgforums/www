  • the Bear
    2 Nov 09, 10:49

    got the recipe for that...

    Marinade

    onion
    shallot
    salt
    sugar
    sesame oil
    pepper
    5-spice powder
    oyster sauce
    light soy sauce
    a touch of MSG (i think can skip this)

    How
    smash and mix everything together into a paste
    slather over pork which has been stabbed many times with a fork
    put remainder into ziplock bag, shove pork in the bag
    let it absorb the goodness for a day

    Preparation
    barbeque for about 10 minutes
    slather it with white vinegar
    barbeque until it's blackened and charred all round
    slice off burnt sides, scrape off the charred bits on the skin
    slice and eat
