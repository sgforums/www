    when i seat in the interviewer panel, these are the common interview questions we ask. even if you feel these questions are nonsense, they usually make or break the interview.

    1. Just be honest. If the job is just a "stepping-stone" just say it and explain the reason why. do it in a professional manner and convince the interview you are clear of your future directions - be it staying on the in job, or looking else where in 3-4 years time.

    2. Tricky. The purpose of this question is that we want to know your background. Tell us your skill-sets and your potential for knoweldge growth. Link them to how you can use them on the job, how it will influence your work, how it will influence the people around you, how it will influence the work environment.
