    Nope, no A4-sized paper here. The standard form factor for paper is 8.5" by 11".

    I don't know if it's a good idea to bring pistol holsters with you. You and I know they're perfectly innocuous items, but the TSA stormtroopers manning the checkpoints at airports operate by a different set of rules, and will likely pull you aside for "extra attention" if they discover holsters in your luggage. Putting them in your checked baggage is no guarantee that they'll get through either; the TSA randomly selects checked luggage to be opened for additional screening after they've been checked in.

    What will your immigration status be upon arriving in the States? It might be worth mentioning that, with very few exceptions, US visitors or residents who are neither PRs nor citizens are not allowed to purchase or own firearms.
