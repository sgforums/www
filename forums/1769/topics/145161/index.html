  • Rhonda
    31 Jul 05, 00:27
    Got back not too long ago from the NDP Rehearsal and managed to snap quite a lotta photos out of which only a small handful were good enough. Ah well, I'm still learning!

    Warning : Those of you who wish to be kept in suspense until National Day itself, don't continue viewing!

    Before the actual parade, Gurmit Singh, Sharon Au, and a few other celebrities were busy getting us all hyped up for the event. These three Charlie's Angels got up and started dancing to the music... sooo cute!


    Whoooooooooooaaaa!! Here they come, a-polluting the air!


    Here they are, the marching contingents! With the famous Singapore skyline as the backdrop, I thought this looked astounding!


    Next, the choppers bearing the giant state flag! I'm surprised the rotary blades looked like they were stationary!!


    The Colours... one of the flag-bearers actually fainted later.


    The Famous Five!


    I've never seen our skies soooo clear and sooo blue!

    Can't remember which planes these were... but the compere mentioned something about a huge transport airplane? Forgive me if I got it wrong!

    And next, they gathered round in their finery, and worshipped an image of the ghastly Merlion, offering their song and dance galore!


    As the sky darkened, the parade field came alive with vibrant light and colour!




    And now, the jewels of the night sky!!




    It was a great day! And all of us received a Fun Pack full of goodies!! Yippeee!!
