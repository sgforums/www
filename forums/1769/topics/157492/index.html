    I was listening to the news today and they announced some statistics regarding home ownership in Singapore.

    So what if we made an impact in somebody elses life, had meaningful friendships, flew first class flights to Amsterdam, smoked weed and hired man-whores, rode on the maiden voyage on the Titanic or on Queen Elizabeth II on a cruise of the Carrebean, or won an arguement and had our balls licked by a myriad of people? so what?

    Is it just about the journey and not the destination? Is this the only way to think so at least have some meaning to our existence?
