    Here's the first meal I cooked in my new kitchen!



    In case you can't guess what was in my lunchbox, it was chai bor ng (Preserved Turnips Omelette) with Stir Fried Broccolini and Oyster Mushrooms!

    Broccolini is one hybrid veg (Kana Chai married da Broccoli and gave birth to da Broccolini! ) that came out in the US about two or three years back and I've been DYING to try it and FINALLY, I got to eat it! I love the broccolini! The stems are long and slender and tasty like the kana chai whilst the heads are like mini broccoli! Brilliant! Easy to prepare too! Just cut a bit off the ends, and cook whole if you're lazy to cut them up!
