    I've read 2 books so far, this month:



    After a good movie, conversation with a good friend, and great chocolate, all's right with the world." ~Laura Jensen Walker

    This book celebrates female friendships; the author truly enjoys her special time with her girl friends. A very enjoyable read!



    A funny book about tea-drinkers & the various types of biscuits available in the market!

    Topics include:

    * How to taste, buy and store biscuits
    * Classification with a really big Venn Diagram
    * Dunking, with tips on how to avoid bottom sludge
    * All these important biscuits groups, plain dunkers, digestives, shortcakes, oats, jammy biscuits, marshmallow things, gingernuts, chocolate covered, fruity, icing, wafers and foriegn ones.

    They even dedicate a page to Australia's Tim Tam biscuits, and one of my childhood favourites, 'Iced Gems' - little biscuits with iced toppings.

    On their website http://www.nicecupofteaandasitdown.com/ the authors have created a yummy display called 'The building of the Biscuit City'. Here's a picture that left me feeling hungry for a biscuit!
