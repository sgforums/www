    for upper limbs

    do pushups

    1) roman pushups

    2) scorpion pushups

    3) dive bomber push ups

    do 5 counts of 4 for each push up.

    start with 1 (rest) 1, 2, 1 (rest), 1, 2, 3, 2, 1 (rest) 1,2,1 (rest), 1

     

    for lower limbs, 20 counts of 4 for each exercise

    1) alternate leg thrusts

    2) chicken backside

    3) ranger hops

    same sequence as above

     

    for core u can do bridging, seated V ups.. etc etc

    u can also do 5 exercises...

    1) 10 burpees

    2) 25 crunches

    3) 10 diamond pushups

    4) 5 counts of 4 ranger hops

    5) 10 counts of 4 jumping jack

     

    so u do 3 stages...

    stage 1 = do 3 sets

    stage 2 = do 2 sets but multiply by 2 (20 burpees, 50 crunches...)

    stage 3 = do 1 set but multiply by 3

    between each stage, do bridging for a minute

     

    we still got somemore exercises like super 60 and whatever...
