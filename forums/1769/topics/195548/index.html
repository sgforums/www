    Did I post up my penang pictures already???

    You can check out the early morning market.. these is at the old shophouses area...



    Err buy some cheap lingerie if you can bear the scrutiny hurhurhurhur


    Take the FREE city bus! This will take you to various places, such as Tourist Information, the shophouses area, the penang 'beach'....


    The Penang 'Beach'... has clean toilets

    We also went to this ... fella's house, it's beautifully blue in colour and did a heritage walk there. VEry interesting as this house is supposed to be THE example of fantastic fengshui.

    Of course, you can eat a lot.. a lot!!!! Check out....


    THE CENDOL is damn nice HERE near Penang Road. Its a small stall outside a small kopitiam with small teensy booths.... you can sit inside the kopitiam and order the CENDOL outside but gotta pay a bit for 'sitting' charges, unless you order a drink from the kopitiam owner as well. It's the ONLY kopitiam with a CENDOL booth hanging outside with lots of people braving their lives to savour the CENDOL.


    Abovementioned Kopitiam has pretty good Penang Char Kway Teow depending on the wife's mood. The Penang Laksa here also not bad.


    ABSOLUTELY imperative to visit this rice dumpling stall. It is... DAMN GOOD. Order the... SUPERBIG Rice Dumpling...

    ... that has.. HUGE chunks of meat, Wonderful CHUNKS of salted EGG... and CHESTNUT.... also don't miss their...
    RED BEAN SOUP which is perfectly done, served well with the dumplings.. not too sweet, perfect perfect PERFECT combi.

    DAMN! I LOVE PENANG FOOD!
