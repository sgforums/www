    Interestingly.... the choice of Samuel L Jackson came from the Ultimate Marvel universe.

    In the mainstream universe, the appearance of Nick Fury that was created was based on James Dean.... the 1940s version of 'cool'.

    When they created the Ultimate Universe, they wanted to create a Nick Fury with a 21st century version of 'cool'. As such, they asked Samuel L Jackson for permission to use his likeness.
