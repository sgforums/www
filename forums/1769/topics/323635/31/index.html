So it’s only logical that today, we live in an imaginary democracy, our news coverage is practically fiction, our leaders justify their salaries based on fairy tales, and our policies come from la-la land. As it turns out, there was no such letter. The IBA President had praised Singapore in a speech during a conference, but then, the IBA also went to tekan Singapore in a recent report, questioning our freedom of speech and how independent our judges are.
