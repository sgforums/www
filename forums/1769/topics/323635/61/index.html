    Originally posted by kopiosatu:


    i already put it up for sale in the SBF.

    but i never sold a bike to anyone in the open market before. what is supposed to happen? i pay for the rest of my bike rite? then what?


    No, you dont have to pay for the rest of your bike instalment.  The other person may either take a loan and that loan covers the rest of the instalments as well as cash payments to you.

    Or if he pays you full cash, you go to the loan companies pay off everything and remind them to release the bike for name transfer.

    You only need the confirmation that the bike is reloaned or paid off.  Go with the buyer to LTA with both your NRICs and effect a transfer there.  He is also required to have his insurance ready.

    Then you just need to terminate your insurance for the bike and take back the remaining unused insurance paid,.
