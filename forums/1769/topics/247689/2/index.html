    Originally posted by Fatum:
    what is the restless leg syndrome ? ....
    RLS is a recognized medical condition shared by nearly 1 in ten adults. Most people experience its symptoms in the evenings. Some indicators include:
    * The compelling urge to move
    * Disturbing sensations in the legs
    * Moving offers temporary relief
    * Trouble resting or falling asleep

    Sufferers often describe their leg sensations as creepy, crawly, tingling or tightening. Getting up and moving their legs offer some relief but the symptoms always return. Only a doctor can determine if you have RLS.
