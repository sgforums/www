Around My Neighbourhood 3 : Hot Air Balloons!

  • Rhonda
    12 Feb 08, 16:12

    During certain seasons of the year, if I'm out of the house at about 6:30am or earlier, I'll be able to spot hot air balloons in the distance!  These rides cost more than A$300 but you'll get to see a lot of Melbourne from the air!  Someday, when I own a Digital SLR camera and have learnt how to use it well, I shall go on that hot air balloon ride and snap photos like crazy! 

    Till then, I love to walk and look up at the hot air balloons.  They are quite magical. 

    This past Sunday, it was an extremely exciting morning because as I walked down the quiet streets of Richmond, I suddenly heard a "Whooooooosh!!  Whoooooooosh!!" sound!  I looked around, and then realised that it was the sound of the hot air balloons blowing gas onto the flame to heat up the air within the balloons!!  That particular Sunday, they were all flying so low, it was amazing!!  I walked alongside them, 'chasing' them on the ground.  I thought I was a tad crazy, until I bumped into an Aussie woman walking towards me on the same street, yelling into her mobile for her husband to wake the kids to watch the balloons because they are flying extremely low today!    Like me, she was also chasing  them and snapping shots! 


    So,  here they are, the low-flying hot air balloons!

    This one had turned the flame up a bit and ended up flying a bit higher... before I snapped out of my fascination and decided to snap shots, they were flying even lower!

     

    ET phone home!  ET phone home! 

    And two more hot air balloons popped up! 

     

    I wonder if the folks up there can see me running after their balloon! 

     

    At one stage, they looked like they were going to smack into the side of the high-rise block!!    Heng ah... it was just an optical illusion! 

     

    Buh bye, balloons!!

     

    This morning, before I left for work, I heard the ol' familiar "Whooosh!" sound of the flames being fed gas, so I threw open my door and... there were the balloons again!    It's really kewl!
