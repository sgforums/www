    Originally posted by Marco_Simone:
    Can I go in take a look first/go for a trial session?
    Yes, both California and Planet Fitness do give trial sessions. You can either get it at the gyms themselves, or through their members (through recommendations), or through promotions in magazines, by mobile phone companies, internet companies, etc etc.

    For Planet Fitness (the Suntec branch), I think its OK to go for the trial sessions. At other places though, I cannot guarantee that they won't bug you all the way with high-pressured sales tactics and make you feel awkward and sign up for the packages.

    Even at gyms which don't offer trial sessions, you can ask for a tour (then notice whether they give you the $2 tour or $50 tour to judge their attitude). Remember that as a consumer, you have the right to see what you're buying!

    Personally, I think the equipment at any gym will make no difference to the normal person (its all the same). If you're the kind looking for something special, then its different. What I'd look out for is the atmosphere - at a gym, this would be dependent on its staff and members. I wouldn't be too comfortable at places where people are overly snooty.

    Its best to view the gym at the times where you're likely to visit (eg. 7 pm on weekdays, etc). Then you can see the atmosphere, crowd and conditions during which you will be there.
