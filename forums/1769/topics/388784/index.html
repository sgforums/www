    After Dinner Game  1 ....Its Call "UP JENKINS"

    Basically Player form 2 teams - A n B.  and sit opposite side infront of a Table.

    Team A

    Take 1 coin with all Same Team member's both hands under the table.  Pass the coin to the same team member. On A given Signal Same Team member Bang both their hands on the Table. keep the Palm Down. The Noise will Cover the sound of the Coin.

    Team B

    The player team need to Guess where is the Coin...by pointing at 1 hand at a time and Shout -- UP JENKINS.

    Now if the hands is empty Team B score 1 point. Until the last hand which is the Coin Team B WIN and will hide the Coin for Team A to Guess.

    If Team B reveal the Coin before the last Hand....Team A won the Game and will hide the coin again.

    The Trick for Team A....Every player that hide the coin need to have a Poker FACE....

    Have Fun.....

    Wink Murderer...have deck of card for everyone to draw... identify only one card as the Murderer..remember only 1 person in the group is a Murderer.  The murderer will wink discretely at the person that means the person he killed.  The person that is being WINKED consider Killed and remain slient but continue to play Fuzzy Duck...until someone guess who is the murderer and both Games end.

    When "A" player utter the phrase be sure he turn his head to face the next person..... Therefore when "A" Player ultter to "B" Player Fuzzy Duck and B Player reply "Does he" A Player have to immediately turn the other direction to "C" player and Ultter " Ducky Fuzz" ....
