    I'm a slow eater... because I enjoy my food, every single bite of it. To savour it, you just have to eat it slowly... how else? Gulp it all down?

    Think of it this way... our tastebuds are located on the tongue, and not along the throat. So, when you let the food linger in your mouth a little longer, the tastebuds get to work, and you enjoy the taste and smell of the food thoroughly.

    Everytime I had to eat fast in the tearoom at work, I end up having a really bad tummyache later or worse, on a few occasions, the bolus got stuck in my oesophagus and I started to choke and cough and tears came out when I tried to swallow. Yucks. I just cannot eat fast.

    There are enzymes in the saliva released in your mouth that aids in the digestion too. You've got to give it a bit of time to work well. And of course, thoroughly masticating (chewing) your food breaks it down and aids digestion too.

    All said, it is healthier to not eat too fast! The Nurse has spoken! hehehehe!!
