    Rhonda

    Its actually not rocket science. Here's what you do:

    Set camera to macro
    Disable flash
    Everything else leave it at full auto
    Go out early in the morning before the harsh sunlight and wind shows up. Or early evening when the light is softer.

    Reminder: avoid harsh sunlight and wind.

    Point your camera and shoot!!

    Easy!!

    P.s. this advise only applicable for small point and shoots. DSLRs are a whole different kettle of fish and require more fiddling with.
