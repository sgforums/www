    Where : labrador park

    When : 2 Feb (Sat), time (own time own target ... the hard core fishos (read: me) will be there by 7am .... ), go inside to meet us there, it's a small park ... )

    What : fishing plus picnic (?) plus beach walk at evening low tide

    Target fish: rabbit fish aka peh toh haer, or CNY fish .... they come in to spawn around this time, and they are fat and juicy

    what to bring: your own fishing rod and reel (a dirt cheap combo set would do if you don't have one), and of course, food .....

    and also bicycles and roller blades and frisbees if you are so inclined .... remote controlled toys would be great there also ....

    what to do : eat drink, play and be merry .... we can make it a picnic outing, or can detour for a gluttony session at vivo afterwards .... there's the bay to explore, similar to chek jawa ... and also the "sentosa tunnel" if you remember your "hey Singapore" ...

    how to get there: by public bus Parks 408 from Harbourfront
