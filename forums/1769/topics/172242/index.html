    Here's where we welcome the newcomers to Club 30!

    Newbies, please report here and feel free to use this space to tell us a little more about yourself so that we can welcome you and make you feel at home!

    Club 30 oldies, please let's do our best to make newcomers feel welcome! Remember, we were all newbies too, once upon a time!


    Here's a little Q&A to help you get acquainted :

    [b]What is Club 30?
    Club 30 is all about a bunch of folks who gather together in this online 'lounge'. We come here to crap, discuss issues, share our thoughts and feelings, and sometimes, we erupt into arguments and name-calling, free-for-all mudfight fests!



    How did Club 30 start?
    It all started when an ex-Moderator, kops21, suggested to Rhonda that she start her own forum in sgForums as a birthday gift to herself.

    Can you be a part of Club 30?
    This is a seemingly odd question that gets asked veyr often by newbies.

    The reassuring answer is... SURE!

    The thing is, in order to feel a sense of belonging, in order to experience comaraderie with us, you'd have to open up and be unreserved in your posts, so that you can get to know us, and vice versa!

    If you're just stepping in here to post, ''Hi! I'm a newbie!'' and then we don't ever hear from you again, that's sad cause what might be potential budding friendships would be nipped in the bud!

    Are there any critieria to joinin Club 30?
    Nope! Unless, of course, you choose to be an absolute pain in the unmentionables and a jerk through and through. If not, you're welcome to join us!

    And don't listen to the 'evil ones' in here who tell you that Club 30 is for those with a waist of 30" or larger!

    We come from different walks of life and there's great diversity in here.

    Let's enjoy each other's company in Club 30!
