    Interesting thing happened this morning!

    Woke up, saw something 'black' in my kitchen sink, went over, rubbed my blurry eyes and then, saw that it was a cockroach!!!

    It had slipped into the basin of my shiny, gleamy, grime-free sink and couldn't crawl out! (Don't be fooled, folks... my sink is usually not shiny and gleamy but I just happened to clean it a few days ago! hahaha! )

    I didn't know what in the world to do because I hate cockroaches but am at the same time, scared of them too. Soooo... I went for a shower to get ready for the day and thought that I wouldn't wanna use insecticide in a kitchen sink because of fear of contamination... then suddenly, I had a Eureka! moment!

    I walked over to the sink, still shampooing my hair, and then plopped generous dollops of shampoo suds on top of the cockroach! I didnt' know what the suds would do to him but well, worth a try!

    A few minutes later, I walked over to the sink and...all was still... Out of curiosity, I flushed away the shampoo suds and WHOA... the cockroach was there, VERY DEAD!!!

    Are shampoo suds THAT toxic??!!! We shampoo our hair all the time but ... if shampoo can kill a cockroach, it must be poisonous, man!!!

    Introducing... Shampoo! The preferred, environmentally friendler option to pesticides!!
