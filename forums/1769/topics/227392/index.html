    Honeybunz, if you're trying to explore, why not join a BodyBalance class at first? It's a combination of Tai Chi, Pilates and Yoga done to a mix of really awesome music. It's one of my favourite classes and it can get quite challenging as well.

    Pilates was designed by physiotherapists, I believe. Professional dancers would practice Pilates as well because it tends to develop long, lean muscles, which of course, enhances the dancer's silhouette! It emphasises your 'core muscles' and a session of Pilates can make you work up a sweat!

    Yoga has a meditative quality to it and there are other aspects to Yoga, like deep breathing, balancing, etc, that I found to be really beneficial to one's overall health. Yoga's not just posing... if done correctly, you can actually find yourself sweating too!
