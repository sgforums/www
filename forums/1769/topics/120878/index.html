    You cannot transfer the entire balance from CPF ordinary a/c to CPF special a/c.

    Do not forget that you also need to have a limit before you can withdraw your CPF money at your old age.

    If she's young, and has no intention of using her CPF ordinary a/c, then by all means go ahead.

    The magic of compounding will work in her favour.

    However, given the fickleminded nature of our govt, there's no guarantee you can see your CPF money if they continuously raise the minimum sum and reduce the withdrawal limit.
