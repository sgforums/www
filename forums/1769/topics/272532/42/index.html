    Originally posted by Wanda:
    Is the bid point system skewed again engine fac or does it also affect other fac negatively, say Arts and Social Sciences?

    Hmm...and all the bidding bec the all courses are now modular huh.
    There are two accounts... Programme and General.

    We Engin guys get 750 points and 250 points respectively per semester. Other faculties get different point allocations... if I'm not wrong, Arts and Social Sciences get 400 points for General.

    The Programme account is ostensibly for the core Engineering modules... so we supposedly get more as a result. In practice, though, our core modules are all virtually guaranteed to us. We end up bidding just one point for each core module.

    On the other hand, the General account is needed to bid for cross-faculty modules. And because of our lower General account point allocation, we face a very severe disadvantage when bidding for those modules. And the really stupid thing is that we absolutely need a few cross-faculty modules that are not offered by Engin.

    To illustrate what I mean.... at the moment I have 4350 Programme Account points (which are almost totally useless) and just 595 General Account points (which I desperately need).
