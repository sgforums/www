    my advice to this...

    DON'T

    my mom bought a few thousand dollars worth of pots and whatever.. they are all "state of the art" and all that bullcrap...

    used them a couple of times, and ended up using the wok, the normal pot and the slow cooker again...

    the very expensive pots are now gathering dust in the cabinets...

    trust me.. they sound damned good and all that but ultimately, cooking is like most things, it's the driver, not the equipment...

    the one thing most cooks MUST have is a stove which can deliver a LOT of heat evenly.. therefore good burners are a must.. ever see a short order cook or those good cooks with all those expensive pots? they will use the normal pots, normal woks, normal frying pans... but they'll need good burners, stoves and ovens...
