    Lets say pork ribs. Get thin strips of them, cut into cubes with the bones in the centre.

    Cubed/diced carrots

    Diced Celery

    Cubed/diced patatos

    Chunks of winter melons (if you like them). Tomatos or Western Spinach. Add them in only when you get home and boil the soup on high for a while, angle the cover so its does not totally cover the crockpot & allows it space to boil, bubble & cook your wintermelons while you go for a shower.

    For a Minestrone Soup style. You can cut a throw in one of those big onion. One will do. So by the time you get home, even w/o the wintermelons, tomatos or western spinach, the patatos, celery & carrots plus the meat can be drunk & eaten straight-away.
