    Chocolate Pavlova

    6 egg whites, whisked like a meringue.. and 300gms of sugar, slowly added to the whisked egg whites..

    add vinegar

    add 3 spoonful of cocoa powder (sieved)

    add 1/2 bar of the best dark chocolate you can find (chopped up)

    fold in gently..

    spoon the stuff onto baking paper which is on a tray...

    bung into oven set at 180 degrees.. (to cook the outside into a crisp quickly) then turn the heat down to 160 for 1 1/4 hours..

    when it's taken out, allow to cool and whack a whole lot of whipped double cream and fruit (raspberries or blackberries are wonderful) and shave a whole pile of chocolate onto it...



    Cold Noodles

    buckwheat noodles, cooked and then chilled quickly in icy water...

    throw over a "sauce" which is more just coating and flavouring.. made of.. vinegar, honey, soy sauce and a dash of sesame seed oil...

    add a lot of scorched or freshly roasted sesame seed, and some finely chopped spring onions (near the root)..

    mix and eat

    oh.. another recipe from the other night...

    Poached Peaches in Mint Julep

    2 cups of water, 2 cups of granulated sugar, way too much Kentucky Bourbon... boil together to make syrup...

    get barely ripe peaches (also okay if they are not ripe).. about 3 to 4 of them.. cut them in half and remove the pit...

    when the julep starts to boil, put the peaches cut-side down and allow to poach... allow to poach about 15 minutes to 45 minutes depending on how ripe the peaches are...

    remove peaches and just remove the skin (should be easy enough)

    allow the remaining syrup to reduce until a treacly consistency... (add some more bourbon)

    pour some over peaches and save the rest..

    garnish generously with chopped mint leaves
