    Steph, it's called the Rat Race. And it's hard to avoid being in the Rat Race here, esp when everyone else is obsessed with running and coming out tops somehow.

    But there are the few of us who choose not to run that crazy rat race. We'll never be 'successful' in the commonly-accepted definition of the word, but we're each 'successful' in our own terms.

    Basically, you need to develop a self-confidence in yourself. You need to have a strong sense of self-worth too, if you're going to run a different race. People around you will be passing comments, even those nearest and dearest to you. It won't be easy to stay on the less-trodden path, but it can be done.

    Think about your exams first... one hurdle at a time!

    Study hard, concentrate, do well. Good grades never hurt anyone!

    Good luck!!
