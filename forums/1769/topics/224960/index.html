    Han May's Star Sapphire!
    Its called: Star Sapphire
    Written by Han May.

    Gist of the story is that a young lady in future, wents out to space to work on a Starliner. Marooned on a plant with the man she loves and eventually returning to her time via the same black hole that maroons her.

    Incidently, she was marooned and married her man 9 years earlier. Time travel story. However, written by a local Singapore writer. I find her idea of telling that story by not forcing on religion, by claiming the Christian faith is called "The Mind" and the followers "believers" somewhat of a correlation to Buddhism teachings.

    In the book, there is concepts of deja vu, time travel and Heaven/Gods's/fate at work.

    A interesting and non intimidating read.

    This book is no longer in print. Does anyone knows how to reprint that book?
