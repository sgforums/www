10 most awesome beers

  • the Bear
    12 Oct 10, 17:02

    here are 10 of the most awesome and disturbing beers known to man at the time of this article... they are weird and insane but awesome!

    I'd try them..

    so.. here they are:

    10 - Kelpie Seaweed Ale

    Yes.  You read it right.  It is beer made from seaweed.  It's a dark beer and you can actually taste the sea in it!

     

    9 - Tactical Nuclear Penguin Beer

    From the Brewdog Company, this beer is 32% alcohol.  That's right.  It's bloody 64 proof!  Sold at around £35 a bottle, and at such strength, no one will accuse you of being a sipper.

     

    8 - Exit 1 Bayshore Oyster Stout

    This dark beer is brewed with oysters in it!! Yes!! OYSTERS!!!

    ewwwww!!!!

     

    7- Sink the Bismarck Beer

    Brewdog Company ups the ante with 41% alcohol in this beer... that's stronger than most grain spirits!!

     

    6 - La Dragonne Beer

    Notice it's in a double boiler getting heated up?  This beer is not carbonated, it is a concoction of fruit and honey.  Supposedly served at 75ºC so that the spices in the beer "fizzle and pop" as it rolls over your tongue!

     

    5 - Schorschbock 40

    As the name implies, it's 40% or 80 proof alcohol.  but it's a beer and meant to be drunk in shots like a grain spirit.. if you chug the stuff like normal beer, you're going to be in need of a stomach pump at the local hospital.

     

    4 - Miyamori Wasabi Dry

    Yes, it's green.  Yes it's made from wasabi.  Yes it will burn your sinuses if you chug the whole pint down like normal lager.

     

    3 - Start The Future Beer

    As if the maniacs from Brewdog are not mad enough, this made by the Dutch stuff is way stronger than the usual grain spirits at an unreasonable 60%.. that's 120 proof!!  I have a feeling the alcohol-content wars is not over yet!

     

    2 - Kwispelbier

    You are not hallucinating from the awesomeness of the other beers. Why should you have all the fun?  Why not your best friend too?  This is steak-flavoured beer specially brewed for your dog!

    I'm not drinking this stuff

     

    1 - End of History Beer

    Awesome does not begin to describe this beer!

    It is not the strongest although it is 55% alcohol or 110 proof.  It does not contain weird stuff like wasabi or oysters or animal parts, but Brewdog has come up with a winner for encasing the bottle of beer in a dead animal!

    Although they claim that the animas used died of natural causes, this beer is so awesome that it deserves the No.1 spot!!

     

    (source: Spike.com)
