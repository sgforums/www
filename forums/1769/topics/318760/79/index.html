    So in the end, it's all a matter of how consistently you drive in a speed range. If you consistently drive at a low speed, you may want to put in some low-speed spoilers, but once you reach a high speed, drag (and fuel consumption) goes up.

    Which is why on aircraft, spoilers are only deployed upon landing to reduce lift and increase drag. It's not economical to have retractable spoilers on cars, though.
