    Introducing yet another bo-liao thread! The ListManiac!!

    A few of us love to list things out so why not combine that list-loving habit with a getting-to-know-you content and satisfy our list-making tendency plus know more about each other? Kill two birds with one stone... although I'd rather it be 'roast two chickens in one oven'!

    OK, the ways I love to waste time include :
    - listening and dancing to music, when I should be doing something else
    - surfing the internet
    - playing computer games
    - putting on makeup very very slowly
    - digging my piles of stuff to find that one little thing I suddenly realised I've misplaced and MUST have
    - sitting back in my chair, staring blankly at nothing in particular, and daydreaming
    - taking the bus instead of the MRT even though the journey is longer, because I dislike MRT journeys
    - preferring to do things using the 'longer' method, just because I prefer it
    - deliberately walking slowly so I can enjoy the walk and the sights around me

    What about you? What ways do you love to waste time?
