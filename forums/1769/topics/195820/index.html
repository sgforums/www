    I hate to 'do' my hair. I know some girls enjoy going to the saloon but I hate sitting in that seat for hours. For a long time I suffered the $10 10 minute haircut and more often than not, come out looking a bit lopsided. Once in a long while, I will go do the highlights or rebond or watever.. but come out thinking I won't do this for at least a year after spending hours in the saloon.

    So today I finally went to 'do' my hair.. thinking that I wish to highlight it. I got wavy natural curl hair, not VERY curl, just wavy.... and i tell u.. its.. such a pain...

    Me: I want haircut and highlight
    Hairdresser: U dont want to rebond ha?
    Me: No I dont want to rebond.
    H: Your hair natural curl should rebond. Easier to tidy
    Me: No. I dont want to rebond.
    H: Ur hair sticks out everywhere 'qiao lai qiao qu' Rebond easier for you.
    Me: I rebonded twice liao. I dont wish to rebond.
    H: Ya.. just telling you, rebond is better. So how you want to cut?
    Me: uhh just cut.. err normal. Layer loh.. err.. 'siew bao' thin it a bit.
    H: Ur hair dont rebond the more thin I make it.. worse it will qiao.
    Me: MM.. then don't siew bao so much
    H: Ya.. cos u never rebond that is why have tendency to qiao.
    Me: MmmM.
    H: Dont cut too many layers also... cos will make it 'qiao'
    Me: Okay.. you 'kan zhe pan'
    -trim trim. trim trim.-
    H: so what colour you want?
    me: -shows magazine- this one
    H: this one must bleach u know
    me: okay.
    H: This type of colour very light leh. Bleach one.
    me: ya.. okay. go ahead.
    H: This girl looks nice cos she got base colour. You dnt have. This colour won't look nice on you.
    Me: ok.. fine.. why dont u recommend me another colour??
    H: This one nice.
    Me: i dont want red base.
    H: This one loh. Or you want this one.. need to bleach u know?
    Me: ok.. this one is fine.
    h: or you want to bleach??
    Me: ... no.. this one is fine.
    H: cos must ask ma.. whether you want to bleach, in case u dont want.
    Me: ok.. that colour is fine.
    H: you want your highlights to be finer or streaks?
    me: Streaks is good.
    H: streaks ah... I make streaks inside and fine highlights outside more nice okay?
    me: okay.
    H: ur fringe want highlight or not
    me: (... NB.. ) yeah.

    After agonising long. I realise she highlight one big fat streak on my fringe. Rest of my hair a little bit of streaks but mostly fine highlights.

    Pain. ful.
