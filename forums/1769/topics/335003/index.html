    For months now, I've sensed this curiosity slowly building up in me... I've been reading a bit here and there about a few featured Australian wines and I think I'd really love to try them, plus they might help me sleep a little better at night, so on the nights preceding my days off, I think I might try the featured wines and start to learn more. 

    I'll definitely go for the 'fruity' or 'flowery' wines first.  I still can't forget my first bottle of Brown Brothers Orange Muscat and Flora, for which I had to purchase my very first corkscrew. 

    Next, I wanna try the Juliet range of wines.  And then there's the Brown Brothers' 2007 Vermentino and Dry Muscat...

    Vermentino :

    This wine has refreshing lifted aromas of peach, passionfruit and tropical fruits and a slight hint of acid provides a crisp, yet delicate finish.

     

    2007 Dry Muscat :

    Smells like freshly crushed grapes! Fruity, musky flavour – fresh and lively. Serve very chilled.

     

    And mmmm... my favourite and discovery of the year - 2008 Orange Muscat and Flora! 

    Orange Muscat and Flora is a blend unique to Brown Brothers.  Orange Muscat forms the backbone of the blend providing aromas of orange blossom to the nose and a fresh citrus hint to the palate, while the Flora contributes colour, texture and mouth-feel to the palate.  The current release displays a vibrant yellow colour and a lifted nose showing aromas of citrus blossom, spice and musk.  The palate is fresh and tangy with a zesty long clean balanced finish.

    I love the bottle shops here!  There are about 6-7 in my neighbourhood and one of them is huuuuuuuuge, almost like Jelita's Cold Storage in size, but all they sell is booze, booze, and more booze!! 

    OK, maybe I shall go start my very own thread chronicling my Wine Discovery Odyssey!    (How the hell do you spell the last word?!)
