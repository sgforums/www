    Let's step indoors for a short while so I can show you a few of the bouquets that have adorned my home and made it cozier.

    Kissing under the Daffodils and Alisia's!


    OK, there's an interesting story behind this next bouquet!


    On Saturday, in Richmond Market, I saw a bunch of vegetables with lots of bunches of tiny yellow flowers on sale. I asked the stallholder what that vegetable was and he said, "Broccoli Flowers". I'd never heard of it so I asked him how it could be cooked and he simply said, "Stir Fried". I don't think he himself knew much about the vege. I bought it anyways, thinking I'll give it a try, but I had too much food in my fridge to cook and the broccoli flowers weren't in tip-top condition for cooking after a few days. Instead of throwing it all away, in a sudden moment of inspiration, I decided to incorporate them in my vase of daffodils in the living room!

    So, VOILA! A pretty result of the marriage between flowers and vegetables!
