    I forgot to report here that on Friday, I completed reading Tony Parson's "My Favourite Wife".    It is a really enjoyable novel set in Shanghai.  It touches on an affair that developed between a British Expat and a China girl when his wife decided to bring their daughter with her back to London, leaving him all alone in Shanghai.  Parson dealt with the issue of love, marriage, and family really well.  I thoroughly enjoyed reading it.
