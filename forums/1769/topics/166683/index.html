    Your Beauty lies in Contradiction. Controversial, unpredictable, and never what anyone expects.

    Your appearance and your personality are two opposite things. Even your
    appearance sends different signals to different people. To some you may look
    innocent and sweet, to others you look mysterious and intimidating at the same time. No one ever knows what to expect with you. You are a little bit of

    guys one minute and the next out shopping at the mall. You seem to be lmost a different person every time you meet someone, but at the same time you know exactly who you are and there is always that one thing that makes you you.

    You enjoy keeping people guessing and people love how completely unpredictable you are.
