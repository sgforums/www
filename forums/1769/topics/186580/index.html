    Was a beautiful day today so we decided to make an impromtu visit to the San Diego Zoo. Hubby said that military get in free on Sundays but we still paid alot just for the kids and I. Entrance totalled $71. Even with a $12 off coupon!!! I usually bring sandwiches and such but this time I didn't since it was unplanned. Big mistake. We were at the park for 6 hours and had to feed the kids twice. First makan bill came to $38 and the second came to $32. Some of the enclosures were empty so I asked hubby, I want a refund for those. Here are some pics from our visit for your viewing pleasure.

    Many other people thought today was a perfect day to be at the Zoo too.


    $3 for a 35 minute bus ride around the park, with commentary, or $10 for an all day pass.


    Cable car, also $3 per person, from one end of the park to another. Would have been more enjoyable if I didn't have a fear of heights.


    Given their showbiz culture, I would have thought their animal shows would be great. Give me our Singapore Zoo presentations anytime. They always get a laugh out of the kids and I.


    Did you know that the skin of the rhino, though it looks tough, is actually very sensitive?


    These giant tortoises have been at the zoo since 1921!


    Camel shedding winter coat. Gorgeous animal.
