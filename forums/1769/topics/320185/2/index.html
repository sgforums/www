    for a start,

    most platelets/plasma donors have to be recommended by the whole blood nurse..

    most of the time, they look at the veins size. Mostly platelets donors are male, but also got female but quite rare.

    platelets donation takes 1 and a half hour to 2 hours. Plasma takes around 45mins.

    the process of platelets donation is the same as normal whole blood donation, one tube connect to a machine, there will be a series of draw and return cycles. So when it draws, you do the palm exercise, then when the machines separated the platelets from ur blood, it will pump ur blood back to you in the return cycle.. at the same time filling ur blood volume with a anti clogging liquid.. me dunno the scientific name..

    average around 8 cycles. might feel cold when return cycle.. because of the liquid..

    yeah there is 3 movie channels available. but please don't donate for the wrong reasons.
