    Well, I lost 3.1 kg in the past two months....

    I simply cut down on fried foods, limited junk food to once a week, ate a lot more fresh fruits and vegetables, and went for healthier alternatives where possible. Even small things like removing the skin off the chicken before you eat it helps.

    And I went running several times a week.... I try to clock 35km per week. Running is good for burning fats, but if you haven't done anything like that before, it's best to consult your doctor first.

    As a general rule of thumb, in whatever diet/exercise programme you follow, you should lose no more than 1kg per week.

    And seriously, say no to Atkins/Xando/Tummitrim and all those fad diets and pills.
