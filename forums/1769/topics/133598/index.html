    On behalf of the patients whose lives depend on that urgent / emergency blood transfusion, thanks for donating blood. Everytime I run a blood transfusion, I always say a prayer for the kind souls who donated. Hey, kops, Bear... maybe I held pints of blood donated by you guys before! hehehe!

    I remember situations when the blood supply in the Blood Bank runs low. I used to speak to the relatives of the patients who needed blood transfusions to let them know that it would be greatly appreciated if they would help to replenish the blood supply by donating blood. But you know what... we've been told to discontinue doing this because there were relatives who complained about this.

    I feel that if someone's blood donation has saved the life of your loved one, it's a gesture of goodwill to replenish the supply by donating blood yourself! Let's be honest, the supply does get depleted and so what's wrong with a gentle reminder to do your part? It is a form of social responsibility but nope... complain about anything and it totally absolves you of any responsibility whatsoever. Go figure.

    To the young, healthy and able-bodied, the blood you donate will save lives.
