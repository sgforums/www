    Added by Elindra

    Some badly taken pics. We must have pics!!!!!!

    I know that we have different food at the 2 tables but at our table we had

    Coffee Ribs (Yum Yum) I think they used just enough coffee and it's very tender and yummy. One of the best Coffee Ribs I've eaten.

     

    Pipa Tofu - Fried Tofu stuffed with seafood and veg with a special sauce. I think it's good too. We forgot to take pics till there were only 2 left :P

     

    Celery with Macadamia Nuts - Out table decided to have some greens. It's really lovely.

     

    HK Style Steamed Bating! Very yummy with no muddy taste and it came with lots of Omega 3!!!! Look at the thick layer of fish fat!!!

     

    Herbal Drunken Prawns - Ok I was very disappointed with this dish. The one we had at Signboard Seafood was WAYYYYYY better. The soup was not fragrant enough but the prawns were extremely fresh and sweet. We should have ordered the cereal prawns instead :x

     

    The crabs! We ordered 1 Chilli and 2 Salted Egg crabs for our table. The Chilli Crab is not bad but seriously The Salted Egg Crab at Kian Seng is the way to go especially since they give crabs filled with roe!!!

    We ordered the Yam Paste with Pumpkin too. TBH for us who like the traditional Yam Paste it was too light for us and didn't have the oomph! But in all fairness I think it's slightly better than the Yam Paste with Gingko Nuts we had the last time
