    here, to decrease confusion:

    woodlands INTERCHANGE = take SMRT 950.. which is 15 minute interval.. after immigration clearance, you can only take another 950 which means if you miss that bus you came in on, you gotta wait another 15 minutes..

    Kranji.. you take 170 or 160 or the express blue-plated 170.. after immigration clearance, you can take EITHER of the 3 buses.. which means you get on another bus quickly because you can take 3 different buses (of which blue-plated 170 is most frequent at around 5 minutes or less frequency)

    so, best and fastest is to go to Kranji MRT station and take the blue plated 170 to Woodlands CHECKPOINT and then take 160 or either of the 2 types of 170

    Woodlands INTERCHANGE and Woodlands CHECKPOINT are totally different places.. Woodlands INTERCHANGE = Causeway Point
