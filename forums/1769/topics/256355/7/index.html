    Don setting up new household right.

    Heres a tip for the bedsheets.

    You will need a total of 4 sets.

    one to use, one for when you are changging for a wash. 3rd set for when there is a accident on your bed and 4th set as standby.

    Start with 3 sets. Retired a set per year.

    Avoid white colours cos they turn yellow or comes with yellow sports with age.

    When you get your mattress, always get the mattress protector on first. Doggie pee, sometimes even periods can seep thru the mattress protector and stain your bed when you dont realise it.

    Try to get a really hard bed. Cos when females are pregnant, the weight of the child will make you sink into a too soft a bed and your back will hurt real bad.

    Try go for 8 inch maximum bed, cos most fitted bedsheets are made with lessor then that height mattress in mind.

    European, australian & USA brands of bedsheets dont come with Bolsters covers cos its not their norm for a bolster.
