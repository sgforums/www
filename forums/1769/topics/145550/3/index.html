    Originally posted by stipe:
    hi mushroom, this is my very first post, i am turning 30 this september (wake me up when september ends....)

    any tips for making a person's 30s the most wonderful time of his/her life?
    Don't know about advice but this is what I learn in my 30s:

    Try to get established somewhat in your career. When you turn 40, it is difficult to find a new job. Employers are not interested to take on a 40 year old and train up from scratch. By all means hop around and find a job you like in your 20s. But decide on one and be good at it in your 30s. Get a niche job where your skills will still be needed for some time.

    My greatest regret: not settling down with a family in my 30s. It is harder when you turn 40: we get more rigid in our ways and enjoy our freedom a bit too much.

    Be careful that in your search for to establish your career you sacrifice the second objective of establishing a family.
