    A little info for the earthquake with regards to juicing:

    1. juicing gets rid of just about all the fiber from the fruit.

    2. in the absense of fiber, all that juice/frutose gets absorbed into your system almost immediately. (fiber would have slowed things down)

    3. this causes a larger insulin spike vs eating the whole fruit

    4. insulin will drive all that glucose/frutose out of your bloodstream(in order to maintain a healthy blood sugar level) into your muscle cells and fat cells.

    5. in other words, what doesn't get stored into your muscle cells will become bodyfat.

    of course, if you've just run several km or something, then it all goes into your muscles to replace the spent energy and won't turn into fat. but in the case of the earthquake, I doubt it.
