    in year 1997 or 1998 i think..

    my ex-company was situated at the ground floor at Jalan Pemimpin, occupiedk the entire warehouse (with super high ceiling) and make the whole place so cozy (music record company).. can smoke openly somemore...

    one fine afternoon, while happily chatting with my ad production guy at my cubicle, pple calling out "FIRE FIRE"....

    we supposed to have a fire drill a week beforehand but din happened...so we bor-chap and continue to work (as if nothing is happening)..

    my IT lady was frantically backing up server...and my handsome big boss was calling out to us to leave our workplace...

    we went "alamak, why so kan-cheong, small matter"..

    suddenly we heard our 2nd boss yelling..."haa!!!!!!" ...
    Apparently, a black charred body just fell from 2nd floor right and landed just outside his fullview (ceiling to floor) glass panel window, outside the bldg.

    I looked out of my cubicle...oh gosh, the entire 2nd floor in full blast fire, and we hardly notice anything.. we ran out...then we realised how serious is the fire...

    outside, the road was jammed, the 4 fire engines cannot get in, our building barrier (for the fire engine) jammed and cannot lift up, must break it..
    the fire hydrant not properly fixed, water leaking everywhere..

    the police cars blocked the way, ...ambulances came... reporters came...

    everything in a mess...

    then people still slowly strolled out ... many complained that they dun even know the fire was so out of controlled...

    ....ironic huh...

    1. me brought my personal bag and hp (and still chatting on ph with MTV),

    3. my colleagues brought files out...and hp.
