    For breakfast, try the very simple yet tasty Deluxe Tuna with Seeded Mustard Sandwich!


    You need to throw together :

    1 can of Tuna in Seeded Mustard, or if you don't have that, just drain a can of tuna in brine and mix seeded mustard with the Tuna

    A few button mushrooms

    A few sliced olives

    Mayo (optional)

    Lettuce and/or Tomatoes and/or Cucumber

    Capers

    And voila! If you like it spicy, a dollop of Peri Peri sauce / Chilli Sauce will spice things up nicely!
