    If the weather gets too hot and mucky, try this naturally sweet cold dessert - Stewed Nashi Pears with Plums!


    Core nashi pears.

    Slice Nashi Pears thinly.

    Throw in a handful of Qing2 Ren2 Mei2 (a type of preserved plums).

    Bring to the boil, then maintain a slow simmer.

    Refrigerate, and enjoy!

    The natural sweetness of the Nashi Pears mean that you don't need to add sugar at all.

    KT's Almost Vegetarian Noodles


    Done slightly over an hour, just use up whatever you have in the fridge or whatever's fresh from your marketing!

    Seasoning : Tomato Ketchup, Kecap Manis, Glory Nyonya Sambal, a little Tau Cheo, sugar to taste.

    Voila!

    OR, to make a really plain and healthier version, just use Tamari Sauce (a denser type of Japanese soy sauce, slightly different method of brewing from the plain soya sauce) and Fish Sauce. The natural sweetness of the fresh vegetables provide a tasty backdrop to a very simple and delicious dish. Bon appetit!
