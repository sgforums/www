    Stuffed Pickled Green Chillies

    Part Un - Stuffing
    dried shrimp
    bit of red chilli
    tumeric (fresh)
    unripe papaya

    Method
    grate the unripe papaya

    pound the crap out of the dried shrimp, red chilli and tumeric into a coarse pasty mess.. heat up a wok with a bit of oil and stirfry that bit for a while to bring out the fragrant oils locked in the stuff..

    after the stuff becomes fragrant (doesn't take long) throw in the papaya and stir around for a very short while just to mix up the stuff..

    remove and allow to cool

    Part Deux - Green Chillies
    get some BIG FAT (must be big and fat) green chillies (not bell peppers), slice them lengthwise and remove the insides... try not to break the chillies as it's the crunch which makes it yummy

    Part Trois - Putting The Thing Together
    get the cooled stuffing and shove into the prepared green chillies... be generous...

    Part Quatre - Pickling
    get a jar which will fit the chillies in.. put the stuffed chillies into the jar

    dissolve a bit of sugar into vinegar to take the edge off the vinegar... a little will do..

    pour the vinegar into the chillies and bung into the fridge

    ready for eating in about 1 to 2 days

    another uber easy & yummy dish. (things that u learn when u r studying alone in a foreign country)

    Ginger & Spring Onion Chicken
    1 chicken chopped up (or deboned chicken)
    Ginger
    Spring Onion
    Soya Sauce
    Oyster Sauce
    Sesame Oil
    Cooking Oil

    Season chicken breast with 1/4tbsp soya sause and 1/2tbsp oyster sause. Season the other chicken parts with 1/4 tbsp soya sause and 3/4 tbsp oyster sause. let them sit for at least 20mins.

    Chop ginger into strips. about 1 big handful should be enough. Same for spring onions, about 1-2 big handful.

    Heat wok. Add 1tbsp cooking oil 3/4tbsp sesame oil. Add ginger and fry till it smells v nice. Add chicken. Mix well and cover. When it is cooked, add spring onions. Mix and serve!
