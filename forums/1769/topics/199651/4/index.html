    more creatures.. these will destroy your fish tank if keep them in a normal glass tank...



    Pistol Shrimp: the thicker claw is its "gun".. the claw creates a bubble which implodes and causes a hugeass shockwave capable of killing its prey up to 6 feet away.. the imploding bubble has been measured to momentarily reach 5,000 degrees and the energy is so intense it gives off light.. the noise from the implosion (causing the shockwave) has been measured at over 200 decibels...



    Mantis Shrimp: there are 2 types of this type of shrimp, one which spears its prey and other which bludgeons it to death... the one which bashes its prey to death uses its two front legs which look like heavy clubs.. they have been known to hit their prey with the impact of a .22 calibre bullet.. the acceleration of the "clubs" have been measured at around 10,500G and reach speeds up to 23m/s..

    both have been known to shatter aquarium glass..
