    songs.. of loss...

    => Big Yellow Taxi - Counting Crows featuring Vanessa Carlton

    well, i know originally done by Joni Mitchell.. but this one really has that wistful hopeful feel which is somehow tinged with regret yet moving on.. looking back with a slightly sad smile of what could have been...

    i love this song

    => Kayleigh - Marillion

    such a sad sweet and tender love song.. i guess quite a number of us have our own Kayleigh in our lives...

    => Say Hello To Heaven - Chris Connell

    he wrote it for Andrew Wood, a dear friend of his who passed on... originally in an album dedicated to Andrew Wood entitled Temple of the Dog... essential listening... even after more than a decade, this song still touches those who listen and think about lyrics of a song written from the heart...

    => Khe Sahn - Cold Chisel

    an Australian Vietnam veteran returns.. and finds himself.. totally lost.. a loss of innocence, a loss of identity, a loss of self, a loss of purpose.. looking, searching.. not knowing what he is looking for..

    => Motorcycle Drive By - Third Eye Blind

    the loss and abandonment of hope.. that your love will be requited... yet.. one feels so alone, yet so alive.... the letting go gives one a strange freedom..
