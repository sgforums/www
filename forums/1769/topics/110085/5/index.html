    Why is it called pot luck?? this is what I was taught by family....
    the general definition....A pot luck is a gathering where every family or person brings a different dish.
    In Singapore it usually means whatever you want to bring...the luck part I guess is for the guests, they're trying their luck on the quality of the food....

    where I grew up and how we practise it in the family (and I'm told is the authentic way of practising potluck).....
    its that the type of dish a person contributes is usually designated based on the first initial of a last name. (so the dish I would have to make would have to be a dish, name starting with B) Thats where the luck part comes in...cos you dont know what dish you're gonna be saddled with whipping up based on your alphabet...we do full names, chinese names, nicknames...whatever we can to keep it changing and fun...and the guests dont ever know what to expect!!
