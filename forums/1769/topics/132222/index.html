    kops, I know what you mean. I hate it when organisations seem to exploit charity for profit, or for publicity or what-not.

    There are WAAAAAAAAAAAAY too many charity shows on TV now. It's really an overkill. Even if they don't profit from the calls made, they profit from the publicity and the no. of viewers so they can sell ad space at a premium.

    And yes, I do get fed-up when there are flag days EVERY Saturday! It makes a stroll down Orchard Road infuriating!
