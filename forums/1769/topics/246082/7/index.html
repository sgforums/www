    strangely, here's something...

    could be that you are shooting in too high res..

    seriously..

    in order to cram a 10MP picture into a 4R, it will require some "squishing"

    and remember.. these are pixels you are talking about... they are not as fine as photons... or grain from film which you can squish almost infinitely...

    the smooth line becomes a stepladder because that's the mathematical formula which will approximate to that smooth line when squished..

    try dropping a 3000 pixel wide pic by a factor of three.. you will get 1000pixels

    try dropping it by a factor of 1.7 and you will get an irrational number...
