    Here's how to elevate your pack of Campbell's Cuppa Soup into 'Gourmet' status!

    Prepare one cuppa Campbell's Cheesy Mushroom Soup with Croutons as instructed on the pack. Stir.

    Pour in half a pack of Campbell's Cream of Mushroom Soup. Stir.

    Add in sliced button mushrooms if you have them. Then, sprinkle in Betty Crocker's Bacos (non-Bacon Bacon bits! ).

    Stir, and then, slurp it all up!!
