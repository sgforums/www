    Here are a few random photos taken from my walks around my neighbourhood that turned out pretty alright.    They were taken in late Spring 2007 - early Summer 2008.  Sometimes, it helps not to take our neighbourhood for granted and see it through different eyes and enjoy their simple beauty!

    First up... "Pine-ing for You"!    Corny, sia?! 

     

    Graffiti Art is very common around Melbourne, esp near Train Stations and in the suburbs.  They might be considered a public nuisance, but I think they are quite talented and creative. 

     

    Flowers and a white gate... what says ''Country" more than this?! 

     

    Sea of Pink...

     

    Followed by a Sea of Blue...

     

    Gemstone?    I drew quite a few stares as I adopted a squatting position to zoom in on this pretty stone.    Heng ah, no handsome men were around! 

     

    This photo was just BEGGING to be taken!!  So chaotic, so picturesque, and perfectly 'natural'!

     


    Walking Home...  I love walking down my street.  So many pretty houses and gardens around! 

     

    The Enchanted Forest... and it's right by my gate!    I just decided to take a different perspective to things that I see daily and voila... this photo has an almost magical quality to it, I feel! 

    These trees actually look like giant stalks of broccoli!    Pretty kewl!

     

     

    And ladies, if you do not use moisturisers religiously, this is what will happen to your skin!
