    IPT= IPPT Physical Training.

    So-called 'pre-RT' for those NSmen who seriously think that they cannot pass IPPT. Must clear eight sessions within five weeks, and the eigth session is the actual IPPT test.

    Those who clear those eight sessions will be protected from RT even if they fail their IPPT.

    The catch is that it's voluntary.... ergo, it's unpaid (except for the 8th session), and it does not count towards the 40 days of NSmen liability.

    The way I see it..... better go for 7x unpaid tekan session than risk 20x paid tekan session. Time lost going for RT is much worse than money gained from it. 

    Besides..... the PTIs are pretty understanding people, if you get my drift.
