    Hmm... goodie, this thread seems alive again.

    Yet another obstacle to love I suddenly thought of is... deception.

    There are folks who say one thing, but mean another. The things they claim make you think that they are such and such a person and that might lead you in the wrong direction for a while but then you slowly come to realise that who they really are is different from what they claim themselves to be and it is the who they claim to be that you have actually fallen in love with.

    Self-deception or a lack of real understanding of self, which misleads others. Kinda complicated, but one possible obstacle.

    Falling in love with the persona... but not the person. Falling in love with the what-could-be's... but not the what-really-is. It happens.
