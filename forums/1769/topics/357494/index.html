    We are hoping to organise 2 activities this June holidays and we need volunteers to help carry them out! Do let us know if you are interested to volunteer!

    1) Board Games Session - Let's play some cool board games.
    (Likely to be held 6 June AM )

    We Need:

    Poster designer (1) : To design poster to help publicise the event
    MC (1-2): Someone to coordinate and create the mood on that day.
    Game Masters (10 - 12): People who learn the game well and teach it to the kids
    as well as Volunteers to assist in the games and help the kids to play
    Entertainers (1 - 3): Can you make cool balloon animals? Can you juggle? can you do simple magic tricks?
    Logistics and Planning (1-2): To assist in overall logistics, planning, publicity.


    2) Baking Session - Bake Daddy some Muffins(?) for Father's Day?
    (Likely to be held 20 June 2009 AM)
    A baking session for youths with special needs to teach and help them to bake something for their Daddies for Father's Day.

    We need:
    Poster designer (1) : Make a poster for this event to help us publicise.Baking Teacher (1) : Don't have to be expert, just be willing to share!
    Assistant bakers (10+) : to assist the kids in their tasks
    Logistics (1 - 2) : To assist in overall logistics and planning.
