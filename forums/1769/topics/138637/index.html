    I guess it's time to change job when it fails to satisfy both your monetary and emotional expectations.

    If the job sucks, you need at least a good salary to keep you going.
    If the money sucks, the job must be challenging and interesting enough to motivate you.

    If both suck, that's when you know it's time to head for greener pastures.

    There'll always be an inertia not to move on, stuck in your comfort zone but once you manage to move on, you may find that the move pays off, more often than not...

    Sidenote: I guess I'm lucky. I totally enjoy what I'm doing in my work. It's both my interest (fanatical!) and job. Monday mornings are no different than Friday mornings and I willingly bring work home. Money could be better though... then again, when is money ever enough???

    Hope you manage to find your true calling!
