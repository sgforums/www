    Hi, have returned Yeah it was Free & Easy. Erm wrote abit of the trip elsewhere, but will repost here heheh. As you can see, we stayed at P Princess at about S$130 a night. Not cheap but very accessible to the shops. Amari is probably more expensive than that.

    BKK was fun! Spent more than I should have .... ate more than I should have ah but I'll postpone looking at the bank balance till month-end. I think my biggest shopping splurge was silk table runners and shawls hahaha. I'm a shawl freak.

    It was hot, but I much prefered the sun to rain

    Holidays are great, but the pile-up at work is terrible!! >__<
    [/quote]
    Well we did most damage at MBK, because it was just downstairs, and it was air-conditioned Can't help it , my SO's an grouch when it comes to heat.

    Personally, my favourite was Chatuchak, for the variety and whole bazaar feel of it. Suam Lom's good too, it's more quiet. But at Chatuchak and Suan Lom, we were overwhelmed by the crowd, the sprawl and the heat, and thus were not in prime shopping condition We didn't stay long, see above reasons re: SO & heat. Can't find a worse partner to go to bazaars with. For both places, I'm sure the shopping will be better in future trips (hypothetical! :p ) when one knows what to expect.

    Ah the food... I tried, I really tried to eat as much variety as I could in such a short time But there's only so much food one can stomach in a day I love the local pork noodles with spicy broth, I wish we had it here. Other notables were coconut ice cream, coconut crispy pancake thingy, desserts.... generous portions at low price. Good stuff.

    Next trip? Till I see the bank balance and do my sums, I don't know, but I'm not hopeful .
