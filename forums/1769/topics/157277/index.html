    We've talked abt this with choco B who's going there soon....Siam square, Mah Boon Krong, weekend Chatuchak mkt, and some night mkt somewhere.

    If you have more time, can go west to Kanchanaburi and spend a couple of days (Bridge on River Kwai, JEATH railway/museum which are WWII relics), or visit the King's summer palace or something (forgot the name) which is a really nice park like 1-2 hrs from BKK (it's not the other palace that looks like temple).

    Best of all, remember to eat the authentic Thai food and snacks. Have fun!
