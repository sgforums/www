    During your admission to any hospital, the hosptal will normally require you or your next-of-kin to sign a document, normally known as "Conditions of Services/Hospital Policies".

    One of the clause will normal state something like that

    CONSENT TO DISCLOSURE OF MEDICAL RECORD AND INFORMATION

    The undersigned irrevocably and unconditionaly authorises:
    (a)the hospital, its medical staff, medical practitioners practising with or at the hospital to have access to and use information and details contained in the patient's medical and other records kept with or by the hospital, its medical staff or medical practitioners practicing with or at the hospital.;
    (b)the disclosure of the patient's said medical and other records (i) whenever and to whoever the law requires; (ii) to such persons as may require the same to faciliate the performance of any required Medisave audit activity as well as such other audit activity sanctioned by the hospital's Medical Advisory/Board and/or Administration; and (iii) pursuant to any request by the Ministry of Health or other relevant government authority; and (c) the transfer and/or release of all or any of the patient's medical records between the hospital and/or establishments.
