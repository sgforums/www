    OK, it's VERDICT TIME!!!

    Four of us ladies trooped down to Carl's Jr to recce the place. It turned out to be pretty hard to find from within Marina Sq because the place is undergoing renovations at some parts and Elindra and I went round and round trying to find the way to the 1st floor where it was.

    It was worse cause we were fuelled by curiosity when we passed by this :


    Finally, we managed to find it, us two goondu-samy's. We waited for the next two goondu-samy's to arrive - tare aka my sayang-panda and Camellias4me. I liked what they displayed at every table :




    I had to take home a few of the cards for laughs!

    And now, for the stars of the night...

    Here's the Guacamole Burger Combo Meal!


    Their combo meals came with fries and refillable drinks that you fill up yourself at the drinks counter. Help yourself to the Coke / Sprite / Zappel / Ice Lemon Tea there! You can change your sides to either onion rings or Chilli Cheese Fries by paying for the difference.

    Onion Rings... whoa!!


    Chilli Cheese Fries


    BBQ Chicken Burger


    Famous Star Burger, the smaller cousin of the two-meat-patties Super Star Burger!


    The cost?
    The Guacamole Combo Meal cost around $10.
    The BBQ Chicken Burger Combo Meal with a change of sides to Chilli Cheese Fries was $9.20.
    The Famous Star Burger with a switch to Onion Rings was slightly over $7.

    I thought the burgers would be as large as those featured in the ad Paris Hilton did for Carl's Jr but I guess they downsized the burgers for the Asian appetite!

    And oh, it was hard to find a seat in that place.
