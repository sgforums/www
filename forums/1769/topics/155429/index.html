    Not just name them, but let us know what's nice!

    I love the buns from Four Leaves Bakery. I was very pleased when they had one outlet at the basement of Tangs. Now, it's been taken over by the Gelato counter.

    I don't really like Bread Talk much except for these few items - Billy Bundle (pandan-leaf-wrapped bun with sambal ikan bilis inside!), the Apple-Cranberry Bread and the Garlic Bread. They used to make really good Garlic Bread, with generous servings of chopped garlic, but recently, their Garlic Bread looks really pathetic, as they seem to be stinging on the garlic and the butter now.

    A lot of neighbourhood bakeries sell great buns too!

    A bakery near NTUC in Bt Merah sells this chicken floss bun with a great addition - a Golden Chicken Bak Kwa 'Coin'. Yummy!!

    In Holland V, there's a unique bakery behind Holland Rd Shopping Ctr with a French name, I suddenly cannot recall the name! They have nice sandwiches as well as a lovely Cream Cheese Blueberry Bun!

    OK, start sharing and recommending!
