  • ptw
    16 Aug 05, 01:45
    Hi everyone, I am new here,

    I hav been on contract for almost 3 yrs before converted to permanent staff. really felt terrible but have no choice cos the economy was so bad then. Salary was very little, no prospect, work hard to have your contract renewed etc etc.

    When I knew that my contract position is to be outsource, I hav no choice but get ready for being jobless soon. My ex broke up with me during tat period and I was devastated. so hopeless and demoralised.

    I nvr gave up hope cos I have no alternate route but to keep searching for any new job vacancy anywhere in SG. I am lucky cos some of the managers from my company supported me for perm position and before I was converted to perm, I received a confirmation from another company but i decided to stay on with my current co.

    During that period, I was very active in attd interviews, rushing for interview after work, taking leave for another and rushing for classes for my final semester in a local poly.

    Just feel that as long as you keep pushing yourself and by keeping yourself occupied be it contract, temp or even on part time basis, all such experiences acumulate and will,in my opinion add certain value in you.

    Just tell yourself, dun ever giv up.
