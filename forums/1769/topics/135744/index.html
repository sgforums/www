    Where?: Kazbar
    When?: 17th June 2005
    Time?: 7ish to 1.30am

    Kazbar in Capital Square is the ideal place to go, if you want to escape from the hectic world for a few hours. This enchanting Middle Eastern cafe and bar will entice you with its intoxicating aromas of Mediterrenean food and shisha (flavoured Turkish tobacco). The decor of Kazbar is equally enticing; upon entering you'll be welcomed with large cushions for relaxing on, day beds and intricate Middle Eastern carpets and craftwork. The ambience screams 'chill-out', making it a very inviting prospect after a long, hard day at work. Those who want an intimate experience can sit in a private candlelit alcove, and those who like to people-watch can go al fresco.

    Kazbar's drinks selection has everything from shooters and cocktails to beers and Middle Eastern wines. There are also some tasty Middle-Eastern snacks to complement the drinks; among them are kebabs, pita bread, charcol-grilled chicken on skewers and also some sugary and syrupy Arabic sweets. Complete this relaxing evening by laying back on the cushions with some shisha, traditionally prepared in a variety of flavours.

    Going to Kazbar will make you understand what true relaxation is all about...
