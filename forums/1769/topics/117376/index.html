    I try to... at least once a year. More often if time and most importantly, finances, allow!

    I used to have a close friend who owned a car and we'd drive up to Malaysia for short trips pretty often, at least once a month. We'd just grab a roadmap and drive along and decide impromptu which state we'd like to stop at and explore. It was spontaneous and very exciting! I'd do it again if I had a car or if I had a close friend who was game to do stuff like this and had a car. hehe! I loved loved loved those road trips!

    I seem to have a habit of visiting the same places more than once. Odd, isn't it? I've been to Genting thrice, Malacca thrice (and soon, four times) and twice to Batam. Hmm... been to the USA twice too but it was to different states so that doesn't count.

    I feel it's very important to travel out of Singapore to relax regularly. Destination is kinda secondary because every place has unique characteristics to be explored and discovered. Travelling is just a really good way of destressing, of being unshackled and just enjoying life and getting the rest and relaxation one needs and very often deserves.
