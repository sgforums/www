    Oooh! I went to watch Batman Begins last night and my verdict?

    4.5 stars!!!

    It was an awesome movie!! I highly recommend it! There's lots of action, kewl costume, and amazing Batmobile... everything about it was right!

    (Especially the scenes of Bruce Wayne sleeping, with his large biceps showing, and all scenes where he appeared topless!)

    I liked how the characters were multi-faceted... the monks he trained with had good intentions but yet, they had dubious and evil motives at the same time; Bruce Wayne himself is, at times, very much an anti-hero persona; etc etc. There was action, intrigue, tender moments, dark moments... and the general doom and gloom of Gotham City was protrayed very well.

    Amazing movie! And, of course, a very very sexy Batman!! The sexiest Batman, I would say!

    BTW, it was the ONLY Batman movie I bothered to watch from beginning to end. Most of the other Batman movies I only watched on TV or watched in the movies and promptly forgot it cause I was so unimpressed. But this one, THIS ONE... oh, it stole my heart and took my breath away!!!

    And, I'm now the happiest girl in the world, knowing that Christian Bale has signed on to star in three more Batman movies!! I am truly blessed!!!
