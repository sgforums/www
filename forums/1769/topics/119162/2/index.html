  • Rhonda
    23 Feb 05, 19:28
    Ah! I just remembered some other stuff that the wet markets have that supermarkets don't!

    - Freshly grated coconuts
    Choose your coconut from a rattan basket full of 'em and watch the vendor grate it for you right before your eyes... available only at the wet markets!

    - Freshly squeezed coconut milk
    Can only find the packet version in the supermarts.

    - Mutton
    Only certain wet markets have this available for sale, and I definitely don't remember any supermarts that sell mutton. Enlighten me!

    - Duck
    I think like mutton, an entire duck might not be found in most wet markets too, much less the supermarts.

    - Authentic, home-made recipes for different curry pastes and powders
    Those mama shops in the dried food sections sometimes sell an array of those for the various types of curries you might feel like cooking that day. Needless to say, they are usually better than the packaged / bottled stuff.

    - Ancillary stores alongside the wet markets that sell kitchen wares
    I love to wander inside and discover the things they have inside - moulds for making curry puffs / soon kuehs, kueh pie tee moulds, steamers, dim sum bamboo baskets, individual ceramic pots for double-boiled soups, pestle and mortar, etc etc ... They're mini treasure-troves sometimes.
