    try this.. is cajun and VERY unhealthy.. but delicious

    get about half a block of butter, a cup of brown sugar and about 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon... combine the mess over low heat on a frying pan/skillet thing...

    stir until it becomes a brown liquid.. add in about 1/4 cup of banana liqueur..

    place in about 4 bananas (sliced lengthwise into 2 or 3 slices)

    slowly cook until the bananas are soft and browned..

    add in some rum (carefully or else everything will catch fire too soon)

    allow to heat and cook for a while then flambe it...

    when it stops burning, place a few bananas in a plate and add vanilla ice cream.. spoon the sauce over the ice cream...
