    Remember I was raving about this special north western thai beef noodles, semi dry, and supper shiok ? ....

    I found it at golden mile ! .....

    on the ground floor, beside thai joint called "Thai Disco 2" .... it's not a resturant, just a food stall in the shopping mall amongst the spices shops ....

    the portions are small, even tiny, so order two at once (that's just for starters) they have the "dry" and the "soup" version, both are shiok ... the "soup" version is actually semi dry, but the soup is so dark and thick and savory, it's almost like a stew .....

    dump in basil, bean sprouts, sugar, chili flakes, crushed peanuts, and get a packet of those dried deep fried pork skin called "crab moo" .... stir everything up .... and viola ! ..... aroi mak mak ! .... kway teow nuea ti Singapore, dee thi soot ! ....

    our group spent over 50 dollars there, so you can imagine how much we ate .... but it was goooooooooooooooooooood

    next up, there's a stall selling the stewed pork trotter on rice just next door .... which, I guess, would be next week's target ...

    muahahahahahahaha .....
