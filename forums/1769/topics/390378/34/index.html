  • fudgester
    19 Feb 10, 22:57

    Frankly speaking..... I think the Premier League clubs are in bad financial shape now precisely because of the formation of the Premier League in the early 90s.

    Back when it was just known as Division 1, all clubs in the top four divisions shared all the TV rights money together.... you didn't hear of clubs going kaput then.

    Then 24-odd clubs broke away to hoard all the TV rights money for themselves by forming the Premier League.... the end result was a huge chasm between the Premiership and the Championship.

    Championship teams that got promoted would often get relegated quickly because they couldn't afford to buy top players to stay in the Premiership, and Premiership teams saw their budget deficits balloon just to keep up with the others. Inflation quickly set in.

    Back when I was in Primary 6 (1995), the transfer of Andy Cole from Newcastle to Man U for 7 million pounds was huge news..... now, 7 million is absolutely laughable....
