  • Rhonda
    27 Aug 05, 02:14
    Originally posted by Fatum:
    say rhonda, do they take spare parts of only accident victims ? ... or anyone whose spare parts can be used ...
    Nope, not just restricted to Road Traffic Accident victims. Anyone who died, and did not sign the form opting themselves out of the HOTA (Human Organ Transplant Act), is eligible for organ donation provided, their condition prior to death renders organ harvesting feasible, ie. they must be 'good candidates' for organ harvesting. Some people die from diseases like End Stage Renal Failure, which would more or less render them totally unsuitable for organ donation.
