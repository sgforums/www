    1). Tau huay: go to Engin canteen. There's a stall specializing in bean curd, soya bean drinks, etc.

    2). Arts: I dunno. I can only advise on Muslim food. The Malay and Western stalls are so-so, while the Indian stall is a rip-off (their 'murtabak' is more like egg prata).

    4). Engin: Western stall and Indonesian Panggang are both excellent. The Western stall makes good chicken baked rice for just $2.20.... it's very creamy and fragrant.
