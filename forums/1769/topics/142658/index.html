    Waaaaaah!! Nobody told me that Taka was having a food fair in their basement! Happened to be there today and then realised that drats, tomorrow is the last day!

    There are lots of food stalls there serving really yummy food! It was super crowded, there's virtually no place for you to sit down and eat, and be prepared to be squeezed and pushed about if you go. It's worth it though... a lotta lotta good food!

    There's vietnamese fried spring rolls with rice paper, gado gado, nyonya kueh, fried vegetable fritters (loooong queue for that stall!), dumplings, shanghainese stir-fried rice cakes, etc etc etc!!

    And, I think I'm going to 'expire' soon...
