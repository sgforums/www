    Is yours a male cat? How old is it?

    If it's a young cat (< 1yr), it's possible that it got lost and didn't know the way home. Male adult cats also have a tendency to roam about - an instinct to mark and conquer more territory, even to the extent of leaving a regular food source. However, it may one day return and turn up at your door again. It's happened before.

    You can try checking with SPCA, the classifieds or local cat websites/ forums to see if anyone has found your cat.
