    A suggestion :)

    YMCA organises this camp for intellectually and physically disabled youths twice a year. I brought my students to one of the camps and it was really fantastic. IN june, the volunteers were mainly students from tertiery but they mentioned that during the year end camps, more working adults join. It can be physically taxing and tiring, but I find that it really challenges both the volunteers AND the beneficieries.

    If you are interested can check out the website :)

    http://www.ymca.org.sg/Web/main.aspx?ID=,a14ea065-85d1-4797-a28c-d862b147bb57

     Oh.. it would be good if you join only if you are able to commit for the days. The previous time almost 50 volunteers pulled out last minute and logistically it was very difficult for the organisers looking for volunteers at the last minute.
