    I've always been of the firm belief that the Prius makes absolutely NO economic sense.

    - You pay a premium for the automobile, but you don't get a choice of colours or options.

    - You get an automobile with relatively anemic performance (110 HP when both the gas engine and electric motor are operating).

    - You have to shell out several thousand dollars to replace all the rechargeable batteries after several years, if you decide to keep the vehicle that long. If you decide to sell the vehicle before replacing the batteries, the resale value takes a huge hit as the potential buyer has to factor in the cost of replacing the batteries.

    - The gas mileage isn't all that much better than other economy vehicles which cost less that the Prius and don't have expensive batteries that need replacing every few years.
