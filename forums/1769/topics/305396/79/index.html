    Originally posted by bluejuice:

    err what is freezer burn?

    Freezer burn occurs when you don't cover meat properly before freezing it.  The dry cold temperature removes all moisture from the food, and the raw meats appear dark brown because it ends up all dried-up.  The meat becomes drier and tougher, and no longer worth your while to cook it.
