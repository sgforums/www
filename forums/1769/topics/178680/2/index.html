    Well, not quite fair to discredit ALL self-help books with one swipe!

    You won't know everything about everything. It's good to have self-confidence but sometimes, one is just clueless and at a loss. Sometimes, one might need some help and advice and reading self-help books from people who are experienced and who might have gone through the same situation might help.

    Self-help books help to explain things and situations to people. And in extreme cases where deep emotional trauma is being experienced, eg. death of a loved one, going through a divorce, aftermath of physical abuse, etc. it might help to read self-help books from counsellors or even from people who were once victims themselves but have since crawled out of the abyss. This can supplement the counselling sessions one might, or might not have.

    Those "How to..." books can be very indispensible at times.
