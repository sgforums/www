    Just been to the website, plan looks great!

    Embarrassed to confess, that because it was a packaged tour, we were pretty much led around, so I don't have precise recommendations with names and addresses for you

    What I think you die die must try and are probably already in your itinerary, are:

    Ice cream. Milk, lavendar and melon flavour. Someone told me cherry blossom flavour tastes like cough syrup

    Hot spring , indoors & outdoors. Don't soak excessively long please, will get giddy LOL. And communal baths

    I'm not sure if they still have it in July, but in the evenings, there are fireworks over Lake Toya. So if you have a room overlooking the lake, remember to stay in after dinner to watch the fireworks V romantic neh

    Ramen . Actually I find they are close to what we have here.

    Seafood - urchin, crab, scallop

    Fruits - strawberries , melon, etc

    If your girl is a shopper, Sapporo has a Burberry Blue Label shop, it's in a dept store near the TV Tower. Tourist == tax-free.
    There is also a factory outlet mall at Chitose called Rera - Coach store got discount! http://www.outlet-rera.com/

    Posh pastry / cake shops - can go in try sample. The cakes, chocolates are very good.
