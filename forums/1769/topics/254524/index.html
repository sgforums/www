    FAVOURITE SOUVENIR FROM TRIP

    Handphone charm - dunno what the hell is it but it's annoyingly cute This is a green-colored character with a ball at its crotch. (Mine is wearing a bear suit). What you do with the doll is, you pull the ball that is attached with a string to the doll, and when the string retracts, the doll shudders "KURU-KURU" I just call it kuru-kuru. If anyone knows what the character is, do enlighten me!!
