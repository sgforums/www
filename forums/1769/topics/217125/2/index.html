    Originally posted by jetta:

    is car ownership there expensive?

    Cars are not too expensive in Oz as compared to Singapore. In Melbourne, one can easily get a brand new litte car for Toyota Echo for A$15k or less. Car rego costs over A$500 a year depending on the type/size/capacity of the car, insurance varies depending on age, location and type of car. The younger the driver (16-25 yo) the more expensive the car insurance will become.

    As for maintaining the car, since labour is generally expensive in Oz so regardless the cheap spare parts, the service can easily cost from $100 - $1000 depending on the type of the service required for the car.

    It is not too bad at the end of the day if it can take you from A to B and you get plenty of pleasure out of driving the car.

    I think a car is a must if you are planning to stay in Oz permanently for not all places can be reached using public transport. You can save so much time driving to places too (not to the city though - best to catch the train in).
