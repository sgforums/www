    DAY 8 (20 Jan 2007)
    Woke up at 4am....got ready and run to Taj Mahal. The hostel we stayed in was only 200m away from Taj Mahal... It was pitch dark and we managed to find our way there ...

    Bought the tickets and upon entering, we had our stuff searched/checked at the entrance by some "grumbly" security personnels. The lady officers (i think it's a police), saw my LED torchlight and showing gesture of keeping it. I snatched it back from her hands and proceed to the lockers to put it.

    For the locker side, someone will mend them...ie...take the stuff from you, go in to the room (which is visible to us), open the locker and SUPPOSEDLY to put our things in there. I noticed that the old man opened my locker, put his hand it in. When he retract his hands from my locker, he seems to be holding something in his hands and try to hide it. He walked over (without looking at me), and pass me the keys. I demanded him to show me what is he holding in his hands. He pretended not to hear and seem dun understand. I screamed at him (while the Korean Tourists are looking on)...and he no choice, walked back to the locker, open the locker, and throw my torchlights and sweets, into the locker finally. He came back (face black black) and gave me the keys again. I asked to see his hands again...he curse and swear and gave me 2 of my sweets back, took one of the sweets and popped into his mouth. I was enraged and the Korean Tourists panicked. All of them started locking and tying their bags...

    I told the Chief police guy and he said : Nothing i can do...! He did not take your torchlight hah? and seems disappointed. OMG!!

    I told them that I am reporting to the Embassy and India Tourism Board in Singapore. and I was here to survey and take pics for me (blaf blaf a bit).. They actually moved away from me...

    I think that I made such a scene, from the corner of my eyes, I saw the police personnels siam me wherever I walked. I gave them real cold stares all the way.
