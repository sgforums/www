    India was my 1st photography trip.

    I have yet to learn to proper technqiues of using a DSLR cammy and how to compose a pic. My cammy was brand new and I spent most of my time fumbling with the settings, just to take a simple picture.

    ..please excuse me as most of my pics are not good and many are erh.. Blur..!

    I like to explore new place and love adventures. Even the 10-day trip was exhausting and we kana setbacks many times, I find India incredible, simply awesiome....



    Day 1....
    Reached Delhi Airport, my 1st hippcup, my tripod kana stolen when my Backpack checked out of the conveyor belt ..KNN! Have to explain to 10 airport officials before they understand what is a TRIPOD!

    After settling the report, we moved to the taxi counter and managed to bargain for a good price to the Railway Station. We immediately bought the tickets (many hand languages) to Allahabad to catch the Kumbh Mela Festival on time...

    so 3 guys and me auntie braved thru' the crowd/the chaos.

    We had our 1st meal, Prata with Mutton (250gram, they charge by the weight) at the Station Cafe with their famous Cha-i (lovely ginger tea) ...

    It's cold and I get to wear my thick jacket and I look like a cool panda!

    The locals treated me very well,.. maybe I am a Chinese Lady/Auntie?

    Dunno what is the detector for..it's placed right in the middle of the street, so as in the Railway Station when it did not work at all...hmm....

    Challenging indeed!

    So we are very careful, and "eye-ball" whoever cross our path!
