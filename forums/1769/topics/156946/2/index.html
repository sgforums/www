    Rhonda, for Melbourne the issue isn't so much temperature as wind-chill. Get a jacket that doesn't let the wind right through it and you're off to a good start. I just picked up this 3/4 Angora wool overcoat that's not too thick, but it's got an excellent inner lining that will keep the wind out, and it works great.

    It IS more sensible to get your clothes here, and if possible, shop off-season to get the best prices. Since you're arriving in the early part of the year, you should be OK because the warm clothing will be priced for clearance.
