    Ok.. how to survive the 4 seasons...

    Summer
    Like the slogan we have here "Slip, Slop, Slap!" aka slip on your sunnies (preferably with UVA & UVB protection), slop on some sunscreen, and slap on a hat!

    Autumn
    Now's the time to start wearing warm, fluffy bed socks esp. when the nights start to get a lil' bit chilly. Good time to also start looking around for long john's/thermal underwear!

    Winter
    A good coat is a must... looks good & keeps you warm, too! It may rain some days, so be prepared with an umbrella! (hmm actually, it will rain regardless of what season it is in Melb, I heard! ) It can get pretty cold in Melb, according to some relatives living there, but if things get too dreary for you, sit by the heater with a cuppa soup and a good ol' book, and you'll be right!

    Spring
    Ooh, my fave season of the year! Beware the pollen, though. Some people get really bad hayfever during Sept/Oct If you do, try not to wash/hang out your clothes during days where pollen count is high. Flowers will be blooming during this season... take a trip to Canberra for the Tulip Festival!
