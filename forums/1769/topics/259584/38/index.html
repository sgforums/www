    Debit cards work in a way that all your debit card transactions amount
    will be earmarked (ie. Put on hold). When you first perform the
    transaction using your Debit Card, our system will check if you have
    sufficient funds in your account. If you do not have sufficient balance
    in your Savings Account, the debit transaction will not be able to go
    through.

    Debit Card is different from Credit card whereby a credit limit will be
    given customer for Credit Cards. Credit Card will not touch on your
    Savings Account's existing balance. Payment to credit card can be done
    when the bill statement is received.

    Hence, you do have sufficient funds for all your previous debit card
    transactions.
