    Err butter rice is simple la
    It's like fried rice
    Dump 1 yellow/white onion into the blender with 1 whole garlic (not clove, the whoel thing skinned) and some ginger and a bit of salt) Sorry hor mine is alot of agaration one (All these really depends on the amount of rice you cooking. I cooked enough rice for today too)
