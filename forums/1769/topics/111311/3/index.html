    True X, here's why I think the photos are fake and doctored...

    In a typical brain surgery, once you incise the scalp, there's a tremendous amount of blood that will ooze out. Observe the hair surrounding the surgical incisions... blood-free. Totally blood-free. Impossible in brain surgery. Patients usually come out of the OT with a shaved head, area immediately surrounding the incision shaved, or, hair heavily matted with blood, iodine and what-not. Not even the tell-tale sign of yellow/brown iodine staining the white hair in that pic!

    Secondly, for a major op like this, sterility would be a huge concern and the area immediately surrounding the surgical incision would be completely covered with the typical sterilised green dressing towel.

    Thirdly, in an op like this, surgical instruments like retractors would be used to pull open the scalp and hold it open. I also think the skull has been cut through too cleanly in this case.

    Also, in a major op like this one, we DO NOT USE oxygen mask at all!!! That would be certain death!! The fella would HAVE been intubated and placed on a ventilator!! I'd seriously like to know which hospital in Japan uses just an Oxygen Mask for ops like these!
