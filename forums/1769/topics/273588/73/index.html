    Originally posted by sbst275:
    Those considered junk food?

    Got better ones ar?
    Yep, the junk food means the ingredients comprise mostly of corn / rice fillers, and whatever little meat they have are meat by-products (eg. chicken meal by product would include bone, feathers, chicken feet, head, organs, etc.) which aren't fit for human consumption, plus junk food have added colouring and are high in salt which may cause urinary tract problems in the cats in the long run.

    The better brands are made with human-grade meat (cats need lots of protein, even more than dogs), with very little carbo component - they can be found at good pet shops.
