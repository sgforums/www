    OK, I was there this morning way before they opened their doors to the public. Despite going there straight after night duty, I was totally awake and no one would know I didn't get to sleep a wink at all the previous night and hark... no coffee imbibed!!

    It was a large hall. The front half was full of books piled on tables whilst the back half was cordoned off for the staff to sort out the boxes and crates of books. It was divided into Childrens' Section to the far right, then Cookery, Fiction, Non-Fiction, Business, and Mandarin. There were novels that were three for $10 indeed but none of the titles were of interest to me. I ended up getting three cookbooks and about five other non-fiction books for light reading.

    I'm really happy with my cookbooks! One of them is a Sri Owen cookbook on Noodles that costs $36++ and it was there, spanking new, for just $8!! I was grinning like a Cheshire cat with that great treasure!!

    I've been to the warehouse sale by MPH in previous years and in terms of variety of titles, I think MPH wins hands down. So, another trip to Expo next month for the MPH Warehouse Sale!
