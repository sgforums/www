    If you can get fresh mushrooms, what you do is to slice it thinly. Stir fry the mushrooms with some oil, then pour in 1/2 cup of oyster sauce and stir fry till the mushrooms are really soft.

    Or use a tablespoon of oyster sauce, mash up some century eggs and store in a fridge for an hour.

    Edit: pour in 1/2 a tablespoon of sesame oil too. Really makes the century egg taste smooth on the tofu. Sprinkle on some chop onion stalks and you are considered a genius!

    Then you scoop the century egg oyster sauce onto cold firm tofu and serve.

    I also like to stir fry oyster sauce with cubed yams. Then transfer the yams and oyster sauce into white cooking rice and viola...you have yam rice!
