    The dailies just at SSO isle alone are (I hope this will help u tare):-

    1 - Further Conversions - kill 5 mechanicals, and once you loot them, activate the crystal cores to convert them.

    2 - Arm the Wards - kill the undead in the same area, until you get 4 mana remnants. Go to a crystal core, right click on the remnants, and turn in both of the first quests.

    3 - Know your Ley Lines - go to the 3 location (Greengil coast, the demon slaying area and Dawnblade village where the blood elves are), and right click on the crystal you are given.

    4 - Battle must go on - Kill 6 random demons in tis area, then find the emissary of hate (likely already dead) and right clik on the banner you are given to plant it on the body.

    5 - Air Strikes must continue - use the item you are given while in flight to bomb the crap out of the guys in the dead scar. May have to do this twice if running after other people.

    6 - Crush the Dawnblade - Kill 6,6,3 of the guys over here. The marksmen are the hardest to find, and beware of the fast respawn time here.

    7 - Don't Stop now - kill Myrmidons on the coast until they drop 3 keys, then use those keys to open the chests on the coast, which contain the ore you need.

    8 - Keeping the enemy at bay - use the oil to burn the sails of the 3 ships, then kill 6 randoms once you land on the boat.

    9 - Disrupt the Greengill coast - Kill Sirens in this area, and when they drop orbs, right click them, and target the murloc slaves.

    10 - Open for business - Collect 5 blood berries. U can get it almost ard the whole isle.
