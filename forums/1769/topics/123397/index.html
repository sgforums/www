    If you love yourself, it would be bliss... if you don't, it'll be torture! hehehe!

    Seriously though, there are some things where similarities are vital. For instance, moral values, or religion. If you love peace and quiet and your idea of relaxation is to stay home doing sedate things whilst your partner loves rowdiness and outdoor activities, then there'll be a huge disparity. Sooner or later, you'll grate on each other's nerves.

    In other things, it's important to stay different. You can in fact end up playing complimentary but opposite roles. For instance, an aggressive person might tone down his / her stance when faced with a calm and collected person. Can you imagine the turmoil if aggressive meets aggressive? Or can you imagine the standstill and absolute inactivity that might occur when Type C meets Type C?

    There's no tried and tested formula. I guess it depends on the alchemy between the two. The willingness to compromise and the severity of the differences also comes into play.
