  • MaNyZeR

    i recommend you one place... you will sure like it.

    its called Hokkaido japanese restaurant... you can type this in google and then go to its website.

    Its buffet style, and you can have sushi, those udon, rice, wraps, chawamushi etc etc etc and they all taste damn fucking nice, esp chawamushi with cheese. The garlic fried rice is nice, but i cannot comment on the sushi because i am not a sushi lover, but my friends love their sushi to the max.

    For sashimi lovers, this is the heaven for you. Their sashimi slices are thick and fresh. Very very fresh and its FREE FLOW!!!!!!!! You can order as much as you want, and the quantity is HUGE and quality is AWESOME!!! I never bluff you, my friends that eat sashimi all say damn nice, much better than sashimi from other places.

    The only downside is that its quite expensive ~$40, and its located at Turf City, Bukit Timah.

    You should go try if you really like sushi and Sashimi.
