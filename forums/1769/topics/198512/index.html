    Location: Wisma Atria
    Dishes Orders:
    1. Fried Prawn Mee
    2. Vegetarian Nasi briyani from Chutney
    3. Naan and Chicken Masala
    4. Beef Noodles
    5. Dao Xiao Mian (Both chicken and Beef)
    6. TOAST
    VERDICT - CMI!!!

    1. Fried PRawn Mee
    Gravy of Prawn Mee don't taste seafoody and fragrant. Chilli taste weird. Prawn okay. Only thing special abt it is the packaging.

    2. Nasi Briyani
    Bismati rice is nice for a gooood reason - it is fragrant and fluffy. BUT for some reason this one is NUAH. OILY AND NUAH. Dhal also CMI.

    3. Naan and Chicken MAsala.
    The PLAIN bismati rice here is much better than the vegetarian Nasi Briyani. Chicken Masala is ok, not spicy. Naan not bad, fragrant.

    4. beef Noodle
    Only Item that is unreservedly nice. Nice Sauce. Nice beef. Nice Beef Balls. Nice Tao Gey. Nothing exciting but nice enough.

    5. Dao Xiao Mian
    The MIAN itself has a nice texture. Unfortunately the soup base really CMI. The beef one is worse than the chicken soup.

    6. Toast
    Ordered Teh C, Kopi C Kosong. 4 slices of THICK toast with butter/sugar, OTAH, Peanut Butter, Hey Bee Hiam.
    Butter sugar is okay. Otah is interesting. Hey bee hiam is okay. Peanut butter is DAMN NICE. Order Kopi C kOsong but in the end not kosong.

    The peanut butter on thick toast is the best of all of them. -nodnod- this one i can imagine having a craving to eat again.
