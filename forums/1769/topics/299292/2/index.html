    This ruins are the fragment of monolithic pillar erected by the Mauryan Empire King Ashoka (272-232 BC). It is made from Chunar Sand Stone with the lions in four directions (now the emblem of the Government of India) on the top of the pillar. There is an inscription on the pillar which warns the monks and nuns against creating schism in the monks committee.
