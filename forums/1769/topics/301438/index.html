    I first read this comic book in the late 80's. I borrowed it from my secondary school's library then.



    It's a really touching comic about a nuclear war that marked the end of the world and how an elderly couple living out in the countryside had no idea that they were probably the sole survivors within a large radius. They had prudently built a bomb shelter, ensuring their survival, but over the days post-explosion, they started to fall ill from the effects of the bomb, but they remained sorrowfully hopeful that help will reach them... someday...

    Really poignant! If you can get your hands on a copy, you won't regret reading it!
