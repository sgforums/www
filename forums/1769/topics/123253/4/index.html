    hey y did u fail on my fav dish ?
    During wrapping ? i guess it is..
    there is a trick to wrap it.

    rem dnt use water on the cooked sushi rice, water will make rice non-stick (common sense)

    Place the a sheet of seaweed, spread rice full on the seaweed
    (common mistake is spreading the rice at the edge of seaweed)

    place the bamboo strip on table with the sushi etc..

    1. fold (dnt roll bamboo strip) 1/4 of the strip inwards and try gather
    the rice to the edge by firming and press alittle

    - release the bamboo strip back to flat
    - shld get a humhhhh... shape of long tube with long seaweed untouch
    infront of the sushi

    2. fold 1/2 of the bamboo inward, and slowly bit by bit roll the
    sushi with a little firm touch.

    Voila... U are done..

    (Practice make perfect) i was clumsy on my first attempt.. heeee
    if i can made it, shldn't be a problem.
