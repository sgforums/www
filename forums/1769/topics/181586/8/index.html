    Garlic Mashed Potatoes (My fave)

    Cut 4 or 5 potatoes into half. No need to peel.
    Place in microwave safe container.
    Add a splash of milk, a dollop of butter and one clove of minced garlic.
    Cover container and microwave for 5 min or until soft enough to mash.
    When done, you can add more butter if you like.
    Voila!
