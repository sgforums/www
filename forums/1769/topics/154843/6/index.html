    Interesting turn of events!

    I just called up Canossaville Children's Home to fix a date and was told that they received so many book donations over the festive period that they no longer need books!! So far, I've only collected from fudgester this afternoon. Phew! Thank goodness I haven't crawled all over the island to collect all the books yet!

    Canossaville referred me to another children's home, the Chin Swee Lan Methodist Children's Home at Serangoon and this home would like to have the books instead. So, fudgester, your books and SL's soft toys will land there.

    I checked to see if the Home would arrange pick-up of books and such from the rest of you but they don't have the resources to do that.

    If you still want to donate your books, I'll urge you to drop off the books at the Home yourself if you're in the vicinity. The phone no. for the Chin Swee Lan Methodist Children's Home is 62854941 and you can either call them first and drop the books off during office hours or you could just drive up anytime and leave them at the guard house.

    I'm quite surprised at the turn of events because last September, when I spoke to them, things seemed a bit different. I guess Canossaville really received a lotta lotta books during the Christmas period.
