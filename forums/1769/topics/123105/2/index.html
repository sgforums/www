    You know, when we're all online, everything's peachy and we interact well and all... but I'm just wondering, how different are we all in real life?

    That's one reason why I love being online, you get to know folks you might otherwise not have given a 2nd glance to in real life. We all tend to gravitate towards people we can clique with and sometimes, in doing so, we rob ourselves of opportunities to get to know folks who might not seem to fit into our typical circle of friends but, who we might actually interact well with on a deep, intense level.

    I mean, how many times have we looked at an acquaintance and think, 'Nah, he/she will only remain an acquaintance' but somehow, situations and opportunities conspire to expose the person in a different light and then, you become close pals.

    Here, in Club 30, we might be as different as Night and Day, your preferred pasttime might not be mine and your way of relaxing might clash with my style but right here in the forum at least, our minds meet when we find we're on the same wavelength and share the same opinions and values and our hearts, for the moment, get soothed knowing that we don't stand alone in our ideals.

    Thanks for sharing a part of yourselves here.
