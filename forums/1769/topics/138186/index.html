    Saaaaad, man!!! All that working out at the gym!! All that happiness over clothes getting looser and what-not.

    So, if you've never done the test, and you think that you've been too happy for far too long and need to get depressed for a change, go try the Body Composition Analyzer!

    BTW, this is what that machine does :

    "InBody 3.0 uses segmental multi-frequency bioelectrical impedance analysis (SMFBIA) to measure body composition.

    Bioelectrical Impedance Analysis (BIA) sends minute electric currents that a patient is unable to feel. It measures biological impedance to measure body composition."

    You can find this, and more info, in http://www.smitechasia.com.sg/product.php?selected_product_id=122.

    Basically, it measures weight, body fat mass and body fat percentage, body water mass, lean body mass, BMI, Obesity Levels, Basal Metabolic Rate, Body Density, etc etc etc.

    After the test, you'll basically get a feel of what your body is composed of!
