    wanna talk abt weird? hmm... here goes.

    i like wantan mee/bak chor mee/fish ball mee/prawn mee with lots of dark soya sauce, the darker the better

    i love chicken feet in dark sauce but not the white cold salad dish kind

    but for vegetarian meehoon, i prefer the white version

    for fried carrot cake, also prefer the white one, dun like the black one

    i dun eat tofu unless it's the round egg kind or the fried (not steamed) agedashi tofu

    i only eat taugua if it's the fried one in lontong - anything else, no thanks.

    i eat tau pok if it's in laksa or in lor zup but not the malay way with taugei etc

    i drink soya bean milk but dun really fancy tauhuey (unless it's super good and smooth, like the one we had at rochor the last time!)

    i adore french beans but dun eat long beans

    i love most vegs bar the red coloured ones because i think there's a weird smell

    i eat pig's intestines, stomach, liver, etc but not tongue, blood, lungs, ears

    i hate peas - will pick every single one out from my fried rice - unless they're super soft

    enuff examples?
