    Welcome to the circle of booklovers! 

    Alrighty... 10 books for 2009.  Will update our list! 

    We just got this thing going for 2009.  For 2008 and previous years, all we had was just a thread talking about the books we have read for that particular month.  We weren't very regular with the thread though and sometimes, we went for months without anyone starting that thread. 

    Now, we're trying to set things in motion by starting a little club.  I noticed that over the years, a few avid readers started to fall off the wagon, including myself, due to work and other responsibilities.  Also, we seem to have Club 30 peeps who never seemed to have stopped reading ardently.  I thought it'll be great to keep up the reading habit and gather like-minded folks together for scoops on books!
