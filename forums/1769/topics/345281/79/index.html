    Singapore -4.5% 4Q08 actual

    So, if Singapore GDP is unchanged in each quarter of this year CY2009 growth will still be -4.5% year on year (bear in mind that QoQ GDP growth has been negative in four of the last five quarters and fell 4.5% in 4Q08 alone. Putting our forecast in context, a -10% GDP decline is not so crazy after all.

    Sadly, we think other economists will need to follow us in revising down their numbers very sharply.

    In most cases 2008 ended so weakly that this "starting point" for 2009 GDP is a (large) negative number vs the full year 2008. This provides the context for the massive downgrades that we have seen in our 2009 growth estimates.
