    If you haven't heard about it yet, listen and listen good!

    Touted to be 'Asia's largest outdoor cinema festival', this event returns to Singapore this month! You'll get to watch movies under the stars at The Padang on a new, 'gargantuan, inflatable screen'!

    The thing is, at $15 per entry and with a 'No refunds. No pets, chairs or raised seating.' rule, it means that even if it rains, you can watch the movie under an umbrella, or forfeit the money you've paid!

    Tickets are available at the door though, which might be a better option cause if it rains, you won't end up wasting money.

    Entry is free IF you dress up as a Batman Begins character or a Fantastic Four character on 17 September!

    Entry is also free on 19 September, but you can donate any amount at the door and all proceeds will go to the Children's Cancer Foundation.

    Here's their movie schedule :

    Thu, 15 Sep, A Lot Like Love
    Fri, 16 Sep, Mr & Mrs Smith
    Sat, 17 Sep, Batman Begins & Fantastic Four
    Mon, 19 Sep, Madagascar
    Tue, 20 Sep, The Island
    Wed, 21 Sep, Shutter
    Thu, 22 Sep, Hitch
    Fri, 23 Sep, Initial D
    Sat, 24 Sep, Harry Potter And The Chamber of Secrets, & Harry Potter And the Prisoner of Azkaban

    Doors open at 6pm, and the movies start at 8:30pm.

    For more details, visit their website : http://www.starlightcinema.com/
