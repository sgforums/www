    Hmm... I love sandwiches! Like...

    - Burger King's BK Deluxe Breakfast 'sandwich' - can buy one and eat half for b'fast, half for lunch!

    - Subway's Sweet Onion Teriyaki Chicken sandwich with focaccia or their Italian or onion bread;

    - Subway's Meatball Sandwich

    I also like sandwiches with omelette and ham, cheese (melted cheese is da best!!) / cheese spread, sometimes luncheon meat with chilli sauce and egg, sometimes chuck some salad in between bread and it becomes a sandwich! haha!

    I made an absolutely deluxe sandwich once - lightly spared margarine / butter on one side of two slices of bread, place them buttered side down on a hot pan / grill to brown, remove. Then, add cheese spread / mayo on the other side, add shredded lettuce, sliced tomatoes, cucumber and hard-boiled egg, and add a slice or two of honey-baked ham. YUMMY!!!

    I love to make sandwiches out of various types of cold cuts. Cold Storage has a pretty interesting selection.

    Another time, when I was in the US and I got hungry in the middle of the night, I raided my friend's fridge and came up with an Onion Bagel Sandwich with generous chunks of crabmeat and mayo inside. YUMMMY!!!!!
