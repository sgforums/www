    Aiyohhh...so many places to eat...so little time

    Changi Village: Nasi Lemak Charlies Corner Steaks; Coffee Shop next to Changi Village Hotel for Zhi Cha (Chilli Crab, Yam Basket, Pai Kut Wong).

    Simpang Bedok: Day Time Market #02 hawker centre~Gong Bao Yong Tau Fu.

    Bedok bus interchange: Ice Jelly & Malay Stall Mee Soto
    Bedok Blk 85 market: bak Chor Mee.

    East Coast Park Village Hawker Centre: BBQ Stingray, Wanton Mee & Coconut drink.

    Marine Parade Central Hawker Centre: Wet Char Kway teow (Near to Macdonald)

    East Coast Road, Katong~katong laksa, Mackeral Otah Otah, Tau kua Pao, Durian Puffs

    Geylang~Beef Kway Teow, Dim Sum, Bean Curd, You tiao, Lor 13 zhi cha (They do the best sambal Kangkong); Turtle Soup, teochew porridge; gong bao frog legs in claypot. tau huay.

    Old Airport Road hawker centre: Char Kway Teow, Lor Mee, Fu Zhou Fishball noodles, ahballing desserts, Western BBQ Garlic sauce Steak. Dish of gong gong. And freshly made oneh oneh.

    macpherson~BBQ Chicken Wings

    Joo chiat~fei fei wanton mee, wang Wang Bak Chor Mee
