    Time flies! November 2007 marks my first year and a half in Melbourne!

    Things have changed tremendously since! I've snapped out of the blues and I've started to creep out of my shell and explore Melbourne and the suburbs. The dangerous thing is, I've discovered shopping hot spots here! *Red lights flash!! Alarms beep!!!

    I've also done a lot on the home front. For the first time since I moved out of my family home when I was aged 21 years, I feel like my rented apartment is truly HOME to me. I've done lots to make it cozy and nice (pardon the perpetual mess and clutter though! ) and I still have lofty plans to make it even better! The only thing that's stopping me is the moolah, or lack of thereof!

    OK, thought I'd just share a few special photos with you peeps to commemorate this milestone!



    This is the very first 'crafty' project I embarked on. And I did it today!

    Bought a lovely box with a glass top, decided to fill up the spaces with the flowers who took pride of place in my house during my first year in Melbourne, afterwhich I decided to dry them, and subsequently, didn't know WTH to do with them... until now! Pretty or not?
