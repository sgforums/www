    When I went home and watched the Korean drama, I almost turned blue laughing at the really terrible subtitles! It was so funny I replayed it again and again, for laughs! Then, a thought occured to me, why not share the good joke here with all of you?

    So, here it is, a small snippet of the entertainment value that can be derived from the English subtitles in Korean dramas! This is a verbatim copy of the subtitle in a scene when boy meets girl and tries to tell her that he went there specially to look for her :

    (B=Boy; G=Girl)

    B : Is what matter? What matter worry?

    G : Have no have no worry the matter!

    B : Please say that see, see I can help you.

    G : True nothing important matter. Thanks for your to you certainly.

    B : Not still too unimportant. True nothing important matter?

    G : Is. But have today when arrive at here? See the friend again if?

    B : Is not. Today is the true.

    G : I?

    B : Does today also want to work here?

    G : Is not because of the no the patient.

    B : The rice gross want to eat?

    G : Is. There is friend taking so eat on time. Work assuredness need strength very much.

    B : Have a meal can't the careful body!

    G : I am a doctor.

    B : Yes, do I am foolish kill before expert. Organism meeting we I to please.



    So... tell me again that there's no entertainment value in Korean dramas?!

    OK, thought you guys might need a proper translation of the above to understand what's really going on! So here's the actual conversation in Mandarin!

    (B=Boy; G=Girl)

    B : Is what matter? What matter worry?
    (Shen Me Shi Mah? Dan Xing Shen Me Shi Mah?)

    G : Have no have no worry the matter!
    (Mei She Me Shi)

    B : Please say that see, see I can help you.
    (Qing Jiang Chu Lai, Kan Kan Wo Ke Yi Bang Ni.)

    G : True nothing important matter. Thanks for your to you certainly.
    (Zhen De Mei She Me. Xie Xie Ni Guan Xing.)

    B : Not still too unimportant. True nothing important matter?
    (Mei She Mo Shi Bu Zong Yao De Mah? Zhen De Mei She Mo?)

    G : Is. But have today when arrive at here? See the friend again if?
    (Shi. Bu Guo... Jing Tian Ni Zhe Me Hui Lai Zhe Li? Kan Peng You Mah?)

    B : Is not. Today is the true.
    (Bu Shi. Jing Tian Shi Zhen De Lai Kan Ni De!)

    G : I?
    (Kan Wo?)

    B : Does today also want to work here?
    (Shi Ah! Ni Jing Tian Ye Yao Zuo Gong Mah?)

    G : Is not because of the no the patient.
    (Shi Ying Wei You Bing Ren, Suo Yi Yao Zuo Gong De.)

    B : The rice gross want to eat?
    (Fan Chi Le Mah?)

    G : Is. There is friend taking so eat on time. Work assuredness need strength very much.
    (Shi. You Peng You Bang Wo Dai Fan Lai, Suo Yi Chi Le. Zuo Ze Fen Gong Zuo Shu Yao Li De.)

    B : Have a meal can't the careful body!

    (Zhi Dao Le! Wo Shi Yi Shen!)

    B : Yes, do I am foolish kill before expert. Organism meeting we I to please.
