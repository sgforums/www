    The last time I went to the Queenstown Branch Library donkey months ago, I was impressed with the views of a huge field of green grass and the odd tree here and there dotting the landscape! The generous glass windows towards the back of the library offer this view and gives you a 'far from the madding crowd' kind of feeling... siiiiiiiiigh...

    When I went up to the 2nd floor, I saw a Reading Room with several ah peks taking their afternoon nap inside, enjoying the air-con, the comfy couches and the silence.

    I like the Orchard Library located two floors above Kinokuniya in Ngee Ann City. They even have pre-selected CD's in wall-mounted players for you to sample! They also have a pretty decent spread of cookbooks, craft books, exercise books, cat books, interior decor books, travel guides and other non-fiction books and magazines.

    I don't like to borrow fiction from the libraries because they're usually well-thumbed through and quite sorry looking. They also don't look very erm... clean. A few of their older titles are so yellowed that you feel an itch coming on just looking at them! Besides, I like to savour fiction and sometimes, end up with overdue fines. I prefer to rent fiction from those 2nd-hand bookshops but sometimes, I just have to buy them because those stores don't offer the titles I'm looking for.
