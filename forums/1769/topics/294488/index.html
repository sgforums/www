    you are NOT young anymore..

    go there.. remember.. being away in a foreign land is an education in itself, which is very very different from the "education" you are going there to obtain...

    go there with an open mind which is filtered... there are many new ideas, views and all there... go live like the locals, learn about the culture there... that's the most important bits which is different from what you will get here at home..

    expand your horizons and your views of the world, learn how they live, how they are.. and remember the lessons learnt when you get back, if you come back...

    and if you can and can afford to, go on trips to see the continent.. make friends, take hugeass road trips... do not forget to look up into the sky at night when you are out there with just wilderness around you... lie back and take in the universe... contemplate..

    in other words, do more than go there and study...

    you'll have the time of your life
