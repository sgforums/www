    The first impression I had of Club 30 (just by its name) was that it was a forum for people in their 30s. It is a very misleading name, I must say. After reading the "about club 30" topic, I am relieved its just a name. I find the topics in this forum more interesting than most. So I decided to start a topic and hope that it can help the forum in some way or another.

    I am a fresh graduate (mid 20s/M), who just started work 2 months back, in April. The transition from student life to being part of the working society has also translated to a change in lifestyle. How was it like for you when you first began to establish your career?

    For me, I could feel the weariness from working all day, Mon-Fri. I grew a desire to do something different during the weekends to break the monotony of work. I was never one to go out often during schooling days, when I was only focused on CCA and sport groups. Nowadays, I enjoy abit of pool, K session, clubbing, checking out new eating or drinking hotspots and other fun stuff. Things which I would not have want to do when I was only a "poor" student.
