    I start off first... I like the restrooms at Great World City. They are very unique and all their restrooms have different themes!

    The ground floor restroom has an African tribal theme. Then there's the Japanese theme, the Dutch theme, the Jungle theme, etc.

    Once you enter, you'll notice the walls of the restrooms are beautifully painted! For instance, in the Dutch themed one, you'll see windmills and field of red poppies painted on the walls. It flows onto the doors of the cubicles too! Turn 360 degrees when you stand in the middle and it's all windmills and poppies! Niiice!
