    Eh... I disagree!

    I ate a great artery-clogger of a cheeseburger at a grease joint in US. It was gooooood but warning - not for the faint hearted cuz you might die of Ischaemic Heart Disease there! hahaha!!

    The joint is exactly like those in the movies where you sit around the counter and watch your breakfast order being cooked on the flat iron slab in front of you. The cooks just slap rashers of bacon to sizzle there, someone wants an egg?... they just crack an egg onto the hot surface, ... most things are cooked on the large griddle. It's fun to watch but oh the amount of grease!!

    Those burgers are superb!!

    And oh, let's not forget the humble Ramly Burger, imported from across the causeway! It's soooooo goooooooood!!! Sometimes, when there's Pasar Malam here, you can find a Ramly Burger stall run by Malays / Indians. If you haven't been initiated into the Ramly fan club, try it! It costs around $2.50.

    You get a Ramly Burger Patty, chicken / beef, sliced in half, paprika and a little pepper and Maggi seasoning sauce put inside, then the burger is grilled to perfection.

    Next, the bun is spread with margarine / butter. Beaten eggs would be poured onto the griddle in a thin layer to cook. Then, divided into squares with the burger patty placed right in the center and the egg is folded to 'envelope' the patty in a tight embrace after spreading generous dollops of mayo and BBQ sauce all over the patty inside.

    Place the egg-wrapped patty on a burger bun, spread chilli sauce, top it with sliced cabbage / lettuce and carrots, and voila! You've got your Ramly Burger!!!

    Sorry for not being 'patriotic'... but we sure don't own great burgers!
