    From I Really Want That!...

    Glow Brick!

    What's better than a night light that helps you find your way to the bathroom without tripping over the cat? A night light that does not need a power source!

    The Glow Brick has a bulb set in a clear solid brick, which recharges from energy in natural light during the day, and glows at night! Since there is no need to stick it in a wall socket, which tend to be placed rather out of the way (and rightly so - since tangles of wires are such an eyesore), you can place your nightlight anywhere you wish!

    Chalk Mug

    A really simple but ingenious product. Acting like a drinkable post-it, the cup surface is actually glazed with a chalk board surface to allow for easy-wipe messaging.

    All you need is a piece of chalk (which it comes with) and you can start right away. You can draw a different design each day - it's like having a new cup all the time.

    Use it to write sweet nothings (or apologies) to your loved one and pleasantly surprise them in the morning with their breakfast coffee... Write down reminders for appointments or to-do stuff... Spread office gossip without paper or email trails... Draw a smiley face and give it a name... Write down the recipe for your current concoction and spread some office cheer... Draw a daily comic strip... The possibilities are endless!

    Then when you've finally run out of ideas, just pour yourself a well-deserved drink and start over.
