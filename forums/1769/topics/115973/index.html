    Anything really nice happen to you recently that just brightened up your day??

    I had a very nice and unexpectedly pleasurably experience yesterday and I'm still enjoying it in my mind today.....

    I went to get a hair cut and the salon was really packed. The shampoo girl who usually washes my hair was busy with someone else and if I wanted her, I'd have to wait for 20mins so I just took the next available person cos i was in a hurry. ..

    It was a shampoo guy...which made me kinda apprehensive because I've never had my hair washed by a guy before....I'm very partcular about who washes my hair and usually guys just dont have the skills....but this guy....oh my....

    He had very big strong hands, was very very gentle with my scalp, scratched my head in all the right spots- never to hard or too soft, exerted the right amount of pressure on all the acupoints, gave a really gooooood neck massage and knew immediately to condition my ends only....
    he rinsed with cool water, just how I like it ... and after he wrapped the towel around my head helped my out of the chair by gently lifting my shoulders off the seat...he reached the salon chair first and turned it around for me, so gentlemanly.... and when I sat, my green tea had been refilled and he served it to me.....as I drank he towel dried my hair, combed it through, said "you have hair like a babies, its so fine"....then he put one hand on my shoulder , gently squeezed it and said with a smile "your stylst will be right with you...."

    and that was it...It was a heavenly 20mins....sigh.....
