    Whilst there will be a handful who chose nursing because they truly want to save lives, you can't rule out those who do so for more material reasons. Besides, with the bad nursing shortage that exists worldwide these days, I don't think we have the luxury of weeding out people who want to enter the profession for 'less noble' reasons.
