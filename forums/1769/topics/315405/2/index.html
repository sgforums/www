    The 2nd nightmare : I dreamt that a few friends and I were at this community fair and somehow, I ended up in the fire engine contingent!  We had to manoeuvre the huge fire truck out of a crammed parking lot and I think we escaped a few narrow scrapes where head-on collisions almost occured!!    Then, we had to sit on a metal platform as it is raised up to more than 20 storeys high and climb down the ladder!!!!    Our legs all went soft and we were all shivering in fright at the top cause the winds were strong and we felt very vulnerable and exposed up there with just skimpy steel rails around the platform!  The wind blasted at us and we could feel the platform shaking and all and several times, we thought we'd crash to the ground and die! 

    Finally, I rallied my mates and myself to start the descent because we couldn't stay up there forever, in case the strong winds blew our platform off!!    So with very shaky legs, we started the long climb down the ladder!!  EEEeeeeeeeee... everything was so shaky!!!  For some strange reason, instead of climbing down facing the rungs of the ladder, I climbed down facing outwards!!!    I was soooo scared of falling all the way down, I woke up! 

     

    Ladder

    But if you are climbing down a ladder, suggests that you are escaping from your spiritual responsibilities. It is an indication of much disappointments.

    Fire Engine
    To see a fire engine in your dream, signifies worries under tense and extraordinary situations.

    Fire Engine / Fire Truck
    To see a fire engine in your dream, suggests that you are tending to the needs of others and overlooking your own needs. You also worry and stress out in situations that are sometimes beyond your control. Stop trying to be in the middle of everying and stop trying to fix things. Use more discretion.

    Height
    To dream that you are at a great height, signifies that you have reached one of your highest goals or objectives.

    To dream that you are afraid of heights, denotes that you are striving for goals that seem beyond your reach.

    Crossed the road, sprinkled it on the other side of the road.  Started to wonder how the powder worked, read the instructions, realised that the powder will transform into snails when it comes into contact with water / air!    Somehow, the snails fight fire!! 

    I watched the powder transform into snails in a longkang.  Also saw a goldfish, two catfishes, and a stingray in the longkang. 

    Went into the aquarium.  Tried to look for a nice-sized tank for myself but couldn't.  The fishes, even the 'normal' ones, were expensive.  I saw a 'Tarantula Fish' that was bright green + turquoise + orange etc!!  It was a brightly coloured exotic tarantula that lived in water!  Somehow, the owner got me a net and told me to fish one out, and I struggled to keep it in the net because it kept trying to crawl out!!    I screamed and yelled until the owner finally took over the net!  Eeeeee-yerh!!!

    Ended up not buying anything.  Went into an old old building where they were training student nurses.  Went into a 'mock ICU' which turned out to be a REAL ICU!  Saw some funny boxy equipment, latest technology or something, that was a high-tech mechanical ventilator.  Ooh'd and aah'd over it.  Looked over to examine the patient being kept alive by it. 

    Then, entered a classroom of nursing students.  All tried to learn something from me but then their tutor entered the room, and they left to dress up for a Chingay Procession, gathering outside a Temple beside the old building!
