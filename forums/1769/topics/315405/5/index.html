    Bizarre!  I dreamt of something resembling Noah's Ark! 

    I dreamt that I was going on a voyage on board this huge cruise vessel where the passenger cabin was on the 9th floor.  It was a short, tall vessel.  There were only about 30 passengers and I was trying to herd the pets, which seem to be all cats, up the ship.  I think there were about 8-10 cats in all and only one other person was helping me.

    So we finally herded the cats on board, and the humans started to climb up the steep stairs.

    We sat down on the plush chairs and cats started to come up to me, one at a time, to be stroked and petted.  Someone commented that the cats recognised me as a cat lover, that's why they come up to me so freely.

    And then, I had an odd golden cat come up to me.  It didn't seem like a cat at all the more I looked at it.  And then it struck me - it was a Golden Monkey! 

    Suddenly, we had to split into pairs to brainstorm.  I was assigned to a guy with a Korean-sounding name.  I couldn't find him, and then I was told that he had paired himself up with his buddy instead, so I had to work alone, which I was secretly pleased about, because I work well alone. 

    And then I woke up because there was a small wet patch on my bed...
