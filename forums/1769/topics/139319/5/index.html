    back from dinner at amk.

    verdict - very good

    had the following:-
    * crab bee hoon - yummilicious! my fav. the crab's huge sweet n fresh and the soup's totally sinfully savoury
    * black pepper crab - quite spicy but very very nice... meat is sweet n juicy.
    * butter crab - sinful but oh so good... again, huge huge huge crab!!!

    we had other zi cha stuff too (11 of us) - not bad but rather typical zi cha - nothing to write home about. i tot the fried mee sua and herbal chicken was not bad. the cereal prawn, pork ribs, claypot tofu and hei zo were rather mediocre.

    oh well, here's my report. tho i think you guys prob wun go there, since it's definitely not halal bcos it's a chinese kopitiam...

    soft shell crabs. hmmm... the famous claypot rice place at geylang serves pretty good ones... nice nice claypot rice too! but again, it's chinese non-halal food...

    hmmm...
