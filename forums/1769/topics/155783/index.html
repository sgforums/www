Heartbeats

  • ShrodingersCat

    Have you listened to the heartbeats of the people you love?

    I listened to my mom and my dad's heartbeats
    through a stethescope...for the first time,
    when I listen to their heartbeats
    and compared it to my own...
    and it is scary because I could actually hear the difference
    theirs were more muffled...
    I could actually hear their hearts trying to work harder
    beating... weaker... softer...
    and for the first time,
    the reality of their mortality strikes me in the face full force

    I kept thinking about this.. even though this happened months ago
    now that I have moved out and starting a new home of my own
    and suddenly the rhythm of the heartbeat will just get into my mind
    and I will be reminded to give my mom a call...
    or take them out for the weekend..
    cos.. u never know.... when those heartbeats will stop.

    Hmm. Just a reminder to pick up the phone and drop a line if you have been busy for too long.
