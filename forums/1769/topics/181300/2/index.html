    As promised, here's the receipe:

    Kueh Serimuka aka Kueh Salat

    Ingredients for custard:
    4 eggs
    180 g sugar
    80 g plain flour
    30 g tapioca flour
    1-1/2 cups thick coconut milk
    1/4 cup pandan juice (I cheated, I use pandan essence)
    1/2 tsp vanilla essence

    Ingredients for rice
    1-1/2 cups coconut milk
    480 g glutinous rice
    1-1/4 tsp salt (I'm not salt incline so I go a bit light on it but enough to make it tasty and lemak)

    Method for custard
    Stir eggs and sugar together in a bowl. Do not beat
    Sift in plain and tapioca flours, mix well
    Gradually pour in coconut milk, pandan juice and vanilla essence
    Strain to remove lumps
    Leave topping aside

    Method for rice
    Pour in coconut milk to cover the wash rice.
    Stir in salt
    Steam for 20-25 minutes.
    When cooked, press down firmly with the back of a spoon.
    Pour in prepared topping and steam for a further 20 minutes over medium heat. (while receipe indicated medium heat....it has always resulted in the custard ballooning because of the eggs. So I have improvized and use between medium and low heat.

    Have fun and enjoy!
