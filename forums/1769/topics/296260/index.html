    Just returned from UK, stayed a few days in London, and visited Wales. It's really just a drive-thru of Wales because the place is so big, and being winter, many of the attractions weren't open. Although the weather was mostly cold, dark, wet, misty and cloudy (yes, it occasionally got a little depressing), the scenery was still breathtakingly beautiful.

    You can't think of the country without its medieval and ancient connections - and those who grew up with tales of King Arthur and Shakespeare would find it hard not to feel transported back in time. Road signs in Wales are in both English and Welsh... but.. place names get really confusing when you can't FIND any familiar vowels!!!!
