    When contacted by The Sunday Times last night, Miss Low said that she had recorded a telephone conversation with Miss Lee in which the latter had admitted faking her injury.

    When she played back the recording, a woman - who she said was Miss Lee - could be clearly heard saying she hated the pageant organiser for what they have done to Miss Low.

    The woman did mention having a back injury, but nowhere in the conversation did she admit to faking it.

    Miss Low said she will be passing the audio recording to Ms Tracy Lee, ERM's event director, today so that the pageant organiser will know that Miss Lee 'has been badmouthing them'.

    Miss Low said she was doing all this to get back at Miss Lee, 23, for exposing her credit card crime to the media.

    'It's an eye for an eye. I know it was her because eyes can see, ears can hear and heart can feel,' she said.

    She claimed that she told Miss Lee about her being under probation some time during the pageant. 'She (Miss Lee) was feeling down at the time. I told her that I'm under probation to show her that her life is not that bad,' she said. 'I trusted her, but she stabbed me in the back.'

    She said she has been suffering from a back injury since 2007 due to prolonged hours of studying. It got worse last Monday when she fell down while chasing her dog down the stairs. She had gone to see a doctor about it, she added.

    She found out that Miss Low had turned on her yesterday morning after a call from a Shin Min reporter.

    She said: 'I feel so hurt and disappointed. I was the only one who stood by her and defended her when people criticised her. I've done nothing to hurt her.'

    She suspected that Miss Low is 'making up stories' either because of her bipolar disorder or to make herself 'look less bad' in the face of the controversy surrounding her in the past two weeks.

    As for Miss Low's probation, she claimed she was never told about it until the media broke the news.

    When asked to respond to what Miss Lee said, Miss Low said: 'Why do I need to make myself look less bad? I look bad enough already. It cannot get any worse than this.

    'I just want people to know that Claire Lee is not as pure as she seems. She knows she has no chance to be chosen (as the next Miss Singapore World). She is on such bad terms with ERM. That is why she faked her back injury.'

    Miss Lee, however, said she is no longer vying for the crown.

    'I don't really want it that badly now, especially after all the lies she (Miss Low) has spread about me. If I'm chosen to represent Singapore, I will do my best. But if I'm not chosen, I won't be angry.'
