    In football you can use the same balls and boots as the top players if you have the money but you have to start somewhere small with friends, don't you? You can't have the big stadiums and training facilities, and make do with traffic cones for goals.

    The same goes with motor racing, same thrills can be had in small, cheaper go-karts that can tire you out and test your racecraft and car control skills like the big machines.
