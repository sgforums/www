    Hi SL, the food is a mix of Japanese (sushi, sashimi, tempura, tepanyaki), western (oysters, cold prawns, scallops, starters, etc.), Chinese paper steamboat (dinner only), Chinese, western & local buffet (satay, otah, etc.) dishes, desserts and ice-cream. Quality-wise, the dishes range from average to good, but it's cheap and there's a good variety to eat. Dinner is $26 net.

    Enjoy yourselves.
