    That's toaster oven... a smaller version of a conventional oven.  Usually you can use that toaster oven for roasting potatoes, grill cheese sandwich, cook some small piece of meat etc....

    Conventional oven cooks the food using infrared heat.  Microwave oven cooks the food using microwave.

    Food cooks faster in microwave oven than in conventional oven but tastes better in conventional oven.
