    I view grocery shopping as one of the necessary minor inconveniences of modern living.

    Fortunately, there are two huge (and I mean HUGE!) grocery chain stores literally within a few minutes' drive of my house, and it's generally not a problem to drive to one of them at a moment's notice to get something that you've just discovered you're out of. Both of these stores have self-service checkout registers at which you scan, bag and pay for the items yourself, so you don't have to stand in line at a regular checkout line waiting for the granny in front of you to fish out pennies from her handbag to pay for her groceries. Plus, the self-service registers usually don't have waiting lines.

    Then there's the mountain of coupons in the Sunday papers that can save you quite a bit on your grocery bills!
