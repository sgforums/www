    I'm just wondering how many of you out there are fond of grocery shopping?

    It doesn't matter if you shop at the supermarts or at the wet markets, as long as you're out hunting for the freshest produce, the freshest yields from the sea, the choicest cut of meat, the chicken that's the most plump and the fruits that are at their seasonal best, you're into grocery shopping!

    I ask this because I realised that the time (and money) I spend in Cole's, a large chain of supermart here, as well as the fervour with which I attend the Richmond Market every Saturday, makes me an avid grocery shopper!

    Not to mention, I get so happy when my friend invites me on a car ride to either the Queen Victoria Market or Boxhill!

    So, this thread is dedicated to those of us who are perpetually in search of stuff for the kitchen and larder / pantry. I guess that includes those of us who are forever searching for new snacks, tit-bits and cookies to squirrel into our workplace cubicle drawers or lockers as well!

    Members of the Grocery Shoppers Guild, make yourselves known!
