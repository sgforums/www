    I mean, why would it have to be an acquired taste if it's really that good?  Nobody, not even babies and animals, have any difficulties getting 'addicted' to sugar and honey.  It's 'easy' to like them, you don't have to 'learn' how to like them, don't have to tolerate liking them.

    I think it's just an industry where the Emperor's New Clothes theory comes into play - a long, long time ago, someone (vineyard owner, maybe) decided, out of self-interest, to put out the word that if you're classy, learned, rich, whatever, you'll love wine, and so everyone else, in a bid to be associated with the classy, rich and learned pack, decided to fake it and like wine! 

    I mean, alcohol... if you really love alcohol, you'd also just as quickly love Dettol or one of those hospital-grade cleaning detergents because they reek of alcohol too! 

    Never underestimate the power of status symbols.
