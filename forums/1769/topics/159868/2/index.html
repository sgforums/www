    Its a little like friendship and... being housemates.

    Loving someone and being able to LIVE with the person can be two completely different stories.

    Great friends have turned enemies from being housemates, simply cos we have spent most of our entire lives building up habits that will be very difficult to break. And it doesn't have to be a BAD habit, just a lifestyle habit.

    More than habits are our value system put entirely to task when you gave to build a life with someone else.

    It is easy to say love conquers all, but can your love encompass freaky habits ranging from the inconvenient to the unhygienic to the completely insane? (I once read a true story of a wife whose husband refused to let her open a bedside drawer through their years of marriage. Finally she opened it and found it filled with... his own nail cuttings)

    Most of us think we love a completely normal person only to discover that even the most normal of us possess idiosyncracies to balance up. Some of us even love people we think are slightly eccentric but prove to be... more than slightly.

    Oh well, you've got to live with them to find out!
