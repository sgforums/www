    "When the Emperor was Divine" by Julie Otsuka

    I finished the book about an hour on my way to the chiropractor this morning. It is an easy read; simple, clean, to the point sentances, which deliver the emotions with an oopmh

    It basically goes through the feelings, emotions, thoughts which the people on the other side of the fence will feel on the enemy grounds, though I think they are being treated a hell lot better.

    I find that the whole book is almost like a circle.... you give up your stuff, you move and dream about your old life and try not to think about the future....you finally get the chance to go back to your old life but you realise that it will never be the same and you carry on dreaming and trying to find your way to move forward and thinking of the 'new' life you just left behind.

    Ok ok I know I sound confusing but I really don't want to let on too much or it will spoil the fun of reading the book.

    It's an interesting book and easy to read too so who wants me to pass the book to them
