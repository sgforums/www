    I like Indomie Mi Goreng Original Flavour. Also Maggi Curry Flavour Instant Noodles and just like the way Qitai likes it, dropping an egg into it makes it much nicer. I did find that the curry flavour noodles tasted much spicier before, now it's too salty and not spicy enough.

    I like the Korean noodles from the Nam Shing brand. (Did I get that name right?) I like the chicken flavour ramen and the spicy mushroom flavour. The seafood flavour isn't bad too although I prefer to eat that with a touch of dark premium soy sauce and chilli sauce. I like to cook the ramen then eat it with just a little liquid so the flavouring's stronger.

    Also, a word of advice, I heard from someone years back that you shouldn't put the seasonings into the pot when you're cooking the noodles because raised to a certain temperature, it might release carcinogenic substances. Rather, cook the noodles in water, pour the seasonings into your bowl, then use the liquid you cooked the noodles in to mix the seasoning and then place the noodles inside the bowl and mix. I don't know if the point has been substantiated or not but better to be safe than sorry!
