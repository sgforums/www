    CSI: New York, the third incarnation of the CSI: Crime Scene Investigation franchise and the spin-off of CSI: Miami, is a crime drama about forensic investigators who use high-tech science to follow the evidence and solve crimes in The Big Apple. Detective Mack "Mac" Taylor is a dedicated and driven crime scene investigator who believes that everything is connected and for everyone there is a story. He and his partner, Detective Stella Bonasera, a workaholic and a jack-of-all-trades, share a passion for the job. They lead a team of experts amid the gritty and kinetic city that never sleeps. Their team includes Danny Messer, a Brooklyn-born investigator with rugged good looks, an unflappable spirit and a colorful family history, and Dr. Sheldon Hawkes, a reclusive coroner who walked away from a promising surgical career after the traumatic loss of two patients. Joining them is homicide Detective Don Flack, an edgy, hardcore investigator with a quick wit, impressive forensic insight and limited patience with potential suspects. Rounding out the team is Aiden Burn, a smart and sexy investigator whose chameleon-like behavior allows her to adapt to any situation at any time. These skilled investigators, who see New York City in a whole different light, follow the evidence as they piece together clues and eliminate doubt to ultimately crack their cases.

    Characters:

    Gil Grissom (William Petersen):
    Grissom is the head of the night shift of the crime lab in the Las Vegas police department. Grissom is an outsider of sorts and is married to his work.
    Specialty: Entomology

    Catherine Willows (Marg Helgenberger):
    Catherine is a single mom trying to raise a daughter while struggling with long hours at the crime lab. A former exotic dancer, Catherine has really worked her way up in the world, newly promoted to head of the swing shift of the crime lab.
    Specialty: Blood-spatter analysis

    Nick Stokes (George Eads):
    Nick has a competetive spirit and wants to be the best he can (as in the first season when he and Warrick competed for a promotion).
    Specialty: Hair and fiber analysis

    Warrick Brown (Gary Dourdan):
    Warrick is a recovering gambling addict and has come a long way since the first season, now a very accomplished CSI.
    Specialty: Audio/visual analysis

    Sara Sidle (Jorja Fox):
    Sara is a very sensitive woman who tends to let cases get the best of her. Especially domestic violence cases due to a dark family past.
    Specialty: Materials and element analysis

    Jim Brass (Paul Guifoyle}:
    Captain of the homicide division of the LVPD, Jim Brass is a tough-as-nails detective who loves getting the criminals and serving them justice.

    Greg Sanders (Eric Szmanda):
    Greg finally passed his proficiency test and is now a regular CSI in the field and out of the lab. Though his deductive CSI skills need polishing and experience, with Grissom as his coach he will be as good as the rest.

    Al Robbins (Robert David Hall):
    The coroner in the CSI lab, Dr. Robbins always helps the gang break cases and solve mysteries.
