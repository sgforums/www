    2. Modernisation of times. As the cultural ideas and perception starts to change so does the way people do things. Women nowadays are very much career driven as their male counterparts and alot are opting to have children later. Alot later than their mothers. But unfortunately due to the biological aspect being unchanged it produced problems. As women gets older so does their chances of miscarriage or infertility. So therefore it lessens the chances of producing a healthier baby able to survive.

    3. Conditions of enviroment. Mortality rates are also a factor. In countries where death is common and the norm, families have more children for the sake of survival. The more children the more possibility of survival. Number playing.
