    Originally posted by shinta:
    but i had a lot of tea on last fri, n sat, and monday too... den when i drank tea this morning.... gastric pain attacked
    Sometimes, it may not directly be the tea, but could be how you have been eating. E.g. sometimes I eat too late, so become too hungry (and stomach becomes gasy), followed by eating too much (coz so famished), so the irregular timing and irregular amount of food intake can give me gastric pain. The kind of food to restrict also vary from person to person, as certain kinds of food affect some more than others. If the gastric is really bad, can take an antacid, e.g. Actal which works quite fast to relieve discomfort.
