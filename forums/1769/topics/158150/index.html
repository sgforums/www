    IMO Ikea furniture is not that sturdy or lasting.

    I purchased a bookshelf and sadly, its shelves warped when loaded with books. I could have overloaded them, past the permissible load weight, but then a cupboard's not much use if it can't bear a full row of books.

    As for the "made of solid wood" claim, only some products are made of wood, others are made from some blend of "wood" like wooden boards hehehe. The facade looks fine, but don't look behind the furniture!

    And then there is the dreaded assembly I purchased a bathroom cabinet, had the renovation workers to fix it up.. and it took 4 men to NOT assemble it They ended up with unused parts and told me we were short of some other parts. I junked the thing and bought a steel rack from Sim Siang Choon.

    I do like the home accessories and knick knacks though, they're fun and well designed. e.g. photo frames, cushions and covers, rugs. Some stuff are innovative and hard to find outside too, like an L shaped shower rod.
