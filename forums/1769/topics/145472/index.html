Jealous because the other race is perceived to be much more successful in your own country and they are amongst the rich, while your own people is struggling to make a living. Instead they grow bitter and jealous and start hating someone else for their own misery. I guess it is easier to blame someone, your fate than trying to do something about it.

    I think, the key to racial harmony and peace here is acceptance and understanding. You can never get the other party to respect you if you try to force your culture and ideals onto them. Then again, sometimes it is just plain hate, because you look different.
