    Date: 1 - 2 July 2006
    Time: from 5.00pm (1 July), 11.00am – 12.00mn (2 July)
    Venue: Civic Plaza @ Ngee Ann City , Orchard Road

    Crab lovers, prepare your pincers for cracking into the largest ever crab feast! Kick-starting the Singapore Food Festival is the Singapore Tiger Chilli Crab Festival - a gastronomic event that features a huge variety of crab dishes and other local favourite crustacean dishes.

    Besides the mouth-watering Chilli Crab, salivate over the sight of an immense range of crab dishes - grilled, baked, deep-fried, sautÃ©ed, stir fried, with rice, noodles or bread. Other favourite crab variations include Crab Cake, Crab Meat Salad, Soft Shell Crab Roll and more, available from more than thirty booths.

    Some of Singaporean's favourite Chilli Crab dishes from restaurants such as Jumbo and Palm Beach will be available at the event. Be sure to try out Breadtalk's “CraBlog” series – a series of buns specially created for the Singapore Food Festival, which features brand-new bread flavours like Chilli Crab, Black Pepper Crab and Curry Crab.

    While you decide which crab dish steals your heart, be tempted by succulent prawns, lobsters, clams and oysters, and don't miss out on Singapore's “Top Ten Must Try” dishes!

    If you are fanatic about eating crabs, try your hand at becoming the Fastest Claw in the East! Winners will enjoy further crab indulgence with dining vouchers from Singapore's best Chilli Crab and crab dishes restaurants.

    Get crabby with fun activities and games like the Crab Dance and three-on-three Crab Football. What's more, crab lovers cum soccer enthusiasts can eat and applaud to their hearts' content at a beer garden within the Civic Plaza. With so much glorious food and happening entertainment, it's a finger-licking-good event, not to be missed!
