    scrambled eggs with straw mushrooms and cajun bacon

    get 2 eggs, beat well with half tsp of salt and 1 tbsp of milk.
    blanch the straw mushrooms in boiling water.
    heat saucepan on low, melt 1 tbsp of butter.
    pour in the eggs, stir eggs around while cooking.
    throw in the mushrooms, continue stirring until eggs are semi-solid, then remove from heat.

    For the bacon, i used streaky bacon and rubbed cajun spices (can be bought in most supermarkets) thoroughly into the meat before frying. The spices really bring out a wonderful smell.

    And i also added sliced cheddar cheese (cut from a block, not kraft singles) on top of the eggs and let it melt...damn nice.

    And yes. I just cooked this for my girlfriend!!!
