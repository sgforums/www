    caption : Our first meal in Tibet - Hui Ethnics Ramien

    Our first meal was this bowl of ramien from a shop recommended by the Lonely Planet.

    caption : The famous or infamous yak butter tea

    This cup of tea is no cheapo cup of tea. They are sold mostly to the tourist in the eating house. This used to the the everyday drinks of the Tibetan but today, milk tea are gradually replacing this drink. The difference between the yak butter tea and the milk tea is that it is salty and have a strong buttery taste. You will have to drink it hot otherwise the buttery taste will make it unpalatable.
