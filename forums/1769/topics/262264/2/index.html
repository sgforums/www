    Dunno leh, they give us a choice - green tea or black tea. The black tea is almost always lipton!

    I think they are generally very curious in most towns because u hardly see any women! Our guide says match made marriages are common and there is still a big communication gap between men and women because there is so little interaction before marriage. (Except with aunties or older women) In the towns and main roads of villages, we saw only men mingling in the streets. Only in the backlanes of the villages will we see women.

    In one or two places I visited, tourists are not very welcome or viewed with more suspicion. This guy pointed his middle finger at me!

    But mostly we were treated with warmth and respect. There really isn't many tourists still especially after Afghan war and all. Lots of Afghan refugees living in pockets here and there. i see for my whole trip less than 10 other tourists besides us.

    I had only 1 time stomach upset, but I think it was because I was too cold, wind too strong.
