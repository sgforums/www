    What really gets me in the Christmas mood is reading US mags like 'Bon Appetit', 'Ladies' Home Journal', 'Good Housekeeping', 'O' (Oprah's mag) and Martha Stewart's 'Living'.

    You see how the Americans celebrate Christmas on a grand scale! You flip through the pages and see lotsa lotsa photos of lovely Christmas trees, chimney decorations, party fancies, etc!

    And then, you get pages upon pages of recipes for Christmas goodies! Soooo fun!!
