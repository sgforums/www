    1. Ben Elton's Postmortem

    A nice humourous English whodunit starring an unlilkely detective inspector and his partner, a female detective sergeant he is lusting after but she's stuck in a relationship with an abusive boyfriend.

    2. Tom Hodgkinson's How To Be Idle

    Title is self explanatory.
