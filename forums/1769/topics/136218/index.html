Is it the Flu or the Common Cold?

  • Rhonda

    Here's some information that might answer that common question and hopefully, clear the confusion!

    How to diagnose Influenza (Flu)

    If you have a fever (usually 39-41 deg C) and 2-3 of the symptoms listed below, you have the flu!

    Common Symptoms : Fever, Headache, Sore Throat, Dry Cough, Lethargy, Myalgia (Muscle Pain)

    Less Common Symptoms : Sneezing, Painful and Watery Eyes, Blocked / Runny Nose


    Flu vs the Common Cold

    Flu
    Onset of symptoms : Sudden
    Fever : High, 38-41 deg C
    Muscle Aches : Severe
    Malaise or Fatigue : Severe
    Sore throat and cough : Early onset
    Chest symptoms : Substernal burning common
    Sneezing : Occasional
    Duration : 7-10 days

    Common Cold
    Onset of Symptoms : Gradual
    Fever : None or mild (less than 38 deg C)
    Muscle Aches : Usually mild
    Malaise / Fatigue : Usually Mild
    Sore Throat and Cough : Mild course, peaking on Day 2-3
    Chest Symptoms : Mild, if any
    Sneezing : Common
    Duration : 3-4 Days


    Hope this information helps!
