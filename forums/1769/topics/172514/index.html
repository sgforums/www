    1. Who has more connections linking their brain cells?
    a. An 8-mth old baby

    a. 100,000
    b. three million
    c. one billion
    d. one thousand trillion

    a. true
    b. false
    c. undetermined

    b. men

    Answers to the Brain Tease!

    1.
    (a) A baby's brain contains about twice as many connections linking brain cells as an adult brain. By the age of 12, that number has been pared down by about 50%, which is all any of us needs.

    2.
    (d) A thousand trillion!!
    About half of these get sculpted away because the infant's experience doesn't require them to be used. In their place, other connective pathways grow stronger and more complex.

    3.
    (a) Tests on sleeping subjects demonstrate that the brain actually differentiates between ''pure'' sounds such as bells, and language.

    4.
    (a) The area of the brain that processes language is larger than women.

    (Therefore, it's not that men cannot nag... they are ill-equipped to nag!! )

    5.
    (a) The brain wins!
