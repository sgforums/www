    I think I ended up with this many pairs of shoes because when I don't feel comfortable in a pair, I stop wearing it and get another pair. 

    For my summer slippers and sandals, it's because I bought them in dirt-cheap sales. 

    Now, I justify all this by saying that for years, when in Singapore, I could never easily find shoes for my feet because I've got large feet.    I could only wear 'auntie' shoes because when I enter a shoe shop, I can't choose the designs but rather, have to ask, "Which designs come in my size?" which would inevitably be awful designs. 

    So... now that I'm here, I can finally get all the lovely shoes I want!! 

    I will not wear high heels though - I've seen mom's bunions, and also met patients who had to undergo surgery to remove their bunions or correct foot problems due to years of wearing high heels, so no thanks!  Besides, I can't walk as much as I do on heels, not comfortably, of course.  I'd rather walk comfortably and have good healthy feet than balance on stilts just so I can turn the horny men on.

    Besides, when you have to run from drug addicts or what-not, you'll run much faster in sneakers than on heels.  Plus, you can hide somewhere quietly too!
