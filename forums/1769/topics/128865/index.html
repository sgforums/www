    The times served us well
    Fed our youth
    Cultivated out angst
    We took the step together
    Into the fearsome unknown

    We grew in different ways
    Somehow time did not intersect us in the same ways
    More apart we grew
    Until we passed each other by in the streets
    It took a second peer
    to realise
    That the person standing right in front
    had shared your youth with you
    But you have chosen not to acknowledge that
    and let her drop out of your life.
