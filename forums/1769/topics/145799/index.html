    back to the masking tape...

    it's friction that causes the blisters...

    what i do with my boots, is that i wear them for a few minutes, and figure out which parts rub.. then stick masking tape over those parts of my foot.. then socks..

    the rubbing will be between the socks and the masking tape.. no blisters...

    OR...

    if you know which part of the shoe is causing the rubbing, use candle wax over that part.. or any other kind of wax that will stick and smooth the rough part away...

    biting isn't going to help
