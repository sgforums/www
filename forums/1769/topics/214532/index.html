    Do yourself and your company a favor when going for the exit interview.

    1. Be objective. Don't target the person but target the action. E.g. Don't say that your manager is irresponsible. Tell them the actions that lead you to think that there's irresponsiblity. And state very clearly that you're targeting the action and not the person.

    2. The fact that the company is having an exit interview means that the company wants to be better in their HR aspect. If you think that they won't act on your words, then think about why the hell are they having an exit interview in the first place.
