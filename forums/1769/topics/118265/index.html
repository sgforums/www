    I'm always amazed at the things some girls do in the name of beauty.

    Make-up is supposed to enhance one's features and to hide flaws, but I think some girls overdo it and instead of beautifying themselves, they do a good job de-beautifying themselves

    I wonder all the time if these females actually look into the mirror before stepping out of the house. Don't they see that the foundation they use is wayyyyy too light and wayyyyy too thick that it makes them look like they are wearing a mask? Can't they see that they use too much eyeshadow and blusher that they look like one of those painted face in the Chinese Opera Troupe.

    I had a nasty shock once late at night near my place. There was this girl whose foundation was applied so thickly that her entire face was soooo white it made her look like a ghost. The amount of blusher and blue eye shadow she applied was so heavy and unblended that it looked totally horrible. She was with her bf.......I really wonder if the bf made any comments or he didn't dare to make any comments

    It is also common sight in the MRT of females wearing too much blusher (most common) or too much eye shadow or eye-liner that has smudged till they look like a panda. Honestly~ in our humid weather is it really necessary to apply so much make-up?

    I have nothing against heavy make-up as long as it is well-applied. Have seen a few examples before and they look great!
