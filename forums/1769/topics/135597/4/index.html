    Aiyah...

    Apple shape means that your waist measurement is larger than your hip measurement. In other words, you've got a lot of abdominal fat.

    Pear shape is kinda like a larger version of the hourglass except it's more bottom-heavy than the perfect hourglass.

    So, for instance,

    Hourglass = 36-30-37
    Apple = 36-38-37
    Pear = 36-30-40

    Like that.
