    Hmmm...Improving guitar skills eh?

    First
    It's important to learn how to read notes...Pick up a couple of scales...The C major scale and a natural minor scale should be nice...
    Learn to be fast and consistant with ur right-hand picking
    Dont forget abt ur lefty too...Most guitarist tend to make a common mistake...They dont use their left pinkies!. This is a very IMPORTANT point..NEVER neglect ur pinkies...
    After learning scaling, u're off to creating ur on solos and stuff...
    There's no fixed pattern on how a solo or riff is created...Mostly by note or chord progression i might say...

    Next, if u're talking abt about developing techniques and such...
    I would say that practicing daily would help alot...
    For me...I do arpeggios/econ picking and alt picking for abt 2 hrs a day
    Followed up by down picking for 30 mins...It helps u build ur muscles for both hands
    Techniques cant be acquired within a day's work...U got to be consistant with ur learning...That's it...

    P.s it's nice that u picked up a guitar...

    Tim^
