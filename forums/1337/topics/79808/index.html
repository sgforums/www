Tao Dulsori (South Korea)

  • DeadPoet

    The panorama of drums resonates with forces as the Dulsori ensemble strikes with percussive gusto. They ignite Korean traditional rhythm with modern pulse, in a potent display of strength, grace and form that connects the masses to the breathtaking rhythm of life.

    The dynamic group will prove that they are more than just musicians. They want to be close to their audience and energise them with special Samulnori performance. In the grand finale, the crowds will find themselves drawn into flag-fighting games, wishing that the evening fun would not end as fireworks light up the sky.

    18 - 20June, 8pm, open field at Yishun Central 1 (opp. Bus Interchange), FOC, abt. 50 minutes.
