    VECTOR PRIME FROM STOMPIN' GROUND

    Stompin' Ground have finally released their latest album, Vector Prime, on the Dies Irae label. Their last album was 1995's Measured By The Richter Scale. There are some fans who felt that Stompin' Ground have regrouped to release the album. But nothing is further from the truth, says Suhaimi Subandie, one of the group's founding members. Stompin' Ground have never disbanded, even though there are only two original members left - Suhaimi and drummer Ayong. Suhaimi also says that the group feels it owes its fans an album. He adds that while some songs were written as long as eight years ago, some were written only a year ago. The guitarist's comment on Vector Prime - "it's a brutal album."

    The album is currently available at Roxy Records (Funan The IT Mall) and HMV.
