    Hello there Sound of Music followers~

    Here's my entry for today...

    0800 - Went to work, assigned to service an aircraft... its rotor blades were due for weight adjustments. Ground run to start at 1000.

    0945 - Finished servicing, aircraft was towed out, waited for pilot. I waited till i need to go to the toilet, on the way there I was told that ground run would commence at 1230. Because the pilot they planned came in at 1200. Dumb planner.

    1015-1330 - Sat around... exchanging ringtones... had lunch (Fried rice n fried chicken), had a chat with the EO (Engineering Officer). Next thing I knew, pilot was out.

    1330 - 1430 - It took 3 ground runs to adjust the weight distribution of the tail rotor blades... Not bad, considering the longest ever was 16 ground runs and you had to stop for 1/2 hr for every 3 startups. Talk kok with pilot while the rotor tunists were adjusting the blades.

    1430 - 1530 - Rained non stop... HEAVY rain... we just sat in the container, talking kok, waiting for rain to stop before the other shift can commence work. Finally the line office called, and we could head home!

    And from the time i hit home, i went jogging, had a shower, i'm having my dinner now and i'm still wondering if i should go watch resident evil!!!!

    Thanks for reading
