    Sorry to sound rude, but i believe it is incorrect to refer to an accoustic guitar as a "folk" guitar. There is no such thing as a "folk guitar". Folk is just a genre of music that was established in the late 60's. And since the signature of the music was mainly accoustic, therefore many non-musicians started labelling guitars that were non-amplified with steel strings as "folk" guitars for lack of proper knowledge.

    just my two cents.
