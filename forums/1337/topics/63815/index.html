    This is from the Arts For Us All [AFUA] website, and I believe... is for the full De Trash event.

    10 magical days. One big stage. Orchard Road will be ablaze with jugglers, comedians, muralists, acrobats, dancers extraordinaire, break dancers and mimes. It's one outrageous party! Blues, rock, gospel, R&B, alternative and soul a spine-tingling musical variety guaranteed to shake your soul. Magic begins - things disappear, body art and more!..

    Also, there will be a visual display of artworks on canvas. Such includes paintings, graffiti and sketching. On top of that, interaction with the streets will be enhanced by having magicians, jugglers and clowns. The canvas for the people's creations will not be restricted by the borders of paper. Human canvases will be parading body art tickling the imaginations. Fret not, they will not be stark naked. Interested pedestrians may also participate in contributing to chalk art where anyone can draw on enclosed pavements for display.

    Each stage performer will be playing about 30 minutes and it will be a mixture of various items from cultural dance to punk rock.
