    What chords are u barring? I got it right through trial n error. There isn't a standard way to barre as different ppl's finger isn't the same.

    You may try pressing your left index finger directly on the fret when u r doing a full F chord. You have to decide exactly which part of the index finger to land on the strings that make minimum buzzing.

    For other chords, u may try to tilt the whole index finger outward abit, depending whether u can get a good barre n how comfortable it is to sustain that barre.

    Sometimes, it could be due to the poor workmanship of the guitar.
