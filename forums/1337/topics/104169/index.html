    This "the four seasons" violin concerto has 4 parts:-

    1) Spring : E major, Allegro - Largo - Allegro, Time - 10:20
    2) Summer : G minor, Allegro non molto - Adagio - Presto, Time - 11:40
    3) Autumn : F major, Allegro - Adagio molto - Allegro, Time - 10:40
    4) Winter : F minor, Allegro non molto - Largo - Allegro, Time - 9:25

    Composed by Antonio Lucio Vivaldi (1678 ~ 1741) ~ known as the "Master of Concertos"
