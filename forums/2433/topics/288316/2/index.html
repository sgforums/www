    Actually, instead of sticking things, what I have always been doing is use styrofoam slices. About the thickness of a bread loaf and the size of approximately 3cm * 7cm. In that way, no need to worry about sticking it somewhere where you do not want to, and no worries at all of load balancing.

    Just whack it in the center of any car and the styrofoam will conform to the shape of the car eventually and offer cushioning as well instead of hard materials.
