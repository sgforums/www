  • Jasonist
    5 Nov 07, 11:36
    as for me, i use erasers... cut them into cubes and wrapped them with matt black stickers (as u can see from the 1st pic)...

    then use cellotape to stick on those cubes and try to find a "good spot" on the undercarriage and stick there... note tat make sure u do not stick on the exhaust pipes or engine section coz may worry those parts do not support the weight of the model, especially on aa, kyosho cars... i can say tat the easiest car to jack up is the gallardo coz the undercarriage is plain and simple...

    oso do check out the balance of the car after u jack it up coz sometimes it might get high on the left side or low on the right side, then u have to re-adjust the jack up cubes again..

    and of coz, when u stick onto the undercarriage for a period of time and if u try to take the jack up cubes off, there will be some cellotape marks on the undercarriage lor... so before u make the decision to jack up ur models, do take note in this oso ya...
