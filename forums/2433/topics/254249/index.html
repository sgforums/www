  • Gallardofever
    13 May 07, 22:30
    Nikko Wheelock and Motorsports are on Orchard and within walking distance from each other.

    For Nikko Millenium, you'll have to take the train from Harbourfront(cruise centre) or Orchard(from the 2 stores) to Cityhall station and take a 20 mins walk vis Citilink to Millenium.

    You can take bus no. 65 from Harbourfront terminal to Orchard if you want to. That will take you straight to Orchard Road. Then from there, you can take bus no. 77 straight to Marina Centre and walk to Millenium. Save you some walking.
