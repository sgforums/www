    Yep, e guys r absolutely right.

    Also recommend u decide on e kinda cars u love,

    & ended up :
    1) Craving for models on higher ends eg. Autoart, Kyosho, den BBR & CMC.
    2) Selling away budget models & restartin a theme frm scratch (waste of $)

    i've started out collecting randomly,
    & spent tonnes of time & money trying to sell away old models,
    jus to rebuild my JDM theme.

    Cos for a 15yr old teenager as u,
    i recommend u decide on e theme, & your first model,
    den save up to buy 1 good model instead of many lower-end ones.
