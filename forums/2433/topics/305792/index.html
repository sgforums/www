    some old info i have. the places may not be there anymore. the opening hours may be incorrect. but still... hope it helps. AND the instructions to get there weren't written by me. DUN shoot me if they are incorrect.

     

    AA Collection Ltd.

    G/F 170 Gloucester Road

    Wan Chai, Hong Kong

    I think it's about the same distance between the Wan Chai and Causeway Bay subway stations. It's near the Immigration Tower. (near the waterfront expressway)

     

     

    Model Car Expo.

    37A, Waterloo Road, Mongkok, HK.

    they carried lots of diecast! You can take the underground train and alight at Yau Ma Tei Station or Mongkok Station but you have to walk around 10-15 minutes to reach there.

     

     

    Shop UG 13, CC Wu Building,

    302-308, Henessy Road, HK.

    Also there's one near Wan Chai in Hong Kong Island named New Ace (HK) Limited. The store is small but packed with lots of lots of diecast especially 1/43. I bought my dealer edition S-Klasse and M6 there. You can take the underground train and alight at either Wan Chai Station (took you about 10-15 minutes to reach there) or errr... I can't remember the station name this one but it locates in front of Times Square.

     

     

    HONG KONG MODEL CARS ASSOCIATION

    Shop 226, 2nd Floor, Richmond Shopping Centre, 107-111 Argyle Street, Mongkok, Kowloon, Hong Kong

    Tel: (852) 637 11 226  email: hkmodelcars@yahoo.com.hk  website: www.hkmodelcars.com

    Business Hours:

    04:30pm - 09:30pm (Monday – Friday)

    02:00pm - 10:00pm (Saturday, Sunday and Public Holidays)
