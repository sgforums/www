    yours photos are improving.

    here is some comments on ur photos.

    the photos appeared to be underexpose, abit dark. especially for the R8 and porsche GT.

    try to use table lamp and better lights to light up your models?

    the 2nd photo, u didn't get the focus area correct.

    i do not know abt the camera you using, whether if it can use manual settings.

    if can, use lower aperture like f/8 or f/11, and longer exposure.

    but make sure you use a tripod or books stacked up to a height u wanted to place your camera.

    the photos will get in more detail, but u needa play around with the shutter speed / exposure time.

    if u get it right, the photo will turn out well.

    the 3rd photo is overexpose, try to shift the lightings away abit or filter it with maybe a white paper or white towel wrapping the lamp or light.

    try to take photos at different angle, as some of your angles are rather high.

    take note when taking photos, try to cover the whole background with the same colour, like white, black or any colour.

    dun cover only the floor, as this will affect the overall image to not so realistic.

    play around more with your camera, composing / framing better shots.

    you are getting there. =)
