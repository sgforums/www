    Image Intensive
    Scale: 1 to 50
    Weight: About 3 kilograms
    Comes with: Lattice fly jib extension. Hook and accessories.

    The box, a bit of 'cheapo' feeling, a 30cm rule as scale.

    The crane itself.

    Stylish steering. All steering modes on the real thing can be done on the model (and this is also one of the reason I would buy it).

    They said that the real crane can lift 200 tons in this configuration. The configuration itself can be done on the model, but not lift 200 tons, nia...
    Price? SGD 450. Sells on the net for 480-550 inclusive of freight.
    Now finding suitable location and time to bring it for an outdoor shoot. Might get some weird looks for people but thats alright...
    Some pains; except for price, the weight is not very friendly, plus the boom's 'hydraulic' piston cannot do too much cycles; my friend changed his once per year when it became loose!
    But I hope this weird collection (I heard only ten odd people like to collect crane and construction related equipment in Sg) can also have its own share in Die Cast toys... Besides, this isn't really much of a toy, not suitable for children!
