    Nikko sells AUTOart, Minichamps, Kyosho, IXO, BBR... etc.

    There are 2 showrooms in Singapore:

    Millenia Showroom
    9 Raffles Boulevard
    #01-91B Millenia Walk
    Singapore 039596
    Tel: 6333 6786

    Wheelock Showroom
    501 Orchard Road #03-11/12
    Wheelock Place
    Singapore 238880
    Tel: 6732 7717

    Toykar retail outlet: the sole distributor of Kyosho that sells AUTOart, Hotwheels, Bbburago and of course Kyosho.

    RETAIL OUTLET
    583, Orchard Road
    B1-11 Forum The Shopping Mall
    Singapore 238884

    Tel: 6732 3197

    Mon - Sat: 11am to 8pm
    Sun: 11am to 7pm

    Gaincorp Asia sells 1/18 Kyosho, 1/43 Ferrari's and some 1/12s.
    Located at 505 Beach Road #02-164. it's at Golden Mile Food Centre, the army market.

    Motorsports sells AA, kyosho, hotwheels (elite), 1/43s etc.
    Located at #03-15 Delfi Orchard by the end of the year.

    Apart from these, there are departmental stores like takashimaya, bhg (formerly seiyu), isetan, og... etc. they carry mainly more affordable brands like maisto, bburago, tomica, tamiya (model kits), nikko rc cars... etc.

    You can also try yahoo! auctions. some of the collectors here, do post their 'for sale' models there to sell.

    Hope you have a clearer picture on where to get your desired scale models on this 'small red dot' called singapore!

    Cheers!
