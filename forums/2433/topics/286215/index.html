    16 Nov 07, 02:13
    let me contribute my 2 cents' worth.

    Nikko sells AA, minichamps, kyosho, IXO, BBR... etc.

    1. wheelock place (orchard mrt)
    2. millenia walk (city hall mrt)

    Toykar retail outlet the sole distributor of kyosho sells AA, hotwheels, bburago and of course kyosho.

    1. forum the shopping mall (orchard mrt)

    these are the main stores.

    there is one named Motorsports, located at Meritus Mandarin Hotel which also sells AA, kyosho, hotwheels (elite)... etc.

    apart from these, there are departmental stores like takashimaya, bhg (formerly seiyu), isetan, og... etc. they carry mainly more affordable brands like maisto, bburago, tomica, tamiya (model kits), nikko rc cars... etc.

    you can also try yahoo! auctions. some of the collectors here, do post their 'for sale' models there to sell.

    hope you have a clearer picture on where to get your desired scale models on this 'small red dot' called singapore!

    cheers!
