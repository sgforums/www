  • djgoh

    Hi Folks

    Since my childhood, I have loved model cars. Used to play with Matchboxes, Corgis and Majourettes model cars.

    I started a collection of 1/18 scale model cars while studying in England when I bought Bburagos, Maistos and Revells and then, when I came to Singapore in 1995, I bought UTs, Kyoshos and Autoarts. I even made a custom built display cabinet to show case the model cars.

    Then, when my collection got to around 50 cars, my interest stopped. Donno why. Probably it was because I ran out of display space.

    I've always wondered if there is an active model car collectors community in Singapore and it is funny that I only discovered this forum recently from someone who had advertised his model cars for sale in a 1/1 scale car forum that I frequent. I guess it wasn't in the interest of the retail shops in Singapore to tell me about the existence of this forum. Anyway, I am glad that I have found you folks here.

    Now, my passion for model car collecting has been revived - but in the 1/43 scale. In the past 8 months, I think I have accumulated 50+ 1/43 model cars, mainly supercars that I can't afford in real life. Well, I guess if I can't afford the real thing, a 1/43 scale car can also do lah.

    So, if you guys have 1/43 scale cars (especially Minichamps, Autoarts, iXos, Schucos and handbuilts) that you want to unload/trade, please let me know. Cheers!
