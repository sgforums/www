Stop building them as i lack of the skill & time to build it and now trying to sell all! Hence switch over to collect diecast!
    The 1st diecast i had was from my wife as a Vday present as she knows im very interested in Cars be it real 1:1 or diecast! It was a Hotwheel Red Honda Civic Coupe as im driving the Honda Civic SIR during that time follow by the Integra Type R DC2 and now the JDM STI! From then on i started collecting diecast cars since late 2004!
    The most diecast i got was when im in San Francisco, Los Angele & Las Vegas during my 13days honeymoon! A total of close to 40 diecast consist of 1:18, 1:24 & 1:43! Most models were the 2 Fast 2 Furious cars from Racing Champion. Was broke after the trip! Now collecting some AA, Kyosho & Minichamp. I like JDM cars especially the JGTC & WRC cars and also cars with decals! Will post my collection in another topic!
    Hope to learn more about diecast from you guys in the forum! Will try to join you guys in meetup(Miss the last showroom visit)! Cheers!
