    Let's begin this poll. If all of us decide which cars we would like to see made in 1:18 scale, sure that we will achieve it.    

    I'm gonna start a massive campaign to promote this idea to all the forums about 1:18 scale. This way, ALL the collectors can will participate in this poll, and we will obtain better results. I wait for your participation.

    It's simple. You can propose models, and I'll update the list in all the forums. You can also vote for the listed models, and I'll add the number of votes on each car.  You can also link an small pic of the model.

    PLEASE, CHOOSE VERY WELL YOUR CAR IN ORDER THAT THE MAJOR NUMBER OF PERSONS COULD LIKE IT. So here we go. Let's start!
