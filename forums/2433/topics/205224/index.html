    Hmm..
    unlike Eddy, i passed on 3rd attempt 1 1/2 yrs ago.
    Now waiting to convert my Class 3 to drive military vehicles.

    My tips for u:
    - Sleep early night b4, cause if not, u wont b able to focus.
    - Jus relax & be yourself. No point being tense-up & end up doing stupid mistakes & last minute corrections. Show e tester you're confident.
    - Dont accl or brake suddenly (smooth accl/deaccl).

    Circuit test:
    - Dont panick with e upslope. Get to biting-point, & ram abit harder, let e car surge abit forward, b4 releasing handbrake. Then ram slightly more to pull-off e slope.
    - Parking sections, it comes with practise.. nothin much i can help.. but jus make sure u REMEMBER to check blind-spots, jus "action action" abit lor.

    Road test:
    - Be alert of e cars in-front. Dont panic. Jus listen to e tester's directions.
    - Singal early, & check blind spots & remember to off signal after lane changes,
    which alot of ppl forget.
    - For U-turns, b4 u stop, ensure u're not too close to e inner-kerb, which will allow u to hav a wider room to turn quickly.
    - Focus further in-front e road & anticipate, instead of only focusing on e car infront. Experienced drivers will always focus far ahead & anticipate e rd condition.

    Tat's as much as i'm able to help u frm my personal drivin experiences, pal.
    All e very best.
