    A little bit of ellaboration on the tiger tattoo. Actually tiger tattoo represents strength, power and able to attract wealth. If lion is known to be the King of the Wood, tiger is regarded as the Lord of the Mountain. Nothing gets in the tiger’s way thus bearer of the tiger tattoo would experience a smooth and obstacle-free life. The head of the tiger carries the Chinese character which means “King (çŽ‹)”. Because tiger is an ultimately strong and fearless animal and is associated with a potent male principle of courage, bravery, dignity and sternness, this “supreme ruler of the mountains” is said to have the power to drive away demons. The eyes of the tiger strikes deep fear into its enemies and terrified them away. Thus the tiger tattoo is able to keep all the bearer’s adversaries away from him. The tiger is a “yang” animal and its strong “yang” energy is able to protect and defense against evil invasion. The claws of the tiger not only serve as a deadly weapon but the scratches on trees done by the tiger serve as territorial markers. Hence it is also believe that the tiger tattoo is able to help a businessman to conquer all his obstacles and expand his business empire. A tiger's roar has the power to paralyze the animal that hears it thus helping bearer of the tiger tattoo to gain higher respect from others. Overall the tiger tattoo can serves the purpose of kongkapan, klaew klaad and maha larp.
