    If you are using a firmware of 3.71 M33 or higher, go and download the custom firmware either 3.80 M33 or 3.90 M33, do not download the one with (Kernel 1.50) cause I think by now most of the firmware should not be using that.

    PM me for the link of the 3.80 custom firmware. ( I do not want to publicize the link for security purpose)

    1-Make sure you have EBOOT.PBP file, 3.80.PBP file. If you do not have the 3.80.PBP file, your update wont run.

    2-Make sure your batt life is up to 78% at least.

    3-Put the file into the directories, PSP/GAME.

    4-Run the update, and you are done.

    5-Press X or O to restart the PSP, do not turn it off yourself, or you will get a nicely brick PSP
