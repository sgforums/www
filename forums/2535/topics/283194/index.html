  • FrozernFire
    16 Dec 07, 00:46
    try pressing the HOME button at your xmb (your psp's main menu). go to the umd driver option, and press left or right until you reach the m33 or sony driver. either one should do. exit the vshmenu.

    if it does not have a vshmenu, completely shut of your psp (not sleep). hold the R trigger, and turn on your psp. you should reach a black and red recovery menu. go to configuration > umd driver > press x until you reach the m33 or sony driver, either one should do. exit the menu, exit the recovery menu
