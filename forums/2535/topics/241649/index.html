    GST
    Its the most common way that salesman use to cheat the public. a few pointer to let know to everyone here. GST stands for goods services tax. NOT ANY AND EVERY SHOP CAN TAKE GST FROM YOU. Under Singapore law only if that show is registered with the government on GST then it can charge you gst as every cent of the gst you pay go straight to the government,

    Every company that are registered with the government on GST WILL HAVE AN GST REGISTRATION NUMBER and this number MUST be show on the company official receipt.

    And if you suspect that the number shown on the receipt are fake you can call up government bodies to check the validity of this reg number against the shop name and details. It will be jail term under Singapore law for anyone who used an fake gst reg num or charge ppl gst but his shop is not registered with gst or claims that he is gst registered but unable to give you the reg number. You can just call government bodies to check on his shop license and everything will be out clear.
