    There are some Pros and Cons about the PSP.

    Pros:
    -Its kinda portable and gives very visual gaming experience.
    -The games can be downloaded and played[provided that you upgrade your software]
    -It can act as a movie player or mp3 player
    -It can go online. E.g. you can use it to surf net or use msn[use ebuddy]

    Cons:
    -If you upgraded/downgraded your firmware, it means that your PSP's warranty is void by Sony. Meaning that Sony won't want to repair it for you because you modified it.
    -If you don't upgrade/downgrade your firmware, you will have to buy those expensive UMD games.
    -The speaker is too soft. Sometimes you won't be able to hear the sound effect of the game you are playing.
    -It does not have a PSP anti virus. Meaning the PSP is vulnerable to PSP viruses which can cause problems to your PSP.

    These are some of the Pros and Cons I have about the PSP. List yours too~
