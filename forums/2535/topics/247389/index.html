    ISO:
    Either you buy in DVDs, or ask some shops to save it into your memory card, with some charges, probably $3~9 bucks according to my cousin who's a regular customer at some shops that sells PSP.

    Alternatively, you can always download it from the net, but bear the risk of it might be a virus.

    Also, it takes quite awhile to search for the correct game and of course time to download them, most games are of quite big file size, so it's gonna take a few hours normally.
