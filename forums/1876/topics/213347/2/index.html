    I'm using the above 3.

    In terms of design, I'd rank 800 first, followed by 810 and 320.

    In terms of user-friendliness, 810, 320, 800.

    810's T9 predictive text is more stable and the user will be able to key in messages at a much faster speed. As for 800 and 320, the T9 is somewhat similar but you cannot input it as fast as the 810.

    KG800's touchpad is sensitive. Do not treat it as a button - it's a touchpad. Users with big thumbs may find difficulty in using in as they might tend to hit the wrong button.
