    Nokia 7260 :

    Stylish , light and elegant hp for fashion people basically. I was deciding between this hp and 6230. I couldn't make up my mind. Both have it's pros and cons. The final decision to get this hp was for it's design and I didn't need the video steaming and mp3 function. What was most important to me was the infra ray, good camera and a FM radio.

    Pros
    - Loud speaker when playing FM radio.
    - Not much hardware problem comparing with earlier colour nokia hps like 7210 or 6610
    - Buttons easy to press
    - Reception for this hp model is pretty good
    - Ample memory space to install your programmes
    - Some nice accessories like hp protecting cover and different headset from other models excluding 7270 and 7280
    -Good colour 65K screen

    Cons
    - Finger prints on the silvery key press especially if you have a sweaty palm , you have to keep wiping it ...
    - Using avi as ringing tone, for my hp , sometimes when there is call , the avi tone will cut off half way for no reason.
    - Using the headset to listen to radio , I find the volume too soft for my liking even through I adjust it to the highest level .

    Conclusion

    Overall, I am happy with the phone, even through it's a fashion phone, I recommand this model to anyone who is thinking of this model and for those who are into fashion. You may not have the mp3 player , bluetooth or video steaming, but it's a good and reliable phone to use.
