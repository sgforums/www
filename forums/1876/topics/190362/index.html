Step by Step on how to recondition your old battery.

    1) Wrap the old batt in newspaper, follow by put them in a plastic bag and seal it.

    2) Put them into the freezer for 3 days.

    3) Remove from freezer and place it at room temperature for 1 day.

    4) Remove the plastic bag and place it at room temperature for 1 day.

    5) Remove newspaper and full charge the batt like it brand new ~ 8hrs.

    This can restore the battery lifespan up to 80%.
