    Check your IMEI, it is composed of 15 digits like this :
    AAAAAA/BB/CCCCCC/D

    AAAAAA = Type of the phone (mass production have more than 1 code)
    CCCCCC = Serial Number
    D = IMEI verifyer

    Check your BB.
    10 = Finland
    20 = Germany
    30 = Korea
    40 = China
    50 = Brazil, USA, Finland
    60 = Hong Kong, China, Mexico
    70 = Finland
    80 = Hungary
    91 = Finland

    You can check your IMEI to find out more info about your nokia handphone .
    Where to find your IMEI ? It's on the box of your handphone.
