    Registration numbers of all SBS air con double deckers a.k.a. superbuses are in 4 digits and starts with the number '9'. Current registration is 9000 to 9690, 9800 to 9849 and 9888.

    So, if you are at SBS bus interchanges, there is the TV displaying 'Next Bus Departure Time'. Shown on the TV, you can look at the bus registration number of the service you are taking and then know if the next bus is a superbus or not (of course NOT applicable for services with only single deckers in the whole fleet). If the number displayed is 4 digits and the first digit is '9', that mean it's a superbus.

    As for non air con double deckers, registration numbers is in 4 digits and starts with '7'.
