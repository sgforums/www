    How to take a bus

    In view of the fact that 2 people already asked me this qns b4, think i better hold a lesson on it

    1. Flag for the bus to stop in advance

    2. Hop onto the bus and tap ur ezlink card on the lower part of the blue reader, u shld hear a 'beep' sound n the light shld be green. (In the case where the light flash red instead n the sound goes 'beep beep beep', it either means that u hv no more $ in ur card or that ur card is faulty. Therefore, make sure u hv coins w/ u n ask the bus driver how much to pay n rem to collect ur tix).

    3. Press the 'Stop' button in advance when u reach ur destination n rem to tap ur ezlink card once again n make sure its 'green' light~ if not go to the front n ask the driver oh ya n 'yes', u can exit n tap ur card at the front

    that will be all for today~
