    Bunny Stew:

    Ingredients:
    1 Bunny (preferably hopy )
    200 gram of potato (diced)
    200 gram of carrot (diced)
    Dark soya sauce
    Corn flour
    Bean paste
    Sugar
    Sesame oil

    Preparation Method:
    Fill remove the skin, intestines and bones of the bunny
    Put the bones into the pot half filled with water
    Cover the pot with the lid and boil the water for 30 minutes
    Remove the bones
    Dice bunny meat into small portions
    Pour the bunny meat, potato, carrot and bean paste into the pot of water
    Pour dark soya sauce and corn flour into pot of water
    Add sugar and sesame oil
    Boil the water till the gravy thickens
    Serve while hot.
