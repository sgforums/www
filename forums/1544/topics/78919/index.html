    The brake pads are new, but using a different material.

    Most of the problems are part of the first year "hiccups" that every all-new model goes through nowadays.

    I believe this problems are caused by rampant platform and parts sharing. Somehow, a part made for another car does not work very well on another car, even though they are made to be so.

    Back then when every brand was unique, such problems were few.

    That's why Toyota/Lexus make the most reliable cars on the road, and score very high in the initial quality surveys. They don't share their parts with any other brand. Each model is built and designed on its own merits!
