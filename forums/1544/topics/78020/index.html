    Saabster: There are many free image hosting sites out there. I use Photobucket

    After you upload your pics (check for maximum file size, etc) you can hotlink your images to anything you post on forums, message boards, auction sites, etc.

    Now go to the album containing the image you want to post. You'll find several options for that image. Cut and paste the one that says "[IMG]your image URL[/IMG] "

    Some sites have the word "IMG" in upper case. This forum requires "img" to be in lower case, so you gotta do that.
