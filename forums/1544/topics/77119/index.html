    I found this on Saabcentral:

    The badges are held on with foam double-stick tape - very easy to remove... the easiest way:

    1) wash the area very well to remove any dust/dirt.
    2) using a hairdryer, heat up the badge to be removed.
    3) while still warm, use dental floss to 'slice' through the tape and remove the badge.
    4) remove remaining adhesive with 3M Adhesive/Glue remover
    5) use a light scratch remover such as Meguiar's ScratchX to lightly polish where the badge was.
    6) wax the areas well.
