Saab 93 vs Volvo S40

  • SwedeSport
    8 Apr 04, 13:23
    My cousin and I collected his Volvo S40 2.4i last night, and I got to drive it to his home.

    It is really a very impressive car. It is just as big as our 93, but with NO rattles or buzz! Built very solid. Door closes with a German thud. The boot also! Suspension is more firm than the 93, so handling is better.

    The features that are in the S40 and not in our 93:
    - Seat belt reminder for ALL seats (front and rear)
    - Retractable wing mirrors and heated
    - Side repeaters and puddle lights on wing mirrors
    - Driver's memory seat and wing mirrors
    - Passenger seat with manual height adjustment
    - Glovebox with lock
    - Sleek brushed metal console and trimmings
    - Firmer seats
    - 4 steel strength types
    - Panic "horning" on remote

    Features in 93, not in S40:
    - Auto dimming rearview mirror
    - Aux port
    - Multi CD changer
    - Turbo power

    Volvo made a mistake when building the car, and included the High Performance sound system instead of the normal one. VERY GOOD! All 8 separate treble and bass woofers on doors. No need to fade here and there. Acoustics is perfect.

    Except for the turbo, I really enjoyed driving the car! If Saab screws up with the all-new 95, looks like my next change might be the all-new S60 lar.

    12 Apr 04, 11:04
    Over the weekend, I had a good chance to be better acquainted with the S40.

    1. Get this, SM does not provide a breakdown triangle with the S40! Penny pinchers!

    2. The balancing weights are attached to the outer edge of the rim, on the outside. So it can be seen! Duh!!

    3. Seat belt reminder only for the driver, SM disabled the rest because some dinky customers got irritated when they placed their "fat ass" bag on the seat and it triggered the alarm!

    4. There are only 2 tiedown loops in the booth, like the 2004 93.

    5. Does not have EBD (Electronic Brakeforce Distribution). The 93 brakes are much, much better. Apparently, this is true for all non-T or non-R Volvos! So if we race with a normal Volvo, and we need to brake in an emergency, the Volvo will be WAY ahead of us!!

    6. No pass-thru ski hatch.

    I think if I can improve the ride quality, my 93 will come up on top.
