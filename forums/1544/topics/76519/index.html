    I've removed my door panel before. After you get the door handle covers off and remove the 2 torx screws, you need quite a strong flat instrument to pry off the panel.

    Here's where you gotta be really careful. Make sure your first point of insertion is exactly as depicted in the GenuineSaab document. Also, DO NOT insert too deep, else you will damage the internal door seal.
    Max depth is about 1cm.

    Don't bother trying to disconnect all the wires and cables attached to the door panel. Just get a low stool or beer crate and place the panel upright on it.
