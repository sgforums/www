    I've driven the new Legacy 2.0i when I was shopping for a new car, and I can tell you it's fab!

    Before testing the Legacy, I drove the Mazda6 and Honda Accord (JDM model, aka Acura in the US).

    Mazda6:----------------------------------------------------------------------That's all I have to say - nothing

    Accord: tractable 150bhp I-VTEC, xenon lights, nice driving position. The let-down to me was that with doors closed, you can still see the painted metal of the window frame - cheap looking in my opinion.

    Legacy: nicest sounding engine, best driving position, biggest boot, frameless windows, classy 7-speaker sound system, most ergonomic interior/fascia design, sharp handling. BUT... brakes only so-so, 138bhp fed thru 4-wheels = underpower + most inefficient drive configuration, A/T is only a 4-shifter.

    SAAB 9-3SS: I bought it cos when I open the bonnet, I don't have to look for a pesky hot metal piece to prop it up.
