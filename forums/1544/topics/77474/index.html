    Hi, talked to Tye about this too, but unfortunately no. They have to change the whole head unit and changer if you want to play DVDs or MP3.
    For VCD, it is just a "adapter box" that they connect to the changer and this allows the changer to decode VCDs. All your hifi controls are unchanged and you can change VCDs from the steering etc. You just need to add a screen somewhere to view the movie.
